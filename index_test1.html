"Sports Frenzy: Trophies and Titles Abound in Cricket, Hockey, and Judo Championships"

2025-02-23T19:44:00+00:00

The HR department at Times of India organised 'Howzzat 2025', an inter-departmental stress-buster cricket tournament. Press Super Kings defeated Top Giants by 30 runs to lift the trophy. Rajat Nishad was adjudged man of the match for his 18 runs and a wicket effort. Shivam Shukla was adjudged man of the tournament while Aditya Murti and Anil Mishra walked away with best batsman and best bowler trophies respectively. SSB Lucknow won in the 2nd Pt Ram Avtar Mishra Men's Hockey Tournament, thrashing Sports College, Lucknow 'A' 4-1, with goals from A Shanti Kumar, Bikash Kujur, Francis Toppo, Parveen Chauhan, and Aman Sharma. The National Para Judo Championships kicked off at Atal Bihari Vajpayee Multi-purpose Hall, with 48 gold, 48 silver, and 96 bronze medals up for grabs. Over 400 players and officials, including 20 international players like Kapil Parmar and Kokila, will participate in the championships.
