Varanasi Drenched in Holi Colors, Culture, and Celebration

2025-03-15T20:05:00+00:00

Holi celebrations in Varanasi began with Holika bonfire on Thursday and continued through Saturday. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath sent special Kachnar flower gulal to Lord Vishwanath Mahadev at Kashi Vishwanath Temple, a tradition started in 2024. Naga Sadhus and saints of seven Shaiva Akharas, who attended the Prayagraj Kumbh, celebrated Holi in Varanasi before departing. Families played Holi in their localities, while youths gathered at ghats along the Ganga, dancing to music from roadside DJs. Crowds dispersed by 1 pm due to hot weather, but roads leading to prominent ghats remained busy. Police dispersed crowds before Juma namaz.
