Uttar Pradesh Cracks Down on Child Marriages

2025-04-29T19:19:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh authorities are intensifying surveillance to prevent child marriages on Akshay Tritiya. ADM (City) Alok Verma in Varanasi led the drive, requesting officials from various departments to maintain strict vigil. Similar efforts are underway in all districts, with officials emphasizing the Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006, which sets the legal marriage age at 21 for boys and 18 for girls. Those involved in child marriages face penalties including imprisonment and fines. Authorities are urging the public to report any suspected cases on the helpline 1098, assuring confidentiality and prompt action. Despite efforts, child marriage cases persist, with four reported in Varanasi in 2024-25 and six in 2023-24.
