Highway Collision Claims Two Lives

2025-04-14T18:58:00+00:00

Two people were killed in a collision between an SUV and a motorcycle on the Varanasi-Azamgarh highway near Gola village in Uttar Pradesh. The SUV driver, Sunil Gupta (35), and the motorcyclist, Neeraj Yadav (32), died from their injuries at Cholapur community health centre. The accident occurred around 12:30 am when Gupta, driving towards Varanasi, reportedly hit Yadav's motorcycle, causing the SUV to overturn. Local residents alerted the police who are investigating the incident.
