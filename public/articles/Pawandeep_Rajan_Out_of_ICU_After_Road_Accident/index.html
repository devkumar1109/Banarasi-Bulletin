Pawandeep Rajan Out of ICU After Road Accident

2025-05-08T13:15:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan, critically injured in a road accident on May 5 in Uttar Pradesh, has been moved out of the ICU and into a private room. His friend Govind Digari shared a picture from the hospital, confirming Rajan's improvement. The accident occurred when Rajan's SUV collided with a truck, resulting in multiple fractures and a head injury. He underwent a six-hour surgery to address major fractures and is currently under observation. A further operation is scheduled in 3-4 days to address remaining fractures and injuries.
