College Proctor Accused of Sexual Abuse

2025-03-18T04:31:00+00:00

Rajneesh, the Chief Proctor of PC Bagla Degree College in Hathras, India, has been booked for allegedly sexually exploiting female students. He allegedly assaulted them under the pretext of helping them pass exams, secure jobs, and make other promises. A case has been registered under multiple sections of the Indian Penal Code (IPC), and three investigation teams have been formed to arrest him. Uttar Pradesh State Women's Commission Vice President Aparna Yadav condemned the incident, emphasizing its seriousness and calling for swift justice. The Chief Minister, Yogi Adityanath, reviewed law and order and district development issues in Varanasi on March 12, addressing concerns about safety and security.
