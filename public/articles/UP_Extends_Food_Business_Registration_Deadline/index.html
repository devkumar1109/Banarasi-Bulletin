UP Extends Food Business Registration Deadline

2025-05-07T13:11:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government extended its food business registration campaign to May 31st. Launched by the Food Safety and Drug Administration (FSDA) in April, the initiative requires all food businesses to register and obtain licenses to ensure compliance with the Food Safety and Standards Act. The state government emphasizes that the campaign prioritizes public health by regulating food operations, as unregulated businesses can pose significant risks. District-level officers are holding camps to facilitate the registration and licensing process for food vendors and restaurant owners. After May 31st, strict action, including business closure, will be taken against non-compliant establishments.
