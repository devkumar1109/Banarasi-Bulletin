Modi's Varanasi Boost: Rs 3884 Crore in Development

2025-04-10T13:05:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Varanasi on April 11th, launching infrastructure and development projects worth Rs 3884 crore. Key highlights include a Rs 652.64 crore six-lane tunnel under NH-31 for Babatpur Airport expansion, enabling 110 daily flights. Other projects encompass an airport area development, water mission, educational institutions, sports facilities, road widening, and a flyover. PM Modi will also launch a Rs 12 crore transport city project in Mohansarai and a Rs 6.15 crore mini stadium in Shivpur. Additionally, Rs 345.12 crore will be allocated for 130 rural drinking water schemes under the Jal Jeevan Mission, while a 400 kV power substation and transmission lines will receive over Rs 1000 crore.
