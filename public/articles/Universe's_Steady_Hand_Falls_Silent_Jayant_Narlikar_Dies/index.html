Universe's Steady Hand Falls Silent: Jayant Narlikar Dies

2025-05-20T10:11:00+00:00

Indian astrophysicist and science fiction writer Jayant Narlikar passed away on May 20, 2025. He was known for challenging the prevailing 'Big Bang' theory in cosmology. Narlikar proposed an alternative model called the 'Steady State' theory, which suggested the universe has always existed and is constantly creating new matter. He was a prolific writer and researcher, contributing significantly to our understanding of the universe.
