Parrot Smuggling Bus Busted in Varanasi

2025-06-01T19:39:00+00:00

Forest officials in Varanasi arrested three smugglers and seized a bus carrying 28 live parrots from Jharkhand. The smugglers, identified as Dhananjay Singh, Abhay Singh alias Kanhaiya, and Md Arif, were apprehended during a raid in the Andharapul area. Md Arif organized the transport of the parrots from Garhwa, Jharkhand. The birds are protected under the Wildlife Protection Act of 1972, and legal proceedings are underway against the accused under various sections of the Act. Forest officials urge citizens to report any illegal wildlife trade to their designated phone numbers.
