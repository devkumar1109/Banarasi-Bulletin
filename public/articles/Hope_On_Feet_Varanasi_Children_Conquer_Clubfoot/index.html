Hope On Feet: Varanasi Children Conquer Clubfoot

2025-04-27T19:07:00+00:00

Since 2019, 70 children in Varanasi have recovered from clubfoot through a health scheme run by the government and the Anushka foundation. 512 children have been treated under the program, with 64 fully recovering. In April 2025, nine children received treatment, with six undergoing surgery. Clubfoot is treatable with the Ponseti method, which involves casting, a minor outpatient procedure, and wearing a brace for several years. Local health workers can refer affected children for free treatment and monitoring through sabalkashi.com. This condition affects one in 800 children.
