UP Burns: Pathak Slams SP Over "Derogatory" Remarks

2025-05-20T09:32:00+00:00

Tensions have escalated in Uttar Pradesh following alleged derogatory remarks against Deputy CM Brajesh Pathak by the Samajwadi Party's official 'X' handle. Posters demanding an apology from SP chief Akhilesh Yadav have appeared in Lucknow. Pathak criticized Yadav's response to a query, accusing him of deflecting from questions about the SP's alleged connections to controversial figures and past scandals. Pathak challenged Yadav to address the alleged dark legacy of the SP, claiming that discussing the party's DNA would reveal connections to notorious individuals and events like the Muzaffarnagar riots, the firing on kar sevaks during the Ram Mandir movement, and various scams.
