CPI(M) Picks New Chief, BJP Celebrates, Nation in Political Stir

2025-04-06T02:50:00+00:00

M A Baby, former Kerala minister, has been elected as the general secretary of the CPI(M) at the 24th party congress meeting. The BJP is celebrating its 45th foundation day on Sunday with programs across Uttar Pradesh, led by state chief Bhupendra Singh Chaudhary. Suvendu Adhikari, BJP leader in West Bengal, participated in a Ram Navami procession, asserting a "saffron wave" in the state and claiming no division among Hindus. The Rashtriya Janata Dal plans to challenge the Waqf Bill in the Supreme Court. Prime Minister Narendra Modi flagged off a new train service between Rameswaram and Tambaram (Chennai) in Tamil Nadu, running on the new Pamban Bridge. The AICC session is taking place in Gujarat, with Sachin Pilot emphasizing the focus on strengthening the party there and restoring its past glory.
