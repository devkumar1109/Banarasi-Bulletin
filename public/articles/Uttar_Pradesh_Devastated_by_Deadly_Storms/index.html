Uttar Pradesh Devastated by Deadly Storms

2025-05-22T18:30:00+00:00

Heavy rain and lightning storms have caused widespread devastation across Uttar Pradesh, India, resulting in the tragic death of 34 people. The severe weather conditions have led to damaged homes, uprooted trees, and power outages in various regions of the state. Rescue and relief efforts are underway to assist those affected by the storms.
