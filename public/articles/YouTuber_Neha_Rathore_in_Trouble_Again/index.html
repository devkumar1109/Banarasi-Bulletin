YouTuber Neha Rathore in Trouble Again

2025-05-21T09:54:00+00:00

A FIR has been registered against YouTuber Neha Singh Rathore in Varanasi for allegedly making derogatory remarks against Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The complaint, filed by the President of Shri Hanuman Sena, accuses Rathore of calling Modi a "coward" and "General Dyer." This is the second case against Rathore this year. In April, she was booked in Lucknow for sedition and other charges related to comments she made about the Pahalgam terror attack.
