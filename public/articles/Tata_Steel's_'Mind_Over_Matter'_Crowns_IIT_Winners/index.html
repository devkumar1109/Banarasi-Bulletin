Tata Steel's 'Mind Over Matter' Crowns IIT Winners

2025-03-10T13:03:00+00:00

Tata Steel concluded its 10th 'Mind Over Matter' innovation challenge with Namita Dubey from IIT (ISM) Dhanbad emerging as the winner. Ravu Ravi Teja from IIT (BHU) Varanasi secured 1st runner-up, and Kurapati Harshavardhan from the same institute won 2nd runner-up. The competition saw a record-breaking 660+ participants from 37 institutions. Winners received cash prizes, certificates, and Pre-Placement Offers to join Tata Steel's R&D team as Management Trainees. Finalists presented their solutions to a jury comprising Tata Steel executives and were mentored by R&D leadership during a six-month internship.
