Jaunpur Car Scam Busted: Gang Members Arrested

2025-03-21T17:43:00+00:00

A robbery gang operating in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, was busted by the Baksha police. The gang, led by Jitendra Prasad Yadav, lured victims by offering second-hand cars at low prices. Upon meeting, the gang would tie up victims, assault them, and steal their cash. One victim, Himendra Nishad, escaped with his companion and reported the incident to the police. The police recovered Rs 11,000, three steel chains, ropes, a country-made revolver, and live cartridges. Six other gang members were also arrested.
