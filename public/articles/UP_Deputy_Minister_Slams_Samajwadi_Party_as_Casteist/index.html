UP Deputy Minister Slams Samajwadi Party as Casteist

2025-05-19T10:24:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak has accused the Samajwadi Party of being founded on casteism and appeasement. He made these statements without providing specific examples or evidence. This accusation comes amidst ongoing political rivalry between the BJP and the Samajwadi Party in Uttar Pradesh. The Samajwadi Party, however, has not yet responded to Pathak's claims.
