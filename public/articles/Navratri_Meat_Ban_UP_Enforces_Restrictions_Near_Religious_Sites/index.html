Navratri Meat Ban: UP Enforces Restrictions Near Religious Sites

2025-03-29T14:31:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has banned the sale of meat and eggs within 500 meters of religious places during Navratri to maintain sanctity. Illegal slaughterhouses have been ordered shut. The ban will be strictly enforced on April 6th, Ram Navami, with no animal slaughter or meat sales allowed. Violators will face action under the UP Municipal Corporation Act and the Food Safety Act. District-level committees comprising police, health, and food safety officials will monitor the situation. This decision reinstates similar orders from 2014 and 2017.
