Yogi Unveils Rs 32,000 Crore UP Projects

2025-03-10T14:52:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath highlighted projects worth Rs 32,000 crore in Sarojini Nagar, including a new convention centre and a Defence Corridor. He also announced the state's first forensic institute to improve crime investigations. CM Yogi emphasized selfless public service and stated that 2.5 crore farmers benefit from the PM Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme. The CM praised PM Modi for improving connectivity, particularly in Meerut, which is home to the new Major Dhyanchand Sports University. This university is expected to significantly advance sports in the state.
