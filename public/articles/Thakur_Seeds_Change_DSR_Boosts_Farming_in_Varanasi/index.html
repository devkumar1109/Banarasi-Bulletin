Thakur Seeds Change: DSR Boosts Farming in Varanasi

2025-06-13T04:21:00+00:00

Union Minister Ramnath Thakur visited Varanasi to promote sustainable farming practices under the Viksit Krishi Sankalp Abhiyan. He reviewed Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) technology demonstrations at Paniyara village, observing its benefits in water conservation, emission reduction, and increased efficiency. ISARC is implementing 200 hectares of DSR cluster demonstrations across eight districts in Varanasi and Gorakhpur divisions. The Minister praised science-backed innovations supporting climate-resilient farming and interacted with farmers who have adopted DSR to conserve water and boost productivity.
