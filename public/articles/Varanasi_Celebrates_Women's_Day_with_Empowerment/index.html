Varanasi Celebrates Women's Day with Empowerment

2025-03-08T20:02:00+00:00

On International Women's Day, Varanasi celebrated equality and empowerment through various events. Mahila Mahavidyalaya, BHU, hosted a workshop and panel discussion on empowering women and girls. BHU also organized sports events and an oratory competition. The Green Women Army showcased their handmade items. DAV PG College held a seminar on gender equality, while Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW) organized a grand ceremony featuring cultural performances and honoring retiring women employees. Vishal Bharat Sansthan's Women's Council discussed the role of women in Indian culture and highlighted the undervaluation of domestic work.
