Preity Zinta's Divine Shivratri Pilgrimage

2025-03-02T18:38:00+00:00

Actress Preity Zinta recently took a religious trip from Prayagraj to Varanasi with her mother. They visited the holy places as part of a pilgrimage culminating in Shivratri celebrations at the Kashi Vishwanath temple. Due to heavy crowds, they had to navigate through the city via car, auto rickshaw and even cycle rickshaw. Preity observed the crowd to be generally good and respectful. She highlighted the joy and happiness her mother experienced throughout the journey, emphasizing the importance of caring for parents. The trip culminated with witnessing the midnight Arti at the temple, which Preity described as a precious blessing.
