HPV Vaccine: Varanasi Fights Cervical Cancer

2025-04-28T19:34:00+00:00

An awareness program in Varanasi focused on HPV vaccine, cervical cancer, and screening. Prof. Sangeeta Rai, head of Obstetrics and Gynaecology at IMS, BHU, emphasized that cervical cancer is the second leading cause of cancer-related deaths among women globally. Regular screening significantly reduces its incidence and mortality. The program, "Do Teeke Zindagi Ke," highlighted the importance of both vaccination and screening. It aimed to educate women and young men, recognizing that awareness is a collective responsibility.
