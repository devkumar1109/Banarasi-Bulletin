UP: From Bimaru to Growth Engine

2025-03-27T12:17:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state has transformed into a 'growth engine' over the past eight years due to honest governance and administrative reforms. He attributes successes like the Maha Kumbh and international recognition of yoga to this progress, contrasting it with the state's past "Bimaru" status. Adityanath highlights new employment opportunities arising from cultural pride, citing Azamgarh's resurgence through infrastructure and education initiatives. He believes these developments will prepare Uttar Pradesh's youth for global opportunities.
