JEE Main 2025: Math Makes Waves in Session 2

2025-04-03T04:05:00+00:00

The JEE Main 2025 Session 2 exam is underway. Day 2 saw two shifts, with students reporting math as the most difficult subject in shift 1, while both math and physics were considered easy in shift 2. The exam is being held across India and 15 international locations. The BTech and BE papers are scheduled for April 2-4, 7-8, while BArch and BPlanning papers are on April 9. Successful candidates will be eligible for JoSAA counselling for admission to NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs. The top 2.5 lakh JEE Main 2025 qualifiers will qualify for JEE Advanced 2025.
