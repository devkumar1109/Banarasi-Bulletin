UP Seeks Rice Boost: DSR Tech Gains Traction

2025-05-27T13:17:00+00:00

Anurag Yadav, Principal Secretary of Uttar Pradesh, visited the International Rice Research Institute – South Asia Regional Centre (ISARC) with a World Bank delegation on May 27, 2025. The visit aimed to strengthen collaborations and scale the adoption of Direct-Seeded Rice (DSR) technology in the state. Discussions centered around scalable strategies, convergence opportunities, and capacity-building approaches to mainstream DSR across diverse agro-ecological zones. Key areas of focus included enhancing seed system readiness, farmer training, policy alignment, and strengthening public-private partnerships. The visit followed a field trip to Paniyara village in Varanasi on May 26, 2025. Yadav emphasized the state government's efforts to boost agricultural productivity, citing the recently launched UP-AGREES project.
