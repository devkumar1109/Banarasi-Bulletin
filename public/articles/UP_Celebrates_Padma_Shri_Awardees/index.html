UP Celebrates Padma Shri Awardees

2025-04-28T18:46:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath congratulated four individuals from the state who were awarded the Padma Shri by President Droupadi Murmu. The recipients are Dr Shyam Bihari Agrawal, Hriday Narayan Dikshit, Ganeshwar Shastri Dravid, and Dr Satyapal Singh. They were honored for their significant contributions to Indian culture and society. Adityanath highlighted their achievements and noted that their work embodies Indian philosophy and life values. The awardees acknowledged the collective effort behind their recognition, dedicating the honor to their supporters and community.
