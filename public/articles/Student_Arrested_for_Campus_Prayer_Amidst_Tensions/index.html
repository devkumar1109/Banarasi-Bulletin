Student Arrested for Campus Prayer Amidst Tensions

2025-03-16T11:06:00+00:00

A student, Khalid Pradhan, was arrested in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, for allegedly offering namaz in an open area of IIMT University. The arrest followed protests by local Hindu groups after a video surfaced showing a group of students praying on campus. The university administration suspended Pradhan and three security personnel, calling for police action. A case was filed under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and the Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008. Police said the incident occurred amid heightened religious tension, coinciding with Holi celebrations and the second Friday prayers of Ramzan.
