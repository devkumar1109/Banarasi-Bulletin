Rajamouli's SSMB29: Varanasi on a Screen

2025-06-17T03:59:00+00:00

SS Rajamouli is filming his upcoming project SSMB29, starring Mahesh Babu, Priyanka Chopra Jonas, and Prithviraj Sukumaran. The team has built a grand Varanasi set at Hyderabad's Ramoji Film City, costing approximately Rs 50 crores, to avoid logistical challenges of shooting in the actual city. Significant portions of the film, including action sequences featuring Mahesh Babu, will be shot on this set. Rajamouli will utilize green screen technology and VFX, with renowned Hollywood studios contributing to the visuals. The production has completed filming schedules in Hyderabad and Odisha and is set to resume shooting in Kenya in July. This international schedule will feature Mahesh Babu and Priyanka Chopra Jonas against a natural backdrop. This film marks Priyanka Chopra Jonas' return to Indian cinema after six years.
