Lightning Strike Claims Life in Uttar Pradesh Village

2025-06-17T08:19:00+00:00

On Monday evening in Dhuma village, Uttar Pradesh, a 62-year-old villager named Ishwar Gond was killed by lightning. The incident occurred during a sudden storm with heavy rain and thunder. Gond was seeking shelter under a tree when lightning struck, killing him instantly. A woman sitting nearby sustained severe burns and is in critical condition at Duddhi Community Health Center. The local administration has instructed a Revenue Department team to assess the damage. They have also urged residents to avoid sheltering in open spaces or under trees during bad weather to prevent similar tragedies.
