Doctor Dies After Bribe Arrest, Corruption Probe Ongoing

2025-06-16T18:52:00+00:00

Dr. Venkatesh Mauwar, superintendent of Bansdih community health center in Ballia, died at SPG divisional hospital in Varanasi on June 17, 2025. Mauwar was arrested on June 12 by a vigilance team while accepting a bribe of Rs 20,000 from a Jan Aushadhi Kendra operator, Ajay Tiwari. Tiwari had alleged that Mauwar had been demanding a monthly bribe of Rs 20,000 for releasing the approval letter for operating the Kendra. Mauwar was suffering from fever, hypertension, and anxiety since his arrest and died during treatment at the hospital. His body was sent for postmortem examination. Further legal action was underway against him at the Varanasi sector office.
