Ballia Birth Under Open Sky Sparks Outrage

2025-05-24T16:03:00+00:00

Four health department employees in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh face disciplinary action after a woman gave birth under an open sky at the Community Health Centre (CHC). Samajwadi Party MLA Jai Prakash Anchal criticized the incident, demanding strict action against the responsible health workers.
