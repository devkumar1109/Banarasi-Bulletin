Andhra Pradesh: Sports Capital Dreams Take Shape

2025-06-12T17:14:00+00:00

The Andhra Pradesh government plans to establish an international sports university and a sports city spanning over 2,000 acres in Vijayawada. The aim is to transform Amaravati into India's 'sports capital'. A proposal to host the 2026 Khelo India Games in Vijayawada has been submitted to the Centre. The Indira Gandhi Municipal Stadium in Vijayawada is being upgraded to host international events, with an estimated cost of ₹70 crore. The state government is also planning to construct stadia in every assembly constituency and develop sports infrastructure in 46,000 government schools.
