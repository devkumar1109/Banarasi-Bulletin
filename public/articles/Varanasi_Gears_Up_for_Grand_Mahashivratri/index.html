Varanasi Gears Up for Grand Mahashivratri

2025-02-26T08:12:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for grand Mahashivratri celebrations, coinciding with the reverse flow of people from the Maha Kumbh. The Kashi Vishwanath Temple will open all four gates for devotees from February 25 to 27, with special darshan arrangements for Akharas. To manage the anticipated crowds, security and logistical measures have been enhanced. Medical facilities, including ORS distribution, will be available. Traffic management plans include designated parking areas, drone surveillance, CCTV monitoring, and additional security personnel. The Kashi area is divided into six zones and 18 sectors for efficient operations and safety.
