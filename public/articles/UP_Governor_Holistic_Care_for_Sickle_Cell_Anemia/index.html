U.P. Governor: Holistic Care for Sickle Cell Anemia

2025-06-09T12:46:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel emphasized the need for accessible sickle cell anemia treatment, focusing on grassroots-level access to medicines at Anganwadi centers and panchayats. She stressed a holistic approach, including nutritional support, social initiatives like adoption and mass marriage schemes for affected youth, and awareness campaigns in tribal areas. Governor Patel also called for improved counseling, bone marrow transplants, and increased testing and research at major hospitals. She directed officials to address the sex ratio imbalance, ensure private hospital registration, and combat female foeticide. She further suggested expanding AYUSH and homeopathy services, including distribution of homeopathic medicines at Anganwadi centers and schools.
