UP Police's Tech-Driven Kumbh Mela Security Triumph

2025-02-25T19:38:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police successfully secured the Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj, which saw over 64 crore pilgrims. This achievement is credited to a modern, technology-driven approach to policing, including 2,700 AI-powered cameras, biometric tracking, and drone surveillance. The UP Police shifted from reactive to predictive policing, anticipating congestion and threats to prevent disruptions. This model, which integrates intelligence agencies, local administrations, and emergency response teams, is considered a benchmark for security management, particularly in large-scale events. The UP Police also proactively addressed digital threats by establishing specialized cybercrime units and taking swift action against 140 social media accounts spreading misinformation during the Kumbh Mela.
