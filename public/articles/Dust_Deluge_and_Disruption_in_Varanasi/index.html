Dust, Deluge, and Disruption in Varanasi

2025-04-13T18:10:00+00:00

A dust storm and heavy showers disrupted power in the Varanasi region on April 13, 2025. Falling trees and branches damaged overhead power lines, causing outages that lasted until late afternoon. The storm also affected wholesale vegetable markets, as dust and debris settled on produce. In Mirzapur, strong winds blew away several pontoons of the under-construction Sikhad-Sindhora bridge. The India Meteorological Department issued a yellow alert for Varanasi and an orange alert for nearby Bihar districts, predicting cloudy conditions and further rainfall on April 18 and 19.
