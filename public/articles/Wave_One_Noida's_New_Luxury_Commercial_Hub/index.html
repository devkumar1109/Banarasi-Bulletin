Wave One: Noida's New Luxury Commercial Hub

2025-03-10T10:02:00+00:00

Wave One, a 40-story commercial building in Noida, is now open. Located in Noida's central business district, it offers luxury office spaces, premium retail outlets, a club area, fine dining, and entertainment. The building features an exclusive entry, a luxurious lobby, dedicated parking, and 31 high-speed lifts. Wave One aims to redefine the commercial landscape with its high-end amenities and strategic location. A religious ceremony, including Path Shri Sukhmani Sahib, Kirtan and Ardaas, was held at the building on March 4th, 2024.
