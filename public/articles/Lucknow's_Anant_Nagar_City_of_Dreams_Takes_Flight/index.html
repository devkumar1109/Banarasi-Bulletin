Lucknow's Anant Nagar: City of Dreams Takes Flight

2025-04-04T11:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the 800-acre Anant Nagar housing scheme in Lucknow, a project worth nearly Rs 7,000 crore. The scheme is located near the international airport, expressway, and Lucknow Ring Road and will accommodate 1.5 lakh people. Anant Nagar will include 10,000 flats, seven plotted sectors, and 5,000 housing units for economically weaker sections and lower income groups under the PMAY scheme. The project also features a 130-acre park and an EduTech City with provisions for primary to higher education, technical, and medical institutes. Adityanath emphasized the importance of modern technology in housing, affordability, and timely project delivery.
