Delhi Eyes UP's Grievance System for Efficiency

2025-05-30T18:46:00+00:00

The Delhi government is interested in adopting Uttar Pradesh's Integrated Grievance Redressal System (IGRS). A team from the UP government presented the system to Delhi Chief Minister Rekha Gupta, highlighting its features that improve the speed, transparency, and accountability of grievance resolution. Delhi officials visited the UP National Informatics Centre to gain hands-on experience with the system. Key features of the UP IGRS include an end-to-end digital grievance lifecycle, real-time escalation matrices, department-wise and district-wise dashboards, and analytics-driven monitoring. Delhi expressed keen interest in replicating and adapting the UP model for its own governance, aiming to empower citizens and ensure responsive governance.
