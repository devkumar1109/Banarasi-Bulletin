Truck Scam: Fake Docs, $500K in Losses

2025-05-24T18:43:00+00:00

A 28-year-old man, Abhishek Yadav, was arrested in Niwari, Madhya Pradesh, for selling trucks to scrap dealers using fake registration documents. He operated by booking trucks from owners through brokers, feigning rental agreements, and then reselling them for profit. Yadav sold 20 trucks worth Rs 4 crore, obtained from Jhansi, Kanpur, and Prayagraj. He contacted truck owners through Facebook brokers, offering inflated rental rates and bribing the brokers to gain their trust. The police investigation was prompted by multiple complaints of missing trucks and suspicious booking agents. Yadav was arrested and sent to jail.
