Varanasi Braces for Dust Storm's Wrath

2025-04-13T18:10:00+00:00

A dust storm and heavy rain disrupted power in Varanasi and surrounding areas on April 13, 2025. Falling trees and branches damaged overhead power lines, causing outages that lasted until late afternoon. Strong winds blew away pontoons from the under-construction Sikhad-Sindhora bridge in Mirzapur. Wholesale vegetable markets were affected by dust and debris. The India Meteorological Department issued a yellow alert for Varanasi and an orange alert for neighboring Bihar districts. Cloudy conditions are expected until April 17, with more rainfall on April 18 and 19. The weather shift has raised concerns for farmers who have yet to harvest their wheat crops.
