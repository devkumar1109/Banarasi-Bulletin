OTT Frenzy: New Releases for Every Mood

2025-02-28T07:00:00+00:00

This weekend sees new releases across various OTT platforms. JioHotstar features "Aashram Season 3 Part 2" with Bobby Deol and "Suits LA," a spin-off of the popular legal drama. Netflix offers the comedy series "Running Point" starring Kate Hudson and the crime drama "Dabba Cartel," featuring Shabana Azmi and Jyotika. Amazon Prime Video presents the true-story based movie "Superboys Of Malegaon" starring Adarsh Gourav, Shashank Arora, and Vineet Kumar Singh, along with "Beetlejuice Beetlejuice," a sequel to the 1988 classic. "Raanjhanaa" is re-released in theaters.
