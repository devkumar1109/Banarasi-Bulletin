Power Outage Plunges Minister into Darkness

2025-03-27T18:47:00+00:00

UP energy minister A K Sharma experienced a power cut during an event celebrating the redevelopment of Hanuman Ghat Temple in Mau district. Sharma had to use his mobile phone's torchlight to read his speech and find his shoes. Following the incident, Purvanchal Vidyut Vitaran Nigam Limited (PVVNL) suspended the SDO Prakash Singh and junior engineer O P Kushwaha. The superintendent engineer and executive engineer of the area were also issued charge sheets and asked to explain the situation.
