Yogi Adityanath Opens Gorakhpur Link Expressway

2025-06-17T13:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath will inaugurate the Gorakhpur Link Expressway on June 20. The expressway will prioritize speed, connectivity, and comprehensive road and commuter safety. On the inauguration day, CM Adityanath will also flag off a dedicated safety fleet.
