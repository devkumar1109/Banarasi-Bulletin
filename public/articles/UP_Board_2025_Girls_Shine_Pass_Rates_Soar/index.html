UP Board 2025: Girls Shine, Pass Rates Soar

2025-04-26T05:31:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has announced the UP Board 2025 results. Girls outperformed boys in both High School (Class 10) and Intermediate (Class 12) exams. The pass percentage for High School was 90.11% (93.87% for girls, 86.66% for boys) and for Intermediate it was 81.15% (86.37% for girls, 76.60% for boys). Institutional candidates performed better than private candidates in the High School exams, but private candidates slightly outperformed institutional candidates in the Intermediate exams. Results are available online at upmsp.edu.in and upresults.nic.in. Students can also check via SMS or DigiLocker. Results are provisional and original marksheets must be collected from schools.
