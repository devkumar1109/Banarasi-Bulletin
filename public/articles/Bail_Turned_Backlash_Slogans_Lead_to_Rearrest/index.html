Bail Turned Backlash: Slogans Lead to Rearrest

2025-06-08T14:43:00+00:00

Abid Khan, an inmate released on bail after nine days in Varanasi district jail, was rearrested after leading a procession with supporters chanting objectionable slogans. The procession, recorded on video, violated legal norms and prompted police intervention. Khan, charged with loot and criminal intimidation, now faces new charges alongside two others. Authorities are seeking to revoke his previous bail. A police official has been suspended for negligence. Investigations are ongoing to identify and apprehend others involved.
