Cattle Smugglers Caught in Police Chase

2025-05-07T19:07:00+00:00

Two cattle smugglers, Sonu Yadav and Sabhajeet Yadav alias Shalu, were arrested in Ghazipur district following a police encounter. The incident occurred when a joint team of Bahariyabad and Sadat police were checking suspicious vehicles. The smugglers attempted to flee in a pickup van, leading to a chase and a crash. During the encounter, the smugglers fired at the police, who retaliated in self-defence, injuring both smugglers. Police recovered two country-made pistols, live cartridges, 11 cattle, and the pickup van from the arrested smugglers. Both Sonu Yadav and Sabhajeet Yadav have previous criminal records.
