Vaish Strikes Gold in North Zone Bowling Tourney

2025-06-12T20:28:00+00:00

Sumit Vaish from Uttar Pradesh won the 1st North Zone Tenpin Bowling Ranking Tournament, defeating Dhruv Sarda from Delhi by 49 pinfall. The tournament, organized by the Uttar Pradesh Tenpin Bowling Association, concluded in Lucknow. Vaish scored 431-382 against Sarda, who won the first game but Vaish dominated the second with a score of 238-177. R Kannan, vice-president of the Tenpin Bowling Federation of India, attended the closing ceremony.
