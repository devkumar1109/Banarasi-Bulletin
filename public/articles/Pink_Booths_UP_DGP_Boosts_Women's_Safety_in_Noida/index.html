Pink Booths: UP DGP Boosts Women's Safety in Noida

2025-04-01T15:07:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh DGP Prashant Kumar inaugurated 11 pink booths (police booths managed by women cops) in Noida, emphasizing their role in ensuring women's safety. These booths allow women to register complaints and receive immediate police assistance. Kumar also inaugurated 11 police outposts, 26 video-walls, and a multi-purpose building at the Gautam Buddha Nagar Police Commissionerate headquarters. The video-walls, set up in collaboration with Yamaha Motor India, will aid in crime control by collecting digital evidence. The DGP praised the Gautam Buddha Nagar Police Commissionerate's efforts in reducing the crime rate and commended Commissioner Laxmi Singh for her leadership and commitment to women's empowerment.
