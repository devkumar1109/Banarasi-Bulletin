Interstate Gun Ring Busted in Bhubaneswar

2025-06-13T16:17:00+00:00

The Odisha Police busted an interstate firearm smuggling racket in Bhubaneswar, arresting five individuals from Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Cuttack district. Authorities seized six firearms, 18 rounds of ammunition, a vehicle, and multiple mobile phones. The gang procured weapons from northern India and distributed them across the country, including Odisha. Police Commissioner Suresh Dev Datta Singh described the operation as a significant breakthrough against organized crime. The seized firearms are undergoing forensic examination to trace their origins and potential involvement in past criminal incidents. Police are intensifying interrogation to uncover the full extent of the racket and identify other operatives.
