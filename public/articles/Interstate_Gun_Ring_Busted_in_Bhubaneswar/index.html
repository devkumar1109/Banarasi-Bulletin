Interstate Gun Ring Busted in Bhubaneswar

2025-06-13T16:17:00+00:00

The Odisha Police busted an interstate firearm smuggling racket in Bhubaneswar, arresting five individuals from Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Cuttack. Six firearms, 18 rounds of live ammunition, a vehicle, and multiple mobile phones were seized. The gang sourced weapons from northern India and distributed them across the country, including Odisha. Police Commissioner Suresh Dev Datta Singh emphasized this as a significant breakthrough against organized crime. The seized firearms are being forensically examined, and the accused are being interrogated to uncover the full scope of the racket. Police will continue efforts to dismantle illegal firearms networks with heightened surveillance and inter-state cooperation.
