Influencers Take Over Travel: Free Trips, Global Boost

2025-04-18T11:30:00+00:00

Tourism boards globally are partnering with influencers to boost travel. This strategy comes as domestic tourism in India surged 45% in 2023, reaching 2,509.63 million visitors. Destinations ranging from Dubai and Saudi Arabia to Bastar and Uttar Pradesh are offering free FAM trips to influencers, sometimes with compensation, to promote their offerings and attract followers.
