Mobile Lights Aid Births Amid Uttar Pradesh Power Cut

2025-05-30T09:36:00+00:00

Four women gave birth at a government health centre in Uttar Pradesh with the assistance of mobile phone lights due to a power outage. The incident highlights the lack of basic infrastructure at some government healthcare facilities in the state. The women and their newborns are reported to be in good health.
