UP's BJP Aims for Third Consecutive Win

2025-03-25T19:02:00+00:00

Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya claims the BJP-led alliance will win a third consecutive term in Uttar Pradesh in 2027. He attributes this to the "good governance and security" provided by the Yogi Adityanath government. Maurya contrasts the previous SP regime, which he describes as plagued by hooligans and rioters, with the current BJP government's success in making UP a "crime-free and developed state." He highlights the implementation of PM schemes benefiting various sections of society and cites 458 completed projects worth over Rs 40,536 crore in Varanasi, including the Kashi Vishwanath Dham and Namo Ghat.
