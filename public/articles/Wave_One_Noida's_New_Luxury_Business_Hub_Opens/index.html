Wave One: Noida's New Luxury Business Hub Opens

2025-03-10T10:02:00+00:00

Wave One, a 40-story commercial building in Noida, is now open to businesses, investors, and retailers. Located in Noida's business district, it offers world-class office spaces, premium retail outlets, a club area, fine dining, and entertainment. The building features an exclusive entry, luxurious lobby, dedicated parking, and 31 high-speed lifts. A religious ceremony, Path Shri Sukhmani Sahib, Kirtan and Ardaas, was held on March 4th, 2024 at Wave One.
