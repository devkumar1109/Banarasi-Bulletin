Uttar Pradesh: Greenhouses Grow Profits

2025-06-11T14:58:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is promoting agricultural innovation by encouraging farmers to adopt greenhouse and polyhouse technology in 44 districts. These structures protect crops from extreme weather, enabling year-round cultivation of vegetables and grains like tomatoes, chillies, and beans. Farmers receive government grants of up to 50% of the construction cost, along with technical training and expert guidance. 24 districts already have operational facilities, while construction is underway in 20 more. This initiative aims to boost farm income through protected farming and ensure a steady supply of fresh produce.
