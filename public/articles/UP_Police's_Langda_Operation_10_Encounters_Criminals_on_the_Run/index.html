UP Police's "Langda" Operation: 10 Encounters, Criminals on the Run

2025-05-28T08:25:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Police launched Operation Langda, a crackdown on criminals with a zero-tolerance policy. In 24 hours, 10 encounters took place across 8 cities, leading to the arrest of numerous high-profile criminals. Notable encounters include: * Lucknow: Rape accused arrested. * Ghaziabad: Constable killer apprehended. * Shamli: Rs 25,000 bounty criminal caught. * Other cities: Encounters with rape accused, theft accused, robbery accused, and an escaped criminal. These operations aim to instill fear in criminals and enhance public safety in the state.
