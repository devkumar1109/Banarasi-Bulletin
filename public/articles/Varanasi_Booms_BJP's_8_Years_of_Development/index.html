Varanasi Booms: BJP's 8 Years of Development

2025-03-25T10:02:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya highlighted the BJP government's achievements, emphasizing "good governance" in its eight-year tenure. He focused on Varanasi, PM Modi's constituency, stating that development projects worth Rs 40,000 crore were completed, with another Rs 15,000 crore worth of projects currently underway. Maurya outlined infrastructure improvements including road construction (826.85 km), bridge building, and the development of tourism projects like Namo Ghat and the Shri Kashi Vishwanath Corridor. He also cited the establishment of new educational institutions and the significant rise in Varanasi's GDP from Rs 22,586.91 crore in 2017 to Rs 43,899.75 crore today. The per capita income has increased to Rs 90,028. Maurya attributed this progress to PM Modi and CM Yogi Adityanath's leadership.
