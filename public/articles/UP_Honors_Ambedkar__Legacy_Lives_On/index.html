U.P. Honors Ambedkar: Legacy Lives On

2025-04-12T15:13:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is hosting statewide events to commemorate Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's 134th birth anniversary. Celebrations will run from April 13th to 14th and include cultural performances, educational programs, and a special march called "Bhim Padyatra." A grand event at the Ambedkar Mahasabha campus will highlight Ambedkar's values of justice, equality, and social reform. A special exhibition will also showcase significant moments from his life. These events aim to educate the youth about Ambedkar's legacy and promote social harmony.
