Mahakumbh Dream Turns Betrayal

2025-03-11T00:30:00+00:00

Raghavendra Rao, a resident of CKW Layout, was arrested for defrauding over 100 people promising a pilgrimage to the Mahakumbh Mela. He ran a travel business, Panchajanya Tours and Travels, advertising a 14-day pilgrimage package. He initially took a small group on a successful trip to gain trust, then collected around Rs 70 lakh from over 100 people for subsequent tours, but vanished with the funds. Investigations revealed he invested the money in a betting app instead. 21 victims have officially lodged complaints, with authorities believing more people may have been duped. Rao is currently being interrogated by police.
