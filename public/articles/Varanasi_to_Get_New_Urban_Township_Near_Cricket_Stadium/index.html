Varanasi to Get New Urban Township Near Cricket Stadium

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi will get its first urban township spanning 150 acres near the new international cricket stadium in Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) approved the project at its recent meeting. The stadium, with a 30,000 seating capacity, is being built at a cost of Rs 451 crore and is expected to be operational by late 2026. VDA also inaugurated a new record room with a storage capacity of 80,000 files and launched a citizen app and complaint management system. The board meeting also approved construction of a main access road and amendments to the Building Construction and Development Bylaws.
