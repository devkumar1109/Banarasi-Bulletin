Uttar Pradesh Kidnapping: Wedding Feud Turns Deadly

2025-05-04T18:55:00+00:00

In Ballia district, Uttar Pradesh, 55-year-old Ajay Tiwari was abducted from his house by over a dozen miscreants in an SUV and 15 motorcycles. The incident occurred after Tiwari and his son had an altercation with two youths from a wedding procession who urinated in front of their house on April 29th. The youths were arrested following an FIR lodged by Tiwari's son. Police have launched a massive search operation to rescue Tiwari and arrest the kidnappers, suspecting the involvement of people associated with the earlier incident.
