Uttar Pradesh Braces for Weather Shift

2025-05-26T07:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is expecting a shift in weather conditions over the next 48-72 hours. The IMD forecasts dark clouds, dust storms, moderate rainfall, wind speeds of 30-40 km/h, and lightning across many districts. Rainfall is predicted in districts like Gorakhpur, Deoria, Kushinagar, Maharajganj, Sant Kabir Nagar, Siddharth Nagar, Basti, Balrampur, Gonda, Shravasti, Bahraich, Sitapur, Lakhimpur Kheri, Pilibhit, Bareilly, Rampur, Moradabad, Bijnor, Muzaffarnagar, Shamli, and Saharanpur. Lucknow may experience slight relief from the heat with partial cloud cover, but humidity is expected to rise. Western UP is also predicted to see light rain over the next two days due to cyclonic circulation. On Sunday, 20 districts, including Agra, Aligarh, Bareilly, Bijnor, Etawah, Meerut, Moradabad, Muzaffarnagar, and Noida, reported rainfall.
