Honeymoon Avoided, Wife Elopes, Groom Relieved

2025-06-17T10:54:00+00:00

A 23-year-old man from Uttar Pradesh expressed relief after his wife eloped with her alleged lover just nine days after their marriage. Sunil's wife, 20, had stayed at her in-laws' house for nine days before going to her maternal home and then running away with her lover. Sunil filed a missing person report, and his wife later appeared at a police station, stating her desire to live with her lover. Both families agreed to a peaceful separation and the return of wedding gifts. Sunil, who had planned a honeymoon, expressed gratitude that the situation did not escalate further, comparing his fate to that of Raja Raghuvanshi, who was allegedly murdered by his wife and her lover during their honeymoon.
