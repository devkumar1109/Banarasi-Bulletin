VOGIC AI Gets Grant to Safeguard Varanasi

2025-03-12T04:42:00+00:00

VOGIC AI received a $50,000 grant from the Toyota Mobility Foundation to develop crowd management and public safety solutions for Varanasi's old city. The startup was chosen from over 110 global innovators in a challenge focused on enhancing safety and accessibility in densely populated urban areas. VOGIC AI will work with the Varanasi Smart City Administration, law enforcement, World Resources Institute, and city planners to implement its Vision-Language AI technology in Varanasi, a busy cultural and religious hub.
