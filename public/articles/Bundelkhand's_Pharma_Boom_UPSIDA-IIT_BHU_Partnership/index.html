Bundelkhand's Pharma Boom: UPSIDA-IIT BHU Partnership

2025-04-10T11:32:00+00:00

UPSIDA and IIT BHU have signed a MOU to establish a pharmaceutical park in Lalitpur, Bundelkhand. The park will span 1,472 acres across five villages and leverage IIT BHU's technical expertise, human resources, and research support. The government aims to attract national and international pharmaceutical companies, generate employment for local youth, and foster a robust industrial ecosystem. IIT BHU will offer specialized training programs, internships, and research opportunities for students and employees of the park's companies. The agreement includes joint campus placement drives, industry-specific course modifications, and faculty-industry exchange programs. The MOU is initially for five years, with the possibility of extension.
