Drone Shield For Puri's Sacred Temple

2025-05-23T07:51:00+00:00

The Odisha government will install anti-drone technology at the Shree Jagannath Temple in Puri to enhance security. Discussions between the Law Department and State Police Department are in advanced stages, with a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) already drafted. The system will intercept and disable any unauthorized drones flying over the temple. The Jagannath Temple Administration is prepared to fund the project. This move comes as a response to increasing drone misuse in sensitive zones across India, raising concerns about surveillance, smuggling, and potential attacks.
