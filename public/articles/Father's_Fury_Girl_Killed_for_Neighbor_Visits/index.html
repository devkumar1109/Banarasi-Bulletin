Father's Fury: Girl Killed for Neighbor Visits

2025-03-06T18:25:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man named Mohit strangled his five-year-old daughter to death for visiting her neighbor's house despite his father's prohibition. Mohit had a conflict with his neighbor, Ramu, and forbade his daughter from going there. After the girl continued visiting, Mohit took her to a mustard field and killed her. He later filed a missing person report to mislead the investigation. Police found the girl's body parts scattered in the fields and apprehended Mohit after he confessed to the crime during questioning. Legal proceedings have been initiated against Mohit.
