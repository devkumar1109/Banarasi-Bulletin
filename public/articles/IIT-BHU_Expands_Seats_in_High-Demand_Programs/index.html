IIT-BHU Expands Seats in High-Demand Programs

2025-06-08T18:46:00+00:00

IIT-BHU Varanasi increased seats in popular departments for JEE qualified students for the 2025-26 academic year. A total of 111 seats were moved from four under-subscribed departments (ceramic, metallurgical, mining, and pharmaceutical engineering) to nine high-demand departments like computer science and engineering, electronics engineering, and mathematics and computing. The total number of seats offered remains unchanged at 1,589. This reallocation aims to better meet student demand and industry needs.
