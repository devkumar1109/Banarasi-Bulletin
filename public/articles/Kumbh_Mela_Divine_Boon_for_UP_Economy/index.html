Kumbh Mela: Divine Boon for UP Economy

2025-02-27T13:11:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela 2025 in Prayagraj concluded on Maha Shivratri, attracting over 66.21 crore devotees. The event significantly boosted Uttar Pradesh's economy, generating over Rs 3 lakh crore ($360 billion) in business activities, exceeding initial projections of Rs 2 lakh crore. The influx of devotees stimulated growth in hospitality, transport, food, and consumer goods sectors. The UP government invested over Rs 7,500 crore to enhance Prayagraj's infrastructure, including new flyovers, roads, railway stations, and an airport terminal, further contributing to economic development.
