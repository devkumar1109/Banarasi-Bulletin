Women's Day: 7 Inspiring Biopic Picks

2025-03-05T13:05:00+00:00

This article recommends seven biopics to watch on Women's Day. "Hidden Figures" tells the story of three Black women who were crucial to NASA's space program. "The Crown" explores the life of Queen Elizabeth II, touching on leadership, politics, and personal struggles. "Erin Brockovich" is based on the real-life legal assistant who fought a corporation responsible for water contamination. "Frida" follows the life of Mexican painter Frida Kahlo, highlighting her physical pain, relationships, and political activism. "The Iron Lady" focuses on Margaret Thatcher, the first female Prime Minister of the UK, and her experiences in a male-dominated world. "Harriet" portrays Harriet Tubman's journey from enslaved woman to abolitionist and conductor on the Underground Railroad. Lastly, "Radioactive" chronicles the life of Marie Curie, the Nobel Prize-winning scientist who discovered radioactivity.
