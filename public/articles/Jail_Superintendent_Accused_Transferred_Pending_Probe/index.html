Jail Superintendent Accused, Transferred Pending Probe

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi District Jail Superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending an inquiry into a harassment complaint filed by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of corruption, harassment, and demanding inappropriate favors. She released a video and complaint letter, leading to her transfer to Naini central jail in Prayagraj. Singh's alleged misconduct includes making obscene gestures, pressuring Kanojia for sexual favors in exchange for benefits, and threatening her job security. Kanojia's daughter has filed a separate complaint against Singh, alleging mental and physical harassment. The investigation into both Singh and Kanojia is ongoing, and further action will be taken based on the findings.
