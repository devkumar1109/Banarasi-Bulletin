Bride Beaten to Death Weeks After Wedding

2025-05-18T06:15:00+00:00

A 26-year-old woman, Aarti Pal, was beaten to death by her 44-year-old husband, Raju Pal, in Varanasi just a week after their marriage. The couple had reportedly been having frequent clashes. Police responded to a neighbor's call and found Aarti grievously injured. She was pronounced dead at the Narpatpur Community Health Centre. Raju Pal has been arrested, and the body sent for post-mortem.
