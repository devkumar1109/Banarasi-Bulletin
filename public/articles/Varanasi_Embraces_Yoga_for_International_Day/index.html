Varanasi Embraces Yoga for International Day

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for International Yoga Day on June 21st. The main event will be held at Kashi Vishwanath Dham. A series of events, Yoga Week, will commence on June 15th, with yoga sessions across the district, including at all ghats. Yoga camps will be organized in villages and urban areas, and schools will conduct daily yoga sessions. Health centers will also host yoga sessions. Yoga organizations are encouraged to conduct daily sessions at their locations, while the youth welfare officer will train Yuvak Mangal Dal members to lead sessions in villages.
