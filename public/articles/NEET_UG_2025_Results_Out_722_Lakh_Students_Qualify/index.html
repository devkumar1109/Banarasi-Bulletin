NEET UG 2025 Results Out: 7.22 Lakh Students Qualify

2025-06-14T19:38:00+00:00

The National Eligibility-cum-Entrance Test Under-graduate (NEET UG) results for 2025 were released today. A total of 22,09,318 candidates appeared for the exam, with 7,22,462 female candidates and 5,14,063 male candidates qualifying. The NTA conducted the exam on May 4th, 2025, across 4,750 centers in India and 14 centers abroad. The results and final answer keys are available on the official NTA website, neet.nta.nic.in.
