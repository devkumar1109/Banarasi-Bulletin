UP's Cyberbullying Epidemic: One Case Every 8 Minutes

2025-04-18T06:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh reported 66,854 cyberbullying cases in 2024, averaging one every eight minutes. Over 45,000 complaints came from individuals aged 16 to 25, with Lucknow recording the highest number of cases at 9,839. UP Police’s Women and Child Safety Organisation (WCSO) has a dedicated cyber unit for such cases, employing a multi-tier resolution system. This involves issuing warnings, counselling, and involving family and friends to deter harassment. 59,771 cases were resolved through these initial steps, with 5,166 cases resolved through counselling. Persistent offenders face FIRs or legal warnings.
