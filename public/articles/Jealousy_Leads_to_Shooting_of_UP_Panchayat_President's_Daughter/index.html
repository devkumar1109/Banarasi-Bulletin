Jealousy Leads to Shooting of UP Panchayat President's Daughter

2025-04-23T13:12:00+00:00

In Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, Shakeel, the son of Kant Nagar Panchayat president Munara Begum, allegedly shot his wife Nigar alias Reena (28) due to suspected infidelity. Nigar is in critical condition at the medical college. Superintendent of Police Rajesh Dwivedi confirmed the incident and stated that Shakeel was absconding but later arrested. Nigar reported to the SP that Shakeel suspected her of infidelity, leading to the shooting.
