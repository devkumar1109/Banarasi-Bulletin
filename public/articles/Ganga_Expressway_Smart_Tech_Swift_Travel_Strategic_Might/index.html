Ganga Expressway: Smart Tech, Swift Travel, Strategic Might

2025-05-04T20:08:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is constructing the Ganga Expressway, which will reduce travel time between Meerut and Prayagraj to 6 hours. The expressway is nearing completion and is expected to be inaugurated in November 2023. The project, initially estimated at Rs 36,230 crore, is utilizing Swiss AI and sensor technology from ETH Zurich and RTDT Labs to monitor road quality in real-time, minimizing the risk of accidents. The Indian Air Force has conducted successful night combat drills on the expressway's airstrip in Shahjahanpur district, highlighting its strategic importance. UPEIDA plans to implement this technology on the Gorakhpur Link Expressway as well.
