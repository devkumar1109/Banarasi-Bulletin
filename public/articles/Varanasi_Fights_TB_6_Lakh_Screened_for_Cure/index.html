Varanasi Fights TB: 6 Lakh Screened for Cure

2025-03-24T17:58:00+00:00

To mark World Tuberculosis Day, over 6 lakh people were screened for TB in Varanasi district. The "100-day Intensive TB Elimination Campaign" launched by the Government of India on December 7, 2024, aims to quickly identify and treat TB cases. 9,781 people underwent the NAT test as part of this initiative. Tuberculosis (TB) is an infectious disease primarily affecting the lungs, caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium tuberculosis. It spreads through the air when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks. If untreated, it can spread to other parts of the body. 310 nutrition kits were distributed to TB patients at various medical units by the Feeding India organization.
