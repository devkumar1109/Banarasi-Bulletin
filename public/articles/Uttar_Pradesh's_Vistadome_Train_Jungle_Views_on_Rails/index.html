Uttar Pradesh's Vistadome Train: Jungle Views on Rails

2025-05-19T12:41:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has launched India's first jungle safari Vistadome train, connecting Katarniaghat Wildlife Sanctuary in Bahraich to Dudhwa Tiger Reserve in Pilibhit. The train, offering stunning forest views through its glass roof, is part of the "One Destination, Three Forests" initiative, encompassing Dudhwa National Park, Katarniaghat, and Kishanpur Wildlife Sanctuary. The service runs every Saturday and Sunday, covering 107 km in 4 hours 25 minutes at an affordable ₹275 per person one way. Future plans include daily service and travel packages from Lucknow.
