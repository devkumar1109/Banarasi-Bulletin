Maurya vs. Yadav: 'PDA' Sparks Political Firestorm

2025-03-17T09:59:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya criticized Samajwadi Party Chief Akhilesh Yadav's concept of 'PDA', calling it a 'hoax' and a 'Parivar Development Agency'. Yadav claims PDA represents unity among backward classes, Dalits, and minorities, but Maurya disputed its authenticity. Yadav defended PDA as the 'shield of the Constitution' and 'reservation', symbolizing unity for 90% of the population. Maurya accused Yadav of supporting controversial figures and questioned his genuine support for historical figures like Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. This exchange highlights deepening political divisions in Uttar Pradesh ahead of upcoming elections.
