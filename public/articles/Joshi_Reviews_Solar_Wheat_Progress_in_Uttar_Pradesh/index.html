Joshi Reviews Solar, Wheat Progress in Uttar Pradesh

2025-04-11T03:40:00+00:00

Union Minister Pralhad Joshi reviewed solar and wheat procurement schemes in Uttar Pradesh. The state reaffirmed its target of achieving 22 GW of solar energy capacity. Joshi praised the progress in PM-KUSUM and PM Surya Ghar schemes, highlighting the benefits of solar energy for farmers. He visited a solar pump project under PM-KUSUM, where a local farmer successfully implemented an on-grid solar power plant for irrigation. Joshi also inspected wheat procurement operations, noting improvements through the e-POP system. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath expressed gratitude for the central government's support in implementing these schemes.
