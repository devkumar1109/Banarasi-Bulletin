Arun Slams Yadav: Divisive Politics, Scholarship Snub

2025-04-22T14:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister Asim Arun criticized Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav, calling him a symbol of divisive politics. Arun accused Yadav of disrespecting Dalit icons and fostering social stratification during his tenure as chief minister. He claimed the public rejected this politics, leading to Yadav's political decline. Arun highlighted the BJP government's focus on inclusive governance, citing Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's slogan "Batoge toh katoge, ek rahoge, to safe rahoge." He alleged Yadav's government stopped student scholarships before the 2017 elections. In contrast, Arun stated the BJP government distributed record scholarships worth over Rs 4,000 crore this year, benefiting 56 lakh students.
