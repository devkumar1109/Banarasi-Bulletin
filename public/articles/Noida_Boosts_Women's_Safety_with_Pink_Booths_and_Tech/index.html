Noida Boosts Women's Safety with Pink Booths and Tech

2025-04-01T15:07:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh DGP Prashant Kumar inaugurated 11 pink booths, 11 police outposts, 26 video walls, and a multi-purpose building at the Gautam Buddha Nagar Police Commissionerate headquarters in Noida. Pink booths are staffed by female police officers and aim to provide a safe space for women to register complaints and receive assistance. The DGP emphasized that these initiatives will enhance women's safety in Noida, a city with a large female workforce. The video walls, installed in collaboration with Yamaha Motor India, will aid in crime prevention and prosecution by enabling the collection of digital evidence. Kumar also praised the Gautam Buddha Nagar Police Commissionerate for its role in reducing crime rates and empowering women.
