Krishna Takes Helm as New UP DGP

2025-05-31T17:36:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991 batch IPS officer, has been appointed as the new Director General of Police (DGP) of Uttar Pradesh, replacing Prashant Kumar. Krishna brings over 30 years of experience to the role, having held various key positions including ASP in Bareilly, Kanpur, and Aligarh, and SP in Firozabad. He has served as SSP in several cities including Etawah, Mathura, Fatehgarh, Bulandshahr, Gautam Buddha Nagar, Agra, and Bareilly. Krishna's appointment comes at a crucial time with many senior officers set to retire. While his recognition as permanent DGP by the UPSC may be impacted, his close relationship with Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, evident in his handling of police recruitment examinations, is notable.
