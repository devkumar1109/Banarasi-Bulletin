CHC Neglect: Woman Delivers on Floor, Staff Transferred

2025-05-25T16:36:00+00:00

A woman gave birth on the floor of a community health center (CHC) in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, after arriving at 2:30 am. The incident was captured in a viral video, prompting an investigation by the Ballia district magistrate. The investigation revealed negligence by four staff members, including the emergency medical officer, shift doctor, and two nurses, who were absent during the woman's arrival. All four staff members have been transferred, and the district magistrate has directed officials to ensure the woman receives government benefits.
