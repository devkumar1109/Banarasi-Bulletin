Modi to Unleash Rs 3,880 Cr Varanasi Development

2025-04-09T18:33:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh on Friday, April 11, 2025, to launch development projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore. Key projects include road infrastructure development like a bridge between Varanasi Ring Road and Sarnath, flyovers, and an underpass road tunnel. He will also inaugurate and lay the foundation stone for transmission substations and associated transmission lines worth over Rs 1,045 crore. Other projects include a Transit Hostel at the Police Line, new administrative buildings at police stations, a Government Polytechnic College, a new Government College, rural libraries, anganwadi centres, and infrastructure for sports.
