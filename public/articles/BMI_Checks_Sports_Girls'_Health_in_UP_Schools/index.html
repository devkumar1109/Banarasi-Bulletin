BMI Checks, Sports, Girls' Health in UP Schools

2025-03-11T19:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel urged the inclusion of Body Mass Index (BMI) measurements in schools to monitor children's health. Addressing students at a sports competition, she stressed the importance of daily exercise for overall well-being, citing India's victory in the ICC Champion Trophy as an example. Patel emphasized the need for girls to participate in sports for physical fitness and to create strong future mothers and children. She instructed officials to promote regular medical checkups for girls in primary schools. Composite School, Pindra won the district-level PM Shri Annual Sports and Cultural Competition.
