Maurya Hails Modi's Decade: Progress and Patriotism

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya praised Prime Minister Modi's 11-year tenure, claiming it transformed service into commitment, good governance into culture, and security into a top priority. He highlighted the upliftment of 27 crore people out of poverty, providing them with basic amenities. Maurya criticized Congress and Samajwadi Party leaders, accusing them of anti-national tendencies. He stated that Rahul Gandhi speaks the language of Pakistan and views Indian politics from a Chinese perspective. Maurya also emphasized India's progress under Modi's leadership in areas like security, economy, technology, infrastructure, and women's empowerment. He cited India's self-reliance in defense, strengthened by indigenous weapons, and successful anti-terrorism operations.
