Bhubaneswar Busts Interstate Arms Racket

2025-06-13T12:15:00+00:00

The Commissionerate Police in Bhubaneswar arrested five individuals for their involvement in an interstate illegal firearms racket. The operation resulted in the seizure of six firearms and live ammunition. The investigation revealed a supply chain spanning Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Odisha. Police suspect the accused may have links to organized crime networks in eastern and northern India.
