UP Schools: Teachers, Infrastructure, and Results

2025-03-23T10:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed officials to ensure all schools in the state have teachers. The government is committed to improving teacher-student ratios and implementing Operation Kayakalp, focusing on 19 key parameters to enhance school infrastructure. This includes separate toilets for boys and girls, drinking water, well-furnished classrooms, electricity, quality furniture, boundary walls, and secure gates. The state is constructing Chief Minister Model Composite Schools (classes 1-12) and Chief Minister Abhyudaya Composite Schools (pre-primary to class 8), equipped with playgrounds, training centers, and courses in craft, clay art, and new-age skills. The UP Board has begun online evaluation of class 10 and 12 exams 2024-25, with 1,34,723 examiners assessing nearly three crore answer sheets. The evaluation process is expected to be completed by April 2, and results are likely to be announced in the last week of April.
