Congress Workers Arrested in UP Shop Dispute

2025-06-07T10:54:00+00:00

Two Uttar Pradesh Congress workers, Anand Pandey and Amit Pathak, close to state Congress chief Ajay Rai, were arrested on charges of robbery, theft, and molestation. The arrests stem from a complaint filed by Sachina Khatoon, a woman who alleged harassment and forceful eviction from her shop. Khatoon claimed Pandey and Pathak, friends of the deceased shop owner, extorted money and pressured her to vacate the premises after his death during the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite a court stay order, they allegedly broke into her shop and stole her belongings. Rai has termed the arrests a "political conspiracy" to defame him.
