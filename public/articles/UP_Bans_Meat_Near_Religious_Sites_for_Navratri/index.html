UP Bans Meat Near Religious Sites for Navratri

2025-03-29T14:31:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has banned meat sales within 500 meters of religious places ahead of Navratri to maintain sanctity. The ban also includes a shutdown of illegal slaughterhouses. Animal slaughter and meat sales will be completely prohibited on April 6th, Ram Navami. District-level committees, comprising police, health, and food safety officials, will monitor these restrictions. Violators will face action under the UP Municipal Corporation Act and the Food Safety Act. The Yogi government cites previous orders from 2014 and 2017 to justify this decision.
