Uttar Pradesh Empowers Women in E-commerce

2025-04-27T19:10:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will provide free e-commerce training to 150 women entrepreneurs associated with Varanasi's ODOP (One District One Product) initiative. This two-day training, conducted by the women-led organization Yuki, will cover topics like online trading, branding, digital marketing, and accessing national and international markets. Varanasi's ODOP, featuring products like Banarasi silk, pink enamelling, lacquerware, and wooden toys, generates an annual turnover of Rs 1,500 crore, with 25% of the 100,000+ artisans being women. The training aims to empower these women entrepreneurs to expand their reach into international markets.
