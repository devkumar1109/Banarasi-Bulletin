UP Builds Five New Cities Around Noida Airport

2025-04-06T16:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is planning five new cities east of Noida to capitalize on the upcoming Noida International Airport. These include New Noida, Heritage City, New Agra, Tappal-Bajna, and IITGN. New Noida, part of the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor, will focus on manufacturing, logistics, and IT, aiming to create 1.2 million jobs by 2040. Heritage City will leverage religious tourism and Braj culture. New Agra will develop a tourism and hospitality district. Tappal-Bajna will be an industrial township, while IITGN will also focus on industrial development. The expansion plan covers 56,000 hectares and aims to stimulate economic growth in the region.
