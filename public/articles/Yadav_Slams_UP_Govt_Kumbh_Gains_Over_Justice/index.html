Yadav Slams UP Govt: Kumbh Gains Over Justice

2025-03-09T20:11:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party (SP) chief Akhilesh Yadav criticized the BJP-led Uttar Pradesh government for failing to maintain law and order and rampant corruption. He condemned the killing of journalist Raghvendra Bajpai in Sitapur, alleging that press freedom is under threat. Yadav accused the government of shielding criminals and corrupt officials while ignoring public concerns and focusing on economic gains from events like the Maha Kumbh Mela. He criticized the government's silence on Bajpai's murder and its lack of transparency regarding casualties at the Kumbh Mela. Yadav also accused the BJP of glorifying criminals and targeting those who uphold democracy. He urged the people of Uttar Pradesh to vote the BJP out in the 2027 elections.
