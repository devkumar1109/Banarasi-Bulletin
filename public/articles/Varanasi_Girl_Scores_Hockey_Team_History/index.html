Varanasi Girl Scores Hockey Team History

2025-04-23T05:37:00+00:00

Pooja Yadav, a 21-year-old from Varanasi, has made history by becoming the first woman from the Purvanchal region to join the Indian women's hockey team. She earned her place after demonstrating dedication at the senior national camp in Bengaluru. Yadav's father, a milkman, supported her hockey aspirations despite facing skepticism from others. He purchased her first pair of playing shoes from a local shop, paying for them by delivering milk until the debt was cleared. Yadav also credits her early experiences playing cricket with boys for developing her confidence and belief in her abilities. She is now part of the 26-member squad traveling to Perth for a tour against Australia from April 26 to May 4.
