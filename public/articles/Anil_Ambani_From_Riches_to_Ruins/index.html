Anil Ambani: From Riches to Ruins

2025-04-13T14:26:00+00:00

Anil Ambani, younger brother of Mukesh Ambani, was once the world's sixth richest person with a net worth of USD 42 billion. This wealth primarily came from inheriting companies like Reliance Communications, Reliance Capital, Reliance Energy, and Reliance Natural Resources after the division of the Reliance Group in 2005. Initially successful, Anil Ambani's businesses later faltered due to poor decisions, leading to substantial debt.
