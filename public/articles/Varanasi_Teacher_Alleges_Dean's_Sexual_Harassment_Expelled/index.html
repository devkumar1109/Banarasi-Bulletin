Varanasi Teacher Alleges Dean's Sexual Harassment, Expelled

2025-05-28T10:08:00+00:00

A female teacher in Varanasi has accused the dean of a private school of sexual harassment. The teacher alleges that the dean snatched her phone, used abusive language, and demanded she visit his flat at night. Following this incident, the school management allegedly expelled the teacher without notice. She claims the incident was recorded on the school's CCTV cameras and alleges the dean has a history of pressuring female teachers for physical relationships with termination as a consequence for refusal. An FIR has been filed against the dean, and police are investigating the matter.
