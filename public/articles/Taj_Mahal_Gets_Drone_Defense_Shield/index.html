Taj Mahal Gets Drone Defense Shield

2025-05-25T15:16:00+00:00

The Taj Mahal will soon be equipped with an anti-drone system to enhance security. This decision follows heightened tensions after India's Operation Sindoor strikes on terror hubs in Pakistan and Pakistan-occupied Kashmir. The system, with a range of 7-8 kilometers, will jam drone signals within a 200-meter radius of the main dome, rendering them inoperable. A dedicated police team is being trained to operate the equipment and respond to potential drone incursions. The installation is expected to be completed soon.
