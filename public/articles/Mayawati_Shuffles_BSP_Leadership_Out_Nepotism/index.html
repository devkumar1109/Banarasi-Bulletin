Mayawati Shuffles BSP Leadership, Out Nepotism

2025-03-03T15:16:00+00:00

Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) chief Mayawati removed Akash Anand, her nephew and national coordinator, from his post. Ramji Gautam, the BSP's sole MP, has been appointed as a new national coordinator alongside Anand Kumar, Mayawati's brother. Gautam, a Jatav Dalit from Uttar Pradesh, has a long history with the BSP, joining the party in 1990 and holding various organizational roles. He is a second-time national coordinator. Mayawati expelled Akash Anand from the party on Monday.
