Influencers Takeover: Tourism's New Travel Trend

2025-04-18T00:00:00+00:00

Tourism boards globally are leveraging social media influencers to attract travelers. This strategy follows a significant surge in domestic tourism in India, which jumped 45% to 2,509.63 million in 2023 from 1,731.01 million in 2022. Destinations like Dubai, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Japan, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, and Bihar are offering free familiarization trips (FAM trips) to influencers, sometimes with compensation, aiming to convert their followers into tourists.
