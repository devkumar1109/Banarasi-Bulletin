UP's Tech Boost: GCC Policy Attracts Global Giants

2025-05-18T13:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched its Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy 2024 to attract Fortune 500 companies and boost foreign direct investment. The policy aims to transform Noida, Lucknow, Kanpur, and Varanasi into tech hubs, creating over 2 lakh high-paying jobs in the next five years. Incentives include land subsidies (30-50%), stamp duty exemption, capital subsidies (up to Rs 25 crore), SGST reimbursement, 5% interest subsidy, and operational subsidies (up to Rs 80 crore). Customised packages are offered to Fortune 500 companies and those with FDI exceeding Rs 100 crore. The policy also supports innovation and startups with cost reimbursements for ideation, IPR subsidies, and grants for setting up centers of excellence.
