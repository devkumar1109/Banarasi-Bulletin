India's Young Guns Aim for SAFF U-19 Glory

2025-05-09T11:33:00+00:00

The 2025 SAFF U-19 Championship kicks off on May 9th at Arunachal Pradesh's Golden Jubilee Stadium. Six teams are competing, divided into two groups of three. Group A consists of Bangladesh, Bhutan, and the Maldives. Group B includes host nation India, Nepal, and Sri Lanka. India head coach Bibiano Fernandes has named Singamayum Shami as captain. Notably, Shami became the youngest goalscorer in Indian Super League history in April 2025.
