No More Paan Masala in UP Assembly

2025-03-10T07:27:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Assembly Speaker Satish Mahana has banned paan masala and gutkha use in the Assembly after an MLA left a stain on the main hall floor. MLAs and staff caught chewing these products will face a ₹1,000 fine, with a second offense resulting in public shaming. The security control room will monitor for violations, and additional CCTV cameras may be installed to cover blind spots. Assembly staff have raised concerns about the difficulty of removing paan masala stains, which require specific cleaning agents. Fixed dustbins, previously removed for security reasons, may be reinstated to encourage proper disposal.
