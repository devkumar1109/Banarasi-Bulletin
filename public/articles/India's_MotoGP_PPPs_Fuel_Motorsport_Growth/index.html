India's MotoGP: PPPs Fuel Motorsport Growth

2025-06-15T19:00:00+00:00

Industry executives believe motorsport in India has significant potential but requires strong public-private partnerships (PPPs) for sustainable growth. They cite the Uttar Pradesh government's deal to host MotoGP as a positive step, which could boost tourism, create jobs, and develop infrastructure. Despite past challenges with Formula One (F1) due to regulatory and taxation issues, executives emphasize the need for supportive policies and government backing to unlock motorsport's economic value and build Brand India. JK Tyre & Industries has invested around ₹200 crore in motorsport development, including infrastructure, talent support, and collaborations. Vicky Chandhok highlights that the lack of government support led to F1's exit, but the UP MotoGP deal offers a promising opportunity.
