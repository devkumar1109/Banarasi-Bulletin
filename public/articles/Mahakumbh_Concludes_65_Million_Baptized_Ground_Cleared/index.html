Mahakumbh Concludes: 65 Million Baptized, Ground Cleared

2025-02-28T11:24:00+00:00

The 45-day Mahakumbh Mela in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, concluded on February 26th with 'Antim Snan' on Mahashivratri. Over 65 million devotees from around the world attended the pilgrimage. Notable attendees included President Droupadi Murmu, Prime Minister Narendra Modi, and various Bollywood celebrities like Katrina Kaif, Anupam Kher, and Akshay Kumar. Photos and videos show the ongoing cleanliness drive at the Mahakumbh ground, with empty ghats and vast spaces now vacant after the large gatherings.
