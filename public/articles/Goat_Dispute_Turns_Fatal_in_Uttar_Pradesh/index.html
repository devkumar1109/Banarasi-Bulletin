Goat Dispute Turns Fatal in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

A 30-year-old man, Awadhesh Kumar, was killed in Ballia district, Uttar Pradesh, following a dispute with his neighbors over a goat eating grains. The incident occurred in Singhpur village when Kumar went to retrieve his goat from his neighbor's house. Two adolescent boys from the neighboring family attacked Kumar with sticks, leaving him unconscious. He was taken to the district hospital where he was declared brought dead. Police have arrested the two juvenile accused and recovered the sticks used in the murder. Kumar's family lodged a named FIR against the accused.
