Goat Dispute Turns Fatal in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

In Ballia district, Uttar Pradesh, a 30-year-old man, Awadhesh Kumar, was killed in a clash with two juvenile boys from his neighboring family. The dispute arose when Kumar's goat entered the neighbor's house and ate grains. The boys attacked Kumar with sticks in a field, leaving him unconscious. He was taken to the district hospital but was declared brought dead. Police have recovered the sticks used in the attack and arrested the juveniles, who were produced before the juvenile justice board.
