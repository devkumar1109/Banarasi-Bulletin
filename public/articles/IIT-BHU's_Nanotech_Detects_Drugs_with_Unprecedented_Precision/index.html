IIT-BHU's Nanotech Detects Drugs with Unprecedented Precision

2025-04-22T17:55:00+00:00

A team of researchers at IIT-BHU has developed a 3D trimetallic nanodendrite-based electrochemical sensing device for highly sensitive and precise drug detection. This device can detect even trace amounts of drugs in pharmaceutical formulations and milk samples. IIT-BHU holds a patent for the technology, with Prof Pranjal Chandra, Rohini Kumari, and Daphika S Dkhar as the inventors. The sensor offers advantages like excellent stability, fast response time, and a single-step modification process. This technology can be used to detect drug overdoses or adulteration in food samples, addressing the issue of antibiotic resistance and contamination concerns.
