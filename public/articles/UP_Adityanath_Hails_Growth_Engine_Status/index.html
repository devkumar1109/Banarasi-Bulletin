UP: Adityanath Hails "Growth Engine" Status

2025-04-14T10:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims his state has become a "growth engine" for India, citing its economic growth and contribution to the nation's development. He attributes the state's success to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's leadership and policies implemented by his government. Adityanath states that Uttar Pradesh is the first state to double its Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) and per capita income in the past eight years. He highlights investor-friendly policies, improved law and order, and the state's transition to revenue surplus as key factors in its economic transformation. Previously, Uttar Pradesh faced economic challenges, with per capita income falling below the national average, according to Adityanath.
