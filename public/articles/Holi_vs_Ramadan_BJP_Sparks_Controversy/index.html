Holi vs. Ramadan: BJP Sparks Controversy

2025-03-13T09:32:00+00:00

BJP leaders are urging Muslims to stay indoors during Holi celebrations, which fall on a Friday coinciding with the holy month of Ramzan. This has sparked controversy in states like Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. In Sambhal, Uttar Pradesh, Circle Officer Anuj Chaudhary suggested Muslims stay home for Friday prayers to ensure "uninterrupted" Holi festivities. His comments were endorsed by UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, who emphasized respect for each other's feelings and suggested Muslims offer prayers after Holi celebrations. BJP leaders across various states have echoed Chaudhary's call, urging Muslims to remain indoors during Holi.
