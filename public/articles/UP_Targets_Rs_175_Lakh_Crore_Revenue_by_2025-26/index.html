UP Targets Rs 1.75 Lakh Crore Revenue by 2025-26

2025-03-18T19:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath aims for a revenue target of Rs 1.75 lakh crore by 2025-26. He emphasized achieving this goal through "mission mode" and direct engagement with traders. Adityanath highlighted the state's success in boosting GST registrations, with a rise in active traders from 17.2 lakh in 2023-24 to over 19.9 lakh in 2024-25. He stressed the importance of utilizing AI tools and data analysis for efficient revenue collection and called for region-specific strategies and training programs to combat tax evasion.
