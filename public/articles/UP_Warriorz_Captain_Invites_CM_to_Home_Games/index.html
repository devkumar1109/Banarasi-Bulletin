UP Warriorz Captain Invites CM to Home Games

2025-03-01T10:01:00+00:00

UP Warriorz captain Deepti Sharma and team owner Jinisha Sharma met Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath in Lucknow. They invited the CM to attend the team's home games at Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium. Jinisha Sharma expressed happiness over the CM's support and said they aim to make the state proud. Deepti Sharma also expressed gratitude for the CM's well wishes. The UP Warriorz are preparing for their home leg in Lucknow during the Women's Premier League (WPL) Season 3.
