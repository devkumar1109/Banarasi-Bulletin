UP Police Dominates All-India Police Basketball Tourney

2025-04-11T19:46:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police won gold medals in both the men's and women's basketball categories at the first All-India Police Handball & Basketball Cluster Tournament 2024-25. The tournament, hosted by the UP Police, concluded in Lucknow on Friday. Punjab Police secured the top spot in the men's and women's handball events. 75 teams representing various police forces and paramilitary units participated in the five-day tournament. DGP Prashant Kumar, the chief guest, emphasized the importance of sports in instilling qualities like decision-making and courage in police personnel. He highlighted UP Police's efforts to promote sports through the UP Police Skilled Sportspersons Recruitment and Promotion Rules-2021.
