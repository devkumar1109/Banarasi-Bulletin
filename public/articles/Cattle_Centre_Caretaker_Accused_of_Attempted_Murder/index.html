Cattle Centre Caretaker Accused of Attempted Murder

2025-05-22T19:15:00+00:00

A caretaker at the Purvanchal Gramin Seva Samiti (PGSS) cattle centre in Gorakhpur, Ravindra Sonkar, was arrested for attempted murder. Sonkar attacked Vivek Singh with a sharp weapon on a busy road, injuring him. Two other PGSS employees who intervened were also attacked. Police apprehended Sonkar at his residence and recovered the weapon. Sonkar has a history of domestic violence. An FIR was registered based on a complaint by one of the injured employees, and legal proceedings are ongoing.
