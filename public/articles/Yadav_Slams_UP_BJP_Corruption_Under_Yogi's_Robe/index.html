Yadav Slams UP BJP: Corruption Under Yogi's Robe

2025-03-23T13:08:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav accused the BJP-led Uttar Pradesh government of corruption, citing the case of suspended IAS officer Abhishek Prakash allegedly involved in corrupt practices while clearing a project. Yadav claimed BJP leaders and bureaucrats have amassed wealth, encroached on land, and engaged in financial misconduct, contradicting the government's claim of a "zero-tolerance" policy towards corruption. He criticized Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, stating that religious attire doesn't equate to good governance, which requires honesty and accountability.
