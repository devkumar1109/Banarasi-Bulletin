PMAY-U: Luxury Homes Elevate Varanasi's Weaker Sections

2025-06-05T13:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, 608 beneficiaries received high-tech, luxury housing under the PMAY-U scheme. This project, inaugurated by PM Modi in 2015, is the first to provide such advanced housing to economically weaker sections. Homes feature amenities like sewage treatment plants, solar-powered lighting, parks, gyms, and 24/7 CCTV surveillance. The project cost approximately Rs 4 lakh per unit, with beneficiaries paying Rs 2 lakh and the remaining subsidized by the state and central governments. The allocation was inclusive, benefiting both Hindu and Muslim families. Beneficiaries expressed immense gratitude for the opportunity to own quality housing, praising the amenities and improved quality of life.
