Kisan Morcha Condemns Hate Speech, Demands Action

2025-03-15T13:38:00+00:00

The Samyukt Kisan Morcha condemned hate speech against minorities by UP Police officer Anuj Kumar Chaudhary and BJP leaders Yogi Adityanath and Raghuraj Singh. They demanded Chaudhary's suspension, apologies from the politicians, and urged the RSS to clarify its stance on minority issues. The Morcha emphasized unity and called for action against those inciting religious hate, highlighting the need to protect the rights of all citizens regardless of religion. They also urged the judiciary to take action against hate speech promoters.
