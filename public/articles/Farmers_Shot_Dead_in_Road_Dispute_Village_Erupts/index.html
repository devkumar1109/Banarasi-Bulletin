Farmers Shot Dead in Road Dispute, Village Erupts

2025-04-08T10:47:00+00:00

Farmer leader Pappu Singh, his son Abhay Singh, and brother Pinku Singh were shot dead in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh, on Tuesday after a dispute over a blocked road escalated. The incident occurred in Akhari village when Pappu Singh argued with former village head Suresh Kumar over a tractor obstructing the road. Suresh Kumar's son and associates arrived and opened fire, killing all three men. Police suspect a political rivalry between Pappu Singh and Suresh Kumar may have contributed to the confrontation. The village erupted in protests with residents demanding justice and police encountered heavy resistance.
