Delhi Eyes UP's Digital Grievance System

2025-05-30T18:46:00+00:00

The Delhi government is interested in adopting Uttar Pradesh's Integrated Grievance Redressal System (IGRS). A Delhi government team visited the UP National Informatics Centre to learn about the system firsthand. UP's IGRS, praised for its efficiency and transparency, features end-to-end digital grievance management, real-time escalation for pending complaints, and citizen updates via SMS/IVRS. The system allows citizens to file, track, and receive updates on grievances from a single platform, simplifying processes like property transactions and pension issues. Delhi officials expressed interest in adapting the system for their state, recognizing its potential to empower citizens and improve governance.
