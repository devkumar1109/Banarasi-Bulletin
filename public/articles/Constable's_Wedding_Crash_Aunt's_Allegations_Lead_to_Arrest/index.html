Constable's Wedding Crash: Aunt's Allegations Lead to Arrest

2025-05-08T18:17:00+00:00

A police constable in Chandauli district, Uttar Pradesh, was arrested at his wedding ceremony. He was accused by his aunt of a long-term physical relationship, promising marriage, and fathering her 4-year-old son. The aunt filed an FIR months ago. The constable was arrested as he attempted to marry another woman, despite the ongoing investigation into the previous allegations.
