UP Gears Up: Handball Cluster Kicks Off with 75 Teams

2025-04-08T00:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-2025 in Lucknow. 75 teams with 1,341 players, including 336 women, are participating in the tournament. Adityanath highlighted the state's commitment to sports development, mentioning over 65,000 sports kits distributed to villagers and investments in infrastructure like 84 stadiums, 67 multipurpose halls, and specialized sports facilities. He also said that over 500 athletes were recruited into the Uttar Pradesh Police force and 740 players are housed in 44 sports hostels across 18 districts. Three sports colleges in Gorakhpur, Lucknow, and Etawah provide world-class training and infrastructure. The CM emphasized the government's focus on grassroots sports and announced enhanced diets for players in sports colleges and hostels.
