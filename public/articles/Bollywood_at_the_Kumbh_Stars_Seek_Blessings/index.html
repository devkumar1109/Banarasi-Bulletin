Bollywood at the Kumbh: Stars Seek Blessings

2025-02-25T18:47:00+00:00

Singer Udit Narayan and his wife Deepa attended the Maha Kumbh Mela 2025 in Prayagraj, expressing gratitude for the opportunity. Narayan thanked the Indian and Uttar Pradesh governments for organizing the event. Many Bollywood celebrities, including Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif, Vicky Kaushal, and Raveena Tandon, also participated in the Mela, taking holy dips and expressing their reverence. Held every 12 years, the Mahakumbh Mela is a major spiritual gathering attracting millions of devotees.
