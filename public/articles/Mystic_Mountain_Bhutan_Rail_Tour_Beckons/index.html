Mystic Mountain: Bhutan Rail Tour Beckons

2025-05-24T06:24:00+00:00

IRCTC's 'Mystic Mountain' rail tour, a 14-day journey from Delhi to Bhutan, launches on June 28, 2025. The tour, accommodating 150 tourists, includes stops in major Indian cities and features a modern deluxe air-conditioned train. In India, the itinerary covers spiritual sites like the Kamakhya Temple in Guwahati and natural wonders like Umiam Lake, Cherrapunji waterfalls, and the Brahmaputra River. Upon entering Bhutan, the tour explores Thimphu, Punakha, and Paro, including iconic landmarks like the Tashichho Dzong and the Tiger's Nest Monastery. The all-inclusive package, priced from ₹1,18,965 per person for Third AC to ₹1,58,850 for First AC (Coupe), covers train travel, hotel stays, meals, and bus transfers.
