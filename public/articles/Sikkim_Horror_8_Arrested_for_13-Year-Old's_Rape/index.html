Sikkim Horror: 8 Arrested for 13-Year-Old's Rape

2025-04-13T06:46:00+00:00

Eight people, including four juveniles, have been arrested in Sikkim for the gang rape of a 13-year-old girl over a year. The girl revealed the abuse to a school counsellor, who reported it to the police. The accused include the woman who initially lured the girl into domestic work and her husband, who then raped her. Two other men were also involved, and four boys also sexually assaulted the victim. A case has been registered under various sections of the BNS and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. This incident follows another horrific case where a 19-year-old girl in Varanasi was allegedly gang-raped by over 23 men for seven days.
