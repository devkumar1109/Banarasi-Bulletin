Ganga Expressway: Adityanath Pushes for Timely Completion

2025-04-27T21:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the ongoing construction of the Ganga Expressway and directed officials to complete the project on time, emphasizing quality standards. The 594-kilometer expressway, connecting Meerut to Prayagraj, will link both ends of the state. Adityanath reviewed construction progress in three districts and witnessed an aerial survey in Shahjahanpur, including a 3.5 km airstrip. A night landing demonstration is planned on the airstrip on May 2 and 3. The expressway, costing Rs 36,230 crore, is crucial for the Pradhan Mantri Gati Shakti Yojana and will connect 12 cities and 518 villages.
