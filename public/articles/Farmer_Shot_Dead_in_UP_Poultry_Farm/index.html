Farmer Shot Dead in UP Poultry Farm

2025-03-09T11:19:00+00:00

A 32-year-old farmer, Rampal, was shot dead by unidentified men in Budaun district, Uttar Pradesh, while sleeping at a poultry farm. The incident occurred around 2:00 am on Saturday night in the Bilsi area. Two men entered the farm, shot Rampal in the head, and fled. Rampal's wife, Ashlekh, reported the incident to the police. Circle Officer Sanjeev Kumar Singh is leading the investigation and statements are being recorded from witnesses, including Rampal's wife.
