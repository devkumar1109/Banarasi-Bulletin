Tech for Good: Yogi Calls for Affordable, Sustainable Solutions

2025-04-07T11:01:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath urged technology institutes to focus on developing affordable and sustainable solutions for societal issues. He emphasized the need for accessible technology, particularly in areas like housing, environment, and sanitation. He challenged institutes to create technologies that enable the construction of eco-friendly, low-cost homes within the budget of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Rs 1.20 lakh). Yogi also highlighted the importance of reducing the environmental impact of construction and advocated for innovative waste management solutions. He stressed the government's commitment to using public funds effectively for the benefit of citizens and called for technology that empowers, rather than controls, people.
