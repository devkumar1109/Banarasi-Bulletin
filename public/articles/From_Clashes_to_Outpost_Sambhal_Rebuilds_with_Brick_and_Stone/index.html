From Clashes to Outpost: Sambhal Rebuilds with Brick and Stone

2025-03-06T02:51:00+00:00

In Sambhal, Uttar Pradesh, a new police outpost is being constructed using bricks and stones from the violent clashes that occurred on November 24, 2023. The violence erupted during an ASI survey of the Jama Masjid, resulting in four deaths and multiple injuries. The outpost, located in Deepa Sarai, is one of 38 being built in the district to strengthen law and order. A young girl named Inaya laid the first brick during the foundation-laying ceremony. The site is near the residence of Samajwadi Party MP Zia-ur-Rahman Barq.
