Wipro Engineer Cracks UPSC, Achieves AIR 28

2025-03-01T00:33:00+00:00

Kajal Jawla, a native of Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, secured an All India Rank (AIR) 28 in the UPSC Civil Service Examination (CSE) 2018. Jawla, a B.Tech graduate in Electronic Communication, worked at Wipro for nine years, earning a salary of Rs 23 lakhs annually. Despite her successful career, she was determined to become an IAS officer and balanced her UPSC preparations with her full-time job. Jawla also worked at Deloitte and EY. She studied three hours daily during her commute and dedicated her off days to full-time studying. Jawla is currently posted in [location not provided].
