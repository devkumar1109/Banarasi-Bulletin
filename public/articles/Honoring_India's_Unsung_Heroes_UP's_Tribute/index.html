Honoring India's Unsung Heroes: UP's Tribute

2025-06-10T14:02:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced plans to build a memorial for Dalit icon Maharaja Bijli Pasi in Lucknow. This move honors unsung heroes from marginalized communities. The government is also constructing statues and initiatives for other historical figures like Maharaja Suheldev, Rani Lakshmibai, and Avantibai Lodhi. These include medical colleges and PAC battalions named after them. A cultural center dedicated to B R Ambedkar is also underway, aiming to provide educational opportunities for SC/ST students. This initiative reflects the BJP government's commitment to recognizing and celebrating the contributions of these figures to India's culture and heritage.
