India's First Woman Rafale Pilot Takes Flight

2025-05-07T05:52:00+00:00

Shivangi Singh, 29, is India's first woman fighter pilot to fly the French-made Rafale jets. She is a Lieutenant in the Indian Air Force (IAF) and her journey began as a child when she visited the Air Force Museum and knew she wanted to become a pilot. Women were first inducted into the fighter pilot ranks in 2015.
