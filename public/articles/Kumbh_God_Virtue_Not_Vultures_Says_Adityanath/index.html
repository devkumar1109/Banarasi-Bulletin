Kumbh: God, Virtue, Not Vultures Says Adityanath

2025-02-24T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath criticized the Opposition for their criticism of the Maha Kumbh, stating that while vultures found corpses and pigs found filth, devotees found God and virtue. He addressed the Assembly, emphasizing that people received what they sought at the religious gathering. Adityanath refuted claims that a particular caste was prevented from visiting the Kumbh, asserting that anyone with good intentions was welcome. He highlighted the government's commitment to faith, contrasting it with the Opposition's alleged indifference during their tenure.
