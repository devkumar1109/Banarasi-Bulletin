Mahakumbh 2025: Faith, Sport, and Global Sanitation

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh 2025 in Prayagraj attracted 62 crore devotees, significantly impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. Over 2 crore devotees visited the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi and 69 lakh booked homestays in Ayodhya. The Khel Maha Kumbh, a sports event during the Mahakumbh, promoted indigenous sports and saw participation from over 800 young athletes. A resolution to include these sports in the 2036 Olympics was passed. Mahakumbh 2025 set a global sanitation benchmark with its "zero discharge" initiative, successfully managing wastewater from over 1.5 lakh toilets daily. Prime Minister Narendra Modi took a holy dip in the Sangam and performed puja, calling for peace, wisdom, good health, and harmony.
