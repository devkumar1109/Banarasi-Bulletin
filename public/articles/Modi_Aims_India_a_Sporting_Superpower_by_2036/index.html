Modi Aims: India a Sporting Superpower by 2036

2025-05-04T20:19:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the 'Khelo India Youth Games 2025' in Patna, Bihar, emphasizing the government's commitment to making India a sporting superpower by 2036. Modi highlighted the government's threefold increase in the sports budget over the past decade, allocating Rs 4,000 crore this year. He stressed the importance of nurturing a sporting culture, linking it to patriotism and enhancing India's soft power. The government is investing in sports infrastructure, organizing events year-round, and incorporating sports into mainstream education. Modi also mentioned the development of a sports city in Bihar and the growing facilities in villages, citing the benefits of the NDA's "double engine" government. He emphasized the economic potential of sports, creating jobs and opportunities in various fields.
