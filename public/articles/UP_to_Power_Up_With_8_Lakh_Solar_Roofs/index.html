UP to Power Up With 8 Lakh Solar Roofs

2025-04-20T13:46:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government aims to install 8 lakh solar rooftop plants by March 2027 under the Pradhan Mantri Surya Ghar Yojana. The current financial year's target is 2.65 lakh units, with a monthly installation rate of 22,000. Over 1 lakh rooftop solar systems are already installed in the state, with 11,000 units installed monthly. The scheme is being closely monitored through the Chief Minister's dashboard. UPNEDA is leading the implementation, with targets set at various levels. Over 2,500 vendors have been empaneled and trained to support the rollout. Loans and credit guarantee schemes are available to vendors. The PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, the world's largest domestic rooftop solar initiative, has reached 10 lakh solar-powered homes. Chandigarh and Daman and Diu have achieved 100% rooftop solar targets for government buildings.
