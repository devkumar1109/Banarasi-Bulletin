"Bhool Chuk Maaf" Remix Fails to Recreate Magic

2025-04-24T10:54:00+00:00

The remix of "Chor Bazari Phir Se" from the upcoming film "Bhool Chuk Maaf" starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi, was released on April 24th. While the song uses familiar lyrics from the original "Love Aaj Kal" track, it fails to capture the magic of the original despite being composed by Pritam, Tanishk Bagchi, and Irshad Kamil. The remix lacks a memorable hook or emotional connection, relying heavily on nostalgia from the original. The film "Bhool Chuk Maaf", directed by Karan Sharma and produced by Dinesh Vijan and Maddock Films, is a quirky take on love, chaos, and second chances, set in Varanasi.
