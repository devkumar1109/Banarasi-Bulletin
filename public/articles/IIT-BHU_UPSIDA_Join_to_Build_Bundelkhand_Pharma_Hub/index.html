IIT-BHU, UPSIDA Join to Build Bundelkhand Pharma Hub

2025-04-09T23:01:00+00:00

UPSIDA signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with IIT-BHU to develop Pharma Parks in Lalitpur district, Bundelkhand. The project will establish pharmaceutical industries across 1,472 acres of land in five villages. IIT-BHU's Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology (DOPET) will be a key partner, providing research, skilled human resources, and technological solutions to companies operating within the Pharma Parks.
