MP vs. Punjab: National Hockey Title Clash

2025-04-13T20:09:00+00:00

Hockey Madhya Pradesh and Hockey Punjab will face off in the finals of the 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025 in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. Hockey Madhya Pradesh defeated Manipur Hockey 5-3 in the semi-final, while Hockey Punjab beat Uttar Pradesh Hockey 4-3. In the quarter-finals, Hockey Madhya Pradesh edged out Hockey Maharashtra in a shootout, Manipur Hockey won against Hockey Unit of Tamil Nadu in a shootout, Hockey Punjab defeated Hockey Haryana 3-2, and Uttar Pradesh Hockey triumphed over Hockey Karnataka 3-1.
