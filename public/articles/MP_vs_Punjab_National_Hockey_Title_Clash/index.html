MP vs Punjab: National Hockey Title Clash

2025-04-13T20:09:00+00:00

Hockey Madhya Pradesh and Hockey Punjab will face each other in the finals of the 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025. Hockey Madhya Pradesh defeated Manipur Hockey 5-3 in the first semi-final, with Yousuf Affan and Ali Ahmad scoring two goals each. Hockey Punjab edged out Uttar Pradesh Hockey 4-3 in the second semi-final, with Jasjit Singh Kular scoring two goals. Hockey Madhya Pradesh advanced to the semi-finals after beating Hockey Maharashtra 4-2 in a shootout. Manipur Hockey secured their semi-final spot by defeating Hockey Unit of Tamil Nadu in a shootout. Hockey Punjab defeated Hockey Haryana 3-2, and Uttar Pradesh Hockey beat Hockey Karnataka 3-1 to reach the semi-finals.
