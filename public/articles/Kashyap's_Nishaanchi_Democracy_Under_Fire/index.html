Kashyap's "Nishaanchi": Democracy Under Fire

2025-06-16T14:26:00+00:00

Anurag Kashyap's political thriller "Nishaanchi", meaning "The Marker", releases on August 23rd, 2025. The film follows Iqbal Khan, a former political strategist who uncovers a scheme to manipulate India's democracy. "Nishaanchi" features Vijay Varma as Iqbal Khan, Radhika Apte as a fearless journalist, Manav Kaul as a corrupt politician, and Sobhita Dhulipala as a digital activist. Pankaj Tripathi makes a cameo appearance as a retired intelligence officer. Shot in various locations across India, including Lucknow, Varanasi, Bhopal, and Delhi, the film is known for its gritty visuals, long takes, and intense storytelling. Kashyap's direction explores the dark side of Indian politics through morally ambiguous characters and shocking twists.
