Blinkit Workers Strike for Fairer Future

2025-04-29T09:53:00+00:00

Over 150 Blinkit delivery workers in Varanasi went on strike demanding better pay, summer-friendly uniforms, and improved working conditions. The Gig and Platform Services Workers Union (GIPSWU) reports the workers are protesting mandatory 12-4 pm shifts, low wages, lack of shaded waiting areas with drinking water, and the use of non-cotton uniforms during the summer. The workers are seeking a higher minimum wage and access to basic amenities. A former Zomato worker revealed that the company incentivizes workers based on delivery speed and volume, making the mandatory shifts economically necessary for maximum earnings.
