Holi Thandai: Cool, Energizing, and Digestive

2025-03-13T17:32:00+00:00

Thandai, a traditional Indian drink enjoyed during Holi, is a blend of spices, herbs, and nuts like almonds, cashews, and melon seeds. It is known for its cooling properties, which balance Pitta dosha and aid digestion. Thandai's ingredients, including cardamom, rose petals, and fennel, combat excess body heat, reduce acidity and inflammation. The drink also promotes digestion, food absorption, and metabolism. Thandai provides a natural energy boost due to its components like almonds and cashew nuts, enhancing mental clarity and vigor.
