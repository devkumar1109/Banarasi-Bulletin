Yogi Blesses 1200 Unions, Empowers UP Couples

2025-05-27T12:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath attended a mass wedding ceremony in Gorakhpur, blessing 1200 couples under the CM Mass Marriage Scheme. The scheme provides financial assistance of ₹1 lakh to couples, eliminating dowry, child marriage, and caste restrictions. CM Yogi highlighted the scheme's evolution, increasing financial support from ₹20,000 in 2017 to ₹1 lakh currently. He emphasized the government's dedication to welfare programs, citing the upliftment of 25 crore people nationwide and 6 crore in Uttar Pradesh from poverty through various schemes. CM Yogi also mentioned the PM Swamitva Yojana, providing house rights to 1.06 crore people in UP.
