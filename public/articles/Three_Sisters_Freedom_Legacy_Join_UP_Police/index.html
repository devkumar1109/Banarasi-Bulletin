Three Sisters, Freedom Legacy, Join UP Police

2025-03-15T22:16:00+00:00

Three sisters from Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, Kushboo Singh (24), Kavita Chauhan (22), and Sonali Chauhan (20), were selected as police constables, a decision announced on Holi. The sisters, granddaughters of a freedom fighter, dedicated four years to rigorous training, with some even competing nationally in sports. They were inspired by social media portrayals of the police profession and a desire to serve the nation. The UPPRPB selected 60,244 candidates from nearly 48 lakh applications for 60,244 constable positions. The selection process included a written exam, document verification, physical efficiency test, and medical examination. The sisters' selection was under the 'dependent of freedom fighter' quota.
