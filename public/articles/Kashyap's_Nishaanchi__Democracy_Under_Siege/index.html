Kashyap's "Nishaanchi": Democracy Under Siege

2025-06-16T14:26:00+00:00

Anurag Kashyap's political thriller "Nishaanchi," meaning "The Marker," is set to release on August 23, 2025. The film follows Iqbal Khan, a former political strategist who uncovers a sinister plot to manipulate India's democracy. He risks his life to expose the truth, facing hired assassins and betrayals. "Nishaanchi" was filmed across India, capturing the gritty realities of politics from bustling streets to secret meeting rooms. The star-studded cast includes Vijay Varma, Radhika Apte, Manav Kaul, Sobhita Dhulipala, and Pankaj Tripathi. Kashyap's signature style promises long takes, intense discussions, and a morally complex narrative.
