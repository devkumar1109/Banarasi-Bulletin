Heat-Tolerant Crops: Minister Urges Science Solutions

2025-06-11T23:06:00+00:00

Union minister of state for agriculture and farmers welfare, Ramnath Thakur, urged scientists to develop heat-tolerant crop varieties during a farmers' dialogue program in Varanasi. Thakur emphasized the importance of research that benefits farmers and encouraged scientists to reach new technologies to them. He inspected a direct seeded rice (DSR) demonstration at a farmer's field, praising its water-saving and production-boosting benefits. The minister also honored five farmers and distributed Digital Soil Health Cards to 10, aiming to improve soil management. During the dialogue, farmers raised concerns about nilgai damage, water scarcity, pest management, market access, and seed availability. Thakur directed quick solutions to these issues.
