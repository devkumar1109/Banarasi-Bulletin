Lucknow's Anant Nagar: 7,000 Crore Smart Township Rises

2025-04-04T11:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the 800-acre Anant Nagar housing scheme in Lucknow, a project valued at nearly Rs 7,000 crore. Located near the international airport, expressway, and Lucknow Ring Road, Anant Nagar will house 1.5 lakh people with 10,000 flats and seven plotted sectors. The scheme includes 5,000 EWS and LIG housing units for over 25,000 people, with 3,000 homes available under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY). Anant Nagar will feature an integrated township design with reserved spaces for educational institutions, high-rise residential buildings, and a 130-acre park. An EduTech City is also planned, providing residents access to primary to higher education, technical, and medical institutes.
