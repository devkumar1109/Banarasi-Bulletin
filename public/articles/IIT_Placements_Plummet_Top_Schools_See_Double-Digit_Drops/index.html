IIT Placements Plummet: Top Schools See Double-Digit Drops

2025-03-27T08:19:00+00:00

Over half of India's 23 IITs experienced a decline in BTech placements of more than 10 percentage points between 2021-22 and 2023-24. This data was revealed by the union government to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Education. Notable drops include IIT Madras (12 percentage points), IIT Bombay (13 percentage points), IIT Kanpur (11 percentage points), and IIT Delhi (around 15 percentage points). In 2021-22, 14 IITs recorded over 90% placements, while in 2023-24, only three (Jodhpur, Patna, and Goa) achieved this benchmark. IIT Dharwad saw the steepest decline, dropping 25 percentage points from 90.20% to 65.56%.
