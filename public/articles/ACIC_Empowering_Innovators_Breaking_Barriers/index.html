ACIC: Empowering Innovators, Breaking Barriers

2025-04-09T07:12:00+00:00

The Atal Community Innovation Centre (ACIC) in Meerut provides full support to young tech innovators, covering project expenses, food, and accommodation. The ACIC evaluates project viability and assists individuals from various backgrounds, including school dropouts and the elderly, regardless of age or education. Currently, youths from districts like Gorakhpur, Mirzapur, Bahraich, and Varanasi are benefiting from the ACIC, with their projects garnering praise from Niti Aayog.
