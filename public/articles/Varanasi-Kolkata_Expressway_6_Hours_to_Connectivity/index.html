Varanasi-Kolkata Expressway: 6 Hours to Connectivity

2025-04-05T08:18:00+00:00

The Varanasi to Kolkata expressway, part of the Bharatmala Project, is underway. Spanning 610-690 km across four states, it aims to reduce travel time from 12-14 hours to 6 hours. The first phase, a 27 km stretch from Varanasi to Chainpur, has already completed land acquisition and is progressing rapidly. The expressway will pass through 18 districts, boosting tourism, agriculture, and industry. Estimated to cost 35,000 crore rupees, the project will be completed in 13 phases and create thousands of jobs. Vehicles will be allowed to travel at speeds up to 120 kmph.
