Krishna Takes Helm: Acting DGP for UP

2025-06-01T01:30:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991-batch IPS officer, has been appointed as the acting DGP of Uttar Pradesh, replacing Prashant Kumar who retired on May 31st. This marks the fifth consecutive "acting" appointment since 2022. Krishna, known for his integrity and innovative approach, previously served as the director general of vigilance and chairman of the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board. He has held key positions like SSP in Agra, Lucknow, and Bareilly, and gained prominence for his actions against kidnapping gangs. Krishna also served as the founding chief of UP's Anti-Terror Squad and implemented technology-driven policing initiatives. He comes from a family with a strong public service background.
