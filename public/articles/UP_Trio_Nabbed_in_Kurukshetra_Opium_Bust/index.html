UP Trio Nabbed in Kurukshetra Opium Bust

2025-03-04T16:46:00+00:00

Three residents of Uttar Pradesh were arrested in Kurukshetra, Haryana for drug trafficking. In the first operation, Narendra Kumar and Yogendra Kumar were apprehended near Pehowa Cut with 1.104 kg of opium. The second operation led to the arrest of Prem Shankar with 1.19 kg of opium near Jindal Chowk. Both arrests were made following tip-offs and intelligence gathered by the CIA-I team. The accused were booked under the NDPS Act and remanded to police custody for further investigation. These arrests are part of Kurukshetra's ongoing efforts to combat drug trafficking.
