Car Meet Turns Violent: Vandals Target NYPD

2025-03-31T17:30:00+00:00

A group of vandals attacked NYPD vehicles during a car meet-up in Ozone Park, Queens. Videos show individuals damaging the cars by hitting windshields with traffic cones, breaking windows, and jumping on them. Three police cars were damaged in the incident, and several individuals have been arrested, with more suspects still being sought. The attack has sparked public anger, with many blaming rising crime on progressive policies. The viral footage has drawn attention to safety and law enforcement challenges in the area. The NYPD continues to investigate and identify all those involved.
