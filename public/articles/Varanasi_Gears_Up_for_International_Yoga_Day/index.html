Varanasi Gears Up for International Yoga Day

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for International Yoga Day on June 21st. A series of events, Yoga Week, will begin on June 15th with the grand opening at Namo Ghat. Yoga sessions will be held across the district, including at all ghats, village panchayats, urban areas, schools, and health and wellness centers. The main event will be held at Kashi Vishwanath Dham on June 21st. District officials are coordinating with yoga organizations and youth groups to ensure a successful celebration.
