UP's Tech-Driven Push for a $1 Trillion Economy

2025-06-18T11:54:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh (UP) aims to achieve a $1 trillion economy by leveraging technology and startups. The state's IT and electronics Principal Secretary, Anurag Yadav, highlights the potential of cities like Prayagraj, Varanasi, and Lucknow to benefit from tech-enabled initiatives promoting tourism, employment, and cultural heritage preservation. UP's IT policy aims to capitalize on existing strengths, including a strong talent pool from institutions like IIT Kanpur. Key strategies include world-class infrastructure, with improved connectivity via expressways and airports, making it attractive for companies and professionals.
