Varanasi Prioritizes Safe Motherhood

2025-04-09T18:37:00+00:00

On Wednesday, Varanasi observed the Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan Diwas (Prime Minister's Safe Motherhood Campaign Day). The day, observed on the 1st, 9th, 16th, and 24th of each month, focuses on better healthcare and prenatal care for pregnant women. Pregnant women at various health centers received free prenatal check-ups, including urine, blood pressure, diabetes, syphilis, and hemoglobin tests. 2,586 women underwent check-ups, with 278 identified as high-risk pregnancies (HRP). These women were referred to higher-level units, provided free medication, and received medical and nutritional counseling. Severe symptoms like high fever, headache, blurred vision, and reduced fetal movement are indicators of HRP, requiring prioritized medical care.
