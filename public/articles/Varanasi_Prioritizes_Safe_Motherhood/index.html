Varanasi Prioritizes Safe Motherhood

2025-04-09T18:37:00+00:00

On Wednesday, Prime Minister's Safe Motherhood Campaign Day was observed in Varanasi district. The day, observed on the 1st, 9th, 16th, and 24th of each month, focused on prenatal check-ups and care for pregnant women. At various health centers, including the District Women's Hospital Kabirchaura and Lal Bahadur Shastri Hospital Ramnagar, pregnant women received free prenatal check-ups, including tests for urine, blood pressure, diabetes, syphilis, and haemoglobin. Counselling was provided to new couples and family planning beneficiaries. A total of 2,586 women underwent prenatal check-ups, with 278 identified as high-risk pregnancies. These women were referred to higher-level units for specialized care.
