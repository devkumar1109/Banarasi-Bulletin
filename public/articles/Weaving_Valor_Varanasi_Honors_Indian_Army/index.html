Weaving Valor: Varanasi Honors Indian Army

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers created special sarees inspired by 'Operation Sindoor' to honor the Indian Army. The sarees feature designs of weapons like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale. This initiative is a tribute to the bravery and strength of the Indian armed forces.
