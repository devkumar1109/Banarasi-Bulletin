BJP Caste Census: Hindu Unity in Question

2025-05-06T03:30:00+00:00

Upper-caste BJP supporters are expressing anger over the party's decision to conduct a caste census. They argue that it undermines the concept of "Hindu unity" promoted by BJP leaders. Anuradha Tiwari, a social media activist, who has previously criticized social justice policies for backward castes, is now voicing her dissent against the caste census. Upper-caste voters, while dissatisfied, feel they have no alternative political options. Critics believe the census will exacerbate caste divisions within Hindu society.
