Free Speech vs. Artistic License: Kamra Row Erupts

2025-03-25T10:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath stated that freedom of expression should not be used to attack or divide the country, commenting on the Kunal Kamra controversy. Maharashtra Deputy CM Eknath Shinde also expressed concern over Kamra's recent joke about him, calling for limits to free speech. Shinde alleged Kamra had previously targeted various individuals and institutions, including the Supreme Court and PM Modi. The controversy arose after Kamra parodied a song from "Dil Toh Pagal Hai," calling Shinde a "traitor." Kamra refused to apologize, stating his right to free speech and expression. Shiv Sena workers, led by Shinde, vandalized the venue where Kamra performed.
