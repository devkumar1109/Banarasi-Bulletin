Heroin Kingpin Kasera Caught, Leads to Pistol Cache

2025-03-23T19:08:00+00:00

Three drug smugglers, including notorious heroin smuggler Nanhe Kasera, were arrested in Mirzapur, Varanasi. During the arrest, heroin worth Rs 6.5 crore was recovered from Kasera. He led police to a location in Rajgarh where country-made pistols were hidden instead of narcotics. Kasera opened fire on police during an attempt to escape, injuring a constable. Police retaliated, injuring Kasera in the leg. He was arrested and hospitalized.
