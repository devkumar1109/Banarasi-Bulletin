Dubai Job Scam: Delhi Call Center Busted

2025-02-25T10:15:00+00:00

Hyderabad cybercrime officials arrested Dharmender Kumar alias Rohan (30) from Uttar Pradesh for job fraud. Kumar, who ran a call center in New Delhi, cheated job seekers with promises of jobs in Dubai. He was involved in five cases across India, including one in Hyderabad where he defrauded a 49-year-old man of Rs 15.8 lakh. Kumar's operation involved posting fake job listings and contacting victims with unsolicited calls, messages, or emails. He conducted fake interviews and created fake employee profiles to build trust and then asked for bank details and personal documents. Victims of cybercrime can seek help by calling 1930 or visiting cybercrime.gov.in.
