Sankat Mochan Sangeet Samaroh: Music Bridges Varanasi

2025-04-18T19:05:00+00:00

The 102nd Sankat Mochan Sangeet Samaroh commenced on April 16th at the Sankat Mochan temple in Varanasi with a flute recital by Pandit Hari Prasad Chaurasia. The week-long festival, coinciding with Hanuman Jayanti, features renowned artistes like Ustad Wasifuddin Dagar, Rahul Sharma, Pravin Godkhindi, and Pt Ajay Pohankar. Notable for its community support and local sponsors, the festival breaks social barriers with no VIP sections, allowing musicians to perform "sewa" inches away from the audience. The festival, concluding on April 21st, aims to preserve traditional Indian classical music and provide a platform for its authentic expression.
