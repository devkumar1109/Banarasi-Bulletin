Gravel Truck Overturns, Kills Four in UP Ambulance Crash

2025-04-26T09:53:00+00:00

Four people, including a pregnant woman, were killed when a gravel truck overturned onto an ambulance in Mirzapur district, Uttar Pradesh. The deceased were Heerawati Devi (25), her mother Malti Devi (45), Suraj Bali Kharwar (27), and Ramu. Two others, Kaushal Kumar Kharwar and Bhandari Sharma, sustained serious injuries and were taken to a trauma center in Varanasi. Heerawati was being transported to Varanasi for medical complications during childbirth. The incident occurred on the Varanasi-Shaktinagar highway near a flyover.
