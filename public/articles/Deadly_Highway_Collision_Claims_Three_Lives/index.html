Deadly Highway Collision Claims Three Lives

2025-06-14T15:57:00+00:00

Three men died and one is critically injured after a speeding car collided with a truck on the Varanasi-Prayagraj highway near Thata village. The accident occurred when the car, attempting to overtake, rammed into the truck, causing it to overturn. The truck driver fled the scene. The deceased have been identified as Aman Yadav (25), Afzal (24), and Arbaaz (27), all residents of Jhunsi in Prayagraj. Aman died instantly, while Afzal and Arbaaz succumbed to injuries during treatment. The injured, Vinay Yadav (24), is in critical condition. Police are reviewing CCTV footage to identify the truck driver. The accident disrupted traffic on the service road for some time until the mangled car was removed.
