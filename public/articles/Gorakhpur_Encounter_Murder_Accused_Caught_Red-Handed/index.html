Gorakhpur Encounter: Murder Accused Caught Red-Handed

2025-06-10T18:01:00+00:00

Jugal Kishore, the prime accused in the murder of history-sheeter Dinesh Nishad, was arrested in an encounter on Monday night in Gorakhpur. Nishad was shot dead on June 7th allegedly by his cousins over a land dispute. Acting on a tip-off, police laid a trap and Jugal opened fire while attempting to flee. He sustained a bullet injury to his left leg and a pistol and two empty cartridges were recovered. An 11-person case was filed, including eight named accused. Police exercised restraint during the encounter, ensuring no collateral damage. The investigation is ongoing and further arrests are expected.
