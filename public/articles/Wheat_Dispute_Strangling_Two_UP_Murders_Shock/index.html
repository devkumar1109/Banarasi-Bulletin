Wheat Dispute, Strangling: Two UP Murders Shock

2025-04-06T16:04:00+00:00

In Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, two nephews, Ravindra and Jaipal, allegedly killed their 65-year-old uncle Prahlad over a dispute concerning the division of their harvested wheat crop. The argument escalated into a physical assault, resulting in Prahlad's death. Meanwhile, in Bijnor, a woman named Shivani was arrested for murdering her husband Deepak. Initially claiming he died of a heart attack, Shivani's story was contradicted by a post-mortem report revealing Deepak was strangled.
