Ganga Darpan: River's Story Unveiled

2025-05-20T21:30:00+00:00

The Ganga Darpan Interpretation Centre in Varanasi aims to promote biodiversity education and river conservation. Developed by the Wildlife Institute of India, the centre features a 3D model of the Ganga River showcasing its diverse wildlife, including dolphins, crocodiles, and turtles. The centre has attracted over 20,000 visitors with its educational programs and workshops. It highlights the river's cultural and historical significance, with displays on empires from the Mauryas to the Mughals. Visitors can learn about 62 bird species found in the area and explore the journey of the Ganga through interactive kiosks and QR codes.
