Uttar Pradesh Dominates, Tops Group C in Football Championship

2025-05-13T14:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh topped Group C in the Swami Vivekananda Men’s U20 National Football Championship after defeating Ladakh 4-2. Pravesh Kumar scored a hat-trick for Uttar Pradesh, while Mohsin Khan added another goal. In the earlier match, Punjab defeated Manipur 1-0. Harmandeep Singh scored the winning goal for Punjab in the 12th minute. Punjab now sits in third place in Group C with three points.
