NSUI Offers Holy Water, Protests Bhagwat's Kumbh Absence

2025-04-04T17:00:00+00:00

NSUI activists attempted to block RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat's convoy in Varanasi on Thursday. They sought to offer him holy water from the Maha Kumbh, protesting his absence from the religious ritual despite promoting Hindutva. The activists, led by Vikas Singh, shouted slogans as Bhagwat's convoy passed through Sigra. Police intervened, detaining several NSUI members. The activists argued Bhagwat engages in Hindutva politics but avoids its practical application.
