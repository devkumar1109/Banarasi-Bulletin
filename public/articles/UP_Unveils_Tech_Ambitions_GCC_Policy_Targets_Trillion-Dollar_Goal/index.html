UP Unveils Tech Ambitions: GCC Policy Targets Trillion-Dollar Goal

2025-05-18T12:59:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched its Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy 2024 to attract Fortune 500 companies and boost foreign direct investment. The policy aims to create 200,000 high-salary jobs over five years by transforming Noida, Lucknow, Kanpur, and Varanasi into tech hubs. Incentives include land subsidies, stamp duty waivers, and support for startups to encourage investment and innovation. The policy aims to help Uttar Pradesh achieve its goal of becoming a trillion-dollar economy.
