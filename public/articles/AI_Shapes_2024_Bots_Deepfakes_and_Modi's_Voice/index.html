AI Shapes 2024: Bots, Deepfakes, and Modi's Voice

2025-04-14T13:52:00+00:00

AI played a significant role in the 2024 Lok Sabha election campaign. The ruling BJP utilized AI tools like 'Bhashini' to overcome language barriers, enabling communication across India's 22 official languages and thousands of regional dialects. Prime Minister Modi employed 'Bhashini' during his Kashi Tamil Sangamam address in Varanasi on December 18, 2023, highlighting its potential in simplifying public communication. AI also facilitated the use of deepfakes, such as one depicting Prime Minister Rahul Gandhi, and enabled the distribution of 50 million robocalls.
