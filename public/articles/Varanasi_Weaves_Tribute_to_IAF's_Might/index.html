Varanasi Weaves Tribute to IAF's Might

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers have created special sarees inspired by 'Operation Sindoor' to honor the Indian Air Force (IAF). The sarees feature designs of weaponry like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale missiles. This initiative showcases the weavers' skill and pays tribute to the IAF's role in national security.
