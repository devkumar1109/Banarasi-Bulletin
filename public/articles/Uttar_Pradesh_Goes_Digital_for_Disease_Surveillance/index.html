Uttar Pradesh Goes Digital for Disease Surveillance

2025-05-01T18:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is the first state in India to launch real-time digital surveillance of vaccine preventable diseases (VPDs). The state government initiated this on May 1st, 2025, using its Unified Disease Surveillance Portal (UDSP). The surveillance focuses on six VPDs: polio, measles, rubella, diphtheria, pertussis, and tetanus. UDSP, launched in May 2023, integrates case-based surveillance data for these diseases, allowing for rapid communication and improved public health response. The platform facilitates accurate data collection, enabling better planning and monitoring of vaccination programs. The platform also enables citizens to access their lab reports online.
