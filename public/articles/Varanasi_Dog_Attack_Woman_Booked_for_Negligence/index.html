Varanasi Dog Attack: Woman Booked for Negligence

2025-03-09T19:32:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a woman named Naina Kushwaha was booked after her dog attacked a four-year-old neighbor. The incident occurred on March 7th when the child was playing outside. The dog, brought from Delhi a month prior, bit the child multiple times. The child's mother, Priya Lakshmi Singh, filed a complaint alleging negligence on Naina's part, claiming that Naina had previously ignored her concerns about the dog defecating outside. Singh further stated that Naina threatened her with dire consequences after the attack. The police lodged an FIR against Naina under sections related to negligent conduct, endangering life, and criminal intimidation. The investigation is ongoing.
