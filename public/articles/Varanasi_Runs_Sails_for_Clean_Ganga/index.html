Varanasi Runs, Sails for Clean Ganga

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, 'Run for Clean Ganga', and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw 6 km run from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat. Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal won the men's and women's categories respectively. Senior citizens also participated, with Ganga Sagar finishing first. The forest department celebrated 'World Water Day' and 'Earth Hour' at Dhakwa Ghat with NGO Dolphin Friends. A dolphin safari was inaugurated by Ravi Kumar Singh, forest conservator, to educate the public on dolphin conservation. Minister Dayashankar Mishra Dayalu emphasized the cleanliness of the Ganga, while Labour minister Anil Rajbhar highlighted his efforts for Ganga purification.
