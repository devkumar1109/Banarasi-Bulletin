Adityanath Reviews CZC Meet Prep in Varanasi

2025-06-17T14:42:00+00:00

Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Varanasi to review preparations for the Central Zonal Council (CZC) meeting scheduled for June 24th. The meeting will be chaired by Union Home Minister Amit Shah and attended by Chief Ministers of Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Uttarakhand, and Uttar Pradesh. Madhya Pradesh CM Mohan Yadav, Chhattisgarh CM Vishnu Dev Sai, and Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami will be present alongside Adityanath. Jal Shakti Minister Swatantra Dev Singh, Labour and Employment Minister Anil Rajbhar, and other ministers accompanied Adityanath on his visit.
