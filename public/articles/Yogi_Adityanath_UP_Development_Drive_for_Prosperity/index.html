Yogi Adityanath: UP Development Drive for Prosperity

2025-04-05T15:41:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Maharajganj and praised the passage of the Waqf Amendment Bill, stating it will prevent illegal encroachment of Waqf land. The bill aims to ensure waqf properties are used for public welfare initiatives like schools, hospitals, and housing projects. CM Yogi pledged to eliminate poverty in Uttar Pradesh within three years and make it the nation's top state. He inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for 629 development projects worth Rs 654 crore, including the Rohin Barrage, which will benefit farmers and generate employment. CM Yogi highlighted the state's economic growth, improvements in education and law and order, and efforts to empower farmers, youth, and women.
