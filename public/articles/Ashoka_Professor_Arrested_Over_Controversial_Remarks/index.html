Ashoka Professor Arrested Over Controversial Remarks

2025-05-18T11:56:00+00:00

Ali Khan Mahmudabad, an Ashoka University academic, was arrested by Haryana police for remarks deemed to "disparage women officers in the Indian armed forces and promote communal disharmony." Mahmudabad, a political scientist specializing in Muslim political thought in late colonial India, is from the family of the Raja of Mahmudabad in Uttar Pradesh. He holds a PhD from Cambridge and was a national spokesperson for the Samajwadi Party from 2019 to 2022. Mahmudabad's father, Mohammad Amir Mohammad Khan "Suleiman," was also a politician and a former Congress MLA. He is known for his multilingualism and studies at both Cambridge and Damascus, Syria.
