Bajpayee: From Grit to Glamour, Next Chapter Beckons

2025-04-24T11:24:00+00:00

Manoj Bajpayee, celebrated for his roles in films like "Satya" and "Gangs of Wasseypur", turned a year older on April 23rd. He expressed a desire to be cast in foreign locales with trendy attire, a departure from his usual gritty roles. While acknowledging the abundance of versatile actors today, Bajpayee reminisced about his early days when he was the go-to choice for realistic characters. He believes his evolution as an actor is evident in his performances in "Gangs of Wasseypur" and "The Family Man", where he took risks that paid off. Bajpayee addressed the criticism of glorifying crime in his earlier works, stating that honesty in portraying the characters is crucial, and their eventual fates serve as lessons. He also shared that "The Family Man Part 3" is nearing completion and promises to be even better than its predecessors.
