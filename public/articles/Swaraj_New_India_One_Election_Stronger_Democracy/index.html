Swaraj: New India, One Election, Stronger Democracy

2025-04-24T18:04:00+00:00

BJP MP Bansuri Swaraj condemned the Pahalgam terror attack, stating that "Modi's new India was fearless and would not be defeated by terrorist forces." Swaraj highlighted the benefits of "One Nation, One Election," claiming it would reduce election expenditure, lessen burden on administration and security forces, and promote long-term policy decisions. She argued that simultaneous elections would decrease voter confusion, increase turnout, and prioritize national interests over regional ones. Swaraj urged women to support "One Nation, One Election" and contribute to strengthening democracy through dialogue and idea exchange.
