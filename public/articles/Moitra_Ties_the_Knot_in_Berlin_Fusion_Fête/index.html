Moitra Ties the Knot in Berlin, Fusion Fête.

2025-06-05T23:30:00+00:00

Trinamool Congress MP Mahua Moitra married senior Biju Janata Dal (BJD) leader Pinaki Misra in an intimate ceremony in Berlin, Germany. Moitra wore a custom pale pink Varanasi brocade saree by Raw Mango, featuring intricate meenakari work and woven with real zari. She paired it with a satin silk blouse and gold heritage jewellery. Misra opted for a custom quilted bundi in pale pink handwoven silk. The wedding was a blend of tradition and modern elegance.
