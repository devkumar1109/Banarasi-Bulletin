Ex-Lover's Rage Claims Varanasi Youth's Life

2025-03-15T20:05:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man, Sunil Kumar, was shot dead outside his residence near DAV College in Varanasi on Friday night. According to police, Sunil was killed by the boyfriend of his ex-girlfriend after an argument. The assailant fled the scene. Sunil was rushed to the BHU Trauma Centre, where he was pronounced dead. Police have registered a case against unidentified killers and are investigating the incident. Sunil's family and neighbors protested his killing by blocking the college road on Saturday, demanding justice. Authorities assured them of swift action and the protesters eventually dispersed.
