Uttar Pradesh Chips In: Semiconductor Hub Emerges

2025-05-23T13:01:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is rapidly becoming a major semiconductor hub in India. Two mega projects, one by HCL-Foxconn and the other by Tarq Semiconductors, are set to invest ₹30,000 crore in the state. These projects will establish semiconductor fabrication units near Jewar airport, focusing on producing display driver chips and integrated circuits. The state government's Semiconductor Policy-2024 offers attractive incentives, including capital subsidies, land discounts, and stamp duty exemptions. With its existing electronics manufacturing base and supportive policies, Uttar Pradesh aims to lead India's semiconductor ecosystem and contribute to the "Digital India" and "Aatmanirbhar Bharat" initiatives.
