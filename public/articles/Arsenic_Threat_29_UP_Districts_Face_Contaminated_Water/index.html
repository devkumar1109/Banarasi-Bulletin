Arsenic Threat: 29 UP Districts Face Contaminated Water

2025-03-27T10:13:00+00:00

Arsenic levels in groundwater exceed safe limits in 29 Uttar Pradesh districts, according to the government's Annual Ground Water Quality Report 2024. Districts with contamination include Saharanpur, Raebareli, Gorakhpur, Aligarh, Azamgarh, Ayodhya, Mirzapur, and Sambhal. Arsenic, though naturally occurring, becomes toxic when it leaches into groundwater. Consuming water with arsenic levels exceeding 0.01 mg/liter can lead to health issues like skin lesions, cancer, cardiovascular diseases, and developmental problems in children.
