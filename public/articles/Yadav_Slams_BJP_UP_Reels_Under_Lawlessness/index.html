Yadav Slams BJP: UP Reels Under Lawlessness

2025-04-13T13:32:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav criticized the BJP government in Uttar Pradesh, alleging a breakdown in law and order. He claimed criminals are operating freely with impunity, while the police are ineffective under pressure from the ruling party. Yadav cited incidents of violence against SP supporters, including an attack on Harish Mishra in Varanasi, as evidence of the deteriorating situation. He accused the BJP of emboldening feudal forces and targeting marginalized communities. Yadav also condemned the removal of statues of Babasaheb Ambedkar, alleging an anti-Dalit agenda. He expressed confidence that the public will vote for the SP in the 2027 elections to restore law and order.
