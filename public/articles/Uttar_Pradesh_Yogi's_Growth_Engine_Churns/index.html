Uttar Pradesh: Yogi's Growth Engine Churns.

2025-03-27T12:17:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state has transformed into a 'growth engine' over the past eight years. He attributes this success to honest governance, administrative reforms, and leveraging cultural heritage. Adityanath cites the successful organization of the Maha Kumbh and global recognition of yoga as examples of progress. He also highlights the resurgence of Azamgarh through infrastructure and education initiatives, creating new employment opportunities. Adityanath emphasizes these developments are positioning the state's youth for global opportunities and restoring pride in their identity.
