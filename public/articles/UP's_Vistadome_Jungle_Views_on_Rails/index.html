UP's Vistadome: Jungle Views on Rails

2025-05-17T12:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has launched India's first Vistadome coach service connecting Katarniaghat Wildlife Sanctuary and Dudhwa Tiger Reserve. The service, offering panoramic forest views through expansive glass windows and a transparent roof, operates on weekends with plans to expand to daily operations. The initiative aims to boost tourism, local employment, and promote the state's biodiversity. The Uttar Pradesh Eco Tourism Development Board launched the service, targeting visitors including school children and social media influencers.
