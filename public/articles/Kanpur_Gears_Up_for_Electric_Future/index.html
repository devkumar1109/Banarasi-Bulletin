Kanpur Gears Up for Electric Future

2025-06-17T02:36:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will invest ₹700 crore to establish an electric vehicle (EV) manufacturing hub in Kanpur. The 500-acre park, near Bhimsen, will be developed under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model and will focus on indigenous manufacturing. The park will house units for manufacturing EV components like electric motors, chassis, steel components, and lithium-ion cells. It will also include facilities for assembling batteries, chargers, and controllers, along with a dedicated R&D centre. The project aims to create employment opportunities, boost local entrepreneurship, and contribute to Uttar Pradesh's economic growth.
