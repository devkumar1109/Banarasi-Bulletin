UP Booms: Adityanath Lauds Growth, Targets "Dynasties"

2025-04-19T19:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized "dynastic" political leaders for resisting inclusive development. He highlighted the state's economic growth from Rs 12.75 lakh crore in 2017 to Rs 30 lakh crore in 2025, with a doubled per capita income. Adityanath cited infrastructure improvements, including six operational expressways and metro connectivity in six cities, attributing the transformation to the BJP-led government. He also pointed to Gorakhpur's progress from a crime-ridden area to a hub for education, healthcare, and entrepreneurship. The Chief Minister praised the media's role in democracy, urging responsibility in the digital age amid concerns about misinformation. He emphasized Prime Minister Modi's vision for India to become the world's third-largest economy by 2027 and a self-reliant nation by 2047.
