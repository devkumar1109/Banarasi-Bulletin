Hospital Administrators Master Disaster Response

2025-03-08T18:54:00+00:00

A six-day disaster management training program concluded at BHU's Skill Center in Varanasi. Organized by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare's Disaster Management Cell and IMS-BHU, the program trained 37 hospital administrators from Gujarat, UP, Rajasthan, Haryana, and Daman and Diu. The training focused on public health emergencies, covering disaster preparedness, hospital safety, triaging critical patients, patient evacuation procedures, and fire control in hospitals.
