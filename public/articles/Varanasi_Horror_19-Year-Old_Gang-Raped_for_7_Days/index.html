Varanasi Horror: 19-Year-Old Gang-Raped for 7 Days

2025-04-07T07:22:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a 19-year-old woman alleges she was gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned home on April 4, reporting the incident to her family. Police have arrested six individuals, including Raj Vishwakarma, Samir, Ayush, Sohel, Danish, and Anmol, based on her complaint. The woman's account details multiple instances of assault at various locations, including a café, hotels, and a warehouse. She claims she was drugged, held captive, and repeatedly raped by different groups of men. Police are continuing their investigation and efforts to apprehend the remaining suspects.
