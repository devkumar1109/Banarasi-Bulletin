BJP Worker Beheaded: Domestic Dispute, Not Politics

2025-05-06T19:57:00+00:00

B Saranya, a 38-year-old BJP functionary and Mahila Morcha organiser, was murdered and beheaded near Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. Her stepson, B Kapilan, and two friends surrendered in Madurai, confessing to the crime. The murder stemmed from a domestic dispute over property, not political motivations, despite Saranya's past arrest for protesting against a minister. Saranya's family ran a travel service and a photocopy shop, and she was walking home from work when she was attacked and killed. The incident has sparked outrage, with BJP leaders criticizing the state government for rising crime rates against women.
