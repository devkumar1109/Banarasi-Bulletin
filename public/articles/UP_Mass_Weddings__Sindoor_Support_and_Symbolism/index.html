UP Mass Weddings: Sindoor, Support, and Symbolism

2025-05-28T13:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's Social Welfare Minister Asim Arun announced that brides participating in the state's mass wedding scheme will receive a 'sindoor daani', a symbol of marriage. This initiative, part of the Mukhyamantri Samuhik Vivah Yojana, aims to uphold tradition and support women's dignity. Financial assistance for couples under the scheme has also increased to Rs 1 lakh from the previous Rs 51,000, along with other gifts. Minister Arun highlighted the success of 'Operation Sindoor' against Pakistan and lauded Prime Minister Modi and Chief Minister Adityanath's leadership for India's economic growth.
