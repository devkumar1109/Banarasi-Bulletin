Yogi's UP: From BIMARU to Business Boom

2025-03-27T18:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has undergone a significant transformation under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's leadership. The state, formerly known as BIMARU, is now a leading destination for business and investment, attracting proposals exceeding ₹45 lakh crore in the past eight years. This growth has created over 60 lakh jobs for youth. The government's focus on security, robust infrastructure, and business-friendly policies has fostered an environment conducive to development. Uttar Pradesh has made substantial strides in poverty alleviation, with over six crore people lifted above the poverty line. The Antyodaya model has provided free food grains, gas connections, and financial assistance to millions of underprivileged citizens. Significant investments have also been made in healthcare infrastructure, increasing the number of medical colleges from 12 to 80.
