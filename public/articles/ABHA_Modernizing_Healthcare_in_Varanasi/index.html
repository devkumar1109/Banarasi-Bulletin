ABHA: Modernizing Healthcare in Varanasi

2025-03-05T17:44:00+00:00

The Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABHA) is transforming healthcare in Varanasi, India by streamlining hospital queues and improving treatment transparency. ABHA provides a unique health ID, similar to a bank account, for each individual, digitally storing medical records and prescriptions. Over 30.5 lakh ABHA IDs have been issued in Varanasi, with 1,378 healthcare providers registered. Patients can access their records digitally using their ID and password, connecting them to government health programs. At government hospitals, OPD slips can be obtained without queuing by scanning a QR code at the ABHA counter. ABHA allows verified doctors and hospitals to receive medical records, facilitating efficient patient care.
