Week-Old Bride Found Dead, Husband Arrested

2025-05-18T07:10:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Amauli village, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, killed his 26-year-old wife, Aarti Pal, just a week after their wedding. This was Raju Pal's third marriage, and clashes reportedly began immediately after the ceremony on May 9th. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death. Neighbors alerted the police, who took Aarti to Narpatpur Community Health Centre where she was pronounced dead. Raju has been arrested, and the body has been sent for post-mortem.
