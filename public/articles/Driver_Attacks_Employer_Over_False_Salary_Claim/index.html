Driver Attacks Employer Over False Salary Claim

2025-03-09T03:46:00+00:00

A 36-year-old driver, Rakesh Maharath, was arrested in Bhubaneswar for assaulting his employer, Swarnalata Behera, over unpaid salary. The incident occurred on March 7 when Rakesh, allegedly intoxicated, demanded outstanding dues. When Swarnalata denied the claim, stating his wages had been paid, Rakesh attacked her and her children with an iron bar, causing serious injuries to Swarnalata and minor injuries to her children. He also damaged Swarnalata's mobile phone before fleeing the scene. Police investigation revealed that Swarnalata had cleared Rakesh's dues several days prior.
