Hate Speech Condemned: Samyukt Kisan Morcha Demands Action

2025-03-15T13:38:00+00:00

Samyukt Kisan Morcha condemned hate speech against minorities by Uttar Pradesh Police officer Anuj Kumar Chaudhary and BJP leaders Yogi Adityanath and Raghuraj Singh. The organization demanded Chaudhary's suspension, apologies from the BJP leaders, and urged unity against hate. They called on the RSS to clarify its stance on minority issues and asked the judiciary to take action against those inciting religious hate. Samyukt Kisan Morcha emphasized the need for communal amity and the protection of all citizens' rights, irrespective of religion.
