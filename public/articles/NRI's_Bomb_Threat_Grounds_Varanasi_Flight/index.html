NRI's Bomb Threat Grounds Varanasi Flight

2025-04-27T13:45:00+00:00

An NRI passenger, Nishant Yohanathan, caused a bomb scare at Varanasi Babatpur Airport on Saturday night. He falsely claimed to have a bomb in his bag while onboard an IndiGo flight to Bangalore. The plane was immediately isolated, passengers detained, and a thorough investigation was conducted. No suspicious items were found, but Yohanathan was taken into custody by the IB-LAU team. The flight departed safely the next morning. A Canadian passport was found on Yohanathan, and he is being questioned by the Phulpur police and intelligence agencies. An FIR will be registered against him.
