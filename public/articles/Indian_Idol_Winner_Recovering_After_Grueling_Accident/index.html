Indian Idol Winner Recovering After Grueling Accident

2025-05-08T13:15:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan, critically injured in a road accident on May 5th in Uttar Pradesh, has been moved out of the ICU and into a private room. His friend Govind Digari shared a picture from the hospital, stating Rajan is doing much better. Rajan's team previously revealed he suffered multiple major fractures and small injuries after his SUV collided with a truck. He underwent a 6-hour surgery to address some of the fractures and is currently under observation. Another surgery is scheduled in 3-4 days to address the remaining fractures and injuries.
