DSP's Holi Warning Sparks Muslim Anger

2025-03-10T05:21:00+00:00

Anuj Chaudhary, a Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) in Uttar Pradesh, sparked controversy for advising Muslims to stay indoors during Holi celebrations, which coincide with Friday prayers during Ramadan. Chaudhary, a former national wrestling champion, received praise from Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath for his statement. His remarks drew widespread outrage.
