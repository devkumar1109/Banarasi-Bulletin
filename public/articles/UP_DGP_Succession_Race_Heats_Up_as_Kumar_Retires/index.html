UP DGP Succession: Race Heats Up as Kumar Retires

2025-05-13T07:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh DGP Prashant Kumar is retiring on May 31, leading to discussions about his successor. Though the state amended rules for DGP selection, no committee has been formed yet. Senior IPS officers retiring include DG Jail PV Ramasastri and DG Telecom Sanjay M. Tarde, impacting the seniority list. Contenders for DGP include Daljit Singh Chaudhary (DG BSF), Alok Sharma (SPG chief), and Rajiv Krishna (Director of Vigilance). Tilottama Verma, DG Training, is a strong contender to become Uttar Pradesh's first woman DGP.
