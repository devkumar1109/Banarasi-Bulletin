Transformer Blast: Power Staff Fired, Mafia Suspected

2025-03-10T18:35:00+00:00

Two power department employees in Ghazipur district were dismissed and two others suspended following a transformer explosion that injured four people. The explosion occurred at an ice factory in Usia village where a 25 KVA transformer was illegally installed. Lineman Mantu Singh Kushwaha and Substation Officer Azad Singh Kushwaha were dismissed, while Junior Engineer Shashikant Patel and SDO Kamlesh Kumar were suspended. The incident prompted an investigation into illegal dealings involving transformers and the electrical system in the district, with allegations of mafia and political involvement. An FIR has been filed against Mantu Singh Kushwaha and a local resident, Saddam Khan.
