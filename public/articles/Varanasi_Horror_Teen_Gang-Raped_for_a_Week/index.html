Varanasi Horror: Teen Gang-Raped for a Week

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over a week-long period between March 29 and April 4. The victim, a Class 12 student, went missing after a friend took her to a hookah bar. She returned home on April 4 and disclosed the horrific ordeal to her family, who subsequently filed a police complaint. The accused include former classmates and individuals the victim had interacted with on Instagram. Police have filed an FIR against all 23 suspects. Six have been arrested, while 11 remain unidentified and are being sought. The victim alleges she was drugged at the hookah bar and taken to different hotels where she was repeatedly assaulted.
