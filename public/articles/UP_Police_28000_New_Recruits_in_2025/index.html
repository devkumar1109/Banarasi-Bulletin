UP Police: 28,000 New Recruits in 2025

2025-03-27T15:52:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will recruit 28,138 police personnel in April-May 2025. This includes 4,543 Sub-Inspector-level positions (4,242 Civil Police, 135 PAC Platoon Commanders, 60 Special Forces Platoon Commanders, and 106 Female Platoon Commanders) and 22,053 Constable-level positions (15,904 PAC, Special Forces, and Women PAC Constables, and 3,245 Civil Police Constables). This recruitment drive is part of CM Yogi Adityanath's plan to strengthen the police force. The UPPRPB has already recruited over 60,000 constables and 1,400 radio division personnel in March 2025. Applications will be open in late April or early May 2025.
