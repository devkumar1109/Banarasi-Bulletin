Bengaluru Honors Vice President Dhankhar

2025-06-08T06:50:00+00:00

MP Lahar Singh Siroya hosted a High Tea in Bengaluru in honor of Vice-President Jagdeep Dhankhar. The event was attended by Karnataka Governor Thaawar Chand Gehlot, Deputy Chief Minister DK Shivakumar, opposition leaders, MLAs, religious leaders, and business entrepreneurs. Siroya praised Dhankhar's commitment to democracy and described him as a true democrat. Dhankhar emphasized the importance of a vibrant democracy and highlighted Bengaluru's role in technological advancement. He stressed the need for peace sustained through strength and technological excellence, offering condolences to the victims of the Pahalgam massacre. Dhankhar also extended an invitation to Bengaluru's business leaders to visit the New Parliament. Siroya presented Dhankhar with a Mysore gold leaf painting of Goddess Chamundeshwari, while the Federation of Karnataka Chambers of Commerce and Industry also felicitated the Vice-President.
