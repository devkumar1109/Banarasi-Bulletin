Golden Gujiyas: Rs 50,000 a Kilo!

2025-03-14T03:21:00+00:00

A sweets shop in Uttar Pradesh's Gonda is selling "Golden Gujiyas" for Rs 50,000 per kg. The price is due to a layer of 24-carat gold and a special dry fruit filling. In Lucknow, a shop made a world record Gujiya, 25 inches long and weighing 6 kilograms, entered in the India Book of Records. Social media users reacted humorously to the expensive Gujiya, with comments ranging from "Gujiya bhi gold ke bhav mein milne lagi hai" to "How many gujiyas can be made from one kidney?" Holi celebrations are in full swing across India.
