JEE Main 2025 Session 2 Results Out

2025-04-19T09:35:00+00:00

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has declared the JEE Main 2025 Session 2 results. Candidates can check their scores, percentile, and other details on the official website, jeemain.nta.nic.in, using their application number and date of birth. The April session exam was held from April 2 to 9 for various papers including BE/BTech, BArch, and BPlanning. The final answer key is also available on the website. The article also mentions the top 10 NITs in India as per the NIRF Engineering Rankings 2024 and provides category-wise cutoffs for JEE Main 2025. Finally, it highlights major changes in JEE Advanced 2025.
