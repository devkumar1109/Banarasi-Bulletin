Maha Kumbh 2025: Faith, Festivities, and Global Impact

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj drew 62 crore devotees, significantly impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi saw over 2 crore visitors since the event began, while over 69 lakh people booked homestays in Ayodhya through the Divya Ayodhya app. Prime Minister Narendra Modi took a holy dip and performed puja at the Sangam. The Khel Maha Kumbh, a sports event during the festival, promoted indigenous sports and aimed to include them in the 2036 Olympics. Maha Kumbh 2025 implemented a 'zero discharge' initiative, managing wastewater from over 1.5 lakh toilets daily and ensuring no polluted water entered the river. This innovative waste management system sets a global benchmark for large-scale events.
