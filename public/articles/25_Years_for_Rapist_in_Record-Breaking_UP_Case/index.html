25 Years for Rapist in Record-Breaking UP Case

2025-05-30T02:53:00+00:00

A man named Khurshid Alam alias Mohammad Hamza was sentenced to 25 years rigorous imprisonment for raping a 5-year-old girl in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh. The verdict was delivered within 26 days of the crime, setting a record in the state under the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita. The Jaunpur SP stated that the investigation, led by Jai Prakash Yadav, was completed in 24 hours and a chargesheet was submitted to the court. The case was registered under sections 65(2) BNS and 5M/6 POCSO Act. The accused was found guilty and sentenced accordingly.
