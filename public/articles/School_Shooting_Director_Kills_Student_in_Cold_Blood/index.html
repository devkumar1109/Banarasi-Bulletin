School Shooting: Director Kills Student in Cold Blood

2025-04-22T17:58:00+00:00

A Class 12 student, Hemant Singh, was shot dead by Ravi Singh, the manager and director of Gyandeep Public School in Varanasi. The incident occurred on Tuesday afternoon when Ravi Singh, along with two others, took Hemant into a room. CCTV footage shows the shooting, and Hemant was later pronounced dead at BHU Trauma Centre. Police arrested Ravi Singh and recovered the pistol used in the crime. Authorities are pursuing the arrest of the two accomplices. Initial investigation suggests Ravi Singh summoned Hemant to discuss his differences with a teacher, leading to the fatal shooting. Forensic experts collected evidence, including the CCTV DVR. Lawyers protested outside the school, demanding the arrest of the accused.
