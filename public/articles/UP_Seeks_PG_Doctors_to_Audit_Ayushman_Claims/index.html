UP Seeks PG Doctors to Audit Ayushman Claims

2025-03-04T05:48:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is seeking postgraduate (PG) doctors to audit reimbursement claims under the Ayushman Bharat scheme. This initiative aims to ensure the accurate and efficient processing of medical reimbursements for beneficiaries of the national health insurance program.
