Boney Kapoor's Film City: Uttar Pradesh's Spotlight

2025-05-27T10:49:00+00:00

Boney Kapoor's company, Bayview Projects LLP, has submitted a layout plan for the International Film City in Greater Noida to the YEIDA. The 1,000-acre project, spanning the Yamuna Expressway, aims to position Uttar Pradesh as a major film production hub. Phase one will cover 230 acres with a Rs 1,510 crore investment and create around 500,000 jobs. The vision includes film studios, a convention center, a museum, and other amenities, attracting national and international productions. Construction will commence after YEIDA's review and approval.
