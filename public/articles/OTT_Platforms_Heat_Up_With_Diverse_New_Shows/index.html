OTT Platforms Heat Up With Diverse New Shows

2025-06-06T19:30:00+00:00

This month, various OTT platforms are releasing captivating shows. Prime Video features "Bhool Chuk Maaf," a sci-fi romance starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi. Netflix presents "Jaat," an action drama starring Sunny Deol and Randeep Hooda, and "Mercy for None," a Korean crime thriller. JioHotstar offers "Tourist Family," a Tamil comedy-drama about an Eelam Tamil family migrating to India. Zee5 releases "Chhal Kapat: The Deception," an investigative thriller, and "Stolen," about two brothers who witness a baby kidnapping. SonyLIV's "Yatra 2" continues the political journey of Y.S. Jagan Mohan Reddy. JioCinema offers "Gaanth," a psychological crime thriller set in Delhi.
