Fake TTE Dupes Passengers, Arrested in Ahmedabad

2025-06-05T00:45:00+00:00

A 45-year-old labourer from Varanasi, Shiv Shankar Jaiswal, was arrested for impersonating a Travelling Ticket Examiner (TTE) at Ahmedabad Railway Station. Dressed in a fake TTE uniform, Jaiswal targeted unsuspecting passengers, promising them confirmed seats and collecting amounts ranging from Rs 1,200 to Rs 1,500 through UPI QR codes. He was caught by an alert RPF officer who questioned him after noticing suspicious behavior. Investigations revealed he had committed a similar scam on May 30 at the same station. Jaiswal confessed to the impersonation during interrogation and was handed over to the Government Railway Police (GRP).
