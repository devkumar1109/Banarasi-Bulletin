Modi's Eastward Development Blitz

2025-05-28T05:37:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will embark on a two-day tour of Sikkim, West Bengal, Bihar, and Uttar Pradesh on May 29th and 30th. In Sikkim, he will participate in the "Sikkim@50" program and lay the foundation stone for and inaugurate development projects, including a 500-bedded district hospital and a passenger ropeway. In West Bengal, PM Modi will lay the foundation stone for City Gas Distribution projects in Alipurduar and Cooch Behar districts. He will then travel to Bihar to inaugurate the new terminal building of Patna Airport and dedicate development projects worth over Rs 48,520 crore in Karakat. Finally, in Uttar Pradesh, PM Modi will lay the foundation stone and inaugurate development projects worth around Rs 20,900 crore at Kanpur Nagar.
