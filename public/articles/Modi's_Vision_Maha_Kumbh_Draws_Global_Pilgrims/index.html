Modi's Vision: Maha Kumbh Draws Global Pilgrims

2025-02-27T15:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath credited Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the success of Maha Kumbh 2025, calling it a global event. Over 66 crore pilgrims visited Prayagraj during the 45-day event, setting new records in religious tourism. Adityanath highlighted Modi's vision of integrating faith and economy, emphasizing the potential of spiritual tourism in Uttar Pradesh. He noted that 64 crore pilgrims visited various religious sites in Uttar Pradesh in 2024. The Maha Kumbh led to the development of five spiritual tourism corridors connecting major religious sites across the state.
