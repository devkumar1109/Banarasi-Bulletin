India Embraces Event-Driven, Unique Travel Experiences

2025-04-01T04:51:00+00:00

Indians are increasingly prioritizing unique travel experiences over traditional sightseeing. They are planning trips around major events like religious gatherings (Maha Kumbh, Ram temple consecration), music festivals (Lollapalooza, Coldplay concerts), and cultural festivals (Hornbill Festival). This trend is driven by a desire for immersive experiences and event-centric itineraries. Coldplay concerts in Mumbai and Ahmedabad saw a significant surge in flight and train bookings, highlighting the impact of such events on domestic travel. Travel platforms like MakeMyTrip and Ixigo report a growing demand for adventure travel, cultural tours, and wellness retreats.
