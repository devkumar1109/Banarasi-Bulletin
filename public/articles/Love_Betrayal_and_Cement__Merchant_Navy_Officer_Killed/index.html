Love, Betrayal, and Cement: Merchant Navy Officer Killed

2025-03-19T06:57:00+00:00

A merchant navy officer, Saurabh Rajput (29), was murdered by his wife, Muskan (27), and her lover, Sahil (25), in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on March 4th when Rajput was allegedly stabbed to death. His body was dismembered, sealed in a cement-filled drum, and recovered by police. Both Muskan and Sahil confessed to the crime during interrogation. They initially attempted to mislead Rajput's family by sending messages from his phone and then went on a vacation. Rajput and Muskan, who had a love marriage in 2016, lived separately due to family disapproval. A case has been registered based on a complaint by Rajput's family.
