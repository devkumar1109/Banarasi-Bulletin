Congress Workers Arrested in UP Eviction Dispute

2025-06-07T10:41:00+00:00

Two local Congress workers, Anand Pandey and Amit Pathak, were arrested in Uttar Pradesh on charges of robbery, theft, and molestation. The arrests follow a complaint by Sachina Khatoon, who alleged harassment and forced eviction from her boutique. Khatoon claims the workers pressured her to vacate the shop after their landlord's death during the pandemic, despite a stay order on eviction. Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai, who has ties to the arrested workers, accuses the government of orchestrating a political conspiracy to tarnish his reputation.
