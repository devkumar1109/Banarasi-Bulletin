Speed Chargers Charge Towards Title Defense

2025-03-01T20:11:00+00:00

Speed Chargers defeated PrimeHealth City 4-1 in a thrilling match, putting them in a prime position to defend their title in the upcoming final against Legends. Rakesh Seth and Rajesh Narain led the charge for Speed Chargers, while Dhruv Seth secured a victory for them in the first match. Ramswaroop Tigerz will face PrimeHealth City for the third place. The winner of the tournament will receive Rs 5 lakh, while the runners-up will get Rs 3 lakh. In other news, Lucknow's Shubham led his team to victory against Amethi in the Senior Open State-Level Men's Handball Championship with seven goals. Mumtaz PG College defeated DAV College by 50 runs in the inaugural match of the 52nd Sundari Devi Memorial Cricket Tournament.
