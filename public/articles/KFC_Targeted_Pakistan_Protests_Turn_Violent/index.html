KFC Targeted: Pakistan Protests Turn Violent

2025-04-19T15:25:00+00:00

Over 20 KFC outlets across Pakistan were attacked this month during anti-Israel protests, resulting in one death and over 160 arrests. Pakistan's Minister of State for Interior, Tallal Chaudhry, linked the attacks to activists from the hardline Islamist party Tehreek-e-Labbaik Pakistan (TLP) but stated no political party officially endorsed the violence. Chaudhry warned of strict action against those attacking businesses, emphasizing that the KFC franchise in Pakistan is entirely locally managed, with over $100 million in investment and 25,000 jobs. Prominent religious leaders have condemned the violence.
