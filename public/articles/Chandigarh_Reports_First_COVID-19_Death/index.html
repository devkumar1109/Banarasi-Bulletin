Chandigarh Reports First COVID-19 Death

2025-05-28T11:25:00+00:00

Chandigarh reported its first COVID-19 death on Wednesday. A 40-year-old man from Firozabad, Uttar Pradesh, who worked in Ludhiana, died at GMCH, Sector 32. He was admitted two days prior with severe respiratory problems and tested positive on Tuesday. The patient had no comorbidities. This marks the first COVID-19 death in Chandigarh, although the country is experiencing a fresh surge in infections. Health authorities are urging citizens to follow preventive measures, including wearing masks, maintaining hygiene, and avoiding crowded places.
