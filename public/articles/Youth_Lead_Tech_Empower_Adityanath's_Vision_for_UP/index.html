Youth Lead, Tech Empower: Adityanath's Vision for UP

2025-03-28T14:53:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath emphasized the importance of youth leadership at the Viksit Bharat Youth Parliament Festival. He stressed the need for collaboration between the legislature, executive, and judiciary for effective governance. Adityanath encouraged youth to develop leadership skills and innovate for societal benefit, noting that the Youth Parliament aims to nurture leaders in various fields. He highlighted the transformative role of technology in governance, citing examples like e-Vidhan, which digitizes legislative processes, and DBT systems for streamlined benefit distribution.
