Central Railway Adds 498 Summer Specials

2025-03-26T13:41:00+00:00

Central Railway will operate 854 Summer Special Trains from Lokmanya Tilak Terminus (LTT) to manage the increased passenger traffic during the summer season. This includes 278 unreserved trains. Initially, 356 trips were announced, with an additional 498 trips added for passenger convenience. The trains will run on various routes, including bi-weekly specials to Danapur, Mau, and Banaras, with specific departure and arrival times. The trains will have various classes of accommodation, including AC-2 Tier, AC-3 Tier, Sleeper Class, and General Second Class.
