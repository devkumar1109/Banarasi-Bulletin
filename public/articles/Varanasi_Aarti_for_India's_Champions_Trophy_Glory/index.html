Varanasi Aarti for India's Champions Trophy Glory

2025-03-09T04:28:00+00:00

Cricket fans in Varanasi performed an aarti at the Sarang Nath Mahadev temple, praying for India's victory in the ICC Champions Trophy final against New Zealand. Fans and coaches expressed confidence in India's strong spin attack and balanced team lineup, highlighting the strength of players like Virat Kohli. The final is scheduled for Sunday at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium. This will be a rematch of a previous encounter between the two teams at the same venue.
