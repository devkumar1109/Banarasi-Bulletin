Modi Lays Foundation for Varanasi's Growth and Unity

2025-04-11T16:11:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for 44 projects worth Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, his parliamentary constituency. He emphasized his government's commitment to "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas" (inclusive development) contrasting it with the Opposition's focus on "parivar ka saath, parivar ka vikas" (family-centric development). Modi highlighted Varanasi's transformation over the past decade, stating it has embraced modernity while preserving its heritage. He cited advancements in healthcare, positioning Kashi as the region's health capital. Modi also spoke about initiatives like Kashi Tamil Sangamam, promoting national unity. He concluded by noting Uttar Pradesh's progress, now a "land of resolution" and leading in GI tagging.
