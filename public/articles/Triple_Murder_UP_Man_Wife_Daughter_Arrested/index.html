Triple Murder: UP Man, Wife, Daughter Arrested

2025-04-27T06:58:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, Nohar Chaudhary, his first wife Vidyavati alias Santola, and her daughter Lakshmi were arrested for the murder of Chaudhary's second wife, Sunita. The police allege that the trio conspired to kill Sunita by strangling her with a dupatta and beating her with a stick.
