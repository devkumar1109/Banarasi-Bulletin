BJP Courts Dalits with Ravidas Outreach in Bihar

2025-06-04T04:59:00+00:00

The BJP is launching a major outreach campaign targeting Dalit voters in Bihar ahead of the upcoming elections. Starting June 20, the party will hold a series of "Sant Shiromani Ravidas Sammelan" events across different districts, culminating in a grand finale in Gopalganj. This focus on the Ravidas community, a significant Dalit group, stems from two key factors: caste arithmetic (Dalits represent 19% of Bihar's voters) and a desire to improve upon last year's Lok Sabha election results where the NDA lost nine seats.
