From Lucknow to Tech Leader: Amit Walia's Journey

2025-03-27T06:03:00+00:00

Amit Walia, CEO of Informatica, discussed his journey from a small family in Lucknow to a successful tech leader. He holds a degree from IIT Varanasi and started his career at Tata Steel before transitioning to Infosys. Walia moved to the US, had a startup experience, and earned an MBA from Northwestern University. He worked at McKinsey for five years before joining Symantec and later exploring the consumer software market.
