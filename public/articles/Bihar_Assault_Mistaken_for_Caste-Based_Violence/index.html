Bihar Assault Mistaken for Caste-Based Violence

2025-04-15T14:47:00+00:00

A viral video showing a man and woman being assaulted by a group of men is falsely claimed to depict a Dalit couple harassed for using an "upper caste road" in Uttar Pradesh. The incident actually took place in Saran District, Bihar, in 2021. Reports from Hindustan and Navbharat Times confirmed the Bihar location and stated the assault occurred on September 27, 2021, in the Chanwar area of Lakshmanchak. The Saran District Administration also confirmed the incident in a press release. Four of the six identified perpetrators were arrested.
