Modi Praises Trump, Recalls "Howdy Modi" Bond

2025-03-17T07:41:00+00:00

US President Donald Trump shared Prime Minister Narendra Modi's three-hour podcast with US podcaster Lex Fridman on his social media platform, Truth Social. In the podcast, Modi praised Trump's "humility" and preparation for a second term, highlighting his dedication to the US even after an assassination attempt. Modi recalled Trump's gesture at the 'Howdy Modi' event and his personal tour of the White House, emphasizing their strong communication and trust. He attributed Trump's past comment about his negotiation skills to the former US President's graciousness. Modi also fondly recalled his long-standing association with Elon Musk, describing their recent meeting as warm and familial.
