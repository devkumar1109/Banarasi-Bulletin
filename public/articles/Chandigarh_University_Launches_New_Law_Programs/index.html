Chandigarh University Launches New Law Programs

2025-05-26T08:47:00+00:00

The Bar Council of India (BCI) has approved Chandigarh University Lucknow to offer 120 seats in LL.B and 240 seats in five-year integrated law programs starting in the 2025 session. The university will offer BA LL.B (Hons), BBA LL.B (Hons), and three-year LL.B programs, blending legal principles with technology and global perspectives. Dr. Abhishek Kumar Singh, with over 12 years of experience in legal academia and expertise in law and technology, has joined as the academic head of the School of Legal Studies at Chandigarh University Uttar Pradesh.
