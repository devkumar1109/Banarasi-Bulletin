BrahMos Facility: UPIDC Strengthens India's Defence

2025-05-12T00:49:00+00:00

Defence minister Rajnath Singh inaugurated a 200-acre BrahMos missile integration and testing facility in Lucknow on Sunday. This facility boosts the Uttar Pradesh Defence Industrial Corridor (UPIDC), which has already created 1,500 jobs and received investment proposals worth ₹34,000 crore. The UPIDC has already seen investments of ₹4,000 crore. BrahMos, a long-range missile jointly developed by India and Russia, was described by Singh as a message of strength to adversaries, a deterrent, and a commitment to safeguarding India’s borders.
