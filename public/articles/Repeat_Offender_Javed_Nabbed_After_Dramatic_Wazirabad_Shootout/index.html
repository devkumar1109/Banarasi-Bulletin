"Repeat Offender Javed Nabbed After Dramatic Wazirabad Shootout"

2025-05-02T08:01:00+00:00

Javed, a history-sheeter from Tagore Garden, was arrested by Delhi Crime Branch's Northern Range-II team on April 26 in Wazirabad. He was wanted for a gangrape case registered at PS Nabi Karim. The dramatic arrest, captured on CCTV, showed Javed attempting to flee and firing at police before being overpowered. Police recovered a pistol, one empty cartridge, and five live cartridges. Javed had been on the run since being released from Tihar Jail and was accused of committing the gangrape immediately after his release. A fresh case under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023, and the Arms Act has been registered. DCP Harsh Indora stated the operation was part of a larger crackdown on serious criminal activities. The investigation into the gangrape case revealed it occurred in May 2024.
