Uttar Pradesh Celebrates Shiva, Faith, and Unity

2025-02-26T14:58:00+00:00

Thousands of devotees celebrated Mahashivratri at Shiva temples across Uttar Pradesh. The festival, marking the union of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, saw pilgrims performing rituals and offering prayers. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath led rituals in Gorakhpur, while Varanasi witnessed a vibrant procession featuring naga sadhus and akharas. Ayodhya and Prayagraj also saw a massive turnout of devotees performing jalabhishek. The celebrations underscored the deep-rooted faith and cultural unity symbolized by Mahashivratri in the state.
