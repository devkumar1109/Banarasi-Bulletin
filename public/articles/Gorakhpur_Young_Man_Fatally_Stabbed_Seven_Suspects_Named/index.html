Gorakhpur: Young Man Fatally Stabbed, Seven Suspects Named

2025-03-09T15:15:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man, identified as Satyam, was stabbed to death in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. His body was found near a government tubewell. Police have named seven suspects and detained four for questioning. The victim may have been ambushed and stabbed to death. Senior Superintendent of Police Gaurav Grover met with the victim's family, who initially resisted the postmortem but were calmed by assurances of a swift investigation.
