India Slams Pakistan for Cross-Border Attacks

2025-05-09T07:43:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath warned Pakistan against terrorism, stating India's zero-tolerance policy. He criticized Pakistan, calling it a "shameless country" directly involved in terrorism, citing the involvement of Pakistani army officials in terrorist funerals. On the night of May 8-9, 2025, Pakistan launched drone and missile attacks on multiple Indian regions, including Jammu, Pathankot, Udhampur, Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Punjab. The Indian Army successfully repelled these attacks, neutralizing several Pakistani military posts along the Line of Control (LoC).
