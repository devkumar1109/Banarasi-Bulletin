UP Weaves Future: Silk Initiative Empowers Rural Women

2025-04-23T12:37:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government aims to increase silk production by connecting 7,500 rural groups to the State Rural Livelihood Mission in 15 districts. The initiative focuses on training, modern technology adoption, and financial assistance to empower silk producers. An agreement between the Silk Department and the State Rural Livelihood Mission targets connecting 50,000 women from 5,000 women's groups to silk production within five years. The state's annual silk production is 400 metric tonnes, with a demand of 3,500 metric tonnes. The 'Mukhyamantri Resham Vikas Yojana' aims to make the state self-reliant in silk production, building on the 28-fold growth in silk exports over the past eight years.
