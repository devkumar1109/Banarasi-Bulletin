Centre Scrutinizes Jal Jeevan Mission Schemes Nationwide

2025-05-20T09:41:00+00:00

The Centre has deployed 100 teams to inspect Jal Jeevan Mission schemes in various states, including Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh. This follows a review meeting led by Cabinet Secretary T V Somanathan on May 8 to assess the mission's progress in providing clean drinking water to rural households. The teams, comprising officials from central government departments, will assess 27 schemes in Madhya Pradesh, 21 in Rajasthan, 18 in Uttar Pradesh, and 16 in Karnataka, among others. The Personnel Ministry has appointed Central Nodal Officers for identified districts to oversee the inspection.
