Parrot Smuggling Busted in Varanasi

2025-06-01T19:39:00+00:00

Forest officials in Varanasi arrested three smugglers for illegally transporting 28 live parrots from Jharkhand. The birds were found packed in a carton box on a bus in the Andharapul area. The arrested individuals are bus driver Dhananjay Singh, conductor Abhay Singh alias Kanhaiya, and Md Arif. Arif, a resident of Varanasi, transported the parrots from Garhwa, Jharkhand. The seized birds are protected under the Wildlife Protection Act of 1972, and legal proceedings are underway against the smugglers under relevant sections of the Act. Forest officials urge citizens to report any illegal wildlife trade activities.
