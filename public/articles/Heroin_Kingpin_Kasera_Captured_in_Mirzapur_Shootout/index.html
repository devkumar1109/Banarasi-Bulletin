Heroin Kingpin Kasera Captured in Mirzapur Shootout

2025-03-23T19:08:00+00:00

Three drug smugglers, including notorious heroin smuggler Nanhe Kasera, were arrested in Mirzapur, Varanasi. Police recovered heroin worth Rs 6.5 crore from Kasera. He led police to a location in Rajgarh where he attempted to escape by firing at the police with a country-made pistol. During the encounter, a constable was injured, and Kasera was also injured in the leg. Kasera is currently hospitalized.
