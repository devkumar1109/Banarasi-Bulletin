Varanasi: World's Second Most Searched Holy City

2025-04-21T07:31:00+00:00

Varanasi has been ranked the world's second most popular religious destination by My Cross, a platform analyzing faith-based tourism trends. Kyoto, Japan, topped the list with a perfect score of 100, while Varanasi scored 78. The study evaluated cities based on visitor numbers, religious sites, online search volume, social media mentions, and accommodation availability. Varanasi topped online search volume with nearly 2.8 million monthly searches, despite receiving fewer annual visitors (11 million) compared to Kyoto (75 million). Varanasi boasts 1,149 accommodations, nearly double Kyoto's, demonstrating its readiness to accommodate more pilgrims.
