Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Allegedly Rape Woman for Days

2025-04-07T10:10:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh was allegedly gang-raped by over 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned on April 4. The survivor disclosed that the accused took her to various hotels and hookah bars where she was repeatedly raped. Six accused have been arrested, and a case has been registered under sections of the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita (BNS) for gang rape, outraging modesty, administering harmful substances, wrongful confinement, and criminal intimidation. Police are investigating to apprehend the remaining 17 accused.
