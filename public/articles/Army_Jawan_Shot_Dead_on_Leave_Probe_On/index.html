Army Jawan Shot Dead on Leave, Probe On

2025-04-10T12:15:00+00:00

Army jawan Vikrant Gurjar, 27, was shot dead in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh. Gurjar, posted in Jammu and Kashmir, was on a four-day leave to testify in the 2021 murder case of his cousin, Rajat. He was found dead near a roadside on Thursday morning with gunshot wounds to the head and chest. SP (Rural) Sagar Jain stated that police are investigating the incident and have sent the body for post-mortem. Senior officers are present in the village with additional police forces deployed as a precaution.
