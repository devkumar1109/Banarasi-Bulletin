UPCOS: Protecting Outsourced Workers' Rights

2025-04-25T21:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is establishing a corporation called Uttar Pradesh Outsource Service Corporation (UPCOS) to regulate the conditions of over 11 lakh outsourced workers in state administration. The corporation aims to address issues like salary deductions, delayed payments, lack of EPF/ESI benefits, and unfair recruitment practices. The CM emphasized the importance of transparency, job security, and social justice for outsourced workers. UPCOS will oversee centralized hiring, ensure reservation for SCs, STs, and OBCs, and mandate direct bank transfers of full remuneration by the 5th of every month. Agencies violating regulations will face strict penalties, including blacklisting, debarment, and legal action.
