Old Rivalry Turns Deadly in Meerut Shooting

2025-03-24T10:55:00+00:00

A 20-year-old man, Manish Prajapati, was shot dead in Gokulpur village, Meerut, following an argument stemming from an old rivalry. The incident occurred on Sunday night when a group of men arrived at Prajapati's home and opened fire. Police have registered a case against the named accused based on a complaint filed by Prajapati's family and are actively searching for the suspects. The murder is attributed to a past clash between Prajapati's family and the accused, which led to an attempted murder case against Prajapati two months ago. He was subsequently released on bail.
