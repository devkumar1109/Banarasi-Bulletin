Narsinghpur Karatekas Shine in Varanasi

2025-05-03T18:46:00+00:00

Six karate players from Narsinghpur, Madhya Pradesh, won 13 medals, including one gold, at a tournament in Varanasi. The competition, organized by United Shito Ryu Karate Association India, saw participation from India, Sri Lanka, Nepal, and Bhutan. India secured the top position, followed by Nepal and Bhutan. Mayamani Lodhi from Narsinghpur won a gold and a silver medal.
