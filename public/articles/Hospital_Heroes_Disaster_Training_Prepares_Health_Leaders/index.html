Hospital Heroes: Disaster Training Prepares Health Leaders

2025-03-08T18:54:00+00:00

A six-day disaster management training program concluded at IMS-BHU in Varanasi. Organized by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare's Disaster Management Cell, the program trained 37 hospital administrators from Gujarat, UP, Rajasthan, Haryana, and Daman and Diu. The training covered topics like preparing for disasters, ensuring hospital safety, triaging critical patients, and evacuating patients safely in case of emergencies like earthquakes or fires. Dr Kavita Meena, associate professor at BHU's department of Anaesthesiology, led the program.
