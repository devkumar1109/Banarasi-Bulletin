Neighbor Dispute Ends in Daughter's Tragic Death

2025-03-06T18:25:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man named Mohit strangled his five-year-old daughter to death for repeatedly visiting her neighbor's house despite his prohibition. Mohit had a conflict with his neighbor, Ramu, and forbade his daughter from going to his house. After discovering his daughter continued to visit the neighbor, Mohit took her to a mustard field and killed her. He then filed a missing person report to mislead the investigation. Parts of the girl's body were found in the field, leading police to question Mohit. During interrogation, he confessed to the crime. The police have initiated legal proceedings against Mohit.
