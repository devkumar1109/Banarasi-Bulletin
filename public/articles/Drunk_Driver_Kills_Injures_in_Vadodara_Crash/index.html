Drunk Driver Kills, Injures in Vadodara Crash

2025-03-14T10:08:00+00:00

In Vadodara, Gujarat, a 20-year-old driver, Rakshit Chaurasia, allegedly under the influence of alcohol, crashed his car into four pedestrians, killing a woman and injuring others. The incident occurred around 12:30 am near Karelibag. Chaurasia, a law student from Varanasi, was apprehended along with Mit Chauhan, the car's owner and a Vadodara resident, who was a passenger during the crash. Video footage shows Chaurasia exiting the heavily damaged car and shouting "another round" while bystanders attempted to restrain him. The car struck two scooters at a high speed (120 km/h), knocking down the riders and dragging them before coming to a halt.
