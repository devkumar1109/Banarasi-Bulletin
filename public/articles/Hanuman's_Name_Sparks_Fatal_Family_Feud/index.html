Hanuman's Name Sparks Fatal Family Feud

2025-06-17T17:49:00+00:00

In Varanasi, 50-year-old Ramesh Ram was beaten to death by his younger brothers, Anil Ram and Subhash Ram, and Subhash's wife, Phulkumari. The incident occurred late on Monday night after Ramesh, a mason, reportedly mentioned the name of Lord Hanuman's mother, Anjani, which Subhash took as a personal insult. The brothers, reportedly fueled by alcohol, began fighting with Ramesh, beating him with sticks. Other family members eventually intervened, taking Ramesh to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead. Police have arrested the three suspects and sent Ramesh's body for a postmortem examination.
