India's AVGC-XR Dream School Takes Shape

2025-05-03T14:55:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Creative Technology (IICT) will be modeled after IITs and IIMs. It will initially offer seven specialized courses at temporary facilities in the NFDC building on Peddar Road, starting within two weeks. The Ministry of Information and Broadcasting has partnered with industry giants like Jiostar, Adobe, Google, YouTube, and Meta to design the courses and provide software for the AVGC-XR sector. Microsoft, Google, and Meta signed MoUs with the ministry to support IICT's development. The government has allocated ₹400 crore for the project, with the land provided by the Maharashtra government. Phase one will be at the NFDC Building, and a larger campus is planned at Goregaon Film City.
