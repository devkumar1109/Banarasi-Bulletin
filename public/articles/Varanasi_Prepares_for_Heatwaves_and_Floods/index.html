Varanasi Prepares for Heatwaves and Floods

2025-05-24T05:11:00+00:00

Varanasi District Magistrate Satyendra Kumar ordered officials to prepare for heatwaves and potential floods. He stressed ensuring adequate shade and drinking water at crowded places, temples, and tourist spots. The DM directed the repair of faulty hand pumps and held village secretaries accountable for water shortages. Kumar also instructed officials to provide sufficient shade, fodder, and water for cows and to arrange green fodder for animals. He urged public awareness regarding heatwave precautions and instructed officials to complete necessary preparations, including tenders for materials, for the upcoming rainy season. The DM emphasized the importance of being proactive in addressing these potential challenges.
