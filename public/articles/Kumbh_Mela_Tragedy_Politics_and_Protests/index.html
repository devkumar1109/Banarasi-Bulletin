Kumbh Mela: Tragedy, Politics, and Protests

2025-02-28T15:36:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh Mela, a major Hindu pilgrimage, concluded on February 26th after a month and a half. Three stampede incidents during the event resulted in deaths, prompting criticism from Mamata Banerjee who labeled it a "Mrityu Kumbh". While the government claimed over 66 crore pilgrims took a holy dip, videos of the incidents led Prime Minister Modi to apologize for inadequate arrangements. Modi, however, also criticized those who "mock and ridicule religion," a stance echoed by Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath who called critics "vultures." The BJP has leveraged the Kumbh for political gain, transforming it into a propaganda tool and portraying itself as central to the event. This has challenged other political parties to find a suitable response.
