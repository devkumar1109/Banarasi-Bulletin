Uttar Pradesh Takes Flight: Rocket Launch Success

2025-06-15T05:28:00+00:00

On June 14th, ISRO successfully conducted a rocket launch test from Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh, a historic first for the state. The rocket reached a height of 1.1 km, deploying a small satellite which landed safely with the help of a parachute. This test, in collaboration with Thrust Tech India Limited, marks a significant step forward for space technology development in Uttar Pradesh. It serves as a precursor to a larger event in October-November where around 900 youth-built satellites will be tested.
