Varanasi: Community Clash Sparks Fear and Stone Pelting

2025-03-06T18:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a video of boys from a particular community pelting stones in the Kotwa area went viral. Police registered a case and are investigating the target. Around 15 Hindu families in the area claim they are being deliberately targeted, citing fear after a recent killing of a Hindu man, Bhaiyalal Patel, by individuals from the same community. The families allege that stones were thrown at their houses and a temple area was converted into a garbage dump. Police are increasing vigilance in the locality.
