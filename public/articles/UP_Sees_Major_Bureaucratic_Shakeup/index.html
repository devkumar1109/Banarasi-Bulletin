UP Sees Major Bureaucratic Shakeup

2025-04-22T14:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath ordered a major bureaucratic reshuffle, transferring 33 IAS officers and 3 senior IPS officers. Key changes include: * Vishal Singh appointed as the new Director of Information. * Varanasi District Magistrate S Rajalingam promoted to Varanasi Divisional Commissioner, replaced by Special Secretary to the Chief Minister Satyendra Kumar. * Hapur DM, Prerna Sharma, appointed Director of SUDA, and Principal Secretary (Stamps and Registration) Amit Gupta became Principal Secretary (Transport) and Chairman of the Uttar Pradesh Transport Corporation. Other notable appointments include Anupam Shukla as DM of Ambedkar Nagar, Indrajit Singh as Special Secretary (Energy), and Gaurav Kumar as Municipal Commissioner of Lucknow.
