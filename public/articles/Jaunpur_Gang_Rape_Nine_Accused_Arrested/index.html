Jaunpur Gang Rape: Nine Accused Arrested

2025-04-22T18:01:00+00:00

A 15-year-old girl was gangraped in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh. Police apprehended all nine accused, including four juveniles, within four hours of the incident. The girl was found abandoned near an exhibition park and reported the assault. Police recovered mobile phones and other items from the accused. The four minors will be sent to the Juvenile Justice Board. The victim was sent for a medical examination and an FIR has been lodged.
