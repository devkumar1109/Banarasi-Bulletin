Seven Underrated Bollywood Gems to Discover

2025-02-24T14:08:00+00:00

This article lists seven underrated Bollywood movies to watch. "Ankhon Dekhi" (2013) follows a man who only believes what he sees, leading to a transformative journey. "Nil Battey Sannata" (2015) is a heartwarming drama about a mother-daughter relationship overcoming educational challenges. "Masaan" (2015), set in Varanasi, explores love, loss, and redemption through interconnected stories. "Titli" (2015) is a gritty drama about a young man escaping his family's violent business. "Filmistaan" (2014) is a comedy-drama about an Indian man mistakenly kidnapped in Pakistan who uses Bollywood films to survive. "Barah Aana" (2009) is a dark comedy-drama about the struggles of the lower middle class in Mumbai. Finally, "Kadvi Hawa" (2017) focuses on climate change and its impact on farmers.
