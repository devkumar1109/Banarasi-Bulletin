Maurya to Banerjee: Watch 'Chhaava' on Aurangzeb's Reign

2025-03-21T09:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya urged West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee to watch the Hindi film 'Chhaava', stressing it provides insights into Aurangzeb's cruelty. Maurya, who attended a screening, highlighted the film's importance in understanding historical narratives. He criticized Akhilesh Yadav for not addressing a statement made by a Samajwadi Party MP about a historical figure known for forced religious conversions. 'Chhaava', based on the literary work of the same name, is a biographical account of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, and has been successful at the box office for five weeks.
