UP Strengthens Police Force: 60,000 New Recruits

2025-06-18T12:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath held a 'Janta Darshan' at Gorakhnath temple in Gorakhpur, addressing public grievances. Over the past eight years, the UP government has recruited 2.16 lakh police personnel, aiming to transform the police force into a "modern police." Union Home Minister Amit Shah distributed appointment letters to 60,244 new civil police constables in Lucknow, highlighting the significant recruitment drive and improvement in law and order under CM Adityanath. The UP Police Constable Recruitment Exam 2024, held across 67 districts, aimed to fill over 60,000 vacancies.
