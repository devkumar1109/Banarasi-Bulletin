BHU Raises MTP Kit Safety Awareness for Youth

2025-04-24T07:46:00+00:00

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) hosted a health awareness session on the safe use of MTP kits and adolescent wellbeing. Prof. Mamta from the Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology emphasized the dangers of improper MTP kit use, stressing the need for medical guidance. The session covered correct usage, potential side effects, and safety precautions. It also promoted menstrual hygiene and highlighted the importance of HPV vaccination for cervical cancer prevention. The event was organized by the Sathiya Kendra at BHU's Institute of Medical Sciences, in collaboration with Mamta Health Institute and the Uttar Pradesh National Health Mission.
