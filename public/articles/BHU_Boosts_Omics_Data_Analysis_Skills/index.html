BHU Boosts Omics Data Analysis Skills

2025-06-10T19:20:00+00:00

A week-long GIAN course on Models and Algorithms For Omics Data Analysis was conducted by BHU's School of Biotechnology from June 2 to 6. The course, under the Ministry of Education's Global Initiative of Academic Networks (GIAN), aimed to enhance India's scientific and technological capabilities. Prof. Rajeev Kumar Azad from the University of North Texas, USA, delivered expert insights during the course. The GIAN initiative seeks to improve academic resources, accelerate quality improvement, and elevate India's scientific standing globally.
