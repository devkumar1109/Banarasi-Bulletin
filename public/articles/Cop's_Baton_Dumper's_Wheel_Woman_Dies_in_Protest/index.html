Cop's Baton, Dumper's Wheel, Woman Dies in Protest

2025-04-22T04:29:00+00:00

A 34-year-old woman in Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, died after being run over by a dumper truck. The incident occurred during a police check when, according to the victim's husband, a sub-inspector struck his motorcycle with a baton, causing his wife to fall off and be hit by the truck. Locals protested the incident, joined by MLA Salona Kushwaha, who demanded an investigation and action against the sub-inspector. Shahjahanpur SP Rajesh Dwivedi confirmed a case has been registered against the sub-inspector and truck driver under section 105 (culpable homicide) of BNS. An investigation is underway, and an FIR has also been filed against those who disrupted traffic during the protest.
