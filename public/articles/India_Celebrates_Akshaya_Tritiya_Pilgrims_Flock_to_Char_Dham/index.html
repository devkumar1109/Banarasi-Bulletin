India Celebrates Akshaya Tritiya, Pilgrims Flock to Char Dham

2025-04-30T06:03:00+00:00

Akshaya Tritiya is being celebrated across India with rituals and a gold rush in holy cities. The Char Dham Yatra has begun with the opening of the Gangotri and Yamunotri temples on April 30, followed by Kedarnath and Badrinath on May 2 and 4. Over 22 lakh devotees have registered for the Yatra, with security intensified after the recent Pahalgam terror attack. In other news, Rahul Gandhi visited the family of Shubham Dwivedi, a victim of the Pahalgam attack, and is currently on a two-day visit to Uttar Pradesh. The Congress party has called for a joint Parliament session to address the terror attack. Meanwhile, the Rs 250-crore Jagannathdham temple, modelled after Puri's original, was inaugurated in Digha, West Bengal.
