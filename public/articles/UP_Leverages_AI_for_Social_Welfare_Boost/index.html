UP Leverages AI for Social Welfare Boost

2025-03-06T16:05:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh social welfare department will utilize artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance the implementation of public utility schemes. A brainstorming program was held in Lucknow to discuss AI's role in the social sector, focusing on reliable data, a positive environment, and bridging the digital divide. Minister Asim Arun announced virtual care assistance for senior citizens and AI integration into department schemes with security measures. AI is increasingly used in UP's social sector. In healthcare, AI-enabled ICUs and medical equipment improve patient monitoring and treatment. AI aids in disease prevention and telemedicine through chatbots. Education benefits from AI-powered Smart Classrooms and personalized learning tools. AI data analytics is also used to reduce dropout rates. In pension schemes, AI streamlines administration, optimizes investments, and resolves member issues.
