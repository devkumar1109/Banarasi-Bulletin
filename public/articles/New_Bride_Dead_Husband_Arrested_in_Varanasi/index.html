New Bride Dead: Husband Arrested in Varanasi

2025-05-18T07:10:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 44-year-old man named Raju Pal has been arrested for murdering his 26-year-old wife, Aarti Pal. This occurred just one week after their wedding. Police report that Aarti was Raju's third wife. Following their marriage on May 9th, frequent clashes arose between the couple. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, resulting in her death. Neighbors alerted the police, who transported Aarti to Narpatpur Community Health Centre, where she was pronounced dead. Raju has been arrested and the body has been sent for post-mortem examination.
