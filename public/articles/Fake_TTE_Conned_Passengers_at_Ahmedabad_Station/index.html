Fake TTE Conned Passengers at Ahmedabad Station

2025-06-05T00:45:00+00:00

Shiv Shankar Jaiswal, a 45-year-old labourer from Varanasi, was arrested at Ahmedabad Railway Station for posing as a Travelling Ticket Examiner (TTE). Jaiswal wore a fake TTE uniform and tricked passengers into paying him between Rs 1,200 and Rs 1,500 through UPI QR codes, promising them confirmed seats. He was caught by an alert RPF officer who noticed his suspicious behaviour and lack of official identification. Investigations revealed he had committed a similar scam on May 30 at the same station. Jaiswal has been handed over to the Government Railway Police (GRP).
