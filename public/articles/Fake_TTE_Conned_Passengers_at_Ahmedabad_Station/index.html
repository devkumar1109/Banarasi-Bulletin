Fake TTE Conned Passengers at Ahmedabad Station

2025-06-05T00:45:00+00:00

Shiv Shankar Jaiswal, a 45-year-old laborer from Varanasi, was arrested for posing as a Travelling Ticket Examiner (TTE) at Ahmedabad Railway Station. Jaiswal wore a fake TTE uniform and tricked passengers into paying him through UPI QR codes, promising confirmed seats. He charged between Rs 1,200 and Rs 1,500 for these non-existent seats. An alert RPF officer caught Jaiswal after noticing suspicious behavior and his inability to provide any official identification or documents. Investigations revealed he had committed a similar scam on May 30th at the same station. He was handed over to the Government Railway Police (GRP).
