Varanasi Fights Disease, Boasts New Health Services

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, chikungunya, and diarrhea. The campaign aims to prevent waterborne and communicable diseases by addressing waterlogging and promoting cleanliness. According to officials, the number of dengue, malaria, and chikungunya cases in 2025 has significantly decreased compared to previous years. SVM Bhelupur Hospital introduced various new services, including ultrasound, digital X-ray, dental department, eye examination, and mental health OPD, to enhance medical care. The campaign will also include the Dastak campaign from April 10 to 31, focusing on 68 dengue hotspots in urban areas and 56 in rural areas.
