Jail Chief Accused of Harassment, Corruption

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending inquiry into a harassment complaint filed against him by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment and harassing her for not complying with his demands. She alleged that Singh made inappropriate advances and pressured her to bring female inmates for sexual exploitation in exchange for benefits. Singh has denied the allegations. An investigation is underway, and the outcome will determine further action. Kanojia's daughter has also filed a complaint against Singh, alleging mental and physical harassment.
