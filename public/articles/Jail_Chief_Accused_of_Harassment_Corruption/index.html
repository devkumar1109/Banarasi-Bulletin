Jail Chief Accused of Harassment, Corruption

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to the Sonbhadra jail pending an inquiry into a harassment complaint filed by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment, harassing her for not fulfilling his demands, and threatening her job security. She also alleged that Singh pressured her for sexual exploitation in exchange for benefits. Singh's colleague Saurabh Srivastava will oversee the Varanasi district jail until further notice. Kanojia's daughter filed a police complaint against Singh, detailing the alleged harassment and threats. Authorities are investigating both complaints and will determine the future course of action based on the findings.
