Varanasi: World's Second Most Sought-After Pilgrimage

2025-04-21T07:31:00+00:00

Varanasi has been ranked the world's second most popular religious destination by My Cross, a platform analyzing faith-based tourism trends. The study evaluated cities based on visitor numbers, religious sites, online searches, social media mentions, and accommodations. Varanasi scored 78 out of 100, trailing only Kyoto, Japan, which received a perfect score. While Kyoto has more visitors and religious sites, Varanasi topped the chart for online searches, receiving nearly 2.8 million monthly searches. Varanasi also boasts 1,149 accommodations, nearly double Kyoto's.
