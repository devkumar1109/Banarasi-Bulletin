Maha Kumbh 2025: Record Pilgrims, Seamless Security

2025-02-26T08:48:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj concluded successfully, attracting over 650 million pilgrims, a record-breaking number. The event was marked by seamless crowd management and advanced technology, including AI-driven monitoring and predictive analytics. Uttar Pradesh DGP Prashant Kumar called it a global benchmark in event security, highlighting the absence of major incidents and the use of non-forceful crowd control strategies. He credited the success to meticulous planning, extensive training of police officers, and coordination between various agencies like the Railways and law enforcement bodies. Despite peak pilgrim numbers exceeding 5 lakh, the event proceeded smoothly.
