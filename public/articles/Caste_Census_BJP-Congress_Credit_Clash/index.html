Caste Census: BJP-Congress Credit Clash

2025-05-03T18:17:00+00:00

The BJP and Congress are engaged in a credit battle over the decision to conduct a caste census. Congress claims the decision was a result of Rahul Gandhi's pressure, citing his repeated calls for a caste census in Parliament. They argue that his persistence forced the BJP-led government to concede. The Congress organized a thanksgiving march in Varanasi, featuring posters of Rahul Gandhi, and praised his courage in challenging the government. Conversely, the BJP celebrated the decision by sharing sweets and praising Prime Minister Modi's visionary leadership. BJP leaders argue that the caste census will usher in a new era of social justice and equality, highlighting the government's commitment to marginalized and backward classes.
