Varanasi Prioritizes Maternal Health on PMSMA Day

2025-04-09T18:37:00+00:00

Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan Diwas (PMSMA Day) was observed in Varanasi on April 10th, 2025, focusing on better healthcare and prenatal care for pregnant women. Across urban and rural health centers, including District Women's Hospital Kabirchaura and Lal Bahadur Shastri Hospital Ramnagar, pregnant women received free prenatal check-ups, including urine tests, blood pressure, diabetes, syphilis, and haemoglobin screenings. 2,586 women underwent check-ups, with 278 identified as high-risk pregnancies (HRP). HRP women were referred to higher-level units, provided free medication, and received medical and nutritional counseling. The initiative aims to reduce maternal and infant mortality rates and ensure better health outcomes for pregnant women.
