UP: Healthcare Hub Takes Shape

2025-03-18T06:09:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has transformed into a healthcare hub over the past eight years. With 80 operational medical colleges (44 government and 36 private), the state now has over 11,800 MBBS and PG seats. Significant expansion includes new super-specialty hospitals, research institutes, and a focus on AYUSH medicine. The government implemented the PPP model to establish medical colleges in Maharajganj, Shamli, and Sambhal. AYUSH medical education also received a boost with the establishment of an Ayurvedic College in Ayodhya and a Homeopathic Medical College in Varanasi. UP currently has 2,110 Ayurvedic, 254 Unani, and 1,585 Homeopathic hospitals.
