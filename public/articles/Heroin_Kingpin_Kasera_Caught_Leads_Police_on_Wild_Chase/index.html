Heroin Kingpin Kasera Caught, Leads Police on Wild Chase

2025-03-23T19:08:00+00:00

Three drug smugglers, including notorious heroin smuggler Nanhe Kasera, were arrested in Varanasi. During the raid, heroin worth Rs 6.5 crore was recovered from Kasera. He led the police to a location in Rajgarh where no narcotics were found, but country-made pistols were hidden. Kasera opened fire on the police while attempting to escape, injuring a constable. The police returned fire, injuring Kasera in the leg. He was arrested and taken to the district hospital.
