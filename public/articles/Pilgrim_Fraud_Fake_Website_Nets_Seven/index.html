Pilgrim Fraud: Fake Website Nets Seven

2025-04-28T13:56:00+00:00

The Odisha Crime Branch arrested seven individuals for creating a fake website, www.neeladribhaktanivas.in, to defraud pilgrims seeking accommodation at Puri's Neeladri Bhakta Nivas. The accused, including Anshuman Sharma, lured pilgrims with promises of accommodation and requested online payments via WhatsApp, diverting funds to a Canara Bank account. The case was initiated based on a complaint from the Shree Jagannath Temple Administration (SJTA) after receiving numerous complaints from the public. The Cyber Crime Unit traced the website's origin to Anshuman Sharma, who created it on May 22, 2024. The fraudulent website has been taken down.
