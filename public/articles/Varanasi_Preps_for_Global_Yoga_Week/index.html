Varanasi Preps for Global Yoga Week

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for International Yoga Day on June 21st. The city will host a series of events called "Yoga Week" starting on June 15th. The main event will be held at Kashi Vishwanath Dham on June 21st. Yoga sessions will take place at various locations throughout the district, including ghats, village panchayats, urban areas, schools, and health and wellness centres. District officials are overseeing preparations and coordinating with yoga organizations and NGOs to ensure a successful celebration of Yoga Day.
