Love Turns Toxic: Woman Drinks Poison Over Rejected Marriage

2025-06-11T19:16:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a 19-year-old girl consumed poison at the Badagaon police station after her 22-year-old male neighbour refused to marry her. The couple, residents of Khanpatti village, had been summoned to the police station to resolve a dispute. The man was adamant about not marrying the girl, prompting her to take the drastic action. Her condition is currently out of danger after being rushed to a local hospital. The girl and the man had a past relationship, and the incident occurred after she left home with valuables and went to his residence.
