UP Cleans for Ambedkar's 134th: Legacy Shines

2025-04-13T14:44:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched a statewide cleanliness drive ahead of Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's 134th birth anniversary. The initiative involved community participation, focusing on cleaning statues, parks, and public spaces across all 75 districts. Efforts were concentrated on Ambedkar-related sites and monuments, including Ambedkar Park in Ayodhya and key landmarks in the Agra division. Both urban and rural areas benefited from the campaign, demonstrating community engagement and dedication to commemorating Ambedkar's legacy.
