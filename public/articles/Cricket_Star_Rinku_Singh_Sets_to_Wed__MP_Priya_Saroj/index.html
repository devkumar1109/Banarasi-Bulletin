Cricket Star Rinku Singh Sets to Wed MP Priya Saroj

2025-06-01T11:12:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Rinku Singh is engaged to Samajwadi Party MP Priya Saroj. Their engagement ceremony will take place in Lucknow on June 8th, followed by a traditional wedding in Varanasi on November 18th at Hotel Taj. The wedding invitations will be extended to prominent figures from cricket, Bollywood, politics, and business. 27-year-old Rinku Singh has represented India in international cricket and plays for Kolkata Knight Riders in the IPL. 26-year-old Priya Saroj is a first-time MP from the Machhlishahr constituency. Their families arranged the marriage after meeting in Aligarh.
