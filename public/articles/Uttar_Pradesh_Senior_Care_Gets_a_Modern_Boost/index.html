Uttar Pradesh: Senior Care Gets a Modern Boost

2025-04-04T01:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is improving healthcare facilities for senior citizens in old age homes. They will receive enhanced healthcare under the Ayushman Bharat scheme. A new program will utilize the professional and business expertise of senior citizens, offering financial incentives to promote self-reliance. Each government-supported old age home accommodates up to 150 residents and operates under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. Face-recognition attendance systems ensure transparency and service delivery. To address legal and family disputes, 216 reconciliation officers have been appointed at the tehsil level. District Magistrate-led committees oversee old age home management and service improvements.
