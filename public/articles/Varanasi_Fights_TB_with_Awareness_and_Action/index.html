Varanasi Fights TB with Awareness and Action

2025-03-24T17:58:00+00:00

World TB Day was observed in Varanasi on March 24, 2025, with a public awareness rally and seminar. 310 nutrition kits were distributed to TB patients at various medical units by Feeding India. The Government of India launched a '100-day Intensive TB Elimination Campaign' on December 7, 2024, to quickly identify and treat TB cases. As of March 24, 2025, 615,733 people were screened for TB in Varanasi district, and 9,781 underwent the NAT test. TB is an infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium tuberculosis, primarily affecting the lungs. It can spread through the air and infect other parts of the body if left untreated.
