Bride's Honeymoon Turns Deadly in Varanasi

2025-05-18T02:46:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Uttar Pradesh's Varanasi district, Raju Pal, has been arrested for murdering his third wife, Aarti Pal (26). The incident occurred just a week after their wedding on May 9th. Clashes arose between the couple immediately after the marriage. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death at a local hospital. Aarti's body has been sent for post-mortem.
