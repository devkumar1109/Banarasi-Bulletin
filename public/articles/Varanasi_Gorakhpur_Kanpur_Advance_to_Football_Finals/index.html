Varanasi, Gorakhpur, Kanpur Advance to Football Finals

2025-04-19T18:19:00+00:00

Varanasi defeated Saharanpur 4-0 to advance to the final of the UP under-20 inter divisional state men football championship. Ashish, Asif, Abhishek, and Karunesh scored for Varanasi. In another semi-final match, Gorakhpur beat Lucknow 1-0 thanks to Amritansh's goal. Kanpur secured a 2-0 win over Ayodhya in extra time, with goals from Shivam Pandey and Vihan Khanna.
