Community, Tech Fuel India's Immunization Drive

2025-04-27T04:30:00+00:00

The UNDP India chief emphasizes the crucial role of community participation and digital innovation in achieving universal immunization. He highlights the need for active community involvement in vaccine campaigns to overcome hesitancy and ensure equitable access. Digital solutions, such as mobile platforms and data analytics, are seen as essential tools for streamlining vaccine distribution, monitoring coverage, and reaching remote populations.
