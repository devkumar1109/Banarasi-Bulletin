Bride Fatally Shot, Husband Arrested After Week-Long Clash

2025-05-17T10:04:00+00:00

A 26-year-old woman, Aarti Pal from Jaunpur district, was killed by her husband Raju Pal in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, just a week after their wedding. This marks Raju's third marriage, with the previous two ending in divorce. Clashes reportedly began immediately after the wedding between Raju and Aarti. Police have arrested Raju and are investigating the case.
