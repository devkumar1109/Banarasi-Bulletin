BJP Bets on OBCs, Brahmins in UP Ahead of Polls

2025-03-17T06:17:00+00:00

The BJP has appointed 70 new district chiefs in Uttar Pradesh. The appointments show a focus on OBCs (Other Backward Classes) and Brahmins, key demographic groups in the state. This move is seen as a strategic effort by the BJP to strengthen its base ahead of the upcoming elections.
