Four Friends Killed in UP Road Accident

2025-05-31T11:05:00+00:00

Four friends from Sidhuapar village, Sunil Kumar, Pradumna Kumar, Arvind Kumar, and Rahul Kumar, died in a road accident on the Gorakhpur-Varanasi highway in Uttar Pradesh. The friends were returning home on a single Apache bike when a speeding Grand Vitara car hit them from the front. The impact was so severe that both the bike and car were damaged. Sunil, Pradumna, and Arvind died at the scene, while Rahul was critically injured and transferred to the district hospital. Arvind had returned from Bangkok just a week prior to the accident, adding to the tragedy for his family. The accident has raised concerns about road safety, with villagers highlighting the dangers of speeding vehicles.
