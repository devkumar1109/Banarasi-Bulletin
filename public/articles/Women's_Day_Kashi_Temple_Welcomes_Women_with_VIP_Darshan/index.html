Women's Day: Kashi Temple Welcomes Women with VIP Darshan

2025-03-08T05:56:00+00:00

On International Women's Day 2025, Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, introduced a special VIP darshan facility for women. Women were granted hassle-free entry through Gate Number 4, designed exclusively for them. This privilege allowed women to avoid the usual temple crowds and perform the sacred 'Jalabhishek'. The initiative also included health checkups for the female devotees.
