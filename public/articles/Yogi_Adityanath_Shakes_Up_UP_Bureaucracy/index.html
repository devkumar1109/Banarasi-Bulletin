Yogi Adityanath Shakes Up UP Bureaucracy

2025-04-22T14:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has ordered a major bureaucratic reshuffle, transferring 33 IAS officers and 3 senior IPS officers. Key changes include Vishal Singh appointed as Director of Information and Kaushal Raj Sharma, former Varanasi Division Commissioner, becoming Secretary to the Chief Minister. S Rajalingam, the outgoing DM of Varanasi, has been promoted to Varanasi Divisional Commissioner, with Satyendra Kumar, Special Secretary to the Chief Minister, assuming the role of Varanasi DM. Other notable appointments include Prerna Sharma, former Hapur DM, as Director of SUDA, and Abhishek Pandey as Hapur DM. Sanjay Kumar Meena, Gorakhpur’s CDO, has been appointed Vice Chairman of the Meerut Development Authority.
