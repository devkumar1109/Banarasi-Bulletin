Civil Defence Drills: Agra, Muzaffarnagar Test Readiness

2025-05-08T12:48:00+00:00

A nationwide civil defence mock drill was held in Agra and Muzaffarnagar on May 8, 2025, assessing preparedness against security threats. In Agra, the drill involved two parts: a demonstration of safety measures at Ganesh Ram Nagar School and an air raid warning simulation. Nearly 700 volunteers participated, including schoolchildren and NCC cadets. Electrical sirens sounded at four locations, followed by a city-wide blackout. In Muzaffarnagar, the drill involved police, fire services, health department, NCC, scout guides, and bomb disposal squads. Schoolchildren participated in the hour-long exercise.
