LSG Surge to 171 Against PBKS

2025-04-01T15:54:00+00:00

In the first home match of IPL 2025, Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) scored 171/7 against Punjab Kings (PBKS). LSG struggled initially, reaching 35/3, but Nicholas Pooran (44) and Ayush Badoni (41) rebuilt the innings. A late cameo by Abdul Samad (27) added crucial runs. Arshdeep Singh was the pick of the bowlers for PBKS, taking 3 wickets.
