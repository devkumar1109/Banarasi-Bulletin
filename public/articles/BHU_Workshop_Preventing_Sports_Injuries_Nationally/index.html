BHU Workshop: Preventing Sports Injuries, Nationally.

2025-04-23T17:43:00+00:00

A three-day workshop on Sports Injury Prevention and Rehabilitation, organized by Banaras Hindu University's University Sports Board, commenced on Tuesday. Professor Vikas Agarwal, a cardiologist from BHU's Institute of Medical Sciences, inaugurated the event, emphasizing the importance of a multidisciplinary approach to athlete care. He stressed the need for integrating cardiovascular fitness with physical rehabilitation for holistic athlete management. The workshop, initially planned as an institute-level program, expanded to a national scale due to high demand. 125 participants, including coaches, athletic trainers, and students, are attending the event.
