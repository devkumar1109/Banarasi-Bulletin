BHU Workshop: Preventing Sports Injuries, Nationally.

2025-04-23T17:43:00+00:00

A three-day workshop on Sports Injury Prevention and Rehabilitation, organized by the University Sports Board, Banaras Hindu University, commenced on Tuesday. Noted cardiologist Prof Vikas Agarwal inaugurated the event, emphasizing the need for a multidisciplinary approach to athlete care, integrating cardiovascular fitness with physical rehabilitation. The workshop, initially an institute-level program, expanded to a national event due to high demand. 125 participants, including coaches, athletic trainers, and students, are attending the workshop.
