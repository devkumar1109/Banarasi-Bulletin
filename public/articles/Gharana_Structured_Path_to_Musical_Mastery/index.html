Gharana: Structured Path to Musical Mastery

2025-05-06T12:17:00+00:00

Pt. Ulhas Kashalkar, a renowned musician, believes that the characteristics of a gharana (musical tradition) are highly beneficial for music learning. He emphasizes that gharana traditions provide a structured and comprehensive framework that guides students in their musical journey. Kashalkar's insights highlight the importance of lineage and mentorship in the transmission of musical knowledge within a gharana.
