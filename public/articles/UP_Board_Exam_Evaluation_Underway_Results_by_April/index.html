UP Board Exam Evaluation Underway, Results by April

2025-03-19T11:46:00+00:00

The UP Board has begun evaluating nearly 3 crore answer sheets for the High School and Intermediate exams. 261 evaluation centers across Uttar Pradesh are involved in the process, which is expected to conclude by April 2nd. Over 84,000 examiners and 8,437 deputy head examiners will assess 1.63 crore High School answer sheets, while 50,601 examiners and 5,471 deputy head examiners will evaluate 1.33 crore Intermediate answer sheets. The UP Board aims to announce the results by the last week of April. A live feed from all evaluation centers is being monitored to ensure transparency. A total of 25.56 lakh students took the High School exam and 25.77 lakh students took the Intermediate exam.
