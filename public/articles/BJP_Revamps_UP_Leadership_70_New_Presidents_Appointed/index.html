BJP Revamps UP Leadership: 70 New Presidents Appointed

2025-03-16T14:04:00+00:00

The BJP has appointed 70 new district unit presidents in Uttar Pradesh, including five women and 26 returning leaders. Vijay Maurya is the new Lucknow district president and Anand Dwivedi will lead the city unit. These appointments are part of the party's strategy to strengthen its presence across six regions in the state. Uttar Pradesh election in-charge Mahendra Nath Pandey said the appointments reflect strategic decisions in high-profile constituencies. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath and BJP state president Bhupendra Singh Chaudhary congratulated the new leaders. 28 more district unit leaders are expected to be announced soon.
