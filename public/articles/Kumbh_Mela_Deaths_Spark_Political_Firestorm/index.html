Kumbh Mela: Deaths Spark Political Firestorm

2025-02-28T15:36:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh Mela in Prayagraj, which concluded on February 26, saw three stampede incidents resulting in deaths. Mamata Banerjee criticized the BJP government for inadequate arrangements, calling it a "Mrityu Kumbh" (death Kumbh). Despite the government claiming over 66 crore devotees participated, videos of the incidents prompted Prime Minister Modi to apologize for any shortcomings. Modi also accused "a group of leaders" of mocking religion, while Yogi Adityanath called those criticizing the event "vultures". The BJP has used the Kumbh as a propaganda tool, elevating its significance beyond a religious event. Other political parties are struggling to counter this.
