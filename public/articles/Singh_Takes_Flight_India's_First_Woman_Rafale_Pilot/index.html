Singh Takes Flight: India's First Woman Rafale Pilot

2025-05-07T05:52:00+00:00

Lieutenant Shivangi Singh, 29, is India's first woman Rafale fighter pilot. She began dreaming of flying at age nine after visiting the Air Force Museum. Women were first allowed to join the Indian Air Force in 1993 and enter fighter pilot training in 2015. Singh is married to another fighter pilot and represents India's increasingly modernized military.
