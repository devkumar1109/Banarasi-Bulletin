Yogi's UP Budget: Prosperity Blooms, No New Taxes

2025-03-04T15:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath presented the 2025-26 budget, highlighting the state's fiscal health and inclusive development. He cited a boatman family earning Rs 30 crore during the Mahakumbh Mela, demonstrating economic prosperity. CM Yogi emphasized efficient fiscal management, strong tax receipts, and high capital expenditure, crediting the success to dedicated workers and Prime Minister Modi's leadership. The budget focuses on comprehensive development without introducing new taxes. He refuted allegations of exploitation of boatmen during the Mahakumbh, showcasing the event's positive economic impact.
