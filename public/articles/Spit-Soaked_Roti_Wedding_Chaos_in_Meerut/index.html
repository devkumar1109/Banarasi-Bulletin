Spit-Soaked Roti: Wedding Chaos in Meerut

2025-02-24T18:33:00+00:00

A viral video from a wedding in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, shows a person spitting on roti while making it. The incident occurred at Prem Mandap and sparked outrage on social media. Local police have detained the individual involved. The incident, which likely took place on February 21, has been confirmed as authentic by Superintendent of Police (city) Ayush Vikram Singh. Authorities promise further action.
