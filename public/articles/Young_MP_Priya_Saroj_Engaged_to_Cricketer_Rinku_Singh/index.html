Young MP Priya Saroj Engaged to Cricketer Rinku Singh

2025-06-07T12:18:00+00:00

Priya Saroj, a Supreme Court lawyer and Samajwadi Party MP, will be engaged to Indian cricketer Rinku Singh on June 8th in Lucknow. Saroj, representing the Machhlishahr constituency, is India's youngest female Parliamentarian at 25. She is the daughter of prominent SP leader Tufani Saroj and began her political involvement at a young age. The engagement ceremony will take place at a 7-star luxury venue. Saroj and Singh met through a mutual connection.
