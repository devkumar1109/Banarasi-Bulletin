Nishad Boasts of Violence for Political Power

2025-03-20T00:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Fisheries Minister and Nishad party chief Sanjay Nishad sparked controversy by claiming in a video that he used violence against seven inspectors to advance his political career. In the video, Nishad alleges he "broke the hands and legs of seven inspectors and threw them in a pit" to reach his current position. He also boasted about his influence, stating he can have District Magistrates and Superintendents of Police transferred within hours by informing the Chief Minister. The UP Congress Committee has demanded legal action against Nishad for his alleged confession of criminal activity.
