Varanasi Woman Alleges Seven-Day Gang Rape

2025-04-07T07:22:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi alleged she was gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned home on April 4, reporting the incident to her family. Police filed an FIR based on her mother's complaint, charging 13 named individuals and 11 unidentified persons under various sections of the BNS Act. Six accused have been arrested, and authorities are working to apprehend the remaining suspects. The woman's account details multiple instances of rape and assault at various locations, including cafes, hotels, and a warehouse, involving different groups of men.
