UP Digitizes Industry: Database for Transparency and Growth

2025-03-31T11:22:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is creating a comprehensive database of major industrial authorities to boost industrial investment and transparency. Led by Invest UP, the database will provide real-time information on vacant and allotted industrial plots, monitor industrial development progress, and ensure proper land use. Key authorities included in the database are UPSIDA, UPEIDA, YEIDA, Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority, NOIDA, GIDA, and SIDA. Skilled survey agencies will map plot occupancy, track industrial unit progress, and assess vacant plots to facilitate data-driven industrial planning. Invest UP will oversee the process to ensure transparent land allocation.
