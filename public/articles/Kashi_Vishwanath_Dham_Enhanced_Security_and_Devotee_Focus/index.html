Kashi Vishwanath Dham: Enhanced Security and Devotee Focus

2025-04-20T18:49:00+00:00

To enhance security at Kashi Vishwanath Dham, a Rapid-Response Unit (RRU) will be deployed and an anti-drone system will be implemented. Security personnel will be trained in using anti-drone technology and crowd regulation, emphasizing a 'No Touch Policy' especially when interacting with women. Commissioner Mohit Agarwal stressed the need to combat chain snatching and pickpocketing, as well as improve staff behavior and cooperation with devotees. Priority arrangements for differently-abled, elderly, and women are also being considered. Selfies with celebrities while on duty are strictly prohibited.
