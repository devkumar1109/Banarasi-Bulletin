UP Unveils Tourism Treasures in Bangkok

2025-06-07T06:29:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will promote the state's tourism and investment potential at the PATA Travel Mart in Bangkok from August 26 to 28. The UP Tourism Department will showcase "cultural, spiritual, and historical richness" with key destinations like Sarnath, Kushinagar, Varanasi, and Ayodhya. An interactive pavilion will feature dynamic visuals and Augmented Reality experiences. The government will also host a roadshow and run tourism advertisements on major Thai television, radio, and digital platforms. Traditional crafts and temple architecture will be highlighted, promoting the Center's "One District One Product" scheme.
