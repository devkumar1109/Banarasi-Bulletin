UP Unveils Tourism Treasures in Bangkok

2025-06-07T06:29:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will showcase the state's tourism potential at the Pacific Asia Travel Association (PATA) Travel Mart in Bangkok from August 26 to 28. The UP Tourism Department will highlight the state's cultural, spiritual, and historical richness, focusing on destinations like Sarnath, Kushinagar, Varanasi, Ayodhya, and the Prayagraj Mahakumbh site. The pavilion will feature an interactive navigation panel, an Augmented Reality feature, and displays of traditional crafts. Besides the exhibition, the government will host a roadshow and run tourism advertisements on Thai television, radio, and in prominent locations across Bangkok.
