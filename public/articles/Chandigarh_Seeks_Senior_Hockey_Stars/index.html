Chandigarh Seeks Senior Hockey Stars

2025-03-16T21:45:00+00:00

Hockey Chandigarh will hold trials on March 17 at 2 pm at the Sector 42 Sports Complex to select the men's senior team. The selected players will compete in the 15th Hockey India Senior National Championship in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, from April 4 to 15. Trials are open to players registered with the local association.
