Red Cross Brings Health to Gorakhpur Jail

2025-02-23T18:32:00+00:00

The Indian Red Cross Society, Gorakhpur branch, organized a health camp at the Gorakhpur district jail on February 24th, 2025. The camp, led by chairman Shivendra Vikram Singh, offered various services including skin disease screening, medication distribution, gynecological check-ups, and eye examinations. A team of doctors from Raji Hospital provided glasses to 30 inmates with vision problems. A total of 120 inmates underwent skin disease screenings, 110 received medicines, and 133 had their eyesight examined. Jail superintendent J.K. Pandey lauded the initiative and pledged continued support for future health programs.
