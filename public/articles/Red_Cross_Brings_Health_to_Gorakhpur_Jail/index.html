Red Cross Brings Health to Gorakhpur Jail

2025-02-23T18:32:00+00:00

The Indian Red Cross Society, Gorakhpur branch, organized a health camp at Gorakhpur district jail on Sunday. The camp, led by chairman Shivendra Vikram Singh, provided health check-ups and medicine distribution for inmates. 120 inmates underwent skin disease screening, 110 received medicines, and 133 had their eyesight examined with 30 receiving glasses. Gynecologist Dr Pooja Shukla conducted health check-ups for female inmates. Jail superintendent J.K. Pandey lauded the initiative and assured continued support for such programs.
