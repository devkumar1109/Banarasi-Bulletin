Arsenic Threat: 29 UP Districts Face Water Danger

2025-03-27T10:13:00+00:00

Arsenic levels in groundwater have exceeded safe limits in 29 districts across Uttar Pradesh, according to the government. Though arsenic is naturally occurring, it can be toxic when it leaches into groundwater. Drinking water with arsenic levels above 0.01 mg/liter can lead to health problems like skin lesions, cancer, cardiovascular disease, and developmental issues in children. The 29 districts affected include Saharanpur, Raebareli, Gorakhpur, Aligarh, Azamgarh, Ayodhya, Mirzapur, and Sambhal.
