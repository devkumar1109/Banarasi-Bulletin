Hindon Soars: New Varanasi, Patna Flights Take Off

2025-04-15T04:37:00+00:00

Air India Express will launch daily flights from Hindon civil terminal to Varanasi and Patna on May 1st. The flights will be operated by Airbus A320 aircraft with a capacity of 180 passengers. Bookings are open, with fares starting at Rs 3,384 for Hindon to Varanasi and Rs 5,573 for Patna to Ghaziabad. Hindon airport has seen increased passenger traffic since becoming Delhi-NCR's second airport in March. The terminal is well-connected via road and metro, making it a convenient option for travelers from surrounding areas. The increase in flights has led to a surge in demand, prompting work on revamping a 1.5km road leading to the terminal.
