Varanasi Runs for Clean Ganga, Celebrates Water Life

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon called "Run for Clean Ganga" and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw 6km participation from residents and foreigners, raising awareness about Ganga purification. Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal won the men's and women's categories respectively. The forest department, in collaboration with NGO Dolphin Friends, organized an event at Dhakwa Ghat to mark World Water Day and Earth Hour. A dolphin safari was inaugurated to educate the public about dolphin conservation efforts.
