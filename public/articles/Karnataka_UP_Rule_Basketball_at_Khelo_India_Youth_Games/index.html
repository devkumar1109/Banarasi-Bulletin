Karnataka, UP Rule Basketball at Khelo India Youth Games

2025-05-15T16:10:00+00:00

Karnataka girls and Uttar Pradesh boys emerged victorious in the basketball competition of the Khelo India Youth Games 2025 held in Patna. Karnataka girls dominated Haryana with a score of 86-61, led by Nidhi Umesh's 27 points and Mahek Sharma's 21 rebounds. Uttar Pradesh boys secured a 68-57 win against Rajasthan in a closely contested match. Key players for Uttar Pradesh were Sanjay Mahakumbh, Anuj Divakar, and Jatin, who contributed significantly to their victory. Tamil Nadu clinched the bronze medal in the boys' category by defeating Delhi 90-69.
