Fake Cash Kingpin Busted in Ghazipur

2025-05-09T15:14:00+00:00

In Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh, a man named Sanjay Verma was arrested for possessing counterfeit currency worth Rs 15,500. Police recovered 31 fake Rs 500 notes, a motorcycle, and a mobile phone during the arrest. The Karanda police, led by Sachin Singh, apprehended Verma as part of a crime prevention campaign. A case was registered against him under sections 179 and 180 of the BNS.
