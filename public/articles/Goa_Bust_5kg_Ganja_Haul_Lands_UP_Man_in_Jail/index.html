Goa Bust: 5kg Ganja Haul Lands UP Man in Jail

2025-04-01T23:57:00+00:00

Sonu Yadav, a 29-year-old resident of Uttar Pradesh, was arrested in Siolim, Goa on April 1, 2025, for possession of 5,010 grams of suspected Ganja worth approximately Rs. 5 lakh. The narcotics were found on a scooter Yadav was riding during a police raid near the Primary Health Centre. An FIR (No. 57/2025) was registered under Section 20(b)(ii)(B) of the NDPS Act, 1985, at Anjuna Police Station. Yadav was arrested and remanded to five days of police custody. PSI Viraj Naik of Anjuna PS is investigating the case.
