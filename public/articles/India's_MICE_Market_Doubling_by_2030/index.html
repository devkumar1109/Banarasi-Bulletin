India's MICE Market: Doubling by 2030

2025-05-05T14:54:00+00:00

India's MICE (meetings, incentives, conferences, and events) market is projected to double in size by 2030, reaching $103.7 billion from $49.4 billion in 2024. This growth is driven by policy decisions, infrastructure modernization, and the development of new MICE destinations like Varanasi and Kochi. India's G20 presidency has also acted as a catalyst, showcasing the country's readiness to host international events. The meetings segment dominates the market with a 42.91% revenue share. MICE tourism benefits the economy by creating business opportunities, spreading knowledge, and improving skills. The government, both at the central and state levels, is actively promoting and supporting the growth of the MICE industry.
