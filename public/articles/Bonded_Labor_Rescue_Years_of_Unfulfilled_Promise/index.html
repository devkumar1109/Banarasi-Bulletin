Bonded Labor Rescue, Years of Unfulfilled Promise

2025-06-09T06:11:00+00:00

Manoj Kumar Anant and his wife Shakuntala Bai were rescued from bonded labor at a brick kiln in Uttar Pradesh in 2022. Although they applied for rehabilitation assistance under the Central Sector Scheme for Rehabilitation of Bonded Labour, 2021, Manoj is still waiting for the full support two years later. His left arm is paralyzed after an accident in March 2025. He emphasizes the critical need for assistance in the initial weeks after rescue.
