India Chokes: Pollution Crisis Cripples Economy

2025-03-28T19:00:00+00:00

India faces a growing economic crisis due to air pollution. Despite the urgency, nearly ₹858 crore allocated for pollution control in 2024-25 remains unspent. Delhi's 2025-26 budget allocates ₹300 crore for emergency pollution control and a new monitoring centre. Air pollution negatively impacts economic activity and workforce productivity. Addressing this requires a coordinated multi-sectoral response.
