UP Emerges as Healthcare Powerhouse

2025-03-18T06:09:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has become a healthcare hub with 80 operational medical colleges (44 government and 36 private) and a growing network of hospitals. In the past eight years, the state has significantly increased medical seats, established new super-specialty hospitals, and advanced AYUSH medical education. The number of MBBS and PG seats has risen, with 5,250 seats in government medical colleges and 6,550 in private undergraduate courses. Additionally, 350 new seats were created in three new medical colleges under the PPP model. The Yogi government has also focused on AYUSH medical education, establishing an Ayurvedic College in Ayodhya and a government homeopathic medical college in Varanasi. The state currently has 2,110 Ayurvedic, 254 Unani, and 1,585 Homeopathic hospitals.
