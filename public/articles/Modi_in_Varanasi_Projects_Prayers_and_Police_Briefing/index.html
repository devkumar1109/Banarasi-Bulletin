Modi in Varanasi: Projects, Prayers, and Police Briefing

2025-04-11T12:57:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi landed in Varanasi today, marking his 50th visit since becoming Prime Minister in 2014. During his visit, Modi will lay the foundation stone and inaugurate development projects worth over ₹3,880 crore. He will also address a public meeting and offer prayers at Guru Ji Maharaj Temple in Varanasi before traveling to Madhya Pradesh. Upon arrival, Modi received a briefing from Varanasi's Police Commissioner, Divisional Commissioner, and District Magistrate regarding a recent rape incident in the city.
