Head Injury, Agitation, and Tragedy in UP

2025-03-27T05:47:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man named Rajiv tragically killed his four children by slitting their throats before taking his own life by hanging. Family members stated that Rajiv had suffered a severe head injury in an accident a year prior. The injury led to him experiencing increased agitation and difficulty managing his emotions. The incident highlights the devastating consequences of untreated head injuries and the importance of mental health support.
