Tirupati Carrom: Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra Triumph

2025-03-08T12:26:00+00:00

The 27th All India Postal Carroms Tournament concluded in Tirupati on March 8th, 2025. Thirteen Postal Circle teams from across India participated in men's and women's categories. Tamil Nadu won the men's team championship, while Maharashtra topped the women's category. World Carrom Champion Komaravalli Srinivas and Member of the Postal Directorate Manju Kumar were present at the closing ceremony. Chief Postmaster General K Prakash praised the participants for their dedication and sportsmanship.
