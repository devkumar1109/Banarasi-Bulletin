UP Braces for Scorching Summer, Bundelkhand Most Vulnerable

2025-04-06T06:14:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is predicted to experience a severe summer with prolonged heat waves from April to June, particularly impacting the Bundelkhand region. Authorities have instructed district administrations to implement preventive measures and hospitals to prepare for an increase in heat-related illnesses. Meteorological officials warn that the extreme heat poses significant risks to human and animal health. The state government is taking steps to mitigate the effects of the heatwave.
