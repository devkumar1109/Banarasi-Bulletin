Varanasi, Bhadohi Police Crack Down on Negligence

2025-03-19T16:28:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Police Commissioner Mohit Agrawal suspended Chandmari and Lalpur outpost in-charges for negligence in investigations. He warned all outpost in-charges and police station heads about facing consequences if they don't improve performance. The commissioner emphasized the importance of timely investigations and citizen satisfaction, citing the Varanasi police commissionerate's seventh position in the state based on the Chief Minister Dashboard. In Bhadohi, Superintendent of Police Abhimanyu Manglik attached Bhadohi police station in-charge Ashwini Tripathi to the Police Lines for dereliction of duty. He replaced Tripathi with inspector Sachidanand Pandey and warned all police personnel to either perform or face consequences.
