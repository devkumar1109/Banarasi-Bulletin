Yogi Adityanath: Tax Transparency, UP's Growth Demands

2025-06-08T06:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath reviewed the State Tax Department, emphasizing transparency, technical efficiency, and strict enforcement in tax collection. He directed officials to crack down on shell companies and fake registered firms, calling tax evasion a national crime hindering development. During a meeting with the 16th Finance Commission, CM Yogi demanded a 50% share of central taxes for Uttar Pradesh, emphasizing the state's achievements over the past 8 years. He also addressed a state-level workshop commemorating 11 years of the Modi government, highlighting India's global standing and the importance of planning for future progress.
