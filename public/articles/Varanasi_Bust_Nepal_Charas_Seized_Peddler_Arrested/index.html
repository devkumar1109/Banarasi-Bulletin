Varanasi Bust: Nepal Charas Seized, Peddler Arrested

2025-03-12T19:02:00+00:00

In Varanasi, the anti-narcotics task force (ANTF) and Lanka police seized 1.534 kg of charas worth Rs 7.5 lakh. Alok Raj Chaubey (20) from Buxar, Bihar, was arrested for bringing the contraband from Nepal. During interrogation, Chaubey confessed to selling the narcotics to local peddlers for distribution.
