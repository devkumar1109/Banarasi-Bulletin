Cong Workers Arrested: Eviction, Harassment Allegations.

2025-06-07T10:41:00+00:00

Two local Congress workers in Uttar Pradesh, Anand Pandey and Amit Pathak, were arrested on charges of robbery, theft, and molestation. The arrests follow a complaint by Sachina Khatoon, who alleged harassment and forced eviction from her boutique. Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai claims the accusations are politically motivated and aim to tarnish his reputation. Rai stated that the workers have close ties to him. Khatoon, however, stated that Pandey and Pathak pressured her to vacate her shop after their landlord's death during the pandemic, despite her obtaining a stay order on eviction in May.
