Uttar Pradesh: Golden Years, Enhanced Care.

2025-04-04T01:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is improving facilities for senior citizens in old age homes. Under the Ayushman Bharat scheme, residents will receive enhanced healthcare. A new program will utilize the professional and business expertise of senior citizens, offering financial incentives to promote self-reliance. Currently, state-run old age homes provide free accommodation, meals, medical care, and entertainment. Over 1,500 senior citizens attended the recent Maha Kumbh, receiving temporary accommodations and medical camps. To address legal and family disputes, 216 reconciliation officers have been appointed at the tehsil level, and DM-led committees oversee old age home management and service improvements.
