Mahesh Kumar Tops NEET UG 2025, Results Out

2025-06-14T13:57:00+00:00

The NEET UG 2025 results are out, with Mahesh Kumar from Rajasthan securing All India Rank 1. Students can check their scores and qualified status on the official NTA website. According to NIRF 2024 rankings, the top 20 medical colleges are: AIIMS Delhi, PGIMER Chandigarh, Christian Medical College Vellore, NIMHANS Bangalore, JIPMER Puducherry, SGPIMS Lucknow, BHU Varanasi, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore, Kasturba Medical College Manipal, Madras Medical College Chennai, Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Saveetha Institute, Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute, AIIMS Rishikesh, AIIMS Bhubaneswar, AIIMS Jodhpur, Vardhman Mahavir Medical College, S.R.M. Institute, King George`s Medical University, and Sri Ramachandra Institute. Qualifying percentile for NEET UG 2025 will be similar to last year, with 50th percentile for general category and 40th percentile for OBC, SC, and ST categories. Counselling dates will be released soon.
