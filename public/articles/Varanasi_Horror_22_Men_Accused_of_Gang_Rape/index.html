Varanasi Horror: 22 Men Accused of Gang Rape

2025-04-07T15:28:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, alleges she was kidnapped and gang-raped by 22 men over several days in March. She went missing on March 29 and was found drugged and abandoned on April 4. After returning home, she disclosed the assault to her father, leading to a police complaint filed on April 6. Six suspects have been arrested, while others remain at large. Police are investigating the case, which includes alleged assaults at multiple locations, including a hookah bar and a hotel. Authorities are proceeding cautiously due to the potential involvement of minors.
