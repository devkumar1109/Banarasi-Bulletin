Ghaziabad: India's Richest District Now

2025-06-10T06:27:00+00:00

Ghaziabad district in Uttar Pradesh is now India's richest district with only 7% of its population living below the poverty line, according to Niti Aayog's 2021 report. This is a significant drop from the 17% poverty rate recorded in 2016. Niti Aayog assessed poverty levels based on factors like nutrition, maternal and child health, birth and death rates, school attendance, and property ownership. Ghaziabad's per capita income is estimated at Rs. 95,216. Despite being a business and engineering hub, Ghaziabad's transformation into a wealthier district surpasses even Noida, Lucknow, and Kanpur.
