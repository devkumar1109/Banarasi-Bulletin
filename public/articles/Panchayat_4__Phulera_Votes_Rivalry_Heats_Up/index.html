Panchayat 4: Phulera Votes, Rivalry Heats Up

2025-05-04T07:03:00+00:00

Amazon Prime Video's popular web series "Panchayat" is returning for its fourth season on July 2, 2025. The teaser, released at Waves 2025, highlights the upcoming election in Phulera and the intensifying rivalry between Pradhan and Bhushan. Season 3 ended with tensions between the Pradhan and MLA escalating, culminating in a shooting incident involving Raghubir Yadav's character. The new season is expected to explore the aftermath of these events, the mending of relationships, and the heated election campaigns. Directed by Deepak Kumar Yadav, "Panchayat 4" brings back the beloved cast, including Neena Gupta, Sunita Rajwar, Jitendra Kumar, and Raghubir Yadav.
