Blue Drum Gift Sparks Law and Order Debate

2025-04-02T07:48:00+00:00

A Samajwadi Party spokesperson gifted a blue drum to Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak, referencing the recent Meerut murder case where a woman dismembered her husband's body and hid it in a blue drum filled with cement. The incident sparked a political debate, with the SP leader criticizing the state's law and order situation. Muskan Rastogi, the wife of the murdered man, and her lover Sahil Shukla, are in jail and have confessed to the crime. The case has significantly impacted the sale of blue drums in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, turning the once common hardware item into a symbol of terror.
