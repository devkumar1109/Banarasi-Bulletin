INDIA Bloc to Fight UP Polls Together in 2027

2025-06-18T11:29:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav stated that the INDIA bloc will contest the 2027 Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections together. Yadav affirmed the alliance's unity, saying, "INDIA bloc is intact and we are firmly with it." He made this announcement to The Hindu.
