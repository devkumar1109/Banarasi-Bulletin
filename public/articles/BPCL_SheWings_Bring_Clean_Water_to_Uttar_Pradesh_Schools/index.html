BPCL, SheWings Bring Clean Water to Uttar Pradesh Schools

2025-04-10T03:33:00+00:00

BPCL and SheWings Foundation launched the "Swachh Jal, Swasth Kal" initiative to install water purification systems in 35 government schools in Gautam Buddha Nagar, Uttar Pradesh. This project aims to provide clean and safe drinking water to schoolchildren. District Magistrate Manish Kumar Verma and Basic Shiksha Adhikari Rahul Pawar supported the initiative. SheWings Foundation Founder Madan Mohit Bhardwaj emphasized the importance of clean water for children's health, while BPCL General Manager Raman Mallik highlighted the company's commitment to building a healthier India through its CSR program. Dr. Praveen Kumar, a public health expert, stressed the health risks associated with contaminated water, particularly for children.
