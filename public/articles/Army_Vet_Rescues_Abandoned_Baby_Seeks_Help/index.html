Army Vet Rescues Abandoned Baby, Seeks Help

2025-04-21T07:25:00+00:00

Khushboo Patani, elder sister of actress Disha Patani and a former lieutenant in the Indian Army, rescued an abandoned baby girl near her Bareilly residence. Khushboo, who is now a fitness coach and entrepreneurial counsellor, shared a video on Instagram documenting the rescue, prompting praise for her bravery and compassion. She appealed to authorities, including Bareilly Police, UP Police, and UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, to ensure the baby's safety and well-being. Khushboo, who is originally from Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, holds a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from DIT School of Engineering. She gained attention on social media for her pictures in her military uniform.
