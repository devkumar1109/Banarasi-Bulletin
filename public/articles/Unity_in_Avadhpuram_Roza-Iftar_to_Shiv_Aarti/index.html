Unity in Avadhpuram: Roza-Iftar to Shiv Aarti

2025-03-17T13:40:00+00:00

A small neighborhood in Avadhpuram, Uttar Pradesh, exemplifies communal harmony through shared religious events. Residents gathered at Shiv Vatika park for a roza-iftar, attended by both Hindus and Muslims, followed by namaz and a Shiv aarti. The Shiv Vatika name was even suggested by a Muslim local, highlighting mutual respect during the temple's construction. This unity showcases the essence of 'Ganga Jamuna tehzeeb' and peace despite recent tensions. Community leaders emphasize respect for all religious beliefs.
