Vaish Rolls to North Zone Bowling Title

2025-06-12T20:28:00+00:00

Sumit Vaish from Uttar Pradesh won the 1st North Zone Tenpin Bowling Ranking Tournament, defeating Dhruv Sarda from Delhi by 49 pinfall. The tournament, organized by the Uttar Pradesh Tenpin Bowling Association, took place at Funtura Bowling Center, Lucknow. Vaish scored 431-382 in the final, overcoming Sarda's initial lead in the first game (205-193). R Kannan, vice-president of the Tenpin Bowling Federation of India, was the chief guest at the closing ceremony.
