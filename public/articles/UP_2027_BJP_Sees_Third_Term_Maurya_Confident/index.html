UP 2027: BJP Sees Third Term, Maurya Confident

2025-03-25T19:02:00+00:00

Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya predicts a third consecutive term for the BJP in Uttar Pradesh in 2027. He attributes this success to the Yogi Adityanath government's achievements in good governance, security, and development, citing the state's transformation from a "sick state" plagued by crime to a "crime-free and developed state." Maurya highlights the implementation of PM schemes for various sectors and projects like Kashi Vishwanath Dham, showcasing the government's commitment to growth and progress. He believes the opposition, even if united, cannot defeat the BJP due to the public's faith in the incumbent government.
