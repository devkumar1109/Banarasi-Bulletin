Red Saree Saves 150 Train Passengers

2025-04-02T01:12:00+00:00

A 70-year-old woman named Omvati Devi from Uttar Pradesh, India saved 150 train passengers by using her red saree to warn the driver of a broken railway track. Omvati Devi noticed the damaged track and, remembering that red signals danger, tied her saree to the broken section and waved it in front of the approaching train. The driver saw the red fabric and stopped the train in time, preventing a potential disaster.
