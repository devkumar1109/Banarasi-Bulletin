IIT Kharagpur: Suicide Probe Seeks System Overhaul

2025-05-03T00:24:00+00:00

An expert committee has been formed to investigate the recent student suicides at IIT Kharagpur. The committee, headed by a former IIT professor, will advise on potential improvements to the IIT system. They will examine various factors, including academic pressure, mental health support, and the overall environment at the institute. The committee's findings and recommendations will be crucial in addressing the concerns raised by these tragic incidents and preventing future occurrences.
