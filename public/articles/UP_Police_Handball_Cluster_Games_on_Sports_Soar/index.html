UP Police Handball Cluster: Games on, Sports Soar

2025-04-08T00:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-2025 in Lucknow. The event features 75 teams and 1,341 players, including 336 women, representing central forces and state police departments. Over the past five years, Uttar Pradesh Police has participated in over 500 international competitions, with medal-winning players being recruited into the force. To boost grassroots sports, over 65,000 men and women in Mangal Dal have received sports kits. The state has invested heavily in sports infrastructure, developing 84 stadiums, 67 multipurpose halls, and various specialized facilities. There are also 44 sports hostels across 18 districts housing 740 players in 16 sports. Three sports colleges in Gorakhpur, Lucknow, and Etawah offer world-class infrastructure and skilled trainers.
