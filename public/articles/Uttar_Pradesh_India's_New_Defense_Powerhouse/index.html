Uttar Pradesh: India's New Defense Powerhouse

2025-05-13T05:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is emerging as a major defense manufacturing hub in India. A BrahMos unit has been inaugurated in the state, signifying its growing role in the sector. The inauguration is expected to be followed by the establishment of facilities for drones, radars, and missiles, further solidifying Uttar Pradesh's position as a key player in India's defense manufacturing landscape.
