Pakistani Spy Snared in Varanasi

2025-05-22T18:17:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested a Pakistani spy, identified as Tufail, in Varanasi. Tufail is accused of sending sensitive intelligence information to his handlers in Pakistan. The ATS had been tracking Tufail for some time before his arrest. The investigation is ongoing to determine the nature of the information transmitted, the methods used by Tufail, and whether he was part of a larger espionage network. Authorities are also working to assess the damage caused by Tufail's alleged activities. Security experts have praised the ATS's swift action in apprehending Tufail.
