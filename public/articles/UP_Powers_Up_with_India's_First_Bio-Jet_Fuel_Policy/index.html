UP Powers Up with India's First Bio-Jet Fuel Policy

2025-06-05T10:20:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched India's first bio-jet fuel policy, aiming to convert agricultural residues into fuel. This will benefit 2.5 crore farmers by creating a market for their waste products like sugarcane bagasse and paddy husk. The policy offers incentives like capital subsidies, GST reimbursement, and land fee waivers. Over 18 companies are planning to invest ₹3,000 crore in the state's bio-jet fuel sector. The initiative aligns with global decarbonization goals and will contribute to India's climate objectives while boosting energy security and creating green jobs.
