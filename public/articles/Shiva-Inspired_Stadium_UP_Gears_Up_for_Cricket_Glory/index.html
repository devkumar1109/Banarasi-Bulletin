Shiva-Inspired Stadium: UP Gears Up for Cricket Glory

2025-03-12T16:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the progress of Varanasi's international cricket stadium, inspired by Lord Shiva. The stadium, the state's third international venue, is expected to host international matches. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone in 2023. The design features crescent-shaped roof covers, trident-shaped floodlights, and ghat-inspired seating. The Uttar Pradesh government allocated Rs 121 crore for land acquisition, while the BCCI contributed Rs 330 crore for construction. The stadium will accommodate over 30,000 spectators.
