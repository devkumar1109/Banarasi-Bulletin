Faculty Get Tech Makeover: Industry Immersions Bridge the Gap

2025-05-17T21:31:00+00:00

In a new trend, engineering college faculty across Andhra Pradesh are being offered internships and industry immersion programs by IT and core companies. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands in rapidly evolving technology fields. The DeepTech Naipunya Foundation launched a program for faculty members at the Tech Mahindra campus in Visakhapatnam, focusing on skills like API development, AI, and cybersecurity. The program, which involves free boot camps led by industry experts, hopes to equip educators with cutting-edge industry knowledge. To facilitate this, some companies are encouraging institutions to hire additional faculty members, allowing existing faculty to undergo training while maintaining academic responsibilities.
