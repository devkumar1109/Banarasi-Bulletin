College Manager Arrested for Student Rape

2025-03-17T18:48:00+00:00

Janardan Yadav, the manager of an intermediate college in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested for raping a Class 10 girl. The incident occurred on March 1st during the UP Board examination when the girl went to her exam center at Yadav's college. Yadav allegedly took the girl to a separate room, raped her, and threatened her with dire consequences if she disclosed the incident. The girl's uncle filed a complaint with the police after she narrated her ordeal. Yadav was arrested from a brick kiln where he was hiding and sent to jail.
