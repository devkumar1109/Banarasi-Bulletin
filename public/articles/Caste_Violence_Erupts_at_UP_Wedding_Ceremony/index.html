Caste Violence Erupts at UP Wedding Ceremony

2025-06-01T05:48:00+00:00

A Dalit family in Uttar Pradesh's Rasra was attacked by a group of men for holding a wedding ceremony in a marriage hall. The attack, which occurred on Friday night, left two individuals seriously injured. The accused allegedly used caste slurs, objecting to the Dalit community using a formal venue for the wedding. An FIR has been registered under relevant sections of the BNS and the SC/ST Act, but no arrests have been made yet.
