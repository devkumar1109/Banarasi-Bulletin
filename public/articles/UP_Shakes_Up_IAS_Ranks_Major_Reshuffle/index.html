UP Shakes Up IAS Ranks: Major Reshuffle

2025-04-22T07:11:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has transferred 33 IAS officers in a major reshuffle. Director Information Shishir has been appointed as special secretary MSME, export promotion, and CEO of the Khadi board. Varanasi Divisional Commissioner Kaushal Raj Sharma takes over as the new secretary to the chief minister.
