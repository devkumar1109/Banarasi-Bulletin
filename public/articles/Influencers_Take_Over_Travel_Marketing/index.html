Influencers Take Over Travel Marketing

2025-04-18T00:00:00+00:00

Tourism boards globally are leveraging influencers to attract travelers. The strategy follows a surge in domestic tourism in India, which rose 45% in 2023 to 2,509.63 million visitors compared to 2022. Destinations like Dubai, Bastar, Saudi Arabia, Japan, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, and Bihar are offering free familiarization trips to influencers, sometimes with additional compensation, to generate interest and conversions.
