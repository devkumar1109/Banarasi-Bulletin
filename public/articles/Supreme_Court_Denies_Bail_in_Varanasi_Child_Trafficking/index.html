Supreme Court Denies Bail in Varanasi Child Trafficking

2025-04-15T16:10:00+00:00

The Supreme Court cancelled bail for 13 accused in three Varanasi child trafficking cases, emphasizing the seriousness of the crime and the need for societal justice. The court criticized the Uttar Pradesh government for its lack of action and failure to challenge the high court's bail orders. The bench noted the urgency for societal balance and protection of human rights, stating that the "cry of the collective for justice cannot be trivialised."
