Wife, Son-in-Law Plot Husband's Murder in UP

2025-03-29T19:22:00+00:00

In Sonbhadra, Uttar Pradesh, Sunita Devi was arrested for plotting her husband Ram Naresh Yadav's murder along with six others, including her son-in-law Alok Yadav and five hired killers. The murder took place on January 25th, with Sunita allegedly paying Rs. 50,000 through Alok Yadav to the killers - Sandeep Kumar (a minor), Ankit Kumar, and Mayank Kol. Police recovered the murder weapon, the deceased's mobile phone, the motorcycle used in the crime, and Rs. 15,000. The motive behind the murder was Sunita's frustration with Ram Naresh's alleged affair and harassment of her and their children.
