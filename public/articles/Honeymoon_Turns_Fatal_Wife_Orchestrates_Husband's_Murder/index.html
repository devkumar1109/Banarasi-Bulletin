Honeymoon Turns Fatal: Wife Orchestrates Husband's Murder

2025-06-09T02:42:00+00:00

Raja Raghuvanshi, an Indore tourist, was allegedly murdered during his honeymoon in Meghalaya. Police investigations revealed his wife, Sonam, orchestrated the crime by hiring assailants. Sonam surrendered to Uttar Pradesh police, while three suspects were arrested in connection to the murder. The arrested men confessed to being hired by Sonam to kill Raghuvanshi. Meghalaya police are continuing their operation to apprehend more individuals involved in the crime.
