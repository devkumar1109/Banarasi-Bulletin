Varanasi: India's Kaleidoscope Calls Tourists Back

2025-03-17T15:00:00+00:00

Varanasi, India's oldest city, attracts tourists from around the world, including Germany and England. Visitors praise its vibrant culture and unique atmosphere, comparing it to Thailand and Leicester Square at Christmas. Despite the bustling streets and crowds, especially during Holi, tourists appreciate the city's beauty, colors, and the sense of calmness amongst the chaos. Many visitors express a desire to return to India, having also explored Rajasthan and Agra. They highlight the warmth and hospitality of the people, finding Varanasi a memorable and captivating experience.
