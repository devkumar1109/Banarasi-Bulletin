Holi Dazzles India: Colors, Unity, and Global Festivities

2025-03-14T06:24:00+00:00

Holi, the festival of colors, is being celebrated with enthusiasm across India. In Varanasi, it has become a popular cultural experience for foreign tourists, who gather at Mint House to participate in the festivities. Indian leaders, including Prime Minister Narendra Modi, President Droupadi Murmu, and Defence Minister Rajnath Singh, extended their warm wishes for Holi, emphasizing its significance in promoting unity, love, and harmony. The festival began on Thursday with Chhoti Holi, and people are celebrating with colors, music, and traditional festivities. Holi symbolizes the triumph of good over evil.
