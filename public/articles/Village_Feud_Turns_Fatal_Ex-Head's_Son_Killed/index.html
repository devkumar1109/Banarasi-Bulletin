Village Feud Turns Fatal: Ex-Head's Son Killed

2025-04-06T10:34:00+00:00

A 26-year-old man, Rajan Awasthi, died during treatment after being shot allegedly due to political rivalry in Bahraich, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on Friday when family members of the current village head, Seema Awasthi, opened fire on Rajan, son of a former village head. One accused, Mrityunjay Awasthi, was arrested after falling from his motorcycle and a pistol was recovered from him. An FIR was registered against three people, including Seema's husband and Shivam Awasthi. Tensions ran high in the village following Rajan's death, but police have deployed teams to maintain law and order. BJP MLA Sureshwar Singh postponed a bike rally scheduled for Sunday in view of the incident.
