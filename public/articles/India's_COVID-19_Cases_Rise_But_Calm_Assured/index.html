India's COVID-19 Cases Rise, But Calm Assured

2025-05-31T15:51:00+00:00

India's active Covid-19 cases have surpassed 3,000, with four deaths reported across Delhi, Karnataka, Kerala, and Uttar Pradesh. Kerala leads with 1,336 active cases, followed by Maharashtra with 467. Delhi has reported 375 new cases. Most cases are mild, and officials have stressed there is no need for panic. They assure adequate testing and treatment infrastructure is available. Kerala reported 189 fresh cases, while Delhi recorded its first Covid-19 death, a 60-year-old woman. Karnataka urged calm and vigilance, while Odisha reported two new cases, bringing the total to seven. Bihar's Patna saw seven new infections, including a medical intern. Maharashtra issued an advisory to hospitals.
