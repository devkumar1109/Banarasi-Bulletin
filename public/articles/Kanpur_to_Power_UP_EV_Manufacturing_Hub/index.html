Kanpur to Power UP EV Manufacturing Hub

2025-06-17T02:36:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will invest ₹700 crore to develop an electric vehicle (EV) manufacturing hub in Kanpur. The 500-acre park, located near Bhimsen, will be implemented under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. The hub will feature units for manufacturing EV components like electric motors, chassis, steel components, lithium-ion cells, batteries, chargers, and controllers. A dedicated R&D center will focus on innovation and advanced EV technologies. An integrated EV components cluster will support SMEs, startups, and local entrepreneurs. The park's proximity to the Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) will facilitate efficient logistics.
