Holi Rush: NER Varanasi Gears Up For Passenger Surge

2025-03-11T07:51:00+00:00

The Varanasi division of North Eastern Railway is preparing for the Holi rush by implementing special measures from March 15 to 18. They are operating special trains and have deployed officials at key stations like Chhapra, Siwan, Ballia, Mau, and Deoria Sadar to manage crowds. Control rooms are monitoring operations, and helpdesks are available 24 hours at major stations to assist passengers. Security forces are managing crowds at platforms, gates, and ticket windows to ensure smooth operations. Platform changes are minimized, and passengers are notified in advance if changes are unavoidable.
