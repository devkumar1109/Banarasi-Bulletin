UP Leads with Real-Time Disease Surveillance

2025-05-01T18:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has become the first state to implement real-time digital surveillance of vaccine-preventable diseases (VPDs). The Unified Disease Surveillance Portal (UDSP), a state-owned platform launched in May 2023, will facilitate real-time case reporting and accurate data collection for six VPDs: poliomyelitis, measles, rubella, diphtheria, pertussis, and tetanus. This initiative, supported by the World Health Organisation's National Public Health Support Network (NPSN), aims to improve public health response, timely disease identification, and vaccination coverage. The UDSP also allows citizens to access their lab reports online, similar to Covid-19 reports.
