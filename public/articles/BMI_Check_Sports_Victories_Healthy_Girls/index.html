BMI Check, Sports Victories, Healthy Girls

2025-03-11T19:42:00+00:00

Governor Anandiben Patel suggested incorporating the Body Mass Index (BMI) into schools to assess children's health. She emphasized the importance of daily exercise, citing India's victory in the ICC Champion Trophy as an example. Patel encouraged girls to participate in sports for physical fitness and to benefit future generations. She instructed officials to promote regular medical check-ups for girls in primary education. The Governor attended the district-level PM Shri Annual Sports and Cultural Competition in Varanasi, where Composite School, Pindra won first place. Shubham Patel and Seel Yadav secured first place in the men's and women's 100-meter races respectively. Parma Vishwas won the district-level Mission Shakti competition.
