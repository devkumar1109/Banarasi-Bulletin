Swaraj: Strong India, "One Nation, One Election"

2025-04-24T18:04:00+00:00

BJP MP Bansuri Swaraj condemned the Pahalgam terror attack, stating that Modi's "new India" is fearless and won't be defeated by terrorism. She emphasized India's determination to confront challenges and make decisions in national interest. Swaraj spoke in Varanasi about "One Nation, One Election," highlighting benefits like reduced election expenditure, less burden on administration and security forces, consistent long-term policy decisions, decreased voter confusion, and increased voter turnout. She urged women to support the policy and become flagbearers of the ideology.
