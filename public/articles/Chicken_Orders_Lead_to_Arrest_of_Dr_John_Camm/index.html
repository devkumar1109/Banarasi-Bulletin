Chicken Orders Lead to Arrest of "Dr. John Camm"

2025-04-10T10:48:00+00:00

Dr. Narendra Vikramaditya Yadav, alias Dr. John Camm, was arrested in Prayagraj for allegedly performing unauthorized cardiac procedures at Mission Hospital in Damoh, Madhya Pradesh, resulting in seven patient deaths. After fleeing Damoh, police traced Camm through his frequent chicken orders with a seller in Prayagraj. He used WhatsApp to share his location for deliveries. Camm admitted to using the alias "John Camm" to avoid racial bias while working in the UK and continued using it in India.
