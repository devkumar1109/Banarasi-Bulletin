Seven Underrated Bollywood Gems

2025-02-24T14:08:00+00:00

This article lists seven underrated Bollywood movies. "Ankhon Dekhi" (2013) follows a man who chooses to believe only what he sees. "Nil Battey Sannata" (2015) is a heartwarming story about a mother and daughter overcoming educational obstacles. "Masaan" (2015) explores love, loss, and redemption in Varanasi. "Titli" (2015) depicts a young man's struggle to escape his family's criminal life. "Filmistaan" (2014) is a comedy-drama about an Indian man mistaken for a fighter in Pakistan. "Barah Aana" (2009) is a dark comedy-drama about the struggles of Mumbai's lower middle class. Finally, "Kadvi Hawa" (2017) focuses on climate change and its impact on farmers.
