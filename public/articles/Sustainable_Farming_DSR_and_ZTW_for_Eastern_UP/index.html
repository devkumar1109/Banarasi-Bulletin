Sustainable Farming: DSR and ZTW for Eastern UP

2025-03-26T12:15:00+00:00

A two-day training on Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) and Zero Tillage Wheat (ZTW) was held at ISARC, Varanasi, for 70 farmers from Eastern Uttar Pradesh. The event aimed to promote sustainable rice-wheat cultivation techniques and featured expert discussions, technology showcases, and field demonstrations. ISARC Director Dr. Sudhanshu Singh inaugurated the training alongside experts from organizations like IRRI, World Bank, Bayer Crop Science, and Savannah. They emphasized the need for agricultural transformation in Eastern Uttar Pradesh. Farmers were introduced to the benefits of DSR and ZTW, including reduced labor costs, water savings, and improved productivity.
