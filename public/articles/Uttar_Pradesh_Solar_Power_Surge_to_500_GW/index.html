Uttar Pradesh: Solar Power Surge to 500 GW

2025-04-30T04:33:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has signed MoUs with the Smart Energy Council of Australia and the Hinduja Group to boost solar energy production. The government aims to generate 500 gigawatts (GW) of solar energy by 2030, transforming regions like Bundelkhand and Vindhya into solar hubs. The Solar Mitra Yojana has been launched to build a skilled workforce for the solar sector. Since 2017, solar energy production in Uttar Pradesh has increased from 288 MW to over 2,800 MW. The state's Solar Energy Policy 2022 sets a near-term target of 2,200 MW, with solar parks planned across municipal corporations and solar grids along expressways and railway tracks.
