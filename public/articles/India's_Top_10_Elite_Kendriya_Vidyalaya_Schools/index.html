India's Top 10 Elite Kendriya Vidyalaya Schools

2025-04-02T00:00:00+00:00

This article lists the top 10 famous Kendriya Vidyalaya schools in India. PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya IIT Powai, Mumbai is recognized as a top school in the city. Kendriya Vidyalaya IIT Chennai, established in 1972, is highly sought after. PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya NMR JNU, New Delhi, since 1981, is considered one of India's finest government schools. PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 2 Delhi Cantt boasts a 7-acre campus. Kendriya Vidyalaya Sector 8, R.K. Puram, New Delhi, excels in holistic development despite its modest size. Kendriya Vidyalaya IVRI, Bareilly, emphasizes academics, sports, and moral values. PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya Ganeshkhind, Pune, focuses on both academics and co-curricular activities. Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 4 AFS, Gwalior, is one of India's earliest Kendriya Vidyalayas. PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya, Ernakulam, established in 1976, has grown significantly with excellent facilities. Kendriya Vidyalaya CECRI, Karaikudi, rounds out the top 10.
