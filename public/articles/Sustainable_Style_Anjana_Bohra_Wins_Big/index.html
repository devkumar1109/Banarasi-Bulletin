Sustainable Style: Anjana Bohra Wins Big

2025-03-01T10:21:00+00:00

Ankita Bohra, founder of Anjana Bohra, is receiving recognition at the Womenpreneur Awards 2025 for her sustainable and inclusive fashion brand. Launched in 2021, Anjana Bohra employs 50 people and supports over 500 artisans across India, focusing on traditional Indian textiles like Bandhani, Banarasi, and Ajrakh. The brand's "Best out of Waste" initiative transforms leftover fabrics into accessories, minimizing waste. Anjana Bohra is known for merging sustainability with luxury, achieving 50% year-on-year revenue growth and 15% profit after tax. The brand collaborates with underrepresented artisan communities, preserving traditional crafts and providing sustainable livelihoods. Anjana Bohra embraces inclusivity in its designs and campaigns, featuring diverse models and offering custom and adaptive fits.
