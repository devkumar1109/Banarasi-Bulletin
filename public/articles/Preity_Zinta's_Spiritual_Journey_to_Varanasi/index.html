Preity Zinta's Spiritual Journey to Varanasi

2025-03-05T10:37:00+00:00

Actress Preity Zinta recently took a religious trip from the Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj to Varanasi with her mother. Due to heavy crowds, they had to navigate through various modes of transportation, including cars, auto rickshaws, and even cycle rickshaws. Despite the challenges, Zinta observed the crowd to be respectful and found the collective energy uplifting. She shared that her mother was exceptionally happy throughout the trip, which Zinta believes is the greatest form of service.
