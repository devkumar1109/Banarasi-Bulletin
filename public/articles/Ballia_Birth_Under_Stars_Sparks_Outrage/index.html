Ballia Birth Under Stars Sparks Outrage

2025-05-24T16:03:00+00:00

Four health department employees in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, are facing action after a woman gave birth under the open sky at a Community Health Centre (CHC). Samajwadi Party MLA Jai Prakash Anchal criticized the incident and urged for strict punishment against the responsible health workers. The incident highlights the lack of adequate facilities and care at the CHC, raising concerns about the quality of healthcare services provided.
