Varanasi Shuts Meat Shops for Navratri, Despite Eid Clash

2025-03-29T02:04:00+00:00

For the first time, Varanasi will close meat, fish, and poultry shops during Chaitra Navratri, beginning Sunday. Mayor Ashok Kumar Tiwari emphasized strict compliance, despite Eid coinciding. The decision was made to respect tradition and accommodate the large influx of pilgrims. The executive committee also banned cigarette sales near religious sites and schools, and approved a licensing fee for vendors. While acknowledging Eid, Tiwari stated that the closure would be strictly enforced, highlighting the significance of Navratri for Hindus.
