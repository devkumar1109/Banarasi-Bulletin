Ayushman Bharat: Labourer's Bones Healed, Hope Renewed

2025-03-09T14:39:00+00:00

Ram Prakash, a labourer from Uttar Pradesh, is receiving free bone treatment at Moti Lal Nehru Divisional Hospital in Prayagraj under the Ayushman Bharat scheme. His son, Jai Narayan, expressed gratitude to Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the scheme, stating that without it, they would have faced immense difficulties affording treatment. Ayushman Bharat, launched in 2017, provides a Rs 5 lakh annual coverage for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization across public and private hospitals nationwide.
