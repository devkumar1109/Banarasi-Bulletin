Fatal Power Line Contact During Religious Construction

2025-05-21T06:07:00+00:00

Four men died and three were injured after coming into contact with a high-voltage power line in Narwar village, Uttar Pradesh. The accident occurred while they were constructing a bamboo structure for a religious event. The deceased include police constable Ravindra Yadav, his brother Abhay, and two others. The injured are receiving treatment for burn injuries. The incident occurred early Wednesday and is highlighting the dangers of erecting temporary structures near electrical lines.
