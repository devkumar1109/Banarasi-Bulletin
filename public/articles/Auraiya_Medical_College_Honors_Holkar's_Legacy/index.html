Auraiya Medical College Honors Holkar's Legacy

2025-05-22T15:24:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced that a medical college in Auraiya will be named after Ahilyabai Holkar. This decision aims to honor Holkar's contributions to society and women empowerment. Adityanath criticized previous governments, particularly the Samajwadi Party, for attempting to divide historical figures based on caste, creed, and religion. He praised Prime Minister Narendra Modi's influence on the current government's "nation first" ideology. Holkar is celebrated for rebuilding temples and promoting societal welfare.
