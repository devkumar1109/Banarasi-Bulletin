UP Pledges Support for "Developed India" by 2047

2025-05-24T16:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath pledged the state's support for India's goal of becoming a "Developed India" by 2047 at the 10th NITI Aayog Governing Council meeting in New Delhi. Adityanath emphasized Uttar Pradesh's commitment to this national vision, highlighting the state's strategic role in achieving a "Developed State" by 2047 as a part of "Team India." The meeting, chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, focused on envisioning "Viksit Bharat@2047."
