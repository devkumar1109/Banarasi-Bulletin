Pandemic Treaty: Lessons Learned, Hope for Future

2025-05-19T21:29:00+00:00

The United Nations is finalizing the Pandemic Preparedness Treaty, aiming to prevent future pandemics. The treaty focuses on shared information, equitable vaccine and therapeutic access, and stronger healthcare systems, learned from the COVID-19 pandemic. Five years after the pandemic's peak, individuals who were on the front lines share their experiences. Dr. Margarita Castrillón, a pediatrician in Buenos Aires, volunteered in ambulance transport, facing exhaustion and fear. She highlighted the kindness of strangers and the importance of family. The pandemic led to lasting changes in her approach to medicine, including always wearing a mask and advocating for better support for healthcare workers. She emphasizes the need for government collaboration and fair treatment of healthcare professionals.
