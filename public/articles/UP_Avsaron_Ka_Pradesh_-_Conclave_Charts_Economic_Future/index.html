UP: Avsaron Ka Pradesh - Conclave Charts Economic Future

2025-03-20T03:26:00+00:00

NDTV India is hosting a conclave titled 'Uttar Pradesh - Avsaron Ka Pradesh' in Lucknow on March 20, 2025. The event will bring together state policymakers and industry leaders to discuss Uttar Pradesh's role in India's economic growth. Key sectors of focus include culture, tourism, sports, and the state's business landscape. Attendees include Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya, Ministers Dinesh Pratap Singh, Daya Shankar Singh, and Nitin Agarwal, Advisor Awanish Kumar Awasthi, singer Malini Awasthi, actor Rajpal Yadav, and several other professionals and cultural figures. The conclave aims to provide a platform for collaboration and a stronger roadmap for the state's future.
