Bronze Suheldev: Statue Sparks Political Feud

2025-06-10T15:57:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath unveiled a 40-foot, 17-ton bronze statue of 11th-century ruler Maharaja Suheldev in Bahraich. Samajwadi Party (SP) president Akhilesh Yadav vowed to replace it with a gold statue if his party wins the next election. The statue unveiling sparked political tensions and reignited debates over the historical recognition of Maharaja Suheldev, known for his 1033 AD victory against Ghazi Salar Masud. The event highlighted accusations of historical neglect and deep political rifts between rivals.
