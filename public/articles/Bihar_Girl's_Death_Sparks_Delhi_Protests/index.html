Bihar Girl's Death Sparks Delhi Protests

2025-03-10T09:09:00+00:00

Opposition MPs protested in Delhi demanding justice for a 17-year-old girl from Bihar who was found dead in her Varanasi hostel room. The MPs, from parties including RJD, CPI(ML) Liberation, and Congress, alleged the girl was raped and murdered, comparing the incident to the 2020 Hathras case. They demanded an inquiry into the death, highlighting that the incident occurred in Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Lok Sabha constituency. According to reports, the girl, who was preparing for NEET exams, was found hanging in her hostel room on February 1. Her family alleges the police conducted a postmortem and performed the last rites without their consent.
