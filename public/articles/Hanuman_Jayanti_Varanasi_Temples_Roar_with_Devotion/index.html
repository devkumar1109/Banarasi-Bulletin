Hanuman Jayanti: Varanasi Temples Roar with Devotion

2025-04-12T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi temples celebrated Hanuman Jayanti with vibrant decorations, aartis, devotional songs, and Ramcharitmanas recitals. The Sankat Mochan Temple hosted special events, including shehnai performances and Rudrabhishek by Brahmin priests. Authorities ensured safety through patrolling and drone surveillance. The Sankat Mochan Sangeet Samaroh, featuring 11 Padma awardees, will soon take place. Deputy Commissioner of Police Gaurav Banswal highlighted coordinated efforts with committees to maintain public order during the celebrations.
