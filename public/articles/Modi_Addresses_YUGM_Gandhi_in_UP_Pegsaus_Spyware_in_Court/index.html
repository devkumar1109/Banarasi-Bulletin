Modi Addresses YUGM, Gandhi in UP, Pegsaus Spyware in Court

2025-04-29T01:45:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will address the YUGM Conclave in New Delhi, a strategic gathering of leaders from government, academia, industry, and the innovation ecosystem. Rahul Gandhi will begin a two-day visit to his parliamentary constituency Raebareli and Amethi, inaugurating a solar plant, a Subhash Chandra Bose statue, and visiting the Rae Coach factory. This visit comes amid a revamp of the Uttar Pradesh Congress unit. The Supreme Court will hear pleas regarding the Pegasus spyware. The Delhi High Court will hear the bail plea of the accused in the December 2023 Parliament breach.
