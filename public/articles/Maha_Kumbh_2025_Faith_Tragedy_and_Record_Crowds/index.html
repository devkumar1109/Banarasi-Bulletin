Maha Kumbh 2025: Faith, Tragedy, and Record Crowds

2025-02-26T21:56:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj concluded on February 26th with over 66 crore attendees taking a dip at the Sangam. The event, which began on January 13th, drew a record-breaking number of devotees. The Kumbh saw both heartwarming and tragic moments. Individual stories like that of “Monalisa,” a 16-year-old garland seller who went viral for her striking appearance, captured public attention. However, stampedes at the mela site and New Delhi Railway Station (NDLS) claimed the lives of over 45 people.
