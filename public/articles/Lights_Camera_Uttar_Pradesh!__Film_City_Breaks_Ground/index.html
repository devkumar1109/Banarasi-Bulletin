Lights, Camera, Uttar Pradesh! Film City Breaks Ground

2025-05-25T01:32:00+00:00

Construction of the International Film City Project in Uttar Pradesh is set to begin by June. Bayview Projects LLP, a company owned by veteran filmmaker Boney Kapoor and backed by Bhutani Group, secured the bid for the project last year. The Uttar Pradesh government aims to establish a world-class film production hub in the state, attracting filmmakers and boosting the local economy.
