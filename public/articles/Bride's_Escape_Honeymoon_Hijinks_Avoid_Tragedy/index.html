Bride's Escape: Honeymoon Hijinks Avoid Tragedy

2025-06-17T10:54:00+00:00

A 23-year-old man from Uttar Pradesh expressed relief after his wife, who he married on May 17, eloped with her alleged lover 10 days after staying at her in-laws' house. Sunil stated he was thankful his situation didn't become as tragic as the Raja Raghuvanshi case, where an Indore man was allegedly murdered by his wife and her lover during their honeymoon. The woman, 20, returned to the Bisauli police station with her family and declared her desire to live with her lover. Both families agreed to an amicable separation, returning wedding gifts and valuables.
