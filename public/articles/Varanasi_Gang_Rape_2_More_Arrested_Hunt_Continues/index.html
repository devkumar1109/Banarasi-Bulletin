Varanasi Gang Rape: 2 More Arrested, Hunt Continues

2025-04-16T06:42:00+00:00

Two more arrests have been made in the Varanasi gangrape case, bringing the total number of arrests to 15. The 19-year-old college student was gangraped for seven days by 23 men. Prime Minister Narendra Modi intervened, directing officials to take strict action. The latest arrests were made based on CCTV analysis, facial mapping, and informant networks. The suspects, Raj Khan and Mohammad Shahbaz, were identified at multiple locations where the victim was held captive and have admitted their involvement. Police are actively searching for remaining suspects who have gone underground. The survivor is battling Hepatitis-B.
