Fake Cardiologist Arrested, 7 Deaths Under Investigation

2025-04-09T01:30:00+00:00

"Fake cardiologist" Narendra Vikramaditya Yadav, who posed as UK-based cardiologist Dr John Camm, has been arrested in Madhya Pradesh. Authorities are investigating claims that 7 patients died under Yadav's care at a Damoh hospital. Yadav allegedly forged medical qualifications to obtain the job. Dr. Camm confirmed that the identity theft began at least 5 years ago, noting the impersonator claimed to be him and to have trained under him. The impersonator even tweeted under Dr. Camm's name, prompting a retweet from Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's account. Indian cardiologists had previously questioned the validity of the impersonator's claims.
