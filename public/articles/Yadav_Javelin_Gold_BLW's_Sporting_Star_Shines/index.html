Yadav Javelin Gold: BLW's Sporting Star Shines

2025-06-09T19:09:00+00:00

Indian athlete Rohit Yadav won the gold medal in the men's javelin throw at the Taiwan Athletics Open 2025, held in Taipei on June 7-8. Yadav achieved a throw of 74.42 meters, defeating top athletes from across Asia. Currently an office superintendent at Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), Yadav's victory highlights the sporting talent nurtured by Indian Railways and BLW. BLW GM Naresh Pal Singh praised Yadav's dedication and hard work, emphasizing BLW's commitment to both locomotive production and fostering sporting excellence.
