Illegal Clinic Tragedy: Mother, Unborn Child Die

2025-03-22T12:06:00+00:00

A pregnant woman and her unborn child died at 'Raj Health Care', an illegally operating facility in Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh. The incident highlights the dangers of seeking medical care at unregistered or poorly regulated healthcare facilities. Authorities are investigating the circumstances surrounding the deaths.
