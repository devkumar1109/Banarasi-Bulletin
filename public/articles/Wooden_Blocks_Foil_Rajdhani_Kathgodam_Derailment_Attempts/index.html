Wooden Blocks Foil Rajdhani, Kathgodam Derailment Attempts

2025-05-20T07:14:00+00:00

Two trains, including the Rajdhani Express traveling from Delhi to Dibrugarh, were targeted for derailment in Hardoi, Uttar Pradesh. On Monday evening, unknown individuals placed wooden blocks on the tracks near kilometer marker 1129/14. The Rajdhani Express's locomotive pilot spotted the obstruction and applied emergency brakes, averting the derailment. The Kathgodam Express, traveling on the same route, was also saved due to its pilot's alertness. Superintendent Neeraj Kumar Jadaun visited the site and investigations are underway by the GRP, RPF, and local police. Hardoi Police confirmed the incident and stated that they are actively investigating.
