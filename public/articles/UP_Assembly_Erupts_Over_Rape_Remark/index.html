UP Assembly Erupts Over Rape Remark

2025-02-24T08:44:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy CM Brajesh Pathak's remark referencing Mulayam Singh Yadav's past statement on rape sparked uproar in the assembly, leading to protests by Samajwadi Party MLAs. Pathak's comment, "Will you also agree with his statement 'Ladko se galti ho jaati hai'," drew criticism from SP leaders who demanded an apology. The budget session, which commenced on February 20, will continue until March 5. Key proposals in the Rs 8.08 lakh crore budget include an AI City, a cybersecurity research park, scooty distribution for meritorious students, four new expressways, and the development of 58 municipalities as smart cities. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath urged the opposition to cooperate for smooth proceedings.
