Kumbh Pilgrims Perish in Mirzapur Crash

2025-02-24T19:44:00+00:00

Four people, including three pilgrims from Telangana and a motorcyclist from Varanasi, were killed in a road accident in Mirzapur. The accident occurred when the pilgrims' SUV collided with a motorcycle and then a stationary truck. The pilgrims were traveling back to Telangana after attending the Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj. Three others in the SUV suffered serious injuries. The Lalganj police are investigating the accident.
