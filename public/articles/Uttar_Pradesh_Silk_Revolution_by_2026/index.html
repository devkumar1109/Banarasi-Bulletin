Uttar Pradesh: Silk Revolution by 2026

2025-04-23T12:37:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government aims to revolutionize its silk industry by 2025-26. The plan involves linking 7,500 rural groups with the State Rural Livelihood Mission, providing training, technology, and financial aid across 15 districts. The initiative targets increased women's participation in silk production, with a goal of involving 50,000 women from 5,000 groups over the next five years. The Mukhyamantri Resham Vikas Yojana will further support increased silk production, leveraging existing demand and promoting traditional artisans. Currently, Uttar Pradesh produces 400 metric tonnes of silk against a consumption of 3,500 metric tonnes. The new drive aims to make the state self-reliant in silk production and boost exports.
