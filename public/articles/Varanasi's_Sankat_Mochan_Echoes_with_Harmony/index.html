Varanasi's Sankat Mochan Echoes with Harmony

2025-04-18T19:05:00+00:00

The 102nd edition of the Sankat Mochan Sangeet Samaroh, a week-long classical music and dance festival, commenced in Varanasi with a flute recital by Pandit Hari Prasad Chaurasia. The festival, held at the Sankat Mochan temple, coincides with Hanuman Jayanti and features renowned artistes including Ustad Wasifuddin Dagar, Rahul Sharma, Pravin Godkhindi, and Pt Ajay Pohankar. Notable for its community support and local sponsors, the festival emphasizes inclusivity with no VIP sections. Musicians perform in close proximity to the audience, creating a unique atmosphere of devotion, artistry, and community.
