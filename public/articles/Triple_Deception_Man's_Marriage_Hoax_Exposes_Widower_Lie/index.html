Triple Deception: Man's Marriage Hoax Exposes Widower Lie

2025-03-22T15:51:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man named Rajan was accused of tricking three women into marriage by falsely claiming to be a widower and an excise department employee. Rajan married Kiran in 2022, persuading her to take a Rs 42 lakh loan for a house. It was later discovered that he was not an excise employee. Police investigations revealed Rajan had also married Sarita in 2014 and deceived a third woman, Neelima Singh, into marriage. Sarita filed a police complaint against him in 2016 after learning about his deception. The women expressed concerns that Rajan might have defrauded other women as well.
