Haryana Dominates Khelo India Para Games

2025-03-27T17:23:00+00:00

Haryana retained the team championship at the second Khelo India Para Games, securing 34 gold medals. Tamil Nadu took second place with 28 golds, followed by Uttar Pradesh with 23 golds. The eight-day tournament featured 1300 athletes competing in six disciplines across three venues in New Delhi. A total of 596 medals were awarded, with Haryana leading the medal count with 104. The Games witnessed 18 national records being broken across various disciplines, including Para Powerlifting and track and field. Notably, 12 female athletes set new national records, highlighting the growing participation of women in para sports.
