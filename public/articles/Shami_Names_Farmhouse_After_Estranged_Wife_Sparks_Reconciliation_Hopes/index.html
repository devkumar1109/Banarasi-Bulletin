Shami Names Farmhouse After Estranged Wife, Sparks Reconciliation Hopes

2025-06-10T14:32:00+00:00

Mohammed Shami, the Indian fast bowler, has named his 150-bighas farmhouse in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh, after his estranged wife, Hasin Jahan. The farmhouse, reportedly worth Rs. 15 crores, features a mango orchard, swimming pool, and cricket pitch. Shami and Jahan have been separated for years following allegations of match-fixing and domestic abuse made by Jahan against Shami. The naming of the farmhouse after her has sparked speculation about a potential reconciliation between the couple.
