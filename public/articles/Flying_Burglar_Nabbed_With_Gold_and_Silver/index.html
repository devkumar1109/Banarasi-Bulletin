Flying Burglar Nabbed With Gold and Silver

2025-04-04T21:34:00+00:00

Rajesh Arvind Rajbhar (32), a notorious burglar from Uttar Pradesh, was arrested in Thane by Mulund Police. Rajbhar flew to Mumbai from Uttar Pradesh, committed 10-12 burglaries between March 13 and March 29, targeting homes in Mulund, Bhandup, Nehru Nagar, and Ulwe, and then fled back by flight. He was apprehended in Kalwa, Thane, before he could commit further crimes. Police recovered 28 tolas of gold and 2 kg of silver stolen by Rajbhar. His brother-in-law, also involved in burglaries, was previously arrested in Solapur. Authorities are investigating if Rajbhar is linked to other crimes across Maharashtra.
