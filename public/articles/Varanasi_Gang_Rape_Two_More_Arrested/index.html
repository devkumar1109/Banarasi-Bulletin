Varanasi Gang Rape: Two More Arrested

2025-04-16T06:42:00+00:00

Two more arrests have been made in the Varanasi gangrape case, bringing the total number of arrests to 15. The 19-year-old college student was abducted and repeatedly gang-raped for seven days by 23 men. The case gained momentum after Prime Minister Narendra Modi's intervention. Raj Khan and Mohammad Shahbaz were identified through CCTV analysis, facial sketches, and informant networks. Both suspects have admitted their involvement. Police are searching for the remaining suspects who are believed to be in hiding. The survivor is currently battling Hepatitis-B.
