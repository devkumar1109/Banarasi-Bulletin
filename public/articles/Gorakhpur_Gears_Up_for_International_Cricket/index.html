Gorakhpur Gears Up for International Cricket

2025-05-19T13:19:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is constructing a fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur, scheduled for completion in 18 months at a cost of Rs 236.40 crore. The stadium, located on a 50-acre site, will feature seven main pitches, four practice pitches, and a seating capacity of 30,000. Built using the EPC model, it will meet global standards set by organizations like the ICC. Accessibility improvements with new roads linking to the Gorakhpur-Varanasi Highway will enhance the stadium's connectivity and its potential as a hub for international sports events.
