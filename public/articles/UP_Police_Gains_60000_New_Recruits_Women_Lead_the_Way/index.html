UP Police Gains 60,000 New Recruits, Women Lead the Way

2025-06-14T10:19:00+00:00

Union Home Minister Amit Shah and CM Yogi Adityanath will distribute appointment letters to 60,244 newly recruited UP Police constables on June 15th in Lucknow. This includes 12,048 women, marking a significant increase in female representation in the security force. The recruitment drive, conducted by the UP Police Recruitment & Promotion Board, was one of the largest and most transparent in the country. Over the past eight years, 8.50 lakh people have received government jobs in UP. In a separate event, CM Yogi Adityanath felicitated 166 meritorious students who topped their respective boards in the 2024-25 exams. Each student received a cash reward of ₹1 lakh, a tablet, a certificate, and a medal.
