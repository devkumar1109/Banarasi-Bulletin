Hate Speech Costs Ansari: MLA Jailed, Faces Disqualification

2025-05-31T15:09:00+00:00

UP MLA Abbas Ansari, son of Mukhtar Ansari, was sentenced to 2 years imprisonment for a hate speech case in 2022. The Mau district court also imposed a fine of Rs 3,000 on Ansari. The controversial speech made during the 2022 assembly polls threatened officials and incited animosity. Ansari represented the Suheldev Bahujan Samaj Party (SBSP), which was allied with the Samajwadi Party at the time. Since the verdict, Ansari faces disqualification from the assembly as per Supreme Court directives. His lawyer plans to appeal the sentence in a higher court. The assembly speaker will now notify Ansari's disqualification, pending the outcome of the appeal.
