Aligarh Murder: CCTV Captures Cold-Blooded Killing

2025-03-15T07:38:00+00:00

A 25-year-old man, Haris, was shot dead in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, while standing outside his house at around 3:17 am on Friday. The incident was caught on CCTV footage, showing four assailants on two motorcycles firing at Haris. He attempted to shield himself but was shot multiple times, eventually collapsing. The attackers fled the scene after firing around a dozen bullets. Haris was taken to a hospital but died from his injuries. Police believe the shooting was the result of a dispute. They are investigating the incident and trying to identify the assailants using CCTV footage.
