"Doctor Death" Recaptured in Rajasthan Ashram

2025-05-20T15:54:00+00:00

Delhi Crime Branch arrested notorious serial killer 'Doctor Death', Devender Sharma, from a Rajasthan ashram. Sharma, a 67-year-old Ayurveda practitioner, was serving multiple life sentences for 21 murders and involvement in an illegal kidney transplant racket. He had jumped parole in August 2023 after being granted it for a 2004 murder case. This was not his first escape; he previously absconded for seven months in 2020. A Crime Branch team, led by Inspector Rakesh Kumar and supervised by ACP Umesh Barthwal, tracked him down to the ashram in Dausa, where he was posing as a priest. Sharma confessed to his crimes during interrogation.
