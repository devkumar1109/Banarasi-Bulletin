Uttar Pradesh Tops Ragging Complaints in Medical Colleges

2025-03-26T02:45:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh reported the highest number of ragging complaints in medical colleges in 2024, with 33 cases, followed by Bihar (17), Rajasthan (15), and Madhya Pradesh (12). The National Medical Commission (NMC) is actively addressing this issue through stringent measures, including regular monitoring, video conferences with medical institutions, and strict penalties for non-compliance. The government has implemented the Prevention and Prohibition of Ragging in Medical Colleges and Institutions Regulations, 2021, to create a zero-tolerance environment. Measures include online anti-ragging undertakings, CCTV installations, and dedicated complaint portals.
