India's AI Takes Flight in Local Languages

2025-06-17T23:00:00+00:00

India is focusing on developing sovereign language models (LLMs) to address the limitations of English-dominant AI. Four startups, Sarvam AI, Soket AI, Gnani AI, and Gan AI, have been selected to create these models. The move aims to ensure AI inclusivity for India's diverse population of over 1.4 billion people who speak 22 official languages and thousands of dialects. English-centric AI models struggle with accuracy, translation, and cultural understanding in Indian languages, highlighting the need for localized solutions. India seeks to become a leader in the AI revolution by catering to its unique linguistic landscape.
