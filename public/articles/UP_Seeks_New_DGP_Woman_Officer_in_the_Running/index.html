UP Seeks New DGP, Woman Officer in the Running

2025-05-24T10:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is searching for a new Director General of Police (DGP) as the current acting DGP, Prashant Kumar, is set to retire on May 31. For the first time in the state's history, a woman officer, Tilotama Varma, is a contender for the position. Varma, a 1990-batch IPS officer, currently serves as DG Training and has a background in the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI). Kumar, who took over as acting DGP in February 2024, has faced criticism for his aggressive policing style. The post has been vacant since May 2022, violating Supreme Court guidelines and drawing judicial censure. Service extensions for UP Police leadership are rare, with only two officers receiving them in the past decade.
