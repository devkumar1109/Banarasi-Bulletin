UP Declared Riot-Free: Shah Credits BJP Rule

2025-06-15T11:16:00+00:00

Amit Shah, Home Minister, declared Uttar Pradesh a riot-free state after eight years of BJP rule. He attributed this achievement to the state government's efforts and law enforcement's strict action against those involved in violence. Shah emphasized the improved law and order situation, citing a significant reduction in riots and communal clashes. This announcement came during a public address in Uttar Pradesh, highlighting the BJP government's focus on maintaining peace and stability in the state.
