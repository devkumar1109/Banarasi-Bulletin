Adityanath: Health, Development, and Infrastructure Drive

2025-04-21T15:14:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath attended the 'Guru Gorakhnath Health Service Yatra 5.0' in Lucknow. He emphasized his government's commitment to the development of the 54 Vantangiya villages, recognizing them and providing facilities including voting rights. In other developments, CM Adityanath inspected the venue for PM Modi's visit to Kanpur and took a metro ride in the city. He also visited Gorakhpur to inspect a four-lane road project and approved development projects worth over Rs 700 crores. He highlighted the importance of road development in Gorakhpur, including widening, strengthening, and constructing new four-lane roads and an overbridge.
