UPPRPB Denies Caste Bias in Constable Recruitment

2025-03-16T11:20:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment & Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has denied allegations of caste-based discrimination in its recent constable recruitment drive. The UPPRPB clarified that candidates are selected based on verified caste certificates, not surnames. The recruitment drive, which filled 60,244 constable vacancies, concluded with the release of final results on March 13. Candidates who passed the written exam underwent document verification and physical tests. The UPPRPB emphasized that original caste certificates are thoroughly verified by a board comprising officials of Sub-Divisional Magistrate and Deputy Superintendent of Police ranks.
