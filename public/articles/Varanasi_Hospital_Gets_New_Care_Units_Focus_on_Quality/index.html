Varanasi Hospital Gets New Care Units, Focus on Quality

2025-04-17T18:12:00+00:00

Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Hospital in Varanasi will soon have a sick newborn care unit (SNCU) and a critical care unit. Dr. Pinky Jowel, MD NHM/secretary of Medical Health and Family Welfare, Uttar Pradesh, approved the request for these units during a hospital inspection. The inspection also included visits to Urban CHC Sarnath and Shivpur, which will be converted into first referral units (FRUs). Dr. Jowel emphasized the importance of quality services, maintaining ENQUAS standards, and improving communication skills of community health officers. She also highlighted the need for effective functioning of Ayushman Arogya Mandir, regular medicine indenting, and availability of medicines according to EDL standards.
