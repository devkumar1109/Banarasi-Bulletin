Swayambhu Suit Stays Put in Varanasi Court

2025-04-12T18:49:00+00:00

A Varanasi court dismissed a plea to transfer the Lord Swayambhu suit from the court of the civil judge senior division to the district court. Anushka Tiwari and Indu Tiwari, representing Jyotirlinga Adi Vishweshwar Virajman, filed the transfer application in September 2024. They argued that several similar cases regarding the deity had been consolidated. The court rejected the application, stating that only parties to the suit can file for its transfer.
