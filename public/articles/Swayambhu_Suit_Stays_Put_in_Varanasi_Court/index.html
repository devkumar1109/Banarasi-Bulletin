Swayambhu Suit Stays Put in Varanasi Court

2025-04-12T18:49:00+00:00

A Varanasi court dismissed a plea seeking the transfer of the Lord Swayambhu suit from the court of the civil judge senior division to the district court. The plea was filed by Anushka Tiwari and Indu Tiwari, representing Jyotirlinga Adi Vishweshwar Virajman, in September 2024. They argued that the suit, along with seven similar cases regarding the ancient idol of Swayambhu Lord Vishweshwar, had been consolidated and should be transferred. The court rejected the plea stating that only parties to the suit can apply for its transfer.
