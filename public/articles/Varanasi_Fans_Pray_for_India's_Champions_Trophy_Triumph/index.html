Varanasi Fans Pray for India's Champions Trophy Triumph

2025-03-04T03:21:00+00:00

Indian cricket fans in Varanasi offered prayers for Team India's victory against Australia in the Champions Trophy semi-final at the Dubai International Stadium. The two teams have a history of intense clashes in ICC ODI tournaments, with each side winning two matches against the other since India's quarter-final win in the 2011 World Cup. Prior to the semi-final, a significant difference in form was observed between the two teams since their last encounter on November 19, 2023. Fans performed the Maha Aarti of Sarangnath Mahadev, hoping for Team India's success.
