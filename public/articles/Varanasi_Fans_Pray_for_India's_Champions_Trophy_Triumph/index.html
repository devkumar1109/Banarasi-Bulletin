Varanasi Fans Pray for India's Champions Trophy Triumph

2025-03-04T03:21:00+00:00

Indian cricket fans in Varanasi prayed for Team India's victory against Australia in the Champions Trophy semi-final at the Dubai International Stadium. The two teams have a history of facing each other in ICC ODI tournaments, with India and Australia each winning two games. India's victories came in the 2019 and 2023 World Cups, while their defeats occurred in the 2015 semi-finals and 2023 final. Since their last encounter on November 19, 2023, both teams have undergone significant changes. Fans performed a Maha Aarti of Sarangnath Mahadev, with priests chanting for Team India's success.
