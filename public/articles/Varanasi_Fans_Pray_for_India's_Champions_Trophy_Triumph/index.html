Varanasi Fans Pray for India's Champions Trophy Triumph

2025-03-09T04:28:00+00:00

Cricket fans in Varanasi held an 'aarti' at the Sarang Nath Mahadev temple, praying for India's victory in the ICC Champions Trophy final against New Zealand. They cited India's strong spin attack and balanced team lineup as reasons for their confidence. Cricket coaches echoed this sentiment, praising players like Virat Kohli and predicting a win for India. The final will take place at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium on Sunday.
