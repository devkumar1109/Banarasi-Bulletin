Varanasi Fans Pray for India's Champions Trophy Triumph

2025-03-04T07:41:00+00:00

Indian cricket fans in Varanasi prayed for Team India's victory against Australia in the ICC Champions Trophy 2025 semi-final. The match takes place at the Dubai International Stadium on Tuesday. India and Australia have faced each other four times in ICC ODI tournaments since 2011, with each team winning two matches. Ahead of the crucial semi-final, fans performed Maha Aarti at the Sarangnath Mahadev temple, expressing confidence in Rohit Sharma's leadership and Virat Kohli's performance. They believe India will reach the final of the tournament.
