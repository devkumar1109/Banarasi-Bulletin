Varanasi Fans Pray for India's Champions Trophy Triumph

2025-03-04T15:43:00+00:00

Indian cricket fans in Varanasi prayed for Team India's victory against Australia in the Champions Trophy semi-final on Tuesday. India and Australia have a history of close encounters in ICC ODI tournaments, with each team winning two out of their last four matches. India's victories came in the 2019 and 2023 World Cups, while Australia won the 2015 semi-final and 2023 final. The match will be held at the Dubai International Stadium. The Indian squad is led by Rohit Sharma and includes players like Virat Kohli, KL Rahul, and Ravindra Jadeja. Australia's captain is Steve Smith, and their squad features players like Alex Carey, Glenn Maxwell, and Adam Zampa.
