Varanasi on High Alert: Leopard on the Loose

2025-05-27T20:59:00+00:00

A leopard attack in Varanasi last Friday left three people injured and prompted a large-scale search operation. The leopard, believed to have entered Varanasi from Chandauli or Kaimur Wildlife Sanctuary, evaded capture despite efforts by forest officials using cages and nets. The injured individuals are currently in stable condition. 12 villages along the Ganga river, with a combined population of 18,000, are on high alert. Villagers are maintaining night vigils and using firecrackers to deter the leopard. The forest department has deployed guards, installed nets and cages, and is conducting intensive combing operations.
