Youth Found Dead, Family Alleges Murder at Wedding

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, alleges murder and has filed a complaint against two relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar. Dinanath stated that Hemant was called to a wedding by the accused on Friday night. Around 11 pm, they claimed Hemant attacked them and fled. The body was discovered the next morning. Police have filed a named FIR against the relatives, who are currently being questioned. The postmortem report and investigation findings will determine further action.
