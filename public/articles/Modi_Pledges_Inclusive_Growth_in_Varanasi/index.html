Modi Pledges Inclusive Growth in Varanasi

2025-04-11T16:11:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for 44 projects worth Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, his parliamentary constituency. He emphasized his government's commitment to "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas" (inclusive development), contrasting it with the Opposition's focus on "parivar ka saath, parivar ka vikas" (family-centric development). Modi highlighted Varanasi's transformation over the last decade, noting its progress in health, infrastructure, and economic development. He lauded the city's emergence as a health capital for Purvanchal and its growing cultural recognition through GI tags for products like tabla, shehnai, and sweets. He also praised Uttar Pradesh's progress, citing its leading position in GI tagging, showcasing the state's cultural and artisanal excellence.
