Kumbh Boost: Trivedi Claims Rs 3 Lakh Crore Economic Impact

2025-02-24T19:47:00+00:00

BJP spokesperson and Rajya Sabha MP Sudhanshu Trivedi claims that the Maha Kumbh festival contributed approximately Rs 3 lakh crore to India's economy, representing 1% of the GDP. He highlighted this while discussing the Union Budget 2025-26 in Varanasi, emphasizing the budget's reflection of Prime Minister Narendra Modi's vision for a developed India by 2047. Trivedi also praised government schemes like Jan Dhan Yojana, Mudra Loan, and Startup India for their impact on economic growth and self-reliance. He noted the success of the Kashi Vishwanath Corridor project, which saw a surge in tourist visits to Varanasi following its completion in 2021.
