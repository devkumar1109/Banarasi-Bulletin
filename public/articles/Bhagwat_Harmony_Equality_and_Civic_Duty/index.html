Bhagwat: Harmony, Equality, and Civic Duty

2025-04-05T19:08:00+00:00

RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat called for social harmony and equality, emphasizing the sharing of temples, water, and resources without caste discrimination. He addressed two sessions at BHU, highlighting the Sangh's centenary year and urging youths to connect with the organization through service and civic duty. Bhagwat stressed the importance of strengthening families and promoting self-reliance by using indigenous products. He also urged academicians to focus on developing values in students and engaging with rural areas. Bhagwat addressed environmental concerns and called for responsible citizenship, including following traffic rules and maintaining cleanliness.
