Adityanath Defends Mahakumbh, Slams Opponents

2025-03-26T06:20:00+00:00

Yogi Adityanath, the Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister, defended the Mahakumbh 2025 in an interview with ANI, responding to opposition criticism. He stated that a judicial commission is investigating stampede deaths during the event and that action will be taken based on its findings. Adityanath criticized Samajwadi Party leader's remarks on Rana Sanga, asserting that those who glorify Aurangzeb and Babur cannot be expected to respect Indian heritage. He accused the Congress and its allies in the INDIA bloc of attempting to influence the 2024 Lok Sabha elections through foreign funding and propaganda.
