U.P. Honors Ambedkar: Celebrations for Social Justice

2025-04-12T15:13:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is organizing statewide events to celebrate Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's 134th birth anniversary. The events, beginning April 13th, aim to educate youth about Ambedkar's life and principles of justice, equality, and social reform. Celebrations include cultural performances, educational programs, the "Bhim Padyatra" march, and a grand event at the Ambedkar Mahasabha campus. A special exhibition will highlight significant moments from Ambedkar's life. These initiatives aim to engage the public and perpetuate his legacy of empowerment and social harmony.
