India's Strike: Balancing Deterrence and Peace

2025-05-08T08:00:00+00:00

Lt Gen PJS Pannu (Retd) discussed the India-Pakistan conflict in an interview with Oneindia. He analyzed India's recent aerial strike on terror bases in Pakistan, emphasizing its strategic aim to disrupt terrorist operations and send a message without escalating the conflict. The strike, a response to the Palgham attack, targeted training areas used by terrorists and the Pakistani army. India maintained a coordinated response involving multiple agencies and high-level political discussions, prepared for potential retaliation. Pannu highlighted the diminished role of global powers in mediating conflicts, placing greater responsibility on countries like India and Pakistan to secure peace. He pointed to Pakistan's internal challenges, including economic struggles and leadership issues, making peace with India crucial for its stability.
