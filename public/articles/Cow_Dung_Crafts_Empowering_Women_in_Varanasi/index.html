Cow Dung Crafts: Empowering Women in Varanasi

2025-03-08T13:00:00+00:00

Rita Tiwari is empowering women in Varanasi through self-employment initiatives using cow dung and urine. This initiative began in 2005 and has grown to employ around 1,000 women, who create products like lamps, incense sticks, statues, and cleaning supplies. Initially, women earned ₹2,000-₹4,000 per month, but now their income has increased to ₹15,000-₹16,000. The products are sold online through platforms like Trade India and Flipkart, generating a monthly turnover of around ₹20 lakh. Rita Tiwari credits her success to her family's support, particularly her husband, and the growing demand for these products.
