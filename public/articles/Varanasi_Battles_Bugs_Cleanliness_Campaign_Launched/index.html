Varanasi Battles Bugs: Cleanliness Campaign Launched

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, and chikungunya. UP ministers Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra flagged off the campaign, urging public cleanliness and waterlogging prevention. MLA Saurabh Srivastava highlighted a significant decrease in disease cases compared to previous years, attributing it to departmental coordination. SVM Bhelupur Hospital introduced new medical facilities, including an ultrasound machine and dental department. The campaign focuses on 68 dengue hotspots in urban areas and 56 in rural areas. A Dastak campaign will run concurrently from April 10 to 31, emphasizing inter-departmental cooperation for disease control.
