Gorakhpur Welcomes You: Seven Grand Gateways Rise

2025-05-28T19:13:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has approved a plan to construct seven grand gateways at the borders of Gorakhpur. The project, proposed by Gorakhpur MP Ravi Kishan Shukla, will symbolize the divine heritage of the Nath Panth and showcase the city's rich cultural legacy. Each gateway will feature depictions of Guru Gorakhnath and other Siddha Yogis, along with their spiritual teachings. The gateways will be built along key entry routes: Deoria, Varanasi, Maharajganj, Sonauli, Kushinagar, Lucknow, and Pipraich. The project, overseen by the Gorakhpur Development Authority, aims to enhance the city's urban aesthetics and cultural pride.
