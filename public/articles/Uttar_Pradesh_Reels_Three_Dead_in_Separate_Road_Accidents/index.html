Uttar Pradesh Reels: Three Dead in Separate Road Accidents

2025-04-21T18:22:00+00:00

Three people, including two youths, died in separate road accidents in Uttar Pradesh. In Ballia, Gaurav Kumar Singh (25) and Shubham Kumar Singh (22) were killed when a speeding bus hit their motorcycle while they were returning from a wedding. The bus driver has been detained. In Varanasi, truck driver Vinod Verma (28) died after his truck collided with another truck. He was trapped in the cabin and later died at the hospital.
