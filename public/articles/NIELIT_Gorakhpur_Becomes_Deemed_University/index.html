NIELIT Gorakhpur Becomes Deemed University

2025-03-11T19:46:00+00:00

The National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT), Gorakhpur, has been granted deemed university status by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY). This makes NIELIT Gorakhpur one of 11 constituent campuses under the ‘Distinct Category' of NIELIT Deemed-to-be University. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and PhD courses in IT and electronics. NIELIT Director General Madan Mohan Tripathi announced the launch of new BTech and MTech programs in emerging fields such as AI, machine learning, blockchain, and cybersecurity. These industry-aligned curricula aim to prepare graduates for immediate employment. Aligned with NEP 2020, NIELIT Gorakhpur aims to enhance accessibility and industry relevance through digital learning tools, promoting regional development and bridging the digital divide.
