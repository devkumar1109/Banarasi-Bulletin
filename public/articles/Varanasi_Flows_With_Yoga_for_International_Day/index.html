Varanasi Flows With Yoga for International Day

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for International Yoga Day on June 21st. The main event will be held at Kashi Vishwanath Dham. A Yoga Week will precede the main event, starting on June 15th with a grand opening at Namo Ghat. Yoga sessions will be held at all ghats and in village panchayats, urban areas, schools, and health and wellness centers. The district magistrate has instructed officials to ensure all necessary preparations are in place. Yoga organizations are encouraged to conduct daily sessions, and youth welfare officers are coordinating with Yuvak Mangal Dal members to train them in yoga protocols for rural areas.
