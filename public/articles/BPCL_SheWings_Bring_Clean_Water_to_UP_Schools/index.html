BPCL, SheWings Bring Clean Water to UP Schools

2025-04-10T03:33:00+00:00

BPCL and SheWings Foundation launched the "Swachh Jal, Swasth Kal" initiative to provide clean drinking water to schoolchildren. The initiative will install water purification systems in 35 government schools in Gautam Buddha Nagar, Uttar Pradesh. This project is supported by District Magistrate Manish Kumar Verma and Basic Shiksha Adhikari Rahul Pawar. SheWings Foundation founder Madan Mohit Bhardwaj emphasizes the importance of clean water for children's health and well-being. BPCL General Manager Raman Mallik states that BPCL is committed to supporting initiatives that contribute to a healthier India. Dr. Praveen Kumar, a public health expert, highlights the health risks associated with contaminated water, especially for children.
