Adityanath: Pratap, Not Akbar, India's True Hero

2025-03-08T15:54:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath unveiled a statue of Maharana Pratap in Dadri, calling him a "true national hero." Adityanath stated that Akbar and Aurangzeb are not national icons, emphasizing that they held the same mindset towards Hindus. He contrasted them with Maharana Pratap, Veer Shivaji, and Guru Gobind Singh, whom he considers India's true heroes. Adityanath lauded Maharana Pratap's dedication to self-respect, faith, and the nation, highlighting his choice to prioritize honor over power.
