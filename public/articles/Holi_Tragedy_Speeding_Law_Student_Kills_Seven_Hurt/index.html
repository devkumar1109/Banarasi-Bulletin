Holi Tragedy: Speeding Law Student Kills, Seven Hurt

2025-03-16T12:14:00+00:00

A speeding car driven by Rakshit Ravish Chaurasia hit a scooter in Vadodara, Gujarat, killing one woman and injuring seven others on Holi eve. Locals apprehended Chaurasia and assaulted him before handing him over to the police. Videos show Chaurasia appearing intoxicated, shouting "Another round" and mentioning "Nikita" while chanting "Om Namah Shivaya". Chaurasia claims he was not drunk and driving at 50 km/hour, but police suspect drunk driving and will conduct a medical test. Chaurasia, a law student from Varanasi, has a valid driving license. He expressed his willingness to meet the victim's family and take responsibility for the accident.
