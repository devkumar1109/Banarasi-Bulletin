Varanasi Celebrates Women: Equality, Empowerment, and Honors

2025-03-08T20:02:00+00:00

To mark International Women's Day, various institutions in Varanasi organized events focused on equality and empowerment. Mahila Mahavidyalaya, BHU hosted a workshop and panel discussion on empowering women and girls, while BHU also organized sports events and an oratory competition. DAV PG College held a seminar on gender equality, and Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW) hosted a ceremony with cultural performances honoring retiring women employees. Vishal Bharat Sansthan's Women's Council discussed the role of Indian women in culture and tradition, and highlighted the undervaluation of domestic women's work.
