Varanasi Prepares for Grand Akshaya Tritiya Mass Wedding

2025-04-25T17:49:00+00:00

Officials in Varanasi conducted a surprise inspection of Sankuldhara Pond in Khojwa to review preparations for a mass wedding event on Akshaya Tritiya (April 30). The event will include a procession with horse-drawn carriages, altars around the pond, a main stage, and arrangements for food and parking. Officials inspected cleanliness, parking, road repairs, street lighting, pond cleaning, and security. They were joined by ADM city Alok Verma, additional municipal commissioner Savita Yadav, deputy director of tourism RK Rawat, social welfare officer, and officials from the waterworks and police department.
