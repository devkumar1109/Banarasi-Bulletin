Apache Heliecopter Makes Emergency Landing in Punjab

2025-06-13T08:16:00+00:00

An Indian Air Force Apache helicopter made an emergency landing in Nangalpur, Pathankot, Punjab. There were no reported injuries or damage. This follows a similar incident on June 6th, where an Apache helicopter made a precautionary landing in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh due to a technical issue. Both pilots in that incident were safe, and the helicopter was deemed serviceable after checks.
