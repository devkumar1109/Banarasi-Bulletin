Purvanchal University Soars in Global Research Rankings

2025-03-17T18:47:00+00:00

Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University secured 2nd position among UP state universities and 125th nationally in the Nature Index Ranking 2025. The university achieved this ranking due to its publications in high-grade research journals, reflecting its research excellence. Purvanchal University's score is 2 with a share of 0.74. The Nature Index evaluates research quality and impact based on publications and contribution to research papers. Vice-chancellor Prof Vandana Singh attributes the achievement to the university's focus on quality research, collaborations, and faculty efforts. Purvanchal University also received an A+ ranking from the NAAC and funding under the DST-PURSE scheme.
