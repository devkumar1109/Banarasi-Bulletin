Death Penalty Sought for Healthcare CEO Murder

2025-04-26T07:31:00+00:00

Federal prosecutors will seek the death penalty for Luigi Mangione, who is accused of murdering UnitedHealthcare CEO Brian Thompson. The Department of Justice (DOJ) believes Mangione intended to send a political message against the healthcare industry by targeting a high-profile individual. Attorney General Pam Bondi supports the death penalty request. Mangione's defense team calls the move "barbaric" and politically motivated, while Mangione plans to plead not guilty to the four-count indictment, which includes firearm-related murder.
