Yogi Cracks Down on Shell Companies, Targets Tax Evasion

2025-06-07T18:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has launched a crackdown on shell companies to combat tax evasion and promote transparency. He instructed officials to utilize technology and strict enforcement measures to identify and target these entities. Adityanath emphasized that tax evasion hinders development and welfare schemes. Suspicious firms under Central GST will be reported for deregistration, while State GST firms will undergo thorough investigations. He called for special strategies in areas with low tax collection and described tax evasion as a "national crime."
