Varanasi Police Crackdown: Immigrants and Crime Targeted

2025-04-30T19:34:00+00:00

The Varanasi police have launched a crackdown on illegal immigrants, particularly Bangladeshis and Rohingyas, following directions from Police Commissioner Mohit Agarwal. Agarwal emphasized increased vigilance at key locations like railway stations, bus stands, temples, and ghats. He directed officials to verify individuals with questionable credentials and take action against those residing illegally. The drive also includes intensified CCTV surveillance, especially in rural areas, and collaboration with village heads to raise awareness about security. Agarwal further mandated a crackdown on drug peddling, cattle smuggling, illegal arms trafficking, and online gambling. He instructed police stations to conduct surprise inspections and increase night patrols.
