BLW Javelin Star Shines in Taiwan

2025-06-09T19:09:00+00:00

Rohit Yadav, an athlete from Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), won the gold medal in the men's javelin throw at the Taiwan Athletics Open 2025. He achieved a throw of 74.42 meters on June 8th in Taipei, Taiwan. This victory highlights the growing talent pool in Indian athletics and BLW's commitment to nurturing sportsmanship alongside its locomotive production.
