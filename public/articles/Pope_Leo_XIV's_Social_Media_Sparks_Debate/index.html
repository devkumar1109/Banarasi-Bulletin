Pope Leo XIV's Social Media Sparks Debate

2025-05-09T06:30:00+00:00

Pope Leo XIV, formerly Robert Prevost, has sparked debate with his social media posts in 2025. While infrequent, his posts reveal a compassionate stance on immigration, supporting Dreamers and advocating for gun reform and climate action. Prior to his papacy, Prevost subtly criticized Vice President JD Vance and questioned Donald Trump's immigration policies. His views have been praised by liberals but have raised concerns among right-wing commentators. Prevost's online activity offers insight into his political leanings as the first American pope, hinting at his potential leadership direction for the global Church.
