Congress Plots 2027 UP Win: Ground Game, Local Focus

2025-06-18T09:38:00+00:00

The Congress party held a meeting in Lucknow to strategize for the 2027 Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections. The meeting focused on strengthening the party's organizational base in the Purvanchal region, with a goal of forming committees at every block and booth level by July. The party emphasized that tickets for the elections will be awarded to active ground workers recommended by district organizations. Congress plans to tailor its election manifesto to local issues in each district, focusing on youth, women, farmers, and employment.
