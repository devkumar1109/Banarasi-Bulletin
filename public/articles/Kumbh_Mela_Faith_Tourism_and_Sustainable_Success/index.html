Kumbh Mela: Faith, Tourism, and Sustainable Success

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj drew 62 crore devotees, significantly impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi saw over 2 crore visitors since the Mahakumbh began, while over 69 lakh people booked homestays in Ayodhya through the Divya Ayodhya app. The event featured the eight-day Khel Mahakumbh, promoting indigenous sports and nurturing young talent, with a resolution to include them in the 2036 Olympics. Maha Kumbh 2025 also implemented a successful "zero discharge" initiative, managing wastewater from 1.5 lakh toilets daily and ensuring no polluted water entered the river. Prime Minister Narendra Modi took a holy dip in the Sangam and performed puja.
