Khan Shatters 400m Record, Maharashtra Dominates

2025-05-13T17:21:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's Kadir Khan broke the 400m meet record twice in 24 hours at the Khelo India Youth Games 2025. On Tuesday, he clocked 47.34 seconds in the final, improving his own record set on Monday. Aditya Pisal from Maharashtra became the fastest boy in the Games with a record-breaking performance. Maharashtra continues to lead the medal tally, while Rajasthan secured second place. Host state Bihar won three silver and one bronze in athletics.
