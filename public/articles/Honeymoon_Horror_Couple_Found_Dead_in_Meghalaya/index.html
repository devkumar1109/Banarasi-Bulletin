Honeymoon Horror: Couple Found Dead in Meghalaya

2025-06-14T19:27:00+00:00

A murder case in Meghalaya has shaken the state. A honeymoon couple, from Gujarat, was found dead in Sohra, a popular tourist destination. The husband, identified as a businessman, was found dead at their hotel room while the wife was found dead in a nearby forest. The police suspect it to be a planned murder and are investigating various angles. The incident has raised concerns about safety for tourists.
