Fatal Highway Crash Kills Two Near Gola Village

2025-04-14T18:58:00+00:00

Two individuals, Sunil Gupta (SUV driver) and Neeraj Yadav (motorcyclist), died in a collision on the Varanasi-Azamgarh highway near Gola village. The accident occurred around 12:30 am on Sunday night when Gupta's speeding SUV hit Yadav's motorcycle, causing the SUV to overturn. Both victims were transported to the Cholapur community health centre but were pronounced dead.
