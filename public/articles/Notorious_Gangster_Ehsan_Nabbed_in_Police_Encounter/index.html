Notorious Gangster Ehsan Nabbed in Police Encounter

2025-04-02T21:01:00+00:00

Ehsan, a 22-year-old notorious gangster from Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested by Uttarakhand Police after a police encounter in the Timli forest area. Wanted in over a dozen criminal cases related to cattle slaughter and smuggling across Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, and Haryana, Ehsan had 18 prior criminal cases and a bounty of Rs 15,000 on his head. On April 1st, police launched an operation at Ehsan's suspected hideout. The following morning, he attempted to flee on a motorcycle and opened fire on police, injuring himself in the leg before being apprehended. A 12-bore pistol and live ammunition were recovered from him.
