AI Guards Divine Kumbh: 64 Crore Devotees Safe

2025-02-26T05:32:00+00:00

The Divine Maha Kumbh, the world's largest religious gathering, has drawn nearly 64 crore devotees. To manage the massive crowds, advanced AI-driven systems are being used. 1,700 cameras, including 500 AI-enabled ones, monitor crowd density and track individuals. Data and alerts are sent to a cloud-based dashboard at Prayagraj's integrated command centre to ensure law and order.
