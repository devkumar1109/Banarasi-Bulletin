Congress Workers Accused in UP Shop Raid, Molestation

2025-06-07T10:41:00+00:00

Two Congress workers in Uttar Pradesh were arrested on charges of robbery, theft, and molestation. The workers, Anand Pandey and Amit Pathak, are alleged to have harassed Sachina Khatoon, a woman who owns a boutique, and forced her to vacate her shop. Khatoon claimed they raided her shop despite a stay order on eviction. Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai, who claims the workers are close to him, has accused the government of orchestrating a political conspiracy to defame him.
