IIT BHU Class of '75 Marks Golden Reunion

2025-03-17T13:28:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University), Varanasi, celebrated the 50th anniversary reunion of its Class of 1975. Around 180 people, including 100 alumni from across the globe, attended the event at the Annie Besant Lecture Theatre. Alumni reminisced about their experiences and career paths, expressing gratitude to the institute for their success. They also pledged support for future generations through a legacy program. IIT (BHU) Director Prof. Amit Patra and Dean of resources and alumni Prof. Hiralal Pramanik emphasized the importance of alumni engagement. The event was coordinated by K Chandrashekhar, who brought the alumni together. A two-minute silence was observed in remembrance of deceased members of the Class of 1975. The reunion highlighted the strong bonds among alumni and IIT (BHU)'s lasting impact on its students.
