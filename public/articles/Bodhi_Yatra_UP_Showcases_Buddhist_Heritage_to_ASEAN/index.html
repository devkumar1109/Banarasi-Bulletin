Bodhi Yatra: UP Showcases Buddhist Heritage to ASEAN

2025-06-08T19:11:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government organized a six-day 'Bodhi Yatra' from June 2 to June 7, inviting 50 delegates from five ASEAN countries to showcase the state's Buddhist heritage. The event, jointly conducted by the Uttar Pradesh Tourism Department and the Union Ministry of External Affairs, aimed to promote major Buddhist sites and strengthen tourism and cultural ties between India and Southeast Asia. Delegates visited key destinations within Uttar Pradesh's Buddhist circuit and participated in B2B meetings, expected to boost tourism and cultural relations. This initiative is anticipated to secure global recognition for Uttar Pradesh's Buddhist sites and contribute to economic growth.
