Yogi Adityanath: UP Focus, National Service.

2025-04-01T11:06:00+00:00

UP CM Yogi Adityanath denied aspirations for the Prime Minister position, stating that his focus is serving the people of Uttar Pradesh. He emphasized that politics is not his primary calling and he is at heart a "Yogi." Adityanath addressed the intersection of religion and politics, stating that both should serve the greater good. He believes politics driven by self-interest creates problems, while politics for the common good provides solutions. He clarified that he considers himself a citizen of the country, prioritizing national security above all else. Adityanath dismissed rumors of a rift with BJP's central leadership, asserting his position is due to the party's support.
