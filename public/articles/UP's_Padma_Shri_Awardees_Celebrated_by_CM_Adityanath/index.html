UP's Padma Shri Awardees Celebrated by CM Adityanath

2025-04-28T19:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath congratulated four individuals from the state who received the Padma Shri award. President Droupadi Murmu presented the awards in New Delhi. The recipients are Dr Shyam Bihari Agrawal, Hriday Narayan Dixit, Ganeshwar Shastri Dravid, and Dr Satyapal Singh. Adityanath praised Dixit's writings for reflecting Indian culture and values, Dravid's scholarship for enriching Indian heritage, and Singh's dedication to para athletics and nurturing talent.
