Kumbh Clash: Yadav Blasts Adityanath's Handling

2025-02-27T15:33:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav accused Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath of mismanagement and political exploitation during the Maha Kumbh. Yadav criticized the abrupt end to the event, alleging it prevented elderly devotees from taking the "Amrit Snan". He contrasted the BJP government's organization with the SP's 2013 Kumbh, which he claimed received global praise. Yadav further alleged inadequate arrangements, poor crowd management leading to stampedes and deaths, and accused the BJP government of hiding the true number of casualties. Deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya refuted these claims, stating the river water remained pure and the allegations were baseless.
