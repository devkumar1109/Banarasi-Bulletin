IIT Madras Reign Supreme in NIRF Rankings 2024

2025-03-03T09:49:00+00:00

The NIRF Rankings 2024 declare IIT Madras as India's top engineering college, followed by IIT Delhi and IIT Bombay. The rankings include 23 state public universities, 22 private deemed universities, 16 IITs, 9 NITs, 7 central universities and 7 private universities. IIT Kanpur, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, NIT Tiruchirappalli, BHU Varanasi, VIT, Jadavpur University, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, and Anna University complete the top 15.
