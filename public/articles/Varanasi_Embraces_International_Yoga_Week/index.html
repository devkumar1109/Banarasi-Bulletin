Varanasi Embraces International Yoga Week

2025-06-16T00:06:00+00:00

International Yoga Week commenced in Varanasi on June 16th, leading up to International Yoga Day on June 21st. The week began with a mass yoga session at Namo Ghat, attended by dignitaries and led by experienced instructors. Simultaneous events were held across the district, including educational institutions, parks, ghats, jails, and village panchayats, organized by the AYUSH department and various yoga institutions. At the Government Ayurvedic College and Hospital, a special yoga session focused on Surya Namaskar, Pranayama, and asanas. Yoga experts emphasized yoga's holistic benefits for mental, physical, and spiritual well-being. Throughout the week, events like group yoga practices, seminars, competitions, and awareness rallies will continue.
