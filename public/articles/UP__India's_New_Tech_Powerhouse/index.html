UP: India's New Tech Powerhouse

2025-03-08T12:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is becoming a major tech hub in India. Microsoft recently inaugurated its India Development Center (IDC) in Noida, highlighting the state's growing potential in AI and cloud computing. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath emphasized the center's role as a significant hub for innovation outside Microsoft headquarters and its contribution to India's tech ecosystem. The state is fostering innovation through modern policies and initiatives like the Nivesh Mitra single-window clearance system. Adityanath pointed to the use of AI in the Maha Kumbh as an example of the state's pro-industry approach.
