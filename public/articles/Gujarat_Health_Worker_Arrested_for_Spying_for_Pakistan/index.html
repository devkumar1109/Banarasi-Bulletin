Gujarat Health Worker Arrested for Spying for Pakistan

2025-05-24T09:39:00+00:00

A contractual health worker, Sahdevsinh Gohil, from Kutch, Gujarat, was arrested by the Gujarat Anti-Terrorist Squad (ATS) for sharing sensitive information about BSF and Navy facilities with a Pakistani spy named Aditi Bharadwaj. Since June 2023, Gohil shared photos and videos of these facilities via WhatsApp for money. He obtained a new SIM card in January 2025 and provided the OTP to the Pakistani agent, allowing her to use WhatsApp from Pakistan. The ATS arrested Gohil based on a tip and after forensic analysis of his phone. An FIR was filed against him and the Pakistani agent under BNS Sections 61 (criminal conspiracy) and 148 (waging war against the government).
