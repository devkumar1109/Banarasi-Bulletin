Swaraj: Fearless India, One Nation, One Vote

2025-04-24T18:04:00+00:00

BJP MP Bansuri Swaraj condemned the Pahalgam terror attack, stating that "Modi's new India was fearless and would not be defeated by terrorist forces." Speaking at a women's conference in Varanasi, Swaraj highlighted the benefits of "One Nation, One Election," emphasizing reduced election expenditure, decreased burden on administration and security forces, and a focus on national interests. She urged women to support the policy and become flagbearers of the ideology, contributing to a stronger democracy.
