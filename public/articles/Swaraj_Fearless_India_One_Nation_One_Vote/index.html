Swaraj: Fearless India, One Nation, One Vote

2025-04-24T18:04:00+00:00

BJP MP Bansuri Swaraj condemned the Pahalgam terror attack, stating that "Modi's new India is fearless" and will not be defeated by terrorism. Swaraj was in Varanasi for a women's conference on "One Nation, One Election". She emphasized the benefits of simultaneous elections, including reduced expenditure, decreased burden on administration and security forces, and a focus on national interests. Swaraj urged women to support "One Nation, One Election," highlighting its potential to strengthen democracy and promote development.
