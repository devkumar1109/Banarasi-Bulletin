North India's Top Summer Escapes 2025

2025-02-25T08:55:00+00:00

News18 suggests ten must-visit destinations in North India for summer 2025. These include: * **Manali & Shimla:** Known for snow-capped mountains, valleys, and adventure sports. * **Rishikesh:** Offers adventure activities like rafting and yoga alongside spiritual experiences. * **Nainital:** Features natural beauty, peaceful atmosphere, and boating opportunities. * **Varanasi:** A spiritual destination for experiencing Ganga Aarti, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and ghats. * **Leh-Ladakh:** Offers adventure opportunities. The article also mentions: * **Mussoorie, Amritsar, Jaipur, and Dharamshala-McLeodganj** as other destinations worth considering.
