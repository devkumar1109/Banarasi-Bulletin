Varanasi Goes Meat-Free for Navratri

2025-03-29T08:31:00+00:00

For the first time, all meat, fish, and poultry shops in Varanasi will remain closed throughout the Chaitra Navratri festival, starting Sunday. The decision was made by the Varanasi Municipal Corporation's executive committee, led by Mayor Ashok Kumar Tiwari, to respect the religious significance of Navratri for Hindus. The Mayor emphasized the importance of following traditions and called for strict compliance. Earlier this month, the corporation enforced the closure of 26 non-vegetarian food shops near Kashi Vishwanath Temple. They also banned cigarette sales near religious sites and schools.
