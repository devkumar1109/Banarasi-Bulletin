Varanasi Goes Meat-Free for Navratri

2025-03-29T02:04:00+00:00

For the first time, Varanasi will close all meat, fish, and poultry shops during Chaitra Navratri, beginning Sunday, March 31st. Mayor Ashok Kumar Tiwari emphasized strict enforcement of this decision despite Eid coinciding with the festival. The executive committee also banned cigarette sales near religious sites and schools, and approved licensing fees for cigarette vendors. The mayor stated that Varanasi is a religious city and requested understanding from the Muslim community, emphasizing the significance of Navratri for Hindus.
