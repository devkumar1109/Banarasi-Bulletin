Jail Superintendent Accused of Harassment, Suspended.

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Umesh Singh, superintendent of Varanasi district jail, has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending an inquiry into a harassment complaint filed by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment and harassing her for not complying with his demands. She alleged that Singh made obscene gestures and pressured her for sexual favors in exchange for benefits, threatening her job if she refused. Singh's alleged misconduct and Kanojia's complaint sparked a departmental investigation. Kanojia and some other staff members were called to headquarters for statements. Kanojia's daughter filed a separate complaint against Singh, detailing the alleged harassment and threats. An ACP stated that the outcomes of the departmental probe will guide the next steps in the case.
