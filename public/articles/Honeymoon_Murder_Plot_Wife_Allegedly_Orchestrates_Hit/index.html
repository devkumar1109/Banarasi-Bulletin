Honeymoon Murder Plot: Wife Allegedly Orchestrates Hit

2025-06-09T07:24:00+00:00

Meghalaya police allege that Sonam Raghuvanshi, during her honeymoon, planned the murder of her husband Raja Raghuvanshi by hiring hitmen. Raja’s brother, Vipul Raghuvanshi, claims Sonam frequently communicated with Raj Kushwaha, a former employee potentially involved in the crime. Vipul expressed surprise at Sonam's alleged involvement, stating they seemed happy before the marriage. He revealed that Raja's mother, Uma Raghuvanshi, demands the death penalty for Sonam if found guilty.
