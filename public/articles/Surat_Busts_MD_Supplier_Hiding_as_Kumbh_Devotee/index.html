Surat Busts MD Supplier Hiding as Kumbh Devotee

2025-02-27T18:29:00+00:00

Surat Detection of Crime Branch (DCB) arrested Mohammad Layeeq Yusuf Shaikh, a wanted MD supplier, from Ambedkar Nagar district in Uttar Pradesh. Shaikh was wanted in seven NDPS cases registered in Surat. DCB teams posed as Maha Kumbh devotees for 15 days to track him, as he had been evading arrest and supplying drugs through various means. Shaikh, initially based in Mumbai, supplied MD to Surat drug peddlers and shifted to sending drugs through buses, trains, and individuals after being caught in an NDPS case in 2022.
