Varanasi Court: Life Sentences for BJP Leader's Murder

2025-06-13T15:34:00+00:00

A fast track court in Varanasi sentenced 16 men to life imprisonment for the murder of BJP leader Pashupati Nath Singh in 2022. Singh was killed after protesting against open drinking near his house, leading to an altercation that escalated into an attack by 30-40 people armed with sticks, rods, and iron rods. His son, Rajan Singh, who intervened, was also injured. Police arrested 16 of the 18 accused, while the trial for the remaining two is ongoing.
