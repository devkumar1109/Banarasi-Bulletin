Hindon Takes Flight: Varanasi, Patna Now Connected

2025-04-15T04:37:00+00:00

Air India Express will launch daily flights from Hindon civil terminal to Varanasi and Patna starting May 1st. The Airbus A320 aircraft will carry 180 passengers on each route. Bookings are open, with fares ranging from Rs 3,384 to Rs 5,573 depending on the route and date. Hindon terminal, which previously served mainly Udan flights, is experiencing increased passenger demand due to its convenience and proximity to Delhi-NCR. It now offers daily flights to major metro cities like Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, and Bengaluru, as well as services to Goa, Jammu, and Bhubaneswar. Star Air and FlyBig also operate flights from Hindon under the regional connectivity scheme. The terminal is undergoing upgrades to handle the increased traffic.
