Holi Night Murder: Girlfriend's Deadly Plot

2025-03-20T06:08:00+00:00

A man, Diljeet, was murdered in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh on Holi night, March 14. The victim was lured to a house by his girlfriend who conspired with her new lover to kill him. The girlfriend and her new boyfriend chased Diljeet on a TVS Splendor and shot him in the chest. CCTV footage captured the incident. Police tracked the accused using technical evidence and informants, and arrested both the girlfriend and her accomplice. The weapon used in the murder is yet to be recovered.
