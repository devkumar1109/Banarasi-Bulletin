Modi's Varanasi Vision: Inclusive Growth and Unity

2025-04-11T16:11:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for 44 projects worth Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, his parliamentary constituency. During his speech, Modi emphasized his government's commitment to inclusive development, contrasting it with the Opposition's focus on family-centric policies. He highlighted Varanasi's transformation over the past decade, becoming a hub for health and economic growth in the Purvanchal region. Modi cited the establishment of major hospitals and initiatives like Kashi Tamil Sangamam as examples of progress and national unity. He also paid tribute to Mahatma Jyotiba Phule on his birth anniversary, acknowledging his contributions to social equality.
