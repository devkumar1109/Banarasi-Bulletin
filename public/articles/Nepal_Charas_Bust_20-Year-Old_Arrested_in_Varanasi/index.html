Nepal Charas Bust: 20-Year-Old Arrested in Varanasi

2025-03-12T19:02:00+00:00

In Varanasi, authorities seized 1.534 kg of charas worth Rs 7.5 lakh. Alok Raj Chaubey, a 20-year-old from Buxar, Bihar, was arrested for the possession. Chaubey confessed to smuggling the charas from Nepal and selling it to local peddlers. He admitted to profiting significantly from this illegal trade for an extended period.
