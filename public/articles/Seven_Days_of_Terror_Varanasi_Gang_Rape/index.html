Seven Days of Terror: Varanasi Gang Rape

2025-04-07T08:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a 19-year-old woman alleged she was gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned home on April 4, reporting the incident to her family. Police have arrested six suspects and are pursuing the remaining accused. The woman's account details multiple instances of rape and assault at various locations, including cafes, hotels, and a warehouse. The charges filed against the accused include gang rape, outraging modesty, administering poison, wrongful confinement, and criminal intimidation.
