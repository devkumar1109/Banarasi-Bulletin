NSUI Protests Bhagwat at Maha Kumbh, Clashes With Police

2025-04-04T17:00:00+00:00

NSUI activists attempted to block RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat's convoy in Varanasi, leading to their detention by police. The activists aimed to offer Bhagwat Sangam water from the Maha Kumbh as a protest, claiming he only engaged in Hindutva politics and avoided its religious practices. They argued that millions of devotees participated in the Maha Kumbh, a symbol of Sanatan tradition, while Bhagwat remained absent.
