Bijnor Rape: Groom Held Hostage, Video Shared

2025-05-16T02:05:00+00:00

A 21-year-old woman was gang-raped by six men in Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh, in front of her fiancé on May 10. The perpetrators, from the same village, filmed the assault and shared the video on social media. The woman reported the crime to the police on Tuesday, leading to the registration of an FIR against three named and three unidentified individuals. Bijnor SP Abhishek Jha confirmed that three of the accused have been arrested, and the remaining suspects are being sought.
