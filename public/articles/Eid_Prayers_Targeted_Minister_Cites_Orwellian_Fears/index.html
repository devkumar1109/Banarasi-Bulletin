Eid Prayers Targeted: Minister Cites Orwellian Fears

2025-03-27T17:52:00+00:00

Union Minister Jayant Chaudhary criticized a Uttar Pradesh police directive against street prayers before Eid, calling it "policing towards Orwellian 1984." The directive, issued by the UP Police, restricts street processions and prayers ahead of Eid, citing concerns about potential law and order issues. Chaudhary expressed concern over the move, arguing that it infringes upon religious freedoms and sets a dangerous precedent.
