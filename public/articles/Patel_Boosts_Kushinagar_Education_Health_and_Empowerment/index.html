Patel Boosts Kushinagar: Education, Health, and Empowerment

2025-03-18T18:56:00+00:00

Governor Anandiben Patel visited Kushinagar and distributed 10 pre-school and health kits to strengthen Anganwadi centers and promote early childhood education. She emphasized self-reliance, skill development, and the importance of cleanliness for children. Patel stressed the key role of women's empowerment in India's development, advocating for equal education and opportunities for both sons and daughters. She also distributed benefits like residential lease certificates, ODOP toolkits, and Ayushman Bharat cards. Patel urged for universal cancer vaccination for girls aged 9-14 and emphasized the need for good healthcare for senior citizens. She called for strict action against social evils like dowry, child marriage, and drug abuse.
