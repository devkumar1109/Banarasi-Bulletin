MDMA Bust: Man Arrested Near Mumbai Mall

2025-04-12T12:24:00+00:00

On Tuesday night, Abhishek Arup Chaudhary, a 33-year-old man, was arrested near Seawoods Nexus Mall in Navi Mumbai for allegedly attempting to sell MDMA tablets worth approximately ₹2.4 lakh. Acting on a tip-off, the Anti-Narcotics Unit apprehended Chaudhary and seized 24 orange MDMA tablets weighing 11.32 grams. Chaudhary confessed to receiving the drugs from an African national named "Jeku" who is currently at large. A case has been registered against Chaudhary under the NDPS Act. Police are actively searching for "Jeku" and investigating the wider drug trafficking network in the region.
