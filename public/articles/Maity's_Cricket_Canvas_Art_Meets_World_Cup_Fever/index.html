Maity's Cricket Canvas: Art Meets World Cup Fever

2025-03-18T06:36:00+00:00

Indian artist Paresh Maity's exhibition 'Cricket's Greatest Canvas' showcased 12 original artworks inspired by the 2023 ICC Men's World Cup. The exhibition, hosted by Art be a Part in collaboration with the ICC, marked Maity's first solo show in Dubai. Maity, known for his vibrant watercolors and murals, captured the passion and spirit of cricket through his art. He was inspired by India's 1983 World Cup victory and used his art to explore the energy and excitement of the sport. The exhibition coincided with the ICC Champions Trophy held in Dubai, where India won the trophy on March 9. Maity painted five matches live and seven while watching television due to logistical constraints.
