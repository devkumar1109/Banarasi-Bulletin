Las Cruces Park Shooting: 3 Dead, 14 Injured in Fight

2025-03-23T07:00:00+00:00

A shooting at Young Park in Las Cruces, New Mexico, on Friday night left three dead and 14 injured. The incident occurred around 10 p.m. following a fight between two groups. Police are investigating and urge witnesses to come forward. 18 victims, aged 16 to 36, were affected. Seven were treated at the scene, while 11 were transported to local hospitals, with two pronounced dead at the scene. Four victims were taken to local hospitals, and one was flown to El Paso for treatment. Currently, three individuals have died, seven have been transferred to El Paso for further care, and four have been treated and released. The status of four other patients is pending.
