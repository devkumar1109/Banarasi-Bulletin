AP Engineering Faculty Embrace Industry Immersion

2025-05-17T21:31:00+00:00

Engineering faculty in Andhra Pradesh are increasingly taking up internships and industry immersion programs offered by IT and core companies. Driven by the rapid pace of technological advancement, companies are collaborating with educational institutions to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands. The DeepTech Naipunya Foundation recently launched a program for faculty members at the Tech Mahindra campus in Visakhapatnam, focusing on emerging technologies like AI, API development, and cybersecurity. These programs are crucial for equipping faculty with practical skills and keeping them abreast of industry trends. To facilitate this transition, some companies are encouraging colleges to hire additional faculty members, allowing existing faculty to undergo hands-on training.
