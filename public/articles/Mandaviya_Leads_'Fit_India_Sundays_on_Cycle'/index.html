Mandaviya Leads 'Fit India Sundays on Cycle'

2025-03-22T13:05:00+00:00

Union Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya will lead a nationwide 'Fit India Sundays on Cycle' movement in Lucknow on March 23rd. He will be joined by the Governor of Uttar Pradesh and the State Sports Minister, cycling 3 kilometers to promote a healthy and active lifestyle. The movement aims to combat obesity and has already been organized in 4200 locations with 2 lakh participants. It also encourages eco-friendly practices to reduce air pollution. The initiative involves various stakeholders, including athletes, coaches, and cycling enthusiasts, and has previously seen participation from prominent sports figures and celebrities.
