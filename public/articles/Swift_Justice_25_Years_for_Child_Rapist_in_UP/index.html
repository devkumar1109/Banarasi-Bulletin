Swift Justice: 25 Years for Child Rapist in UP

2025-05-30T02:53:00+00:00

In Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, a man named Khurshid Alam alias Mohammad Hamza was sentenced to 25 years rigorous imprisonment for raping a 5-year-old girl. This swift justice, delivered within 26 days of the crime, marks the first such instance in the state under the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita. Investigator Jai Prakash Yadav completed the investigation and filed a chargesheet within 24 hours. The 'Operation Conviction' campaign, along with effective advocacy by the Monitoring Cell and Prosecution, led to the speedy verdict. The court sentenced Alam under sections 65(2) BNS and 5M/6 POCSO Act, along with a fine of Rs 50,000.
