DECA: BHU Artists Paint Modern India's Soul

2025-04-15T18:43:00+00:00

Ten young artists from the Faculty of Visual Arts at Banaras Hindu University are showcasing their work in an exhibition titled 'DECA' to mark World Art Day. The three-day exhibition, which began on Tuesday at the Ahivasi Art Gallery, explores contemporary themes like human relationships, identity, and socio-cultural transitions. Each artist brings a unique perspective shaped by their experiences, reflecting both individual artistry and the strength of collaboration. Curated by Suresh Chandra Jangid, the exhibition delves into traditional Indian art forms, abstract symbolism, and environmental consciousness.
