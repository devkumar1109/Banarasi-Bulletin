Rooster Row: Land Dispute Turns Violent in Ballia

2025-03-24T14:44:00+00:00

In Ballia, India, a couple, Suraj Ram and Sheela, has been booked for allegedly poisoning and beating a one-year-old rooster belonging to their neighbor, Aarti Devi. The incident stemmed from a longstanding land dispute between the parties. Aarti Devi filed an FIR against the couple under various sections, including BNS 325 (mischief by killing animal), 115(2) (voluntarily causing hurt), 352 (intentional insult), and 351(3) (criminal intimidation). The rooster's carcass has been sent for postmortem examination. Police have initiated an investigation into the matter.
