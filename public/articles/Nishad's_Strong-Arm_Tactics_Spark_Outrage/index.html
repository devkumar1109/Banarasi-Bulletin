Nishad's "Strong-Arm" Tactics Spark Outrage

2025-03-19T09:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister Sanjay Nishad sparked controversy with remarks about using "strong-arm tactics" against police. During a speech in Sultanpur, Nishad boasted about breaking the hands and legs of seven inspectors, claiming he did so to assert his authority. He also stated he directly escalates complaints to higher officials, including the Chief Minister, if justice is delayed and threatened to transfer District Magistrates and Superintendents of Police who don't act accordingly. The Uttar Pradesh Congress has called for legal action against Nishad.
