IIT-BHU's Drone Network: Limitless, Offline Communication

2025-05-24T05:11:00+00:00

IIT-BHU in Varanasi has developed a drone-based communication system that operates without internet connectivity and offers virtually unlimited range. The system, led by Prof. Hari Prabhat Gupta, utilizes autonomous drones to create a dynamic mesh network for transmitting data across large distances, even in areas without infrastructure. This technology enables real-time image and text sharing in remote, obstructed, or high-interference regions, making it suitable for defense, border surveillance, disaster response, and more. The system is scalable, low-cost, and intelligent, with potential applications in national security, emergency rescue operations, and off-grid scenarios. IIT-BHU is continuing to develop the system, including integrating AI for autonomous path optimization and encryption for secure transmissions.
