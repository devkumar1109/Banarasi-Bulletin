Varanasi Fans Pray for India's Champions Trophy Glory

2025-03-09T04:28:00+00:00

Cricket fans in Varanasi performed an 'aarti' at the Sarang Nath Mahadev temple, praying for India's victory in the ICC Champions Trophy final against New Zealand. Fans and coaches are confident in India's strong spin attack and balanced team, highlighting the team's potential to win. Virat Kohli's performance is also expected to be crucial. The final, taking place at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium, will be a rematch of an earlier encounter between the two teams at the same venue.
