Power Cut Dims Energy Minister's Speech

2025-03-27T18:47:00+00:00

During an event in Mau district, Uttar Pradesh Energy Minister A K Sharma was caught in an embarrassing situation when a power cut plunged the area into darkness. This occurred while Sharma was speaking at the redevelopment ceremony of Hanuman Ghat Temple. He had to use the torchlight from mobile phones to continue his speech and even find his shoes. Following the incident, Purvanchal Vidyut Vitaran Nigam Limited (PVVNL) suspended the SDO and a junior engineer of the concerned area. Additionally, the superintending engineer and executive engineer were issued charge sheets and asked to provide explanations.
