PMAY-U: Luxury Homes For Varanasi's Weaker Sections

2025-06-05T13:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, 608 beneficiaries received high-tech, luxury homes under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U). The project, inaugurated by PM Modi in 2015, is the first to provide such advanced housing to economically weaker sections under the scheme. These homes come with amenities like sewage treatment plants, solar-powered lighting, parks, gyms, and 24/7 CCTV surveillance. Each unit cost approximately Rs 4 lakh, with beneficiaries paying Rs 2 lakh and the remaining cost subsidized by the state and central governments. Beneficiaries expressed gratitude for the opportunity to own quality homes, praising the amenities and improved quality of life.
