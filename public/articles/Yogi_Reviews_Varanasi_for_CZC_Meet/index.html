Yogi Reviews Varanasi for CZC Meet

2025-06-17T14:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Varanasi to review preparations for the Central Zonal Council (CZC) meet on June 24. The meeting, chaired by Union Home Minister Amit Shah, will bring together Chief Ministers of Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Uttarakhand, and Uttar Pradesh. Accompanying Adityanath were several Uttar Pradesh ministers, including Jal Shakti Minister Swatantra Dev Singh and Labour and Employment Minister Anil Rajbhar.
