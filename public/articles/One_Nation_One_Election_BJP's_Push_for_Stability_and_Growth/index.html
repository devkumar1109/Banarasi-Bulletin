"One Nation, One Election": BJP's Push for Stability and Growth

2025-02-27T18:45:00+00:00

BJP national spokesperson Sudhanshu Trivedi said "One Nation, One Election" aligns with the Constitution's original intent and is necessary for economic development and political stability. He argued that frequent elections hinder development and cited the 1996-97-98 elections, where Atal Bihari Vajpayee highlighted the detrimental effects of frequent polls. Trivedi emphasized the need for a stable government, citing the 2014-present period under Narendra Modi as an example of stable governance leading to long-term development plans. He also pointed to the success of the Jan Dhan Yojana, which has benefitted millions through direct benefit transfers. Trivedi believes "One Nation, One Election" will improve administrative efficiency, reduce inflation, and foster collaboration between ruling and opposition parties.
