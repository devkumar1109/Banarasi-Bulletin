NIT Student Dies by Suicide, Second in Months

2025-03-03T08:25:00+00:00

A final-year student, Ayansh Sharma, from Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, studying a dual degree program at NIT Hamirpur, was found dead by suicide in his hostel room on Sunday evening. Sharma was found hanging from a ceiling fan, and fellow students alerted the authorities. The incident is under investigation by the Hamirpur Superintendent of Police. This is the second suicide of a student at an NIT campus in recent months. Last September, a student at NIT Patna died by suicide in her hostel room due to personal and family reasons.
