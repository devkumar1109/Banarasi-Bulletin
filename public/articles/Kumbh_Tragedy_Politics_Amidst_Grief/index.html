Kumbh Tragedy: Politics Amidst Grief

2025-02-25T14:25:00+00:00

A stampede during the Maha Kumbh Mela in Uttar Pradesh resulted in 30 deaths. Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav criticized UP CM Yogi Adityanath's comment calling the opposition "vultures" who found "dead bodies" at the Kumbh. Yadav accused Adityanath of exploiting the event for political gain and urged sympathy instead of anger in response to the tragedy. Adityanath defended the state's arrangements, stating that everyone found what they were seeking at the Kumbh, while also criticizing the previous SP government for alleged neglect.
