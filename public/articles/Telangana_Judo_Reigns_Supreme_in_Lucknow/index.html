Telangana Judo Reigns Supreme in Lucknow

2025-02-26T19:20:00+00:00

Telangana won the overall championship in the sub-junior category of the National Para Judo Championships held in Lucknow. They secured two gold and one bronze medal. Uttar Pradesh came in second with one gold, three silver, and two bronze medals. Savita from Karnataka and Riku Gujjar from Madhya Pradesh were named the best judokas in the girls' and boys' categories, respectively. In the 2nd Late Pandit Ram Avtar Mishra State Open Hockey Tournament, Sports Hostel Lucknow secured a semi-final spot after defeating Sports College Lucknow 'A' 8-1 and Sports Hostel Jhansi 7-3. In the Lucknow Golf League 2025, Legends defeated defending champions Speed Chargers 4-1 to advance to the final.
