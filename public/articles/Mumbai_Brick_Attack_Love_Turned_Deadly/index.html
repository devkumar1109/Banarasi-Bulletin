Mumbai Brick Attack: Love Turned Deadly

2025-05-27T06:04:00+00:00

A 19-year-old man from Varanasi, Rahul Singh, was arrested for attacking a 17-year-old girl in Kandivali, Mumbai. He smashed a brick on her head and her phone after she ignored his calls and advances. The victim sustained serious injuries and was hospitalized. Singh traveled to Mumbai specifically because the girl stopped answering his calls. He has been charged under the BNS and POCSO Act.
