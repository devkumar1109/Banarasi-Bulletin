Varanasi Police Fail Performance Review, Face Retraining

2025-05-20T13:50:00+00:00

In Varanasi, over 150 police sub-inspectors were sidelined for retraining after failing a first-of-its-kind commissionerate performance review. Over 100 scored zero, and more than 50 couldn't draft basic FIRs. Performance was particularly poor in handling public grievances, with 100% failing to address complaints on the CM's dashboard, IGRS. 180 officers were removed from field duties and attached to the lines. 6 officers were deemed "star performers". Retraining includes physical drills, legal education, FIR writing, investigation techniques, and public interaction protocols.
