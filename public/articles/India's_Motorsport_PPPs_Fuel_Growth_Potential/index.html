India's Motorsport: PPPs Fuel Growth Potential

2025-06-15T19:00:00+00:00

Industry executives believe India's motorsport sector holds significant potential but needs strong public-private partnerships (PPPs) for sustainable growth. They cite the Uttar Pradesh government's deal to host MotoGP as a positive step, highlighting its potential to boost tourism, employment, and infrastructure. Despite past challenges with Formula One (F1), where regulatory and taxation hurdles led to its exit, executives emphasize the need for supportive policies and government backing to unlock motorsport's economic value and promote Brand India. JK Tyre & Industries, a major player in the sector, has invested approximately ₹200 crore in developing motorsport infrastructure and talent.
