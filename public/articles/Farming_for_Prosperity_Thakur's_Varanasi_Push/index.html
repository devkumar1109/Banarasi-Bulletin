Farming for Prosperity: Thakur's Varanasi Push

2025-06-12T22:30:00+00:00

Union Minister Ramnath Thakur visited Varanasi as part of the Viksit Krishi Sankalp Abhiyan, urging agricultural scientists to prioritize farmer prosperity by reducing farming costs and increasing incomes. He emphasized the importance of communicating agricultural innovations directly to farmers. The campaign focused on sustainable practices, including diversified farming, water-saving techniques, and beekeeping. Deputy Director General (Horticulture) Sanjay Kumar Singh directed officials to combat the use of adulterated seeds and chemicals. Experts highlighted the need for improved agricultural productivity in Uttar Pradesh, recommending practices like timely sowing, field leveling, and incorporating green manure crops. ICAR-Indian Institute of Vegetable Research and the International Rice Research Institute – South Asia Regional Centre showcased climate-smart agricultural innovations, including Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) technology.
