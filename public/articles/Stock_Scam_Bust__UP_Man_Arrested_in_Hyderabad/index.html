Stock Scam Bust: UP Man Arrested in Hyderabad

2025-04-22T07:24:00+00:00

Hyderabad cyber crime police arrested 23-year-old Prateek Shukla from Uttar Pradesh for his involvement in a stock trading fraud. Shukla acted as an account supplier for three bogus online investment companies: 5paisa Capital Ltd, Barclays, and Shanda Capital Group Ltd. The accused lured victims through a WhatsApp group, promising double or triple profits through stock market trading. They used fake screenshots to show virtual profits and later blocked withdrawals after victims invested large sums. Shukla is linked to nine cases across India, including one in Hyderabad. Police urge citizens to be cautious of online investment scams offering unrealistic returns and to report any instances of fraud.
