Sankat Mochan: Young Virtuosos Captivate Varanasi

2025-04-19T19:13:00+00:00

The third night of the Sankat Mochan Music Festival in Varanasi featured 20 young virtuosos. U Rajesh began with a Carnatic mandolin performance supported by pakhawaj and fusion instruments. Saurav-Gaurav Mishra performed Kathak dance accompanied by renowned musicians. Pt. Vishwa Mohan Bhatt and Pt. Salil Bhatt enchanted the audience with Raga Jog on Mohan veena and Satvik veena. Omkar Havaldar captivated the crowd with his melodious rendition of Raga Abhogi Kanara. Deepika Varadarajan from Chennai performed vocals in Raga Bageshree, while Pt. Abhay Rustam Sopori infused the evening with his santoor rendition of Raga Vibhas. Rajendra Sejwar presented a vocal performance, accompanied by Pt. Kubernath Mishra, Pt. Dharmnath Mishra, and Sandeep Mishra. Pt. Harish Tiwari concluded the concert with a khayal performance in Raga Bhatiyar.
