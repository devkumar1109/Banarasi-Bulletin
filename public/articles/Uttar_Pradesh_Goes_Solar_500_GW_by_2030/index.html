Uttar Pradesh Goes Solar: 500 GW by 2030

2025-04-30T04:33:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government signed MoUs with the Smart Energy Council of Australia and the Hinduja Group to boost solar energy production, aiming for 500 GW by 2030. The state aims to develop Bundelkhand, Vindhya, and surrounding areas into solar energy hubs, generating local employment in manufacturing, installation, maintenance, and grid integration. The Solar Mitra Yojana will build a skilled workforce for the growing sector. Since 2017, solar energy production has increased from 288 MW to over 2,800 MW. The state's Solar Energy Policy 2022 targets 2,200 MW in the near term, with solar parks planned in municipal corporations and solar grids along expressways and railway tracks.
