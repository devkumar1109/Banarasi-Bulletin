UP Board Exams: Eid Holiday, CCTV Watch, Results by April 25th

2025-03-31T06:09:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has declared March 31st as an Eid holiday, with the evaluation of class 10 and 12 answer sheets resuming on April 1st. CCTV surveillance will be implemented at evaluation centers to ensure fairness. The UPMSP will conclude the evaluation process by April 3rd. Students who missed their class 12 practical exams will have a chance to appear on April 7th and 8th. The UP Board results for over 20 lakh candidates are expected to be released by April 25th.
