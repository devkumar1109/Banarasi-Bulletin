Kumbh Concludes at Kashi Vishwanath

2025-02-26T18:41:00+00:00

The Prayagraj Maha Kumbh 2025 pilgrimage for seven Shaiva Akharas concluded with prayers at Kashi Vishwanath Temple on Mahashivratri. Grand Peshwai processions with deities, flags, and weaponry, marched through Varanasi. Naga Sadhus celebrated inside KV Dham, performing rituals and offering 'abhishek' to Lord Vishwanath. The processions began before dawn, culminating at Kashi Vishwanath Dham between 6 am and 9 am, where officials welcomed the seers and Sadhus.
