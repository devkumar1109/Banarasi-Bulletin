Modi's Varanasi Push: Development for All, Not Just Family

2025-04-11T16:11:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for 44 projects worth Rs 3,880 crore in his constituency Varanasi. Modi emphasized his government's commitment to "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas" (inclusive development), contrasting it with opposition's alleged focus on "parivar ka saath, parivar ka vikas" (family-centric development). He highlighted Varanasi's transformation over the last decade, citing improved healthcare and its emergence as a regional health hub. Modi also praised the city's cultural heritage, noting its GI-tagged products like tabla, shehnai, and sweets. He stressed Uttar Pradesh's progress, citing its top ranking in GI tagging and its growing international recognition.
