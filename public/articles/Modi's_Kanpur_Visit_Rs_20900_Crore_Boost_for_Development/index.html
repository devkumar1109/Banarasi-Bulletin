Modi's Kanpur Visit: Rs 20,900 Crore Boost for Development

2025-05-28T11:34:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Kanpur on May 30th to inaugurate and lay the foundation stone for development projects worth Rs 20,900 crore. Key projects include the inauguration of the Kanpur Metro's Chunniganj to Kanpur Central section, costing over Rs 2,120 crore, and the foundation stone for a 220 kV substation in YEIDA. Modi will also inaugurate power projects worth Rs 17,630 crore, including the Panki Thermal Power Extension Project and three units of the Ghatampur Thermal Power Project. Other inaugurations include two rail overbridges, a tertiary treatment plant, and the foundation stone for road projects under the Defence Corridor project.
