Lover Kills Girlfriend, Fled With Body

2025-03-03T02:25:00+00:00

A 22-year-old man, Vishal Sahani, has been arrested for the murder of his girlfriend, Ananya, in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh. Sahani and Ananya had been in a relationship since 2019, but Ananya's family had forced her into a marriage that ended shortly after. Following an argument at Ananya's rented house, Sahani allegedly killed her with a frying pan, packed her body in a trolley bag, and dumped it in bushes. Police apprehended Sahani at Jaunpur Junction while he was attempting to flee to Kerala.
