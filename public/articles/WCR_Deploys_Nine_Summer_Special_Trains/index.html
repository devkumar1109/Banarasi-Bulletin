WCR Deploys Nine Summer Special Trains

2025-04-06T13:32:00+00:00

West Central Railway (WCR) is operating nine summer special trains in April to handle the increased passenger volume during the season. These trains will run between various cities including Jabalpur to Ayodhya, Pune, Bandra Terminus, and Coimbatore; Rani Kamalapati to Saharsa, Agartala, and Hadapsar (Pune); Rewa to CSMT; and Sogaria to Danapur. Train schedules and departure days vary, with some services starting on April 8th or 10th. The special trains will run weekly, offering additional travel options for passengers during the peak season.
