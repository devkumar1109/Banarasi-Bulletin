Samajwadi Veteran Chhote Singh Yadav Dies at 80

2025-06-13T18:58:00+00:00

Chhote Singh Yadav, a founding member of the Samajwadi Party and three-time MP from Kannauj, passed away in a Lucknow hospital after a prolonged illness. He was 80 years old. Akhilesh Yadav, the party chief, expressed condolences, acknowledging Chhote Singh's significant contributions to regional politics. Chhote Singh served as an MP from 1980, 1989, and 1991 and was also an MLA from Chhibramau. He was known for his work in the cooperative movement.
