Modi's Funds Jab, CPI(M) Elects New Chief

2025-04-06T02:50:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi, during a visit to Rameswaram, indirectly criticized the Tamil Nadu government, stating that despite increased central funding, some entities complain about funds. Former Kerala minister M.A. Baby has been elected as the new general secretary of the CPI(M) at the 24th party congress meeting. The Karnataka BJP is launching a 16-day agitation across the state, starting Monday, to protest against price rise and the Muslim quota in government contracts. The Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) plans to challenge the Waqf Bill in the Supreme Court, while BJP leader Jagdambika Pal claims the Waqf Board was granted excessive powers during the Congress-led UPA government. The Bihar Congress chief stated there is no rift within the INDIA bloc following a meeting with Tejashwi Yadav. Rajeev Chandrasekhar unveiled BJP Help Desk logos in Kerala to promote central welfare schemes and welcomed new members into the party.
