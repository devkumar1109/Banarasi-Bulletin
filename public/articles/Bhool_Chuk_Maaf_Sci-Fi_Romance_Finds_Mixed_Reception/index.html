"Bhool Chuk Maaf": Sci-Fi Romance Finds Mixed Reception

2025-05-24T16:04:00+00:00

Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi's sci-fi romantic comedy "Bhool Chuk Maaf" had a decent opening day collection on Friday, May 23. The film, set in Varanasi, received mixed reviews from both audiences and critics. Some found the movie lacking freshness and similar to Rao's previous work, while others enjoyed its lighthearted comedy. The movie's theatrical release was also controversial.
