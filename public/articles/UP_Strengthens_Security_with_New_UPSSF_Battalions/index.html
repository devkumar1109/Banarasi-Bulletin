UP Strengthens Security with New UPSSF Battalions

2025-04-20T00:56:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government, under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, is strengthening the state's security infrastructure by constructing new campuses for the Uttar Pradesh Special Security Force (UPSSF). A third battalion will be established in Prayagraj on 42.03 acres of land in Arail area, with residential and non-residential facilities. Additionally, a fourth battalion will be built in Mathura. This move aligns with CM Adityanath's broader security strategy.
