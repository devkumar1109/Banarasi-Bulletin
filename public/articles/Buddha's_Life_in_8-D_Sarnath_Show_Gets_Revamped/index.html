Buddha's Life in 8-D: Sarnath Show Gets Revamped

2025-05-09T15:13:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is revamping the light and sound show at Sarnath to depict Lord Buddha's life using advanced 8-D technology. The updated show will feature 4K quality, 3-D effects, and a 7-channel audio system for an immersive experience. The duration will increase from 25 to 45 minutes to cover all locations associated with Lord Buddha's life. The show, initially in Hindi and English, will utilize an analogue digital surround system and laser-based technology for enhanced sound and picture quality. It is expected to launch in the next two months, offering a captivating experience for visitors. The original show, inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in November 2020, is currently screened on the Dhamkha Stupa in the late evening.
