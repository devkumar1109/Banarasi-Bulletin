Shiv Baraat Shift: Tradition vs. Kumbh Mela Crowd

2025-02-25T10:18:00+00:00

The annual 'Shiv Baraat' procession in Varanasi, typically held on Maha Shivratri, has been rescheduled to the following day due to expected large crowds from the Kumbh Mela. This marks the first change in the 42-year tradition. The decision has sparked political controversy, with Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai criticizing the BJP government for interfering with Varanasi's cultural practices. Rai has urged Prime Minister Modi, who is the MP for Varanasi, to ensure the procession follows its traditional schedule.
