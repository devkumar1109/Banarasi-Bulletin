Swaraj: "New India" Fearless, Backs "One Nation, One Election"

2025-04-24T18:04:00+00:00

BJP MP Bansuri Swaraj condemned the Pahalgam terror attack, stating that Narendra Modi's "new India" is fearless and won't be deterred by terrorism. Swaraj emphasized that Modi's India faces challenges with determination and prioritizes national interest. She spoke at a women's conference in Varanasi focused on "One Nation, One Election," highlighting its potential benefits like reduced election expenditure, decreased burden on administration and security forces, and a focus on national development. She urged women to support this policy and become flagbearers of the "One Nation, One Election" ideology.
