REACH Trial: Global Effort to Enhance Maternal Care

2025-04-12T18:46:00+00:00

The Mother and Child Health (MCH) department at Banaras Hindu University (BHU) launched the REACH Trial, a global clinical study aiming to improve maternal healthcare. The trial, conducted in collaboration with Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College (JNMC), will take place across 27 tertiary-level hospitals in seven countries: Argentina, Kenya, India, Nigeria, Uganda, South Africa, and the United Kingdom. 6,200 women will participate in the study over 54 months, with 12 months dedicated to preparation, 30 months to enrollment, and 12 months for analysis, publication, and dissemination of results. The JNMC Women's and Children's Health Research Unit is leading the trial in India, with BHU Varanasi, PGIMER Chandigarh, MGIMS Wardha, SCB Medical College Cuttack, and Ballari Medical College participating.
