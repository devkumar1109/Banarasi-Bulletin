Kashi Vishwanath Dham: Enhanced Security, Special Care

2025-04-20T18:49:00+00:00

To enhance security at Kashi Vishwanath Dham, a Rapid-Response Unit (RRU) will be deployed and an anti-drone system will be added. Security personnel will be trained in using anti-drone technology and crowd control. A "No Touch Policy" is being implemented, particularly for women, during frisking processes. Police officers are instructed to monitor junior staff to ensure compliance. Priority arrangements for differently-abled, elderly, and women visitors are being suggested, along with a special guidance system during peak times. Selfies with celebrities during duty hours are prohibited.
