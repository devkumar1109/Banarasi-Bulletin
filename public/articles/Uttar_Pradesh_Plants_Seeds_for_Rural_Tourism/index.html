Uttar Pradesh Plants Seeds for Rural Tourism

2025-05-16T16:29:00+00:00

On World Agri-Tourism Day (May 16, 2025), Uttar Pradesh celebrated with programs across 18 districts. These programs, part of the state's 'Agri Rural and Gange Gram Rural Tourism' initiative, aimed to promote rural tourism. Events included tree plantations, folk song competitions, boating competitions, and awareness programs on scarecrow building. Tourism and Culture Minister Jaiveer Singh highlighted UP's growing tourism sector, emphasizing the potential and development of 234 villages as rural tourism destinations. These villages offer local products, cuisine, and handicrafts, boosting local income.
