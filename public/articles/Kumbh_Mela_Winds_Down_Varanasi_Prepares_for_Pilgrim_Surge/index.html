Kumbh Mela Winds Down, Varanasi Prepares for Pilgrim Surge

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj concludes on Maha Shivratri, with Varanasi anticipating a massive influx of pilgrims. Authorities expect a record crowd due to pilgrims traveling from Prayagraj. To manage the surge, Varanasi has implemented strict traffic management and diversion plans, including barricades and vehicle restrictions. Security arrangements are in place to ensure smooth public movement and prevent traffic congestion. Designated holding areas at railway stations guide passengers to their trains, while waiting arrangements are provided for those traveling to other destinations.
