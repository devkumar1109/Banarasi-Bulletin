Modi Eyes 2036 Olympics, Varanasi Development Boom

2025-04-11T08:04:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi stated India is actively pursuing the opportunity to host the 2036 Olympic Games. He urged the youth of Varanasi to commence training with the aim of participating in the Olympics. Modi also inaugurated several development projects in Varanasi, with a total value exceeding Rs 3,880 crore. These projects include the CT ropeway. He emphasized the improved healthcare infrastructure in the region, highlighting the availability of major hospitals in proximity to residents. Modi also mentioned the Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana, a scheme providing free healthcare for senior citizens.
