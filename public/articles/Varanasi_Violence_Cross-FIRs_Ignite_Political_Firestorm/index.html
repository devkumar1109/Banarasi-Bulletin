Varanasi Violence: Cross-FIRs Ignite Political Firestorm

2025-04-13T19:13:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party functionary Harish Mishra was attacked in Varanasi, leading to multiple FIRs. Avinash and his accomplices were arrested after an FIR was lodged against them for attempted murder and rioting, based on Mishra's complaint. Two more FIRs were filed, one against Harish and other SP members for assaulting Avinash, and another by Karni Sena for defamation. Alok Kumar Singh, Karni Sena's district president, clarified that Avinash and his friend were not associated with the organization. The incident sparked political tension, with SP leaders demanding action against the attackers and Akhilesh Yadav condemning the state's law and order situation.
