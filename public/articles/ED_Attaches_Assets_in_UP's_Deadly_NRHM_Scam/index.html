ED Attaches Assets in UP's Deadly NRHM Scam

2025-03-11T20:58:00+00:00

The Enforcement Directorate (ED) has attached assets worth Rs. 89.84 lakh in connection with the NRHM scam in Uttar Pradesh. The scam involved the misuse of funds allocated for rural healthcare services, leading to irregularities, corruption, and the deaths of two Chief Medical Officers (CMOs). The CBI is investigating the case, alleging embezzlement of crores of rupees. The scam came to light after the murders of CMOs Vinod Arta and B P Singh in 2010 and 2011, respectively. The death of Deputy CMO Y S Sachan in custody further fueled public outrage over alleged corruption in the NRHM.
