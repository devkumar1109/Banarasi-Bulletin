South India Fears Political Dilution in Delimitation

2025-03-03T09:22:00+00:00

A political controversy is brewing in South India over proposed redrawing of Lok Sabha and Assembly constituencies (delimitation). Southern states, particularly Tamil Nadu, may face minimal changes or even a reduction in seats, while northern states like Uttar Pradesh and Bihar are projected to gain significantly. This is due to India's uneven population growth, with northern states experiencing higher growth rates compared to southern states which have successfully implemented population control measures. Southern states are concerned about the long-term impact of declining birth rates on political representation. Chief Ministers of Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu have publicly voiced these concerns.
