Adityanath Tightens Grip on Varanasi Security

2025-05-12T16:11:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed officials in Varanasi to increase security measures and vigilance against "urban Naxals" following the success of Operation Sindoor. He emphasized the need for strict and swift action against these elements. Adityanath also instructed officials to ensure timely issuance of vital certificates like birth and death certificates and to crack down on crime, including looting and chain snatching. The CM directed strict action against cow smuggling and illegal mining in border districts and ordered the auction of confiscated vehicles used by cow smugglers. He called for faster progress on various projects, including river rejuvenation and a new divisional office, and instructed the municipal corporation and development authority to improve drinking water and sanitation services.
