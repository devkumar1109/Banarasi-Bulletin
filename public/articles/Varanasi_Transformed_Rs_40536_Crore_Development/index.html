Varanasi Transformed: Rs 40,536 Crore Development

2025-03-27T19:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister Ravindra Jaiswal highlighted extensive development work in Varanasi over the past eight years, attributing it to the "double-engine" government of the BJP at the Centre and in the state. Over Rs 40,536 crore worth of projects, including roads, bridges, drinking water, sewage, education, health, and tourism, were completed in the district. An additional Rs 15,000 crore worth of projects are currently underway. Key achievements include 102 completed road and bridge projects worth Rs 22,408 crore, 34 Smart City projects worth Rs 877.65 crore, and 60 completed sewerage and drinking water projects worth over Rs 1,000 crore. He also mentioned the completion of 43 health projects including a Cancer Institute, a 430-bed super specialty hospital, and a maternity wing.
