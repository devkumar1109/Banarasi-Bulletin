IIT Kanpur Releases JEE Advanced AAT Results

2025-06-08T04:26:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur (IITK) has released the JEE Advanced 2025 Architecture Aptitude Test (AAT) results. Candidates can check their scores on jeeadv.ac.in using their registration number, date of birth, and phone number. To be eligible for BArch admission at IIT Kharagpur, IIT (BHU) Varanasi, and IIT Roorkee, candidates must qualify the JEE Advanced AAT and secure at least 75% in their class 12 board exam (65% for SC, ST, and PwD categories). The seat allocation will be based on category-wise All India Rank (AIR) in the JEE Advanced 2025. The Joint Implementation Committee of JEE Advanced 2025 will determine the qualifying cut-off score, but a rank list for AAT will not be released.
