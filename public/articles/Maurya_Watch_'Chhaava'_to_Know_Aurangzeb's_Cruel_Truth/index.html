Maurya: Watch 'Chhaava' to Know Aurangzeb's "Cruel" Truth

2025-03-21T09:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya urged West Bengal Chief Minister to watch the Hindi film 'Chhaava', stating it reveals the "cruel" reality of Aurangzeb. Maurya, who attended a screening with colleagues, highlighted the film's importance in understanding historical narratives. He criticized Akhilesh Yadav, leader of the Samajwadi Party, for not addressing a statement made by a party MP regarding a historical figure known for forced religious conversions. 'Chhaava', a biographical drama starring Vicky Kaushal, depicts the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
