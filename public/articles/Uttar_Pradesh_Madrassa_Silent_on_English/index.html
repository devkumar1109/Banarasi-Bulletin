Uttar Pradesh Madrassa: Silent on English

2025-04-28T07:40:00+00:00

A surprise inspection of a madrassa in Uttar Pradesh revealed that none of the Class 10 students could write their names in English. This finding highlights a potential lack of English language instruction in the madrassa. The news was published by The Hindu on April 28, 2025.
