Delhi Storm Diverts Flights to Varanasi

2025-05-25T18:31:00+00:00

Due to a severe storm in Delhi, five flights, including one international, were diverted to Varanasi's Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport on Saturday night. The diverted flights included four IndiGo flights bound for Delhi and one Air India flight. The storm grounded air traffic at Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport, resulting in the diversion of 49 flights, including 17 international flights.
