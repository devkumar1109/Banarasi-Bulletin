Delhi Storm Diverts Flights to Varanasi

2025-05-25T18:31:00+00:00

Due to a storm in Delhi, five flights were diverted to Varanasi's Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport late Saturday. The diverted flights included four IndiGo flights bound for Delhi and one Air India flight. The storm caused a temporary halt in air traffic at Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport, resulting in the diversion of around 49 flights, including 17 international flights. The diverted flights landed in Varanasi after 2 am and departed after a one-hour stay. Airport and airline officials made arrangements for the passengers' comfort.
