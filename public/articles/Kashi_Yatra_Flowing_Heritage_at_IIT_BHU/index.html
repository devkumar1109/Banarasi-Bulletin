Kashi Yatra: Flowing Heritage at IIT BHU

2025-03-07T15:02:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) in Varanasi inaugurated "Kashi Yatra," a prestigious cultural festival, themed "Cultural Flow: The Cultural Heritage." The festival, running from March 7 to 9, celebrates India's rich cultural legacy. IIT (BHU) director Prof Amit Patra emphasized the festival's importance in promoting cultural harmony and artistic expression, connecting the younger generation to their roots. The opening ceremony featured Bharatanatyam dancer Geeta Chandran and classical musician Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt. Kashi Yatra 2025 will showcase diverse Indian culture through music, dance, literature, theatre, and folk art events, competitions, and workshops.
