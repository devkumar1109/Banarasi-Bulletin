Viral Sex Videos Spark UP BJP Scandal

2025-05-30T16:04:00+00:00

Over 130 obscene videos allegedly featuring the son of a BJP District Mahila Morcha president have gone viral in Uttar Pradesh's Mainpuri, sparking a political controversy. The BJP leader has reportedly been unreachable, and opposition parties are criticizing the incident and demanding action. The son is accused of misconduct and disputes with his wife, who claims he burned her with cigarettes. Allegedly, the man created videos with his girlfriend and showed them to his wife as a form of abuse. The source of the leaked videos remains unknown, but speculation points to the man, his girlfriend, or other individuals. The BJP has remained silent on the issue, while opposition parties, including the Samajwadi Party, Bahujan Samaj Party, and Congress, are condemning the incident and targeting the ruling party.
