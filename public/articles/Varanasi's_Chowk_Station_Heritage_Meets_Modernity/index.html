Varanasi's Chowk Station: Heritage Meets Modernity

2025-05-02T14:40:00+00:00

The Commissioner of Police, Mohit Agarwal, inaugurated the renovated Chowk Police Station in Varanasi. The 125-year-old building was preserved and refurbished as a heritage building with modern facilities. The renovated station features an attractive entrance gate, a visitor room, and a park for tourists. Agarwal emphasized the importance of providing security, easy access, and smooth traffic flow for visitors and tourists. Future renovations of other police stations, including Dashashwamedh, Kotwali, will follow this model. Architect Priya Singh and others involved in the project were honored with certificates of appreciation.
