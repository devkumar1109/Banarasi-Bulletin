UP: Growth Engine, Doubling Income, Modi's Vision Realized

2025-04-14T10:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims his state has become a growth engine for India, contributing to national development and achieving Prime Minister Narendra Modi's vision of a developed India. He states that Uttar Pradesh is the first state to double its GSDP and per capita income in the past eight years. Adityanath attributes this growth to investor-friendly policies, removing unnecessary regulations, and improving law and order. He also highlights that Uttar Pradesh is now a revenue-surplus state, unlike previously when it struggled to pay employee salaries on time.
