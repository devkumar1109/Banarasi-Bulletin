Drunk Driving Rage: Vadodara Crash Claims Life

2025-03-15T15:09:00+00:00

A 23-year-old man, Rakshit Chaurasia, allegedly driving under the influence of alcohol, crashed his car into multiple vehicles in Vadodara, killing one woman and injuring four others. CCTV footage shows the speeding car colliding with two scooters, knocking the riders off and dragging them. Chaurasia claimed the car lost control due to potholes and denied being drunk. However, eyewitness videos show him appearing disoriented and saying "Another round?". He has expressed regret and wishes to meet the deceased's family. The incident has sparked outrage on social media, with Janhvi Kapoor condemning the act as "appalling and enraging."
