Varanasi: Clean Ganga, Smart City Focus

2025-03-17T18:47:00+00:00

Urban Development Minister AK Sharma reviewed municipal corporation and water corporation projects in Varanasi. He emphasized quality drain cleaning and progress on closing drains flowing into the Ganga. The minister requested updates on the Shujabad and Raman sewage plants and instructed the use of Phytoremediation and Geotube methods for drain treatment. Sharma ordered daily cleanliness inspections and water ATM installation in Varanasi. He also sought progress reports on sewer line installation surveys in 18 wards and directed timely completion of surveys for remaining wards.
