Silver Jubilee Feast: Hanuman Temple Celebrates

2025-04-12T03:13:00+00:00

The Pachmatha Hanuman Temple in Jabalpur is celebrating its silver jubilee with a three-day event. A unique feature is a "Maha Thali" containing 56 traditional Indian dishes from across the country, including Kashmir, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Bihar. Weighing 5,000 kg, a massive laddu is also being offered. The celebration coincides with Hanuman Jayanti, with devotees fasting, offering prayers, and chanting Hanuman mantras. Jagadguru Raghav Devacharya emphasized the importance of staying connected to Hanuman's spirit to understand and express Hindu identity.
