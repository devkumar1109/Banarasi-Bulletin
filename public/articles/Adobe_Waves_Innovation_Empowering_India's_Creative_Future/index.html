Adobe Waves Innovation: Empowering India's Creative Future

2025-05-01T17:59:00+00:00

Adobe announced major initiatives at the WAVES Summit to boost India's creative economy. With over 8,500 employees in India, Adobe plans to expand further with a new Noida office opening in early 2026. Key initiatives include: expanding Adobe Express with eight Indian languages, one million localised templates, and lower pricing; launching the AI-powered Creative Experience Studio (ACES) with TCS for personalised content; partnering with IICT to offer free Creative Cloud access and curriculum development; collaborating with Accenture to upskill students and create internship opportunities; and supporting WAVES Bazaar sellers with discounted subscriptions and tools. Adobe also emphasizes ethical AI governance in its expansion. These moves align with India's "Create in India, Create for the World" vision and strengthen Adobe's commitment to empowering Indian creators.
