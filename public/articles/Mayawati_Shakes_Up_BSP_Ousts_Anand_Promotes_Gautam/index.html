Mayawati Shakes Up BSP, Ousts Anand, Promotes Gautam

2025-03-04T02:47:00+00:00

Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) chief Mayawati removed Akash Anand as national coordinator and replaced him with Ramji Gautam and party vice-president Anand Kumar. Gautam, a Jatav Dalit and BSP's sole MP, previously served as national coordinator. He joined the BSP in 1990 and held various organizational roles, including sector-level president and Assembly unit general secretary in Lakhimpur Kheri. Mayawati later expelled Akash Anand from the party. Gautam is known for his early support of BSP ideology, attending rallies of Kanshi Ram and Mayawati during his youth. He later worked in the Reliance Group after completing an MBA.
