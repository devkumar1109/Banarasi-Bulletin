UP ATM Gang Busted in Chennai

2025-05-26T09:00:00+00:00

Chennai police apprehended a gang from Uttar Pradesh specializing in ATM tampering and cash theft. The gang was allegedly responsible for a series of ATM robberies in the city. Police investigations revealed sophisticated methods used by the gang to access and withdraw cash from ATMs. The bust highlights the growing threat of cybercrime and the need for enhanced security measures at ATMs.
