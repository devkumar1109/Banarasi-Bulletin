Cyberbullying Surge: UP Grapples With Online Harassment

2025-04-18T06:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh recorded 66,854 cyberbullying cases in 2024, averaging one every eight minutes. Over 45,000 complaints were filed by individuals aged 16 to 25, with Lucknow reporting the highest number (9,839). The state's Women and Child Safety Organisation (WCSO) has a dedicated cyber unit for women and children, equipped to handle these cases. They utilize a multi-tiered resolution system, starting with warnings and counseling, escalating to police involvement if necessary. This system has been successful, resolving 59,771 cases through warnings and counseling. The WCSO is also expanding awareness campaigns and collaborating with social media platforms to address the issue.
