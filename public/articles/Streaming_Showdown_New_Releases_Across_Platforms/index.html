Streaming Showdown: New Releases Across Platforms

2025-05-05T07:00:00+00:00

This week sees the premieres of several new movies and shows across various OTT platforms. Netflix offers "Britain and The Blitz," a documentary about World War II, the second season of survival competition "The Devil's Plan," and sports docuseries "Full Speed Season 2". Action film "Last Bullet" starring Alban Lenoir and Nicolas Duvauchelle, romantic series "Forever," and heartwarming comedy "Nonnas" featuring Vince Vaughn and Susan Sarandon also premiere on Netflix. Amazon Prime Video brings "Gram Chikitsalay," a family series starring Amol Parashar and Vinay Pathak, while JioHotstar releases the second season of crime mystery series "Poker Face" and comedic travel series "Conan O'Brien Must Go." ZEE5 will premiere the Bengali vigilante heist film "Bohurupi."
