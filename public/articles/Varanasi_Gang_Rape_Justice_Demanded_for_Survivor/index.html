Varanasi Gang Rape: Justice Demanded for Survivor

2025-04-16T10:22:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi was allegedly drugged and gang-raped by 23 men over six days (March 29 to April 4, 2025). 13 suspects have been arrested so far. Prime Minister Narendra Modi, during his visit to Varanasi, ordered the strictest action against the perpetrators and called for systemic reforms to prevent such crimes. A senior police officer was removed from the case due to allegations of negligence. The survivor's family and activists demand swift justice and stronger protections for women. Uttar Pradesh police have registered an FIR against all 23 men, and the case has been fast-tracked in court. Women's rights organisations have organized protests demanding justice and comprehensive reforms in police training, victim support, and accountability mechanisms.
