Navratri fervor: Security tightens at temples

2025-03-30T09:59:00+00:00

Navratri celebrations saw large crowds at temples in Sambhal and Varanasi on Sunday, with significant market activity. Authorities implemented tight security measures in both cities due to past communal tensions. Illegal slaughterhouses were closed near religious sites in Sambhal as a precaution. In Varanasi, heightened security included patrols and aerial surveillance of sensitive areas during the temple rituals.
