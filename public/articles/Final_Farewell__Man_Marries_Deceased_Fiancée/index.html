Final Farewell: Man Marries Deceased Fiancée.

2025-06-16T14:21:00+00:00

Sunny Madhesia symbolically married his deceased fiancée Priyanka Madhesia in Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh, hours before her funeral. Priyanka had committed suicide following a dispute with Sunny. Sunny fulfilled her last wish by performing wedding rituals, including applying sindoor and circling her bier. Both families consented to the unique ceremony. The couple had planned to wed in November, highlighting the tragic turn of events.
