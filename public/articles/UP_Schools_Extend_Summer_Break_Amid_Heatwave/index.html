UP Schools Extend Summer Break Amid Heatwave

2025-06-14T05:14:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Basic Education Council extended school holidays for classes up to 8th grade until June 30th due to the severe ongoing heatwave. The initial reopening date was June 16th. While schools remain closed, teachers and staff are required to be present. Academic activities will resume on July 1st. Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak urged citizens to take precautions during the heatwave, noting hospitals are on high alert for heat-related emergencies. The India Meteorological Department predicts gradual respite from the heatwave in Northwest India starting June 14th. Ayodhya District Magistrate Nikhil Tikaram Funde highlighted the administration's efforts to mitigate the heatwave's impact, including providing water and shade for residents and tourists.
