Yogi Adityanath Unveils Grand Lucknow Housing Scheme

2025-04-04T07:53:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the Anant Nagar Housing Scheme in Lucknow, a Rs 7,000 crore project spanning 800 acres. The scheme aims to provide world-class residential facilities, with 10,000 flats, 7 plotted sectors, and 5,000 EWS and LIG housing units. It will accommodate 1.5 lakh people and feature an integrated township design including educational institutions, high-rise buildings, and green spaces. A 130-acre park and EduTech City are also planned. Adityanath emphasized the government's priority on "ease of living" and highlighted the use of modern technology for faster construction. The scheme is expected to boost the state's economy and align with the government's vision of a USD one trillion economy.
