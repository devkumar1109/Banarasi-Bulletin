Kumbh Pilgrims Flood Varanasi for Shivratri

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela concludes on Maha Shivratri (Wednesday) in Prayagraj, leading to an anticipated surge of pilgrims in Varanasi. The Varanasi administration and police expect the largest-ever crowd in the district on Wednesday, as pilgrims traveling from Prayagraj will visit the Kashi Vishwanath temple. To manage the influx, a comprehensive traffic management plan is in place, including barricades, traffic diversions, and vehicle restrictions. Varanasi Police Commissioner Mohit Agarwal emphasized the security arrangements to ensure smooth public movement and prevent traffic congestion. Designated holding areas are set up at railway stations to guide passengers and manage the rush.
