UP Seeks New DGP: Contenders Emerge

2025-05-13T07:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh DGP Prashant Kumar is retiring on May 31, leading to a search for his successor. The state government recently amended DGP selection rules, but no committee has been formed yet. Senior IPS officers Sandeep Salunke, Daljit Singh Chaudhary, and Renuka Mishra are prominent contenders. Chaudhary (BSF DG), Alok Sharma (SPG DG), and Rajiv Krishna (UP Police Recruitment and Promotion Board and Director of Vigilance) are strong possibilities. Tilottama Verma, serving as DG Training, is a potential frontrunner as the state's first woman DGP. She has extensive experience, including a long stint with the CBI.
