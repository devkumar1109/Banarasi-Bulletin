Adityanath: Democracy Thrives on Unified Pillars

2025-03-28T08:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that good governance is achieved when the three pillars of democracy - the legislature, executive, and judiciary - function effectively in unison. He emphasized the importance of these pillars working together for the betterment of governance.
