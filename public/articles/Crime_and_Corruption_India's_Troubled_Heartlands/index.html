Crime and Corruption: India's Troubled Heartlands

2025-05-02T11:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh and Bihar have seen numerous high-profile crimes. The Shravan Kumar case in 2023 exposed caste prejudices, while the Vikas Dubey encounter in 2020 highlighted the gangster-politician nexus. Sushil Jaiswal poisoned six relatives in Bihar over two years for property, and a child trafficking ring was uncovered in Bhabua in 2021. The Unnao Rape and burn case (2017-2019) involved a BJP MLA, and the Muzaffarpur Shelter Home Horror (2018) saw the sexual abuse of 34 minor girls. The Gorakhpur Oxygen Tragedy (2017) resulted in the deaths of over 60 children due to oxygen supply failure. An Aurangabad honour killing in 2022 and the Sitamarhi Witch-hunt Case (2019) also shook the nation. The Kasganj Custodial Death (2021) raised questions about police brutality. Unsolved mysteries include the Ballia Headless Corpse Case, Bihar's 'Ghost Train Robberies', and the disappearance of the Mirzapur Red Diary.
