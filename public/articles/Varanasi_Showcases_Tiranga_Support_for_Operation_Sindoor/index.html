Varanasi Showcases Tiranga Support for Operation Sindoor

2025-05-24T18:47:00+00:00

A grand Tiranga Yatra was held in Varanasi on Saturday to support the Indian Armed Forces' successful execution of Operation Sindoor. Organized by the BJP, the event aimed to promote patriotism and solidarity. Thousands participated, including students, NCC, NSS, ex-servicemen, and officials. The yatra was flagged off at 4:55 pm by Deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya and BJP state president Bhupendra Chaudhary. It passed through Lohamandi, Lahurabir, and Jagatganj before culminating at Sampurnanand Sanskrit University.
