Varanasi Drenched in Color, Celebrates Holi with Festive Spirit

2025-03-15T20:05:00+00:00

Holi celebrations began in Varanasi on Thursday night with Holika bonfire and continued through Saturday with color play. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath sent special Kachnar flower gulal, prepared by the National Botanical Research Institute in Lucknow, to be offered to Lord Vishwanath Mahadev at Kashi Vishwanath Temple. Naga Sadhus and saints from seven Shaiva Akharas, who attended the Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj, celebrated Holi in Varanasi before leaving for their ashrams and mutts. Varanasi witnessed widespread Holi celebrations with families playing colors in their localities and youths gathering at ghats along the Ganga. Celebrations continued despite hot weather, and roads leading to prominent ghats remained crowded until late afternoon.
