Varanasi's Transformation: Tourism Revamp Underway

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

The Secretary of India's Ministry of Tourism, V Vidyavathi, has set a two-month deadline for officials to implement visible changes in Varanasi to enhance its tourism appeal. The focus is on improving 16-20 key locations, including temples, ghats, and Sarnath. Prioritized improvements include infrastructure development like ghat beautification, better roads, utilities, and transportation. The ministry also emphasizes promoting spiritual tourism, local crafts (like Banarasi sarees), and cultural events. Training for local artists, guides, and integrating local communities into tourism initiatives are key aspects. Online promotion and signage for tourists are also crucial. The meeting also discussed expanding tourist offerings to include yoga centres, wellness options, nearby eco-tourism sites, and historical landmarks.
