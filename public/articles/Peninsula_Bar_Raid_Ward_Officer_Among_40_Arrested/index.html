Peninsula Bar Raid: Ward Officer Among 40 Arrested

2025-04-29T18:43:00+00:00

A Kalyan-Dombivli ward officer, two contractors, and 34 others were arrested in a raid on Peninsula Bar in Ulhasnagar. The raid, conducted by Thane crime branch, found the bar operating beyond permissible hours with dancers allegedly engaging in "obscene behavior." 24 dancers, six staff members, and seven customers, including the ward officer, were apprehended. Approximately Rs 20,000 in cash was also recovered. After a six-hour panchnama, all individuals were released with notices as the case was registered as bailable.
