Yogi Adityanath Honors 'Matri Shakti' on Ram Navami

2025-04-06T06:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath performed the 'Kanya Pujan' ceremony at Gorakhnath Temple in Gorakhpur on Ram Navami. This ritual involves worshipping young girls as representations of Goddess Durga. Adityanath washed the feet of nine girls, adorned them with vermillion and sandalwood paste, offered gifts, and served them a special meal. The ceremony highlighted the importance of 'Matri Shakti' (the power of motherhood) and gender respect. Devotees and temple officials joined Adityanath in prayers and rituals. Ram Navami celebrations at the temple included cultural programs and spiritual fervor. Adityanath's participation in the 'Kanya Pujan' ceremony emphasizes his commitment to cultural values and community harmony.
