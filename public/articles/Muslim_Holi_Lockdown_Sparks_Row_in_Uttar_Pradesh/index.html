Muslim Holi Lockdown Sparks Row in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-12T15:26:00+00:00

A former top police official in Uttar Pradesh criticized a DSP in Sambhal for asking Muslims to stay indoors during Holi celebrations. The incident sparked controversy and raised concerns about communal harmony. The former police officer condemned the DSP's actions, stating they were inappropriate and could incite tension.
