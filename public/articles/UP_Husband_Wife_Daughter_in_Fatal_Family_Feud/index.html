UP Husband, Wife, Daughter in Fatal Family Feud

2025-04-27T06:58:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man named Nohar Chaudhary, his first wife Vidyavati alias Santola, and her married daughter Lakshmi have been arrested for the murder of Chaudhary's second wife, Sunita. According to police, the trio conspired to kill Sunita by strangling her with a dupatta and beating her with a stick. This incident highlights a serious crime and the potential for domestic violence within a family.
