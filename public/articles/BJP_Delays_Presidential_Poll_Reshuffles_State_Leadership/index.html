BJP Delays Presidential Poll, Reshuffles State Leadership

2025-04-16T18:36:00+00:00

The BJP will begin the election process for its national president after April 20, delaying it from February 2025 due to state elections. Current president JP Nadda's term was extended until June 2024. A recent high-level meeting with PM Modi and top BJP leaders, including Amit Shah and Rajnath Singh, focused on organizational reshuffling. Decisions on state presidents for Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, West Bengal, and two-three other states were made in the meeting, with announcements expected soon. The BJP is looking for new leadership in West Bengal ahead of the 2024 assembly elections.
