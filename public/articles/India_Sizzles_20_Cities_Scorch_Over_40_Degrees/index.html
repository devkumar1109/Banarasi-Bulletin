India Sizzles: 20 Cities Scorch Over 40 Degrees

2025-04-21T15:18:00+00:00

Over 20 Indian cities experienced temperatures exceeding 40 degrees Celsius on Monday, marking the hottest day of the season. Prayagraj recorded the highest temperature at 44.3 degrees Celsius, followed by Kanpur at 44 degrees Celsius. Other cities surpassing 40 degrees included Varanasi, Jhansi, Hamirpur, Churk, Sultanpur, Agra, Banda, and Lucknow. The Indian Meteorological Department predicts no respite from the heatwave this week, with both minimum and maximum temperatures remaining high. Isolated heatwave conditions are expected in southern Uttar Pradesh.
