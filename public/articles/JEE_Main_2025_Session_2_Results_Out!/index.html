JEE Main 2025 Session 2 Results Out!

2025-04-19T09:35:00+00:00

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has declared the JEE Main Result 2025 Session 2. Candidates can check their results and final answer key on the official website, jeemain.nta.nic.in. The April session of JEE Main 2025 was conducted from April 2 to 9 for various papers, including BE/BTech, BArch, and BPlanning. The result includes details like NTA scores, roll number, percentile, and more. Category-wise cutoffs have also been released, ranging from 93.09% to 100% for different categories like UR-ALL, EWS-ALL, OBC-ALL, SC-ALL, and ST-ALL. Major changes are expected in JEE Advanced 2025.
