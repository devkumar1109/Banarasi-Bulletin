Mahakumbh Mela Concludes: Millions Soak, Stars Shine, Clean Up Begins

2025-02-28T11:24:00+00:00

The 45-day Mahakumbh Mela in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, concluded on February 26th with 'Antim Snan' on Mahashivratri. Over 65 million devotees attended the event, including President Droupadi Murmu, Prime Minister Narendra Modi, and various Bollywood celebrities. Notable attendees included Katrina Kaif, Anupam Kher, Tamannah Bhatia, Rajkummar Rao, Vicky Kaushal, Hema Malini, Guru Randhawa, Akshay Kumar, and Adah Sharma. A cleanliness drive is currently underway at the Mahakumbh site, with images and videos showcasing the transformation of the once bustling area.
