Bihar Girl's Death: Modi's Seat Rocked by Protest

2025-03-10T09:09:00+00:00

Opposition MPs in India are protesting the death of a 17-year-old girl from Bihar who was found dead in her hostel room in Varanasi. They allege she was raped and murdered, demanding justice and comparing the incident to the 2020 Hathras case. The girl, who was preparing for the NEET exams, was found hanging on February 1st. Her family claims the police conducted the postmortem and funeral without their consent. The incident occurred in Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Lok Sabha constituency.
