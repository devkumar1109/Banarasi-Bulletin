Varanasi Embraces Masane Ki Holi: Ashes, Rituals, and Celebration

2025-03-11T06:12:00+00:00

Aghoris and Naga Sadhus celebrated Masane Ki Holi in Varanasi, marking the start of Holi festivities in the city. The celebrations at Harishchandra Ghat involved smearing pyre ashes, symbolizing the cycle of life and death. A procession featuring Baba Masan Nath culminated at the ghat, where devotees performed rituals and offered prayers. In Pandeypur, a unique Holika Dahan setup resembled a cremation ground, highlighting the essence of 'Masane Ki Holi'. The event was marked by chants, traditional drums, and the presence of tourists and devotees from around the world.
