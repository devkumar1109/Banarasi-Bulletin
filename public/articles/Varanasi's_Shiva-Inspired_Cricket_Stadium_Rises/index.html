Varanasi's Shiva-Inspired Cricket Stadium Rises.

2025-04-25T03:46:00+00:00

Varanasi is constructing a new 30,000-seater cricket stadium, the Varanasi International Cricket Stadium, costing Rs 452 crore. The stadium, expected to be completed in April 2026, will feature Shiva-inspired architecture, including a crescent-shaped canopy, trident-shaped floodlights, and seating arrangements inspired by Kashi's ghats. The stadium will have nine pitches, including four red soil, three black soil, and a couple of mixed wickets, a novelty previously adopted only by the Ekana Stadium and Narendra Modi Stadium. It will also house a 100-capacity trainee dormitory for a proposed academy. Other features include solar panels, a water recycling system, EV recharging stations, and a high-end drainage system. The stadium could host IPL matches as early as next season.
