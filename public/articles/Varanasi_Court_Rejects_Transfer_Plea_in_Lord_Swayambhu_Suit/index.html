Varanasi Court Rejects Transfer Plea in Lord Swayambhu Suit

2025-04-12T18:49:00+00:00

A Varanasi court dismissed a plea to transfer the Lord Swayambhu suit from the civil judge senior division court to the district court. The plea, filed by Anushka Tiwari and Indu Tiwari on behalf of Jyotirlinga Adi Vishweshwar Virajman, sought transfer under section 24(1)(b) of CPC. The court ruled that only a party to the suit can file a transfer application, as the applicants were not parties to the original suit, the transfer application was dismissed.
