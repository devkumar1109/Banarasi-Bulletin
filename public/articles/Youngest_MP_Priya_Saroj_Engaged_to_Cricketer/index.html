Youngest MP Priya Saroj Engaged to Cricketer

2025-06-09T11:26:00+00:00

Priya Saroj, 26, is one of India's youngest MPs and the youngest woman MP, representing the Machhlishahr constituency in Uttar Pradesh. She is the daughter of Samajwadi Party leader Tufani Saroj. Saroj defeated BJP leader B.P. Saroj in the 2024 election with a margin of 35,850 votes. Her campaign focused on unemployment, women's empowerment, farmers' rights, and marginalized communities. She is also a member of the Parliamentary Committee on Social Justice and Empowerment and served on the committee reviewing the Income Tax Bill, 2025. Before entering politics, Saroj earned a BA from Delhi University and an LLB from Amity University, practicing law at the Supreme Court of India. On June 8th, Saroj was engaged to Indian cricketer Rinku Singh, known for his performance in the IPL for the Kolkata Knight Riders.
