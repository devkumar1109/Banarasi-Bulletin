IIT-BHU, UPSIDA Join Forces for Bundelkhand Pharma Hub

2025-04-09T23:01:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority (UPSIDA) signed a MoU with IIT-BHU to develop Pharma Parks in Lalitpur district, Bundelkhand. Pharmaceutical industries will be established across 1,472 acres of land in five villages. IIT-BHU's Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology (DOPET) will be a key knowledge and technology partner, providing research, skilled human resources, and innovative solutions to companies operating within the parks.
