Holi's Harmony Threatened by Rising Communal Tensions

2025-03-16T23:30:00+00:00

Holi, traditionally a festival of unity, is facing increasing communal tensions, leading to a rise in riots across India. Recent weeks have seen clashes in two states, raising concerns about the future of the festival. In the past, Holi celebrations were marked by peace committees, comprising district officials, priests, and sadhus, who worked to ensure harmony. These efforts are now crucial as cultural dissensions threaten India's social fabric. The need for peace and harmony during Holi is more urgent than ever.
