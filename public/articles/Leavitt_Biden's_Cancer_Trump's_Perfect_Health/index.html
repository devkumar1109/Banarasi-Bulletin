Leavitt: Biden's Cancer, Trump's "Perfect" Health

2025-05-19T18:01:00+00:00

Karoline Leavitt, speaking at a press conference, addressed President Biden's recent cancer diagnosis, downplaying the issue and highlighting former President Trump's "perfect health." Leavitt criticized Biden's medical care, suggesting White House doctors may have missed early stages of his prostate cancer. In contrast, she praised Trump's recent physical exam results and his endurance, citing his ability to work full days despite a recent Middle East trip. Leavitt emphasized Trump's strong health and work ethic, drawing a contrast between the two leaders' approaches to their health and responsibilities.
