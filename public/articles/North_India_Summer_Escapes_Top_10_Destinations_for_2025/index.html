North India Summer Escapes: Top 10 Destinations for 2025

2025-02-25T08:55:00+00:00

News18 has published a list of the top 10 must-visit destinations in North India for Summer Vacation 2025. The list includes: Manali and Shimla in Himachal Pradesh for mountain views and adventure sports; Rishikesh and Nainital in Uttarakhand for adventure and natural beauty; Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh for spiritual experiences; Leh-Ladakh in Jammu and Kashmir for adventure; Mussoorie for scenic beauty; Amritsar for historical and cultural significance; Jaipur for forts and palaces; and Dharamshala-McLeodganj for a peaceful retreat.
