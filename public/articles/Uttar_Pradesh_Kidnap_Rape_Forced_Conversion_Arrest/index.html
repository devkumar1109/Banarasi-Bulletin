Uttar Pradesh: Kidnap, Rape, Forced Conversion Arrest

2025-06-10T16:10:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a girl was allegedly kidnapped, raped, and forced to convert. Four men, Meraj, Seraj, Meruddin alias Merajuddin Ansari, and Haseena Khatoon, have been arrested. The victim's mother filed a complaint accusing the four men of kidnapping. Police registered a case under sections of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act. The investigation revealed that Meraj raped the girl during her confinement, aided by Haseena Khatoon. Additional charges under BNS section 87 (kidnapping to compel for marriage) and the Uttar Pradesh Prohibition of Unlawful Conversion of Religion Act 2021 have been added.
