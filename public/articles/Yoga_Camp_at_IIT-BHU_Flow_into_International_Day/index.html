Yoga Camp at IIT-BHU: Flow into International Day

2025-06-03T19:52:00+00:00

IIT-BHU will host an 11-day yoga camp from June 11 to celebrate the 11th International Day of Yoga on June 21. Daily yoga sessions will be held from 6am to 7am at the Gymkhana Ground. Participants with 100% attendance will receive a certificate. Free registration is open until June 10th at the Gymkhana Office.
