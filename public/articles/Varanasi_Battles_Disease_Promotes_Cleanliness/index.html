Varanasi Battles Disease, Promotes Cleanliness

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, chikungunya, and diarrhea. The campaign, flagged off by ministers Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra, aims to prevent waterborne illnesses and promote cleanliness. Officials highlight a significant decrease in reported cases of these diseases compared to previous years. SVM Hospital Bhelupur introduced new facilities like an ultrasound machine, digital X-ray, and mental health services to improve medical care. District health authorities identified 68 dengue hotspots in urban and 56 in rural areas as focus points for the campaign. A Dastak campaign will run concurrently from April 10 to 31.
