UP Sets Stage for Handball Glory

2025-04-08T00:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-2025 in Lucknow. 75 teams with 1,341 players, including 336 women, are participating in the tournament. Adityanath highlighted the state's commitment to sports, mentioning over 65,000 sports kits distributed to villagers and heavy investment in sports infrastructure, including 84 stadiums, 67 multipurpose halls, and specialized facilities. Over 500 athletes have been recruited into the Uttar Pradesh Police force, and 740 players reside in 44 sports hostels across 18 districts. Three sports colleges in Gorakhpur, Lucknow, and Etawah provide world-class infrastructure and skilled trainers.
