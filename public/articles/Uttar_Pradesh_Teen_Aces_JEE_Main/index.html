Uttar Pradesh Teen Aces JEE Main

2025-03-06T04:50:00+00:00

Chirag Singh, a 17-year-old from Uttar Pradesh, topped the JEE Main January 2025 exam with a 100 percentile score. He studied at Allen Online and balanced school and coaching, dedicating time to revision and spending time with family for stress relief. Chirag aims to pursue Computer Science Engineering at IIT Bombay, followed by Delhi and Kanpur. Despite his impressive score, he will sit for the April session to potentially improve his marks. Over 12.58 lakh students took the JEE Main exam, with 14 candidates achieving a 100 NTA score, including one female candidate.
