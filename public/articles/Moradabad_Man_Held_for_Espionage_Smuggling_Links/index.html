Moradabad Man Held for Espionage, Smuggling Links

2025-05-19T05:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Special Task Force (STF) arrested Shahzad from Moradabad for alleged espionage for Pakistan's ISI. Intelligence inputs revealed his involvement in cross-border smuggling and espionage activities threatening national security. Shahzad allegedly shared sensitive information about India's security infrastructure with his ISI handlers and engaged in smuggling cosmetics, clothes, spices, and other goods across the India-Pakistan border. His activities are believed to have been ongoing for several years, with frequent trips to Pakistan raising security concerns. He has been booked under Indian law provisions related to espionage and national security breaches.
