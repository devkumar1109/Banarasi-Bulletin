Fake Cop Frauds Women Out of Lakhs

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

A 26-year-old man, Munfed, from Mathura was arrested in Varanasi for duping multiple women of lakhs of rupees by posing as a police public relations officer. He used fake appointment letters and a police uniform to lure victims seeking government jobs. Munfed confessed to scamming at least seven women, including Anita Yadav of Mainpuri who lost Rs 16 lakh. He collected personal details of police officials from social media to create his fraudulent persona. The Deputy Commissioner of Police (crime) is investigating the case.
