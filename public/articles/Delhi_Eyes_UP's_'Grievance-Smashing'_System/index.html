Delhi Eyes UP's 'Grievance-Smashing' System

2025-05-30T18:46:00+00:00

Delhi government is interested in adopting Uttar Pradesh's Integrated Grievance Redressal System (IGRS). A team from Delhi presented the UP model to Delhi Chief Minister Rekha Gupta, highlighting its effectiveness in speeding up, transparenting, and holding accountable grievance resolution across departments. Delhi officials visited UP's National Informatics Centre to experience the system firsthand. UP's IGRS, which includes a CM helpline, features end-to-end digital grievance handling, real-time escalation matrices, and analytics-driven monitoring. The Delhi government praised UP's structured approach and expressed interest in adapting it for its own use, aiming to empower citizens and improve governance responsiveness. UP's system also includes a district ranking mechanism used by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath to track grievance resolution progress.
