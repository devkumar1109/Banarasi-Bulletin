Bakery Owner Kills Girlfriend, Found in Drain

2025-02-27T15:11:00+00:00

A 25-year-old woman from Uttar Pradesh, Priya Singh, was found murdered in Vasai, Maharashtra. Her boyfriend, Amit Singh, a 28-year-old bakery owner, has been arrested. Priya went missing on December 29, 2024, from Gorakhpur. Her phone was later found on a Delhi-bound train, suggesting an attempt to mislead the investigation. Phone tower locations led authorities to Vasai, where the crime branch of Mira Bhayandar Vasai Virar police apprehended Amit. He confessed to strangling Priya during an outing and dumping her body in a drain.
