UP Cabinet: Healthcare, Wheat, and Stamp Reform

2025-03-10T10:53:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Cabinet approved 19 proposals including wheat procurement at the MSP of Rs 2,425 per quintal from March 17 to June 15. 6,500 procurement centers will be established across the state. The Cabinet approved the establishment of a new medical college in Ballia, utilizing 14.05 acres of land from the district jail. A nursing college will be set up in Bulandshahr using 4,570 sq meters of land from the Government Agricultural School. Additionally, revised sanctions were granted for a 300-bed gynaecology block with a 100-bed paediatric block at the Uttar Pradesh Institute of Medical Sciences in Saifai. The government will withdraw physical stamp papers valued between Rs 10,000 and Rs 25,000 to prevent misuse.
