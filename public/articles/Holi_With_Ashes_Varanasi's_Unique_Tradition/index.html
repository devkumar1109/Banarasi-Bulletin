Holi With Ashes: Varanasi's Unique Tradition

2025-03-11T10:40:00+00:00

Masan Holi, a unique tradition involving playing Holi with ashes from funeral pyres at Varanasi's Manikarnika Ghat, is underway. The celebration, observed on the day after Rangbhari Ekadashi, saw a large crowd including Sadhus and Aghoris. Safety measures are in place with police, NDRF, and PAC teams deployed along the Ganga and cameras monitoring the situation. Other Holi celebrations include a 1700-year-old tradition with the Lord Shiva and Nandi procession in Jaipur, and a 'Holi Milan' programme in Delhi attended by various political leaders. Rangbhari Ekadashi was observed at Baba Kashi Vishwanath Dham with devotees sprinkling colors on idols of Baba Kashi Vishwanath and Mata Gauri. Banke Bihari temple in Vrindavan also commenced Holi celebrations.
