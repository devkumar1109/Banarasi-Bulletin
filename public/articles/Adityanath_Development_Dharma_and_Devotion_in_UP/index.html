Adityanath: Development, Dharma, and Devotion in UP

2025-05-27T15:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath held 'Janta Darshan' at Gorakhnath temple in Gorakhpur, addressing public grievances. Previously, he inaugurated and laid the foundation for 528 development projects worth Rs 1,515 crore in Sant Kabir Nagar. CM Adityanath announced plans to develop Baba Tameshwarnath Dham into a grand pilgrimage site, similar to Ayodhya and Kashi, with a dedicated corridor and no displacement of residents. He highlighted the success of similar projects like Ram Temple, Kashi Vishwanath Dham, and Maa Vindhyavasini Dham, emphasizing the government's commitment to Sanatan Dharma. The inaugurated projects cover sectors like tourism, roads, drinking water, healthcare, education, sports, and urban infrastructure. CM Adityanath also visited an exhibition showcasing Bakhira's brass industry, praising the success of the ODOP scheme in supporting local artisans.
