Yogi Adityanath Warns Pakistan: "Terrorism Will Swallow You"

2025-05-14T06:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath stated that "one day terrorism will swallow Pakistan." He linked this to the success of Operation Sindoor, a recent military operation conducted by India in response to Pakistani actions. Yogi also asserted that those who threaten India's security will face severe consequences. He praised Prime Minister Narendra Modi for visiting the Adampur Air Base and boosting the morale of the armed forces. During the operation, India reportedly utilized BrahMos missiles.
