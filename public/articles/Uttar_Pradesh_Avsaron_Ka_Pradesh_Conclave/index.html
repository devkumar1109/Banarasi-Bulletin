Uttar Pradesh: Avsaron Ka Pradesh Conclave

2025-03-20T03:26:00+00:00

NDTV India will host 'Uttar Pradesh - Avsaron Ka Pradesh', a conclave in Lucknow on March 20, 2025. The event will feature Uttar Pradesh policymakers, including Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya, and industry leaders. Discussions will focus on key sectors like culture, tourism, sports, and the state's business landscape. The conclave will also reflect on the recent Mahakumbh. Notable attendees include actors Rajpal Yadav and Akshara Singh, singer Malini Awasthi, and business leaders Mayank Agrawal and Vimal Shukla.
