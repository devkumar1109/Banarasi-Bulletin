Varanasi Gets Cricket Stadium, Urban Township

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi will get its first urban township spanning 150 acres near the new international cricket stadium in Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority approved the project at its recent meeting. The stadium, costing Rs 451 crore and with a 30,000-seat capacity, is expected to be completed by the end of 2025 and host its first international match in 2026. The VDA also inaugurated a new record room with space for 80,000 files, bringing its total storage capacity to 2,55,266 files. A citizen app and complaint management system portal were also launched, allowing residents to register complaints easily. Additionally, the board approved a new access road between Transport Nagar Scheme and NH-2, and contracted RITES Ltd for designing and supervising construction projects. Amendments to building bylaws were also approved to align with government guidelines for capital investment.
