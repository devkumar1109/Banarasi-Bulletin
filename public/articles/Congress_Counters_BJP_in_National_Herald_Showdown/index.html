Congress Counters BJP in National Herald Showdown

2025-04-21T09:26:00+00:00

Congress leaders will hold press conferences in 57 cities from April 21-24 to counter the BJP's allegations in the National Herald case. Prominent leaders like Pawan Khera, P. Chidambaram, Shashi Tharoor, Gaurav Gogoi, and Ashok Gehlot will participate. The Congress calls the charges "politically motivated" and a "lie-sheet," asserting no crime was committed. They plan to hold "Samvidhan Bachao" rallies nationwide from April 25 onwards, culminating in a door-to-door campaign from May 20-30. The ED filed a chargesheet against Sonia Gandhi, Rahul Gandhi, and others, accusing them of laundering Rs 988 crore in the National Herald case.
