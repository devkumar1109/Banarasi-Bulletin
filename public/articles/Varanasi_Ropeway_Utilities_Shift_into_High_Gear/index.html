Varanasi Ropeway: Utilities Shift into High Gear

2025-02-28T07:35:00+00:00

The Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) is accelerating utility shifting for its upcoming ropeway project. VDA Vice Chairman Pulkit Garg convened a meeting with officials from NHLM, Jal Nigam, and other departments to expedite the process. Discussions focused on traffic diversion plans, sewer and stormwater pipeline construction, and trial pit construction at Godowliya crossing. Jal Nigam is tasked with preparing line diagrams for sewer and stormwater pipelines by March 1st. NHLML will conduct a joint inspection of the pipelines at Godowliya crossing on February 8th and submit a report. Garg emphasized the need for swift completion of utility shifting to ensure smooth project progress.
