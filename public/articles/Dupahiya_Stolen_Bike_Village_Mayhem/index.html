Dupahiya: Stolen Bike, Village Mayhem

2025-03-07T06:00:00+00:00

"Dupahiya," a new web series starring Gajraj Rao and Renuka Shahane, is set in the fictional village of Dhadakpur, Bihar. The show revolves around a stolen motorcycle, which becomes a catalyst for various subplots. The series focuses on the preparations for a wedding and the efforts to find the stolen motorcycle. The show boasts a large ensemble cast, including Shivani Raghuvanshi, Bhuvan Arora, Yashpal Sharma, and Brijendra Kala. "Dupahiya" is described as a "clean, family entertainment" show with a lighthearted tone and a focus on rural life. The series aims to leave viewers smiling and features elements of humor, romance, and social commentary.
