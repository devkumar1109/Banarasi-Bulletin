Noida Amps Up Women's Safety with Pink Booths

2025-04-01T18:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh DGP Prashant Kumar inaugurated 11 new pink booths in Noida, aimed at enhancing women's safety. These booths are strategically located in areas like Sector-104 Market, metro stations, and educational institutions. Kumar emphasized their role in providing a safe space for women to register complaints and receive immediate police assistance. The inauguration also included 11 police outposts and a multi-purpose building at the GB Nagar police commissionerate. Video walls for enhanced surveillance and smart policing were also unveiled at 26 police stations. DGP Kumar lauded the commissionerate system in cities like Noida, highlighting its success in reducing crime rates significantly. He praised GB Nagar Police for its achievements against gangster networks, cybercrime, drug trafficking, and illegal call centers.
