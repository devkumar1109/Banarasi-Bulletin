Farmer Leader, Son, Brother Gunned Down in Road Rage Fury

2025-04-08T10:47:00+00:00

In Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh, farmer leader Pappu Singh, his son Abhay Singh, and brother Pinku Singh were shot dead in a triple murder following a road dispute. The incident occurred in the Hathgaon police station area when a heated argument between Pappu Singh and former village head Suresh Kumar escalated. Suresh Kumar's son and associates opened fire, killing all three men. Police suspect political rivalry between Suresh Kumar and Pappu Singh contributed to the confrontation. Inspector General (IG) of Police Prem Kumar Gautam confirmed the identities of the victims and is investigating the case. Following the murders, villagers protested and blocked roads, demanding action against the accused. A heavy police presence has been deployed to maintain order.
