MP Meets Cricket Star: Priya Saroj Weds Rinku Singh

2025-06-02T06:54:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party MP Priya Saroj is marrying cricketer Rinku Singh on November 14th at Varanasi's Hotel Taj. Priya, daughter of three-time MP Tufani Saroj, won her first parliamentary election in 2024, defeating BJP's BP Saroj. A lawyer by training, she previously practiced at the Supreme Court. Priya and Rinku will be engaged on June 8th. The wedding is expected to be attended by prominent Samajwadi Party politicians, cricketers, film personalities, and industrialists.
