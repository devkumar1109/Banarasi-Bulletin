Delhi's Cyber Cops Battle Tech Crime Wave

2025-05-24T21:49:00+00:00

Delhi's 15 cyber crime police stations, staffed by around 700 officers, received over 100,000 complaints last year. They are facing a shortage of staff and dealing with tech-savvy criminals. Cyber criminals operate across borders, making investigations complex. A typical day at a cyber police station involves officers handling numerous complaints of financial fraud, often exceeding 25-50 per day per station. Officers conduct raids and investigations across India to apprehend suspects and recover stolen funds. The national cybercrime helpline number, 1930, is used to register complaints and initiate action.
