BJP Leader Murdered Over Drinking Row

2025-06-13T15:34:00+00:00

A fast track court in Varanasi sentenced 16 men to life imprisonment for the murder of local BJP leader Pashupati Nath Singh in October 2022. The incident occurred after Singh protested against open drinking near his house, leading to an altercation that escalated into a violent attack by 30-40 people armed with sticks, rods, and iron rods. Singh and his son, Rajan, who intervened, were seriously injured. Singh died at the hospital, while Rajan recovered after months of treatment. Police arrested 16 of the 18 accused, with the trial for the remaining two ongoing.
