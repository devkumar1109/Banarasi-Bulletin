AyodhYatra App: Virtual Pilgrimage to Holy City

2025-03-06T06:45:00+00:00

The Ayodhya Development Authority (ADA) launched the AyodhYatra app in partnership with 1 Play Group, offering a virtual pilgrimage experience. Available on Android and iOS, the app features live darshans, personalized pujas, and 360-degree temple tours of sites like the Ram Mandir, Kanak Bhawan, Hanuman Garhi, and Saryu Ghats. Users can also participate in live-streamed pujas from over 20 temples and experience Ayodhya's festivals through metaverse technology. The app aims to expand to include other spiritual sites like Kashi Vishwanath, Krishna Janmabhoomi, and Mahakaleshwar. AyodhYatra also offers personalized astrology services. This initiative aims to make Ayodhya a global spiritual destination and capitalize on the rising trend of digital devotion.
