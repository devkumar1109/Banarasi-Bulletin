UP's Law-Order Boom: Convictions Soar, Crime Drops

2025-02-24T13:24:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath praised the state's improved law-and-order situation, citing a drop in crime rates according to the National Crime Records Bureau. Adityanath highlighted successful convictions, particularly in crimes against women, with 51 death sentences and thousands of prison terms issued between July 2023 and December 2024. He attributed the progress to reforms, including strengthening the Anti-Terrorism Squad and recruiting over 1.56 lakh police personnel. The state's legal system, with its focus on convictions and enhanced security measures, is now considered a model for the country.
