Shami Receives Death Threat, Police Investigate

2025-05-05T18:02:00+00:00

Sunrisers Hyderabad pacer Mohammad Shami received a death threat via email during IPL 2025. The email, sent by Rajput Sindar, also demanded a ransom of Rs one crore. Shami's brother, Haseeb, filed an FIR with the Cyber Crime Police Station in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh. The FIR was registered under sections of the Indian Penal Code, Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008, and police are investigating the matter. Shami has taken six wickets in nine matches for SRH in the current IPL season.
