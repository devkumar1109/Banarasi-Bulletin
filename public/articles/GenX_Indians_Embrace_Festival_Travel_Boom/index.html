GenX Indians Embrace Festival Travel Boom

2025-03-15T00:00:00+00:00

GenX Indians are increasingly planning trips around festivals, both domestically and internationally, seeking unique cultural experiences. Popular domestic festivals like Holi in Mathura-Vrindavan, Pushkar, and Varanasi, Diwali in Varanasi, and Durga Puja in Kolkata are attracting more visitors each year. Lesser-known regional festivals like Dol Jatra in West Bengal, Wangala in Meghalaya and Assam, Bundi Utsav and Matsaya festivals in Rajasthan, and Manjal Kuli in Kerala are also gaining popularity. Festival tourism is driving year-round travel demand as Indians immerse themselves in diverse traditions. Families, including multiple generations, are choosing festival trips as a way to bond and explore new destinations.
