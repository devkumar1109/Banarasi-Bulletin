Maha Kumbh Healthcare Legacy: Tech Boost for UP.

2025-03-05T09:07:00+00:00

World-class medical equipment from the Maha Kumbh Mela Central Hospital will be distributed across Uttar Pradesh to improve healthcare. This includes advanced machines, beds, and a 10-bed AI-enabled ICU. The equipment will be allocated to districts based on their needs, pending approval from the Prayagraj divisional Commissioner. The initiative aims to modernize healthcare services in the state, following the successful treatment of over 6 lakh patients during the Maha Kumbh. Additionally, a unique language translation device used at the Central Hospital, capable of translating 22 regional and 19 international languages, will be implemented in other hospitals to improve communication between doctors and patients.
