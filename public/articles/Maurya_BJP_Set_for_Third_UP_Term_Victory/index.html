Maurya: BJP Set for Third UP Term Victory

2025-03-25T19:02:00+00:00

Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya claims the BJP-led alliance will secure a third consecutive term in Uttar Pradesh in the 2027 elections. He attributes this to the Yogi Adityanath government's achievements in transforming the state from one plagued by crime and rioting to a developed and secure state. Maurya highlights the effective implementation of PM schemes benefiting women, youth, farmers, and employment, citing projects like Kashi Vishwanath Dham and Namo Ghat as examples of the government's development efforts in the region. He believes the opposition, even if united, cannot defeat the BJP due to the public's satisfaction with the current government's performance.
