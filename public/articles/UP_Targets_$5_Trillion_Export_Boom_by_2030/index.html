UP Targets $5 Trillion Export Boom by 2030

2025-04-21T21:01:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is set to approve a new export policy for 2025-30, aiming for $61.5 billion (₹5 trillion) in exports by 2030, a threefold increase. While this is an ambitious goal, especially considering global trade challenges, Uttar Pradesh has potential in sectors like electronics and engineering, where it has a strong industrial base. The state's key export destinations include the US, UAE, Vietnam, UK, Nepal, and Germany.
