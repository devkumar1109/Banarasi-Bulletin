Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train: Speedy Link on Track

2025-03-24T13:29:00+00:00

The Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train is a proposed 760 km high-speed rail corridor connecting Varanasi and Howrah. It will pass through key cities including Buxar, Arrah, Patna, Gaya, Dhanbad, Asansol, Durgapur, and Burdwan. The train is designed for speeds up to 350 km/h, aiming to reduce travel time between the cities to just over two hours. The project is in the Detailed Project Report (DPR) stage with surveys underway to finalize the route and assess land requirements. In Bihar, approximately 260 km of the route will be elevated. Construction is expected to begin after railway approval.
