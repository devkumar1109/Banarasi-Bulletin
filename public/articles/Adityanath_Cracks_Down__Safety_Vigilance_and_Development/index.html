Adityanath Cracks Down: Safety, Vigilance, and Development.

2025-05-12T16:11:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed officials to increase vigilance against "urban Naxals" following the success of Operation Sindoor in Varanasi. He emphasized strict and swift action against such elements. Adityanath also instructed officials to ensure timely issuance of vital certificates like birth and death records. He ordered a crackdown on looting and chain snatching incidents. The CM directed strict action against cow smuggling and illegal mining in border districts. He also expressed dissatisfaction with the pace of some projects and instructed departments to expedite work on projects like the Varuna River rejuvenation and the integrated divisional office.
