Mizoram Girls Ace Hockey, Eyed for Olympics

2025-04-23T14:37:00+00:00

Five under-16 girls from Mizoram are among 15 selected by the Sports Authority of India (SAI) for training at National Centres of Excellence (NCOEs) for hockey. This selection follows the 2024-25 season of the ASMITA league, aimed at identifying future stars for the 2026 and 2032 Olympics. The girls, chosen alongside players from Haryana, Jharkhand, Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh, represent the growing passion for hockey in India. SAI High-Performance Director Piyush Dubey highlighted the league's role in discovering talent like Sunelita Toppo and Sujata Kujur, now representing India. In the final phase of the ASMITA sub-junior league, SAI Shakti won the team championship, defeating Madhya Pradesh Hockey Academy 2-1.
