Kumbh Pilgrims Flood Varanasi for Maha Shivratri

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela concludes in Prayagraj on Maha Shivratri, Wednesday, with a significant surge in pilgrims expected to travel to Varanasi. Varanasi anticipates its largest-ever crowd on Maha Shivratri due to the influx from Prayagraj. The Varanasi administration and police have implemented comprehensive crowd management plans, including strict traffic management and diversion strategies to mitigate congestion. Barricades and vehicle restrictions are in place, with designated holding areas at railway stations to manage passenger flow. Security arrangements have been made to ensure smooth movement and prevent traffic congestion.
