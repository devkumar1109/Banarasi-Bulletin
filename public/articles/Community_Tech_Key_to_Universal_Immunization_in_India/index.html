Community, Tech Key to Universal Immunization in India

2025-04-26T23:00:00+00:00

The UNDP India chief emphasizes the crucial role of community participation and digital innovation in achieving universal immunization. They stress that these factors are vital for ensuring equitable access to vaccines and overcoming challenges in reaching marginalized populations. The emphasis on digital innovation highlights the potential of technology to enhance vaccine delivery and monitoring systems, particularly in remote areas. The UNDP India chief's statement underscores the need for a multi-pronged approach that combines traditional outreach strategies with modern technological solutions to effectively combat vaccine hesitancy and ensure widespread immunization coverage.
