Trump Reshapes HHS, Sparking Health and Pension Fears

2025-04-01T16:00:00+00:00

President Trump has ordered a major restructuring of the U.S. Health and Human Services Department (HHS), potentially leading to the firing of up to 10,000 employees. An executive order has stripped federal health agencies, including the FDA, CDC, NIH, and CMS, of collective bargaining rights. These cuts could impact healthcare oversight, medical research, and public health funding. Critics warn of potential harm to disease prevention, pandemic response, and federal healthcare programs. Concerns have also been raised about the impact of misinformation regarding Social Security, fueled by individuals like Elon Musk, who have labeled it a Ponzi scheme. This has caused anxiety and insecurity among seniors reliant on these programs.
