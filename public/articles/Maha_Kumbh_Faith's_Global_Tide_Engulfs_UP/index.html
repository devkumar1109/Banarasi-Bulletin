Maha Kumbh: Faith's Global Tide Engulfs UP

2025-02-25T12:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's Maha Kumbh festival drew a record-breaking 64 crore devotees, surpassing attendance at global religious gatherings like Mecca and the Vatican. CM Yogi Adityanath attributed this success to the state's enhanced global profile and redefined image as a spiritual tourism destination. He highlighted 16 crore devotees visiting Ayodhya alone in 52 days, with millions flocking to other religious sites. Adityanath addressed concerns regarding the Ganga's pollution, citing scientific reports confirming its suitability for bathing. He refuted claims of missing individuals, attributing the smooth reunification of pilgrims to advanced systems.
