Uttarakhand Renames Places, Sparks Political Row

2025-04-01T06:22:00+00:00

Uttarakhand Chief Minister Pushkar Singh Dhami announced the renaming of 11 places across four districts, citing a desire to honor Indian culture and great personalities. The changes include renaming Aurangzebpur to Shivaji Nagar in Haridwar district and Miyanwala to Ramji Wala in Dehradun district. Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav criticized the move, suggesting Uttarakhand should be renamed "Uttar Pradesh-2". Acharya Dr. Vipin Joshi of the Tapkeshwar Mahadev Temple supported the renaming, calling it a step aligning with Sanatan culture.
