Varanasi Walls Bloom with Art and Color

2025-03-05T17:47:00+00:00

The Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) has launched a wall painting project in residential colonies to promote art and culture. The project began in Shastri Nagar and Kanshi Ram residential colonies, with 14 walls being painted in Shastri Nagar. Prominent artists from India and Cambodia are participating. The VDA is using its establishment fund to cover the cost of the workshops, which are being organized in collaboration with the Curve and Colours organization. The colorful murals are expected to enhance the city's tourist appeal and create a unique aesthetic vibe.
