Bay of Bengal Shakes: Tremors Felt in Kolkata and Puri

2025-02-25T06:29:00+00:00

A 5.1 magnitude earthquake struck the Bay of Bengal at 6:10 AM, with tremors felt across West Bengal, including Kolkata. The earthquake's depth was recorded at 91 km by the National Center for Seismology. While no damage has been reported, residents shared their experiences on social media. Kolkata, located in Seismic Zone III, faces moderate earthquake risks. The tremor was also felt near Odisha’s Puri, a region prone to seismic activity.
