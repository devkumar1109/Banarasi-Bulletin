UP's Padma Shri Awardees Honored by Adityanath

2025-04-28T18:46:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath congratulated four individuals from the state who received the Padma Shri award from President Droupadi Murmu. The recipients are Dr Shyam Bihari Agrawal, Hriday Narayan Dikshit, Ganeshwar Shastri Dravid, and Dr Satyapal Singh. They were recognized for their significant contributions to Indian culture and society. Adityanath praised their achievements, stating that their work embodies Indian philosophy and life values. The awardees dedicated the honor to their supporters and community.
