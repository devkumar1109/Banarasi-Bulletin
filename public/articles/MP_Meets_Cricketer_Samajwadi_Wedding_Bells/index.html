MP Meets Cricketer: Samajwadi Wedding Bells

2025-06-02T15:54:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party MP Priya Saroj and cricketer Rinku Singh are getting married. Their engagement is scheduled for June 8th in Lucknow, followed by the wedding on November 18th at the Taj Hotel in Varanasi. Priya Saroj, a first-time MP representing the Machhlishahr constituency, is known for her legal expertise and youthful energy. She defeated BJP veteran BP Saroj in the 2024 election. Rinku Singh plays for the Kolkata Knight Riders in the IPL. Priya's father, Tufani Saroj, a three-time MP and current MLA, is overseeing the wedding preparations. While no celebrities have been officially invited, Samajwadi Party leaders Akhilesh Yadav and Dimple Yadav, as well as Rinku Singh's cricketing colleagues, may attend.
