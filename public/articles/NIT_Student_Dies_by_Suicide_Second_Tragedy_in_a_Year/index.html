NIT Student Dies by Suicide, Second Tragedy in a Year

2025-03-03T08:25:00+00:00

A final-year student of NIT Hamirpur, Ayansh Sharma, from Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, died by suicide on Sunday evening. He was found hanging in his hostel room. Police are investigating the incident and have informed Sharma's family. This incident follows a similar case last year where a student at NIT Patna committed suicide in the girls hostel, citing personal and family reasons.
