Foxconn Eyes Bigger India Play with Uttar Pradesh Deal

2025-04-15T10:51:00+00:00

Foxconn, Apple's top global vendor, is in talks with the Uttar Pradesh government to establish an electronics manufacturing unit in the state. Discussions were initiated during land allocation for Foxconn's HCL joint venture, Vama Sundari, which will build a semiconductor assembly and test facility. The Uttar Pradesh government offered 300 acres of land under YEIDA for Foxconn's new unit. While details about the type of products to be manufactured are yet to be finalized, the discussions are in their early stages. Foxconn, the largest iPhone producer in India, has not commented on the development.
