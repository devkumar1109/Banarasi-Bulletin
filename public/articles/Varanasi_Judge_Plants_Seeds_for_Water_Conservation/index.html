Varanasi Judge Plants Seeds for Water Conservation

2025-06-05T18:57:00+00:00

On World Environment Day, Varanasi District Judge Jai Prakash Tiwari inaugurated a plantation drive and awareness rally, emphasizing water conservation. He highlighted the limited availability of drinkable water and the importance of saving it. Tiwari suggested storing water from RO machines for household use and planting trees to control temperature and save electricity. The event was organized at IIT-BHU, led by Dean Prof NK Mukhopadhyay, Administrative In-charge Prof Ranjit Mohanty, and Registrar Rajan Srivastava.
