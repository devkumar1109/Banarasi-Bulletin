Varanasi Judge Plants Seeds for Water Conservation

2025-06-05T18:57:00+00:00

On World Environment Day, Varanasi District Judge Jai Prakash Tiwari inaugurated a plantation drive and awareness rally, emphasizing the importance of water conservation. Tiwari highlighted the limited supply of drinkable water and the dependence of life on freshwater storage. He urged residents to conserve water at home, suggesting ways to reuse water from RO machines and plant trees to create a green environment. The event was organized at IIT-BHU, led by Dean of Faculty Affairs Prof NK Mukhopadhyay, Administrative In-charge Prof Ranjit Mohanty, and Registrar Rajan Srivastava.
