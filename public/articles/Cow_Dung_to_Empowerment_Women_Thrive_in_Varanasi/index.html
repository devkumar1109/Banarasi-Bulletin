Cow Dung to Empowerment: Women Thrive in Varanasi

2025-03-08T13:00:00+00:00

Rita Tiwari's initiative in Varanasi is empowering women through self-employment by utilizing cow dung and urine to create various products. Started in 2005, the project now employs around 1,000 women who work from home, producing items like lamps, incense sticks, statues, and cleaning supplies. The women's monthly income has risen from ₹2,000-₹4,000 to ₹15,000-₹16,000. Rita's inspiration stemmed from Ajit Mahapatra and training in Deolapar. During the pandemic, she aimed to support both cows and people by creating employment opportunities. Products are sold online through platforms like Trade India and Flipkart, generating a monthly turnover of around ₹2 crore.
