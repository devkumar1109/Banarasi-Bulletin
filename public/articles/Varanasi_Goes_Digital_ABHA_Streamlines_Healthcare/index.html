Varanasi Goes Digital: ABHA Streamlines Healthcare

2025-03-05T17:44:00+00:00

The Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABHA) is digitizing healthcare services in Varanasi to improve patient experience and treatment transparency. ABHA provides a unique 14-digit health ID, similar to a bank account, storing medical records and prescriptions. Over 30.5 lakh ABHA IDs have been created in the district, with 1,378 healthcare facilities and 2,313 doctors registered. Patients can access their records digitally using their ID and password, enabling seamless connection with government health programs. ABHA counters allow patients to obtain OPD slips without queuing. Citizens can add family member details to their ABHA accounts. Verified doctors and hospitals receive medical records through the ABHA health ID card.
