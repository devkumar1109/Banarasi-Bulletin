IIT Bhilai: Rs 1,400 Crore Expansion, Doubling Capacity

2025-05-16T09:20:00+00:00

IIT Bhilai will undergo a major expansion with a Rs 1,400 crore investment, increasing its student capacity to 2,750. The expansion includes two new engineering departments, one science department, a large advanced laboratory, three hostels, a visitor hostel, and residential quarters for staff. A 15,000 square meter research park, Chhattisgarh's first, will be established. This phase will also see the construction of a sports complex, an administrative building, and a large auditorium. The institute expects to receive double the laboratory equipment compared to the previous phase, featuring state-of-the-art technology.
