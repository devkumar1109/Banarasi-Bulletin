Yogi's Sports Push: Stadiums, Colleges, and Talent Hunt

2025-05-23T06:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced plans to build one mini-stadium in each assembly constituency and one sports college in each mandal. He emphasized completing existing sports infrastructure projects on time and with high quality. CM Yogi directed officials to accelerate the construction of sports colleges in the remaining 12 divisions, aiming to develop them as centers of excellence. He proposed integrating sports competitions with MP-MLA level tournaments and linking them with the State Sports University. The CM stressed a transparent selection process for athletes and called for organizing competitions at various levels to identify talent.
