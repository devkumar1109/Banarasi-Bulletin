Varanasi Gunman Caught in 24-Hour Dragnet

2025-04-04T17:02:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Rajesh Singh alias Banti, wanted in 33 cases, was arrested within 24 hours of shooting at Nishant, a man returning home from work. The incident occurred after a minor dispute over overtaking. Police recovered an illegal pistol, live and used cartridges from Banti, who sustained a bullet injury during the cross-fire. He was apprehended during an encounter under operation ‘Chakravyuh’. Banti admitted to the shooting, which took place near Lamahi village.
