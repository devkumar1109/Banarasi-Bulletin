UP Gears Up: Sports Surge Under Adityanath

2025-04-08T00:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-2025 in Lucknow, featuring 75 teams and 1,341 players. The CM highlighted the state's focus on grassroots sports, noting that over 65,000 men and women in Mangal Dal across villages have received sports kits. He emphasized the government's investment in sports infrastructure, including 84 stadiums, 67 multipurpose halls, and specialized facilities. Adityanath also mentioned the recruitment of over 500 athletes into the Uttar Pradesh Police force and the establishment of three sports colleges with world-class infrastructure and trainers. He revealed that the state's third international cricket stadium is under construction in Varanasi.
