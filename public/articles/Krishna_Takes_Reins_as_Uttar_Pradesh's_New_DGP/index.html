Krishna Takes Reins as Uttar Pradesh's New DGP

2025-06-01T04:20:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991-batch IPS officer, has been appointed as the new Director General of Police (DGP) of Uttar Pradesh. He replaces Prashant Kumar, whose retirement took effect on Saturday, May 31, 2025. Krishna was serving as the Director General (DG) of Vigilance in the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board prior to his appointment as DGP.
