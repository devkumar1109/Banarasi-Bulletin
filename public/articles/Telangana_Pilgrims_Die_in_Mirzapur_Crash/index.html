Telangana Pilgrims Die in Mirzapur Crash

2025-02-24T19:44:00+00:00

Four people, including three pilgrims from Telangana, died in a road accident in Mirzapur. The accident occurred when their SUV collided with a motorcycle, then crashed into a truck. The pilgrims, Venkat Reddy, Mal Reddy, and Chitthi (35), were traveling back to Telangana after attending the Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj. The motorcycle rider, Ravi Shankar Rajbhar (20), also died in the crash. Three others in the SUV sustained serious injuries and were hospitalized.
