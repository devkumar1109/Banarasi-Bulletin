Buddha Relic Embarks on Sacred Journey to Vietnam

2025-05-01T09:21:00+00:00

A large gathering offered prayers to the Holy Buddha Relic at the National Museum in New Delhi before it embarked on a special Indian Air Force flight to Vietnam. The relics will be in Vietnam from May 2 to May 21, traveling through four cities, including Ho Chi Minh City, where the United Nations Day of Vesak (UNDV) event will be held. Vietnam's Ambassador to India and Sri Lanka's Acting High Commissioner also offered prayers. The Indian delegation will be led by Minister of Parliamentary Affairs and Minister of Minority Affairs, Kiren Rijiju. The relics will be enshrined and venerated at important sites, including Thanh Tam Monastery, Ba Den Mountain, Quan Su Monastery, Hanoi and Tam Chuc Monastery.
