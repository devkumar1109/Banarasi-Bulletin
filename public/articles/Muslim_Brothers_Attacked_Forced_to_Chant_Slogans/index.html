Muslim Brothers Attacked, Forced to Chant Slogans

2025-03-12T13:49:00+00:00

Two Muslim brothers, Niyaz Ahmed and Khurshid Ahmed, were brutally assaulted in Varanasi's Dindayalpur village while collecting donations for Holi. A group of about a dozen individuals attacked them with sharp weapons and sticks, leaving them with severe injuries. Niyaz was attacked after returning from prayers and was forced to chant “Jai Shri Ram”. Khurshid, who intervened, was also beaten and forced to chant the slogan. A viral video shows Niyaz with a bandage on his head and bloodstained shirt. An FIR has been filed at Sarnath Police Station, and legal proceedings will follow.
