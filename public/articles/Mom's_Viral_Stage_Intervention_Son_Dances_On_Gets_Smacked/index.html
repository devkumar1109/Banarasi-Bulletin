Mom's Viral Stage Intervention: Son Dances On, Gets Smacked

2025-04-15T13:15:00+00:00

A video from a village celebration in eastern Uttar Pradesh or Bihar went viral showing a mother chastising her son for dancing on stage with performers. The son, who joined the orchestra dancers, was hit repeatedly with a stick by his mother, who stormed the stage in front of the entire crowd. Despite the mother's anger, the son continued to grin throughout the incident before fleeing the stage. The viral video, shared on Instagram, has sparked laughter and debate online, showcasing the dynamics of village life and the strong authority of mothers.
