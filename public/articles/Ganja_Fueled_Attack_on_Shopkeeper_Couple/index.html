Ganja Fueled Attack on Shopkeeper Couple

2025-02-24T18:08:00+00:00

In Penamaluru, Krishna district, a shopkeeper couple, Mothrapu Abraham and Ratna Kumari, were attacked Sunday night by three youths under the influence of ganja. The accused, Venigalla Dharani (21), Erra Abhishek (24), and a 24-year-old from Tadigadapa, attacked the couple with sticks, beer bottles, and bricks after Abraham asked them to wait while attending to other customers. The couple sustained severe injuries and were hospitalized. Police registered a case based on the couple's complaint and launched an investigation.
