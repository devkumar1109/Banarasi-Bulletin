26 Years Later, Killer Caught for 1999 Ransom Murder

2025-04-26T00:58:00+00:00

Vinod Kumar Gupta (49) was arrested in Uttar Pradesh for the 1999 murder of Bhiwandi powerloom owner Jigar Mahendra Mehta. Gupta, along with Raju Mehta (absconding) and Kamlesh Uppadhyay (already arrested), kidnapped Mehta for ransom. After rendering Mehta unconscious, they killed him and dumped his body in Thakur Pada farmland. They then demanded Rs 10 lakh from Mehta's family. Gupta, who was a student at Jija Bai College at the time, was lured into the crime by Raju Mehta. He had been hiding in Delhi and Uttar Pradesh for the past 26 years and ran a medical store.
