Yogi Adityanath Kicks Off All India Police Handball

2025-04-07T11:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-25 in Lucknow, with 75 teams participating. CM Yogi emphasized the importance of sports for a healthy lifestyle, noting that Prime Minister Modi has played a key role in promoting sports culture across India. He highlighted that Uttar Pradesh has recruited over 500 medal-winning athletes who have represented India in international competitions into the Uttar Pradesh Police force through a special recruitment process. The Chief Minister also inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for various developmental projects worth Rs 91 crore at the Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology in Gorakhpur.
