CSSH: Affordable World-Class Healthcare

2025-03-26T07:03:00+00:00

Chhatrapati Shivaji Subharti Hospital (CSSH) in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, is a tertiary care hospital focused on making world-class healthcare affordable. Unlike corporate hospitals prioritizing profits, CSSH aims to make high-end medical care accessible to middle and lower-income families. The hospital utilizes state-of-the-art technology and a research-driven approach to provide cutting-edge treatments at significantly lower costs. CSSH prioritizes patient benefit by investing in the latest medical advancements, even if they are not immediately profitable.
