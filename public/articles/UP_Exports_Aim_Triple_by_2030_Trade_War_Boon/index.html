U.P. Exports Aim Triple by 2030: Trade War Boon

2025-04-26T07:32:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government aims to triple its exports by 2030, capitalizing on the US-China trade war. The Yogi Adityanath government sees this as an opportunity for the state, citing improved law and order, infrastructure, and a skilled workforce. Chief Minister Adityanath highlights the success of the One District One Product (ODOP) scheme, which has boosted exports from Rs 88,967 crore to over Rs 2 lakh crore since its launch. Infrastructure developments, including expressways, airports, and waterways, are expected to further attract investment.
