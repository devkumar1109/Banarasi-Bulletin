Love, Lies, and a Brutal Murder

2025-03-25T02:21:00+00:00

In Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh, a 25-year-old man named Dilip Yadav was murdered by a contract killer just 15 days after his marriage. His wife, Pragati, and her lover, Anurag alias Bablu alias Manoj Yadav, hired the killer for Rs 2 lakh. Dilip succumbed to his injuries on March 21 after being found injured in a field on March 19. Pragati was unhappy in her arranged marriage to Dilip and continued a relationship with Anurag. She allegedly told Anurag that if Dilip was eliminated, they could live a comfortable life together. The accused woman, her lover, and the killer have been arrested.
