Uttar Pradesh: Exporting Big, Aiming for ₹5 Trillion

2025-04-18T20:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh aims to significantly boost its exports, targeting over ₹5 trillion by 2030. The state is revising its export policy for 2025-30 to achieve this goal. The revised policy will focus on expanding into new markets in Latin America and Eastern Europe. To support this growth, the government plans to increase incentives for exporters and enhance infrastructure support.
