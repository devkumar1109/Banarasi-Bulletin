AYUSH Expansion: UP Embraces Holistic Healthcare

2025-05-17T13:35:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed the establishment of integrated AYUSH colleges in every division of the state, housing Ayurveda, Unani, and Homoeopathy systems on a single campus. This initiative aims to strengthen AYUSH medical services and health education. Adityanath also mandated Naturopathy and Yoga Centres in all government institutions and directed the completion of all sanctioned AYUSH posts. He emphasized establishing health and wellness centers in every district, either government-run or through PPP models. The CM prioritized the construction of the AYUSH University and urged private investment in the sector, emphasizing quality assessment of private AYUSH institutions. He promoted Panchakarma therapy in Ayurveda and called for the scientific presentation of India's traditional medicine systems on a global platform. International Yoga Day preparations were reviewed, with instructions for celebrations across all levels of government and trained masters for public yoga training. MoUs with Ayurvedic medicine manufacturers like Dabur, Baidyanath, and Patanjali were also encouraged to ensure a regular supply of essential medicines.
