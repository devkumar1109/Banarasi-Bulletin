Bird Flu Shuts UP Zoos for Safety

2025-05-20T16:29:00+00:00

Due to a rising bird flu scare in Uttar Pradesh, all major zoos in the state have been shut for another week. This closure affects zoos in Lucknow, Gorakhpur, Kanpur, Varanasi and the Etawah lion safari. The decision was made to prevent further virus spread among animals and protect public health. Bird flu-related deaths have been confirmed in Kanpur and Gorakhpur zoos. A lion, peacock, and duck died in Kanpur, while several animals died in Gorakhpur. The Central Zoo Authority has formed a five-member team to investigate the impact of avian influenza at the Gorakhpur Zoo. After reviewing the findings, further decisions about zoo operations will be made.
