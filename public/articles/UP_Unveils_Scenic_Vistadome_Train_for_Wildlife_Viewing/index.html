UP Unveils Scenic Vistadome Train for Wildlife Viewing

2025-05-17T12:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched the country's first Vistadome coach service connecting Katarniaghat Wildlife Sanctuary and Dudhwa Tiger Reserve. The service offers panoramic views of the forest through expansive glass windows and a transparent roof. Currently operating on weekends, the service aims to expand to daily operations. The initiative aims to boost tourism, generate local employment, and promote the state's biodiversity. The Uttar Pradesh Eco Tourism Development Board launched the service, which is expected to attract visitors including school children and social media influencers.
