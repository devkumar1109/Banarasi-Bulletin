Ganga Darpan: Diving into River's Heart

2025-05-20T21:30:00+00:00

The Ganga Darpan interpretation centre in Varanasi, developed by the Wildlife Institute of India, promotes biodiversity education and river conservation. The centre features a 3D model of the Ganga River showcasing its diverse wildlife, including dolphins, crocodiles, turtles, and over 62 bird species. It has attracted over 20,000 visitors through workshops, educational sessions, and programs. The centre aims to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote responsible Ganga river management. It highlights the river's historical and cultural significance, showcasing its importance from ancient times to modern day.
