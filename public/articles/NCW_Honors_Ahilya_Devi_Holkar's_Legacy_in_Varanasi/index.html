NCW Honors Ahilya Devi Holkar's Legacy in Varanasi

2025-05-29T18:45:00+00:00

The National Commission for Women (NCW) will host a two-day program in Varanasi to celebrate the 300th birth anniversary of Punyashlok Ahilya Devi Holkar, a renowned ruler known for her social welfare and women's empowerment initiatives. The program, held at RUDRAKSH International Cooperation and Convention Centre on May 31st, will feature a keynote address by NCW chairperson Vijaya Rahatkar and a special address by Dr. Kumar Vishwas. An exhibition showcasing Holkar's life and legacy will also be inaugurated. Celebrations begin on May 30th with a lecture by Rahatkar at Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith University, followed by a tribute at Shubham Lawn and Banquet, a painting competition, and a rangoli competition at NAMO Ghat. The NCW aims to honor Holkar's contributions and inspire future generations.
