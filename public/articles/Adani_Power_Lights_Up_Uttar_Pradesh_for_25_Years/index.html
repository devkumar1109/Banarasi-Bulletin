Adani Power Lights Up Uttar Pradesh for 25 Years

2025-05-07T12:37:00+00:00

Adani Power has secured a 25-year contract to supply 1,500MW of electricity to Uttar Pradesh, India. The power will be generated from a new 1,600MW thermal power project in the state, awarded through a competitive bidding process. The Uttar Pradesh cabinet, led by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, approved the procurement, highlighting the state's commitment to long-term energy planning.
