Pandey Oberves Railways Kabaddi Team at Federation Cup

2025-04-29T19:28:00+00:00

Arvind Kumar Pandey, coach of NER kabaddi team, has been appointed as observer for the Indian Railways team at the Senior Federation Cup (Men's) Kabaddi Championship in Maharashtra from May 2. Three players, Sunil Kumar, Pravesh, and Anil Kumar, will represent Indian Railways, while Nitin Pawar will play for UP state team.
