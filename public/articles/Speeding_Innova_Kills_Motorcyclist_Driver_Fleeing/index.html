Speeding Innova Kills Motorcyclist, Driver Fleeing

2025-04-06T06:08:00+00:00

Naveen Gupta, a 42-year-old private worker, died in Kanpur after being hit by an Innova during a high-speed race with a BMW. The accident occurred near the South Cricket Academy, throwing Gupta into a parked car. The Innova driver, identified as Ayush Mishra, fled the scene but was later arrested. CCTV footage showed the Innova speeding at around 100 km/h before hitting Gupta's bike. Mishra was found to be inebriated at the time of his arrest. The Innova was registered to Gopal Bajpai, a contractor, and bore the logo of a central investigation agency. Police have filed a case against Mishra under relevant sections.
