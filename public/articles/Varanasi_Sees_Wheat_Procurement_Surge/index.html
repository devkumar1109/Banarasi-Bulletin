Varanasi Sees Wheat Procurement Surge

2025-04-21T18:19:00+00:00

Wheat procurement in Varanasi division increased by 3,746.88 metric tons this year, totaling 9,711.59 metric tons across Varanasi, Chandauli, Ghazipur, and Jaunpur districts. The government has implemented measures to benefit farmers, including direct purchase from their homes, waiving transportation costs and middlemen. A target of 127,500 metric tons has been set for the division, with 332 procurement centers operating under seven agencies. An average of 9,000 quintals of wheat is purchased daily. Payments are made directly to farmers' Aadhaar-linked accounts within 48 hours. This year's support price is Rs 2,425 per quintal, Rs 150 higher than last year. Farmers selling up to 100 quintals are exempt from verification.
