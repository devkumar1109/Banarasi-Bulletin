Eight Years of UP Progress: Adityanath's Development Drive

2025-03-24T08:13:00+00:00

The Yogi Adityanath government in Uttar Pradesh has completed eight years in power, marked by significant development and progress. Key achievements include: * **Rural Development:** Over 2.62 crore toilets constructed, free ration distribution to 14.7 crore people, 1.86 crore Ujjwala LPG connections, and 56 lakh houses built for the underprivileged. * **Law and Order:** Establishment of police commissionerates, recruitment of 1.5 lakh police personnel, and a focus on crime reduction. * **Infrastructure:** Development of expressways, highways, and airports, boosting connectivity and economic growth. * **Healthcare:** Increased focus on healthcare infrastructure and services, including the establishment of new medical colleges and hospitals. The government has also implemented various welfare schemes and initiatives aimed at empowering women and promoting social development.
