Caste Census Approved: Varanasi Celebrates

2025-05-01T07:02:00+00:00

The Union Cabinet has approved a caste-based census, a decision celebrated by residents of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. The residents expressed joy with traditional festivities like playing drums and smearing gulal (colored powder). They lauded Prime Minister Narendra Modi for this "historic decision," considering it a landmark step towards inclusive policymaking. The announcement was made by Union Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw, who stated the data will be included in the upcoming national census in a transparent manner. The national census, originally scheduled in 2020, was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
