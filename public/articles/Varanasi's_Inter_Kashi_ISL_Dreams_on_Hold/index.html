Varanasi's Inter Kashi: ISL Dreams on Hold

2025-04-14T05:14:00+00:00

Inter Kashi, a two-year-old football club based in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, is on the brink of promotion to the Indian Super League (ISL) after a successful season in the I-League. The club's president, Prithiijit Das, aims to establish a football culture in the state, leveraging its large population and economic potential. Inter Kashi made headlines by hiring Spanish coach Antonio Habas, the most successful coach in ISL history, who was drawn to the club's vision and transparency. Despite the potential promotion, the club faces challenges in establishing itself in a state dominated by cricket and hockey. The outcome of a disputed match against Namdhari FC, involving an alleged ineligible player, remains pending, potentially impacting Inter Kashi's title claim and promotion.
