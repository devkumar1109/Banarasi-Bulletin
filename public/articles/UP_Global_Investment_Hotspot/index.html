UP: Global Investment Hotspot

2025-03-21T20:20:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has become a global investment destination, attracting companies seeking scale and talent. Chief Secretary Manoj Kumar Singh highlighted the state's business-friendly policies, skilled workforce, and robust infrastructure as key factors. Deloitte, a multinational firm, inaugurated its office in Lucknow, citing UP's growth as a testing hub, electronics manufacturing center, and potential semiconductor hub. Deloitte CEO Romal Shetty emphasized the opportunities for businesses and professionals in UP, noting the state's goal to achieve a one trillion-dollar economy by 2030.
