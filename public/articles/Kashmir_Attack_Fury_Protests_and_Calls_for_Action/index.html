Kashmir Attack: Fury, Protests, and Calls for Action

2025-04-23T12:02:00+00:00

A terrorist attack in Pahalgam, Jammu and Kashmir, killed at least 26 people, mostly tourists, sparking widespread condemnation in Uttar Pradesh. Samajwadi Party president Akhilesh Yadav criticized BJP for politicizing the tragedy and failing to ensure security. Protests and condolences were held in Lucknow and Varanasi. Religious and community leaders demanded swift action against the perpetrators and urged the government to prevent future incidents.
