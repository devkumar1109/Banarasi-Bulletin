BJP Ups Ante in UP: OBCs and Brahmins Take Charge

2025-03-17T06:17:00+00:00

The BJP has appointed 70 new district chiefs in Uttar Pradesh. The appointments reflect a focus on OBCs and Brahmins, key demographic groups in the state. This move is seen as a strategic effort by the BJP to strengthen its base ahead of the 2024 general elections.
