Varanasi Transformed: Rs 40,536 Crore in Development

2025-03-27T19:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister of State for stamp and court registration fees Ravindra Jaiswal highlighted extensive development work in Varanasi over the past eight years. Over 458 projects worth Rs 40,536 crore across sectors like roads, bridges, water, education, health, energy, tourism, and urban development were completed, benefiting the public. Key achievements include 102 road and bridge projects worth Rs 22,408 crore, 34 Smart City projects worth Rs 877.65 crore, 60 sewerage and drinking water projects worth Rs 1,000 crore, 32 education projects worth Rs 512 crore, and 43 health projects worth Rs 1,870.36 crore. Construction work on additional projects worth over Rs 15,000 crore is ongoing.
