Yadav Son Sparks Row in Kashi Vishwanath Temple 'Red Zone'

2025-06-15T03:20:00+00:00

Tej Pratap Yadav, son of RJD leader Lalu Prasad Yadav, sparked controversy after a video surfaced showing him inside Kashi Vishwanath Temple's restricted 'Red Zone' in Varanasi. This zone, encompassing the sanctum sanctorum within the Kashi Vishwanath Corridor, prohibits mobile phones and unauthorized access. The temple administration has ordered a probe by the CRPF and local police, demanding strict adherence to security rules. The investigation will verify the video's authenticity and identify any personnel responsible for the breach. Appropriate legal action will be taken against any individuals found violating temple regulations.
