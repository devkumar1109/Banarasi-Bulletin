UP Braces for Scorching Summer Heat Wave

2025-04-06T08:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is predicted to experience a harsh summer from April to June with above-normal temperatures and intense heat waves. Daytime temperatures are expected to consistently exceed 40 degrees Celsius, with nighttime temperatures also remaining unusually high. The Bundelkhand region, comprising seven districts of Jhansi and Chitrakootdham division, will be most affected. Temperatures in cities like Kanpur, Varanasi, Prayagraj, and Fatehpur have already crossed 40 degrees Celsius in the first week of April, exceeding seasonal averages. The weather department warns that temperatures will frequently touch 40 degrees Celsius and may even surpass 45 degrees Celsius.
