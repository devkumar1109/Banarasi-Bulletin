Varanasi: Boosting Logistics, Tackling Heatwave

2025-05-05T19:00:00+00:00

Varanasi's Division Commissioner, S Rajalingam, has instructed officials to accelerate the development of a multi-modal terminal and a freight village. The multi-modal terminal at Ramnagar will feature a sewage treatment plant and a jetty. The freight village, a 100-acre project on the Varanasi-Chandauli border, will be connected to NH-7, NH-2, a 5.1 km railway line, and National Waterway-1. This will establish a freight corridor from northeast to south India, facilitating seamless cargo transportation to Haldia. The commissioner also emphasized prompt resolution of revenue disputes and preparedness for the heatwave in Chandauli district.
