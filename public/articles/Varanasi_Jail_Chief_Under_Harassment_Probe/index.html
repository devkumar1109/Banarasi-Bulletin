Varanasi Jail Chief Under Harassment Probe

2025-03-18T14:05:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has launched an investigation into harassment allegations against Varanasi Jail Superintendent Umesh Singh by Deputy Jailer Meena Kanojia. Kanojia, who was subsequently transferred, accused Singh of inappropriate remarks and personal intrusions. The investigation, led by Amita Dubey, will include two women officers from Lucknow. UP Congress chief Ajay Rai criticized the government for transferring the complainant instead of taking action against the accused.
