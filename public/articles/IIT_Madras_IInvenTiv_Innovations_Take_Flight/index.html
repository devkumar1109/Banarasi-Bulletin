IIT Madras IInvenTiv: Innovations Take Flight

2025-02-28T18:38:00+00:00

The 3rd edition of IInvenTiv, a research and development fair at IIT Madras, showcased innovations from over 40 institutions. Highlights include a low-flying "WIG" craft from IIT Madras that can travel 1600 km in 3 hours at 600 per seat, and a portable weather station from IIT (BHU) Varanasi that provides microclimatic information to farmers in local languages. IIT Tirupati displayed porous radiant burners that can save up to 50% fuel in cooking. Other innovations included drones, underwater vehicles, AI-based collision avoidance systems, neonatal jaundice meters, and cow-dung-based construction material. The exhibition aims to connect industry and government with higher educational institutions to finalize long-term goals. Union Education Minister Dharmendra Pradhan highlighted India's emergence as the world's third-largest startup ecosystem with a valuation exceeding $354 million.
