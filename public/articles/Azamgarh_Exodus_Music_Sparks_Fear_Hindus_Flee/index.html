Azamgarh Exodus: Music Sparks Fear, Hindus Flee

2025-06-16T04:25:00+00:00

In Azamgarh's Chhota Pura, approximately 40 Hindu families are selling their homes due to alleged harassment and violence stemming from playing music during religious events. The families claim the district administration failed to address their complaints after a June 3rd clash between communities. While police have assured increased security presence, the families feel compelled to relocate due to ongoing threats and concerns for their safety.
