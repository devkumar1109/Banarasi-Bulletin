UP Celebrates 8 Years of Progress: 'Growth Engine' Exhibition

2025-03-20T12:03:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will celebrate 8 years of its 'Seva, Suraksha, Sushasan' policy with a three-day exhibition event from March 25th to 27th across state districts. The events, themed 'UP: Growth Engine of Bharat,' will highlight achievements of the state and central governments in the past eight years. Exhibits will showcase successes of welfare schemes, infrastructure projects, new industrial units, and flagship initiatives like expressways, hospitals, medical colleges, and universities. Chief Secretary Manoj Kumar Singh conducted a video conference with divisional commissioners and district magistrates to ensure smooth execution of the events.
