Love, Debt, and a Suitcase: Killer's Scheme Unfolds

2025-06-06T12:58:00+00:00

Satendra Yadav was arrested for the murder of his partner, Neelesh, in Uttar Pradesh. Neelesh's body was found in a suitcase. Police say Satendra strangled Neelesh due to financial disputes stemming from a Rs 5.5 lakh loan Neelesh had given him, and suspicions of infidelity. Satendra disposed of Neelesh's body near a canal road to avoid toll taxes. Authorities have gathered evidence, including CCTV footage and the vehicle used in the crime. A charge sheet is expected to be filed against Satendra.
