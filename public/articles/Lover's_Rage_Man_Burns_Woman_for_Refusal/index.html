Lover's Rage: Man Burns Woman for Refusal

2025-03-12T05:13:00+00:00

In Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, a 28-year-old man, Umesh, disguised as a woman, set his married lover, Rekha (30), on fire inside her home after she refused to elope with him. Both sustained critical injuries and are currently hospitalized in Agra. Police investigations reveal that Umesh was the brother of Rekha’s sister-in-law and they had a relationship that began last year. Rekha had previously left home with Umesh but was brought back by police after a complaint from her family. No formal complaint has been filed yet, but legal action is expected once Rekha is able.
