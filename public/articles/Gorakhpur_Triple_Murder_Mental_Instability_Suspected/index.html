Gorakhpur Triple Murder: Mental Instability Suspected

2025-02-28T14:56:00+00:00

In Gorakhpur, a 24-year-old man, Ram Dayal, allegedly killed his grandfather, grandmother, and grand-uncle with a spade on Friday. Police suspect Ram Dayal was mentally unstable, citing his history of aggression and a recent accident. Locals reported he had brandished a weapon two days prior, and his mother stated his behavior became increasingly erratic after the accident. The family has a history of mental health issues, with the grandfather receiving psychiatric care in the 1990s and the eldest son exhibiting mental health problems upon release from prison. Police recovered the murder weapon and are investigating the motive.
