UP Floods Fight: Dredging, Drones, and Dams

2025-02-28T17:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is implementing a comprehensive strategy to address the state's recurring flood issues. His plan prioritizes dredging silt-laden river channels and, where dredging is not feasible, constructing embankments and anti-erosion measures. To ensure precise planning, drone surveys are being used for data collection. The state has already completed 1,575 flood mitigation projects, benefiting millions. Adityanath emphasizes timely project execution and strict budget adherence to enhance public safety and minimize costs.
