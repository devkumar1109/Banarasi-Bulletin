Varanasi Gets Multi-Modal Logistics Hub

2025-03-12T06:59:00+00:00

The National Highways Logistics Management Limited (NHLML) and the Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to establish a state-of-the-art Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP) in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. The 150-acre park, witnessed by Union Ministers Nitin Gadkari and Sarbananda Sonowal, will be connected to National Highway 7, the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor, and National Waterway-1. Strategically located near Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport, the MMLP is expected to boost India's logistics sector, enhance trade efficiency, and create job opportunities. The park aims to simplify logistics, lower freight costs, and increase India's trade competitiveness.
