Uttar Pradesh: Viral Video Exposes Child Abuse

2025-05-24T13:22:00+00:00

A viral video shows a mother in Bulandshahar, Uttar Pradesh, physically abusing her children. The video, recorded by a family member, depicts the woman beating the minors with slippers, throwing them to the ground, and verbally abusing the person filming. Despite the presence of an elderly woman, no intervention was made to stop the abuse. The video, posted on X, sparked widespread outrage online. The police are investigating the incident.
