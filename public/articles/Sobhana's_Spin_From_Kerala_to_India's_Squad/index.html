Sobhana's Spin: From Kerala to India's Squad

2025-03-08T13:30:00+00:00

Asha Sobhana, a cricketer from Kerala, shared her inspiring journey on Women's Day 2025. She rose from humble beginnings to represent India, initially as a fast bowler before transitioning to leg spin. Her WPL debut with Royal Challengers Bangalore proved pivotal, leading to her national team selection. Sobhana highlighted the league's positive impact on domestic players like Sajana and Saima Thakur. She emphasized women's safety and the importance of self-defense skills, advocating for martial arts training. Sobhana credited her parents, coaches, and mentors for their unwavering support throughout her career. Playing alongside stars like Smriti Mandhana and Harmanpreet Kaur has been a valuable learning experience for her.
