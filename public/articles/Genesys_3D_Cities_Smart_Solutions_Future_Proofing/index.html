Genesys: 3D Cities, Smart Solutions, Future Proofing.

2025-05-21T09:17:00+00:00

Genesys International Corporation utilizes AI and precision geospatial data to create detailed 3D digital models of cities, aiding in urban planning, infrastructure development, and environmental management. Their technology, including India's first 5-centimeter precision 3D digital twin for urban areas, helps visualize the impact of projects like road construction and assess solar potential for sustainable energy initiatives. Genesys integrates sensor data, such as CCTV feeds and air quality readings, into their platform for applications like identifying unpaid property taxes. Their AI-powered navigation map incorporates augmented reality and intelligent speed assistance to enhance the driving experience. The company's expertise extends beyond mobility, addressing challenges in agriculture, disaster response, and climate resilience.
