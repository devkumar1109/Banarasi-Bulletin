Advani's Silver at World Matchplay

2025-04-12T16:36:00+00:00

Pankaj Advani won a silver medal at the WBL World Matchplay Billiards Championship in New Delhi after losing to David Causier in the final. Advani initially led 2-0, but Causier fought back to win the best-of-15 contest 8-7. The final was a closely fought match with both players displaying intense skills.
