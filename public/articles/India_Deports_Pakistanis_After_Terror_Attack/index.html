India Deports Pakistanis After Terror Attack

2025-04-25T10:43:00+00:00

Following the Pahalgam terror attack, the Indian government revoked visas for all Pakistani nationals and ordered their immediate deportation. Over 1,000 Pakistani nationals have been identified in Uttar Pradesh, and preparations are underway to deport them. Union Home Minister Amit Shah instructed all state chief ministers to identify and deport Pakistani nationals within their states. While the official deportation order is yet to be received by Uttar Pradesh, the process of identifying individuals has already begun. Pakistani citizens are returning to Pakistan via the Wagah border.
