Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand Triumph in Hockey National Championship

2025-03-05T15:14:00+00:00

Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand won their Division A matches in the 15th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship in Panchkula. Madhya Pradesh secured a crucial three points thanks to Neelu Dadiya's 53rd-minute penalty corner conversion in the final quarter. No further details about the Jharkhand match were provided.
