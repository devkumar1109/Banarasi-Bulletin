Acid Mistaken for Water: Second Fatal Case in Months

2025-03-22T01:13:00+00:00

A 78-year-old woman, Rajbala Devi, died in Meerut after accidentally consuming acid, mistaking it for water. She had been experiencing depression following her husband's death six months prior. Police are investigating the presence of the acid bottle in her home. This incident follows a similar case in January where a 19-year-old woman with mental challenges died after consuming acid from her home's toilet. In that case, the woman had a history of head trauma.
