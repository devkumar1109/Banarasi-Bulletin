Wedding Joy Turns to Tragedy in Bijnor Accident

2025-06-02T15:49:00+00:00

A couple in Uttar Pradesh's Bijnor district died in a road accident while returning home after distributing wedding cards for their son's June 9th ceremony. 62-year-old Khub Singh and his 56-year-old wife Lali Devi were struck by an unidentified vehicle on the Nagina-Dhampur road. They were taken to a community health centre where doctors pronounced them dead. The bodies were sent for post-mortem.
