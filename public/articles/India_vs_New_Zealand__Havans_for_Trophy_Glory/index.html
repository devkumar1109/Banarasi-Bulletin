India vs. New Zealand: Havans for Trophy Glory

2025-03-09T03:25:00+00:00

The ICC Champions Trophy 2025 final match between India and New Zealand is scheduled for 2:30 PM today. Ahead of the highly anticipated match, Indian cricket fans in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, performed a 'havan' (traditional Hindu fire ritual) at the Radha Madhav Temple, praying for India's victory. Devotees, dressed in team colors, expressed their unwavering belief in the team's success. Fans across the country are eagerly awaiting the match, with many taking to social media to express their support for Team India.
