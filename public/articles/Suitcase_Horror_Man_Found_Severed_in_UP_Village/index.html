Suitcase Horror: Man Found Severed in UP Village

2025-04-21T15:27:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Patkhauli village, the mutilated body of a man was found stuffed in a suitcase. The suitcase was discovered by a farmer in his field on Sunday morning. The body was discovered to be severed into two parts: the upper and lower halves. Police are investigating the incident.
