Dowry Deaths: Two UP Women Perish, Justice Sought

2025-04-09T15:22:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Pilibhit, a 26-year-old woman named Sonkali was burned alive by her husband, Naresh Kumar, and his parents on March 30th for not meeting their dowry demands. Sonkali had been subjected to physical and mental abuse since her marriage in 2017. She died on April 6th, narrating the events to her brother before passing. A case of dowry death has been registered against the accused. Separately, in Budaun, a 21-year-old woman, Tarawati, died allegedly by consuming poison at her in-laws' home eight months after her marriage. Her father alleges she was killed by her in-laws for dowry, claiming they frequently beat her for it.
