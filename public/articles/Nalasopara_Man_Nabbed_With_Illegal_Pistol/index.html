Nalasopara Man Nabbed With Illegal Pistol

2025-05-31T14:35:00+00:00

The Crime Branch Unit-2 of Vasai arrested a 23-year-old man, Shubham Kumar Rajnath Tiwari, in Nalasopara (East) for possessing an illegal country-made pistol and a live cartridge. Police believe he intended to use the weapon to intimidate people. The arrest was made on May 30, 2025, based on a tip-off. A search of Tiwari's bag revealed the firearm and ammunition, valued at ₹45,400. He has been charged under IPC Section 3 and 25(1B)(a) of the Arms Act and Section 37(1)/135 of the Maharashtra Police Act, 1951. The operation was led by Police Inspector Sameer Ahirrao and commended by police officials for their swift action in ensuring public safety and eliminating illegal arms.
