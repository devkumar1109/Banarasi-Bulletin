Bail Jumper's Procession Sparks Outrage

2025-06-08T14:36:00+00:00

Abid Khan, a Varanasi jail inmate previously imprisoned for loot and criminal intimidation, was rearrested after being released on bail. A video surfaced showing Khan leading a procession with supporters chanting objectionable slogans. Police detained Khan and two others involved, while a local officer faces suspension for negligence. Authorities are pursuing further arrests and seeking to revoke Khan's bail.
