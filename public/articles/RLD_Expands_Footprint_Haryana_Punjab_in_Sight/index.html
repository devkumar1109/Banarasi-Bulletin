RLD Expands Footprint: Haryana, Punjab in Sight

2025-04-28T02:37:00+00:00

Jayant Chaudhary's Rashtriya Lok Dal (RLD) is expanding its footprint beyond West UP, aiming for Haryana and Punjab. The party appointed former MLA Jagjeet Singh Sangwan as its Haryana president. A meeting in Ludhiana on May 4 will finalize the Punjab president. The RLD plans to contest the 2027 Punjab Assembly elections, whether alone or in alliance, and the next Haryana Assembly elections. The party will leverage the legacy of Chaudhary Charan Singh, particularly among Jat and Jat Sikh voters, who are significant in both states. RLD state units are being established nationwide, already present in UP, Rajasthan, Delhi, J&K, Jharkhand, Kerala, and Telangana.
