Akshay Kumar Dives into Kumbh, Praises Yogi's Improvements

2025-02-24T07:54:00+00:00

Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar took a holy dip at the Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj and praised Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath for the improved arrangements compared to his 2019 visit. Kumar noted significant enhancements and thanked the CM, police, and workers for their efforts. The event, which began on January 13 and concludes February 26, has attracted celebrities like Vicky Kaushal, Sonali Bendre, and Vijay Deverakonda.
