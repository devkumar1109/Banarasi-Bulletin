Leopard Kills Woman, Saves Daughter in Brazen Attack

2025-05-25T09:30:00+00:00

In Bahraich, Uttar Pradesh, a 38-year-old woman, Zarina Bano, was killed by a leopard while protecting her 17-year-old daughter, Nagma. The attack occurred on the roof of their house around 2 am. Zarina bravely confronted the leopard, allowing her daughter to escape. The forest department has set up traps to capture the leopard. They are also advising residents to avoid sleeping on rooftops at night. The family has been given an ex-gratia of Rs 10,000. This is not the first leopard attack in the village; two children from the same family were killed in previous attacks in the past two years.
