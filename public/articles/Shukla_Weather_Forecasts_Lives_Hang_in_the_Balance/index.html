Shukla: Weather Forecasts, Lives Hang in the Balance

2025-05-20T06:46:00+00:00

Jagadish Shukla, a prominent meteorologist, emphasizes the importance of accurate weather predictions, particularly for India's monsoon season, which significantly impacts the livelihoods of rural communities. Shukla's new book, "A Billion Butterflies," explores his journey from a rural village to MIT, highlighting his passion for understanding weather patterns. He views seasonal predictions as crucial for mitigating the effects of droughts and supporting vulnerable populations. Shukla criticizes flawed forecasts and political interference, advocating for science-led approaches to improve prediction accuracy and assist India's rural poor.
