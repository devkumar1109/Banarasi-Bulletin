Adani Green Energy Wins 1,250 MW Pumped Hydro Project

2025-02-25T14:47:00+00:00

Adani Green Energy (AGEL) has secured a Letter of Award from Uttar Pradesh Power Corporation Limited (UPPCL) for a 1,250 MW energy storage capacity project. The Panaura Pumped Hydro Storage Project, located in Sonbhadra district, Uttar Pradesh, has a 40-year minimum commitment and is expected to be operational within six years. This project aligns with India's clean energy goals and AGEL's plan to add over 5 GW of hydro PSP capacity by 2030. AGEL, India's largest renewable energy company, currently operates a 11.9 GW renewable portfolio and aims to reach 50 GW by 2030.
