Shami's Farmhouse: A "Hasin" Tribute in Amroha

2025-06-11T09:07:00+00:00

Mohammed Shami, the Indian cricketer, recently purchased a 150-bighas farmhouse in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh, named 'Hasin'. This name is believed to be a tribute to his ex-wife, Hasin Jahan. The couple has been separated since 2018 following allegations of domestic abuse and match-fixing made by Hasin Jahan against Shami. Despite the separation, Shami co-parents their daughter, Aaira Shami. Shami bought the land in 2015 and built the farmhouse over time.
