Uttar Pradesh Aims for 8 Lakh Solar Rooftops

2025-04-20T13:46:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government aims to install 8 lakh solar rooftop plants by March 2027 under the Pradhan Mantri Surya Ghar Yojana. The current financial year's goal is 2.65 lakh units, with a monthly target of 22,000 installations. Over 1 lakh rooftop solar systems are already installed in the state, with 11,000 units installed monthly. The Uttar Pradesh New and Renewable Energy Development Agency (UPNEDA) is implementing the scheme, with targets set at various levels. As of April 2025, over 2,500 vendors have been empaneled, and 1,800 have received training. Loans and credit guarantee schemes are available to vendors. The PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is the world's largest domestic rooftop solar initiative, achieving 10 lakh solar-powered homes by March 10, 2025.
