UP's Sacred Electric Bus Route Rolls Out

2025-04-22T17:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government plans to launch an electric AC bus service connecting major religious cities like Kashi, Prayagraj, Ayodhya, and Sonbhadra. Five charging stations are being set up, four in Varanasi and one in Sonbhadra, to facilitate the service. The electric buses can travel up to 150 kilometers on a single charge and will have features like CCTV cameras, GPS systems, and panic buttons for passenger safety. The air-conditioned buses can seat around 28 passengers and are expected to be operational within a month.
