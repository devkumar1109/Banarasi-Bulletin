DECA: BHU Artists Paint Collective Journeys

2025-04-15T18:43:00+00:00

Ten young artists from the Banaras Hindu University's Faculty of Visual Arts are showcasing their work in an exhibition called "DECA" at the Ahivasi Art Gallery. The three-day exhibition, which started on Tuesday, features paintings from each artist, exploring themes of human relationships, identity, socio-cultural changes, and cosmic symbolism. Their influences range from traditional Indian art to abstract symbolism, rural-urban contrasts, and environmental consciousness. Curated by assistant professor Suresh Chandra Jangid, "DECA" signifies the collective strength of the artists and their individual journeys as Master's students.
