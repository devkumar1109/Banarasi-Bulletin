UP Assembly Passes Rs 8 Lakh Crore Budget

2025-03-04T14:35:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Assembly has passed a budget of over Rs 8 lakh crore for the financial year 2025-26. Finance Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna presented the budget on February 20th, which was supported by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. The Appropriation Bill seeking approval for the budget was passed with a majority vote after a discussion in the Assembly. Assembly Speaker Satish Mahana announced the budget's passage.
