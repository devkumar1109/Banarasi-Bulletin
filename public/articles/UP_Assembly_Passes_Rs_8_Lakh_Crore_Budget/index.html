UP Assembly Passes Rs 8 Lakh Crore Budget

2025-03-04T14:35:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Assembly has approved a budget of over Rs 8 lakh crore for the financial year 2025-26. Finance Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna presented the budget on February 20th, which was backed by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath and a majority of Assembly members. The budget was passed after a discussion and a majority vote. The Assembly Speaker, Satish Mahana, officially announced the budget's passage.
