Russia Unleashes Drone Barrage on Ukraine

2025-06-09T14:27:00+00:00

Russia launched its largest overnight attack on Ukraine, firing 479 drones and missiles in retaliation for the destruction of 40 of its aircraft. Ukrainian air defenses reported intercepting many of the drones and missiles. Russian state media released footage showing a precision strike on a tactical airfield in Rivne, Ukraine.
