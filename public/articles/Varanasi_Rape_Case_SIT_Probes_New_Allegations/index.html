Varanasi Rape Case: SIT Probes New Allegations

2025-04-17T21:31:00+00:00

An eight-member SIT has been formed to investigate the Varanasi gang-rape case after the families of the arrested accused raised inconsistencies in the 19-year-old complainant's claims. The families questioned why a missing person's report wasn't filed for five days and why the complainant didn't report the alleged rape immediately upon being recovered from her friend's house. The SIT, headed by DCP Pramod Kumar, will examine new facts and ensure a transparent investigation. 14 individuals have been arrested so far. The complainant alleged she was drugged, confined, and raped for five days between March 29 and April 4.
