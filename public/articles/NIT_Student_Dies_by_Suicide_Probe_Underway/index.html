NIT Student Dies by Suicide, Probe Underway

2025-03-03T08:25:00+00:00

A final-year student at NIT Hamirpur, Ayansh Sharma from Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, died by suicide on Sunday. He was found hanging in his hostel room. Police are investigating the incident and have informed Sharma's family. This incident follows a similar suicide last year at NIT Patna, where a student died by suicide in her hostel room due to personal and family reasons.
