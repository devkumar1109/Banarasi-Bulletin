Modi's Vision: Kumbh Mela Breaks Records, Boosts Tourism

2025-02-27T15:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath attributed the success of Maha Kumbh 2025 to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's leadership, calling it a global event. Adityanath highlighted Modi's vision of integrating faith and economy, emphasizing spiritual tourism's potential. Over 66 crore pilgrims visited Prayagraj for the Maha Kumbh, setting new records in religious tourism. The event led to the development of five spiritual tourism corridors connecting key religious sites across Uttar Pradesh. These corridors facilitated the movement of millions of devotees, boosting tourism and economic activity in the state.
