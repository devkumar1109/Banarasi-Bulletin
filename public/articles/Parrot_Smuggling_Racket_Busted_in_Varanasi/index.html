Parrot Smuggling Racket Busted in Varanasi

2025-06-01T19:39:00+00:00

On Sunday, forest officials in Varanasi busted a racket smuggling 28 parrots from Jharkhand. Three smugglers, bus driver Dhananjay Singh, conductor Abhay Singh alias Kanhaiya, and Md Arif, were arrested. The birds were found packed in a carton box on a bus during a raid in the Andharapul area. Md Arif, a resident of Varanasi, transported the birds from Garhwa, Jharkhand. The seized parrots are protected under the Wildlife Protection Act of 1972, and the smugglers face legal proceedings under various sections of the Act. Forest officials urge citizens to report any illegal wildlife trade to their provided phone numbers.
