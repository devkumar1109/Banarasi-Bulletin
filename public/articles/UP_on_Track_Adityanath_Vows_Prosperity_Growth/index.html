UP on Track: Adityanath Vows Prosperity, Growth

2025-04-05T10:11:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that the state will become prosperous within three years by eliminating poverty. He aims to make Uttar Pradesh's economy the top one in India. Adityanath inaugurated the Rohin Barrage in Maharajganj and laid the foundation stones for 629 developmental projects worth Rs 654 crore. He contrasted the BJP government's focus on "one district, one medical college" with previous governments' alleged "one district, one mafia" approach. Adityanath also declared that Uttar Pradesh is no longer a "BIMARU" state, rejecting the outdated label that categorized it as economically backward.
