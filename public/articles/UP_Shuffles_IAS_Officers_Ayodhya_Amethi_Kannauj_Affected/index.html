UP Shuffles IAS Officers: Ayodhya, Amethi, Kannauj Affected

2025-04-15T16:08:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government transferred 16 IAS officers, including those in Ayodhya, Amethi, and Kannauj. District Magistrates of six districts, including Ayodhya, were among those transferred. Notable changes include: Chandra Vijay Singh (Ayodhya DM) becoming Special Secretary, Establishment and Industrial Development; Nikhil Tikharam Funde (Chandauli DM) transferring to Ayodhya; and Nisha (Amethi DM) becoming Director of National AYUSH Mission. This reshuffle follows a previous transfer of 9 IAS officers in the state.
