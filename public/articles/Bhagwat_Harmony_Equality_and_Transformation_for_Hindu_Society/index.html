Bhagwat: Harmony, Equality, and Transformation for Hindu Society

2025-04-05T19:08:00+00:00

RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat emphasized social harmony and equality during a visit to BHU, Varanasi. He stressed sharing of resources like temples, water, and cremation ghats without caste discrimination, stating that Hindu society encompasses all Indians regardless of faith. Bhagwat outlined five transformations for the Sangh's centenary year, including social construction, expansion of Shakhas, and focus on educational institutions. He called for re-strengthening families, promoting self-reliance through indigenous products, and fulfilling civic duties like wearing helmets and following traffic rules. Speaking to academicians, Bhagwat urged them to instill values in students and engage with rural areas to spread knowledge.
