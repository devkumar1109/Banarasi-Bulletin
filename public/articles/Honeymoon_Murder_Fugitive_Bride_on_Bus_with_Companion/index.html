Honeymoon Murder: Fugitive Bride on Bus with Companion

2025-06-15T10:09:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi, accused of murdering her husband Raja Raghuvanshi in Meghalaya, allegedly travelled with an Uttar Pradesh woman named Ujala Yadav on a bus from Varanasi to Ghazipur on June 8. Ujala claims Sonam covered her face and was accompanied by two men at the Varanasi railway station. During the bus journey, Sonam borrowed Ujala's phone, typed a number, and deleted it before returning it. Sonam also instructed Ujala to avoid social media and not watch news reports about the honeymoon murder case. Ujala recognized Sonam from news reports the next morning and informed authorities. Sonam was subsequently traced to a dhaba in Nandganj, Ghazipur.
