UP Assembly Wraps: Promises Made, Technology Enhanced

2025-03-05T13:21:00+00:00

The 2025 Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly session concluded on Wednesday after 72 hours and 56 minutes of proceedings. Ten sub-sessions were held, with 69 hours and 20 minutes dedicated to legislative activities and 3 hours and 36 minutes to adjournments. Speaker Satish Mahana thanked party leaders, while Parliamentary Affairs Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna urged members to fulfill their promises made during the session. Leader of Opposition Mata Prasad Pandey highlighted the positive impact of technology on legislative processes.
