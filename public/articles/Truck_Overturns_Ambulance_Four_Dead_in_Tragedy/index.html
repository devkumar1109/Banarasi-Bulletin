Truck Overturns Ambulance, Four Dead in Tragedy

2025-04-26T09:53:00+00:00

In a tragic incident on the Varanasi-Shaktinagar highway, a gravel-laden truck overturned onto an ambulance, killing four people. The deceased include a pregnant woman (Heerawati Devi), her mother (Malti Devi), the ambulance driver (Suraj Bali Kharwar), and another staff member (Ramu). Two others sustained serious injuries and were transported to a trauma center. Heerawati was being transferred to Varanasi for medical complications during her pregnancy. The accident occurred near a flyover in Mirzapur district. Police used heavy machinery to remove the truck and rescue the victims.
