"Record 62 Crore Devotees Dive into Maha Kumbh Spirit"

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

Here is a summary of the news in 150 words: The Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj has witnessed a record 62 crore devotees taking a holy dip at Triveni Sangam in just 42 days. The event has had a significant impact on the cities of Kashi and Ayodhya, with over 2 crore devotees visiting the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi and 69 lakh people booking homestays in Ayodhya. The event featured a grand gathering, a sports event, impressive waste management, and various cultural activities. Prime Minister Narendra Modi also took a holy dip and performed puja at the Sangam. The Khel Maha Kumbh, a sports event, saw participation from over 800 young athletes and renowned sports personalities. The event also featured a "zero discharge" initiative, which ensured that no polluted water entered the river, setting a global sanitation benchmark.
