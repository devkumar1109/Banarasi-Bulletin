UP at 8: Growth Engine Celebrates

2025-03-24T20:51:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is celebrating eight years of the Yogi Adityanath government with a three-day event starting March 27th. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath will lead the celebrations in Gorakhpur, while Deputy Chief Ministers Brajesh Pathak and Keshav Prasad Maurya will preside over events in Lucknow and Varanasi respectively. Ministers and other officials will be stationed in all 75 districts to highlight the state government's achievements under the theme "UP: The growth engine of the government of India." Events will include cultural programs, exhibitions showcasing district achievements, and public address by ministers and public representatives. The government will also focus on its CM Yuva Udyami Yojna, offering loans to youth for entrepreneurship.
