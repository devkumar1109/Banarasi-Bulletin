UP Partners for Solar Power Surge

2025-04-22T14:24:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Smart Energy Council of Australia and Hinduja Group to promote renewable energy. The agreement aims to develop solar skill development programs, facilitate technology transfer, build solar and green hydrogen value chains, and support investment and student exchange initiatives. The partnership will focus on building a future renewable workforce, commercializing advanced solar technologies, expanding solar supply chains, and exploring opportunities for green hydrogen and energy storage. The MoU is effective from 2025 to 2027 and is expected to contribute to Uttar Pradesh becoming a renewable energy hub and India's goal of achieving 500GW renewable energy capacity by 2030.
