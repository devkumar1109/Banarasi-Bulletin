Hanuman Jayanti Lights Up Varanasi

2025-04-12T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi's temples celebrated Hanuman Jayanti with vibrant decorations, aartis, devotional songs, and Ramcharitmanas recitals. The Sankat Mochan Temple hosted special events, including shehnai performances and Rudrabhishek by Brahmin priests. Authorities ensured safety through patrolling and drone surveillance. The Sankat Mochan Temple also featured performances by various devotional groups. Following the celebrations, the 102nd Sankat Mochan Sangeet Samaroh, a major Indian classical music festival featuring Padma awardees, will take place. Deputy Commissioner of Police Gaurav Banswal highlighted the coordinated efforts with local committees for public order.
