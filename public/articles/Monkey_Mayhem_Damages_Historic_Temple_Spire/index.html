Monkey Mayhem Damages Historic Temple Spire

2025-03-19T09:50:00+00:00

Monkeys damaged a gold-plated spire (Kalash) on the Baikuntheshwar Mahadev section of the Kashi Vishwanath temple in Varanasi during Mangla Aarti on Tuesday. The gold plating was a donation from Maharaja Ranjit Singh in the 18th century. The damaged Kalash was secured and repairs, initiated by temple authorities with experts and artisans, commenced Wednesday. The temple administration is seeking assistance from the Varanasi Municipal Corporation to control the monkey menace. This incident comes after the temple underwent recent renovations, including gold plating in 2021.
