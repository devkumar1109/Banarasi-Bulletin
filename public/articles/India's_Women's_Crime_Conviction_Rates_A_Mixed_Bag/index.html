India's Women's Crime Conviction Rates: A Mixed Bag

2025-03-08T08:28:00+00:00

India's conviction rate for crimes against women in 2022 was 25.3%, down from previous years. Uttar Pradesh had the highest conviction rate at 70% for every 100 completed trials, followed by Bihar at 61%. Six states, including Odisha, West Bengal, and Gujarat, had conviction rates below 10%. Andhra Pradesh and Assam were the worst performers, with conviction rates below 5%. The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) released this data.
