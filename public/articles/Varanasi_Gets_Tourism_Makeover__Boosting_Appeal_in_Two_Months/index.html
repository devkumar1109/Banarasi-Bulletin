Varanasi Gets Tourism Makeover: Boosting Appeal in Two Months

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

India's tourism secretary, V Vidyavathi, has given authorities two months to implement visible changes in Varanasi to enhance its tourism appeal. The focus is on upgrading 16-20 key locations, including temples, ghats, and Sarnath. Improvements include beautification, cleaning, infrastructure development, and improved transportation. There's a push to promote spiritual tourism, local crafts like Banarasi sarees, and cultural events. Training for local guides and artists is also emphasized. Online promotion and engaging tourists with nearby eco-tourism sites are additional strategies.
