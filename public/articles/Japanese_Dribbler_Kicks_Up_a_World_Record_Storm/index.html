Japanese Dribbler Kicks Up a World Record Storm

2025-04-17T15:22:00+00:00

Japanese national Nozomu Hagihara is dribbling a football across India, aiming to set a Guinness World Record for the longest distance dribbled. His 2,000-kilometer journey started in Kolkata on March 3rd and will conclude at the Japanese Embassy in New Delhi by May 15th. Hagihara, who has lived in India for four years, is using this challenge to express his love for the country and its people. He has already reached Varanasi as part of his journey.
