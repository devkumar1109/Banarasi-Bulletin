Stray Dog Attacks: Rising Danger in India

2025-03-09T05:32:00+00:00

A young woman in Rajasthan's Alwar was severely attacked by a pack of stray dogs while walking outside her home. The incident, captured on CCTV, shows the woman struggling to escape the dogs before sustaining multiple injuries. In a separate incident in Uttar Pradesh, a six-year-old girl was fatally mauled by a pack of stray dogs. The girl was attacked while relieving herself in a field and died from her injuries despite being taken to a hospital. These incidents highlight the growing threat of stray dog attacks, resulting in both injuries and fatalities.
