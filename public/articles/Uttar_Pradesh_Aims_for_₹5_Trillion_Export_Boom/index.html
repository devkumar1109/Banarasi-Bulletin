Uttar Pradesh Aims for ₹5 Trillion Export Boom

2025-04-18T20:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh aims to significantly boost exports, targeting over ₹5 trillion by 2030. The state is revising its export policy for 2025-30, focusing on new markets in Latin America and Eastern Europe. The policy will include increased incentives for exporters and improved infrastructure support. This move aligns with the Central government's strategy to diversify India's export destinations. Uttar Pradesh, the second-largest state economy and GST contributor, seeks to solidify its position as a major trade player.
