Rapti River Tragedy: One Dead, 13 Saved

2025-06-15T19:34:00+00:00

A boat carrying 14 villagers capsized in the Rapti river in Gorakhpur, resulting in the death of a 55-year-old man, Pawaru Yadav. The villagers were traveling to Deoria for a land measurement exercise. 13 passengers managed to swim to safety, while Yadav drowned and his body was later recovered. The incident occurred 20 meters from the riverbank when the boat overturned. Police from both Gorakhpur and Deoria responded to the scene, deploying divers for the rescue and recovery operation. An inquiry is underway to determine the cause of the capsize and if any safety protocols were breached.
