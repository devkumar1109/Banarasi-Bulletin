Shiva-Inspired Cricket Stadium Rises in Varanasi

2025-03-12T16:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the construction of Varanasi's international cricket stadium, inspired by Lord Shiva. This will be the state's third such venue, following Kanpur and Lucknow. The stadium's design features crescent-shaped roof covers, trident-shaped floodlights, and a seating arrangement inspired by the ghats. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone in 2023. The Uttar Pradesh government allocated Rs 121 crore for land acquisition, and the BCCI contributed Rs 330 crore for construction. The stadium, expected to accommodate over 30,000 spectators, is anticipated to host international and domestic matches.
