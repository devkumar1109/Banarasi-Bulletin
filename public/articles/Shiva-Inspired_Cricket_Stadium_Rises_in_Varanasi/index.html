Shiva-Inspired Cricket Stadium Rises in Varanasi

2025-03-12T16:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the construction of Varanasi's new international cricket stadium, inspired by Lord Shiva. The stadium, the state's third international venue, is expected to host international matches. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone in 2023. The design incorporates elements like crescent-shaped roof covers, trident-shaped floodlights, and seating inspired by ghats. The project received a significant financial investment, with the Uttar Pradesh government allocating Rs 121 crore for land acquisition and the BCCI contributing Rs 330 crore for construction. The stadium will accommodate over 30,000 spectators upon completion.
