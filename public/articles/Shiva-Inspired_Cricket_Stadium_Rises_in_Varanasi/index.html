Shiva-Inspired Cricket Stadium Rises in Varanasi

2025-03-12T16:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the construction of a new international cricket stadium in Varanasi, inspired by Lord Shiva. This will be UP's third international cricket venue, following those in Kanpur and Lucknow. The stadium's design incorporates elements like crescent-shaped roof covers, trident-shaped floodlights, and a seating arrangement inspired by the ghats. The project, which includes a Rs 121 crore land acquisition by the UP government and a Rs 330 crore contribution from the BCCI, is expected to accommodate over 30,000 spectators. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone in 2023.
