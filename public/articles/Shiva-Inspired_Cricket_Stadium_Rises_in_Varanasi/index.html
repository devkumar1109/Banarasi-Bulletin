Shiva-Inspired Cricket Stadium Rises in Varanasi

2025-04-25T03:46:00+00:00

Varanasi is constructing a new 30,000-seater cricket stadium, the Varanasi International Cricket Stadium, costing Rs 452 crore. The stadium, designed with Shiva-inspired architecture, is set to be completed in April 2026. Features include a crescent-shaped canopy representing Shiva's moon, trident-shaped floodlights, and seating inspired by Kashi's ghats. The stadium will have nine pitches (four red soil, three black soil, and two mixed) and a proposed academy with dormitories for 100 trainees. The stadium incorporates sustainable features like solar panels, a water recycling system, and EV recharging stations. It also boasts a high-end drainage system for quick ground preparation after rain.
