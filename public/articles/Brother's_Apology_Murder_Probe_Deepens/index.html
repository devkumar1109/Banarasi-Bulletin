Brother's Apology, Murder Probe Deepens

2025-06-11T23:38:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi's brother, Govind, met Raja's family in Indore and apologized, stating he would ensure Sonam faces punishment. He had previously searched for Sonam's body in Shillong and rushed to her aid in Ghazipur when she was arrested. The police are probing how the Meghalaya honeymoon murder occurred. Four other accused, including Raj Kushwaha, were produced in court. Kushwaha allegedly paid the killers Rs 50,000 to carry out the murder, covering travel and accommodation expenses. The assailants traveled to Indore separately by train and destroyed their phones after the incident.
