UP Arms Up: Defense Manufacturing Hub Emerges

2025-05-13T05:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is emerging as a major defense manufacturing hub in India. A BrahMos unit has been inaugurated in the state, signifying a significant step towards bolstering domestic defense capabilities. The inauguration is expected to be followed by the establishment of units specializing in drones, radars, and missiles, further solidifying UP's position in the defense sector.
