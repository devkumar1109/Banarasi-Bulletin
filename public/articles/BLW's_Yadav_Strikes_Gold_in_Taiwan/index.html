BLW's Yadav Strikes Gold in Taiwan

2025-06-09T19:09:00+00:00

Rohit Yadav, an employee of Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), won the gold medal in the men's javelin throw at the Taiwan Athletics Open 2025. Yadav achieved a throw of 74.42 meters, surpassing competitors from across Asia. BLW General Manager Naresh Pal Singh praised Yadav's dedication, highlighting BLW's role in nurturing sporting talent alongside its locomotive production expertise. Yadav's victory underscores India's growing strength in athletics and its readiness for future global competitions.
