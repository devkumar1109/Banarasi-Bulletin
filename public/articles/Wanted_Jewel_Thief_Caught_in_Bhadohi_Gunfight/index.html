Wanted Jewel Thief Caught in Bhadohi Gunfight

2025-02-28T06:51:00+00:00

Sachin Maurya, a wanted criminal with a 25,000 rupee bounty, was arrested in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh following an encounter with Gyanpur police and district SWAT. Maurya was wanted for a necklace robbery that occurred on February 21st near Marchwar village. Police recovered an illegal firearm, ammunition, and the motorcycle used in the crime. Maurya sustained a gunshot wound to the leg and is currently hospitalized in stable condition. His accomplice escaped during the encounter and efforts are underway to apprehend them.
