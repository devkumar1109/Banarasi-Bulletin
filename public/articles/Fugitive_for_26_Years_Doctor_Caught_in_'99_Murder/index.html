Fugitive for 26 Years, Doctor Caught in '99 Murder

2025-04-26T00:58:00+00:00

Vinod Kumar Gupta (49), absconding for 26 years, was arrested in Uttar Pradesh in connection with the 1999 murder of Bhiwandi powerloom owner Jigar Mahendra Mehta. Gupta, along with Raju Mehta (still absconding) and Kamlesh Uppadhyay (arrested earlier), kidnapped Mehta, demanding a ransom of Rs 10 lakh. They murdered Mehta at his factory, stole his valuables, and disposed of his body on farmland. Gupta, a medical student at the time, was lured into the crime by Raju Mehta. He had been living in Delhi and Uttar Pradesh, even opening a medical store, until his arrest on April 22.
