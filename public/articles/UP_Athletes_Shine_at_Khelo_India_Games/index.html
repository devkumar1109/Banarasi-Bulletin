UP Athletes Shine at Khelo India Games

2025-05-25T21:38:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi praised the performance of three Uttar Pradesh athletes - Sheikh Zeeshan, Qadir Khan, and Tushar Chaudhary - at the Khelo India Youth Games 2025 in Bihar. Zeeshan, a 17-year-old triple jumper, broke the national record with a jump of 15.66 meters and won a silver medal. Qadir Khan broke the national record twice in the 400-meter race. Tushar Chaudhary won gold in weightlifting, lifting a total of 289 kg. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath also commended their achievements, highlighting their discipline and determination. Modi also mentioned that 26 records were broken at the Games.
