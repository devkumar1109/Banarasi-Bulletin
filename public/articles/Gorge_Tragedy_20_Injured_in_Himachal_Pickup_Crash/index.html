Gorge Tragedy: 20 Injured in Himachal Pickup Crash

2025-04-30T20:45:00+00:00

A pick-up truck carrying 20 people, including two children, overturned and fell into a gorge near Baddi, Himachal Pradesh, injuring 15 seriously. The accident occurred around 6:37 pm. The injured, mostly laborers from Uttar Pradesh, were heading towards Manpura. Four people were taken to a Baddi health facility, two of whom were referred to Nalagarh community health center due to severe injuries. Thirteen passengers from Nalagarh sustained head injuries, two of whom were transferred to PGI Chandigarh. The injured have been identified as Sanket, Aakash, Cheenu, Anshika, Gulshan, Sanu, Mahi, Sannu, Nargis, Nasreen, Sonam, Santosh, Amreena, and Ra.
