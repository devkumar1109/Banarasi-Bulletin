Aide's Threat, Baby's Death: Pune Police Probe

2025-05-24T23:31:00+00:00

Pune police searched the office and home of Nilesh Chavan, an aide to an expelled NCP member, in connection with the death of Vaishnavi Hagawane. Chavan allegedly threatened Vaishnavi's father at gunpoint when he sought custody of his nine-month-old grandson. Police are investigating whether Vaishnavi's death was a suicide or murder. Chavan's laptop was seized for forensic analysis. Pune police also issued firearms licenses to Chavan's brothers, Shashank and Sushil, in 2022. A proposal has been submitted to cancel their licenses.
