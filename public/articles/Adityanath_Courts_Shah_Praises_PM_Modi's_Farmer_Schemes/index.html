Adityanath Courts Shah, Praises PM Modi's Farmer Schemes

2025-06-10T06:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath paid a courtesy visit to Union Home Minister Amit Shah in New Delhi. Adityanath also addressed the Kisan Samman Samaroh in Auraiya, highlighting government initiatives to support farmers since 2014. These include the Soil Health Card Scheme, PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, and loan waivers for 86 lakh farmers in 2017. He emphasized the importance of farmers in the government's political agenda under Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Adityanath mentioned the positive impact of these schemes, citing PM Kisan Samman Nidhi as the world's largest direct benefit transfer scheme, with over Rs 3.25 lakh crore transferred to farmers' accounts.
