Streaming Wars Heat Up This Weekend

2025-02-28T07:00:00+00:00

This weekend sees new releases across various OTT platforms. JioHotstar premieres "Aashram Season 3 Part 2," a Prakash Jha directorial featuring Bobby Deol, and "Suits LA," a spin-off of the popular legal drama. Netflix offers the comedy series "Running Point" starring Kate Hudson and the crime drama "Dabba Cartel" with an ensemble cast including Shabana Azmi and Jyotika. Amazon Prime Video releases the true-story based "Superboys Of Malegaon" starring Adarsh Gourav and Vineet Kumar Singh. Theatres see a re-release of Aanand L Rai's "Raanjhanaa" starring Dhanush and Sonam Kapoor. JioHotstar also brings the sequel "Beetlejuice Beetlejuice" featuring Michael Keaton and Winona Ryder, alongside new cast members.
