Maha Kumbh Clean, Healthy, and United

2025-03-01T17:51:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Health Minister Brajesh Pathak refuted claims by opposition leaders, including Akhilesh Yadav, that pilgrims fell ill after bathing in the Sangam during the Maha Kumbh. Pathak assured that no cases of skin diseases were reported despite the large number of attendees. He praised the cleanliness measures at the event, emphasizing their success in maintaining the purity of the Ganga. Pathak highlighted the Maha Kumbh's cultural significance, describing it as a global symbol of India and Indianness that unites people from diverse backgrounds.
