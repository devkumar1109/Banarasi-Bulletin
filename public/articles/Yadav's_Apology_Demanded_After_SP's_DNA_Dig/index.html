Yadav's Apology Demanded After SP's "DNA" Dig

2025-05-20T09:32:00+00:00

Posters demanding an apology from SP chief Akhilesh Yadav appeared in Lucknow following alleged derogatory remarks made by the SP's official 'X' handle against UP Deputy CM Brajesh Pathak. Pathak criticized Yadav's response to a query, accusing him of deflecting from questions about the SP's alleged connections to controversial figures and past scandals. Pathak challenged Yadav to address the SP's alleged dark legacy, claiming that discussing the party's "DNA" would reveal connections to notorious figures like Atiq Ahmed and Mukhtar Ansari, the Muzaffarnagar riots, and scams.
