UP Leads Green Building Boom in India

2025-03-08T20:08:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is a frontrunner in sustainable urbanisation, ranking second in India for green building adoption with over 1,400 projects covering 1.6 billion square feet. Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak highlighted UP's commitment to integrating sustainability, citing the IGBC Healthcare Building Rating System and the mandate for green government buildings. UP follows Maharashtra in green building projects, exceeding Karnataka, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, and Telangana. Key green initiatives in Lucknow include the metro rail stations, Charbagh railway station, and Ekana International Cricket Stadium. UP pioneered green building incentives, offering additional floor area ratio (FAR) for IGBC-rated projects, a model adopted by other states.
