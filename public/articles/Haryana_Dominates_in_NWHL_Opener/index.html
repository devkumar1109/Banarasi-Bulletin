Haryana Dominates in NWHL Opener

2025-03-18T15:39:00+00:00

On March 18, at the National Women’s Hockey League 2024-2025, Hockey Haryana defeated Manipur Hockey 7-1, with Saavi and Kirti scoring two goals each. Hockey Association of Odisha won 1-0 against Hockey Bengal, with Dipika Barwa scoring the lone goal. Hockey Madhya Pradesh also secured a victory.
