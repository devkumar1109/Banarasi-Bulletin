Yogi's Crackdown: IAS Officer Suspended for Bribery

2025-03-20T15:18:00+00:00

Abhishek Prakash, a 2006 batch IAS officer and former District Magistrate of several Uttar Pradesh districts, has been suspended by the Yogi Adityanath government. Prakash, an IIT Roorkee alumnus, served as Secretary, Industrial Development Department, and CEO of Invest UP until his suspension. He is accused of demanding a 5% commission for approving a solar project, as alleged by complainant Vishwajeet Dutt. This action reflects CM Yogi's strict stance against corruption in Uttar Pradesh, which has led to various investigations and actions against IAS and PCS officers. Seven suspended IAS and PCS officers recently received new postings, including Ghanshyam Singh as Special Secretary, Forests.
