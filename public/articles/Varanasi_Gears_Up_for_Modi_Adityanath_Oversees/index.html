Varanasi Gears Up for Modi, Adityanath Oversees.

2025-04-03T19:10:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is in Varanasi to oversee preparations for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's upcoming visit. Adityanath emphasized the importance of ensuring all projects scheduled for inauguration and foundation-laying are ready. This visit comes amidst preparations for the Delhi Assembly elections in 2025.
