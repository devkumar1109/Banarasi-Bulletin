UP GST Soars, Markets Dip, Gold Glistens

2025-03-03T16:30:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Goods and Services Tax (GST) department has collected ₹1,000 crore for two consecutive months. The Indian stock market saw a slight decline, with the SENSEX dropping 112.16 points to 73,085.94 and the NIFTY falling 5.40 points to 22,119.30. Global crude oil prices decreased by $39.00 to $6,079.00 per barrel. Gold prices saw an increase of ₹1,083.00 to ₹85,302.00 per unit, while silver prices rose by ₹1,585.00 to ₹94,472.00 per unit.
