Varanasi Gears Up for Modi's Arrival, Adityanath Reviews

2025-04-03T19:10:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Varanasi to oversee preparations for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's upcoming visit. Adityanath emphasized the readiness of all projects scheduled for inauguration and foundation-laying during the PM's trip. The meeting took place at the Circuit House auditorium in Varanasi.
