Holi Turns Violent: Clashes, Injuries, and Death in UP

2025-03-15T14:48:00+00:00

Holi celebrations in Uttar Pradesh were marred by violence, with clashes reported across several districts. Incidents stemmed from forced color application, caste-based disputes, and alcohol-related altercations. Injuries and arrests were reported in Mathura, Farrukhabad, Kaushambi, Sant Kabir Nagar, Sultanpur, and Barabanki. Notably, a shooting incident in Farrukhabad left six injured and a death occurred in Barabanki due to Holi-related activities. Police have increased security measures and are investigating the incidents.
