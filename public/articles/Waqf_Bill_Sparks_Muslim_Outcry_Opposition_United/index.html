Waqf Bill Sparks Muslim Outcry, Opposition United

2025-03-31T02:30:00+00:00

The Waqf Amendment Bill is a highly controversial piece of legislation expected to be tabled in Parliament. The All India Muslim Personal Law Board is protesting, claiming it is against the Muslim community. Opposition-ruled states have passed resolutions against the bill, and opposition parties in Parliament are united in their opposition. The bill comes after a six-month Joint Parliamentary Committee (JPC) report, which included 300 pages of dissent from opposition parties. Concerns have been raised about potential protests on the scale of the Citizenship (Amendment) Act and the bill's impact on upcoming Bihar assembly elections. The Waqf Amendment Bill aims to address issues within the Waqf system, including a lack of inclusivity and representation for marginalized communities.
