Varanasi, Agra Reign Supreme in State Chess

2025-04-04T17:57:00+00:00

Samridhi of Varanasi and Sanchay Dubey of Agra won the women's and men's titles respectively at the state-level open chess championship held in Sitapur. Samridhi scored 6 points, while runner-up Tanya Verma of Kanpur secured 5.5 points. In the men's category, Sanchay Dubey achieved 7.5 points, with Prakhar Tripathi of Varanasi finishing as runner-up with the same score. Shreyansh Singh of Agra secured third place with 7 points.
