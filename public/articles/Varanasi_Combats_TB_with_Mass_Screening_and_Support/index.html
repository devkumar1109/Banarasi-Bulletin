Varanasi Combats TB with Mass Screening and Support

2025-03-24T17:58:00+00:00

To commemorate World TB Day on March 24, 2025, over 6 lakh people were screened for tuberculosis in Varanasi. The '100-day Intensive TB Elimination Campaign' initiated by the Government of India led to this extensive screening effort. Out of those screened, 9,781 individuals underwent the NAT test. The campaign aims to swiftly identify and treat TB cases within the district. Dr. Piyush Rai, District tuberculosis officer, emphasized that TB is a contagious disease primarily affecting the lungs, but can spread to other organs if left untreated. 310 nutrition kits were also distributed to TB patients across various medical units in Varanasi by the Feeding India organization.
