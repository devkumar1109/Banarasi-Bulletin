Cool Comfort at Kashi Vishwanath Dham

2025-04-17T18:16:00+00:00

To combat the scorching heat at Kashi Vishwanath Dham in Varanasi, new cream-colored jute mats have been laid on the shrine floor. These mats were chosen from samples provided by the Jute Corporation of India as they stay cooler without needing frequent watering, unlike the previous red cotton mats. The temple authorities have also installed German hangers and canopies for permanent shade from the Ganga Dwar entry point to the main temple. Additional arrangements include mist fans, drinking water, glucose, ORS, sugar candies, and cold beverages like lemon water.
