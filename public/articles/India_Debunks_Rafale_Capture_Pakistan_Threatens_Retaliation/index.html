India Debunks Rafale Capture, Pakistan Threatens Retaliation

2025-05-12T05:48:00+00:00

India has debunked Pakistan's false claim that IAF's Rafale pilot Shivani Singh was captured during Operation Sindoor. The Indian Air Force confirmed all pilots are safe. Shivani Singh, India's first woman Rafale pilot, is a warzone veteran and international representative. Pakistan, meanwhile, has refused to de-escalate tensions with India and threatened a response for the damage caused during Operation Sindoor.
