Bomb Threat on IndiGo Flight Causes Varanasi Airport Panic

2025-04-27T11:30:00+00:00

A Canadian passenger on an IndiGo flight from Varanasi to Bengaluru claimed to have a bomb, triggering a panic and security scare at Varanasi airport on Saturday. The passenger was immediately detained for questioning by security agencies. The aircraft was moved to an isolation bay for a thorough inspection, but no explosives were found. After clearance from security agencies, the flight departed for Bengaluru on Sunday morning. A detailed investigation into the incident is underway.
