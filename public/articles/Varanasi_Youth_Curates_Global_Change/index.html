Varanasi Youth Curates Global Change

2025-04-08T01:30:00+00:00

Sashwat Pandey, a 21-year-old graduate of St Stephen's College, Varanasi, has been selected as one of the youngest curators in the World Economic Forum's Global Shapers Community. He will attend the Global Shapers Annual Summit in Geneva. Sashwat is known for his leadership roles in organizations like IAPSS and Y20 India, where he represented India at international events. He aims to empower Varanasi's youth by focusing on education, governance, and youth empowerment.
