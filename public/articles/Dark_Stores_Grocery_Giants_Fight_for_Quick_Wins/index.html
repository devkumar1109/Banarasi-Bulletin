Dark Stores: Grocery Giants Fight for Quick Wins

2025-05-18T00:00:00+00:00

Reliance Retail, More, and Spencer's are adopting dark stores to compete with quick commerce platforms like Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart, and Zepto. These dark stores, mini warehouses serving a 2-3 km radius, enable faster deliveries and address the growing demand for instant grocery shopping. Reliance Retail, using its existing store network for deliveries within 3 km, is setting up dark stores in areas with high volume and limited reach. More Retail has already established 45 dark stores with plans to add 100 more, focusing on high-density micro markets like New Delhi and potentially Mumbai.
