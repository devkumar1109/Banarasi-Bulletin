Bollywood Summer Blockbusters 2023

2025-04-02T07:50:00+00:00

This summer, Bollywood boasts several highly anticipated releases. Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi star in the romantic drama "Bhool Chuk Maaf," set in Varanasi, premiering on April 10th. Akshay Kumar takes on a historical role in "Kesari Chapter 2: The Untold Story of Jallianwala Bagh," exposing the truth behind the massacre, releasing on April 18th. Sunny Deol returns with the action-packed "Jaat," facing off against Randeep Hooda, hitting theaters on April 10th. Akshay Kumar leads the star-studded cast of the comedy "Housefull 5," promising chaos and laughter, arriving on June 6th. Sidharth Malhotra and Janhvi Kapoor star in the cross-cultural romance "Param Sundari," premiering on July 25th.
