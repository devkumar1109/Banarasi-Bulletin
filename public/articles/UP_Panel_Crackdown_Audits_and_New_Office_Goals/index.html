UP Panel: Crackdown, Audits, and New Office Goals

2025-04-24T18:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly's Estimates Committee has directed officials to crack down on illegal buses, install prepaid electricity meters in the Hindon floodplains, and conduct an audit of the health department's budget. The transport department was instructed to target buses with expired registrations and expedite the implementation of a city transport system. The power department was tasked with identifying large electricity defaulters. The health department was asked to ensure state-run health programs benefit all eligible citizens and provide a report on budget utilization within 15 days. The committee also requested the stamp department to collaborate with the district administration to find suitable land for a new office to enhance registration services.
