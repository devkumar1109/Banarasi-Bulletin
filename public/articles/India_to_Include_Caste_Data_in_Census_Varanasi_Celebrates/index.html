India to Include Caste Data in Census, Varanasi Celebrates

2025-05-01T09:26:00+00:00

The Indian government has decided to include caste-based data in the upcoming national census. Union Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw announced the decision, stating it will be conducted in a 'transparent manner'. This decision has been met with celebration in Varanasi, with residents expressing joy through music, dancing, and the use of colours.
