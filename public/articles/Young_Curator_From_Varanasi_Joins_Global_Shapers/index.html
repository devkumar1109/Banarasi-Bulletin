Young Curator From Varanasi Joins Global Shapers

2025-04-08T01:30:00+00:00

Sashwat Pandey, a 21-year-old graduate of St Stephen's College, has been selected as one of the youngest curators in the World Economic Forum's Global Shapers Community. Pandey, from Varanasi, will attend the Global Shapers Annual Summit in Geneva. He was chosen for his leadership potential, dedication, and ability to inspire youth participation. He previously held leadership roles in organizations like IAPSS, Y20 India, and the World Youth Festival. Pandey aims to empower Kashi's youth through initiatives focused on education, governance, and youth empowerment.
