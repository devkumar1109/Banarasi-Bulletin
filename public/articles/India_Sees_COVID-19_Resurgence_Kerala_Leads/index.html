India Sees COVID-19 Resurgence, Kerala Leads

2025-05-31T15:53:00+00:00

Active COVID-19 cases in India have surpassed 3,000, with 4 reported deaths across Delhi, Karnataka, Kerala, and Uttar Pradesh. Kerala leads with 1,336 active cases, followed by Maharashtra with 467. Delhi has reported 375 new infections. While most cases are mild, authorities urge vigilance and emphasize robust testing and treatment infrastructure. Kerala recorded 189 new cases, while Delhi reported its first COVID-19 fatality, a 60-year-old woman. Karnataka reported a request for calm and vigilance, while Odisha confirmed seven cases with all patients stable and exhibiting mild symptoms. Bihar's Patna recorded seven new infections, including a medical intern, and Maharashtra's health department issued an advisory to hospitals.
