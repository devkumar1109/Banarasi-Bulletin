"Kumbh 2025: Sacred Splash of Faith and Feats"

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

Here is a summary of the news in 150 words: The Mahakumbh 2025 in Prayagraj has attracted 62 crore devotees in just 42 days, making it a massive spiritual gathering. The event has had a significant impact on Kashi and Ayodhya, with over 2 crore devotees visiting the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi and 69 lakh people booking homestays in Ayodhya. The event has also featured a grand gathering, sports event, impressive waste management, and cultural activities. Notable attendees include Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who took a holy dip in the Sangam and performed puja. The Khel Maha Kumbh, a sports event, saw participation from over 800 young athletes and renowned sports personalities, with a resolution to include India's indigenous sports in the 2036 Olympics. The event's "zero discharge" initiative has set a global sanitation benchmark, with successful wastewater management from over 1.5 lakh toilets daily.
