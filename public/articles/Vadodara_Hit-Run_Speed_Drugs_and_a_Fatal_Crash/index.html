Vadodara Hit-Run: Speed, Drugs, and a Fatal Crash

2025-04-21T17:18:00+00:00

Rakshit Chaurasia, accused in the Vadodara hit-and-run case, was driving at 140 kmph and under the influence of drugs when he crashed his friend's Volkswagen car into three vehicles, killing Hemali Patel. The Event Data Recorder (EDR) report confirmed the high speed, while forensic tests revealed Chaurasia and two co-accused had consumed cannabis. Chaurasia, who has been in judicial custody since March 17, is opposing a bail plea.
