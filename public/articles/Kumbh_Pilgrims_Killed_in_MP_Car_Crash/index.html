Kumbh Pilgrims Killed in MP Car Crash

2025-02-25T07:30:00+00:00

Three Kumbh pilgrims were killed and seven others injured in a car accident in Madhya Pradesh's Rewa district. The incident occurred on National Highway-30, 40 km from the district headquarters, as the SUV carrying the pilgrims collided with a truck. The deceased were traveling back from the Kumbh Mela.
