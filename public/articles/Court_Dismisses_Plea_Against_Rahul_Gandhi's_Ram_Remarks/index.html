Court Dismisses Plea Against Rahul Gandhi's Ram Remarks

2025-05-28T01:20:00+00:00

A Varanasi court dismissed a plea against Rahul Gandhi regarding his remarks on Lord Ram. Advocate Harishankar Pandey filed the complaint, alleging Gandhi called Lord Ram a "mythological and fictional figure" during a speech at Brown University. The court deemed the petition "non-maintainable" under the Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) 2023, stating prior permission from the government or district magistrate is required in such cases. Pandey plans to seek permission from the district magistrate and refile the petition. He categorized Gandhi's remarks as "hate speech" that hurt the sentiments of Sanatan Dharma followers.
