UP's Export Ambitions: $5 Trillion Target by 2030

2025-04-21T09:30:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is developing a new export policy for 2025-30, aiming for $61.5 billion (₹5 trillion) in exports by 2030, a tripling of current levels. The ambitious target faces challenges including global trade uncertainties and tariffs. However, the state has potential in electronics and engineering goods, leveraging its existing industrial base. Key export destinations for Uttar Pradesh include the US, UAE, Vietnam, UK, Nepal, and Germany. The policy's success hinges on the state's ability to capitalize on emerging opportunities and overcome existing hurdles.
