Yamuna Expressway Upgraded: Safety First on Wheels

2025-04-01T10:11:00+00:00

The Yamuna Expressway in Uttar Pradesh has implemented all 21 road safety standards recommended by IIT Delhi. The recommendations, made in 2019, included additional signage, removal of lane systems, rumble strips, noise-reducing crash attenuators, audible shoulder markings, and shoulder guard rails. YEDA has also implemented eight specific safety measures suggested by IIT Delhi, and has deployed resources like patrol vehicles, ambulances, and cranes. Three trauma centers are located near the expressway, and motorists can access quick assistance through a dedicated app and toll-free number. YEDA is also enforcing road safety regulations, including breath tests, helmet and seat belt usage, and speed limits.
