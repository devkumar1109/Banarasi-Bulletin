UP @ 8: Growth Engine Celebration

2025-03-24T20:51:00+00:00

To celebrate eight years of the Uttar Pradesh government, a three-day mega event will commence on Tuesday, led by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath in Gorakhpur. Deputy Chief Ministers Brajesh Pathak and Keshav Prasad Maurya will oversee celebrations in Lucknow and Varanasi respectively. Each district will have a minister, MLC, or commission member leading the event, highlighting the state government's achievements under the theme "UP: The growth engine of the government of India." Elaborate programs will include cultural events, short films showcasing government achievements and the Prayagraj Maha Kumbh, and exhibitions displaying welfare schemes, projects, and industrial growth. Ministers will address press conferences, honor achievers, and promote schemes like the CM Yuva Udyami Yojna. Debates, discussions, and Jan sabhas will further engage the public.
