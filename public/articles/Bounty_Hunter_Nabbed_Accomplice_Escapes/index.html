Bounty Hunter Nabbed, Accomplice Escapes

2025-02-28T06:51:00+00:00

A wanted criminal, Sachin Maurya, was arrested in an encounter by Gyanpur police and SWAT in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh. Maurya, with a 25,000 rupee bounty on his head, was involved in a necklace robbery in Marchwar village on February 21st. Police recovered a firearm, ammunition, and the motorcycle used in the crime. Maurya, who has previous cases for robbery and theft, was injured during the encounter and is receiving treatment. His accomplice managed to escape and is still at large.
