Cattle Smugglers Nabbed in Sonbhadra Police Encounter

2025-04-02T18:52:00+00:00

Five cattle smugglers were arrested in an encounter with police in Sonbhadra district, Uttar Pradesh. Police rescued 39 cattle from the smugglers. The encounter took place near the Chakariya border while police were acting on a tip-off about the smugglers' movement towards Bihar-Jharkhand. One smuggler, Qurban Ali, was injured and admitted to a hospital. His four accomplices, Ramadhar, Rajdev, Veerbhan, and Munna Agariya, were also arrested. Police recovered illegal firearms and cartridges along with the cattle. A case has been registered against the smugglers under the Prevention of Cow Slaughter Act, Animal Cruelty Act, and Arms Act.
