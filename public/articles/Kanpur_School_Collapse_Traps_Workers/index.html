Kanpur School Collapse Traps Workers

2025-06-16T16:54:00+00:00

An under-construction school building in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, collapsed on Monday evening, trapping several workers under debris. The collapse occurred while workers were present at the site. Local police, fire department, and other concerned authorities rushed to the scene to initiate a rescue operation. Emergency teams, including police and administration personnel, are actively involved in the efforts. The exact cause of the collapse is yet to be determined, but a possible violation of construction norms is suspected.
