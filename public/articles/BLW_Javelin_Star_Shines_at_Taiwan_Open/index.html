BLW Javelin Star Shines at Taiwan Open

2025-06-09T19:09:00+00:00

Rohit Yadav, an athlete from Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), won the gold medal in the men's javelin throw at the Taiwan Athletics Open 2025. He achieved a throw of 74.42 meters, competing against top athletes from across Asia. This victory highlights the growing sporting talent within India and BLW's commitment to nurturing athletic potential beyond its core operations.
