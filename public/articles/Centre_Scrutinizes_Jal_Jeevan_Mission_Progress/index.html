Centre Scrutinizes Jal Jeevan Mission Progress

2025-05-20T09:41:00+00:00

The Centre has deployed 100 teams to inspect Jal Jeevan Mission schemes in various states, including Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh. This follows a review meeting led by Cabinet Secretary T V Somanathan on May 8 to assess the mission's progress in providing clean drinking water to rural households. The teams, comprising secretaries, joint secretaries, and directors from central government departments, will assess 27 schemes in Madhya Pradesh, 21 in Rajasthan, 18 in Uttar Pradesh, and 16 in Karnataka. Inspections will also be conducted in other states like Odisha, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, West Bengal, Andhra Pradesh, Jharkhand, Punjab, Sikkim, Meghalaya, Chhattisgarh, and Goa.
