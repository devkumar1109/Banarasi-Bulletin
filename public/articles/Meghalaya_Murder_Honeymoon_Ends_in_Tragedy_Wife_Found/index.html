Meghalaya Murder: Honeymoon Ends in Tragedy, Wife Found

2025-06-09T02:30:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi, initially suspected of orchestrating her husband's murder in Meghalaya, was found near Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh, claiming she was abducted. She is now in police custody and will be interrogated by Meghalaya Police. Three suspects, Raj Kushwaha, Vishal Chauhan, and Akash Rajput, have been arrested and are in Indore police custody. A fourth suspect, Anand, was also arrested. Police believe the murder was pre-planned. Raja and Sonam's honeymoon trip to Shillong ended tragically when Raja's body was recovered. Sonam's family denies any involvement in the murder.
