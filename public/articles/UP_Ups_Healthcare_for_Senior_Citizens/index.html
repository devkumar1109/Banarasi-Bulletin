UP Ups Healthcare for Senior Citizens

2025-04-04T01:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is enhancing healthcare facilities for senior citizens in old age homes. Under the Ayushman Bharat scheme, elderly residents will receive improved healthcare. A new program will utilize the professional expertise of senior citizens, offering financial incentives to promote self-reliance. Currently, state-run old age homes provide free accommodation, meals, medical care, and entertainment. The government also offers old-age pensions and facilitates religious pilgrimages. To address legal and family disputes, 216 reconciliation officers have been appointed at the tehsil level, and DM-led committees oversee old age home management and service improvements.
