Nepal Bus Crash Injures 25 Indian Tourists

2025-04-19T07:04:00+00:00

At least 25 Indian tourists were injured in a bus accident in Nepal's Dang district on Friday. The accident, suspected to be caused by brake failure, occurred while the tourists were en route to Pokhara. 19 injured tourists were admitted to a health center in Tulsipur, Uttar Pradesh, which shares a border with Nepal. Three are in critical condition and receiving treatment in a Nepal hospital. Most of the tourists are from Lucknow, Sitapur, Hardoi and Barabanki districts of Uttar Pradesh.
