Professor Outwits Cyber Fraudsters

2025-05-22T19:15:00+00:00

A retired BHU professor and medical practitioner, Dr. PR Gupta, was saved from cyber fraud by the prompt action of a bank manager and the police in Varanasi. Two days prior, a fraudster impersonating a Supreme Court investigator called Dr. Gupta, instructing him to remain silent and make daily calls. The scammer then arranged a video call with a person claiming to be a senior officer, demanding money transfers from Dr. Gupta to settle a false investigation. The bank manager, noticing Dr. Gupta's distress, alerted the police who apprehended the fraudsters.
