Varanasi Development Focus: Adityanath Aids Overcrowded Shelter

2025-05-13T13:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed development projects in Varanasi, focusing on transportation, tourism, and construction management. He met representatives from Apna Ghar Ashram, which shelters the homeless. The ashram director requested government land or buildings due to overcrowding, with 600 residents currently housed in a facility designed for 400. Adityanath assured support and suggested expanding the ashram's facilities to other districts in Uttar Pradesh.
