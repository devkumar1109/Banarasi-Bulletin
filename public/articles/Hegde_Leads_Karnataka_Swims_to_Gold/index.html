Hegde Leads, Karnataka Swims to Gold

2025-05-06T16:52:00+00:00

Maharashtra's Aditi Hegde won gold in the girls' 200m freestyle swimming event at the Khelo India Youth Games 2025 in Bihar. Karnataka dominated swimming, securing four gold medals through Krish Sukumar, Manvi Verma, and Sri Charani Tumu. Delhi's judokas, Stanzin Deechan and Gargi Tokas, also won gold in the 63kg and 40kg categories respectively. Rajasthan claimed their first gold medals through mixed team 10m air pistol and judoka Ashwin Bharadwaj in the 81kg category. Prachi and Mayank Chaudhary of Rajasthan won the mixed team 10m air pistol final, edging out Uttar Pradesh. In archery, Maharashtra's Smaran Sarvesh and Ujjwal Bharat Olekar will compete in the boys' recurve final, while Maharashtra will also have an all-female compound final.
