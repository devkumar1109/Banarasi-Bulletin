IIT-BHU Open House: Your Future Starts Here!

2025-06-06T15:13:00+00:00

IIT-BHU will host an online open house session for JEE Advanced 2025 qualifiers on June 11, from 3:30 pm to 6:30 pm. The session will be chaired by IIT-BHU Director Prof Amit Patra and feature key academic and administrative leaders. Prospective students can interact with the leadership team, learn about the admission process, hostel facilities, campus security, and other aspects of life at IIT-BHU. Registration is mandatory and closes on June 10. Registered candidates will receive a link to access the online session via Google Meet.
