Uttar Pradesh Blooms With 'Balvatika Abhiyan'

2025-04-15T13:50:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched the 'Balvatika Abhiyan,' a two-week campaign (April 16-29) focusing on early childhood education. The initiative aims to transform existing Anganwadi centers into Balvatikas, providing structured learning opportunities for children aged 3-6. This campaign, named 'Sunehari Shuruaat Balvatika Ke Saath,' seeks to improve pre-primary schooling and encourage community involvement in early education. The 'Balvatika Abhiyan' includes community activities like parades, village assemblies, interactive sessions with role plays and storytelling, and creative contests for children and parents. The campaign aims to highlight the importance of early education and ensure enrollment of children aged 3-6 in Balvatika classes.
