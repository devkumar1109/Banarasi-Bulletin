UPMSP Board Exams: Evaluation Begins Under Tight Security

2025-03-16T07:02:00+00:00

UPMSP has begun the evaluation of 10th and 12th answer sheets for the 2025 board exams. The process will take place at 261 centers across the state from March 19 to April 2. Strict security measures are in place, including CCTV surveillance, designated personnel at entrances, and training for evaluators to ensure accuracy. In 2024, over 29 lakh students appeared for the 10th exam and over 24 lakh for the 12th exam. The UPMSP also reported 9 cases of cheating and 14 impersonators during the 2025 exams.
