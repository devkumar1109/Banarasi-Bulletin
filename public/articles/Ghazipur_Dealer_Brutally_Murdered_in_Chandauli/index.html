Ghazipur Dealer Brutally Murdered in Chandauli

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29), from Ghazipur district was murdered in Chandauli district on Thursday night. Pawan was attacked by unknown assailants while transporting water to his construction site near Naikot village. He was brutally beaten with bricks and rods and later died at the hospital. Police have launched an investigation into the case. Senior police officials visited the crime scene and several teams are working to solve the murder.
