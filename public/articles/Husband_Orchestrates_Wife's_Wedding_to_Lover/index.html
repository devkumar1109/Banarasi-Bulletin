Husband Orchestrates Wife's Wedding to Lover

2025-04-01T15:57:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Sant Kabir Nagar district, a husband named Bablu orchestrated his wife Radhika's marriage to her lover Vikas. Bablu, fearing for his safety due to recent murders in the state, chose this unconventional solution. He confirmed the affair upon returning to his village and arranged a traditional Hindu wedding at a Shiva temple. Bablu even witnessed the marriage and ensured legal formalities were completed. However, just days later, Radhika was summoned by the police.
