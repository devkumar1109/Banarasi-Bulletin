IIT-Roorkee Fuels UP Youth Skills for Global Success

2025-05-01T11:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Skill Development Mission (UPSDM) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with IIT-Roorkee to provide technical training and skill development opportunities to UP youth. The collaboration will offer advanced training, innovation-driven learning, research opportunities, and startup support under the guidance of IIT-Roorkee experts. This partnership aims to enhance the employability of UP youth and prepare them for national and international competition. The MoU was signed by Additional Mission Director Priya Singh on behalf of UPSDM and authorized representatives from IIT Roorkee.
