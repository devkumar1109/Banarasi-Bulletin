U.P.'s Heritage Reimagined: Temples to Tourism Boom

2025-03-26T10:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's Tourism Policy 2022 focuses on promoting both well-known and lesser-known heritage sites. The government is developing historic temples, forts, palaces, and religious sites with improved infrastructure and accessibility. Digital promotion through virtual tours and social media is also key. Local communities are engaged through homestay programs and handicraft exhibitions, benefiting local businesses. Religious and heritage tourism in Uttar Pradesh are intertwined. Religious sites like Varanasi, Ayodhya, and Mathura hold historical and architectural significance, appealing to both pilgrims and history enthusiasts. This combination provides visitors with a rich and diverse experience.
