Varanasi's Urban Boom: Stadium, Township, and Reforms

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi will get its first urban township spanning 150 acres near the new international cricket stadium at Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority approved the project. The cricket stadium, costing Rs 451 crore and with a 30,000-spectator capacity, is expected to be completed by the end of 2025 and host an international match in 2026. The VDA also inaugurated a new record room with a capacity of 80,000 files and launched its citizen app for complaint registration. Other approvals included a main access road between Transport Nagar Scheme and NH-2, and amendments to building bylaws for capital investment reforms. RITES Ltd. was appointed as the expert agency for DPR, design, and construction supervision of VDA projects.
