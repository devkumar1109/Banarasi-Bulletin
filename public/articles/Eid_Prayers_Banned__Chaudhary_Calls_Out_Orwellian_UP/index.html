Eid Prayers Banned: Chaudhary Calls Out "Orwellian" UP

2025-03-27T17:52:00+00:00

Union Minister Jayant Chaudhary criticized a Uttar Pradesh police order prohibiting street prayers ahead of Eid, calling it "policing towards Orwellian 1984." He expressed concern over the restrictions on religious practices and the potential for communal tension. The order, issued by the UP police, aims to maintain law and order during the festive season but has drawn criticism from various quarters for infringing upon religious freedom.
