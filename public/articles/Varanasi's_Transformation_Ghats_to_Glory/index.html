Varanasi's Transformation: Ghats to Glory

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

India's tourism secretary, V Vidyavathi, has given Varanasi officials two months to implement visible changes to boost tourism. The focus is on 16-20 key locations, including temples, ghats, and Sarnath, with emphasis on sustainable, long-term improvements. Improvements include ghat beautification and cleaning, better roads, electricity, water supply, and transportation. Promotion of spiritual tourism, local crafts like Banarasi sarees, and cultural events are also key. Training for local artists, craftsmen, and guides is crucial, along with involving locals in tourism-related businesses. The secretary directed authorities to run beautification campaigns, remove clutter, install signage, and promote Varanasi online. Financial assistance will be provided to promote yoga, wellness, and Ayurveda centers. Connecting tourists to nearby eco-tourism sites and historic locations is also encouraged to extend their stay.
