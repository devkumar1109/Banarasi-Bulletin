UP's Terai: Biodiversity Meets Ecotourism Gold

2025-05-08T05:06:00+00:00

The Terai region of Uttar Pradesh, known for biodiversity hotspots like Dudhwa and Pilibhit, is emerging as a major ecotourism destination. Lakhimpur Kheri is a key area with the government developing modern facilities near Chandan Chowki and Sharda Barrage. These include eco lodges, tenting, glamping, a tent city, and water sports. Tourists will also experience the Tharu community's cultural heritage through programs and craft exhibitions. The Yogi government is promoting ecotourism through initiatives like the Tourism Policy 2022 and the UP Eco-Tourism Development Board, aiming to attract tourists, create jobs, and protect the environment.
