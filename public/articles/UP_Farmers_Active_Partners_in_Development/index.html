UP Farmers: Active Partners in Development

2025-05-04T11:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that farmers will be active partners in the state's development, not just beneficiaries. He highlighted UP's agricultural contribution, noting that it accounts for 11.41% of India's agricultural land but contributes 20.89% to national food grain production. Food grain production in UP has risen from 557.46 lakh metric tons in 2016-17 to over 725.12 lakh metric tons in 2024-25. The CM emphasized seed quality, directing officials to develop climate-specific seeds and increase payments to farmers for seed purchases. He also stressed the importance of natural farming, technology-based innovations, and transparency in farm equipment distribution. Over 2.81 crore farmers have benefited from the PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana.
