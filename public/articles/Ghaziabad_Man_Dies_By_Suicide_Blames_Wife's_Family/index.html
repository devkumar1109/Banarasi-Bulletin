Ghaziabad Man Dies By Suicide, Blames Wife's Family

2025-04-19T13:33:00+00:00

A 34-year-old man from Ghaziabad, Mohit Tyagi, died by suicide after consuming poison. He accused his wife and her relatives of harassment in a suicide note. Mohit worked at a private firm and had a son with his wife, Priyanka Tyagi, whom he married in 2020. His brother alleges Mohit was under emotional stress due to the harassment. Mohit's family filed a complaint against Priyanka, her brother, sister-in-law, and maternal uncles. An investigation is underway. The couple's marriage reportedly deteriorated within months, and they had a son in 2021.
