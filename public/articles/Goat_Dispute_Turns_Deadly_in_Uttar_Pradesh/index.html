Goat Dispute Turns Deadly in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

A 30-year-old man, Awadhesh Kumar, was killed in Ballia district, Uttar Pradesh, following a dispute with his neighbours over their goat eating grains. Kumar's family reported that their goat entered the neighbour's house and began consuming food grains. When Kumar intervened, a quarrel ensued, leading to threats. Later that evening, two juvenile boys from the neighbour's family attacked Kumar with sticks in a field, leaving him unconscious. He was transported to the district hospital but was pronounced dead. Police recovered the sticks used in the assault and apprehended the juvenile suspects, who have been presented before the juvenile justice board.
