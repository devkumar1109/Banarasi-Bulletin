Kapil Dev Meets Yogi Adityanath, PGTI

2025-05-02T07:09:00+00:00

Kapil Dev, 1983 World Cup-winning captain and current PGTI chief, met Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath in Lucknow. Kapil Dev, a renowned cricketer with a distinguished career, has also served as a coach, commentator, and chancellor of the Sports University of Haryana. He assumed the PGTI presidency in June 2024, having previously served as vice president and board member. Under his leadership, PGTI launched the Adani Invitational Golf Championship 2025, a Rs 1.5 crore tournament held at Jaypee Greens Golf Spa Resort, marking PGTI's return to the venue after 11 years.
