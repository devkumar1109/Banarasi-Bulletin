Land Dispute Turns Deadly: Former Friend Murders Supervisor

2025-03-31T07:40:00+00:00

Pankaj Kumar, a 34-year-old construction supervisor, was murdered on April 16, 2024, in Pune. His body was found in a duct of an under-construction building. The Kondhwa police investigation revealed Kumar was killed with a sharp weapon on the fifth floor and the body was then thrown into the duct. The murder was initially disguised as a fall. The investigation led to Suresh Maniram Arya, 34, a former colleague of Kumar's, who fled to Uttar Pradesh. Police suspect their friendship soured due to a land dispute in their native village.
