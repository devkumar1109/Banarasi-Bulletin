Maurya Dubs Opponents "Liars," Welcomes Caste Census

2025-05-02T07:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya criticized Congress and Samajwadi Party as "automatic lying machines" for misleading the public. He welcomed the caste census decision, terming it a step towards "Congress mukt Bharat" and empowering backward communities. Maurya stated that PM Modi's vision surpasses opposition pressures, exemplified by the timely caste census decision. He highlighted that the opposition's attempts to corner the government on this issue have failed, acknowledging PM Modi's commitment to work for everyone's benefit. Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav, while welcoming the census, called it a victory for marginalized groups (PDA).
