Bride's Third Wedding Turns Deadly

2025-05-18T02:46:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Uttar Pradesh's Varanasi district, Raju Pal, has been arrested for murdering his wife, Aarti Pal (26), just a week after their wedding. This was Raju's third marriage, and clashes reportedly began immediately after the wedding. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death at a local hospital. The police have arrested Raju and sent Aarti's body for post-mortem.
