BHU Workshop: Heart of Sports Injury Care

2025-04-23T17:43:00+00:00

A three-day workshop on Sports Injury Prevention and Rehabilitation, organized by Banaras Hindu University, began on Tuesday. Noted cardiologist Prof Vikas Agarwal highlighted the need for a multidisciplinary approach to athlete care, emphasizing the connection between cardiac health, performance, and rehabilitation. Prof Agarwal stressed the importance of integrating cardiovascular fitness with physical rehabilitation for holistic athlete management. The workshop, initially an institute-level program, expanded to a national event due to high participation from across India. 125 participants, including coaches, athletic trainers, and students, are attending the event.
