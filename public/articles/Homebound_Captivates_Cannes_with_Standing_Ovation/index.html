"Homebound" Captivates Cannes with Standing Ovation

2025-06-03T23:17:00+00:00

Neeraj Ghaywan's film "Homebound," produced by Martin Scorsese and Karan Johar, received a nine-minute standing ovation at its Cannes premiere. The film, Ghaywan's second feature after "Masaan" (2015), explores themes of Dalit humiliation, alternate sexuality, and betrayal within the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Ghaywan, a former engineer and MBA graduate, has dedicated himself to cinema, using his films to highlight social issues affecting marginalized communities, including Dalits, Muslims, and women. "Geeli Pucchi," his short film included in the Netflix anthology "Ajeeb Dastaan," also addresses these themes.
