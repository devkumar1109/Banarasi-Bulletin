Varun Joins "Raanjhan" Fan Club, Kriti Films "Tere Ishk Mein"

2025-06-15T04:03:00+00:00

Varun Dhawan expressed his admiration for Kriti Sanon's song "Raanjhan" from the film "Do Patti," to which Kriti playfully welcomed him to the "club" of fans. Kriti is currently filming the final schedule of her movie "Tere Ishk Mein" with Dhanush in Mumbai. The film, directed by Aanand L Rai, is expected to release in 2025. Sources describe Kriti's performance as demanding and emotionally impactful. "Tere Ishk Mein," produced by Aanand L Rai and Bhushan Kumar, features music by AR Rahman and lyrics by Irshad Kamil. Varun Dhawan recently completed filming in Scotland for his upcoming comedy "Hai Jawani Toh Ishq Hona Hai," directed by David Dhawan.
