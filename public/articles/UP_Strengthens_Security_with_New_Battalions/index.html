UP Strengthens Security with New Battalions

2025-04-20T00:56:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government, under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, is prioritizing security infrastructure strengthening. To achieve this, the government is rapidly constructing new campuses for the Uttar Pradesh Special Security Force (UPSSF) battalions. A 3rd battalion will be built in Prayagraj, with residential and non-residential facilities on 42.03 acres of land in the Arail area, including 22 acres in Deoria village and 19 acres in Bhaipur village. Simultaneously, a 4th battalion will be established in Mathura, aligning with CM Yogi Adityanath's security vision.
