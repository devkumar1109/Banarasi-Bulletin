Varanasi Cruises to Final, Goraakhpur Edges Closer

2025-04-19T18:19:00+00:00

Varanasi defeated Saharanpur 4-0 to advance to the final of the UP under-20 inter divisional state men football championship. Ashish, Asif, Abhishek, and Karunesh scored for Varanasi. In another semi-final, Goraakhpur beat Lucknow 1-0 with Amritansh scoring the lone goal. Kanpur also reached the semi-final after defeating Ayodhya 2-0 in extra time.
