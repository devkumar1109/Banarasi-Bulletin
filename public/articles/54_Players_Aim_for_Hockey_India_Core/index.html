54 Players Aim for Hockey India Core

2025-04-24T10:32:00+00:00

Hockey India has named a 54-member core probable group for the senior men's national coaching camp, starting April 25th in Bengaluru. 38 players are retained from the existing core group. 16 new players were selected based on their performance in the 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025 held in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. The initial camp will run until April 30th, after which the group will be reduced to 40 players. These 40 players will continue training in the next phase of the camp from May 1st to 25th.
