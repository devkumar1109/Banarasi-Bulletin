Vision 11, CSK Extend Winning Fantasy Partnership

2025-03-27T08:13:00+00:00

Vision 11, a leading Indian sports fantasy gaming platform, has renewed its partnership with the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) for the third consecutive year. This collaboration makes Vision 11 the official fantasy sports partner of the five-time IPL champions. Founded in 2021 by Parth Rawal, Vision 11 boasts over 20 million users and has rapidly grown in popularity. The CSK, led by Ruturaj Gaikwad and guided by MS Dhoni, continue their quest for more IPL titles with Vision 11 by their side.
