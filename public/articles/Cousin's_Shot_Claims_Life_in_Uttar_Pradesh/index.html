Cousin's Shot Claims Life in Uttar Pradesh.

2025-05-25T08:53:00+00:00

An 18-year-old youth, Pradeep, was shot dead by his cousin, Sarthak, in Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on Saturday when Pradeep was standing outside his house. Sarthak allegedly used a licensed rifle to shoot Pradeep, who died instantly. Pradeep's family informed the police several hours after the incident. Police have collected evidence and will register a case against Sarthak, who is currently absconding.
