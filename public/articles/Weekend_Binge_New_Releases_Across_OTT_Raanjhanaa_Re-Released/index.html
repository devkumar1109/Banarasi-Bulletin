Weekend Binge: New Releases Across OTT, "Raanjhanaa" Re-Released

2025-02-28T07:00:00+00:00

This weekend sees several new releases across OTT platforms. JioHotstar features the highly anticipated "Aashram Season 3 Part 2" with Bobby Deol, and the "Suits LA" spin-off. Netflix adds the comedy series "Running Point" starring Kate Hudson and the crime drama "Dabba Cartel" with a star-studded cast including Shabana Azmi and Jyotika. Amazon Prime Video offers the true story-based film "Superboys Of Malegaon" and the "Beetlejuice Beetlejuice" sequel. In theaters, "Raanjhanaa" sees a re-release.
