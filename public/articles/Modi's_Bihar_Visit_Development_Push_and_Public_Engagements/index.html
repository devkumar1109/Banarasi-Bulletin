Modi's Bihar Visit: Development Push and Public Engagements

2025-05-17T13:23:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Bihar on May 29 for a two-day trip. He will inaugurate the newly constructed terminal at the Jai Prakash Narayan International Airport in Patna and address a public meeting in Vikramganj, Rohtas district. During his visit, PM Modi will also inaugurate and dedicate several development projects in Bihar, including roads, electricity, and airport projects. He will also lay the foundation stone for projects worth crores of rupees, such as a four-lane highway from Patna to Sasaram and Varanasi to Ranchi, a 600 MW power plant in Navinagar, and the expansion of Bihta airport. This is PM Modi's first visit to Bihar after Operation Sindoor.
