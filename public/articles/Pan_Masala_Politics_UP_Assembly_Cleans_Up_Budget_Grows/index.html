Pan Masala Politics: UP Assembly Cleans Up, Budget Grows

2025-03-04T07:11:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh assembly speaker Satish Mahana revealed that some MLAs were spitting pan masala inside the House. He personally cleaned the affected area and identified the MLA responsible through video footage but chose not to disclose the name to avoid embarrassment. Mahana urged the MLA to confess and called on all members to maintain decorum and cleanliness. The incident sparked discussions on discipline within the Assembly. Meanwhile, Uttar Pradesh finance minister Suresh Kumar Khanna presented the state budget for 2025-26, which is 9.8% higher than the previous year's budget. CM Yogi Adityanath emphasized the budget's focus on infrastructure development.
