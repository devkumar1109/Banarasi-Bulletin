Prayagraj Tent House Engulfed in Flames

2025-04-19T02:50:00+00:00

A fire erupted in a tent house godown in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh on Saturday. The blaze generated thick smoke that enveloped the sky. Fire tenders arrived at the scene and are actively working to extinguish the fire. Authorities have confirmed no casualties at this time.
