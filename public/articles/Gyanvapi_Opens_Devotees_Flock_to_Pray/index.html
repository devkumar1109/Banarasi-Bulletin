Gyanvapi Opens: Devotees Flock to Pray

2025-04-02T18:52:00+00:00

Thousands of devotees joined plaintiffs in praying to Shringar Gauri at Gyanvapi, Varanasi, on the fourth day of Chaitra Navratri. This marked the first time since the Babri Masjid demolition that there were no restrictions on the number of devotees visiting the site. The Kashi Vishwanath Dham administration facilitated the rituals, arranging offerings and supporting the event. Gyanvapi Mukti Mahaparishad, an organization supported by the RSS, organized the gathering, claiming it demonstrated the growing strength of the pro-Hindu government. The group pledged to liberate all ancient historical sites, including Gyanvasi complex. They alleged that previous governments obstructed worship due to appeasement politics, while the current government facilitates religious practices.
