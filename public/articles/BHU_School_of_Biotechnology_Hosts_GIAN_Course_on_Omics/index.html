BHU School of Biotechnology Hosts GIAN Course on Omics

2025-06-10T19:20:00+00:00

The School of Biotechnology at BHU hosted a week-long GIAN course on "Models and Algorithms For Omics Data Analysis" from June 2nd to 6th. This initiative, under the Ministry of Education's Global Initiative of Academic Networks (GIAN), aims to enhance India's scientific and technological capabilities. Prof Rajeev Kumar Azad from the University of North Texas, USA, delivered expert insights during the course.
