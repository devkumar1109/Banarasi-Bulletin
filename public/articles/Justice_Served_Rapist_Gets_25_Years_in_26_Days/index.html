Justice Served: Rapist Gets 25 Years in 26 Days

2025-05-30T02:53:00+00:00

A man named Khurshid Alam alias Mohammad Hamza was sentenced to 25 years rigorous imprisonment and fined Rs 50,000 for raping a 5-year-old girl in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh. This is the first time in the state where a conviction in a child rape case has been achieved within 26 days under the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita. The investigation was completed within 24 hours and the prosecution successfully advocated for a swift verdict under the ‘Operation Conviction' campaign. The accused was found guilty under sections 65(2) BNS and 5M/6 POCSO Act.
