Modi Power Up: UP Infrastructure Boom

2025-05-30T12:53:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated several infrastructure and power projects in Uttar Pradesh. This included the Kanpur Metro's new section connecting Chunniganj to Kanpur Central, the Panki Thermal Power Plant extension, and the Ghatampur Thermal Power Project. He also opened widened sections of G.T. Road and inaugurated 132 kV substations in Greater Noida. Foundation stones were laid for a new 220 kV substation in YEIDA and road projects, including the widening of Gauria Pali Marg and a road connecting the Kanpur defence node. Modi also distributed certificates and cheques to beneficiaries of various government schemes.
