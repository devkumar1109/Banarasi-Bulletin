Rai's "Lemon-Chili" Jibe Lands Him in FIR

2025-05-08T10:10:00+00:00

Varanasi police have filed an FIR against Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai for his 'Neebu-Mircha' jibe on Rafale fighter jets. The FIR, filed based on a complaint by Pradeep Kumar Gupta of Rashtriya Hindu Shakti Vahini, alleges that Rai's comments were prejudicial to national integration and promoted public mischief. The complaint claims Rai's statement demoralized the army and incited communal tensions. Rai made the "Neebu-Mircha" (lemon and chili) jibe during a May 5 press conference, displaying a toy airplane and sarcastically referring to it as a Rafale.
