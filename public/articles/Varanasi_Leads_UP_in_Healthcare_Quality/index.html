Varanasi Leads UP in Healthcare Quality

2025-03-20T18:11:00+00:00

Varanasi topped Uttar Pradesh in NQAS certification, a national quality assurance standard for healthcare facilities. Four urban primary health centers – Madanpura, Pandeypur, Town Hall, and Durgakund – achieved certification along with four Ayushman Arogya Mandirs (Barai, Singhpur, Ajgara Pratham, and Lalpur). Varanasi secured first place in UP with a total of 29 certified centers. The NQAS certification is awarded based on seven standards, including maternal and child health, family planning, and disease management. Currently, 65 more Ayushman Arogya Mandirs in the district are awaiting certification, with results for five urban primary health centers and 36 Ayushman Arogya Mandirs pending.
