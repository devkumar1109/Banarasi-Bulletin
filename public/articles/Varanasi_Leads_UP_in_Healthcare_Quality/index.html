Varanasi Leads UP in Healthcare Quality

2025-03-20T18:11:00+00:00

Varanasi topped Uttar Pradesh in NQAS (National Quality Assurance Standards) certification. Four urban primary health centers - Madanpura, Pandeypur, Town Hall, and Durgakund - and four Ayushman Arogya Mandirs - Barai, Singhpur, Ajgara Pratham, and Lalpur - received the certification. Varanasi secured the first position in UP with 29 centers receiving NQAS certification. The certified centers scored between 82% and 93% based on seven standards, including care during pregnancy and childbirth, childhood health services, family planning, and management of communicable and non-communicable diseases. The certification process is ongoing, with assessment completed for five more primary health centers and 36 Ayushman Arogya Mandirs.
