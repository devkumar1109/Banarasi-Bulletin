Love Triangle Turns Deadly in Lucknow Murder

2025-06-16T20:26:00+00:00

A car driver in Lucknow, Sanjay, was murdered on Sunday night with a sharp-edged weapon. The attack stemmed from an alleged love affair Sanjay was involved in with a married woman, Meera. Two men entered Sanjay's house, attacked him, and fatally injured him. Sanjay's mother pleaded for mercy, but the assailants continued the assault. Meera alleges her husband, Sunil, was behind the murder due to his anger over her relationship with Sanjay. Police have detained four suspects, including Sunil, and are investigating the case. No formal complaint has been registered yet.
