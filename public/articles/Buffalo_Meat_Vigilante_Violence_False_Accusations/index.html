Buffalo Meat, Vigilante Violence, False Accusations

2025-05-28T20:50:00+00:00

Four men were assaulted by cow vigilantes in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, after being wrongly accused of transporting beef. Forensic tests have confirmed that the meat in their vehicle was buffalo meat. Two FIRs were filed, one by the victims' families and another by the accused, alleging extortion and cow slaughter respectively. Four individuals have been arrested in connection with the incident. The victims were allegedly beaten with rods, their vehicle set ablaze, and their belongings stolen. The case highlights the dangers of vigilantism and the need for due process.
