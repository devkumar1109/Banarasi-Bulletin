Shah in Chennai: BJP Seeks AIADMK Alliance Reboot

2025-04-11T01:56:00+00:00

Union Home Minister Amit Shah's visit to Chennai is crucial for the BJP as it seeks to revive its alliance with the AIADMK ahead of the 2024 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections. The AIADMK has set conditions for the alliance, including choosing its own Chief Minister candidate and making all final decisions related to the alliance. Shah's visit aims to address these issues and determine the future of Tamil Nadu BJP chief Annamalai, who was involved in the previous alliance break-up. Shah will also meet state BJP functionaries and RSS ideologue S Gurumurthy. Meanwhile, developments in BJP ally PMK saw its founder S Ramadoss replace his son Anbumani Ramadoss as president.
