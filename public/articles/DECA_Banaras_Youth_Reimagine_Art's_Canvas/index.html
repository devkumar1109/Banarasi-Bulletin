DECA: Banaras Youth Reimagine Art's Canvas

2025-04-15T18:43:00+00:00

To mark World Art Day, 10 young artists from Banaras Hindu University's Faculty of Visual Arts are showcasing their work in a three-day exhibition titled 'DECA'. Each artist brings a unique perspective shaped by their experiences, exploring themes like human relationships, identity, socio-cultural transitions, and cosmic symbolism. Influences range from traditional Indian art forms to abstract symbolism and environmental consciousness. The exhibition, curated by Suresh Chandra Jangid, serves as a platform for these emerging artists to express their creative voices.
