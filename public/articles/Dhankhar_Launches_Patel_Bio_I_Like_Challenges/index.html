Dhankhar Launches Patel Bio: "I Like Challenges"

2025-05-01T13:46:00+00:00

Vice President Jagdeep Dhankhar humorously launched Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel's biography, titled 'Chunautiyan Mujhe Pasand Hain' (I Like Challenges), in Lucknow on Thursday. Dhankhar likened marriage to a lifelong challenge, drawing laughter from attendees, including Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. Governor Patel announced that the book, priced at Rs 500, will support education for underprivileged children.
