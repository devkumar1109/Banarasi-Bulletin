Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train: Speeding Towards a New Era

2025-03-24T13:29:00+00:00

The Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train is a proposed 760 km high-speed rail corridor connecting Varanasi and Howrah, passing through key cities like Buxar, Arrah, Patna, Gaya, Dhanbad, Asansol, Durgapur, and Burdwan. The train is designed for speeds up to 350 km/h, aiming to reduce travel time between the cities to just over two hours. The project is currently in the Detailed Project Report (DPR) stage, with surveys underway to finalize the route and assess land requirements. Construction is expected to begin once railway approval is granted. The route will include approximately 260 km of elevated tracks within Bihar.
