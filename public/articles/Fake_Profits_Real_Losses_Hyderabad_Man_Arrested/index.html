Fake Profits, Real Losses: Hyderabad Man Arrested

2025-04-22T07:24:00+00:00

Hyderabad cyber crime police arrested 23-year-old Prateek Shukla from Uttar Pradesh for his involvement in a stock trading fraud. Shukla worked as an account supplier for three bogus online investment companies – 5paisa Capital Ltd, Barclays and Shanda Capital Group Ltd. He allegedly lured victims into investing by sharing fake screenshots of profits in a WhatsApp group. A 68-year-old victim from Hyderabad lost Rs 52,29,500 after being misled by the group. The police urge citizens to be cautious of online investment scams, particularly those promising high returns in short periods. Victims of cyber crime fraud are advised to contact 1930 or visit https://cybercrime.gov.in/ for assistance.
