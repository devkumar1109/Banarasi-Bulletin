Kumar Recalls Dismissing Tendulkar in Ranji Final

2025-05-07T15:37:00+00:00

Bhuvneshwar Kumar recently shared a memorable moment from his career: dismissing Sachin Tendulkar in the 2008-09 Ranji Trophy final. Kumar, in his second season, bowled an inside edge that was caught by a fielder who was positioned unexpectedly. Kumar took six wickets in the match and scored 121 runs, though Uttar Pradesh lost to Mumbai by 243 runs. Kumar also credited Virat Kohli for transforming India's Test team by bringing aggression to the format.
