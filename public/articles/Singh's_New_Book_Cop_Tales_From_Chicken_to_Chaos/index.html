Singh's New Book: Cop Tales, From Chicken to Chaos

2025-06-01T06:02:00+00:00

Former Uttar Pradesh DGP O P Singh's new book, "Through My Eyes: Sketches from A Cop’s Notebook," recounts anecdotes from his 37-year career. One story involves a caterer who fed former Prime Minister Chaudhary Charan Singh's entourage and was denied a Rs 7,000 bill due to the mention of "chicken" on the menu. Another anecdote describes a home minister being forgotten at a railway station. Singh, a 1983-batch IPS officer, retired in 2020 after heading the CISF and NDRF. His previous book, "Crime, Grime and Gumption," focused on case files from his time as an IPS officer.
