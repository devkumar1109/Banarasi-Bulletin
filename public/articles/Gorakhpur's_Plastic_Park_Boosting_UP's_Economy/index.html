Gorakhpur's Plastic Park: Boosting UP's Economy

2025-06-16T11:55:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's first plastic park, spanning 88 acres in Gorakhpur, is nearing completion. Developed by GIDA, the park aims to boost the downstream plastic processing industry, investment, production, exports, and employment. Production has begun in several units, with CM Yogi Adityanath set to inaugurate three major companies with a combined investment of Rs 120 crore. Technoplast Packaging Private Limited is the largest investor with Rs 96 crore, followed by Om Flax Industries Private Limited (Rs 17 crore) and Gajanan Polyplast (Rs 7 crore). Three additional companies have secured land, with a combined investment of Rs 640 crore, creating 1,200 jobs. CIPET will establish a skill training centre, while a Common Effluent Treatment Plant will address environmental concerns. The Central Government is contributing Rs 281 crore for infrastructure and a grant of Rs 69.58 crore for the Common Effluent Treatment Plant.
