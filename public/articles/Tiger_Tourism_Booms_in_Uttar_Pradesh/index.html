Tiger Tourism Booms in Uttar Pradesh

2025-06-15T17:40:00+00:00

The seven-month ecotourism session in Uttar Pradesh's tiger reserves and wildlife sanctuaries ended on Sunday. The Pilibhit Tiger Reserve (PTR) saw a record 56,289 tourists this season, a significant increase from the previous year's 54,378. Revenue also rose to ₹1.60 crore. PTR's Chuka ecotourism site, known for its scenic beauty and unique boat safaris, attracted a significant number of both domestic and foreign tourists. However, Dr. Amitabh Agnihotri criticized the revenue-sharing model, where only 10% of ecotourism revenue goes to tiger reserves and sanctuaries, arguing that retaining the full revenue could benefit conservation efforts.
