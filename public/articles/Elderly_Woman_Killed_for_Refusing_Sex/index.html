Elderly Woman Killed for Refusing Sex

2025-05-28T02:04:00+00:00

A 65-year-old woman, Sawari Devi, was killed by her romantic partner, Dinesh, in Kaushambi, Uttar Pradesh. Devi refused to engage in sexual activity, citing poor health. Dinesh, believing her excuses, attempted to force himself upon her. When she resisted, he strangled her with a cloth. The incident took place on May 28, 2025.
