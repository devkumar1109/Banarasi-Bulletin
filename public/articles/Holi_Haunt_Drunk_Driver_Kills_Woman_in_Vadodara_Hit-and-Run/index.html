Holi Haunt: Drunk Driver Kills Woman in Vadodara Hit-and-Run

2025-03-16T12:14:00+00:00

A fatal hit-and-run accident occurred in Vadodara, Gujarat, on Holi eve. A speeding car driven by 20-year-old law student Rakshit Ravish Chaurasia collided with a scooter, killing a woman and injuring seven others. Chaurasia, claiming he was not intoxicated, stated he was driving at 50 km/hour. However, eyewitness videos suggest he was drunk, shouting "Another round" and mentioning "Nikita" while chanting "Om Namah Shivaya." Police are investigating the case, including conducting a medical test to determine if Chaurasia was driving under the influence of alcohol. He has been apprehended and faces charges related to the accident.
