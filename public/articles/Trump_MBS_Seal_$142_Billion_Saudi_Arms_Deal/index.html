Trump, MBS Seal $142 Billion Saudi Arms Deal

2025-05-14T11:00:00+00:00

President Donald Trump and Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman signed a $142 billion arms deal in Riyadh on May 13, 2025. This is the largest defense agreement in U.S. history, providing Saudi Arabia with advanced military equipment and technology for air, space, coastal defenses, border security, and communication systems. The deal aims to strengthen the strategic partnership between the two countries, including cooperation in energy, defense, and justice sectors. Memorandums of understanding were signed covering energy, defense, mining, ammunition, training, health capabilities for the Saudi Armed Forces, and space weather monitoring. Agreements also involved collaboration between the FBI and Saudi Ministry of Interior, and the U.S. and Saudi Departments of Justice.
