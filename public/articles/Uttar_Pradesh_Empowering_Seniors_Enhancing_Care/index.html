Uttar Pradesh: Empowering Seniors, Enhancing Care

2025-04-04T01:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is enhancing healthcare for senior citizens in old age homes by integrating them into the Ayushman Bharat scheme. To promote self-reliance, a program will utilize the professional and business expertise of senior citizens, offering financial incentives. Currently, state-run old age homes provide free accommodation, meals, medical care, and entertainment. Over 1,500 senior citizens participated in the recent Maha Kumbh with the government providing temporary accommodations and medical camps. To address legal and family disputes, 216 reconciliation officers have been appointed at the tehsil level, and DM-led committees oversee old age home management.
