IIT Roorkee Upskills UP Youth for Global Jobs

2025-05-01T11:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Skill Development Mission (UPSDM) has partnered with IIT Roorkee to provide technical training and skill development opportunities to UP youth. Under this MoU, young people will gain access to advanced technical training, research opportunities, and startup support, mentored by IIT Roorkee experts. This collaboration aims to enhance the employability of UP youth on both national and international platforms. The agreement aligns with UPSDM's Vision-2025, which focuses on digital technology, joint projects, and skill upgradation.
