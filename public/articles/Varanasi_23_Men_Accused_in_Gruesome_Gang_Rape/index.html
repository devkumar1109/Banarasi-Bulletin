Varanasi: 23 Men Accused in Gruesome Gang Rape

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh was allegedly kidnapped and gang-raped by 23 men over a week, between March 29 and April 4. The ordeal began when a friend took her to a hookah bar, where she was allegedly drugged and taken to various hotels. Some of the accused were former classmates and individuals she knew through Instagram. Her family filed a police complaint after she returned home on April 4. Police have filed an FIR against all 23 accused, with six in custody and 11 still unidentified.
