UP Bicycle Repairman's Son Tops UPSC

2025-04-24T00:43:00+00:00

Iqbal Ahmed, a resident of Uttar Pradesh, secured 998th rank in the 2024 UPSC examination. Despite facing financial hardship, his family supported his passion for studies. His father, Maqbool, a bicycle repairman, worked tirelessly to provide for his four children. Iqbal's success story is an inspiration to UPSC aspirants across India.
