Varanasi Man Arrested in Pakistani Spy Sting

2025-05-24T05:43:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested Mohammad Tufail from Varanasi for suspected involvement in a Pakistani spy network. Tufail allegedly spread extremist propaganda among Muslim youth through WhatsApp groups, sharing content from radical preachers like Maulana Saad Rizvi and promoting Sharia law as the foundation of governance. Over 800 mobile numbers linked to these groups are now under surveillance by the ATS. Tufail is believed to have travelled to various locations within Uttar Pradesh and Punjab for secret meetings and gatherings with local youth. The arrest could lead to further revelations about the network's activities.
