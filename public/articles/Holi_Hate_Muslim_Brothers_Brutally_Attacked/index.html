Holi Hate: Muslim Brothers Brutally Attacked

2025-03-12T13:49:00+00:00

Two Muslim brothers, Niyaz Ahmed and Khurshid Ahmed, were brutally assaulted in Varanasi's Dindayalpur village during a Holi donation drive. A group of about a dozen individuals attacked them with sharp weapons and sticks, leaving them with severe injuries. The brothers were forced to chant "Jai Shri Ram" and faced religious slurs. Niyaz was attacked after returning home from mosque prayers, while Khurshid intervened to stop the assault on his brother. An FIR has been filed at Sarnath Police Station, and the police are investigating the incident. The attack has sparked outrage on social media, with demands for swift justice.
