IIT Bhilai to Boom: Rs 1,400 cr Expansion Unveiled

2025-05-16T09:20:00+00:00

IIT Bhilai will undergo a major expansion with a Rs 1,400 crore investment approved by the Central Government. This will increase the institute's student capacity to 2,750 from the current 1,250. The expansion includes two new engineering departments, a science department, advanced laboratories, three hostels, a visitor hostel, and staff residential quarters. A full-sized sports complex and facilities like an administrative building and auditorium will also be built. A key feature is the establishment of Chhattisgarh's first research park, spanning 15,000 square meters, modelled after similar facilities at IIT Madras and IIT Bombay. Upon completion, the campus will accommodate 12,000 students, 1,200 faculty, and 1,320 support staff.
