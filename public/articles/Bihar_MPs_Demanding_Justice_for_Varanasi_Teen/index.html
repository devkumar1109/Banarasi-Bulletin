Bihar MPs Demanding Justice for Varanasi Teen

2025-03-10T08:07:00+00:00

Opposition MPs from Bihar protested outside Parliament, demanding justice for a 17-year-old girl found dead in her hostel room in Varanasi. Allegations of rape and murder have been made, drawing comparisons to the 2020 Hathras case. The incident occurred in PM Modi's Lok Sabha constituency. MPs from the RJD, CPI(ML) Liberation, and Congress participated in the protest, raising concerns about the postmortem and last rites handled by the police. They called for a thorough investigation into the matter.
