Jail Official Accused of Casteist Harassment

2025-03-19T10:43:00+00:00

A deputy jailer's daughter in Varanasi filed a complaint alleging sexual and caste-based harassment of her mother, a Scheduled Caste officer, by Umesh Singh, a senior official. Neha Shah's complaint states that her mother suffered months of mental and physical harassment from Singh. The incident highlights concerns about caste discrimination and workplace harassment within the Uttar Pradesh jail system.
