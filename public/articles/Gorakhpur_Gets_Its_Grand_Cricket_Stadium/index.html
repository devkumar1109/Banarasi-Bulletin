Gorakhpur Gets Its Grand Cricket Stadium

2025-05-19T11:35:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will have its fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur, a project championed by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. Construction has begun on the 50-acre site at Tal Nadaur, estimated to cost Rs 236.40 crore and be completed in 18 months. The stadium, 23.6 km from Gorakhpur airport, will have seven main pitches, four practice pitches, and a seating capacity of 30,000. Designed to meet ICC standards, it will host international cricket matches and other large events. The stadium will feature East and West stands with 14,490 seats each, a North Pavilion with VIP and media galleries, and parking for 1,500 vehicles. Amenities include concourses, restrooms, and merchandise stores.
