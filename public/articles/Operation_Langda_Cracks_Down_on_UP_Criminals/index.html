Operation Langda Cracks Down on UP Criminals

2025-05-28T08:25:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Police launched "Operation Langda" targeting criminals with a zero-tolerance approach to crime. In the last 24 hours, 10 encounters occurred across 8 cities, resulting in the arrest of numerous high-profile criminals. Notable arrests include a rape accused in Lucknow, a constable killer in Ghaziabad, and a Rs 25,000 bounty criminal in Shamli. Encounters also took place in Jhansi, Bulandshahr, Baghpat, Agra, Jalaun, Ballia, and Unnao, leading to the apprehension of individuals accused of rape, murder, robbery, theft, and cow smuggling.
