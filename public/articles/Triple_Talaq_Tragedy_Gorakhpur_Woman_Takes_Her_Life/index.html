Triple Talaq Tragedy: Gorakhpur Woman Takes Her Life

2025-05-01T07:25:00+00:00

In Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, 24-year-old Saniya died by suicide after her husband, Salauddin, allegedly pronounced triple talaq over the phone. Saniya had been enduring dowry harassment from her in-laws since her marriage on August 7, 2023. Her mother, Asiya, had filed a complaint with the local police, but no action was taken. A sub-inspector, Jay Prakash Singh, has been suspended for his inaction. Saniya's family alleges persistent abuse despite attempts at reconciliation. After Salauddin abandoned her, Saniya returned to her maternal home and took her own life following the phone call.
