ATM Skimming Gang Busted in Chennai

2025-05-26T09:00:00+00:00

Chennai police have arrested a gang from Uttar Pradesh involved in ATM tampering and cash theft. The gang specialized in installing skimming devices on ATMs to steal card information and then withdrawing cash using cloned cards. This operation highlights the increasing sophistication of ATM fraud and the importance of vigilance against such crimes.
