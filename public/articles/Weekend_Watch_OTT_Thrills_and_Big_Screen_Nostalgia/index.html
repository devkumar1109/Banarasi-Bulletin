Weekend Watch: OTT Thrills and Big Screen Nostalgia

2025-02-28T07:00:00+00:00

This weekend sees new releases across various OTT platforms. JioHotstar offers the latest installment of "Aashram" with Bobby Deol and Chandan Roy Sanyal, along with the legal drama "Suits LA" and the horror sequel "Beetlejuice Beetlejuice". Netflix features the comedy series "Running Point" starring Kate Hudson and the crime drama "Dabba Cartel" with Shabana Azmi and Jyotika. Amazon Prime Video releases the true story-based film "Superboys Of Malegaon" starring Adarsh Gourav and Shashank Arora. Finally, the Bollywood film "Raanjhanaa" starring Dhanush and Sonam Kapoor gets a theatrical re-release.
