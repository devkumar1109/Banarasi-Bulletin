Teen Girl Attacks Shopkeeper Over Return

2025-05-04T12:42:00+00:00

A 15-year-old girl attacked a shopkeeper in Hapur, Uttar Pradesh with a blade after he refused to return or exchange goods. The incident was captured on CCTV and footage has been submitted to the police. The video shows the girl engaging in a verbal altercation before attacking the shopkeeper. A woman, possibly a customer or relative, intervened and restrained the girl. The incident has raised concerns about youth violence and mental health.
