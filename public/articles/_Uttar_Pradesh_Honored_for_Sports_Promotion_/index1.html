"UP, UP, and Away: Uttar Pradesh Wins Top Honors at TOI Sports Awards"

2025-02-23T19:59:00+00:00

Here is a 150-word factual summary of the news article: The Times of India (TOI) hosted an award show in Lucknow, honoring outstanding sportspersons. Uttar Pradesh was awarded the Best State for Sports Promotion, with Sports Minister Girish Chandra Yadav receiving the award from Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. Special recognition was given to athletes from UP, including Vijay Sharma, Shivpal Singh, Shaili Singh, Annu Rani, Mohd Aman, and Akash Kumar. Young athletes Gukesh Dommaraju and Manu Bhaker were also awarded for their achievements. Gukesh became the youngest world chess champion, while Manu Bhaker became the first Indian to win two medals in the same Olympics. Suhas LY, a para badminton player, received the Change Agent of Sports award, and Sumit Antil, a record-breaking javelin thrower, received the Para Sportsperson Award. Kapil Dev received the Lifetime Achievement Award from PT Usha. The event was attended by notable sports personalities, including Vijendra Singh, Dilip Tirkey, and Yogeshwar Dutt.
