Varanasi Fights Disease with "Dastak" Campaign

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, chikungunya, and diarrhea. The campaign aims to curb the spread of waterborne and other diseases. The district identified 68 dengue hotspots in urban areas and 56 in rural areas. The Dastak campaign, focusing on house-to-house visits, will run from April 10 to 31. In 2025, only 22 dengue cases, 2 malaria cases, and 18 chikungunya cases have been reported compared to significantly higher numbers in 2023 and 2024. SVM Bhelupur Hospital introduced new services, including an ultrasound machine, digital X-ray, dental department, eye examination, and mental health OPD.
