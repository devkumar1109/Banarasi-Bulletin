UP's Holy Sites: Ayodhya, Varanasi, Prayagraj Shine

2025-04-29T14:13:00+00:00

A recent survey by Deloitte, commissioned by the Union Ministry of Tourism and Culture, identifies Ayodhya, Varanasi, and Prayagraj as the top spiritual and tourist destinations in Uttar Pradesh, attracting both domestic and international visitors. The survey, 'Maha Kumbh Spiritual Survey–2025', involved 1,093 foreign tourists and 351,295 domestic pilgrims. Findings show that pilgrims spend an average of Rs 5,877.63 per visit, significantly contributing to UP's GSDP. Ayodhya was the top overall favorite destination, followed by Prayagraj and Varanasi. While domestic tourists mainly travel with family, foreign tourists prefer solo trips.
