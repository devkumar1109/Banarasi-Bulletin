Varanasi Horror: Rape, Outrage, and Calls for Reform

2025-04-16T10:22:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi was allegedly drugged and gang-raped by 23 men over six days in March 2025. 13 men have been arrested, and a senior police officer was removed due to allegations of negligence. Prime Minister Narendra Modi intervened, demanding the strictest action against the perpetrators and calling for systemic reforms to prevent such crimes. The survivor's family and activists are demanding swift justice and stronger protections for women. The incident sparked nationwide outrage and criticism of the police response, with calls for improved police training, victim support, and accountability mechanisms. Women's rights organizations have organized protests, highlighting the systemic challenges and the need for comprehensive reforms to address gender-based violence.
