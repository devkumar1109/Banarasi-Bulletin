Drunk Driver's Crash Kills One, Injures Four

2025-03-14T10:08:00+00:00

A drunk driver, Rakshit Chaurasia, killed a woman and injured four others in a car crash in Vadodara, Gujarat. The accident occurred around 12:30 am on Thursday near the Karelibag area. Chaurasia, a 20-year-old law student from Uttar Pradesh, was reportedly driving at 120 km/hour and rammed his car into two scooters. Video footage shows Chaurasia exiting the heavily damaged car and shouting "another round" while bystanders attempted to restrain him. Mit Chauhan, a passenger in the car, was also arrested.
