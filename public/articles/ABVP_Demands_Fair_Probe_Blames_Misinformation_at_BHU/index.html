ABVP Demands Fair Probe, Blames "Misinformation" at BHU

2025-04-20T15:05:00+00:00

The ABVP (student wing of RSS) is demanding a fair investigation into alleged irregularities in the Hindi department's admission process at BHU. They claim that the issue stems from departmental negligence, not ABVP's actions, and criticize a "deliberate campaign of misinformation" against their unit secretary. The ABVP also urges BHU administration to curb "anti-academic and disruptive elements" to ensure a safe learning environment. They accuse certain politically affiliated student organizations of exploiting admission process discrepancies to create unrest on campus and demand their exclusion.
