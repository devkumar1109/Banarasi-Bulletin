Baba Sivanand, Yoga Guru, Dies at 128

2025-05-04T05:12:00+00:00

Padma Shri awardee Baba Sivanand, a spiritual guru, died in Varanasi on Saturday night at the age of 128 due to health complications. He was admitted to BHU Hospital on April 30. Born in 1896 in present-day Bangladesh, Baba Sivanand led a life of austerity and discipline, following a strict regimen of yoga, boiled food, and sleeping on a mat. He received the Padma Shri in 2022 for his contributions to yoga and spirituality. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath paid tributes to Baba Sivanand, calling his life and teachings an inspiration.
