Varanasi Leads in Youth Athletics, Chess, and Racing Wins

2025-03-02T19:44:00+00:00

Varanasi won the UP State Youth Athletics Championship 2025, securing three gold, four silver, and four bronze medals. Saharanpur and Sonbhadra shared the runner-up position. Ashita (Lucknow) won gold in girls' long jump, while Komal Yadav (Lucknow) won gold in girls' 400m. Rashi Singh (UPA) won two gold medals. In heptathlon, Varun Rajput (Etawah) topped the boys' category and Priya Mishra (Sitapur) won the girls' division. Medhansh Saxena won the 43rd Shivani Cup Special Open Chess Tournament with an unbeaten score of six points. Anay Agarwal (U-9), Jayesh Kishore Arya (U-13), and Ujjwal Raj Srivastava (U-16) were the age-category champions. The Inheritor, owned by JN Mathur, won the Army Cdr's Cup at the Lucknow Race Course. Lucknow, Mau, Gorakhpur, and Amethi Hostel qualified for the semifinals of the State-Level Open Senior Men's Handball Tournament.
