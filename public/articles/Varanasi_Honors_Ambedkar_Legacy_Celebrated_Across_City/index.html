Varanasi Honors Ambedkar: Legacy Celebrated Across City

2025-04-14T18:49:00+00:00

Various institutions in Varanasi commemorated Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's birth anniversary on April 15th. A program at the Collectorate, chaired by district magistrate S Rajalingam, highlighted Ambedkar's role in drafting the Indian Constitution. Minister Ravindra Jaiswal paid tribute at Ambedkar Park, emphasizing the importance of following Ambedkar's ideals. The Varanasi Development Authority also celebrated the day with tributes and officials and employees participated in a two-day national seminar and competitive programs at BHU, concluding with a blood donation camp and a seminar on Ambedkar's contributions to justice, brotherhood, equality, economics, and women's empowerment.
