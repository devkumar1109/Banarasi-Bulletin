Sambhal Prepares for Peaceful Holi-Ramzan Harmony

2025-03-07T12:37:00+00:00

In Sambhal, Uttar Pradesh, authorities are implementing security measures to ensure peaceful Holi and Ramzan celebrations. Seven companies of the Provincial Armed Constabulary (PAC) have been deployed, and peace committee meetings are being held at the neighborhood level. The district is divided into six zones and 29 sectors for enhanced security monitoring. Authorities are urging residents to report any rumors promptly to prevent unrest and maintain harmony during the festivals.
