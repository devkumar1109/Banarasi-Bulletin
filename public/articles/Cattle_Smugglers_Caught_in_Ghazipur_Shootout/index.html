Cattle Smugglers Caught in Ghazipur Shootout

2025-05-07T19:07:00+00:00

Two cattle smugglers, Sonu Yadav and Sabhajeet Yadav alias Shalu, were arrested following an encounter with police in Ghazipur district, Uttar Pradesh. The encounter occurred early Wednesday morning when police attempted to stop a pickup van carrying the smugglers and 11 cattle. The smugglers opened fire, injuring two police officers. Police retaliated, injuring both smugglers in the legs. Two country-made pistols, live cartridges, and the pickup van were recovered from the scene. Both arrested smugglers have previous criminal records.
