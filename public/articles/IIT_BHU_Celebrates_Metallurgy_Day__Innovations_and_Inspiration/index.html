IIT BHU Celebrates Metallurgy Day: Innovations and Inspiration

2025-04-18T07:38:00+00:00

IIT (BHU)'s Metallurgical Engineering Department celebrated Metallurgy Day 2025 with a memorial lecture and awards ceremony. T.K. Nandi, Director of DRDO Industry Academia Centres of Excellence (DIA CoE), delivered the T.R. Anantharaman Memorial Lecture on "From the Puzzles to Solutions: Navigating the Complex Field of Materials Development." Nandi discussed the challenges and advancements in materials development, inspiring students. Prof. N.K. Mukhopadhyay, Dean of Faculty Affairs at IIT BHU, emphasized the importance of interdisciplinary education in his presidential speech. The event also recognized student achievements with awards presented by Prof. Srinivas.
