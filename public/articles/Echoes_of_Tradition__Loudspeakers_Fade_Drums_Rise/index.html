Echoes of Tradition: Loudspeakers Fade, Drums Rise

2025-03-18T11:55:00+00:00

In 2022, the Uttar Pradesh government banned loudspeakers at religious sites. This has led to a resurgence of traditional wake-up calls during Ramzan. Mohammed Suhail, dressed in traditional attire, drums through Sambhal's streets at 3 am to alert Muslims about the 'sehri' meal. muezzins now call out 'azaan' from rooftops without amplification, marking a cultural shift in Islamic practices.
