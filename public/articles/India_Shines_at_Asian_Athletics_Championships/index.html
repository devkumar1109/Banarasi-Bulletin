India Shines at Asian Athletics Championships

2025-05-28T12:28:00+00:00

India won gold in the 4x400m mixed relay at the Asian Athletics Championships with a time of 3:18.12sec. Tejaswin Shankar secured silver in the decathlon with a score of 7618, while Praveen Chithravel won silver in the men's triple jump with a best effort of 16.90m. Rupal Chaudhary and Pooja clocked silver in the women's 400m (52.68sec) and 1500m (4:10.83sec) respectively. Yoonus Shah earned a bronze in the men's 1500m. This brings India's total medal count to 8 at the Championships.
