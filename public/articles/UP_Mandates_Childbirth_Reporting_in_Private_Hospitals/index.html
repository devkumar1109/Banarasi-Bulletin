U.P. Mandates Childbirth Reporting in Private Hospitals

2025-06-13T05:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has made childbirth reporting mandatory for private hospitals. This new policy aims to improve data collection and monitoring of maternal and child health outcomes in the state.
