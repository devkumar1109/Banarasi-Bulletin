UP Outsource Corp: Better Pay, Better Work for 11 Lakh

2025-04-25T21:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is establishing a corporation, the Uttar Pradesh Outsource Service Corporation (UPCOS), to improve the working conditions of over 11 lakh outsourced employees in state administration. The corporation aims to address issues like salary deductions, delayed payments, lack of EPF/ESI benefits, and unfair recruitment practices. Key provisions include direct bank transfers of full remuneration by the 5th of each month, mandatory EPF and ESI contributions, and strict action against violating agencies. UPCOS will also ensure reservation for SCs, STs, and OBCs in contract employment. The proposal will be presented to the Cabinet for approval.
