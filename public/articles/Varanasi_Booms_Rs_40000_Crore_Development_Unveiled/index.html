Varanasi Booms: Rs 40,000 Crore Development Unveiled

2025-03-25T10:02:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya highlighted Rs 40,000 crore worth of development projects completed in Varanasi, PM Narendra Modi's constituency, over the past eight years. Maurya emphasized infrastructure improvements, including road projects worth Rs 22,408 crore, new bridges, and the development of tourism sites like Namo Ghat and Shri Kashi Vishwanath Corridor. He also cited the establishment of new educational institutions and the significant rise in Varanasi's GDP from Rs 22,586.91 crore in 2017 to Rs 43,899.75 crore. Maurya attributed this progress to PM Modi and CM Yogi Adityanath's leadership.
