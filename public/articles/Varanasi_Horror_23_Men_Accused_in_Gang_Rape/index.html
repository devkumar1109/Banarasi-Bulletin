Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Accused in Gang Rape

2025-04-12T00:48:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over six days in Varanasi between March 29 and April 4. The men drugged her and moved her between hotels and a hookah bar. The victim's mother wants to meet Prime Minister Modi to demand the harshest punishment for the accused. Police arrested 12 people in connection with the case. The survivor is reportedly not keeping well due to the trauma. Prime Minister Modi, during a visit to Varanasi, ordered the strictest action against those involved.
