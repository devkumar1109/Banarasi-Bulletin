Meth Lab Bust: Thane Police Seize Rs 2.30 Crore Drugs

2025-04-30T18:20:00+00:00

The Thane Crime Branch dismantled a methamphetamine manufacturing operation in Uttar Pradesh. They seized 1.14 kg of the drug, valued at over Rs 2.30 crore, and arrested three individuals: a cloth merchant, a chemistry graduate, and the operator of the manufacturing operation. The operation was uncovered following a tip-off that led to the initial seizure of 336 grams of MD. Investigations are ongoing to identify further connections within the drug trade network.
