Yogi's Journey: From Monk to Power

2025-06-05T12:25:00+00:00

Yogi Adityanath's biopic, "Ajey: The Untold Story of a Yogi," will release in theaters on August 1, 2025. The film's announcement poster features Anant Joshi as Yogi Adityanath and features the voice of Paresh Rawal. The movie is based on Shantanu Gupta's book "The Monk Who Became Chief Minister" and chronicles Yogi Adityanath's journey from a monk to a powerful political leader. Directed by Ravindra Gautam and produced by Ritu Mengi, the film stars Dinesh Lal Yadav, Ajay Mengi, Pavan Malhotra, Rajesh Khattar, Garima Vikrant Singh and Sarwar Ahuja. It will be released in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam with music composed by Meet Bros.
