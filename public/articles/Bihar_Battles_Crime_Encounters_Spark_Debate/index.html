Bihar Battles Crime: Encounters Spark Debate

2025-06-14T15:10:00+00:00

Bihar Police conducted five encounters across Patna, Muzaffarpur, and Vaishali between June 12 and June 14, resulting in several arrests and injuries to alleged criminals. The encounters followed a similar pattern, with accused attempting to escape, opening fire on police, and being shot in the leg. While police deny mimicking Uttar Pradesh's "Operation Langda," the encounters have raised concerns. Patna saw three encounters in three days: Ishu Kumar was shot during an arrest attempt, Angesh Kumar was injured while fleeing a weapon recovery operation, and Vivek Kumar was shot while resisting arrest. In Vaishali, Rajiv Mali was injured in an exchange of fire with STF and local police.
