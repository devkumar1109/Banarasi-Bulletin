Swaraj: Fearless India, One Nation, One Election

2025-04-24T18:04:00+00:00

BJP MP Bansuri Swaraj condemned the Pahalgam terror attack, stating that "Modi's new India is fearless" and will not be defeated by terrorism. Swaraj, speaking at a women's conference in Varanasi on "One Nation, One Election," highlighted the benefits of simultaneous Lok Sabha and Assembly elections, including reduced expenditure, decreased burden on administration and security forces, and a focus on national interests. She emphasized that "One Nation, One Election" would contribute to a more effective and efficient democracy.
