North India: 10 Summer Escape Destinations

2025-02-25T08:55:00+00:00

News18 suggests ten must-visit destinations in North India for summer vacation 2025. Manali and Shimla in Himachal Pradesh are ideal for mountain views and adventure sports. Rishikesh in Uttarakhand offers adventure and spirituality, while Nainital provides natural beauty and a peaceful environment. Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh is perfect for a spiritual journey, with iconic sites like Ganga Aarti and Kashi Vishwanath Temple. Leh-Ladakh in Jammu and Kashmir is a destination for adventurous travelers. Other suggestions include Mussoorie, Amritsar, Jaipur, and Dharamshala-McLeodganj.
