Varanasi Man Kills Third Wife After May Marriage

2025-05-17T10:04:00+00:00

Raju Pal from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested for murdering his third wife, Aarti Pal (26). Their marriage took place on May 9th, and shortly after, clashes began. Aarti, originally from Jaunpur district, was the third woman Raju had married. He had two previous marriages that ended in separation. Police are investigating the motive behind the killing.
