JioHotstar's Crime Thriller Lineup Thrills.

2025-04-26T05:00:00+00:00

JioHotstar offers several crime thriller web shows. "The Night Manager," a Hindi adaptation of the British series, stars Aditya Roy Kapur and Anil Kapoor in an espionage tale. "Rudra: The Edge of Darkness," featuring Ajay Devgn, is a psychological thriller based on "Luther" and follows a detective's descent into the city's darkness. "Asur" blends forensic science with Indian mythology, with Barun Sobti and Arshad Warsi investigating a serial killer linked to the asura Kali. "Saas, Bahu Aur Flamingo" reimagines the "saas-bahu" trope with a drug cartel, starring Dimple Kapadia, Radhika Madan, and others. Lastly, "Kaala" features Avinash Tiwary as Ritwik Mukherjee, a dedicated police officer tackling a powerful criminal organization.
