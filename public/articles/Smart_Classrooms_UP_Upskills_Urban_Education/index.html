Smart Classrooms: UP Upskills Urban Education

2025-06-13T14:52:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Urban Development Department is implementing a project to establish 2,700 smart classrooms in urban government schools at a cost of Rs 324.56 crore. 1,780 classrooms are already operational. This initiative aims to provide technology-enabled learning opportunities for students from economically weaker sections, leading to increased enrollment rates. The project is part of larger efforts like the Smart City Mission and Kayakalp to improve the quality of education in government schools.
