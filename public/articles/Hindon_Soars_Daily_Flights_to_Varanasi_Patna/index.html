Hindon Soars: Daily Flights to Varanasi, Patna

2025-04-15T04:37:00+00:00

Air India Express will launch daily flights from Hindon civil terminal to Varanasi and Patna starting May 1st. An Airbus A320 aircraft will operate these routes, accommodating 180 passengers. Bookings are open, with fares ranging from Rs 3,384 to Rs 5,573 for the first week of May. Hindon terminal has become increasingly popular as a second airport option for Delhi-NCR, offering convenience and cost-savings for passengers from Ghaziabad, Noida, and surrounding areas. The terminal is well-connected via the Hindon Elevated Road and Delhi Metro. With the addition of these routes, Hindon now offers flights to all major metro cities, Goa, Jammu, and Bhubaneswar. Other airlines operating from Hindon include Star Air and FlyBig under the regional connectivity scheme.
