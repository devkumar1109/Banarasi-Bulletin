Adityanath Preps Varanasi for Modi's Grand Arrival

2025-04-03T19:10:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Varanasi to oversee preparations for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's upcoming visit. Adityanath emphasized the importance of completing all projects scheduled for inauguration and foundation-laying during PM Modi's visit. The meeting took place at the Circuit House auditorium.
