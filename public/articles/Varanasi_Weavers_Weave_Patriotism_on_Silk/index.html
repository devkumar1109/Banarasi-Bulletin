Varanasi Weavers Weave Patriotism on Silk

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers created special sarees inspired by the Indian Air Force (IAF) to commemorate 'Operation Sindoor'. The sarees feature designs of weapons like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale, honoring the Indian Army. This initiative showcases the weavers' artistic talent and their patriotism.
