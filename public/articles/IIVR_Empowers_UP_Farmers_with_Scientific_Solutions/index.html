IIVR Empowers UP Farmers with Scientific Solutions

2025-05-30T21:36:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR) in Varanasi conducted a farmer awareness program reaching 2,063 farmers across six districts of Uttar Pradesh. Over 50 scientists engaged in direct discussions with farmers, addressing challenges such as Nilgai menace, irregular rainfall, and soil health issues. Farmers received information on natural farming, integrated pest management, and government schemes. IIVR director Rajesh Kumar emphasized the institute's commitment to delivering scientific solutions to farmers. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath lauded the 'Viksit Krishi Sankalp Abhiyan,' calling it a foundation for 'Viksit Bharat Abhiyan' and highlighting the importance of scientists working directly with farmers.
