Groom Arrives in JCB, Bulldozers for Send-Off

2025-02-24T10:21:00+00:00

A groom in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, opted for a unique send-off involving nearly a dozen bulldozers instead of the traditional luxurious cars. The procession, featuring the groom arriving on a JCB, surprised onlookers on Thursday, February 23, 2025, as the bride, Karishma, prepared to leave for her new home. This unconventional choice stands out from the usual extravagant baraat setups, showcasing a departure from tradition in a memorable way.
