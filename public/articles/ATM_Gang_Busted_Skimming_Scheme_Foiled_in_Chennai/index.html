ATM Gang Busted: Skimming Scheme Foiled in Chennai

2025-05-26T09:00:00+00:00

Chennai police apprehended a gang from Uttar Pradesh involved in ATM tampering and cash theft. The gang specialized in using skimming devices to steal card data and PINs, enabling them to withdraw funds fraudulently. This bust signifies a major victory for law enforcement in combating ATM-related crimes in the city.
