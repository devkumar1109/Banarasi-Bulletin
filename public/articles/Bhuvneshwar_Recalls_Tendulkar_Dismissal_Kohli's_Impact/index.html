Bhuvneshwar Recalls Tendulkar Dismissal, Kohli's Impact

2025-05-07T15:37:00+00:00

Bhuvneshwar Kumar reminisced about dismissing Sachin Tendulkar in the 2008-09 Ranji Trophy final. Kumar, playing in his second season, got Tendulkar out on a 15-ball duck in the first innings. Despite Kumar's six wickets and 121 runs, Uttar Pradesh lost the match by 243 runs. Kumar attributed his success in Test cricket to Virat Kohli's leadership, highlighting Kohli's emphasis on aggression in the format.
