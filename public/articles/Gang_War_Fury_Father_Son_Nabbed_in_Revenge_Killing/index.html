Gang War Fury: Father, Son Nabbed in Revenge Killing

2025-05-06T19:40:00+00:00

Dheeraj Hiranwar and his son Ritik have been arrested for the murder of Avinash Bhusari, part of the ongoing Hiranwar-Sheikhu gang war. Dheeraj allegedly funded the murder as revenge for his son Pawan, who was killed by the Sheikhu gang in January. The arrest follows the flight of Dheeraj and his son to Mumbai after the murder. Simran Lokhande's brother, Rishabh Wankhede, who helped them escape, was also arrested. Fifteen others have been arrested in connection with the case, but Shaheed Shaikh, a key Hiranwar aide, remains at large. Police have recovered three firearms and cartridges.
