Varanasi's Pink Hues: Gulabi Meenakari Blossoms

2025-05-02T14:43:00+00:00

Gulabi Meenakari, Varanasi's pink enameling art, is experiencing a revival thanks to GI tag recognition in 2015 and ODOP status in 2018. The annual business of this traditional craft is now estimated at Rs 12-15 crore, employing around 250-300 people, 30% of whom are women. Government initiatives, including training, loans, grants, and marketing efforts, have played a crucial role in reviving the craft, attracting young artisans back to the art form. Orders are now coming in from both domestic and international markets, particularly for women's jewelry and corporate gifting. Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath have been instrumental in promoting Gulabi Meenakari on the global stage.
