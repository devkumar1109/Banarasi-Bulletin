"Masaan" Mural Blooms in Varanasi

2025-04-05T11:02:00+00:00

A massive mural depicting actors Richa Chadha and Vicky Kaushal from the 2015 film "Masaan" was unveiled in Varanasi's Sigra neighborhood. The multi-storey mural, created by Curves and Colors with support from the Varanasi Development Authority, has been praised by Chadha who expressed her gratitude to the artist and her belief in cinema's enduring power. "Masaan," directed by Neeraj Ghaywan, explores themes of life, death, and longing set against the backdrop of Varanasi. The film received critical acclaim for its storytelling and performances, earning international recognition.
