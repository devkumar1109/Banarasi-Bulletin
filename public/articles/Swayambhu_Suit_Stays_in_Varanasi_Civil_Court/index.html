Swayambhu Suit Stays in Varanasi Civil Court

2025-04-12T18:49:00+00:00

A Varanasi court dismissed a plea seeking the transfer of the Lord Swayambhu suit from the civil judge senior division court to the district court. Anushka Tiwari and Indu Tiwari, representing Jyotirlinga Adi Vishweshwar Virajman, filed the plea in September 2024. They argued that the suit and seven similar cases concerning the ancient idol of Swaymbhu Lord Vishweshwar vs Anjuman Intazamia Masajid should be consolidated in the district court. The court dismissed the application, stating that only parties to the suit can request its transfer.
