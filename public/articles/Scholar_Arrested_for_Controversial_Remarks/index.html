Scholar Arrested for Controversial Remarks

2025-05-18T11:56:00+00:00

Ali Khan Mahmudabad, an Ashoka University academic, was arrested by Haryana police for remarks deemed to disparage women officers in the Indian armed forces and promote communal disharmony. Mahmudabad, a political scientist specializing in Muslim political thought in late colonial India, was also a national spokesperson for the Samajwadi Party from 2019 to 2022. He was considered close to SP president Akhilesh Yadav during his time in the party. Mahmudabad is from a politically connected family, his father, Raja Sahib Mahmudabad, was a former Congress MLA.
