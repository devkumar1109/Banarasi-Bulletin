IIT Bhilai Gets Rs 1,400 Crore Expansion Boost

2025-05-16T09:20:00+00:00

The Central Government approved a Rs 1,400 crore expansion plan for IIT Bhilai, increasing student seats to 2,750 from the current 1,250. This Phase 1, Stage B development will include two new engineering departments, one science department, three hostels, residential quarters, a sports complex, and a research park, the first in Chhattisgarh. The expansion aims to boost technical education, regional growth, innovation, and support local startups. IIT Bhilai's long-term plan envisions accommodating 12,000 students, 1,200 faculty, and 1,320 staff upon completion.
