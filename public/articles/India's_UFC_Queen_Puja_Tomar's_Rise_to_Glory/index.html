India's UFC Queen: Puja Tomar's Rise to Glory

2025-03-07T13:45:00+00:00

Puja Tomar, India's first woman to win a UFC bout, boasts a 9-4-0 MMA record. Tomar, a former 5-time national Wushu champion, trains at Soma Fight Club in Bali and is currently sponsored by Fittr. Her net worth is estimated at $250,000 to $500,000. Tomar is on a 5-match winning streak, having debuted in the UFC with a victory against Rayanne dos Santos. Her next UFC fight is scheduled against Shauna Banon on March 22, 2025. Tomar began training in martial arts at age 12 after her father's death, relying on farming for income before her combat sports success.
