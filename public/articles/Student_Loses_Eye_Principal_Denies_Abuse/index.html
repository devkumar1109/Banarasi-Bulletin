Student Loses Eye, Principal Denies Abuse

2025-02-27T11:04:00+00:00

A Class 3 student in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, allegedly lost vision in one eye after an incident at Bhogpur Mithoni Primary School. The student's mother, Jyoti Kashyap, accused the principal, Geeta Karal, of beating her daughter, leading to the injury. Kashyap filed a complaint with the District Magistrate. The principal denied the allegations, stating that the injury occurred when a classmate accidentally hit the student's face with her elbow. Karal claimed the student already had weak eyesight. The situation escalated when Kashyap requested a medical certificate from the principal, which was denied. Moradabad Basic Siksha Adhikari (BSA) Ajit Kumar has initiated an inquiry into the incident.
