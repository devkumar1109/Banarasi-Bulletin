"Stress Balls and Stick Handlers: Times of India's Cricket Tournament and More Sports Action"

2025-02-23T19:44:00+00:00

The HR department at Times of India organised 'Howzzat 2025', an inter-departmental stress-buster cricket tournament. Press Super Kings defeated Top Giants by 30 runs to lift the trophy, with Rajat Nishad as man of the match. Shivam Shukla was adjudged man of the tournament, while Aditya Murti and Anil Mishra received best batsman and best bowler trophies. In hockey, Lucknow SSB thrashed Sports College 4-1 in the 2nd Pt Ram Avtar Mishra Men's Hockey Tournament. The National Para Judo Championships began at KD Singh 'Babu' Stadium, with over 400 players and officials from 20 states participating, and 192 medals up for grabs, including international players Kapil Parmar and Kokila.
