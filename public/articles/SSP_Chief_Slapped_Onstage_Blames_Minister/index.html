SSP Chief Slapped Onstage, Blames Minister

2025-06-11T17:07:00+00:00

In Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, Mahendra Rajbhar, national president of the Suheldev Swabhiman Party (SSP), was slapped multiple times on stage by party worker Brijesh Rajbhar during an event commemorating Maharaja Suheldev's victory day. Mahendra Rajbhar filed a complaint against Brijesh, alleging political motivation behind the assault. He accused Uttar Pradesh Cabinet Minister and SBSP president Om Prakash Rajbhar of masterminding the attack, claiming Brijesh had met with him prior. The incident occurred during a 'bhoomi pujan' ceremony for a statue of Maharaja Suheldev in the Rajbhar-dominated Zafarabad Assembly constituency.
