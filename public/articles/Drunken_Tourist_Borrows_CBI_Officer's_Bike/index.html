Drunken Tourist Borrows CBI Officer's Bike

2025-03-06T02:29:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a tipsy man mistakenly rode home on a CBI officer's motorbike after mistaking it for his own. CCTV footage showed the man, who had been drinking, taking the bike. The CBI officer, in town investigating a railway exam paper leak, realized his bike was missing and contacted the police. The man returned the bike to the police the next day with an apology. No charges were pressed as the CBI team was focused on celebrating a successful raid that resulted in 26 arrests and the seizure of Rs 1.2 crore.
