Drunk Driver's Crash Kills Woman, Injures Four

2025-03-14T10:08:00+00:00

A drunk driver, Rakshit Chaurasia, a 20-year-old law student from Uttar Pradesh, killed a woman and injured four others in Vadodara, Gujarat. The accident occurred at 12:30 am on Thursday near Karelibag. Footage shows Chaurasia, heavily intoxicated, shouting "another round" after the crash while bystanders attempted to apprehend him. The injured victims were left scattered on the ground. Chaurasia was driving at 120 km/hour and crashed into two scooters, knocking down the riders and dragging them. Mit Chauhan, a student and the car's owner, was also arrested. Chaurasia is facing charges for the fatal accident.
