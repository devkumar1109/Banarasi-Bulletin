Homeguard Takes Life at Police Office, Family Cites Illness

2025-05-26T11:09:00+00:00

A 42-year-old homeguard, America Patel, committed suicide by hanging at the Police Commissioner's camp office in Varanasi on Sunday night. Patel had been suffering from health issues for an extended period and his family informed the police that he had been distressed due to his condition. Patel's colleagues found him hanging after a search when he was not seen for a prolonged time. The body has been sent for post-mortem and further investigation is underway.
