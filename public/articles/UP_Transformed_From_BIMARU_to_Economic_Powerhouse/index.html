UP Transformed: From BIMARU to Economic Powerhouse

2025-03-24T07:40:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state has transformed from a lagging region ("BIMARU" state) to India's economic powerhouse in the last 8 years. He attributes this success to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's policies and the "double-engine government." Adityanath highlights significant agricultural growth, with the growth rate rising from 5% in 2016-17 to over 13.5% currently. He cites the government's decision to waive farmers' loans worth Rs 36,000 crore as a key factor, leading to a 20% increase in food grain production since 2016-17.
