Streaming Heat: June's Must-Watch True Stories and Thrillers

2025-05-30T10:28:00+00:00

Prime Video will release "Ground Zero" on June 27, a true story about BSF Lt Commandant Narendra Nath Dhar Dubey. "Stolen" (June 4) follows two brothers witnessing a child kidnapping, while "Bhool Chuk Maaf" (June 6), set in Varanasi, explores Ranjan's life before marriage. Netflix will premiere "Jaat" (June 5) about a man confronting a crime boss, "Titan" (June 11) investigating the Titanic submersible tragedy, and "Straw" (June 6) about a single mother in a Tyler Perry film. "Grenfell Uncovered" (June 20) examines a deadly London fire, and "Our Times" (June 11) presents a couple's time-traveling invention. JioHotstar releases "Kesari Chapter 2" (June 13), revealing the truth about the Jallianwala Bagh Massacre. Netflix also adds the classic "Kindergarten Cop" (June 10).
