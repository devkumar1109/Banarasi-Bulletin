Dubai Return Ends in Gruesome Suitcase Murder

2025-04-21T15:27:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Patkhauli village, a farmer discovered a suitcase containing the severed upper and lower body parts of a man in a field. Police are investigating the murder, which is believed to be the work of the victim's wife and her lover. The victim was a man who recently returned from Dubai. The couple allegedly killed him and mutilated his body before stuffing it into a suitcase.
