India's Creative Boom: Boosting Global GDP

2025-03-16T11:02:00+00:00

India's participation in the global creative economy is growing, significantly contributing to world GDP. UNESCO's Creative Cities Network (UCCN) emphasizes the role of creativity in urban development. Creative cities integrate cultural industries like music, art, and design into their economic and social frameworks. Creative industries, generating over $2.25 trillion globally, encompass four categories: Heritage, Arts, Media, and Functional Creations. India's creative economy, valued at over $200 billion, employs millions and boasts a global influence through sectors like Bollywood, music, handicrafts, and IT.
