Macron's Southeast Asia Power Play

2025-05-25T20:00:00+00:00

French President Emmanuel Macron has embarked on a six-day Southeast Asia tour, beginning in Vietnam. The tour aims to position France as a third power in the region, balancing the influence of China and the US. Macron plans to visit Vietnam, Indonesia, and Singapore, seeking energy deals, nuclear partnerships, and influence in Indo-Pacific politics. He will promote fair trade, sovereignty, and regional peace, addressing the Shangri-La Dialogue in Singapore and meeting key ASEAN leaders. Macron's focus on energy, nuclear partnerships, and fair trade highlights France's commitment to sustainable development and equitable economic practices. His emphasis on regional peace aims to contribute to stability in Southeast Asia.
