Bounty Hunter Nabbed After Sonbhadra Shootout

2025-02-25T18:11:00+00:00

A criminal named Vishal alias Algu Yadav, carrying a Rs 25,000 bounty, was arrested in Sonbhadra district, Uttar Pradesh following an encounter with police on Monday night. Yadav, involved in multiple loot cases, sustained a bullet injury in his leg during the crossfire. Police recovered a country-made revolver and a motorcycle from him. He was admitted to the district hospital for treatment.
