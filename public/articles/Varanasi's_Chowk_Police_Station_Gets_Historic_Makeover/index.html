Varanasi's Chowk Police Station Gets Historic Makeover

2025-05-02T14:40:00+00:00

The Commissioner of Police, Mohit Agarwal, inaugurated the renovated Chowk Police Station in Varanasi. The 125-year-old building was preserved and modernized, featuring an attractive entrance, visitor room, and park for tourists. The renovation aims to provide security, ease access, and smooth traffic flow for the lakhs of devotees visiting Kashi Vishwanath Dham daily. The CP highlighted the importance of police cooperation and service. Future renovations of other police stations will follow this model. Architect Priya Singh and others involved in the project received certificates of appreciation.
