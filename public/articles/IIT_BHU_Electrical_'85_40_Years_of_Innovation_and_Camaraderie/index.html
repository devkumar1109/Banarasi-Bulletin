IIT BHU Electrical '85: 40 Years of Innovation and Camaraderie

2025-03-04T10:01:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi, celebrated the 40th reunion of its Electrical Engineering batch of 1985. The event, organized by the Student Alumni Interaction Cell (SAIC), brought together alumni, faculty, and students. Highlights included a meeting with the Director and Dean, a session with faculty reflecting on department advancements, classroom and laboratory visits, and a student-alumni interaction. Alumni reminisced with a cycle ride to Visvesvaraya Hostel and enjoyed a traditional lunch at Rajputana Hostel. A friendly cricket match added to the camaraderie. Director Prof. Amit Patra emphasized the alumni's success as a testament to IIT (BHU)'s strong foundation and their contributions to various fields. The reunion fostered nostalgia, strengthened bonds, and inspired current students.
