Honeymoon Horror: Wife, Boyfriend, Killers Arrested

2025-06-13T08:12:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi, a wife on honeymoon in Meghalaya, is the prime suspect in the murder of her husband, Raja Raghuvanshi. Police found a mangalsutra and a ring left behind by Sonam at a homestay, leading them to suspect her involvement. Raja’s body was found in a gorge near Weisawdong Falls on June 2. Sonam surrendered in Uttar Pradesh on June 9, and her boyfriend, Raj Kushwaha, and three alleged contract killers were also arrested. The couple vanished on May 23 after checking out of a homestay. A tour guide reported seeing the couple with three Hindi-speaking men while trekking back from Nongriat village, a detail that aided the investigation. All five accused have confessed to their roles in the murder.
