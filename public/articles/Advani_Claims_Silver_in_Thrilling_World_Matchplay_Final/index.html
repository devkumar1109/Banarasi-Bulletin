Advani Claims Silver in Thrilling World Matchplay Final

2025-04-12T14:12:00+00:00

Indian cueist Pankaj Advani won a silver medal at the WBL World Matchplay Billiards Championship in New Delhi. He lost to David Causier of Britain in a tight final, 8-7. Advani started strong with a 2-0 lead but Causier fought back to win the deciding frame.
