Speeding Car Crash Claims Three Lives on Highway

2025-06-14T15:57:00+00:00

Three men died and one was critically injured in a car accident on the Varanasi-Prayagraj highway near Thata village. The speeding car, traveling from Prayagraj, collided with a truck while attempting to overtake. The impact caused the car to overturn multiple times before coming to a halt. The truck driver fled the scene. The deceased were identified as Aman Yadav, Afzal, and Arbaaz, all residents of Prayagraj. Police are reviewing CCTV footage to identify the truck driver. The accident disrupted traffic on the service road for a period.
