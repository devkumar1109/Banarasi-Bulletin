Uncle, Aunt Arrested for Nephew's "Dishonor" Killing

2025-04-11T04:37:00+00:00

A couple in Bahraich, Uttar Pradesh, Chetram Gautam and Sundari Devi, has been arrested for the murder of their 22-year-old nephew, Bhanu Pratap. Bhanu's body was found in a sack near a school on March 29 with a rope around his neck. Police investigated through CCTV analysis, mobile phone records, and witness interviews, leading to the couple's arrest. They confessed to killing Bhanu after finding him in a compromising situation with a girl from their family. The couple strangled Bhanu, placed his body in a sack, and dumped it near a school. Bhanu's father had initially filed a complaint accusing Chetram and his family of involvement in the murder. The case has shocked the local community and highlights the dangers of extreme reactions to perceived dishonour.
