Kumbh: God, Virtue Found, Critics See Filth

2025-02-24T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized Opposition leaders for their criticism of the Maha Kumbh, stating that people received what they sought from the religious gathering. He claimed "vultures found corpses," "pigs found filth," while devotees found God, virtue, and clean arrangements. Adityanath addressed allegations of caste discrimination at the Kumbh, stating that no caste was prevented from visiting and anyone with ill intentions would face consequences. He contrasted his government's focus on faith with the previous administration's lack of involvement.
