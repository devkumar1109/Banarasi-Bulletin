UP's Healthcare: 10 Crore Covered, Infrastructure Boosted

2025-02-25T16:07:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that 10 crore people in the state benefit from free health insurance under the Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat and Mukhyamantri Jan Arogya Yojana, covering treatment costs up to ₹5 lakh. He highlighted the government's efforts to strengthen healthcare infrastructure, including recruiting skilled medical faculty and establishing super-specialty blocks in medical colleges. Previously, UP had only 17 government medical colleges, but now each district has at least one. The government has also set up blood banks in every district and is focusing on paramedical and nursing education. The SGPGI, with eight new departments, has expanded its bed capacity from 1,200 to accommodate increased patient needs.
