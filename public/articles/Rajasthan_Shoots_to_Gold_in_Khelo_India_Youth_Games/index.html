Rajasthan Shoots to Gold in Khelo India Youth Games

2025-05-05T11:37:00+00:00

Rajasthan's Prachi and Mayank Choudhary won the first gold medal in the Khelo India Youth Games, defeating Uttar Pradesh in the 10m air pistol mixed team final with a score of 15-15. Prachi's sharpshooting secured the victory for Rajasthan. Delhi won bronze, defeating Haryana in the 10m air pistol mixed team final. This is Prachi's redemption after being part of a silver-winning team previously.
