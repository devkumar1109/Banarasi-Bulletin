Uttar Pradesh Braces for Stormy Weekend

2025-04-17T07:25:00+00:00

The India Meteorological Department (IMD) has issued an alert for thunderstorms, rain, and strong winds (30-50 km/hour) in 36 districts of Uttar Pradesh from April 18 to 20. Eastern and central Uttar Pradesh are expected to be most affected. Districts including Banda, Chitrakoot, Prayagraj, Varanasi, Gorakhpur, Kanpur Nagar, Saharanpur, and Meerut are included in the alert. The Terai belt will see light showers on April 17, with heavier rain and thunderstorms predicted for eastern Uttar Pradesh from April 18. Night temperatures are expected to be unusually warm and humid in several districts, including Aligarh, Mathura, Hathras, Agra, Firozabad, and Etawah. Maximum temperatures are predicted to rise by 2-3 degrees Celsius over the next few days. Conditions are expected to stabilize by April 21.
