Varanasi Reels: Condemnation and Calls for Action

2025-04-23T17:40:00+00:00

Following the Pahalgam terror attack, protests and prayers were held across Varanasi. Kashi Vishwanath Dham hosted an Ati Rudra recital, while the BJP postponed its Waqf Amendment awareness drive. Muslim institutions like Anjuman Intejamia Masajid and the Muslim Mahila Foundation condemned the attack. A protest march organized by Vishal Bharat Sansthan and Muslim Women's Foundation culminated in a symbolic cremation of terrorists. Demands were made for action against Pakistan and for religious leaders to issue a Fatwa against terrorists. The BJP Kashi region president and spokesman announced the postponement of workshops due to the mourning for the victims.
