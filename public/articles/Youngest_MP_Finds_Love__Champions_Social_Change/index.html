Youngest MP Finds Love, Champions Social Change

2025-06-09T11:26:00+00:00

Priya Saroj, a 26-year-old Samajwadi Party MP representing Machhlishahr constituency in Uttar Pradesh, is one of India's youngest MPs and the youngest woman MP. Daughter of senior Samajwadi Party leader Tufani Saroj, she defeated BJP leader B.P. Saroj in the 2024 election. Saroj's campaign focused on unemployment, women's empowerment, farmer's rights, and marginalized community upliftment. She is a member of the Parliamentary Committee on Social Justice and Empowerment and served on the committee reviewing the Income Tax Bill, 2025. Before politics, Saroj was a lawyer practicing at the Supreme Court of India. On June 8th, she became engaged to Indian cricketer Rinku Singh, known for his performance in the IPL with the Kolkata Knight Riders.
