Adityanath Opens Atal School, Aims for Literacy Boost

2025-04-01T13:53:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the Atal Residential School in Bareilly's Nawabganj on Tuesday. The school, built at a cost of Rs 73.25 crore, will cater to students from class 1 to 12, including Bal Vatikas (kindergartens). Adityanath expressed confidence that the school will contribute to nation-building. Atal Residential Schools are designed to provide inclusive education and combat illiteracy, targeting underprivileged sections of society. The government plans to expand these schools to 57 districts in phases, eventually reaching 350 tehsils, 825 blocks, and Nyay Panchayats.
