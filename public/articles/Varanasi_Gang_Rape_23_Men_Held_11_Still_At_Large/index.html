Varanasi Gang Rape: 23 Men Held, 11 Still At Large

2025-04-09T04:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. The victim was found unconscious on April 4th and subsequently revealed the horrifying ordeal. Police arrested nine accused, who were sent to judicial custody. The victim’s father stated that she went missing on March 29th after visiting a ghat with a friend. She was lured and intoxicated before being subjected to the sexual assaults. The police are actively searching for the remaining accused, 11 of whom remain unidentified. The Uttar Pradesh Police are investigating the case under relevant sections.
