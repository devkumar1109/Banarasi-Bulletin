Varanasi Cop Impersonator Dupes Job Seekers

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

Munfed (26) from Mathura was arrested by Varanasi police for duping government job seekers of lakhs of rupees. He posed as Deepak Kumar Ranawat, public relations officer of the Varanasi police commissionerate. Munfed collected personal details of police officials from social media to create fake appointment letters. He confessed to conning at least seven women, including Anita Yadav of Mainpuri, who lost Rs 16 lakh. Police recovered fake appointment letters, jewelry, a police sub-inspector's uniform, and a nameplate from Munfed. Ranawat has been removed from his position and attached to police lines.
