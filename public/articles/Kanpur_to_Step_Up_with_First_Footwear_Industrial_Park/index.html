Kanpur to Step Up with First Footwear Industrial Park

2025-05-16T04:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will establish its first footwear industrial park in Kanpur's Ramaipur area. The park will span 83 acres and include 75 industrial plots and two warehouse plots. It will cater to various footwear manufacturing needs, including uniform shoes, sports shoes, and finished leather products. The Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority (UPSIDA) will implement a plug-and-play model, providing essential infrastructure and a modern common facility centre (CFC) with laboratories, design units, and training centres. Investors will benefit from a favorable payment structure and digital application process through the Nivesh Mitra portal. The park aims to boost local industries, create employment opportunities, and support Uttar Pradesh's economic growth.
