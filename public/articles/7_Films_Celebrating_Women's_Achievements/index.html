7 Films Celebrating Women's Achievements

2025-03-05T13:05:00+00:00

This article recommends seven biographical films to watch on Women's Day. "Hidden Figures" tells the story of three African American women who were instrumental in NASA's space program. "The Crown" explores the life of Queen Elizabeth II, covering themes of leadership, political challenges, and personal struggles. "Erin Brockovich" is based on the true story of a legal assistant who took on a corporation responsible for water contamination. "Frida" focuses on the life of Mexican painter Frida Kahlo, highlighting her physical pain, relationships, and political activism. "The Iron Lady" portrays Margaret Thatcher, the UK's first female Prime Minister, and explores themes of leadership, ambition, and navigating a male-dominated world. "Harriet" tells the story of Harriet Tubman, a former enslaved woman who became a leading abolitionist and conductor on the Underground Railroad. Finally, "Radioactive" chronicles the life of Marie Curie, a Nobel Prize-winning scientist who discovered radioactivity.
