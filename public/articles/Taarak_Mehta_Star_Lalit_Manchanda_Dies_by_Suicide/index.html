"Taarak Mehta" Star Lalit Manchanda Dies by Suicide

2025-04-24T14:59:00+00:00

Actor Lalit Manchanda, known for his roles in "Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah" and "Crime Patrol", died by suicide on April 21 at his residence in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. Police confirmed the death and stated it was a case of suicide based on the discovery of his body hanging. No suicide note was found, and initial investigations suggest no foul play. The news has shocked fans and the television industry.
