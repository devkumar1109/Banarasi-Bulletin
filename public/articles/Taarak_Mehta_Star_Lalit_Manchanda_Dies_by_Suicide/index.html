"Taarak Mehta" Star Lalit Manchanda Dies by Suicide

2025-04-24T14:59:00+00:00

Actor Lalit Manchanda, known for his roles in "Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah" and "Crime Patrol," died by suicide on April 21 at his residence in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. His body was found hanging, and the police have confirmed it as a case of suicide. No suicide note was recovered, and preliminary investigations indicate no foul play. The news has deeply saddened fans and the television industry.
