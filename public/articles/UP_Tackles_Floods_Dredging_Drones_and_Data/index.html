U.P. Tackles Floods: Dredging, Drones, and Data

2025-02-28T17:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has ordered a comprehensive review of river conditions to address the state's persistent flood issues. He prioritizes dredging silted river channels and, if not feasible, implementing embankments and anti-erosion measures. Drone surveys will be used for precise data collection. Adityanath highlighted the completion of 1,575 flood mitigation projects, safeguarding vast regions. He emphasized timely project execution and strict budget adherence to enhance public safety and minimize costs.
