India's Healthcare: A Decade of Transformation

2025-03-08T17:22:00+00:00

India has witnessed an unprecedented improvement in health facilities over the last decade, according to Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. He highlighted the expansion of AIIMS from six to 22 in the last 10 years and the establishment of 40 new medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh since 2017. Adityanath also mentioned the increase in free dialysis, CT scan, and MRI facilities across districts and the ongoing initiative "Mukhyamantri Arogya Mela" to inform people about health schemes. He stated that Uttar Pradesh has the highest number of beneficiaries under the Ayushman Bharat scheme (10 crore) and plans to extend health insurance to other essential service providers. Adityanath further emphasized the potential of India as a global leader in health tourism.
