Varanasi MMLP: Multi-Modal Logistics Hub Launches

2025-03-12T12:29:00+00:00

On March 11th, the National Highways Logistics Management Limited (NHLML) and the Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) signed a MoU to establish a state-of-the-art Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP) in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Spanning over 150 acres, the MMLP will be strategically positioned, connected to National Highway 7, the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor, and National Waterway-1. It is also located 30 km from Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport. The park is expected to boost India's logistics ecosystem, enhance connectivity, cargo movement, and economic activity in the region, creating investment and job opportunities. Union Ministers Nitin Gadkari and Sarbananda Sonowal witnessed the signing ceremony.
