Varanasi's First Urban Township Rises Near Cricket Stadium

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi will get its first urban township spread across 150 acres near the new international cricket stadium at Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) approved the project at its recent board meeting. The stadium, built at a cost of Rs 451 crore, is expected to be completed by the end of 2025 and host its first international match in 2026. The VDA also inaugurated a new record room with a capacity of 80,000 files and launched a citizen app for complaint registration. Approvals were granted for a main access road between Transport Nagar Scheme and NH-2. RITES Ltd. has been contracted for DPR, design, and construction supervision of VDA projects.
