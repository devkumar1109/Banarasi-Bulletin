Yogi Adityanath: Clean Exams, Transparency, and Sports Success

2025-06-13T00:46:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath accused previous governments of promoting cheating in school board exams. He claimed exam centers were used for wholesale cheating and transfer-posting became an industry. Adityanath stated his government has implemented a transparent system with CCTV surveillance, boundary walls, and adequate infrastructure at exam centers, reducing exam duration to 13-14 days. He also highlighted his government's transparent recruitment process, claiming over 800,000 youths have been given government jobs. He emphasized the increased recruitment of women in the police force, noting that over 12,000 women were recruited compared to only 10,000 from 1947 to 2017. Adityanath praised Uttar Pradesh's performance at the 68th National School Sports Competition, where 363 players won 179 medals. He urged students to protect national property and report any damage.
