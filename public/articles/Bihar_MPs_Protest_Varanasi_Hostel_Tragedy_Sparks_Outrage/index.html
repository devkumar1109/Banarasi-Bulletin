Bihar MPs Protest: Varanasi Hostel Tragedy Sparks Outrage

2025-03-10T08:07:00+00:00

Opposition MPs from Bihar protested outside Parliament demanding justice for a 17-year-old girl found dead in her hostel room in Varanasi. Allegations of rape and murder have been made, drawing comparisons to the 2020 Hathras case. The incident occurred in PM Modi's Lok Sabha constituency. MPs from the RJD, CPI(ML) Liberation, and Congress are demanding a thorough inquiry and expressing concern over the police handling of the postmortem and last rites.
