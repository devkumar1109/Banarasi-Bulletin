UP Upskills OBC Youth with AI, Drones, and Cybersecurity

2025-04-24T13:30:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is modernizing its computer training scheme to include AI, drone technology, and cybersecurity courses, aiming to improve job prospects for educated but unemployed OBC youth. The initiative, launching in 2025-26, will revamp the curriculum and train instructors to meet evolving tech demands. The Backward Classes Welfare Department is preparing a proposal and seeking budget approval. Minister Narendra Kashyap emphasized the government's commitment to inclusive development and aligning skills with industry needs. Physical verification of beneficiaries and a robust monitoring system will ensure transparency.
