BJP Leader's Son Scandal Sparks Outrage

2025-05-30T16:04:00+00:00

Over 130 obscene videos allegedly featuring the son of a BJP women's wing president in Uttar Pradesh's Mainpuri district have gone viral, sparking a political controversy. The BJP leader remains unreachable, while opposition parties are demanding action. The woman claims her husband has been involved with other women for four years and physically abused her. She alleges that he created videos with his girlfriends to psychologically torture her. The videos' leak remains unclear. The BJP has not commented, and police have not filed a case, leading to accusations of preferential treatment. Opposition parties, including the Samajwadi Party, Bahujan Samaj Party, and Congress, are criticizing the incident and the BJP's silence.
