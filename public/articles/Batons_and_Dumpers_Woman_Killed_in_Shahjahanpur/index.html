Batons and Dumpers: Woman Killed in Shahjahanpur

2025-04-22T04:29:00+00:00

A 34-year-old woman in Uttar Pradesh's Shahjahanpur died after being run over by a dumper truck. The incident occurred during a police check when a sub-inspector allegedly hit the woman's husband's motorcycle with his baton, causing her to lose balance and fall. Locals, including MLA Salona Kushwaha, protested the incident, demanding a probe and action against the policeman. Shahjahanpur SP Rajesh Dwivedi stated that a case was registered against the sub-inspector and the truck driver under culpable homicide charges. An FIR was also filed against protestors who blocked traffic.
