Duterte Arrested: ICC Holds Him for Drug War Crimes

2025-03-11T17:00:00+00:00

Former Philippine President Rodrigo Duterte was arrested in Manila on a warrant from the International Criminal Court (ICC) for crimes against humanity related to his anti-drug campaign. The campaign, which took place from 2016 to 2022, resulted in numerous extrajudicial killings. Human rights groups have long called for justice for the victims, while Duterte's supporters claim the arrest is politically motivated. The ICC's involvement highlights international concern over Duterte's actions during his presidency. Duterte's supporters argue his policies were necessary to combat drug-related crime, but human rights advocates insist on accountability for the alleged abuses.
