Meghalaya Honeymoon Murder: Crime Scene Reconstructed

2025-06-17T23:44:00+00:00

Meghalaya Police successfully reconstructed the crime scene of Raja Raghuvanshi's murder. The reconstruction involved the accused, Sonam Raghuvanshi and three others, and provided a clear picture of the events. The SIT revealed that Raja was hit three times with a machete - first by Vishal, then by Anand, and finally by Akash. A second machete used in the murder is still missing and is being sought by SDRF. Sonam confessed to the crime, admitting she destroyed Raja's phone after the murder. All five accused, including Sonam, Akash, Vishal, Raj, and Anand, were arrested. Raja and Sonam, a newlywed couple from Indore, had gone missing during their honeymoon in Meghalaya. Raja's body was found in a gorge near Sohra.
