AI's Growing Footprint in 2024 Indian Elections

2025-04-14T03:35:00+00:00

The 2024 Lok Sabha election campaign saw significant use of AI, according to the Global Elections & AI Tracker. AI was used to facilitate communication across India's diverse languages through tools like 'Bhashini', which PM Modi used during an event in Varanasi. The AI was also used to generate content, including deepfakes of political figures, spread misinformation, and make automated political calls. Over 50 million robocalls were made in the two months leading up to the election using AI technology.
