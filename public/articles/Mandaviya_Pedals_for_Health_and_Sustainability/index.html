Mandaviya Pedals for Health and Sustainability

2025-03-23T10:47:00+00:00

Union Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya led over 500 riders in Lucknow during the 'Fit India Sundays on Cycle' initiative. He emphasized the importance of cycling for health, character building, and combating obesity and air pollution. The event also featured Zumba performances by athletes from the local National Centre of Excellence. Mandaviya urged citizens to incorporate cycling into their daily routines for a healthier and more sustainable future. In Delhi, the initiative also took place, but details weren't provided.
