Uttar Pradesh: India's Sweet Dominion

2025-05-03T12:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is known as the "Sugar Bowl of India" due to its dominance in sugarcane cultivation and sugar production. The state contributes over 35% of India's total sugar output. Uttar Pradesh's fertile plains, abundant water supply, and favorable climate make it ideal for sugarcane farming. The sugarcane belt in western UP, with districts like Meerut, Muzaffarnagar, and Saharanpur, is densely populated with sugar mills. Sugarcane is crucial to Uttar Pradesh's economy, supporting over 4 million farming families and fueling over 120 sugar mills. Its byproducts, like molasses and ethanol, also contribute to energy and alcohol production.
