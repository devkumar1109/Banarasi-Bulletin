UP Blooms: Indo-Israel-Dutch Tech Transforms Agriculture

2025-06-04T22:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is strengthening its agricultural sector with new Centres of Excellence (CoEs) using Indo-Israel-Dutch technology. The state currently ranks second in India for such centers. New CoEs are being established in Mirzapur (dragon fruit), Bundelkhand (citrus fruits), and Barabanki (flower-vegetable cultivation). Israeli experts are guiding farmers in citrus cultivation in Barua Sagar, while Dutch experts are sharing advanced cultivation techniques in Sonikpur village. These CoEs offer training and demonstrations on modern agricultural practices, aiming to boost productivity, diversify crops, and replace outdated methods. Uttar Pradesh already has existing CoEs in Basti, Kannauj, Saharanpur, and Lucknow, specializing in fruits, vegetables, ornamental plants, and other areas.
