Varanasi Gang Rape: Nine Arrested, Sex Racket Suspected

2025-04-08T17:01:00+00:00

Nine men have been arrested in connection with the gang rape of a 19-year-old girl in Varanasi. The arrests follow a week-long investigation into the incident, where the victim was allegedly drugged and assaulted by 23 individuals. The suspects, aged between 19 and 28, include businessmen, shop and hotel staff, and students. One of the accused operates a hookah bar and is suspected to be the leader of a sex racket. Police are continuing to search for the remaining suspects, including three identified and 11 unidentified individuals. The arrested accused were produced before the court and sent to jail.
