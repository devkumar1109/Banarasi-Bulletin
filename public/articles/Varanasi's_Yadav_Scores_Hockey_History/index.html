Varanasi's Yadav Scores Hockey History

2025-04-23T05:37:00+00:00

Pooja Yadav, a 21-year-old from Varanasi, has become the first woman from the Purvanchal region to be selected for the Indian women's hockey team. Yadav earned her place in the 26-member squad touring Australia from April 26 to May 4 after impressing at a national camp in Bengaluru. Her father, Mahendra, a milkman, supported her hockey aspirations despite facing societal pressure. He purchased her first pair of shoes by promising to deliver milk until the cost was covered. Yadav also credits playing cricket with boys in her youth for building her confidence and determination.
