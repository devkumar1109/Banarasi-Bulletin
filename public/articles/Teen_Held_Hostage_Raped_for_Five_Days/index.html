Teen Held Hostage, Raped for Five Days

2025-03-03T15:43:00+00:00

A 17-year-old girl from Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, was allegedly held hostage and raped for five days by a man in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. The accused, whom the girl met two years ago at a wedding function, threatened to make a video of her viral if she didn't meet him in Jhansi. Fearing for her safety, the girl went to Jhansi where the accused held her captive and raped her. After returning to Gwalior, she confided in her parents, who filed a police complaint at the University police station on March 1. The Additional Superintendent of Police (ASP, Crime Branch) Krishna Lalchandani confirmed the incident and stated that an investigation is underway.
