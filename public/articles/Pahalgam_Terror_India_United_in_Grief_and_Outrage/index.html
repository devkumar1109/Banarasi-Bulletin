Pahalgam Terror: India United in Grief and Outrage

2025-04-25T18:02:00+00:00

The Pahalgam attack, which killed 26 people, sparked protests across Uttar Pradesh. Hindus, Muslims, traders, and political leaders united in condemnation. Rallies, candlelight marches, and signature campaigns were held, with effigies of Pakistan burned. Traders observed shutdowns in various cities like Kannauj and Ghaziabad. In Muzaffarnagar, Muslims wore black badges and held a signature campaign demanding action against the perpetrators. Islamic scholar Allama Tauqeer Raza Khan urged calm and unity, praising Kashmiri Muslims who helped save lives. BJP MP Atul Garg highlighted diplomatic measures taken by the Centre in response to the attack.
