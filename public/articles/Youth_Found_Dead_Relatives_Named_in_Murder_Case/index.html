Youth Found Dead, Relatives Named in Murder Case

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth from Barki village, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, alleged murder and filed a complaint against two relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar. Hemant was last seen with the accused on Friday night, allegedly after being called to a wedding ceremony. The police have lodged a named FIR against the accused, sent the body for postmortem, and are investigating the incident. Further action will be taken based on the autopsy report and investigation findings.
