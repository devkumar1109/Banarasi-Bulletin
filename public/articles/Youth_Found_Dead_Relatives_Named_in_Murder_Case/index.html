Youth Found Dead, Relatives Named in Murder Case

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth from Barki village, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, alleged murder and filed a police complaint naming two relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar, as accused. Dinanath stated that Hemant was invited by the accused to a wedding ceremony on Friday. Around 11 pm, they informed him that Hemant attacked them and fled. The police have lodged a named FIR against the accused, are questioning them, and have sent the body for postmortem. Further action will be taken based on the autopsy report and investigation findings.
