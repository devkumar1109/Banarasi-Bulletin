Hijab Ripped, Couple Attacked in Muzaffarnagar

2025-04-14T14:49:00+00:00

In Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, a 20-year-old Muslim woman and a Hindu man were assaulted on April 12. The woman was stripped of her hijab, and both were beaten by a group of men. The incident occurred while they were returning from collecting a loan EMI for Utkarsh Small Finance Bank. A video of the attack went viral, sparking widespread outrage. Police registered an FIR and arrested six individuals under IPC sections related to assault, rioting, and outraging modesty. Muzaffarnagar City DSP Raju Kumar stated that further arrests will be made based on identification from the video, and strict legal action will be taken.
