Maize MSP Boost: UP Farmers Reap Rewards

2025-06-08T11:01:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced that the state will soon open maize procurement centers and declare a Minimum Support Price (MSP) for the crop. He stated that maize has become a profitable third crop for farmers, cultivated on over 5 lakh hectares, earning them up to Rs 2.5 lakh per hectare. Adityanath credited PM Modi with prioritizing farmers' welfare, highlighting initiatives like the Soil Health Card scheme, PM's agricultural irrigation scheme, and crop insurance. He emphasized the PM-KISAN scheme, which has benefited over 12 crore farmers nationwide, with Rs 85,000 crore transferred to 2.86 crore farmers in UP. Adityanath also mentioned that his government has provided jobs to 8 lakh youths and will be offering appointment letters to 60,000 new police recruits on June 15.
