Varanasi Gang Rape: 14 Arrested, Hunt Continues

2025-04-16T09:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, the police arrested Raj Khan, bringing the total number of arrests in the gang rape case to 14. A 19-year-old girl was allegedly drugged and raped by 23 individuals over a week. Prime Minister Modi sought updates on the investigation during his visit to Varanasi. DCP Chandrakant Mina, who led the investigation, has been reassigned to the DGP office. 12 of the 23 identified accused have been arrested, with efforts continuing to apprehend the remaining individuals. The survivor was diagnosed with hepatitis B during a medical examination.
