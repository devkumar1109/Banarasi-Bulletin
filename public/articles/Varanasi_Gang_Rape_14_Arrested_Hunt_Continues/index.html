Varanasi Gang Rape: 14 Arrested, Hunt Continues

2025-04-16T09:30:00+00:00

Police in Varanasi have arrested Raj Khan, bringing the total number of arrests in the gang rape case to 14. The victim, a 19-year-old girl, was allegedly drugged and raped by 23 individuals over a week. Twelve of the 23 accused have been identified, with 11 already arrested. The case came to light on April 5 when the victim's parents filed a complaint. Prime Minister Modi, during a visit to Varanasi, sought updates on the investigation from police officials. DCP Chandrakant Mina, who was leading the investigation, has been reassigned.
