Indian Boxers Punch Their Way to Semifinals

2025-03-26T07:26:00+00:00

World Championship bronze medallist Simranjit Kaur and Commonwealth Games bronze medallist Jaismine Lamboria advanced to the semifinals of the 8th Elite Women’s National Boxing Championship. Simranjit won via unanimous decision against Prachi, while Jaismine secured an RSC win against Hannah Joy. Kalaivani S upset 2022 Youth World Champion Devika Ghorpade in the Lightflyweight category. Reigning champion Anamika Hooda also moved to the semifinals with a win over Mehak Dharra. In the heavyweight division, two-time defending champion Nupur advanced to the semifinals against Ipsita Vikram. Sonu, silver medallist from the previous edition, will face Simranjit Kaur in another highly anticipated match.
