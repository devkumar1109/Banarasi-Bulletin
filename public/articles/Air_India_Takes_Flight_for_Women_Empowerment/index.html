Air India Takes Flight for Women Empowerment

2025-03-08T06:54:00+00:00

To celebrate International Women's Day, Air India operated 18 international and domestic flights managed entirely by women crews, both in the air and on the ground. The airline also launched the #HerMatters initiative to support women employees by assisting them with critical life events, promoting better work-life balance. Additionally, Air India will conduct career sessions in Delhi-NCR girls' schools to encourage young women to pursue careers in aviation.
