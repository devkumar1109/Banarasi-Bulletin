Varanasi's Tourism Transformation: 2-Month Makeover Plan

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

India's tourism secretary, V Vidyavathi, has given Varanasi officials two months to implement visible changes to boost tourism. The focus is on 16-20 key locations including temples, ghats, and Sarnath. Improvements include ghat beautification, better roads, electricity, and water supply, along with transportation upgrades. Promoting spiritual tourism, local crafts like Banarasi sarees, and cultural events are also key. Training and improved facilities for local artists and guides are emphasized. Digital platforms and campaigns to remove visual clutter will be used to attract tourists. The government will financially assist the promotion of yoga, wellness, and Ayurveda centers in Varanasi. Connecting tourists to nearby eco-tourism sites and historical landmarks is also being considered to extend their stays.
