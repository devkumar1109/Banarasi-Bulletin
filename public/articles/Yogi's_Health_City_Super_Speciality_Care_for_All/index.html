Yogi's Health City: Super Speciality Care for All.

2025-03-10T07:20:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the Sharda Care Health City Super Speciality Hospital. The hospital caters to various medical specialties, including Anesthesiology, Cardiology, Critical Care, Dermatology, Diabetes and Endocrinology, ENT, Gastroenterology, Medicine, Nephrology, Neurology, Obstetrics-Gynaecology, Oncology, Ophthalmology, Orthopaedics, Pediatrics-Neonatology, Psychiatry, Pulmonology, Radiology, Surgery, Urology, Laboratory Medicine, Diet, Nursing, Paramedical, and Physiotherapy.
