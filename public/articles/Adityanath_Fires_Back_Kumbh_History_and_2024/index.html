Adityanath Fires Back: Kumbh, History, and 2024

2025-03-26T06:20:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, in an interview with ANI, addressed various issues. Regarding the Mahakumbh 2025, he refuted opposition criticism, noting that many opposition leaders had also visited the event. He stated that a judicial commission is investigating the stampede deaths during the previous Mahakumbh and will take action based on its findings. Adityanath criticized Samajwadi Party leaders for their remarks on Rana Sanga, accusing them of glorifying historical figures like Aurangzeb and Babur while neglecting Indian heroes like Maharana Pratap and Shivaji Maharaj. He also accused the Congress and its allies of attempting to influence the 2024 Lok Sabha elections through money and propaganda, with the help of foreign powers.
