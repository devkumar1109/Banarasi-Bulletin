Rikhiram Sharma's Sitar Symphony for Mental Wellness

2025-05-05T14:59:00+00:00

Renowned sitarist Rishab Rikhiram Sharma held a sold-out "Sitar for Mental Health" concert in Hyderabad on May 4th. The concert blended Indian classical ragas, global melodies, and mental health awareness. Despite logistical challenges, Sharma delivered a powerful performance, captivating the audience with traditional ragas, devotional pieces, and modern adaptations of popular songs like Harry Potter and Game of Thrones themes. A highlight was a jugalbandi with supporting musicians from across India, creating a rich and layered sonic experience. Sharma also teased an unreleased composition inspired by Manikarnika Ghat in Varanasi, which was met with enthusiastic applause.
