BHU VC Search: Deadline Looms, High Court Sets Six-Week Ultimatum

2025-03-14T00:02:00+00:00

The Allahabad High Court has set a final deadline for the appointment of a Vice-Chancellor for Banaras Hindu University (BHU). The court has ordered the university to complete the process within six weeks. This comes after the court previously extended the deadline on multiple occasions. The BHU has been facing criticism for the delay in appointing a VC, leading to uncertainty and administrative challenges. The high court's final deadline aims to bring an end to the prolonged process and ensure smooth functioning of the university.
