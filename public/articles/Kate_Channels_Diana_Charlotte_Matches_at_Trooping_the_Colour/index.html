Kate Channels Diana, Charlotte Matches at Trooping the Colour

2025-06-15T01:10:00+00:00

Princess Kate made her 13th public appearance since joining the royal family in 2011 at Trooping the Colour. She wore a turquoise and white Catherine Walker coat dress with a matching Juliette Botterilll Milliner hat, echoing a similar outfit worn by Princess Diana in 1992. Princess Charlotte also wore a turquoise dress, matching her mother. The pair waved from a carriage alongside Prince George and Prince Louis. Prince William participated in the parade on horseback.
