MatCare Brings Advanced Care to Varanasi Mothers and Children

2025-03-10T03:14:00+00:00

MatCare has inaugurated its first maternity and child healthcare hospital in Mahmoorganj, Varanasi. The hospital, inaugurated by Varanasi Mayor Ashok Tiwari, provides advanced infrastructure, including Level III NICU, modern operating theaters, and induction rooms. It offers a range of services including high-risk pregnancy management, minimally invasive gynaecological surgeries, neonatal surgical interventions, fetal medicine services, and postnatal rehabilitation. A team of specialists, including paediatricians, gynaecologists, obstetricians, neonatologists, and fetal medicine specialists, will provide expert care. MatCare aims to bridge the gap in access to quality maternity and child healthcare in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.
