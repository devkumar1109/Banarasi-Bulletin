Chandigarh Records First COVID-19 Death

2025-05-28T11:25:00+00:00

Chandigarh reported its first COVID-19 death on May 28, 2025, involving a 40-year-old male from Firozabad, Uttar Pradesh. The patient, who worked in Ludhiana and had no comorbidities, succumbed to the infection at GMCH, Sector 32 after being admitted with severe respiratory problems. He tested positive for COVID-19 on Tuesday. This marks the first COVID-19 death in Chandigarh despite the nation facing a surge in infections. Health authorities urge citizens to follow preventive measures, wear masks, maintain hygiene, and avoid self-medication.
