Chandigarh Records First COVID-19 Death

2025-05-28T11:25:00+00:00

Chandigarh reported its first COVID-19 death, a 40-year-old man from Firozabad, Uttar Pradesh, who was referred from Ludhiana. He was admitted with severe respiratory problems, tested positive, and died at GMCH, Sector 32. The patient had no comorbidities. This marks the city's first COVID-19 death, and the eighth nationwide amidst a recent surge. Health authorities are investigating the variant responsible and urging citizens to follow preventive measures like wearing masks, maintaining hygiene, and avoiding crowded places.
