Varanasi Rape: 14 Arrested in Gruesome Six-Day Assault

2025-04-16T16:05:00+00:00

Varanasi Police arrested 14 individuals in connection with the gang rape of a 19-year-old woman. The alleged incident occurred over six days between March 29 and April 4, during which the woman was drugged and moved between multiple hotels in the city. Police conducted searches at over 100 hotels and hookah bars as part of the investigation.
