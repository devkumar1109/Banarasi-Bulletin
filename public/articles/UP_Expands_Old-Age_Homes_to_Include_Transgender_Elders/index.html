UP Expands Old-Age Homes to Include Transgender Elders.

2025-04-30T13:51:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has expanded old-age home facilities to include transgender individuals aged 60 and above. This initiative aims to provide shelter and ensure a dignified and secure life for elderly members of the transgender community. The government stated its commitment to caring for this vulnerable population.
