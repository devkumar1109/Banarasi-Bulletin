Jealousy Turns Deadly: Man Shoots Wife in UP

2025-04-23T13:12:00+00:00

In Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, Shakeel, son of Kant Nagar Panchayat president Munara Begum, allegedly shot his wife, Nigar alias Reena (28), suspecting her of infidelity. The incident occurred at their home in Kant town. Nigar is in critical condition at the medical college. Shakeel fled after the shooting but was later arrested. Superintendent of Police Rajesh Dwivedi visited the medical college to inquire about the incident. Nigar reportedly stated that her husband shot her due to his suspicion of her infidelity.
