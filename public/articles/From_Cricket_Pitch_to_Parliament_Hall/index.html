From Cricket Pitch to Parliament Hall

2025-06-01T05:37:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Rinku Singh will marry Member of Parliament Priya Saroj in a lavish ceremony. Their engagement will take place on June 8, 2025, in Lucknow, followed by the wedding on November 18, 2025, in Varanasi. Priya Saroj, 25, is the youngest Lok Sabha MP from Machhlishahr, representing the Samajwadi Party. Rinku Singh, 27, gained fame playing for the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) in the IPL and was part of India's T20 World Cup-winning squad in 2025. The wedding is expected to be attended by prominent figures from politics, cinema, and the industry.
