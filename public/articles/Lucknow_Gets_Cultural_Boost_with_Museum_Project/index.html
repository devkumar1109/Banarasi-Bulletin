Lucknow Gets Cultural Boost with Museum Project

2025-03-29T15:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will build a new museum at Lucknow's Rashtra Prerna Sthal. The project, aligned with Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's vision, has a budget of over Rs 65 crore. Construction will start soon by the Lucknow Development Authority, with an initial installment of Rs 22.55 crore already disbursed. The museum will be curated and feature artistic installations at Rashtra Prerna Sthal in Basant Kunj Yojna Sector-J. Additionally, the project includes a cafeteria at the State Museum's Old Kothi and library expansion. This expansion has a budget of Rs 4.87 crore and will be executed by the Uttar Pradesh Project Corporation Limited.
