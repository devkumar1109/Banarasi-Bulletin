Pathak Slams UP Health Center, Demands Better Care

2025-04-17T18:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak conducted a surprise inspection at the Harnahee Community Health Centre, criticizing health officials for irregularities. He emphasized the need for full-time doctor availability at government hospitals and urged periodic community meetings to build trust. Pathak banned private practice by government physicians and warned of further unannounced inspections. He stressed the importance of improving public healthcare to surpass private facilities.
