UP Boasts Progress Under Yogi's Eight Years

2025-03-26T00:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak stated that the state has seen unprecedented improvement in various sectors during the last eight years of the Yogi Adityanath government. Pathak highlighted improvements in infrastructure, including roads, flyovers, electricity, water, education, and healthcare. He emphasized the government's commitment to the poor, backward, and underprivileged, stating that Uttar Pradesh will play a crucial role in India's development. BJP MLA Rajeshwar Singh, also present at the event, praised the Yogi government's achievements, citing job creation, improved ease of doing business ranking, and substantial investment. He also mentioned a significant decrease in crime, with numerous encounters, arrests, and the imposition of stringent laws against criminals.
