UP Boosts Security: Two New UPSSF Battalions Planned

2025-04-20T00:56:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's government is strengthening security infrastructure by constructing new campuses for the Uttar Pradesh Special Security Force (UPSSF). The 3rd Battalion will be established in Prayagraj with residential and non-residential facilities on 42.03 acres of land in Arail area. A 4th battalion will be built in Mathura. This initiative aligns with CM Yogi Adityanath's vision for enhanced security in the state.
