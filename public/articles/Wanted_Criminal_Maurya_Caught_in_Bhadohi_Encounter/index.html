Wanted Criminal Maurya Caught in Bhadohi Encounter

2025-02-28T06:51:00+00:00

Sachin Maurya, a wanted criminal with a 25,000 rupee bounty, was arrested in an encounter by Gyanpur police and district SWAT in Bhadohi. Maurya was wanted for robbery and had a history of criminal activity in Bhadohi and Prayagraj. He was arrested after snatching a necklace from a woman in an e-rickshaw. Police recovered an illegal firearm, ammunition, and the motorcycle used in the crime. Maurya sustained a gunshot wound to the leg during the encounter and is in stable condition. His accomplice escaped and efforts are underway to apprehend him.
