Drunk Driver's Fury: Vadodara Crash Claims Life

2025-03-15T15:09:00+00:00

A car crash in Vadodara, Gujarat, killed a woman and injured four others. 23-year-old Rakshit Chaurasia, allegedly intoxicated, lost control of his car, hitting multiple vehicles. Chaurasia initially claimed he wasn't drunk and blamed potholes for the crash, but expressed remorse and wished to meet the victim's family. CCTV footage shows the speeding car colliding with scooters, dragging the riders. Janhvi Kapoor condemned the incident on social media, calling the behavior "appalling and enraging."
