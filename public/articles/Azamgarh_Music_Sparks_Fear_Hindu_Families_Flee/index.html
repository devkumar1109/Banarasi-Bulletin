Azamgarh: Music Sparks Fear, Hindu Families Flee

2025-06-16T04:25:00+00:00

In Azamgarh's Chhota Pura, approximately 40 Hindu families are selling their homes after facing alleged harassment and violence for playing music during religious and wedding events. The situation escalated following a clash on June 3rd. Families claim the district administration failed to address their concerns, leaving them feeling unsafe. Police have acknowledged the situation and promised increased security presence to ensure the families' safety.
