Indian City Rents Soar Post-Pandemic

2025-05-15T05:57:00+00:00

After a pandemic-induced decline in urban rental demand, Indian cities are experiencing a surge in rents. The post-pandemic urban reset, driven by the return of employees to offices and offices reopening, has fueled this increase. Cities like Bengaluru, Mumbai, Chennai, and Delhi NCR are witnessing bidding wars for quality housing as rental demand catches up. The surge is a response to suppressed demand during the pandemic, when many people left cities for their hometowns.
