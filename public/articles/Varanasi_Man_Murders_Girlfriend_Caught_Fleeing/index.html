Varanasi Man Murders Girlfriend, Caught Fleeing

2025-03-03T02:25:00+00:00

Vishal Sahani, a 22-year-old man from Varanasi, was arrested for murdering his girlfriend, Ananya, in Jaunpur. They had been in a relationship since 2019. An argument escalated into a physical altercation where Vishal hit Ananya with a frying pan. He then packed her body in a trolley bag and dumped it in bushes near a hospital. Police recovered the murder weapon and identified Vishal through CCTV footage. He was attempting to flee to Kerala when apprehended at Jaunpur Junction.
