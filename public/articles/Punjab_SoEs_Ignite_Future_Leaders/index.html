Punjab SoEs Ignite Future Leaders

2025-05-30T07:35:00+00:00

The Punjab School Education Department conducted a three-day orientation program for newly admitted Class IX students across all Schools of Eminence (SoEs) from May 26 to 28. The program featured expert talks, alumni success stories, and interactive activities, aiming to inspire and motivate students. Speakers included Dr. Sandeep Singh Sandhu, a tech professional; Principal Secretary Rahul Bhandari; DSP Karamjeet Kaur, alumna; Dr. Manjit Singh Rataul, Senior Medical Officer; Prof. Amandeep Kaur Dhaliwal; and Colonel Paramjit Singh. The program emphasized the importance of reading, lifelong learning, resilience, and service to the nation, encouraging students to dream big and achieve their aspirations.
