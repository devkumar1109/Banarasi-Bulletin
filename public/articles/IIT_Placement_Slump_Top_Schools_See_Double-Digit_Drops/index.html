IIT Placement Slump: Top Schools See Double-Digit Drops

2025-03-28T06:15:00+00:00

Placement percentages for BTech students at over half of India's 23 IITs dropped by more than 10 percentage points in 2023-24 compared to 2021-22. This information was revealed by the union government to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Education. Notable drops include IIT Madras (12 percentage points), IIT Bombay (around 13 percentage points), IIT Kanpur (11 percentage points), and IIT Delhi (around 15 percentage points). In 2021-22, 14 IITs recorded over 90% placements. In 2023-24, only three IITs (Jodhpur, Patna, and Goa) achieved that mark. IIT Dharwad experienced the sharpest decline, with placements dropping by approximately 25 percentage points. IIT Kharagpur saw the smallest drop at 2.88 percentage points.
