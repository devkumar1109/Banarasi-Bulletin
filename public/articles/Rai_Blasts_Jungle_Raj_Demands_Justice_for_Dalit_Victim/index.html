Rai Blasts "Jungle Raj," Demands Justice for Dalit Victim

2025-04-14T15:34:00+00:00

UP Congress president Ajay Rai criticized the BJP government's handling of law and order, calling it "Jungle Raj." He cited the recent burning death of a Dalit youth in Karchhana, Prayagraj, as an example of the deteriorating situation. Rai demanded justice for the victim's family, including compensation, a job, and a permanent house. He also criticized the BJP and RSS for their alleged hypocrisy in claiming to support Dalits while incidents of violence against them continue. Rai visited the victim's family and pledged the Congress party's support.
