VOGIC AI: Smart Crowd Control for Varanasi

2025-03-12T16:43:00+00:00

VOGIC AI, an Indian startup, received a $50,000 grant from the Toyota Mobility Foundation to develop a crowd management solution for Varanasi's old city. The company's Vision-Language AI technology analyzes CCTV and drone footage, turning it into actionable intelligence. This allows for proactive security measures by identifying potential issues like overcrowding. VOGIC AI will collaborate with local authorities, the World Resources Institute, and city planners to implement the solution. The startup is also working with the Indian Army, demonstrating the potential of its technology in defense and security applications.
