UP Aims for ₹5 Trillion Export Boom by 2030

2025-04-18T09:05:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh aims to increase its exports to over ₹5 trillion by 2030, nearly tripling its current level. The state government is revamping its export policy, focusing on new markets in Latin America and Eastern Europe. Incentives for exporters will be increased and infrastructure support enhanced to achieve this goal. The new policy is expected to be approved shortly, aligning with the Central government's efforts to diversify India's export destinations.
