Uttar Pradesh Rocked by Brutal Crimes Against Men, Children

2025-03-05T02:02:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Auriya district, a six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her 18-year-old relative. The incident occurred in a farmland where the accused was working. The girl, a Class-1 student, is in stable condition after being treated at a community health center and then referred to the district hospital. Police have formed three teams to arrest the accused. Separately, in Ghaziabad, four men allegedly barged into the house of a 42-year-old man and mutilated his private part while he was asleep. The victim, Sanjay Yadav, is undergoing treatment in Meerut. His son, Prince Yadav, filed a complaint stating the assailants rendered Yadav unconscious with an intoxicant before inflicting the injury.
