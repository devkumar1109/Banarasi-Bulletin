Gorakhpur Gears Up for Cricket Glory

2025-05-19T11:35:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will soon have its fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur. Construction has officially begun on the 50-acre site at Tal Nadaur, Gorakhpur, with an estimated investment of Rs 236.40 crore. The stadium, a dream project of Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, is expected to be completed in 18 months. It will feature seven main pitches and four practice pitches, with a seating capacity of approximately 30,000 spectators. The stadium will meet international standards set by the ICC and will be a multipurpose venue capable of hosting various large-scale events. Construction includes entry gates, security checkpoints, stands, pavilions, and parking for 1,500 vehicles. Amenities like concourses, restrooms, and video boards will be provided for spectator convenience.
