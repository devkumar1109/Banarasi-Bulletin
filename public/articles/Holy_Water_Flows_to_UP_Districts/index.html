Holy Water Flows to UP Districts

2025-03-03T10:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has ordered the distribution of holy water from Triveni Sangam to all 75 districts. The first tanker carrying 12,000 litres of sacred water arrived in Varanasi, with plans to distribute 20,000 litres to residents who missed the Maha Kumbh. Four tankers transported the water, which is being stored at the Chetganj Fire Station. The distribution will occur according to government directives. A total of 21 fire tenders initially transported the holy water, and it will be sent to all districts in four phases.
