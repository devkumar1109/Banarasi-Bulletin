Bhuvneshwar's Lucky Duck, Kohli's Test Fire

2025-05-07T15:37:00+00:00

Bhuvneshwar Kumar shared a special moment with Sachin Tendulkar in the 2008-09 Ranji Trophy final. Kumar dismissed Tendulkar for a 15-ball duck in the first innings of the match, which Mumbai won by 243 runs. Kumar took six wickets and scored 121 runs in the game. He described the wicket as a stroke of luck, an inside edge that went to a fielder positioned behind short-leg. Kumar also credited Virat Kohli for transforming the Indian Test team's aggression.
