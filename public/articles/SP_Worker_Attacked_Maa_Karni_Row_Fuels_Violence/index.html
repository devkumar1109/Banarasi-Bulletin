SP Worker Attacked, "Maa Karni" Row Fuels Violence

2025-04-13T14:31:00+00:00

A Samajwadi Party (SP) worker, Harish Mishra, was allegedly attacked with a knife by two individuals outside his house in Varanasi. Locals intervened, saving Mishra and apprehending the attackers. Avinash Mishra, one of the accused, claimed the attack stemmed from a previous statement made by Harish Mishra against "Maa Karni." Police have detained three individuals and registered cases based on complaints from both parties. Akhilesh Yadav, SP chief, condemned the attack, calling it a symbol of lawlessness in Uttar Pradesh.
