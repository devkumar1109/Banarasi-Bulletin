Varanasi, Gorakhpur Stun Rivals, Reach Final

2025-04-19T18:19:00+00:00

Varanasi defeated Saharanpur 4-0 to advance to the final of the UP under-20 inter divisional state men football championship. Ashish, Asif, Abhishek, and Karunesh scored for Varanasi. In another semi-final, Gorakhpur edged out Lucknow 1-0, with Amritansh scoring the winning goal. Kanpur secured their spot in the semi-finals by defeating Ayodhya 2-0 in extra time.
