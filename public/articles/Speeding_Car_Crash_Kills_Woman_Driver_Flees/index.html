Speeding Car Crash Kills Woman, Driver Flees

2025-03-15T03:47:00+00:00

A speeding car driven by Rakshit Chaurasia, a law student from Varanasi, crashed into a two-wheeler in Vadodara, Gujarat, killing a woman and injuring others. The incident, captured on CCTV, showed the car traveling at over 100 kmph before colliding with the two-wheeler. Chaurasia's friend, Meet Chauhan, who owned the car, fled the scene. Chaurasia, who was allegedly drunk, was beaten by the crowd before being handed over to the police.
