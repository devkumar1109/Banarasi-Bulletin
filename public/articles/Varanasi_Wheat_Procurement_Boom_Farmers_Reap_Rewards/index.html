Varanasi Wheat Procurement Boom: Farmers Reap Rewards

2025-04-21T18:19:00+00:00

Wheat procurement in Varanasi division has significantly increased this year, reaching 9,711.59 metric tons across Varanasi, Chandauli, Ghazipur, and Jaunpur districts. This represents a rise of 3,746.88 metric tons compared to 2024. The government has implemented measures to ensure fair prices for farmers, including purchasing wheat directly from their homes, waiving verification for sales up to 100 quintals, and offering Rs 2,425 per quintal (Rs 150 higher than last year) plus Rs 20 for cleaning and sieving. Payments are made within 48 hours to farmers' Aadhaar-linked accounts. With 332 procurement centers and a target of 127,500 metric tons for the division, the government aims to benefit farmers through these initiatives.
