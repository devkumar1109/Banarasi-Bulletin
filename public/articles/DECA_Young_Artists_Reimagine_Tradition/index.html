DECA: Young Artists Reimagine Tradition

2025-04-15T18:43:00+00:00

Ten young artists from the Faculty of Visual Arts at Banaras Hindu University are showcasing their work in an exhibition titled "DECA" at the Ahivasi Art Gallery. The three-day exhibition, which began on Tuesday, explores contemporary themes such as human relationships, identity, socio-cultural transitions, and cosmic symbolism. The artists, who are all Master's students in painting, draw inspiration from traditional Indian art forms, abstract symbolism, rural-urban contrasts, and environmental consciousness. Curated by Suresh Chandra Jangid, the exhibition provides a platform for these emerging artists to express their creative voices and embark on a new artistic journey.
