Sikkim Shocker: 8 Arrested for Year-Long Rape of 13-Year-Old

2025-04-13T06:46:00+00:00

Eight people, including four juveniles, have been arrested in Sikkim for allegedly raping a 13-year-old girl for nearly a year. The abuse came to light when the girl confided in a school counsellor. The accused include the woman who lured the victim with domestic chores, her husband, two men who were paid for sex, and four boys. The Child Welfare Committee lodged the complaint, leading to the arrests. The victim is receiving counseling and medical treatment. Police have registered a case under the BNS and POCSO Act. This incident follows a case in Varanasi where a 19-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by over 23 men for seven days.
