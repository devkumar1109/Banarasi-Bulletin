Retired Professor Dodges Online Scam

2025-05-22T19:15:00+00:00

A retired BHU professor, Dr. PR Gupta, was almost scammed out of his savings by cyber fraudsters. They impersonated Supreme Court investigators and pressured him to prematurely withdraw his fixed deposits. Dr. Gupta, feeling distressed, went to his bank. The alert bank manager, noticing his concern, contacted the police. Inspector Lanka Shivakant Mishra and the cybercrime cell intervened, preventing the fraud. Dr. Gupta had received calls from a young man claiming he was under investigation and instructed to transfer money to settle the matter.
