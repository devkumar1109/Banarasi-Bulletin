Data-Driven Plan to Tame Varanasi's Crowds

2025-03-20T18:09:00+00:00

Varanasi Municipal Corporation presented a data-driven plan to manage increasing crowds in the city. Developed by Toyota Mobility Foundation, WRI India, and Urban Lab, the plan analyzed crowd data from the past three years, identifying peak crowd periods and locations. Areas around Dashashwamedh Ghat, Godowlia to Maidagin, and Assi are the most crowded, particularly during festivals like Dev Deepawali and Maha Shivaratri. The plan suggests crowd mapping, creating a holding area in Beniabagh, and advance assessment of crowd growth. The "Sustainable Cities Challenge" workshop, involving global experts, aims to find data-driven solutions for crowd management and enhance pedestrian experience in Varanasi.
