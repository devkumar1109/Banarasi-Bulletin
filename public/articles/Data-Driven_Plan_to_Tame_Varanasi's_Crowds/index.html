Data-Driven Plan to Tame Varanasi's Crowds

2025-03-20T18:09:00+00:00

To combat increasing crowds in Varanasi, the Varanasi Municipal Corporation presented a data-driven plan developed by Toyota Mobility Foundation, WRI India, and Urban Lab. The plan, based on crowd surveys from the past three years, highlights that crowd levels surged after 2023, particularly during festivals. The most congested areas are from the church to Dashashwamedh ghat, Godowlia to Maidagin, and the Assi area. The plan suggests utilizing crowd mapping, creating a holding area in Beniabagh, and pre-assessing crowd growth. The "Sustainable Cities Challenge" workshop, involving 10 global experts, aims to identify data-driven solutions for managing crowd movement and enhancing pedestrian experience in Varanasi.
