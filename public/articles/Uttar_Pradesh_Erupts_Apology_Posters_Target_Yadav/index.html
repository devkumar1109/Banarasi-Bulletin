Uttar Pradesh Erupts: Apology Posters Target Yadav

2025-05-20T09:32:00+00:00

Tensions are high in Uttar Pradesh after posters demanding an apology from Akhilesh Yadav surfaced in Lucknow. The posters condemn alleged derogatory remarks made by the Samajwadi Party's official 'X' handle against Deputy CM Brajesh Pathak. Pathak criticized Yadav's response to a question about the SP's alleged connections to controversial figures and past scandals, accusing him of deflecting with a poorly written "thesis." Pathak challenged Yadav to address the SP's "DNA," claiming that it would reveal links to figures like Atiq Ahmed and Mukhtar Ansari, and past controversies such as the Muzaffarnagar riots and scams.
