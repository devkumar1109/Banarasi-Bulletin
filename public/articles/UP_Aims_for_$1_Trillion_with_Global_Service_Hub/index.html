UP Aims for $1 Trillion with Global Service Hub

2025-05-07T06:18:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government approved the Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy, aiming to establish the state as a "global service centre" and contribute to its $1 trillion economy goal. The policy seeks to attract global companies in IT, banking, healthcare, engineering, and next-generation technologies by offering incentives. UP has a large talent pool in various sectors, making it attractive for multinational companies looking for cost-effective, high-quality work. Software and IT companies are establishing GCCs focusing on machine learning, cloud computing, and AI-driven development. The policy is expected to attract more companies like Microsoft, which recently laid the foundation for a 10,000-seater development centre in Noida.
