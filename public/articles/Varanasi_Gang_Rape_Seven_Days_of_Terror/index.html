Varanasi Gang Rape: Seven Days of Terror

2025-04-09T04:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. The victim's father reported that she went missing on March 29th after visiting a ghat with a friend and was found unconscious on April 4th. Police have arrested nine accused and sent them to judicial custody, while searching for the remaining 14, 11 of whom are unidentified. The victim alleges she was intoxicated and repeatedly raped by the men, indicating a planned attack. The police are investigating the case under relevant sections of the law.
