Varanasi Gang Rape: Seven Days of Terror

2025-04-09T04:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over a seven-day period. The victim was found unconscious on April 4th and, after treatment, disclosed the horrifying ordeal to police. Nine accused have been arrested and sent to judicial custody, while the remaining 14 are still at large. The police are investigating the case, which involves the victim being lured, intoxicated, and repeatedly assaulted. The victim's father has urged Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath for justice, emphasizing the need for severe punishment to deter future crimes.
