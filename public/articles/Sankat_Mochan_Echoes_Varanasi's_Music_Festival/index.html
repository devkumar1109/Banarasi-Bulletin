Sankat Mochan Echoes: Varanasi's Music Festival

2025-04-18T19:05:00+00:00

The 102nd Sankat Mochan Sangeet Samaroh, a week-long classical music and dance festival, commenced in Varanasi on April 16th with a flute recital by Pandit Hari Prasad Chaurasia. The festival, held at the Sankat Mochan temple, coincides with Hanuman Jayanti and concludes on April 21st. This year's lineup features renowned artists like Ustad Wasifuddin Dagar, Rahul Sharma, Pravin Godkhindi, Pt Ajay Pohankar, and Ajoy Chakrabarty. Notable is the debut of young tabla prodigy Vivek Pandya. The festival, sustained by community support and local sponsors, emphasizes community engagement and breaking social barriers. Musicians perform in close proximity to the audience, fostering a sense of shared devotion and artistic experience.
