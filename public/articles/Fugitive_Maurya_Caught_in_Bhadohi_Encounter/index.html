Fugitive Maurya Caught in Bhadohi Encounter

2025-02-28T06:51:00+00:00

A wanted criminal, Sachin Maurya, with a 25,000 rupee bounty, was arrested in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh, following an encounter with Gyanpur police and district SWAT. Maurya was wanted for robbery and had prior criminal cases in Bhadohi and Prayagraj. He was involved in a necklace snatching incident on February 21 near Marchwar village. Police recovered an illegal firearm, ammunition, and the motorcycle used in the crime. Maurya sustained a leg injury during the encounter and is hospitalized in stable condition. His accomplice escaped and is being hunted.
