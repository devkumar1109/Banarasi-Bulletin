High-Speed Race Ends in Fatal Kanpur Crash

2025-04-06T06:08:00+00:00

A motorcyclist, Naveen Gupta (42), died in Kanpur after being hit by an Innova during a high-speed race with a BMW. The accident occurred near the South Cricket Academy, throwing Gupta into a parked car. The Innova driver fled but was later arrested as the car's number plate fell off. CCTV footage showed the Innova speeding at around 100 km/h when it hit Gupta's bike. The Innova was registered to a contractor, Gopal Bajpai, and the driver was identified as Ayush Mishra, who was found to be intoxicated. Police are investigating eyewitnesses' claims of a race and have filed a case against the driver.
