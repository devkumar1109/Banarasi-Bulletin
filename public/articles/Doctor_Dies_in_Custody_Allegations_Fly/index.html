Doctor Dies in Custody, Allegations Fly

2025-06-17T14:05:00+00:00

Dr. Venkatesh Maur, superintendent of Bansdih Community Health Centre (CHC) in Ballia, died in Varanasi district jail after being arrested for allegedly accepting a bribe. Doctors in Ballia suspended outpatient services in protest, demanding a CBI inquiry into the death, alleging foul play. Dr. Maur was arrested by the Vigilance Establishment while accepting Rs 20,000 from a PMBJAK operator. The PMSA president termed the death a "pre-planned murder" and claimed Dr. Maur was healthy before arrest. Retired IPS officer Amitabh Thakur also demanded an inquiry, alleging Dr. Maur was subjected to inhuman treatment and extortion by jail officials.
