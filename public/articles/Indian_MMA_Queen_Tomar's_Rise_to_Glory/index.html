Indian MMA Queen: Tomar's Rise to Glory

2025-03-07T13:45:00+00:00

Puja Tomar, the first Indian woman to win a UFC bout, boasts a 9-4-0 MMA record. The Strawweight fighter, trained in Wushu, holds a 5-match winning streak. Her UFC debut victory against Brazilian fighter Rayanne dos Santos solidified her position in Indian MMA. Tomar's next UFC fight is scheduled against Irish fighter Shauna Banon on March 22, 2025. Despite financial challenges and limited sponsorship, Tomar, who trains at Soma Fight Club in Bali, Indonesia, is estimated to have a net worth of $250,000 to $500,000. Fittr is her current sponsor.
