Uttar Pradesh Rolls to Handball Final Win

2025-03-29T20:08:00+00:00

Hosts Uttar Pradesh defeated Maharashtra 13-7 to advance to the final of the 47th National Junior Girls Handball Championship. They will face Himachal Pradesh, who beat Rajasthan 20-16 in the other semi-final. Rajasthan and Maharashtra shared the bronze medal. Uttar Pradesh led 6-5 at halftime and dominated the second half, with Suman scoring four goals and Ananya contributing three. UP's Usha was named Player of the Match. The final will be held on Sunday and will be attended by Deputy Chief Minister Brijesh Pathak.
