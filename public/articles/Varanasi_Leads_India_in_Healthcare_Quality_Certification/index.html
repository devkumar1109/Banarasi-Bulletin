Varanasi Leads India in Healthcare Quality Certification

2025-06-12T23:29:00+00:00

Varanasi district has become the first in India to have 104 medical units certified with the National Quality Assurance Standards (NQAS). These certified units include Shri Shiv Prasad Gupta Divisional Hospital, Community Health Center Cholapur, 10 urban and rural primary health centers, and 92 Ayushman Arogya Temples (health sub-centers). The certification reflects the district's commitment to quality healthcare and is a result of continuous efforts to strengthen medical and health facilities at the community level. Ayushman Arogya Temples scored well on seven standards set by the government, including maternal and child health, family planning, and management of communicable and non-communicable diseases.
