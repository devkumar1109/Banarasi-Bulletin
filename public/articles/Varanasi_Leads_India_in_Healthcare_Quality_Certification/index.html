Varanasi Leads India in Healthcare Quality Certification

2025-06-12T23:29:00+00:00

Varanasi district has become the first in India to have 104 medical units certified with the National Quality Assurance Standards (NQAS). These certified units include Shri Shiv Prasad Gupta Divisional Hospital, Community Health Center Cholapur, 10 urban and rural primary health centers, and 92 Ayushman Arogya Temples (health sub-centers). The certification reflects the commitment to quality in health services and covers seven standards set by the government of India, including care in pregnancy and childbirth, neonatal and infant health services, and management of communicable and non-communicable diseases. All remaining Ayushman Arogya temples in rural areas are enrolled for NQAS, while urban and rural community and primary health centers are undergoing the assessment process.
