Bribe Case Doctor Dies in Jail

2025-06-16T18:52:00+00:00

Dr. Venkatesh Mauwar, superintendent of Bansdih community health center (CHC) in Ballia district, passed away on Monday at SPG divisional hospital in Varanasi. He was arrested on June 12 by a vigilance cell team while accepting a bribe of Rs 20,000 from a Jan Aushadhi Kendra operator. Mauwar was suffering from fever, hypertension, and anxiety since his imprisonment. His condition worsened when his relatives visited him at the jail. He was referred to the SPG hospital where he died during treatment. The vigilance cell had arrested Mauwar after the Jan Aushadhi Kendra operator alleged that the CHC superintendent demanded a monthly bribe of Rs 20,000 for releasing the approval letter for operating the Kendra.
