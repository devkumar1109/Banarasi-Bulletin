UPSIDA, IIT-BHU Join to Build Bundelkhand Pharma Hub

2025-04-09T23:01:00+00:00

UPSIDA signed an MoU with IIT-BHU to develop Pharma Parks in Lalitpur district, Bundelkhand. The parks will span 1,472 acres across five villages and house pharmaceutical industries. IIT-BHU's Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology (DOPET) will be a key partner, providing research, skilled human resources, and technological solutions to companies within the parks.
