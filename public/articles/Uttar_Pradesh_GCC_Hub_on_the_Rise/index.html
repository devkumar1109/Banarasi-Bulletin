Uttar Pradesh: GCC Hub on the Rise

2025-06-09T19:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is aiming to become a hub for Global Capability Centres (GCCs) by offering subsidies, reduced operational costs, and a skilled workforce. A conclave will be held in Lucknow on June 11th to discuss this strategy with industry leaders. The state boasts 8,375 higher education institutions and 1.25 lakh polytechnic seats, providing a pool of skilled labor in technology, digital engineering, finance, and other sectors. The government aims to connect this talent with industry needs, creating jobs and offering cost-effective setups for companies. The conclave will feature participation from industry leaders like Microsoft India, MAQ Software, Tata Consultancy Services, and NASSCOM.
