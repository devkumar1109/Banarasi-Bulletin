BHU, UPSIDA Team Up for Bundelkhand Pharma Parks

2025-04-09T23:01:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority (UPSIDA) signed a MoU with the Indian Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University (BHU) to develop Pharma Parks in Lalitpur district, Bundelkhand region. The project will establish pharmaceutical industries across 1,472 acres spanning five villages. BHU's Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology (DOPET) will be a key knowledge and technology partner, providing research, skilled human resources, and innovative solutions to companies operating within the parks.
