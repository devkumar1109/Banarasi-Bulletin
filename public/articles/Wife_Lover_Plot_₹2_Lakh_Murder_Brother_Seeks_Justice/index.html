Wife, Lover Plot ₹2 Lakh Murder, Brother Seeks Justice

2025-03-26T07:22:00+00:00

In Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh, a man named Dilip Yadav was killed by contract killers hired by his wife, Pragati, and her lover, Anurag Yadav. Police identified Anurag and arrested him along with his aide, Ramji Nagar. Anurag had allegedly planned the murder for ₹2 lakh and was in a four-year relationship with Pragati. The brother and sister of the accused have both spoken out, demanding justice for the victim and punishment for those involved, regardless of their family ties. Police are continuing to investigate and search for other individuals involved in the crime.
