Bali Bound Air India Diverted to Varanasi

2025-06-18T18:26:00+00:00

An Air India flight (AI 2146) from Bali to Delhi was diverted to Varanasi due to bad weather on Tuesday afternoon. The flight, carrying 187 passengers, landed at Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport at 4:20 pm. After a two-hour delay, the aircraft took off for Delhi at 7 pm when weather conditions improved. The flight originated from Denpasar, Indonesia, and was scheduled to land in Delhi around 2:30 pm.
