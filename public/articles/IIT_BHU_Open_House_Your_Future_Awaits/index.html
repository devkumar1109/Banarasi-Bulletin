IIT BHU Open House: Your Future Awaits.

2025-06-05T13:55:00+00:00

IIT BHU will host an online Open House Session for JEE Advanced 2025 qualified candidates on June 11, 2025, from 3:30 to 6:30 pm. The session will be chaired by Director Prof. Amit Patra and attended by other key academic and administrative leaders. Interested candidates must register through forms.gle/konz7bdWuuqscSFTA by 6 pm on June 10, 2025. The session will provide insights into the admission process, hostel facilities, campus security, and other aspects of life at IIT BHU. Candidates can interact with the leadership team and have their queries addressed.
