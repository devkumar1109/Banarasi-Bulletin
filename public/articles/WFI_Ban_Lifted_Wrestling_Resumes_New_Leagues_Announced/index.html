WFI Ban Lifted: Wrestling Resumes, New Leagues Announced

2025-03-11T09:12:00+00:00

The Wrestling Federation of India (WFI) ban has been revoked by the Union Sports Ministry. WFI President Sanjay Singh expressed gratitude to PM Modi, HM Amit Shah, and Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya for the decision. The suspension hindered camps and events that benefited wrestlers, particularly those from underprivileged backgrounds. Singh announced the launch of wrestling leagues by June-July and again by November-December, aiming to provide athletes with increased exposure and opportunities.
