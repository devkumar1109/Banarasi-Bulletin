Yogi Slams "Dynasties," Boasts UP's Economic Growth

2025-04-19T19:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized "dynastic" political leaders for resisting inclusive development, stating they prioritize personal gain over public interest. He highlighted the state's economic growth under the BJP government, with the economy expanding from Rs 12.75 lakh crore in 2017 to Rs 30 lakh crore and per capita income doubling. Adityanath also cited infrastructure improvements, including six operational expressways, and cited the 2025 Mahakumbh in Prayagraj as an example of effective governance. He emphasized the importance of responsible media in the digital age, warning against misinformation and deepfakes. Adityanath reiterated the government's commitment to making India a self-reliant and developed nation by 2047.
