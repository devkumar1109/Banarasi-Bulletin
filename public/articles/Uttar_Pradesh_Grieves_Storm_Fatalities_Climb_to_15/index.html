Uttar Pradesh Grieves: Storm Fatalities Climb to 15

2025-05-22T15:09:00+00:00

At least 15 people died in Uttar Pradesh due to severe storms and heavy rainfall. Incidents included a unipole falling on a car, a tree crushing a man, and a collapsed wall trapping a worker. Lightning strikes killed two men in Saharanpur district. Winds toppled trees, damaged property, and caused widespread power outages. A fire erupted in an oil factory in Budaun after the storm caused a portion of the building to collapse, sparking an oil fire. A subsequent explosion occurred but did not result in casualties. A constable died in Bijnor when his motorcycle collided with a fallen tree. CM Yogi Adityanath has ordered relief and compensation measures.
