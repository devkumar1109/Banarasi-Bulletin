Punjab Triumphs, Clinches Hockey National Title

2025-04-16T13:22:00+00:00

Hockey Punjab defeated Hockey Madhya Pradesh 4-1 to win the Senior Men’s National Championship 2025 in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. Punjab had previously lost to MP in the pool stage but bounced back with victories against Haryana and Uttar Pradesh in the quarter-final and semi-final respectively. Jugraj Singh scored a brace in the final and finished the tournament as the second-highest goalscorer with five goals. Coach Rajinder Singh attributed the team's success to regrouping after the loss to MP and shifting from zonal-marking to man-marking. Captain Hardik Singh highlighted the team's attacking strategy and expressed satisfaction with the win.
