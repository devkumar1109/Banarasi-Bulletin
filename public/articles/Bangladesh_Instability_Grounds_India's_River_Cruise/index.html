Bangladesh Instability Grounds India's River Cruise

2025-05-21T18:43:00+00:00

Due to political instability in Bangladesh following the ouster of the Sheikh Hasina government, India has decided to defer the world's longest river cruise from Varanasi to Dibrugarh via Dhaka. The cruise, operating on the 3,200-km India-Bangladesh Protocol (IBP) route, has seen a decline in foreign tourist interest due to adverse travel advisories. Started in January 2023, the cruise transported around 60 passengers until now. While cargo movement on the IBP route continues uninterrupted, passenger services are suspended. India plans to resume the cruise after elections and political stability are restored in Bangladesh.
