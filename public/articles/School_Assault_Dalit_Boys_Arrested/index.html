School Assault: Dalit Boys Arrested

2025-05-21T15:15:00+00:00

Five young boys, aged 12-15 and from the Dalit community, were arrested for allegedly raping a 12-year-old girl inside a school in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on May 8, when the boys lured the girl to the school building, drugged her, and committed the crime. They filmed the assault and threatened to post the video online if she spoke out. A neighbor alerted the girl's mother after seeing the video, leading to a formal complaint filed on May 20. The accused, who are students in classes 7 and 9, have been taken into custody and medically examined. They will be presented before the Juvenile Justice Board.
