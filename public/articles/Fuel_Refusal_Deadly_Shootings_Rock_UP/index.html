Fuel Refusal, Deadly Shootings Rock UP

2025-04-10T07:51:00+00:00

In Bulandshahr, Uttar Pradesh, petrol pump manager Raju Sharma (30) was shot dead after refusing to fill fuel in a bottle. Two men on a motorcycle demanded the service, which violates safety regulations. The altercation escalated, leading one of the men to shoot Sharma. He died in the hospital. Police have registered an FIR and are reviewing CCTV footage to identify and apprehend the assailants. In a separate incident, a 28-year-old metal trader was shot dead in his sleep in Vivoli village, Etawah district.
