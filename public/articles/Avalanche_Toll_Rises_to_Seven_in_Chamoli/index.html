Avalanche Toll Rises to Seven in Chamoli

2025-03-02T12:00:00+00:00

Three more bodies were recovered from the avalanche-hit BRO camp in Chamoli, raising the death toll to seven. One worker remains missing. 46 rescued workers were brought to a military hospital in Jyotirmath, with one airlifted to AIIMS, Rishikesh due to a spinal cord injury. Three workers are in critical condition. The avalanche trapped 54 workers, but one was on unauthorized leave and survived. The search operation utilizes helicopters, sniffer dogs, and thermal imaging technology due to anticipated inclement weather on Monday. Uttarakhand Chief Minister Pushkar Singh Dhami visited the emergency operation centre and urged officials to expedite the rescue efforts.
