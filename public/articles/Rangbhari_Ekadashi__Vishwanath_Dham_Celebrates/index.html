Rangbhari Ekadashi: Vishwanath Dham Celebrates

2025-03-09T19:30:00+00:00

Tribal devotees from Sonbhadra offered Palash Gulal to Lord Vishwanath at Kashi Vishwanath Dham during Rangbhari Ekadashi celebrations. Naga Sadhus also arrived to offer turmeric to the deity. Offerings were sent from Kashi Vishwanath temple to Lord Krishna's birthplace temple in Mathura. A ceremonial worship, Shivarchanam, was held with silver idols of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. On Sunday, the silver palanquin of Lord Vishwanath's mobile idol was taken out in the temple courtyard, attracting thousands of devotees who observed the tradition of applying turmeric to the idols.
