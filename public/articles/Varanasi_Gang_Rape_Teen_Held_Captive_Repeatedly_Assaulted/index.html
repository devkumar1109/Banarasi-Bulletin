Varanasi Gang Rape: Teen Held Captive, Repeatedly Assaulted

2025-04-07T13:40:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly gang-raped by seven men over a week, from March 29 to April 4. The victim, a 12th-grade student, was lured to a hookah bar by a friend and drugged. She was then taken to various hotels where she was allegedly held captive and repeatedly raped. Six men have been arrested in connection with the crime, while the remaining suspects are still at large. Police have registered an FIR under multiple sections, including gang rape, wrongful confinement, criminal intimidation, and administering harmful substances. The victim's statement revealed that some of the accused were her social media friends and former classmates.
