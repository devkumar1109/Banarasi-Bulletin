Surat: Man Kidnaps, Rapes Six-Year-Old Girl

2025-03-15T23:56:00+00:00

In Surat, a six-year-old girl was kidnapped and raped by a 25-year-old man named Ajay Verma. The incident occurred in the Katargam area while the girl slept with her mother on a handcart. Verma took the girl to Katargam sports complex where he raped her before dropping her back at the original location. The Surat Crime Branch arrested Verma within five hours of the crime, utilizing CCTV footage and human intelligence. The victim is hospitalized and underwent surgery. Police have registered an offense under various sections of the BNS Act for kidnapping and rape.
