Agnihotri Ignites IFFRS 2025 in Gorakhpur

2025-03-21T17:35:00+00:00

The International Film Festival on Religion and Spirituality (IFFRS 2025) is taking place in Gorakhpur. Filmmaker Vivek Agnihotri, known for "The Kashmir Files," delivered a masterclass on storytelling and creativity. Over 15 films were screened, including "Mumukshu: Mrityu Ki Abhilasha" and "Khatu Shyam Mandir Ki Yatra." The festival, sponsored by the Uttar Pradesh Tourism department, showcases films from India and internationally. The closing ceremony will feature director Madhur Bhandarkar and a masterclass on acting by casting director Parag Mehta. 38 films were shortlisted from 125 global entries.
