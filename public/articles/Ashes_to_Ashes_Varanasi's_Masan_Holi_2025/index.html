Ashes to Ashes: Varanasi's Masan Holi 2025

2025-03-10T08:22:00+00:00

Masan Holi, a unique celebration in Varanasi, will be held on March 11, 2025, at Manikarnika Ghat and Harishchandra Ghat. Instead of colors, this Holi involves playing with ashes of pyres. The tradition is believed to bring happiness, prosperity, and blessings from Lord Shiva. Celebrated for six days from Rangbhari Ekadashi, Masan Holi marks the second day of the festivities. It is believed that on this day, Lord Shiva plays Holi with his followers at the ghats. The use of pyre ashes symbolizes the transience of life and the cyclical nature of existence.
