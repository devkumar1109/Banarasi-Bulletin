Lucknow COVID Surge: KGMU Professor Critical

2025-06-14T00:22:00+00:00

Five new COVID-19 cases were reported in Lucknow, including Professor Sandeep Tiwari, Head of the Trauma Surgery Department at KGMU, who is on ventilator support due to a sudden deterioration in his condition. The city has recorded 28 cases this season, with 20 currently active. Other new cases include a 7-year-old girl, a 26-year-old woman, and a 79-year-old man with comorbidities. Across Uttar Pradesh, 50 new cases were reported, with Gautam Buddha Nagar accounting for 35 and Ghaziabad for 8. The state now has 251 active cases.
