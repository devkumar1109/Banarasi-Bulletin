BJP Leader Shoots Family, Suspects Infidelity

2025-03-22T12:11:00+00:00

BJP leader Yogesh Rohilla allegedly shot his wife and three children in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh. His son and 11-year-old daughter died, while his wife and another child are in critical condition. Rohilla, a BJP executive member, suspected his wife of infidelity, according to police. He was apprehended at the scene with the weapon used in the crime. Saharanpur SSP Rohit Sajwan confirmed the incident and stated that Rohilla himself called the police. Local authorities are conducting a thorough investigation.
