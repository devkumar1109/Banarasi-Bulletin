Cow Smuggler Shot, Arrested in Muzaffarnagar

2025-04-19T06:31:00+00:00

A cow smuggler named Imran was injured and arrested after a police encounter in Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh. Circle Officer (Jansath) Yatendra Singh Nagar stated that Imran fired at the police, prompting retaliation. Imran sustained bullet injuries during the exchange and was subsequently apprehended.
