Varanasi Prepped for Modi's Grand Arrival

2025-04-03T17:33:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed preparations in Varanasi for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's upcoming visit. Adityanath instructed officials to ensure all projects slated for inauguration and foundation-laying are ready. He also addressed the issue of land subsidence at Namo Ghat, ordering immediate repairs. Beautification of the Varuna Riverfront was another point of emphasis. The meeting, held at the Circuit House auditorium, included presentations on development projects by Commissioner Kaushal Raj Sharma and a security briefing by Police Commissioner Mohit Agarwal.
