Debt, Doubt, and Death: UP Man Kills Partner

2025-06-06T12:58:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, Satendra Yadav confessed to strangling his partner, Neelesh, due to financial disputes and infidelity suspicions. Neelesh had lent Yadav Rs 5.5 lakh, which he failed to repay, escalating tensions. Yadav disposed of Neelesh's body in a suitcase near a canal road. Police recovered the vehicle and CCTV footage supporting Yadav's confession. Authorities will file a charge sheet against him.
