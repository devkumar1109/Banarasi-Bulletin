U.P. Aims to Be Sports Goods Global Hub

2025-04-08T17:10:00+00:00

The FICCI Sports Committee - Uttar Pradesh Chapter launched with a Round Table Discussion on the sports goods manufacturing industry in Uttar Pradesh. The event, held at IMT Ghaziabad, brought together 20 sports manufacturers, policymakers, sportspersons, and academics. Key speakers included Kanishka Pandey, Head - Centre for Sports Research at IMT Ghaziabad and Chair of the new FICCI Committee, and Padma Shri Ashok Dhyanchand, son of Major Dhyan Chand. The committee aims to address challenges, unlock opportunities, and position Uttar Pradesh as a global hub for sports goods manufacturing through policy reforms, research advocacy, and global competitiveness.
