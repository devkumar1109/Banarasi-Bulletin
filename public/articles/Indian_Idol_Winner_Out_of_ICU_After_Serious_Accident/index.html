Indian Idol Winner Out of ICU After Serious Accident

2025-05-08T13:15:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan, who was critically injured in a road accident on May 5th in Uttar Pradesh, has been moved out of the ICU and into a private room. Rajan suffered multiple fractures and a head injury after his SUV collided with a truck. He underwent a six-hour surgery to address some of his major fractures. He is currently under observation in the Medical ICU and is expected to undergo further surgeries in the next few days.
