Modi Attacks Opposition: Family Over Nation

2025-04-11T18:12:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi criticized opposition parties for prioritizing family interests over national welfare during a rally in Varanasi. He inaugurated projects worth over ₹3,880 crore, highlighting his government's commitment to inclusive growth. Modi emphasized Uttar Pradesh's transformation into a hub of competence and accomplishment, citing its leadership in GI-tagged products and improved healthcare facilities. He stated that while his government focuses on "sabka saath, sabka vikas (together with all, development for all)," some parties prioritize "parivar ka saath, parivar ka vikas (work for gains and well-being of one family)."
