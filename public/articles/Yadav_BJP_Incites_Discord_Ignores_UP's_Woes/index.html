Yadav: BJP Incites Discord, Ignores UP's Woes

2025-03-19T05:46:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav accused the BJP of endangering religious sites and creating communal tensions to divert attention from pressing issues. He claimed the BJP opposes temple construction by others and objects to religious events promoting unity like the Neja Mela. Yadav urged the BJP to learn from historical figures like Swami Vivekananda and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who promoted harmony and inclusivity. He criticized the BJP government's handling of the Kumbh Mela, alleging mismanagement and a lack of transparency. Yadav also criticized the media for favoring the ruling party and neglecting issues like investment and pollution in Uttar Pradesh.
