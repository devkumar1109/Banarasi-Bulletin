Ayushman Bharat: Lifeline for Uttar Pradesh Family

2025-03-09T20:09:00+00:00

Ram Prakash, a resident of Uttar Pradesh, is receiving free bone treatment at Moti Lal Nehru Divisional Hospital in Prayagraj under the Ayushman Bharat scheme. His son, Jai Narayan, expressed gratitude to Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the scheme, stating it has been a lifeline for their family. Ayushman Bharat, launched in 2017, is the world's largest government-financed health insurance scheme, providing coverage of Rs 5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization in public and private hospitals nationwide. Ram Prakash's case highlights the scheme's impact on providing affordable healthcare to the underprivileged.
