Japanese Dribbler Kicks Record-Breaking Journey Across India

2025-04-17T15:22:00+00:00

Japanese national Nozomu Hagihara is dribbling a football across India, aiming to set a Guinness World Record for the longest distance dribbled. Hagihara began his 2,000-kilometer journey in Kolkata on March 3rd and is traveling to the Japanese Embassy in New Delhi. He expects to reach his destination by May 15th. Hagihara, who has lived in India for four years, sees the journey as a way to connect with the country and its people.
