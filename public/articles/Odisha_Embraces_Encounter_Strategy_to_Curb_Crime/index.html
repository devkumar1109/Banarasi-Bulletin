Odisha Embraces "Encounter" Strategy to Curb Crime

2025-04-12T16:09:00+00:00

Odisha Police have conducted over 15 encounters in the past three months to combat crime. Their strategy, which involves direct confrontations with criminals when deemed necessary, mirrors tactics employed in Uttar Pradesh. A recent incident on the Jharsuguda-Belpahar road involved suspects Sheikh Ashish and Abhishek Barik. Police are taking a proactive approach to address various criminal activities, from petty theft to serious offenses, aiming to enhance public safety.
