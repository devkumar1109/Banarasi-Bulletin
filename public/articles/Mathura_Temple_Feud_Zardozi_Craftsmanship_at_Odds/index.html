Mathura Temple Feud: Zardozi Craftsmanship at Odds

2025-03-17T11:25:00+00:00

The Banke Bihari temple in Mathura, known for its intricate zardozi idol attire, faces controversy due to Hindutva politics. Hindu leader Dinesh Sharma Falahari questioned the involvement of Muslim artisans, claiming it's inappropriate. A group also demanded a ban, citing religious differences. However, the temple firmly rejected these demands, stating they don't discriminate against any community. Senior priest Gyanendra Kishore Goswami praised the skill and dedication of Muslim artisans, who have traditionally crafted the deity's clothes. He emphasized that devotees ensure purity before making offerings.
