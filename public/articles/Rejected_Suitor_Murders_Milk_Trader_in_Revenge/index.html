Rejected Suitor Murders Milk Trader in Revenge

2025-03-04T19:35:00+00:00

Milk trader Rajesh Yadav (50) was brutally murdered in Jaunpur district, Uttar Pradesh. The accused, Rahul Yadav (25), was arrested and confessed to the crime, stating that it was revenge for being rejected in his marriage proposal to Rajesh's daughter who had recently passed away. Tension gripped the village, prompting a heavy police deployment. Rajesh, a Samajwadi Party worker, was found dead near the village telephone exchange with his throat slit. The incident sparked outrage among villagers, who clashed with police. Senior officials assured swift action and investigation is underway.
