Exam Hall Assault: College Manager Arrested

2025-03-17T18:48:00+00:00

Janardan Yadav, the manager of an intermediate college in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested for raping a Class 10 girl during the UP Board examination. The incident occurred on March 1st when the girl went to the exam center at Yadav's college. He allegedly took her to a separate room, raped her, and threatened her with dire consequences if she disclosed the incident. The girl's family filed a complaint with the police, alleging that Yadav, who is the national vice-president of a political party's Shikshak Sabha, had also threatened them. Yadav was arrested from a brick kiln near Kharaipur and sent to jail.
