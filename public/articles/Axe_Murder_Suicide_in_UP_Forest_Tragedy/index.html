Axe Murder, Suicide in UP Forest Tragedy

2025-05-08T02:13:00+00:00

In Sonbhadra, Uttar Pradesh, Rajendra Gurjar, 38, allegedly murdered his wife Rita, 35, with an axe during an argument in the forest. He then hanged himself using her saree. The couple had gone to the forest to collect chironji nuts. Police received information about the incident around 4 pm on Wednesday, despite it occurring around noon. Authorities recovered the bodies and are investigating further. This incident follows a recent case in Shahjahanpur where a man shot his wife over suspicion of infidelity.
