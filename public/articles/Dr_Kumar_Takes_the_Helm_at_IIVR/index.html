Dr. Kumar Takes the Helm at IIVR

2025-04-07T07:07:00+00:00

Dr. Rajesh Kumar has been appointed as the new Director of the ICAR-Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR), Varanasi. The appointment was approved by the President of the ICAR Society and is effective for five years. Dr. Kumar brings over two decades of experience in vegetable research and seed science, with a focus on chillies, tomatoes, and garden peas. He began his career at IIVR in 1999 and has held increasingly senior positions, including Senior Scientist and Principal Scientist. Dr. Kumar's expertise in vegetable improvement and participatory seed production will be valuable to the IIVR's mission.
