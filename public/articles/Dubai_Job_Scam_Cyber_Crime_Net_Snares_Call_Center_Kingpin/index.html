Dubai Job Scam: Cyber Crime Net Snares Call Center Kingpin

2025-02-25T10:15:00+00:00

Hyderabad cybercrime officials arrested Dharmender Kumar alias Rohan (30) from Uttar Pradesh for job fraud. Kumar ran a call center in New Delhi and cheated job seekers with promises of jobs in Dubai. He is involved in five cases across India, including one in Hyderabad where he defrauded a 49-year-old man of Rs 15.8 lakh. Kumar's operation involved posting fake job advertisements on job portals and social media. He used professional language and conducted fake interviews to build trust with victims, then tricked them into revealing personal and financial information.
