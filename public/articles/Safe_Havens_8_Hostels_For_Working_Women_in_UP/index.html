Safe Havens: 8 Hostels For Working Women in UP

2025-04-16T19:07:00+00:00

Under the SASCI scheme, eight hostels for working women with a capacity of 500 each are approved in Uttar Pradesh. These hostels will be built in Lucknow, Gautam Budh Nagar, and Ghaziabad, aiming to provide safe and affordable housing. The Centre allocated Rs 381.56 crore, with the first installment of Rs 251.8296 crore already released. The state finance department transferred the funds to the women welfare department, and a construction agency has been appointed. Work is expected to begin soon.
