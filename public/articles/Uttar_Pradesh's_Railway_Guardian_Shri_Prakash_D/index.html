Uttar Pradesh's Railway Guardian: Shri Prakash D.

2025-05-14T06:01:00+00:00

IPS Shri Prakash D. serves as the Additional Director General of Police (Railways) in Uttar Pradesh, leading a force of 6,000 personnel responsible for security on the state's busy railway network. The GRP operates across 65 police stations and 43 outposts, covering 9,500 kilometers of track and 1,136 railway stations, escorting 780 trains daily. The GRP focuses on crime prevention, passenger safety, and maintaining law and order. Initiatives include Mahila Help Desks, police booths, public address systems for safety awareness, mobile outposts on trains, and special teams to prevent drugging and theft. During the 2025 Mahakumbh, the GRP successfully managed security and crowd control for over 400 million pilgrims, despite the influx of 13,000 trains.
