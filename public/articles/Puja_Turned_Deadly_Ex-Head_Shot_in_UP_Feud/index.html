Puja Turned Deadly: Ex-Head Shot in UP Feud

2025-06-07T19:32:00+00:00

A former village head, Ramesh Rai, was shot in the head during a puja in Mau, Uttar Pradesh, due to an old enmity. He was referred to Varanasi for treatment. The incident has led to demands for action from the Congress party. The police are investigating the matter.
