Pathak Slams UP Hospital, Demands Better Care

2025-04-17T18:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak conducted a surprise inspection at the Harnahee Community Health Centre, criticizing health officials for irregularities. He emphasized the need for full-time doctor availability and improving government hospitals to surpass private facilities. Pathak reprimanded the chief medical officer for restricted working hours and mandated periodic community meetings to build trust in public health services. He also warned against private practices by government physicians and stated plans for unexpected facility inspections.
