Varanasi Battles Diseases, Boasts Health Upgrades

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to combat communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, chikungunya, and diarrhea. The campaign includes anti-larvae spraying, fogging, and a Dastak campaign from April 10 to 31. 68 dengue hotspots were identified in urban areas and 56 in rural areas. The number of dengue, malaria, and chikungunya cases in 2025 has significantly decreased compared to previous years, attributed to the coordinated efforts of various departments. SVM Hospital Bhelupur introduced new facilities, including an ultrasound machine, dental department, and mental health OPD, to enhance medical services.
