UP Fuels Youth Entrepreneurship with ₹48 Cr Loan Scheme

2025-03-10T00:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced a ₹48 crore loan distribution program for 1,070 young entrepreneurs in Meerut. The "CM Young Entrepreneur Scheme," launched in January, has received over 2.67 lakh applications, surpassing its one-lakh target. The scheme offers interest-free loans of up to ₹5 lakh each, with the government covering the interest cost. It prioritizes SC, ST, and women entrepreneurs. Adityanath highlighted the state's economic progress, citing ₹15 lakh crore in investments, 7 lakh new jobs, and 7.5 lakh government jobs for youth. He also noted UP's top ranking in MSME units, ₹2.25 lakh crore in exports, and infrastructure development, including the extension of the Ganga Expressway to Haridwar.
