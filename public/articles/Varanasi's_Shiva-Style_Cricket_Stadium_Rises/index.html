Varanasi's Shiva-Style Cricket Stadium Rises

2025-04-25T03:46:00+00:00

Varanasi is constructing a new 30,000-seater cricket stadium, the Varanasi International Cricket Stadium, costing Rs 452 crore. Scheduled for completion in April 2026, the stadium will feature Shiva-inspired architecture, including a crescent-shaped canopy, trident-shaped floodlights, and seating inspired by Kashi's ghats. The stadium, built by L&T, is designed to be sustainable with solar panels, a water recycling system, and EV recharging facilities, making it a model for future cricket venues in India. It will have nine pitches, including red, black, and mixed wickets, and will house a cricket academy with dormitories for 100 trainees. The stadium is expected to host IPL matches as early as next season.
