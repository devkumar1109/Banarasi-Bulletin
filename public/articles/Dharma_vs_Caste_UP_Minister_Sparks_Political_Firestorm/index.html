"Dharma vs. Caste: UP Minister Sparks Political Firestorm"

2025-06-11T12:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister Mayankeshwar Sharan Singh accused the Samajwadi Party and Congress of dividing 'Sanatan Dharma and Hindutva' while promoting casteism and social disintegration. Singh praised PM Modi's leadership and India's progress in defense and economic self-reliance. The SP and Congress denied the allegations, accusing the BJP of fostering social division and inter-group hatred. Opposition leaders criticized BJP's political strategy as divisive and polarizing, emphasizing their commitment to inclusive politics.
