Varanasi Girl Scores Hockey History

2025-04-23T05:37:00+00:00

Pooja Yadav, a 21-year-old from Varanasi, has made history by becoming the first woman from the Purvanchal region to join the Indian women's hockey team. Yadav's selection comes after her impressive performance at the senior national camp in Bengaluru. She will be part of the 26-member squad touring Australia from April 26 to May 4. Yadav's father, a milkman, supported her hockey aspirations despite facing societal pressures. He even made a payment plan with a local shopkeeper to buy her first pair of hockey shoes. Yadav attributes her early exposure to cricket with boys for instilling her belief in her abilities.
