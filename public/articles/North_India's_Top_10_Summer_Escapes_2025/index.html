North India's Top 10 Summer Escapes 2025

2025-02-25T08:55:00+00:00

News18 English Edition recommends ten must-visit destinations in North India for a perfect summer getaway in 2025. The list includes: * **Manali & Shimla, Himachal Pradesh:** Known for snow-clad mountains, lush valleys, adventure sports, and beautiful views. * **Rishikesh & Nainital, Uttarakhand:** Rishikesh offers adventure and spirituality with river rafting, camping, and yoga, while Nainital is known for its lakes, peaceful environment, and boating. * **Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh:** A spiritual destination offering the experience of Ganga Aarti, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and ghats. * **Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir:** A destination for adventure seekers. * **Mussoorie, Amritsar, Jaipur, and Dharamshala-McLeodganj:** Other recommended destinations offering diverse experiences.
