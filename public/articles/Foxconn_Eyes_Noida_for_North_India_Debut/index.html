Foxconn Eyes Noida for North India Debut

2025-04-14T16:08:00+00:00

iPhone maker Foxconn is in talks with the Uttar Pradesh government to establish its first facility in North India in Noida. Discussions center around a potential 300-acre site under the Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority (YEIDA). While details on the manufacturing products are yet to be finalized, this move follows Foxconn's existing investment in the Vama Sundari project, a joint venture with HCL for an outsourced semiconductor Assembly and Test facility in YEIDA. This new facility, if realized, could significantly boost Uttar Pradesh's electronics manufacturing sector.
