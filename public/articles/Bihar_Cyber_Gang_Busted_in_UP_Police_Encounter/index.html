Bihar Cyber Gang Busted in UP Police Encounter

2025-03-20T19:44:00+00:00

Four cyber fraudsters from Bihar were apprehended in an encounter with Ballia police in Uttar Pradesh. The encounter took place around 2 am on Thursday in Haldi area. One of the accused, Baccha Lal, sustained a leg injury during the exchange of fire. Police recovered two country-made revolvers, live cartridges, 63 ATM cards, a car, and Rs 7,688 cash from the gang. They allegedly targeted innocent customers by changing their ATM cards under the pretense of helping them withdraw money. The gang operated between Ballia and Delhi, committing crimes in multiple districts of Uttar Pradesh and Delhi. Many cases were registered against the gang members in various locations.
