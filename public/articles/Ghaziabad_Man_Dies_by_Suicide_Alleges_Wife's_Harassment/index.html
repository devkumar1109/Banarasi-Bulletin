Ghaziabad Man Dies by Suicide, Alleges Wife's Harassment

2025-04-19T13:33:00+00:00

A 34-year-old man, Mohit Tyagi, from Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, died by suicide after consuming poison. He accused his wife Priyanka Tyagi and her relatives in a suicide note of harassing him. Mohit worked at a private firm and had a son with his wife. They married in 2020, and their marriage reportedly deteriorated within months. Mohit's brother, Rahul Tyagi, claims Mohit was emotionally distressed due to the harassment. The family filed a complaint against Priyanka, her brother, sister-in-law, and maternal uncles. Police are investigating the matter.
