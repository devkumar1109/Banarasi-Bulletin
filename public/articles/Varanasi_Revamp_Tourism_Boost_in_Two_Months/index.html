Varanasi Revamp: Tourism Boost in Two Months

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

The Indian Ministry of Tourism has given Varanasi two months to create visible improvements for tourists. Secretary V Vidyavathi emphasized sustainable and long-term changes, focusing on 16-20 key locations including temples, ghats, and Sarnath. Priorities include infrastructure development like ghat beautification, improved roads, utilities, and transportation. Promoting spiritual tourism through temple development, local crafts like Banarasi sarees, and cultural events are also key. Training for local artists, craftsmen, and guides is vital, as is involving the community in tourism businesses. Online promotion and attracting tourists to neighboring eco-tourism sites and historical locations are also part of the plan.
