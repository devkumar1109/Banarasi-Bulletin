Varanasi Revamp: Tourism Boost in Two Months

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

The Secretary of the Ministry of Tourism, V Vidyavathi, has set a two-month deadline for officials to implement visible changes in Varanasi to enhance its tourism appeal. Key areas of focus include beautification and cleaning of ghats, improved infrastructure like roads, electricity, and water supply, and enhanced transportation facilities. The government aims to promote spiritual tourism by developing major temples and religious sites, while also boosting local economy through the promotion of Banarasi sarees, wooden toys, and by training local artists, craftsmen, and guides. The plan also involves organizing cultural events and festivals, promoting Varanasi online, and encouraging the involvement of local people in tourism-related businesses.
