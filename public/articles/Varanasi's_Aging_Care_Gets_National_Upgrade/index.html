Varanasi's Aging Care Gets National Upgrade

2025-03-03T18:18:00+00:00

The Regional Geriatric Centre in Varanasi is being upgraded to a 200-bed National Centre of Aging (NCA) by December. Approved in February 2024 and inaugurated by Prime Minister Modi, the NCA, built by HLL Infra tech services ltd, is one-third complete. The NCA will offer specialized tertiary care, including an OPD with geriatric sub-specialities, diagnostic facilities, physiotherapy, and a private ward. It aims to increase postgraduate MD geriatric seats to 15 per year, train healthcare professionals, and work with primary and secondary care services to provide comprehensive elderly care.
