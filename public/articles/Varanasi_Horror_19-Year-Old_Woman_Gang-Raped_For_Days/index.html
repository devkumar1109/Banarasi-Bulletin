Varanasi Horror: 19-Year-Old Woman Gang-Raped For Days

2025-04-07T08:45:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi has alleged that she was gang-raped by 23 men over a seven-day period. She went missing on March 29 and returned home on April 4, reporting the incident to her family. Police have arrested six individuals and are actively pursuing the remaining suspects. The woman's detailed account states that she was taken to various locations, drugged, and assaulted repeatedly. Charges have been filed under various sections of the BNS, including gang rape, outraging modesty, administering poison or harmful substances, wrongful confinement, and criminal intimidation.
