BJP's Presidential Shuffle: Post-April 20

2025-04-16T18:36:00+00:00

The BJP will begin the election process for its national president after April 20, following a high-level meeting attended by PM Modi and top leaders. Key state president announcements are expected soon, including for Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, and West Bengal. JP Nadda, the current president, extended his term to June 2024 for the Lok Sabha elections. The election for a new national president was delayed but will commence post-April 20. State president elections are ongoing, with major states like those mentioned above yet to be filled. The BJP aims to announce new state presidents in the coming days, particularly for West Bengal ahead of next year's assembly elections.
