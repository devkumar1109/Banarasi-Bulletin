Bangladesh Turmoil Grounds Indian River Cruise

2025-05-21T18:43:00+00:00

Due to political instability in Bangladesh following the ousting of the Sheikh Hasina government, India has decided to defer the world's longest river cruise from Varanasi to Dibrugarh via Dhaka. Adverse travel advisories have reduced interest from foreign tourists, the cruise's primary target audience. The cruise, operating on the 3,200-km India-Bangladesh Protocol (IBP) route, has transported around 60 passengers since its launch in January 2023. While cargo movement on the IBP route continues, passenger services are suspended until elections and political stability are restored in Bangladesh.
