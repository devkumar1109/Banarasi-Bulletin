Maharashtra Dominates, Records Fall at Khelo India

2025-05-13T00:22:00+00:00

Sairaj Pardeshi of Maharashtra won the gold medal in the 81kg weightlifting category at the Khelo India Youth Games, breaking national records in snatch, clean and jerk, and total lift. Maharashtra leads the medal tally with 35 gold medals. In athletics, four meet records were broken: Jithin Arjunan set a new long jump record, Kadir Khan in the under-18 400m, Hansraj Dhayal in the boys' discus throw, and Saif Farooq Chafekar in the boys 110m hurdles. Kerala moved up to sixth place on the medal table after winning all three kalarippayattu gold medals on offer.
