Rajasthan Shoots to Gold in Khelo India Mixed Pistol

2025-05-05T11:37:00+00:00

Rajasthan won the first gold medal in the Khelo India Youth Games in the 10m air pistol mixed team final. Prachi and Mayank Choudhary from Rajasthan defeated Uttar Pradesh 15-15, securing the victory due to Prachi's shooting. Delhi won the bronze medal, defeating Haryana 16-14.
