Preity Zinta Quashes Political Rumors, Focuses on Family and Films

2025-04-30T10:56:00+00:00

Actress Preity Zinta clarified she has no plans to enter politics, despite visiting religious sites like the Maha Kumbh Mela and Kashi Vishwanath temple. Rumors of a potential BJP affiliation arose after these visits. Zinta, known for films like "Lakshya" and "Dil Chahta Hai," explained that her pride in her Indian heritage doesn't equate to political aspirations. She currently resides in Los Angeles with her husband and twins, emphasizing her desire for her children to maintain their Indian identity. Zinta will return to acting after a seven-year hiatus in Rajkumar Santoshi's film "Lahore 1947".
