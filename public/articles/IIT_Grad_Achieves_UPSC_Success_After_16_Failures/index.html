IIT Grad Achieves UPSC Success After 16 Failures

2025-05-06T13:29:00+00:00

Abhinandan Yadav, an IIT Guwahati graduate, left his private job in Gurugram to pursue a career in government service. Despite facing 16 failures in previous government exams, he persevered and cleared the UPSC CAPF exam in 2024 on his first attempt. He is now an Assistant Commandant in the Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF). Yadav hails from Khojapur village in Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh, and always aspired to serve his country. He dedicated himself to studying for the exams while managing his job and household responsibilities.
