Uttar Pradesh: Old-Age Homes Now Open to Transgender Elders

2025-04-30T13:51:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has expanded old-age home facilities to include transgender individuals aged 60 and above. This initiative aims to provide shelter, dignity, and a secure life for elderly members of the transgender community. The government stated in a press release that these individuals will receive comprehensive care and support within the existing old-age home infrastructure.
