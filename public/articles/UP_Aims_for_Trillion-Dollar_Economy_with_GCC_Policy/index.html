UP Aims for Trillion-Dollar Economy with GCC Policy

2025-05-07T06:18:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has approved the Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy to attract global investment and establish the state as a "global service centre". The policy aims to help UP achieve its $1 trillion economy goal by incentivizing companies in IT, banking, healthcare, engineering, and next-generation technologies to invest in the state. UP boasts a large talent pool in various sectors, making it an attractive destination for multinational companies seeking cost-effective and high-quality work. The policy is expected to attract more GCCs, similar to Microsoft's recent 10,000-seater development center in Noida, to the state.
