Uttar Pradesh: Two Rape Cases, One Arrest, One Shootout

2025-06-04T09:38:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man was arrested in Banda, Uttar Pradesh, after allegedly raping a three-year-old girl. Police rescued the injured child from a forest and apprehended the suspect following a brief shootout. The accused sustained a gunshot wound to his leg and is receiving treatment. In a separate incident, Zuber, a madrassa operator in Bareilly, was arrested for raping a woman teacher at the institution. Zuber allegedly lured the teacher to the madrassa on a holiday and assaulted her. The teacher's family filed a complaint after finding her unconscious.
