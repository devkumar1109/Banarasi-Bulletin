Bail Broken: Jailbird's Procession Sparks Rearrest

2025-06-08T14:36:00+00:00

Abid Khan, a Varanasi jail inmate, was rearrested after being released on bail. Khan was originally jailed on May 29 for loot and criminal intimidation. He was granted bail on June 6, but was subsequently detained after a video surfaced of him leading a procession with supporters chanting objectionable slogans. Police arrested Khan and two others involved, and suspended a local officer for negligence. Authorities are seeking further arrests and to revoke Khan's bail.
