Kanpur Gets ₹700 Crore EV Manufacturing Hub

2025-06-17T02:36:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will invest ₹700 crore to establish an electric vehicle (EV) manufacturing hub in Kanpur. The 500-acre EV park, located near Bhimsen along the Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC), will be developed under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. The park will manufacture electric motors, chassis, steel components, and lithium-ion cells, along with facilities for assembling batteries, chargers, and controllers. A dedicated research and development (R&D) center will focus on innovation and advanced EV technologies. The project aims to create employment opportunities, boost local entrepreneurship, and contribute to Uttar Pradesh's economic growth.
