Varanasi Gets Multi-Modal Logistics Boost

2025-03-12T12:29:00+00:00

The National Highways Logistics Management Limited (NHLML) and the Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to develop a state-of-the-art Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP) in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. The MMLP, spanning over 150 acres, will be strategically located near National Highway 7 and connected to the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor and National Waterway-1. This multi-modal connectivity, along with proximity to Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport, aims to boost India's logistics ecosystem, enhance cargo movement, and stimulate economic activity. The project is expected to generate significant investment and job opportunities, aligning with India's vision of simplifying logistics, reducing freight costs, and improving trade competitiveness.
