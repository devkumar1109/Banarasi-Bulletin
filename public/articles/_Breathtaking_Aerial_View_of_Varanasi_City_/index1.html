"Varanasi Takes Flight: Breathtaking Aerial Video Soars to Viral Heights"

2025-02-23T12:42:00+00:00

Here is a summary of the news article in 150 words: A stunning aerial video of Varanasi has gone viral on social media, showcasing the city's spiritual landmarks and divine energy. The video, shared by photographer Anvesh Patel on Instagram, features breathtaking drone footage of Varanasi, presented through a voiceover mimicking a flight captain's narration. It captures the splendor of the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, and provides a mesmerizing view of the Manikarnika Ghat, where life and death converge. The aerial shots of the Ganga Aarti bring the divine energy of Varanasi alive, showing the rhythmic chanting and devotional aura. The video has garnered over 850,000 views and struck a chord with viewers, who commented on the emotional impact of Varanasi's eternal spirit. Known for its ancient temples, ghats, and cultural roots, Varanasi is revered as one of India's holiest cities, captivating the hearts of millions worldwide.
