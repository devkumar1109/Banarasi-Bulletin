Jealousy Turns Deadly in UP: Man Kills Wife, Buries Body

2025-04-12T06:43:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man murdered his wife and buried her body in a field. The arrest came after the man's brother informed the authorities. Investigations revealed the man was having an affair with a differently-abled woman and frequently chatted with her on the phone. His wife strongly disapproved of the affair, leading to a heated argument on the night of the incident, culminating in the fatal assault.
