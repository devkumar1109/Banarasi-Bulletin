Kanpur School Lintel Collapse, Workers Trapped

2025-06-16T16:54:00+00:00

An under-construction school lintel collapsed in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh on Monday evening, trapping several workers. Rescue operations are underway, involving the local police, fire department, and other concerned departments. The collapse occurred while workers were present at the site. The exact cause is yet to be determined, but a possible violation of construction norms is suspected. Emergency teams, including police and administration personnel, are actively involved in the rescue efforts.
