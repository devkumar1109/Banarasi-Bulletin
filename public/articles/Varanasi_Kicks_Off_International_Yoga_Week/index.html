Varanasi Kicks Off International Yoga Week

2025-06-16T00:06:00+00:00

International Yoga Week began in Varanasi on June 16th, leading up to International Yoga Day on June 21st. The inaugural event took place at Namo Ghat, attended by dignitaries and led by yoga instructors. Simultaneous yoga sessions were held across the district at ghats, parks, educational institutions, jails, and village panchayats. The Government Ayurvedic College and Hospital also hosted a special yoga session with mass participation. Throughout the week, various events will be organized, including group yoga practices, seminars, competitions, and awareness rallies.
