Counterfeit Cash Caper: Brahmanpura Man Arrested

2025-05-09T15:14:00+00:00

Sanjay Verma of Brahmanpura village was arrested in Ghazipur's Karanda area for possessing counterfeit currency worth Rs 15,500. Police recovered 31 fake Rs 500 notes, a motorcycle, and a mobile phone from him. A case has been registered against Verma under sections 179 and 180 of the BNS and further legal proceedings are underway. The arrest was made as part of a campaign against crime and criminals.
