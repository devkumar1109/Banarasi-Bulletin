Varanasi Welcomes International Yoga Week

2025-06-16T00:06:00+00:00

International Yoga Week commenced in Varanasi with a mass session at Namo Ghat on June 16th, in preparation for International Yoga Day on June 21st. The event was attended by dignitaries including MLA Neelkanth Tiwari and District Magistrate Satyendra Kumar. Yoga sessions were held at various locations across the district, including educational institutions, parks, ghats, jails, and villages. The week-long celebrations are organized by the AYUSH department and various yoga institutions, featuring events like group yoga practices, seminars, slogan competitions, awareness rallies, and street plays. At the Government Ayurvedic College and Hospital, Principal Prof Neelam Gupta emphasized the holistic benefits of yoga and its integration with Ayurveda.
