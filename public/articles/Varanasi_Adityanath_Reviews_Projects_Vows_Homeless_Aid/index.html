Varanasi: Adityanath Reviews Projects, Vows Homeless Aid

2025-05-13T12:45:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed development projects in Varanasi, focusing on transportation, tourism, and construction management. He met with representatives from Apna Ghar Ashram, which houses the homeless. The ashram, designed for 400 residents, currently shelters 600 due to a lack of space, forcing some to sleep outdoors. Director K Niranjan requested government land or buildings to accommodate the increasing number of homeless individuals. Adityanath assured support and suggested expanding ashram facilities to other districts.
