Noida "Honor Killing": Blue Dupatta, Deadly Family Dispute

2025-03-23T14:06:00+00:00

A woman in Noida was murdered in an alleged "honour killing" after her family disapproved of her relationship. She was strangled by her brother, allegedly after she wore a blue dupatta to a wedding. The police have arrested the brother and are investigating the case.
