Ex-Lover Kills Woman, Hurts Son in Mirzapur

2025-03-02T17:59:00+00:00

A 45-year-old man, Rajendra Bind, was arrested for the killing of his former girlfriend, Malti, and the attempted murder of her physically challenged son in Mirzapur district. Rajendra confessed to killing Malti after she allegedly threatened and demanded money from him. He killed her and dumped her body in a field. He also threw Malti's son, Raunak, off a bridge. Malti's body was found on Saturday, leading to the filing of an FIR and the subsequent arrest of Rajendra.
