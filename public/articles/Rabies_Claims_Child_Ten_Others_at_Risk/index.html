Rabies Claims Child, Ten Others at Risk

2025-04-13T12:39:00+00:00

A three-year-old boy in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, died suspected of rabies after being bitten by a rabid dog over a month ago. Ten other children, also bitten by the same dog, did not receive medical treatment. While the cause of death cannot be definitively confirmed without testing, the district health authorities are treating it as a rabies case and have launched a preventive campaign. This includes distributing anti-rabies vaccines and urging the public to seek immediate medical attention after any animal bite. The incident highlights a lack of awareness and access to post-bite care in rural areas.
