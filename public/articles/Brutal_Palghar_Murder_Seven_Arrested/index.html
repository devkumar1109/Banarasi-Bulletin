Brutal Palghar Murder: Seven Arrested

2025-05-21T23:30:00+00:00

Seven individuals, including a minor, have been arrested for the brutal murder of 36-year-old Abhishek Ram Singh in Palghar, Maharashtra. The victim was found dead on May 16 with severe head injuries inflicted by a sharp weapon. The incident occurred between May 15 and May 16 at Ambat Goad Ground in Pasthal. The accused confessed to gathering for a party and encountering the victim sleeping at the ground. They allegedly verbally abused, assaulted him, and fatally struck his head with an iron rod. The Palghar Police Local Crime Branch (LCB) swiftly investigated the case, detaining the suspects and recovering evidence. The motive behind the attack is still under investigation, but police are exploring the possibility of past enmity.
