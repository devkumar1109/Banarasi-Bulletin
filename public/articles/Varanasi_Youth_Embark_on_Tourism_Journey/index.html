Varanasi Youth Embark on Tourism Journey

2025-05-03T05:19:00+00:00

The Ministry of Tourism's Varanasi office inaugurated a Youth Tourism Club (YTC) at Seerin Soni Public School. The objective is to enhance students' understanding of tourism, heritage, and culture. Assistant Director Pavas Prasoon emphasized the importance of India's cultural heritage and assured the YTC of the ministry's full support in promoting travel and tourism awareness. The inauguration ceremony included cultural performances by students and the assignment of various responsibilities to the YTC team.
