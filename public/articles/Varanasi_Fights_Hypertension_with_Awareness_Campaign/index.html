Varanasi Fights Hypertension with Awareness Campaign

2025-05-17T23:31:00+00:00

To mark World Hypertension Day, the health department in Varanasi launched a month-long public awareness campaign and screening activities. The campaign aims to promote hypertension prevention, early detection, and treatment, with the theme "measure your blood pressure accurately, control it, live longer." Awareness programs and screening camps will be held at various locations, including district hospitals, CHCs, PHCs, bus stands, railway stations, and urban slums. Dr. YB Pathak, the deputy CMO, emphasized the importance of regular blood pressure checks for individuals over 30, stating that testing is available at all CHCs. He highlighted that hypertension can lead to serious health issues like heart attack, stroke, and kidney disease. Preventive measures include maintaining a balanced diet, regular exercise, avoiding smoking and alcohol, and managing stress.
