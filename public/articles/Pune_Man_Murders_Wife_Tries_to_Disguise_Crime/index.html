Pune Man Murders Wife, Tries to Disguise Crime

2025-05-06T08:33:00+00:00

A 28-year-old man from Pune's Dhayari area allegedly strangled his 26-year-old wife, Babita Nisar, following a domestic dispute. The incident occurred around midnight while their eight-year-old son was present. Rakesh Nisar attempted to dispose of the body by placing it in a gunny bag and transporting it on his bike. Police on night patrol apprehended him at 1:30 am near Bhumkar Bridge. He was found carrying the body in a gunny bag and was subsequently arrested. A case under Section 103 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) has been registered against Rakesh at Nanded City Police Station. An investigation is underway to determine the motive behind the murder.
