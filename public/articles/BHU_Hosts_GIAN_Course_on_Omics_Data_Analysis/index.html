BHU Hosts GIAN Course on Omics Data Analysis

2025-06-10T19:20:00+00:00

The School of Biotechnology at BHU hosted a week-long Global Initiative of Academic Networks (GIAN) course on "Models and Algorithms For Omics Data Analysis" from June 2 to 6. The course, organized under the Ministry of Education's GIAN initiative, aimed to enhance India's scientific capabilities. Prof. Rajeev Kumar Azad from the University of North Texas, USA, delivered expert insights during the course. The GIAN initiative seeks to strengthen academic resources, improve quality, and elevate India's scientific and technological standing globally.
