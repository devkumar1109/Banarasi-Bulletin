BHU Trauma Centre Embraces Digital Healthcare Revolution

2025-05-01T06:06:00+00:00

The Trauma Centre of BHU's Institute of Medical Sciences (IMS) launched digital initiatives focused on patient-centric healthcare. Key developments include the implementation of PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System) to replace film-based diagnostics, mobile PACS integration, and an app-based grievance redressal mechanism called 'e-X-Ray'. Other initiatives include empanelment and integration with the National Digital Health Infrastructure, a dedicated Trauma Centre website, and a seamless e-payment gateway. The launch ceremony was attended by prominent figures including Mayor Ashok Tiwari, BHU ex-officiating VC and rector professor VK Shukla, and BHU VC professor Sanjay Kumar.
