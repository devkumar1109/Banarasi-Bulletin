Trainside Delivery: YouTuber Praises India's Efficiency

2025-04-12T05:05:00+00:00

British YouTuber George Buckley was amazed by the efficiency of food delivery to his train seat in India while traveling from Delhi to Varanasi. During a five-minute stop at Kanpur Central station, Buckley, with the help of a fellow passenger, ordered a sandwich and Oreo milkshake through Zomato and received it promptly. Impressed by the service, Buckley lauded the experience in his video, suggesting the UK learn from India's model. The video, which garnered over 800,000 views, sparked discussions about the convenience and efficiency of such services. This incident follows a similar surprise expressed by German YouTuber Alex Welder, who was impressed by the modern metro systems in Delhi and Agra.
