Indian Idol Winner Pawandeep Rajan in ICU After Accident

2025-05-06T10:06:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan is in ICU after a 6-hour surgery following a car accident in Uttar Pradesh on May 5th. Rajan suffered multiple fractures and a severe head injury. He is reported to be stable and conscious. Initially treated in a nearby hospital, he was later shifted to a Noida hospital. Rajan underwent surgery to address major fractures, and is expected to undergo further surgeries after a period of rest. His team expressed gratitude for the support and prayers from fans.
