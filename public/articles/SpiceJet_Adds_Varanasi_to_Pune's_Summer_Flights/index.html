SpiceJet Adds Varanasi to Pune's Summer Flights

2025-03-30T18:45:00+00:00

SpiceJet launched a Pune-Varanasi flight on March 30, marking the addition of a new domestic destination to the Pune airport's summer schedule. The flight operates six days a week, except Saturdays, at 12:30 am from Pune. Despite 104 out of 109 available slots being occupied by airlines, no new international flights were included in the schedule. Pune airport director Santosh Dhoke highlighted that the airport continues to strengthen its domestic network with direct connections to 34 destinations across India, including Singapore, Dubai, and Bangkok. The summer schedule saw a 5.5% increase in weekly flights nationwide compared to last year, with Indian carriers operating 25,610 weekly flights from 129 airports.
