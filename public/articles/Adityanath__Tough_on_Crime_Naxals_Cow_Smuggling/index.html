Adityanath: Tough on Crime, Naxals, Cow Smuggling

2025-05-12T16:13:00+00:00

UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed heightened vigilance against "urban Naxals" and affiliated organizations following the success of Operation Sindoor in Varanasi. He emphasized strict and swift action against them. Adityanath also instructed officials to ensure timely issuance of birth and death certificates and crack down on crime, including looting and chain snatching. The CM directed strict action against cow smuggling and illegal mining in border districts, and ordered the auction of confiscated vehicles used by cow smugglers. He also urged speeding up of various infrastructure projects, including the rejuvenation of the Varuna River. Adityanath stressed better functioning of the municipal corporation and development authority, focusing on improved drinking water supply and sanitation.
