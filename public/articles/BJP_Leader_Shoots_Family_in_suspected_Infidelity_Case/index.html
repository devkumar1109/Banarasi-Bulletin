BJP Leader Shoots Family in suspected Infidelity Case

2025-03-22T12:11:00+00:00

BJP leader Yogesh Rohilla allegedly shot his wife and three children in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh. His son and 11-year-old daughter died, while his wife and another child are critically injured. Rohilla, who suspected his wife of infidelity, was apprehended at the scene and the weapon used has been recovered. Saharanpur SSP Rohit Sajwan confirmed the incident, stating that Rohilla was under mental stress. The incident took place in Sangatheda village, causing panic among residents. Police have launched an investigation and are conducting a detailed inquiry.
