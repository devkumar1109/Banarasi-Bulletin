Holi's Harmony Under Siege: Unity Lost in Discord

2025-03-16T23:30:00+00:00

Communal tensions are increasingly marring Holi celebrations in India, with a rise in riots reported in recent weeks. The article highlights concerns that the festival, traditionally a symbol of unity and universal kinship, is losing its essence. The author recalls past Holi celebrations in Varanasi and Allahabad, where peace committee meetings involving district officers and religious leaders were held to ensure harmony. This contrasts with the current situation, where Holi is being overshadowed by rising communal discord, threatening India's social fabric. The article emphasizes the urgent need for peace and harmony to prevent Holi from becoming a relic of the past.
