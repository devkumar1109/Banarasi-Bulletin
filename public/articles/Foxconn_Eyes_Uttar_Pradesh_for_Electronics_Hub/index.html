Foxconn Eyes Uttar Pradesh for Electronics Hub

2025-04-14T10:38:00+00:00

Foxconn is in talks with the Uttar Pradesh government to establish an electronics manufacturing unit in the state. The discussions were initiated during the land allocation for Foxconn's HCL joint venture, Vama Sundari, which will focus on outsourced semiconductor assembly and testing. While details about the type of products Foxconn plans to manufacture are yet to be finalized, the state government highlighted the availability of 300 acres of land under the Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority (YEIDA). This potential investment comes amidst India's burgeoning mobile phone manufacturing sector, with exports exceeding Rs 2 lakh crore in FY 2024-25. Uttar Pradesh already houses production units for major mobile phone brands and accounts for over 80% of the country's smartphone market share.
