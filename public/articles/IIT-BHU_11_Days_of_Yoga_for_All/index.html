IIT-BHU: 11 Days of Yoga for All

2025-06-03T19:52:00+00:00

IIT-BHU will host an 11-day yoga camp from June 11 to June 21 to celebrate the 11th International Day of Yoga. The camp will feature daily yoga sessions from 6am to 7am at the Gymkhana Ground. Participants who attend all sessions will receive a certificate of participation. Free registration is open until June 10th at the Gymkhana Office, open from 10am to 5pm on weekdays. IIT-BHU encourages faculty, staff, students, and residents to participate.
