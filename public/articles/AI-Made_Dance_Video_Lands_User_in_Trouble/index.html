AI-Made Dance Video Lands User in Trouble

2025-03-30T06:18:00+00:00

An Instagram user, identified as Priyanshu Rocks, has been booked by Meerut police for uploading an AI-generated video of a police inspector dancing with Muskan Rastogi, a woman accused of murdering her husband. The video, shared widely on social media, was deemed to be malicious and potentially damaging to the reputation of the UP Police. The police traced the Instagram ID and filed a case under the Information Technology Act Section 66. The cyber cell is investigating the case. Muskan Rastogi and her boyfriend Sahil Shukla are currently in jail for the murder of Saurabh Rajput, whom they allegedly killed and dismembered in March.
