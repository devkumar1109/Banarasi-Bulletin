U.P. Invests Big in 57 Smart City Transformations

2025-04-28T11:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will invest ₹40,000 crore to transform 57 municipalities into smart cities. ₹145 crore has already been sanctioned. The project aims to improve "ease of living" by implementing digital systems for administrative functions, including CCTV surveillance, AI-driven civic service management, chatbot-based citizen interaction, and integrated cowshed management systems. The smart cities will feature state-of-the-art infrastructure like CC roads, digital libraries, exhibition centres, and auditoriums. They will also incorporate measures to control pollution, ensure uninterrupted power and water supply, and provide smart parking and traffic management. This initiative seeks to create inclusive and sustainable growth, boosting local economies through increased investment, tourism, job creation, and environmental protection.
