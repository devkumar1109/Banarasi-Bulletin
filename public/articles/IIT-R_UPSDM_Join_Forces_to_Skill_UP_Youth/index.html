IIT-R, UPSDM Join Forces to Skill UP Youth

2025-05-01T11:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Skill Development Mission (UPSDM) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with IIT-R to provide technical training and opportunities to UP youth. Under this collaboration, youth will access advanced technical training, innovation-driven learning, research opportunities, and startup support guided by IIT-R experts. This partnership aims to enhance the employability of UP youth and equip them for national and international competitiveness. The MoU was signed by UPSDM's Additional Mission Director Priya Singh and representatives from IIT Roorkee.
