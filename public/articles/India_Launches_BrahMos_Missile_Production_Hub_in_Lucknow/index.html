India Launches BrahMos Missile Production Hub in Lucknow

2025-05-09T02:33:00+00:00

India's Defence Minister Rajnath Singh will inaugurate a BrahMos missile manufacturing unit in Lucknow on May 11. The unit, a joint venture between DRDO and Russia's NPO Mashinostroyeniya, aims to produce 80-100 missiles annually. Worth Rs 300 crore, the unit is part of Uttar Pradesh's Defence Industrial Corridor, announced in 2018. The corridor spans six nodes, with Lucknow being a key hub. Approximately 80 acres are dedicated to the BrahMos unit, while 117 hectares have been allotted to 12 companies in the Lucknow node. Aligarh is set to launch the second phase of land allocation for the corridor.
