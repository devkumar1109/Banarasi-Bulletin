Train Tragedy: Girl Raped After Leap From Moving Train

2025-03-29T04:11:00+00:00

A 15-year-old girl from Etah, Uttar Pradesh, was raped near Bareilly railway station after jumping off a moving train. She had mistakenly boarded the wrong compartment and realized her family had disembarked. Panicked, she jumped off the train at the behest of an unidentified man who then attacked her. The accused is suspected to be in his 50s and remains unidentified. The girl is in critical condition and underwent surgery. Police have registered an FIR under BNS sections 65-1 (rape), 352-2 (criminal intimidation) and Pocso Act. The incident occurred on Thursday evening at Bareilly railway station.
