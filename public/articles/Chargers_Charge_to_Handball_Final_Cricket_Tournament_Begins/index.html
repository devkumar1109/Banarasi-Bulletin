Chargers Charge to Handball Final, Cricket Tournament Begins

2025-03-01T20:11:00+00:00

In Lucknow's Premier Handball League, Speed Chargers defeated PrimeHealth City 4-1, setting up a title defense against Legends in the final on Sunday. Rakesh Seth and Rajesh Narain led the Chargers' victory. Ramswaroop Tigerz will face PrimeHealth City for the third-place finish. In the Senior Open State-Level Men's Handball Championship, Lucknow defeated Amethi 19-12, with Shubham scoring seven goals. Other matches saw Amethi beat Basti 24-16, Gorakhpur defeat Prayagraj 19-14, and Varanasi beat Muzaffarnagar 17-11. Mumtaz PG College won the inaugural match of the 52nd Sundari Devi Memorial Cricket Tournament, defeating DAV College by 50 runs.
