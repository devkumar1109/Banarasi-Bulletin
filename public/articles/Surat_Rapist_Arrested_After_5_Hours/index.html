Surat Rapist Arrested After 5 Hours

2025-03-15T23:56:00+00:00

In Surat, a 25-year-old man named Ajay Verma was arrested within five hours of kidnapping and raping a six-year-old girl. The crime occurred in the Katargam area where the victim and her mother were sleeping on a handcart. Verma kidnapped the girl while she slept and took her to Katargam sports complex, where he raped her. He then returned her to the spot where her mother was sleeping. The incident came to light when the victim's mother found blood on her daughter's body. The girl is currently in ICU after undergoing surgery.
