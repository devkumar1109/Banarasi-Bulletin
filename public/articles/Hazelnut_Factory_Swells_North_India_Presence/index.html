Hazelnut Factory Swells North India Presence

2025-04-28T04:52:00+00:00

The Hazelnut Factory, a cafe and artisanal sweet chain, is investing Rs 20 crore to open 12 new outlets in North India this fiscal year. They aim to reach Rs 100 crore in revenue this year, up from Rs 55 crore last year. The brand is focusing on cities like Varanasi, Noida, Bareilly, Agra, Meerut, and Muradabad. They currently operate 11 company-owned and -operated outlets, with one franchise-operated location. The Hazelnut Factory plans to open a new facility in Noida to cater to the expanding network. Revenue is currently divided between coffee/cafe (20-25%), bakery (30-35%), artisanal sweets (25-30%), and savory items. The brand aims to enter the packaged sweets market and increase online sales, which currently account for 25% of revenue.
