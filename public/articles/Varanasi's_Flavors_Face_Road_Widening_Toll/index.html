Varanasi's Flavors Face Road Widening Toll

2025-06-18T16:11:00+00:00

Two iconic Varanasi eateries, Pahalwan Ki Lassi and Chachi Ki Kachori, were demolished as part of a road-widening project. The demolition sparked criticism from opposition parties and local residents who view it as a cultural loss. The eateries were popular and held sentimental value for many, including alumni of Banaras Hindu University. The local administration defends the move, citing public convenience and promising compensation for affected shopkeepers. Varanasi Mayor Ashok Tiwari assures that the essence of Varanasi's flavors will persist in a new location.
