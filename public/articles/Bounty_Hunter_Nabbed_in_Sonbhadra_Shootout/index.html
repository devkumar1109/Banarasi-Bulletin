Bounty Hunter Nabbed in Sonbhadra Shootout

2025-02-25T18:11:00+00:00

A criminal carrying a Rs 25,000 bounty was arrested in an encounter in Sonbhadra district. Identified as Vishal alias Algu Yadav, the criminal was involved in several loot cases. Police received a tip-off and, during a checking drive, attempted to stop Yadav who was riding a motorcycle. Yadav fired at the police, who returned fire in self-defense, injuring him in the leg. A country-made revolver and motorcycle were recovered from him. Yadav was admitted to a district hospital.
