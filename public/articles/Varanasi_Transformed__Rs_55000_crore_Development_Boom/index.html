Varanasi Transformed: Rs 55,000 crore Development Boom

2025-03-25T10:02:00+00:00

In the last eight years, Varanasi has seen development projects worth Rs 40,000 crore completed and Rs 15,000 crore worth of projects currently underway. 458 projects across sectors like roads, bridges, healthcare, education, and tourism have been finished. Road infrastructure received Rs 22,408 crore investment, with 102 projects completed, including the Ring Road and various national and state highways. Tourism projects worth Rs 721 crore were also undertaken, including Namo Ghat and the Shri Kashi Vishwanath Corridor. Varanasi's GDP has nearly doubled from Rs 22,586.91 crore in 2017 to Rs 43,899.75 crore today, with per capita income rising to Rs 90,028. These developments are attributed to the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath.
