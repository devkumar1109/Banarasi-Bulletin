VOGIC AI Secures Grant for Varanasi Safety Solution

2025-03-12T04:42:00+00:00

VOGIC AI, an Indian startup, received a $50,000 grant from the Toyota Mobility Foundation to develop a crowd management and public safety solution for Varanasi's old city. The startup was chosen from over 110 global innovators in a challenge focused on improving safety and accessibility in densely populated urban areas. VOGIC AI will work with Varanasi Smart City Administration, law enforcement, the World Resources Institute, and city planners to adapt its Vision-Language AI technology for real-world use in Varanasi, a major cultural and religious center.
