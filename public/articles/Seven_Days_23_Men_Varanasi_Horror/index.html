Seven Days, 23 Men: Varanasi Horror

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. The victim, a Class 12 student, went missing on March 29 after visiting a hookah bar with a friend. She returned home on April 4 and reported the incident to her family, who filed a police complaint. According to police, the girl was drugged at the hookah bar and taken to different hotels where she was sexually assaulted. Some of the accused were her former classmates and individuals she had connected with on Instagram. Six of the 23 accused have been arrested, while the remaining 17 are still at large.
