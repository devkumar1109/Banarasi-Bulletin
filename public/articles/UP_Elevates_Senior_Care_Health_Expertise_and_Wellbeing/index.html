UP Elevates Senior Care: Health, Expertise, and Wellbeing

2025-04-04T01:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is enhancing facilities for senior citizens in old age homes. They plan to improve healthcare through the Ayushman Bharat scheme. A new program will utilize senior citizens' professional expertise, offering financial incentives to promote self-reliance. Currently, state-run homes provide free accommodation, meals, medical care, and entertainment. Over 1,500 seniors attended the Maha Kumbh with government support. To address grievances, 216 reconciliation officers have been appointed. District Magistrate-led committees oversee old age home management and service improvements.
