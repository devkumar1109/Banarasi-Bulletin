Modi Slams Opposition, Hails UP's Growth

2025-04-11T18:12:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi criticized opposition parties, particularly the Congress and Samajwadi Party, for prioritizing family interests over national welfare during a rally in Varanasi. He inaugurated projects worth over ₹3,880 crore, emphasizing his government's commitment to inclusive growth. Modi highlighted Uttar Pradesh's transformation, noting its leadership in GI-tagged products and improved healthcare facilities. He said UP is becoming a hub of competence and accomplishments, with several products gaining global recognition and GI tags. Modi contrasted his government's "sabka saath, sabka vikas" approach with the opposition's "parivar ka saath, parivar ka vikas" philosophy.
