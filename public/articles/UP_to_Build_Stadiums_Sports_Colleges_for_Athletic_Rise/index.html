UP to Build Stadiums, Sports Colleges for Athletic Rise

2025-05-23T11:57:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced plans to construct one mini-stadium in each assembly constituency and one sports college in each mandal. He emphasized timely completion and high-quality construction of all sports infrastructure projects. Currently, three divisions have operational sports colleges, and construction is underway in three others. CM Yogi directed officials to accelerate the establishment of colleges in the remaining 12 divisions. The CM proposed integrating all sports competitions with MP-MLA level tournaments and linking them with the State Sports University to provide international-level facilities. He stressed a transparent selection process for athletes and called for organizing competitions at various levels to identify and nurture talent.
