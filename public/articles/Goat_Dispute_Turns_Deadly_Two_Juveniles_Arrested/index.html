Goat Dispute Turns Deadly, Two Juveniles Arrested

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

In Ballia district, Uttar Pradesh, a 30-year-old man, Awadhesh Kumar, was beaten to death by two juvenile boys from his neighboring family. The incident stemmed from a dispute over Kumar's goat eating food grains near the neighbor's doorstep. After a quarrel, the boys attacked Kumar in a field with sticks, leaving him unconscious. He was taken to the district hospital but was declared brought dead. Police have recovered the sticks used in the attack and arrested the juvenile accused. A named FIR has been lodged, and the body sent for postmortem.
