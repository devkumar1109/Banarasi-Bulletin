Ghazipur Dealer Brutally Murdered in Chandauli Attack

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29) from Ghazipur, was murdered in Chandauli district on Thursday night. While transporting water to his construction site, he was attacked by assailants with bricks and rods. The assailants chased and thrashed him before fleeing the scene. Police arrived and took him to the hospital, where he was pronounced dead. Senior police officials are investigating the case.
