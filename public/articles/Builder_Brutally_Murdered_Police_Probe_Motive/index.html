Builder Brutally Murdered, Police Probe Motive

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29), from Ghazipur district was murdered in Chandauli district on Thursday night. He was attacked by unknown assailants while transporting water to his construction site near Naikot village. The assailants used bricks and rods to attack him, and he succumbed to his injuries at the hospital. Police are investigating the case.
