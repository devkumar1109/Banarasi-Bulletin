Yogi Rewards Mahakumbh Workers, Raises Sanitation Wages

2025-02-27T08:59:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath announced a Rs 10,000 bonus for sanitation and health workers involved in the Mahakumbh in Prayagraj. From April, the minimum wage for sanitation workers in Uttar Pradesh will be Rs 16,000. Temporary health workers will receive direct bank transfers and be linked to Ayushman Bharat Yojana for health coverage. Yogi Adityanath also honored sanitation and health workers, distributing certificates under the Swachh Kumbh Kosh and Ayushman Yojana. He joined them for lunch to acknowledge their efforts. Earlier, CM Yogi and his cabinet participated in a cleanliness drive at Arail Ghat.
