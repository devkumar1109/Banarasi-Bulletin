Caste Census: Congress Thanks Gandhi, BJP Celebrates Modi

2025-05-03T18:17:00+00:00

Following the BJP's celebration of the decision to conduct a 'caste census', the Congress organized a thanksgiving march in Varanasi led by UPCC chief Ajay Rai. Rai credited Rahul Gandhi's pressure for the government's decision, highlighting the BJP's previous opposition to the census. He praised Gandhi's courage and honesty in challenging the government. The Congress march culminated at Dr. BR Ambedkar's statue after paying tribute to Swami Vivekananda. Meanwhile, BJP leaders held events welcoming the 'caste census' and praising PM Modi's leadership. BJP Kashi region president Dilip Patel called it a historic step towards social justice and inclusion. The BJP plans to further celebrate the announcement with intensified campaigns across its units.
