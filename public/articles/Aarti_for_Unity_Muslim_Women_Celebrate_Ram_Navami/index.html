Aarti for Unity: Muslim Women Celebrate Ram Navami

2025-04-06T19:26:00+00:00

Muslim women in Varanasi celebrated Ram Navami by performing aarti for Lord Ram, expressing happiness over the passing of the Waqf Amendment Bill. They believe the bill was passed with Lord Ram's grace and view it as a step towards unity and communal harmony. Led by Nazneen Ansari of the Muslim Mahila Foundation, the women sang songs and chanted "Jai Siya Ram", emphasizing the cultural significance of Ram Navami. They condemned divisive statements by religious and political leaders, stating that their aarti is a message of love and peace. Vishal Bharat Sansthan president Rajeev Shriguruji praised the Muslim women for upholding the tradition, calling it a message of unity and goodwill.
