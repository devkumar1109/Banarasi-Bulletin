Maha Kumbh 2025: Spiritual Boom for UP's Economy

2025-03-06T10:24:00+00:00

Maha Kumbh 2025 significantly boosted spiritual tourism in Uttar Pradesh, attracting 65 crore devotees and tourists to various pilgrimage sites. The event led to the development of five major spiritual tourism circuits, connecting prominent religious sites like Ayodhya, Kashi, Mathura-Vrindavan, Prayagraj, and Chitrakoot. Infrastructure investments of Rs 7,300 crore facilitated smooth arrangements, including the development of roads, flyovers, and corridors. The event saw the use of facial recognition technology and CCTV cameras for real-time tracking and visitor counting. Maha Kumbh resulted in an economic growth of Rs 3.5 lakh crore for Uttar Pradesh.
