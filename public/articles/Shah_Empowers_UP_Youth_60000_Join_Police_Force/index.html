Shah Empowers UP Youth: 60,000 Join Police Force

2025-06-15T08:45:00+00:00

Union Home Minister Amit Shah is in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, to distribute appointment letters to 60,244 candidates selected in the largest-ever police recruitment exam conducted by the UP Police. This marks a "historic moment" for the state, strengthening law and order and empowering youth through employment. Out of the 60,244 constable posts filled, 12,048 were allocated to women, a first for such a large number of female candidates securing appointments in any Indian security force. The exam was held across 67 districts in August 2024, with 1,174 examination centers set up for a transparent and technology-driven recruitment process.
