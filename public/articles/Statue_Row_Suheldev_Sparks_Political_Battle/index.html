Statue Row: Suheldev Sparks Political Battle

2025-06-10T15:57:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath unveiled a 40-foot bronze statue of 11th-century ruler Maharaja Suheldev in Bahraich. Samajwadi Party (SP) president Akhilesh Yadav responded by promising to erect a gold statue of Maharaja Suheldev in Lucknow if his party wins the next election. The unveiling sparked political tensions and renewed debate over the historical recognition of Maharaja Suheldev, particularly his victory against Ghazi Salar Masud in 1033 AD. Both parties accused each other of neglecting historical legacies.
