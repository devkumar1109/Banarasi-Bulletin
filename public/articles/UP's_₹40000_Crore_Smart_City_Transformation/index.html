UP's ₹40,000 Crore Smart City Transformation

2025-04-28T11:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh plans to transform 57 municipalities into smart cities with a ₹40,000 crore investment. The project, already allocated ₹145 crore in initial funding, aims to improve "ease of living" through digital systems for administrative efficiency and transparency. Smart features include CCTV surveillance, AI-driven civic service management, chatbot interactions, and integrated cowshed management. The initiative will also focus on sustainable development, with measures to control pollution, ensure uninterrupted power and water supply, and promote local economies through investment, tourism, and job creation. Infrastructure development will include CC roads, junction redesigns, digital libraries, exhibition centers, and auditoriums.
