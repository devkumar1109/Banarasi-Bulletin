Fatal Bus-Truck Crash Claims Three Lives

2025-04-28T19:46:00+00:00

Two people died and 22 others were injured in a bus-truck collision near Rania in Kanpur Dehat on Sunday night. The accident occurred at 1:30 am when a Janrath bus traveling from Agra to Varanasi collided with a truck. The deceased are identified as co-driver Bhupendra Singh and passenger Rajendra Prasad. Six critically injured passengers were referred to Lala Lajpat Rai Hospital in Kanpur. The injured were transported to the district hospital, where Dr. Shriprakash declared Rajendra Prasad dead. Bus driver Jagveer Singh succumbed to his injuries later.
