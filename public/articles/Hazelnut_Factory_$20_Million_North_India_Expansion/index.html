Hazelnut Factory: $20 Million North India Expansion

2025-04-28T04:52:00+00:00

The Hazelnut Factory, a cafe and artisanal sweet chain, is investing Rs 20 crore to open 12 new outlets in North India this fiscal year. The brand, currently operating 11 outlets, plans to expand to cities like Varanasi, Noida, Bareilly, Agra, Meerut, and Muradabad. They are establishing a new facility in Noida to support the expansion. Revenue is expected to reach Rs 100 crore this fiscal year, up from Rs 55 crore last year. The Hazelnut Factory is targeting 50+ outlets across 5 states in the next 3 years. Bikaji invested Rs 131 crore for a 53.02% stake in the company in 2024.
