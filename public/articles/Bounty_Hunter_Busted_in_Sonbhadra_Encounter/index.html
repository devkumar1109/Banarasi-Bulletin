Bounty Hunter Busted in Sonbhadra Encounter

2025-02-25T18:11:00+00:00

A criminal with a Rs 25,000 bounty on his head was arrested in Sonbhadra district following an encounter with police. The criminal, identified as Vishal alias Algu Yadav, was involved in several loot cases. Police received a tip-off and cordoned off Takiya Road, leading to a chase. During the chase, the criminal opened fire on the police, who retaliated in self-defence, injuring him in the leg. A country-made revolver and motorcycle were recovered from him.
