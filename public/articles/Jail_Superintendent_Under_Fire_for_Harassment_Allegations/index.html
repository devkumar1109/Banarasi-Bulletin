Jail Superintendent Under Fire for Harassment Allegations

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending an inquiry into a harassment complaint filed against him by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment, harassing her for not fulfilling his demands, and threatening her job security. She also alleged Singh pressured her for sexual favors in exchange for benefits. Kanojia was transferred to Naini central jail in Prayagraj after filing her complaint. Singh's deputy Saurabh Srivastava will oversee Varanasi district jail until further notice. An investigation is underway, and the outcome will determine the next steps. Kanojia's daughter filed a separate complaint against Singh, detailing similar allegations of harassment and threats.
