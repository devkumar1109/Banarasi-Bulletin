India Shocked: Two Defense Personnel Die Mysteriously

2025-03-29T06:04:00+00:00

An Indian Air Force civil engineer, SN Mishra (51), was shot dead in his sleep at his official residence in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. An unidentified assailant fired through the window, causing a chest wound that proved fatal. In a separate incident, Lieutenant Colonel Diwarkar Singh (49) was found dead in the parking area of his housing complex in Ranchi. Preliminary investigation suggests he may have fallen from a building, but police are investigating all possibilities, including suicide. Singh's family claimed he was under work-related stress.
