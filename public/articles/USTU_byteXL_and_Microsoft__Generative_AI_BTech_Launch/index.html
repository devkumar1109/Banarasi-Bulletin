USTU, byteXL, and Microsoft: Generative AI B.Tech. Launch

2025-06-02T07:40:00+00:00

USTU and byteXL have launched a 4-year B.Tech. program in Generative AI & ML in collaboration with Microsoft. The program, designed to prepare students for high-growth tech careers, will be offered at USTU in North Mumbai. The curriculum emphasizes a "Learn + Work + Network = Placement" formula, blending academic theory with real-world experience and industry connections. Students will gain practical skills and exposure to cutting-edge technologies through the program, making them highly competitive in the job market.
