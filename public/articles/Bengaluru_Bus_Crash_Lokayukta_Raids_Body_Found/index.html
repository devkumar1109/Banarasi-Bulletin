Bengaluru Bus Crash, Lokayukta Raids, Body Found

2025-05-09T00:30:00+00:00

A BMTC bus crashed into a road divider at Sangolli Rayanna Circle in Bengaluru, injuring two workers. The injured were transported to KC General Hospital and then to another facility. The bus driver and conductor sustained minor injuries. Karnataka Lokayukta Police conducted raids at 24 locations, uncovering disproportionate assets worth over Rs 26.5 crore belonging to four government officials. The half-burnt body of a 27-year-old man named Madhusudan was found near Anekal forest. Preliminary investigations suggest suicide due to financial and health issues, but a case has been registered and further investigation is underway. The Karnataka High Court ruled that forest produce is presumptively owned by the State government, with the burden of proof on individuals accused of illegal transportation.
