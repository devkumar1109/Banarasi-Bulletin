Yogi Adityanath: Transparency, Tech Drive UP Tax Growth

2025-05-02T16:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath emphasized transparency, accountability, and technology usage in the state's tax administration. He stressed the need to combat tax evasion and build a taxpayer-friendly system. The State Tax Department collected Rs 1,14,637.54 crore in 2024-25 and aims for Rs 1.75 lakh crore in 2025-26. April collection reached Rs 9,986.15 crore, deemed satisfactory but requiring improvement. Yogi highlighted zones exceeding 60% of their monthly targets and urged underperforming zones and sectors to improve. He directed officials to prepare regional action plans and utilize IT tools, AI, and data analytics for a more transparent tax system.
