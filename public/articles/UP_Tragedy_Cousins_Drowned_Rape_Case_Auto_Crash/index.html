UP Tragedy: Cousins Drowned, Rape Case, Auto Crash

2025-05-16T10:11:00+00:00

Two minor cousins, Razia (10) and Shazia (12), drowned in a pond in Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh while grazing buffaloes. Their bodies were recovered from the pond and pronounced dead at the hospital. In other news, a youth was sentenced to 25 years in prison for raping a minor in Chitrakoot. An auto rickshaw accident in Sonbhadra killed two people and injured eight others. Noman Ilahi, arrested for spying for Pakistan, was brought to his house in Shamli for searches where passports and documents were recovered. New state-of-the-art microbiology labs are being set up in five major cities of UP: Varanasi, Kanpur, Mirzapur, Bareilly, and Aligarh.
