Hospital Heroes: Disaster Training Prepares Public Health Workforce

2025-03-08T18:54:00+00:00

A six-day disaster management training program concluded at BHU's Skill Center in Varanasi. Organized by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare's Disaster Management Cell and IMS-BHU, the program focused on public health emergencies for hospital administrators. 37 representatives from Gujarat, UP, Rajasthan, Haryana, and Daman and Diu participated. The training covered disaster preparedness, hospital safety, triaging critical patients, patient evacuation procedures, and fire control in hospitals.
