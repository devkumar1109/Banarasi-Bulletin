Honeymoon Murder: Wife Plots Husband's Death

2025-06-09T14:45:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi, wife of slain businessman Raja Raghuvanshi, has been arrested for allegedly orchestrating her husband's murder during their honeymoon in Meghalaya. The Meghalaya Police apprehended three contract killers involved in the murder and tracked Sonam down after a 17-day interstate manhunt. Sonam allegedly conspired with her lover and two associates to murder Raja before their honeymoon trip. The police claim to have uncovered chilling details of the plot, suggesting Sonam meticulously planned the crime.
