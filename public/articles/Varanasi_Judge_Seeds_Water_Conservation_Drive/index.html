Varanasi Judge Seeds Water Conservation Drive

2025-06-05T18:57:00+00:00

On World Environment Day, Varanasi District Judge Jai Prakash Tiwari inaugurated a plantation drive and awareness rally, emphasizing water conservation. He highlighted the limited availability of drinkable water and the importance of saving it. Tiwari suggested storing water discharged from RO machines for plants and cleaning, conserving 2-3 buckets daily. He also advocated for planting trees to control temperature and save electricity. The plantation program was held at IIT-BHU, led by Dean Prof NK Mukhopadhyay, Administrative In-charge Prof Ranjit Mohanty, and Registrar Rajan Srivastava.
