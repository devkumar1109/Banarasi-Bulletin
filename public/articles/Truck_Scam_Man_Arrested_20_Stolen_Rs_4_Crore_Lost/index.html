Truck Scam: Man Arrested, 20 Stolen, Rs 4 Crore Lost

2025-05-24T18:43:00+00:00

A 28-year-old man, Abhishek Yadav, has been arrested in Varanasi for selling trucks to scrap dealers using fake registration documents. Yadav operated by booking trucks from owners under the guise of renting them, then selling them for profit. He sold 20 trucks worth Rs 4 crore from Jhansi, Kanpur, and Prayagraj. Yadav used Facebook brokers to contact truck owners, offering higher rental rates and bribing brokers to gain their trust. He paid half the rental amount upfront and then sold the trucks to a scrap dealer named Shafiq alias Chakki in Nagpur for Rs 5 lakhs each. The investigation began after multiple complaints about missing trucks.
