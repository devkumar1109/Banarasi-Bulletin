UP's New Liquor Policy: More Shops, More Choices

2025-04-02T10:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's new excise policy for 2025-26 is now in effect, bringing several changes. Separate foreign liquor and beer shops have been replaced with composite shops to increase retail density. Country liquor shops can now sell beer, and low-alcoholic bars have been introduced in major cities. Bars and premium retail vends are permitted in IT and ITeS establishments exceeding 20,000 square meters. Liquor tasting is allowed in wineries, breweries, and distilleries, and retail shops can open within these facilities. A new 90 ml category has been added to the IMFL regular category. For the first time in seven years, all liquor shop licenses will be allocated through an e-lottery system.
