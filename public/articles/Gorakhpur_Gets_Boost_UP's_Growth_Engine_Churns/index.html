Gorakhpur Gets Boost: UP's Growth Engine Churns

2025-04-05T19:17:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated and laid the foundation stones for 107 development projects worth Rs 1,640 crore in Gorakhpur. He stated that UP is no longer under the influence of casteism, dynastic politics, or mafia, and has emerged as a growth engine under Prime Minister Narendra Modi's leadership. Key projects include a ring road, a theme park, a residential complex, and a convention center. The CM highlighted UP's progress in infrastructure, tourism, and housing, contrasting it with the previous governments' focus on appeasement politics and vote banks. He emphasized the state's economic transformation, citing its rise from 7th to 2nd largest economy since 2017. He attributed this progress to the state's investor-friendly environment and safe conditions.
