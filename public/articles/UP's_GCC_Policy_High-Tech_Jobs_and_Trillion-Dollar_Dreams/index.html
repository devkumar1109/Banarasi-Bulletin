UP's GCC Policy: High-Tech Jobs and Trillion-Dollar Dreams

2025-05-18T12:59:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government launched the GCC Policy 2024 to attract Fortune 500 companies and boost foreign direct investment. The policy aims to develop Noida, Lucknow, Kanpur, and Varanasi into major technology and digital service hubs, creating over 200,000 high-salary jobs in five years. Incentives include land cost subsidies and stamp duty waivers to encourage investment. The policy also prioritizes innovation and startup support. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath envisions making Uttar Pradesh a trillion-dollar economy through this initiative.
