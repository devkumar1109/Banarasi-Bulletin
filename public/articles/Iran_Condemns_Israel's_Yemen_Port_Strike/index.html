Iran Condemns Israel's Yemen Port Strike

2025-05-06T20:00:00+00:00

Iran condemned Israel's military strike on Yemen's Hodeidah port, calling it a "crime" and a violation of the UN Charter. The attack followed a Houthi strike on Ben Gurion Airport. Iran urged the international community to pressure Israel to cease its actions in Gaza, highlighting the escalating tensions in the region. Israel's actions have drawn criticism, with concerns about further escalation and the humanitarian impact on civilians. Efforts for peace have been unsuccessful, with both Israel and Iran maintaining strong positions and accusing each other of wrongdoing.
