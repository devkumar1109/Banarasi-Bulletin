Yogi Welcomes LSG Ahead of IPL 2025

2025-03-17T17:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath greeted the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) team, ownership, and coaching staff at his residence on Monday. The meeting took place ahead of the IPL 2025 season, where LSG will play their first match against Delhi Capitals on March 24th in Visakhapatnam under the captaincy of Rishabh Pant. CM Adityanath felicitated the entire LSG team. The squad includes notable players like Aiden Markram, David Miller, Rishabh Pant, Nicholas Pooran, Mitchell Marsh, and Ravi Bishnoi amongst others.
