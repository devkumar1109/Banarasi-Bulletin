Yogi Welcomes LSG Ahead of IPL 2025

2025-03-17T17:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath greeted the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) team, owner Sanjiv Goenka, and coaching staff at his residence ahead of IPL 2025. LSG's opener is against Delhi Capitals on March 24 in Visakhapatnam, with Rishabh Pant as the new captain. The team received felicitations from Yogi Adityanath. The LSG squad includes retained players like David Miller and Nicholas Pooran, along with new additions like Aiden Markram and Rishabh Pant.
