Metropolis Expands in UP With Scientific Pathology Acquisition

2025-03-03T09:55:00+00:00

Metropolis Healthcare Limited acquired Agra-based diagnostic chain Scientific Pathology for INR 55-83 crore through a slump sale transaction. The acquisition aims to expand Metropolis' presence in Western Uttar Pradesh, a rapidly growing diagnostic market estimated at ₹2,000 crore. The deal includes Scientific Pathology's laboratories and collection centers in Agra and surrounding areas. Metropolis will transition from a wholly owned subsidiary to part of the Metropolis Healthcare Group. This acquisition strengthens Metropolis' network and improves access to diagnostic services in the region, leveraging Scientific Pathology's 40-year legacy and B2C focus.
