Open Sky Delivery: Ambulance Driver in Hot Water

2025-05-23T17:01:00+00:00

A woman in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, gave birth outside a community health center under the open sky. The incident occurred when an ambulance driver allegedly dropped her off at the gate instead of taking her to the labor room. A video of the delivery has gone viral, prompting the Chief Medical Officer to order an investigation. A three-member team led by the Additional Chief Medical Officer has been formed to probe the matter and submit a report. The incident is being treated seriously, and action will be taken against those responsible after the inquiry.
