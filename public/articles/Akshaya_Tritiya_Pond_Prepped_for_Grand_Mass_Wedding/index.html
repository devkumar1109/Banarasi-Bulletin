Akshaya Tritiya: Pond Prepped for Grand Mass Wedding

2025-04-25T17:49:00+00:00

Officials in Varanasi conducted a surprise inspection of Sankuldhara Pond to review preparations for a mass wedding on Akshaya Tritiya (April 30). The event will feature a procession with horse-drawn carriages, altars around the pond, a main stage, and arrangements for food and parking. Inspectors reviewed cleanliness, parking, road repairs, lighting, pond cleaning, and security. Officials from various departments, including the ADM, municipal commissioner, tourism, social welfare, waterworks, and police, accompanied the divisional commissioner and DCP.
