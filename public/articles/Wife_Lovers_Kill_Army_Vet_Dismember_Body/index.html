Wife, Lovers Kill Army Vet, Dismember Body

2025-05-14T10:59:00+00:00

In Ballia, India, Devendra Ram, a 62-year-old retired army electrician, was murdered by his wife, Maya, and three of her lovers: Mithilesh Patel, Anil Yadav, and Satish Yadav. The crime occurred on May 8th. Police recovered human limbs and the torso from a field and a well, respectively. The head is believed to have been dumped in the Ghagra river. Maya initially filed a missing person report for her husband, but confessed to the crime after police found evidence and questioned her. The suspects were arrested following a police encounter. Anil Yadav sustained a bullet injury during the confrontation. The weapon used in the murder was also recovered.
