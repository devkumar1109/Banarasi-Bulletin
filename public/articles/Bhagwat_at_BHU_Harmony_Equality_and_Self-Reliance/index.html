Bhagwat at BHU: Harmony, Equality, and Self-Reliance

2025-04-05T19:08:00+00:00

RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat addressed two sessions at BHU, Varanasi, emphasizing social harmony and equality in the Sangh's centenary year. He called for sharing temples, water, and resources equally, regardless of caste or religion, stating that Hindu society encompasses all Indians. Bhagwat highlighted the need to strengthen families and promote larger units, emphasizing self-reliance and the use of indigenous products. He urged people to fulfill their civic duties, such as wearing helmets, following traffic rules, and maintaining cleanliness. Bhagwat also stressed the importance of teachers, particularly in IT, medical, and social fields, engaging with rural areas to spread values among students.
