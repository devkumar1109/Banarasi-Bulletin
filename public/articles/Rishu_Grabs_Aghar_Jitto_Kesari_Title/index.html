Rishu Grabs Aghar Jitto Kesari Title.

2025-06-14T00:00:00+00:00

Wrestler Rishu from Kathua won the 60th Aghar Jitto Kesari Dangal title in Reasi, defeating Wrestler Shakti from Noida. The main bout lasted 13 minutes. Rishu received Rs 20,000, a traditional Gurj (Gadda), and the Aghar Jitto Kesari Patta, while Shakti received Rs 11,000. ADC Reasi Rakesh Kumar was the chief guest and distributed Rs 2,00,000 in cash prizes. The event saw 37 bouts officiated by Naresh Kumar (Pappu Lakri) and his team. Other winners included Surjit Singh, Amir Khan, Anil Kumar, and Chain Singh.
