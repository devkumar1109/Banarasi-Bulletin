Varanasi Rape: Modi Demands Justice, Reforms

2025-04-16T04:52:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi was allegedly drugged and gang-raped by 23 men over six days in March 2025. 13 suspects have been arrested, and a high-level investigation is underway. Prime Minister Modi intervened, demanding strict action and systemic reforms to prevent such crimes. The survivor's family and activists are demanding justice. The police response has been criticized for delays and procedural lapses, leading to the removal of a senior officer. Women's rights organizations are protesting, calling for comprehensive reforms to address gender-based violence. This case highlights the need for stronger law enforcement mechanisms and societal changes to protect women.
