Radiologist Shortage: Justice Delayed for Rape Victims

2025-04-23T07:41:00+00:00

The Allahabad High Court has expressed concern over a shortage of radiologists, which is leading to delays in examinations for rape victims. This delay can have significant consequences for the victims, as it can hinder the justice process and their access to timely medical care.
