Varanasi's Ashes Holi: Tradition Meets Devotion

2025-03-10T04:41:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Naga Sadhus and Aghoris will celebrate 'Chita Bhasm ki Holi' (Holi with funeral pyre ashes) on Monday at Harishchandra Ghat and Tuesday at Manikarnika Ghat. This unique tradition, also known as 'Masane ki Holi', involves special prayers and rituals. Organizers have condemned opposition from groups claiming it violates tradition, emphasizing its religious significance and historical practice in Kashi. Aghori Baba Kapali Chandeshwar, head of Aghorpeeth Harishchandra Ghat Kashi, stated that the tradition holds a special place in Kashi's religious customs and is a celebration of Lord Shiva's abode. He highlighted that Kashi, considered a great cremation ground, is beyond ordinary rules and has unique traditions like this Holi celebration.
