Varanasi's Divine Cricket Haven Takes Shape

2025-04-25T03:46:00+00:00

Varanasi is constructing a new 30,000-seater cricket stadium, the Varanasi International Cricket Stadium, estimated to cost Rs 452 crore. Construction is expected to be completed by April 2026. The stadium's design incorporates Shiva-inspired elements like a crescent-shaped canopy, trident-shaped floodlights, and seating arrangements inspired by Kashi's ghats. The stadium will feature nine pitches (four red soil, three black soil, and two mixed wickets), making it unique in India. It will also include dormitories for 100 trainees of a proposed academy, solar panels, a water recycling system, and EV recharging facilities. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for the stadium in September 2023.
