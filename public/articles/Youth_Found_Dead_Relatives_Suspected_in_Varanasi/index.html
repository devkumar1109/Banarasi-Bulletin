Youth Found Dead, Relatives Suspected in Varanasi

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of a youth, Hemant Kumar Bind, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, alleged murder and filed a police complaint. Dinanath stated Hemant was called to a wedding by relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar, on Friday night. They later informed him Hemant attacked them and fled. Police are questioning the two relatives and awaiting autopsy and investigation results before taking further action.
