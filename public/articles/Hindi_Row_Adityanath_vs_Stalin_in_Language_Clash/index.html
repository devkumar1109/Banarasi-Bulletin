Hindi Row: Adityanath vs Stalin in Language Clash

2025-03-27T07:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath accused DMK leader MK Stalin of using divisive politics by opposing the three-language policy and Hindi language. Stalin retorted that Adityanath's criticism is "black comedy" and that Tamil Nadu opposes language imposition, not languages themselves. Stalin stated that the DMK's stance has rattled the BJP. Adityanath believes that learning all languages is important and questions why the DMK opposes Hindi.
