Khukri Rum: Nepal's Spirit Lands in India

2025-03-19T09:47:00+00:00

Nepal’s iconic Khukri Rum has launched in India, targeting the premium spirits market in Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Goa. The brand, known for its XXX, White, and Spice variants, is priced between Rs 1,700 - 2,000. Khukri aims to capitalize on the growing demand for premium spirits in India, which is projected to see a 5.2% CAGR in the rum segment by 2031. Initially imported, Khukri plans to expand into the "bottled in India" segment in the future. The brand is available in retail outlets and hospitality venues and is confident about its success in the Indian market.
