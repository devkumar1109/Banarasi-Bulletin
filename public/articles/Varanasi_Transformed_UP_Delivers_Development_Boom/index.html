Varanasi Transformed: UP Delivers Development Boom

2025-03-27T19:00:00+00:00

In the past eight years, the Uttar Pradesh government, in conjunction with the central government, has completed extensive development projects in Varanasi, benefiting the public. Over 458 projects worth over Rs 40,536 crores were inaugurated, covering sectors like roads, bridges, drinking water, sewage, education, health, energy, tourism, urban development, and police welfare. Significant projects include 102 road and bridge construction projects worth over Rs 22,408 crores, 34 Smart City projects worth Rs 877.65 crores, 60 sewerage and drinking water projects worth over Rs 1,000 crores, 32 educational projects worth over Rs 512 crores, 43 health-related projects worth Rs 1,870.36 crores, and six sports projects worth Rs 128.07 crores. Construction on numerous additional projects is underway, with a combined value exceeding Rs 15,000 crores.
