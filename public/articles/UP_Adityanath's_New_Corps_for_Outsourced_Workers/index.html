UP Adityanath's New Corps for Outsourced Workers

2025-04-25T21:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is establishing the Uttar Pradesh Outsource Service Corporation (UPCOS) to improve working conditions for the state's 1.1 million outsourced employees. Concerns raised include salary deductions, delayed payments, lack of EPF/ESI benefits, unfair recruitment, and workplace harassment. The corporation will centralize hiring, ensure fair remuneration, and guarantee reservation for SCs, STs, and OBCs in contract employment. UPCOS will mandate direct bank deposits by the 5th of each month and ensure timely EPF/ESI contributions. Service providers violating regulations will face blacklisting, debarment, financial penalties, and legal action.
