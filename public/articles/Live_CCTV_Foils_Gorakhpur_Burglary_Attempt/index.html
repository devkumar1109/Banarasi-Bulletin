Live CCTV Foils Gorakhpur Burglary Attempt

2025-03-08T07:58:00+00:00

Three burglars from Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, were arrested after attempting to rob a house in Kahaw village. The homeowner, residing in Mumbai, monitored the live CCTV feed and alerted his brother, the village head, and the police. The prompt response led to a two-hour standoff, during which one burglar attempted to escape via a rope but was apprehended. Police recovered two firearms from the burglars. The incident occurred on the night of March 6th and 7th. Villagers, angered by the attempted robbery, assaulted the burglars before handing them over to the police.
