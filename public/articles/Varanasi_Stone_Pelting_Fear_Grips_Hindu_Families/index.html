Varanasi Stone Pelting: Fear Grips Hindu Families

2025-03-06T18:30:00+00:00

After a video showing boys from a particular community pelting stones in Varanasi's Kotwa area went viral, police registered a case. 15 Hindu families in the area allege they are being targeted with stones, living in fear after the killing of Bhaiyalal Patel in February. Police arrested some suspects in Patel's murder, while others remain at large. The Hindu families installed CCTV cameras after the killing, capturing the stone pelting incident. They also claim that land near a temple was deliberately converted into a garbage dump. Police are investigating the video to determine the target of the stone pelting and are maintaining a vigil in the area.
