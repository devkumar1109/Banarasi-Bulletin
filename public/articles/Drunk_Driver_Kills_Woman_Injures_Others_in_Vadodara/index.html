Drunk Driver Kills Woman, Injures Others in Vadodara

2025-03-14T10:53:00+00:00

A drunk driver, 20-year-old law student Rakshit Chaurasia, killed a woman named Hemani Patel in Vadodara, Gujarat. The accident occurred around 12:30 am on Friday when Chaurasia's car crashed into several two-wheelers. Patel, who was buying Holi colors with her minor daughter, died instantly. Three others, including the child, were hospitalized. Eyewitnesses reported Chaurasia's intoxication and him shouting "another round" after the crash. He was arrested and is from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Chaurasia's friend, Mit Chauhan, who was in the car, is also under investigation.
