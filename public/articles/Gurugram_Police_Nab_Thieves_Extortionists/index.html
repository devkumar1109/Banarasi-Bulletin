Gurugram Police Nab Thieves, Extortionists

2025-04-25T03:42:00+00:00

The Gurugram police arrested three individuals in separate theft cases. Akash, from Uttar Pradesh, was apprehended for bike theft and confessed to another theft in Gurugram. Shubham Pandey and Harsh, also from Uttar Pradesh, were arrested for bike theft. Akash has four prior theft, robbery, and snatching cases against him in Gurugram, while Shubham has three theft cases and Harsh has four theft and snatching cases. Police recovered three stolen bikes. In a separate incident, three men were arrested for extortion in Gurugram. Amit Bhati alias Bobby, Lalit, and Karan, all residents of Bandhwari village, were apprehended near Red Light T-Point on Gurugram-Faridabad Road.
