BHU Trauma Center Goes Digital for Enhanced Care

2025-05-01T06:06:00+00:00

The BHU Trauma Centre unveiled digital initiatives to enhance patient-centric healthcare. The center implemented a Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS), replacing film-based diagnostics with advanced digital imaging technology. Other initiatives include mobile PACS integration, an app-based grievance redressal system called 'e-X-Ray', integration with the National Digital Health Infrastructure, a dedicated Trauma Centre website, and a seamless e-payment gateway. The launch ceremony was attended by city officials and BHU representatives, highlighting the importance of these advancements in trauma care delivery.
