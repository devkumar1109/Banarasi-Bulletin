Yogi Reviews Varanasi Development, Addresses Ashram Needs

2025-05-13T13:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed ongoing development projects in Varanasi, focusing on transportation, tourism, and construction management. He met with representatives from Apna Ghar Ashram, which shelters the homeless. The ashram director requested government land or buildings to accommodate the growing number of residents, currently exceeding its capacity. Adityanath assured support and suggested expanding ashram facilities to other districts in Uttar Pradesh.
