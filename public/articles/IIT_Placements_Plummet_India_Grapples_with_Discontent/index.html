IIT Placements Plummet, India Grapples with Discontent

2025-03-27T14:48:00+00:00

Placement rates for BTech students at over half of the IITs have dropped by more than 10 percentage points since 2021-22. Lawyers are opposing the transfer of a judge to the Allahabad High Court. The Tamil Nadu Assembly passed a resolution against the Waqf Bill. In Jammu and Kashmir, women are taking up low-paying jobs due to a worsening unemployment crisis. A school in Shimla cancelled its Eid celebration after threats from a Hindutva group. Language changes are being identified as an early sign of Alzheimer's. Writer Rana Dasgupta won the $175,000 Windham-Campbell Prize.
