Electrician'sgrisly end: Wife, lovers plot murder.

2025-05-14T10:59:00+00:00

A retired electrician, Devendra Ram (62), was murdered and dismembered by his wife, Maya (55), and three of her lovers in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh. Human limbs were found in a field, leading to the arrest of Maya and one lover, Mithilesh Patel. The victim's torso was recovered from a well, and the head is believed to have been dumped in the Ghagra river. Two more lovers, Anil Yadav and Satish Yadav, were arrested after a police encounter. The murder took place on May 8th, and Maya initially filed a missing person report. However, police investigation revealed her involvement and the planned nature of the crime. Devendra was last seen visiting his parental village and frequently spent time there.
