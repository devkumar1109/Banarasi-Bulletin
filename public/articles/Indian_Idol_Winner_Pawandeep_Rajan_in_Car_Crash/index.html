Indian Idol Winner Pawandeep Rajan in Car Crash

2025-05-05T12:16:00+00:00

Singer Pawandeep Rajan, winner of Indian Idol Season 12, was involved in a serious car accident on May 5, 2025, at 3:40 AM on National Highway 9 in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh. His car collided with a stationary truck. Rajan sustained multiple limb fractures and is currently stable and conscious at Fortis Hospital in Noida. He will undergo several surgeries. The accident occurred while Rajan was traveling to Delhi from Champawat in Uttarakhand for a program. Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami wished for his speedy recovery.
