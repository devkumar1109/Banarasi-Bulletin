Indian Idol Winner Pawandeep Rajan in Car Crash

2025-05-06T07:45:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan was involved in a road accident in Uttar Pradesh, fracturing both legs, suffering a head injury, and injuring his right arm. He is currently in stable condition at Fortis Hospital in Noida and will undergo multiple surgeries. Two other people in the car, including Pawandeep's friend and the driver, also sustained severe injuries. The accident occurred at 2:30 am when the singer's car collided with a parked truck. Initial investigations suggest the driver dozed off, leading to the collision.
