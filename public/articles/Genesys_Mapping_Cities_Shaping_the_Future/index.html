Genesys: Mapping Cities, Shaping the Future.

2025-05-21T09:17:00+00:00

Genesys International Corporation, a leader in geospatial technology, has been creating 3D digital models of cities for nearly 20 years. These highly accurate models, with five-centimeter precision, are used for urban planning, infrastructure development, and sustainability analysis. Genesys' platform integrates diverse data sources, including sensor data and elevation models, to support applications like flood management, electric vehicle routing, and property tax identification. Their recent AI-powered navigation map features augmented reality and intelligent speed assistance, enhancing the driving experience. This technology helps visualize the impact of infrastructure projects, optimize EV routes, and improve city planning for a more sustainable future.
