Bride's Third Wedding Turns Deadly in Varanasi

2025-05-18T07:10:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested for murdering his 26-year-old wife, Aarti Pal, just one week after their wedding. This was Raju Pal's third marriage, and clashes reportedly began immediately after the wedding ceremony on May 9. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death. Neighbors alerted the police who took her to a local health center where she was declared dead. Raju has been arrested, and the body has been sent for post-mortem.
