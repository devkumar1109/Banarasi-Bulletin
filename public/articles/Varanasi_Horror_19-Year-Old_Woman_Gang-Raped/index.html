Varanasi Horror: 19-Year-Old Woman Gang-Raped

2025-04-07T20:58:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi alleges she was kidnapped and gang-raped by 22 men over several days. She went missing on March 29th and was found drugged and abandoned on April 4th. After returning home, she disclosed the assault to her father, leading to a police complaint on April 6th. Police have arrested six suspects and are pursuing the remaining perpetrators. The assaults reportedly occurred at various locations, including a hookah bar, hotel, lodge, and guest house. Authorities are treating the case with sensitivity due to the potential involvement of minors among the accused.
