Family Tragedy: UP Relatives Killed in Rajasthan Crash

2025-04-18T19:00:00+00:00

Two members of a family from Uttar Pradesh were killed and five others injured in a car-truck collision on the Dausa-Kothun highway in Tonk district. The accident occurred around 7 am near Hingonia turn when the family's Ertiga collided with a container truck while traveling to the Khatu Shyam Ji temple. Lakshmi (60) and Bhagwan Singh (32) were declared dead at a government hospital in Niwai. The five injured, including a teenager, are receiving treatment for serious injuries. The deceased and injured are relatives from Kandola village in Hapur district.
