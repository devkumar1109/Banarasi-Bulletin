Academic Arrested for Controversial Remarks

2025-05-18T11:56:00+00:00

Ali Khan Mahmudabad, an Ashoka University academic and former Samajwadi Party spokesperson, was arrested in Haryana for allegedly making remarks that disparaged women officers in the Indian armed forces and promoted communal disharmony. Mahmudabad, a political scientist specializing in Muslim political thought in late colonial India, holds a PhD from Cambridge University and is known for his multilingualism (Hindi, Urdu, and English). He was a close associate of SP president Akhilesh Yadav from 2018 to 2022 but has been inactive in politics since then. Mahmudabad comes from a politically active family; his father, Raja Sahib Mahmudabad, was a former Congress MLA and fought a long legal battle to reclaim his ancestral property.
