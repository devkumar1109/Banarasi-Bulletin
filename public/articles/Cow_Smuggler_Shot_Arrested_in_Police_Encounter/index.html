Cow Smuggler Shot, Arrested in Police Encounter

2025-04-19T06:31:00+00:00

A cow smuggler was injured and arrested in Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh following a police encounter. The co-driver, Imran, opened fire on the police, leading to a retaliatory gunfire exchange. Imran sustained bullet injuries and was subsequently apprehended by the police. Circle Officer (Jansath) Yatendra Singh Nagar confirmed the incident.
