Mahakumbh Mela 2025 Concludes in Prayagraj

2025-02-28T12:04:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh Mela 2025 in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, concluded on Mahashivratri with the 'Antim Snan'. Over 65 crore devotees attended the event, taking a holy dip in the Triveni Sangam. The next Kumbh Mela will be held in Trimbakeshwar, Nashik, Maharashtra in 2027, on the banks of the Godavari river. Trimbakeshwar is home to the Trimbakeshwar Shiva Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas.
