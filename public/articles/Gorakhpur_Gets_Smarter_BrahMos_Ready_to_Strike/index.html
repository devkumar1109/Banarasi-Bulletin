Gorakhpur Gets Smarter, BrahMos Ready to Strike

2025-05-12T10:01:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the Urban Facilitation Centre and a senior citizen day care centre in Gorakhpur, furthering the city's smart city development. He also commended the BrahMos Missile Integration Unit in Lucknow, emphasizing its role in strengthening India's defense capabilities and reducing reliance on imports. Adityanath stated that the missiles manufactured in Lucknow will "shake the enemy," referring to the recent targeting of a Pakistani aircraft by a BrahMos missile. This facility aligns with the "Make-in-India" initiative. Adityanath urged citizens to remain vigilant against anti-national elements.
