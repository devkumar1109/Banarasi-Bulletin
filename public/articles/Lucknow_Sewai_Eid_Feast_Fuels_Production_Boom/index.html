Lucknow Sewai: Eid Feast Fuels Production Boom

2025-03-19T06:43:00+00:00

As Eid approaches, Sewai production is booming in Lucknow, India. Factories like India Wheat Sewai are working around the clock to meet the high demand for this traditional delicacy. The factory produces 2,000-2,500 kilograms of Sewai daily, supplying local markets and other states. Sewai production involves mixing wheat and water, processing it into strands, drying them in the open air, and baking them in wooden furnaces for a unique flavor. Skilled workers ensure the authentic taste throughout the process. Lucknow Sewai is priced between Rs. 50 and Rs. 100 per kilogram and is highly sought after, with customers travelling from across India to purchase it.
