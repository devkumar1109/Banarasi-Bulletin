OTTs Overflow with Diverse New Series

2025-06-06T19:30:00+00:00

This month, several OTT platforms offer diverse new shows. Prime Video presents "Bhool Chuk Maaf," a sci-fi romance starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi, where a man relives the same days, seeking redemption. Netflix releases "Jaat," an action drama featuring Sunny Deol and Randeep Hooda, centered on loyalty, legacy, and justice. The Korean crime thriller "Mercy for None" also premieres on Netflix, following a reformed gangster uncovering secrets behind his brother's death. JioHotstar offers "Tourist Family," a Tamil comedy-drama exploring the journey of an Eelam Tamil family migrating to India due to Sri Lanka's economic crisis. Zee5 presents "Chhal Kapat: The Deception," an investigative thriller examining morality and manipulation, and "Stolen," a story of two brothers grappling with guilt after witnessing a baby kidnapping. SonyLIV releases "Yatra 2," continuing the political journey of Y.S. Jagan Mohan Reddy, while JioCinema offers "Gaanth," a psychological crime thriller set in Delhi's dark suburbs.
