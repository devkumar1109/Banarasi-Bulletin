Namaz Row: Student Arrested in UP University

2025-03-16T11:06:00+00:00

A student, Khalid Pradhan, was arrested in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh for allegedly offering namaz in an open area of a private university. The arrest followed protests by local Hindu groups after a video surfaced showing students offering namaz on the campus. The university administration suspended Pradhan and three security personnel. A case was filed against Pradhan under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and the Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008, based on a complaint by Kartik Hindu. The incident occurred during Holi celebrations, which coincided with the second Friday prayers of Ramzan.
