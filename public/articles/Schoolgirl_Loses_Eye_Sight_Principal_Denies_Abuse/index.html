Schoolgirl Loses Eye Sight, Principal Denies Abuse

2025-02-27T11:04:00+00:00

A Class 3 student in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, named Himanshi, allegedly lost vision in one eye following an incident at Bhogpur Mithoni Primary School. Himanshi's mother, Jyoti Kashyap, accused Principal Geeta Karal of beating her daughter, causing the injury. Principal Karal denied the allegations, claiming Himanshi already had weak eyesight and that the injury occurred when a classmate accidentally hit her with her elbow. Kashyap demanded a medical certificate from the principal, which was denied, leading to an altercation. The District Magistrate has ordered an inquiry into the incident.
