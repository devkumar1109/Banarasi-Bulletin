Counterfeit Cash Bust: Man Arrested, Rs 15,500 Seized

2025-05-09T15:14:00+00:00

Sanjay Verma, a resident of Brahmanpura village, was arrested by the Karanda police in Ghazipur for possessing counterfeit currency worth Rs 15,500. Police recovered 31 counterfeit notes of Rs 500 denomination, along with a motorcycle and a mobile phone from him. A case has been registered against Verma under sections 179 and 180 of the BNS Act, and further legal proceedings are underway.
