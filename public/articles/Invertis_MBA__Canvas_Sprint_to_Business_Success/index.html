Invertis MBA: Canvas Sprint to Business Success

2025-05-31T12:32:00+00:00

Invertis University, a top private MBA college in India, recently concluded a 10-day Business Model Canvas workshop. The workshop focused on experiential learning, incorporating the "Number Sprint" game to simulate real-world business challenges. Students engaged in strategic thinking, teamwork, and decision-making, honing essential business skills. Faculty members actively participated, fostering an atmosphere of innovation and critical thinking. The MBA program at Invertis is recognized for its industry-relevant curriculum, holistic development framework, and modern pedagogical tools. Admission is based on merit, group discussion, personal interview, or national-level exam scores like CAT, MAT, XAT, and ATMA.
