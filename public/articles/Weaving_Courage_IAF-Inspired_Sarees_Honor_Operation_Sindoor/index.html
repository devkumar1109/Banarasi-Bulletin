Weaving Courage: IAF-Inspired Sarees Honor Operation Sindoor

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers created special sarees inspired by the Indian Air Force (IAF) to honor 'Operation Sindoor'. The sarees feature designs of weapons like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale, symbolizing the IAF's strength and capabilities. This tribute recognizes the role of the Indian Army in the successful operation.
