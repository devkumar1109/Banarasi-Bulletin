IIT Bhilai Gets Rs 1,400 Crore Boost, Doubles Capacity

2025-05-16T09:20:00+00:00

The Central Government approved a Rs 1,400 crore expansion plan for IIT Bhilai, increasing its student intake capacity to 2,750. This signifies a more than doubling of the current 1,250 student capacity. The expansion will also establish Chhattisgarh's first dedicated research park spanning 15,000 square meters. Phase 1, Stage B of the project will include two new engineering departments, one science department, a large advanced laboratory, three hostels, a visitor hostel, staff residential quarters, and a sports complex. The expansion aims to boost technical education, regional growth, foster innovation, and support local startups.
