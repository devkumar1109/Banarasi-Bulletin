Modi in Varanasi: Development, Olympics, and Health Boost

2025-04-11T08:04:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Varanasi and inaugurated development projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore. He emphasized India's efforts to host the 2036 Olympics, urging Kashi's youth to begin training. Modi highlighted improvements in Varanasi's health infrastructure, stating that big hospitals are now closer to residents. He also mentioned the Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana, a scheme providing free treatment for the elderly.
