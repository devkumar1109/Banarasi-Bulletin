Teja Vidyalaya Students Shine in National Quiz

2025-03-13T13:56:00+00:00

Teja Vidyalaya students Palla Hruthik and Kondru Naveen Kumar secured fourth place in the national FIT India Quiz competition, winning Rs 5 lakh. The competition, organized by the Sports Authority of India and Union Ministry of Sports, was held online and involved 32 teams from across India. Sun Beam School, Varanasi won first place and Rs 25 lakh, while Assisi Vidyaniketan, Ernakulam came second with Rs 15 lakh, and DAV School, Gurugram secured third place and Rs 10 lakh. Principal Ustela Rama Devi congratulated the winners.
