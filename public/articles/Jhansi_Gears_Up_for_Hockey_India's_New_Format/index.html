Jhansi Gears Up for Hockey India's New Format

2025-04-03T18:11:00+00:00

The 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025 will be held from April 4 to 15 at the Major Dhyan Chand Hockey Stadium in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. The tournament will use a new Division-based format, with 30 teams divided into Division A, B, and C. Division A features the top 12 teams, including defending champions Hockey Association of Odisha and runners-up Hockey Haryana. Teams compete for the championship title, while Division B teams fight for promotion to Division A. Division C teams aim for promotion to Division B.
