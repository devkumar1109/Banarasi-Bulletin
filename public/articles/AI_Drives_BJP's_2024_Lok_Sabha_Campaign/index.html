AI Drives BJP's 2024 Lok Sabha Campaign

2025-04-14T13:52:00+00:00

AI played a significant role in the 2024 Lok Sabha election campaign. The ruling BJP used AI technology, including a tool called 'Bhashini,' to overcome language barriers by navigating India's 22 official languages and thousands of regional dialects. Prime Minister Modi himself showcased 'Bhashini' during a speech in Varanasi, highlighting its ability to simplify communication. AI was also employed for tasks like generating 50 million robocalls. These developments underscore the growing influence of AI in Indian politics.
