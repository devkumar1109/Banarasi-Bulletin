Teen's Blade Attack After Refund Refusal Spark Outrage

2025-05-04T07:39:00+00:00

A 15-year-old girl attacked a shopkeeper in Hapur, Uttar Pradesh with a blade after he refused to accept returned items. The girl allegedly had a history of purchasing items, returning them after use, and demanding refunds. The shopkeeper refused this time, leading to the girl attacking him with a blade, injuring his hands and stomach. The incident was captured on video and widely shared on social media. Locals claim the girl is mentally unwell and undergoing treatment. The shopkeeper was hospitalized, and a complaint has been filed against the girl. An investigation is ongoing.
