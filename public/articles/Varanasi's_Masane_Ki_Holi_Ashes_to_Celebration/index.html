Varanasi's Masane Ki Holi: Ashes to Celebration

2025-03-11T06:12:00+00:00

Aghoris and Naga Sadhus celebrated Masane Ki Holi in Varanasi, marking the start of Holi festivities in the city. The celebrations took place at Harishchandra Ghat, with participants smearing themselves with pyre ashes, symbolizing the cycle of life and death. Devotees performed rituals, lighting lamps and offering prayers at the cremation ground. The procession culminated at Harishchandra Ghat, featuring drummers, artists, and hundreds of devotees. The event had a surreal atmosphere, with chants, traditional drums, and cremation pyres in the background. Masane Ki Holi is a unique celebration that honors both life and death.
