Modi's Varanasi Boost: Rs 3,884 Crore in Development Projects

2025-04-10T07:35:00+00:00

PM Modi will visit Varanasi on April 11th to launch development projects worth Rs 3,884 crore. Key projects include a Rs 652.64 crore six-lane tunnel under NH-31 for Babatpur Airport expansion, increasing its capacity to 110 flights daily. Other projects include a water mission, government degree and polytechnic colleges, a hockey stadium, police infrastructure, road widening, and a flyover. The visit also includes launching a Rs 12 crore transport city project in Mohansarai and a Rs 6.15 crore mini stadium in Shivpur. Rs 345.12 crore will be allocated for 130 rural drinking water schemes under the Jal Jeevan Mission, and over Rs 1,000 crore for a 400 kV power substation and transmission lines.
