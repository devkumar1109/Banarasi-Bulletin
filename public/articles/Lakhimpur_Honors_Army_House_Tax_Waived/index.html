Lakhimpur Honors Army: House Tax Waived

2025-05-19T16:24:00+00:00

Lakhimpur Nagar Palika in Uttar Pradesh has waived house tax for families of serving and retired Indian Army personnel. This decision, announced on May 19, 2025, honors the army's response to the April 22 Pahalgam terror attack in Jammu and Kashmir. The tax relief applies to all families of Army personnel residing within the Palika limits. Nagar Palika President Dr Ira Srivastava stated the move aims to ease the burden on these families and show sincere gratitude for their sacrifices. Local residents have welcomed the gesture, and the initiative is seen as a model for other municipalities nationwide.
