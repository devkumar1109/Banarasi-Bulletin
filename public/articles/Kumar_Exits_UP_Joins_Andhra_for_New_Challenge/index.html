Kumar Exits UP, Joins Andhra for New Challenge

2025-06-11T03:28:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Saurabh Kumar has switched teams, leaving Uttar Pradesh for Andhra Pradesh ahead of the domestic season. Kumar has a strong first-class record for Uttar Pradesh, with 324 wickets and 2374 runs. He has also played 39 List A and 33 T20 matches, taking 51 wickets and scoring 340 runs in List A. Kumar was part of the Punjab Kings in the IPL and has been selected for the Indian national team but hasn't made his debut yet. He expressed his gratitude to Uttar Pradesh Cricket Association and his excitement for this new chapter with Andhra Pradesh.
