UPPCB Goes Digital, Green for Cleaner Future

2025-06-10T16:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Pollution Control Board (UPPCB) is planning a major expansion to improve environmental governance. The plan, awaiting administrative approval, includes revising fee structures, launching a transparent tech-enabled portal, and establishing new regional offices in all divisional headquarters and key industrial districts. Specialized waste management and research cells will also be created. These initiatives aim to address escalating pollution levels caused by rapid industrialization and urbanization, promote better compliance, and raise public awareness about sustainability.
