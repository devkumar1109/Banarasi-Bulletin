Yogi Prepares Varanasi for Modi's April Visit

2025-04-03T19:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed preparations for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's visit to Varanasi in the second week of April. He inspected the public meeting venue and directed officials to ensure foolproof security, crowd control, and amenities for participants. Yogi emphasized the speedy revamp of cremation ghats, including Manikarnika and Harischandra, before the monsoon. He instructed quality checks at the newly constructed Namo Ghat after a portion caved in. He also directed progress on beautification of the Varuna Riverfront and expansion of the National Forensic University. The CM lauded the completion of the Jal Jeevan Mission in all 211 village panchayats and instructed quality checks on completed projects. He stressed cleanliness campaigns, banned single-use plastics, and eased map approvals in villages under the Varanasi Development Authority.
