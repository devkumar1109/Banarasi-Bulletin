Revenge Killing: Milk Trader Slain, Accused Confesses.

2025-03-04T19:35:00+00:00

Milk trader Rajesh Yadav (50) was brutally murdered in Jaunpur district, Uttar Pradesh. The accused, Rahul Yadav (25), confessed to the crime, stating his motive was revenge for being rejected in his pursuit to marry Rajesh's daughter, who passed away a month ago. Tensions flared in the village, prompting heavy police deployment. Rajesh was found dead near the village telephone exchange with his throat slit. The Line Bazar police faced anger from the deceased's family and supporters. Rahul was arrested before Rajesh's body reached the village following a postmortem examination.
