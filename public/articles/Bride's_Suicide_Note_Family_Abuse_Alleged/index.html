Bride's Suicide Note: Family Abuse Alleged

2025-05-25T09:28:00+00:00

A 23-year-old newlywed, Samreen, died by suicide in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. She left behind a video accusing her husband, Junaid, and in-laws of severe mental harassment. Samreen claimed they tormented her following a miscarriage, criticizing her eating habits and disconnecting power to her room. She alleged her in-laws and husband repeatedly told her to end her life. Her husband was reportedly in Bengaluru at the time. Samreen's father was informed by her about the abuse and arrived at her house to find her dead.
