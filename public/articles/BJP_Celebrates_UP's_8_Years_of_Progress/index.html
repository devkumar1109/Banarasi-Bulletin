BJP Celebrates UP's 8 Years of Progress

2025-03-23T16:01:00+00:00

The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) will celebrate eight years of the Yogi Adityanath-led government in Uttar Pradesh from March 24 to April 14. The party will organize district-level programs highlighting the government's schemes for social and economic advancement, farmer welfare, women and youth empowerment, and its zero-tolerance policy on crime. Workshops will be held in every district, and beneficiary fairs and development seminars will be organized in each assembly. The BJP Mahila Morcha will engage with women at the village level, and on Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's birthday (April 14), cleanliness programs will be conducted along with discussions on social justice and harmony.
