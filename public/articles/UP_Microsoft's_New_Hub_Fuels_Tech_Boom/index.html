UP: Microsoft's New Hub Fuels Tech Boom

2025-03-08T12:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is emerging as a major tech hub in India. Microsoft inaugurated its new India Development Center (IDC) in Noida, highlighting the state's potential in AI and cloud computing. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath emphasized the center's role in boosting India's tech ecosystem and stated that the state's policies are designed to support innovation. Uttar Pradesh is attracting investment with its modern policies and initiatives like the Nivesh Mitra single-window clearance system. Adityanath cited examples like AI use in the Maha Kumbh to showcase the state's pro-industry approach.
