Holi Bloodbath: Lover's Plot Turns Deadly

2025-03-20T06:08:00+00:00

A man was murdered in Varanasi on Holi night, March 14. The victim, Diljeet, was lured by his girlfriend to a house where her new boyfriend shot him in the chest. The couple had been planning the murder for a month. CCTV footage shows the accused chasing Diljeet on a TVS Splendor and shooting him. Police tracked the accused using technical evidence and informants. After questioning Diljeet's girlfriend, they arrested both the girlfriend and her new boyfriend. The weapon used in the murder has yet to be recovered.
