Varanasi Runs, Sails for Clean Ganga on World Water Day

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, 'Run for Clean Ganga', and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw participants run 6 km from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat. Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal won the men's and women's categories respectively. Alongside the marathon, the forest department organized an event at Dhakwa Ghat, highlighting water and energy conservation. Ravi Kumar Singh, forest conservator, inaugurated the 'Dolphin Safari' to educate the public about dolphin conservation efforts.
