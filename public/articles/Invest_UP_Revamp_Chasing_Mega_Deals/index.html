Invest UP Revamp: Chasing Mega Deals

2025-04-10T00:27:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is revamping Invest UP, its investment promotion agency, following controversy and allegations against a senior officer. The revamp includes increasing staff, setting up an "industry chasing cell" as a single contact point for mega investment approvals, and potentially opening offices in Delhi, Mumbai, and Bengaluru. The industry chasing cell will assist investors with project reports, land allocation, site visits, financial support, and skilled manpower. The Delhi office aims to connect with the Union government, trade organizations, and embassies. Mumbai will focus on financial institutions, MNCs, and private equity firms, while Bengaluru will target technology companies, R&D centers, and startups.
