Holi Harmony: Uttar Pradesh Celebrates Together

2025-03-15T03:16:00+00:00

Holi celebrations in Uttar Pradesh were marked by interfaith harmony and unity. Despite coinciding with the second Friday prayers of Ramzan, festivities unfolded peacefully across the state, including in Barabanki, Lucknow, and Mathura-Vrindavan. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath participated in celebrations, showering the crowd with flower petals and 'gulal'. Security was heightened in several areas due to the overlapping religious observances, with drones and CCTV deployed to ensure safety. Despite some minor security incidents, Holi celebrations underscored the spirit of togetherness and cultural diversity in Uttar Pradesh.
