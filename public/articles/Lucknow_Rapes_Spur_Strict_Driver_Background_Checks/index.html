Lucknow Rapes Spur Strict Driver Background Checks

2025-03-24T09:53:00+00:00

Following the rape and murder of a woman in Lucknow, the Uttar Pradesh government has mandated strict background checks for all auto-rickshaw and e-rickshaw drivers. This measure aims to prevent future incidents and ensure only drivers with clean records operate. CM Yogi Adityanath expressed disappointment in the Lucknow Police for lapses in vigilance, stating that stricter monitoring of auto and e-rickshaw drivers could have prevented the crime. He warned police officials across the state of strict action against negligence.
