RSS Gears Up for 2027 UP Election Push

2025-04-10T08:09:00+00:00

The Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) is expanding its efforts in Uttar Pradesh ahead of the 2027 state elections. The organization is focusing on strengthening its presence at the grassroots level through increased membership drives and organizational restructuring. The RSS aims to bolster the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)'s electoral prospects in the crucial state. The BJP is the political arm of the RSS. This intensified outreach reflects the RSS's commitment to playing a pivotal role in shaping the political landscape of Uttar Pradesh.
