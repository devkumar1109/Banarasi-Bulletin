Ikea India Soars: Sales Boom, Prices Dive

2025-02-28T12:23:00+00:00

Ikea India achieved over 45 billion euros in revenue in FY24, defying global market challenges and establishing India as a key growth market. To drive affordability, Ikea India lowered prices by 16-39% over the past two years, offering nearly 1,000 products under Rs 200. Online sales, representing 30% of business, are the fastest-growing channel with 300 million online and offline visitations. Expanding its reach, Ikea is launching online delivery in Delhi-NCR and nine satellite cities in North India starting March 1, targeting the country's largest home furnishings market.
