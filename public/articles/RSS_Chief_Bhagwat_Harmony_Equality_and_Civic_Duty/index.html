RSS Chief Bhagwat: Harmony, Equality, and Civic Duty

2025-04-05T19:08:00+00:00

RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat called for a society based on harmony and equality, emphasizing the sharing of temples, water, and resources without caste discrimination. Speaking at Banaras Hindu University, Bhagwat highlighted the Sangh's centenary year and its focus on social construction. He urged youths to connect with the RSS through service, hospitality, and civic duty. Bhagwat also addressed environmental concerns, advocating for self-reliance and the use of indigenous products. He stressed the importance of strong families and promoting civic duties like wearing helmets, following traffic rules, and maintaining cleanliness. He urged academicians to focus on developing values among students, particularly those in IT, medical, and social fields, and to engage with rural areas.
