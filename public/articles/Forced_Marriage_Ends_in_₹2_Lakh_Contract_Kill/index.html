Forced Marriage Ends in ₹2 Lakh Contract Kill.

2025-03-25T07:29:00+00:00

In Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh, 28-year-old Dileep Yadav was killed in a ₹2 lakh contract murder just two weeks after his forced marriage. His 22-year-old wife, Pragati Yadav, conspired with her lover, Anurag alias Manoj, to hire contract killer Ramji Chaudhary. Pragati had been in a relationship with Anurag for four years before her arranged marriage to Dilip. After five days at her marital home, Pragati returned to her parents' house and plotted Dilip's murder with Anurag. On March 19, Dilip was lured to a secluded area and shot in the head. He died on March 21. Police arrested all three accused based on CCTV footage and WhatsApp call records. Pragati allegedly sold her jewelry to fund the murder. This case highlights the issue of forced marriages in India.
