Fake Puri Shrine Website Lands Duo in Jail

2025-04-28T13:14:00+00:00

Two individuals, Anshuman Sharma from Agra, Uttar Pradesh, and Aravind Kumar from Pratapgarh, Uttar Pradesh, have been arrested by the Odisha Police's CID-Crime Branch for defrauding pilgrims using a fake website. The website, www.neeladribhaktanivas.in, impersonated the official site for Niladree Bhakta Nivas in Puri, tricking pilgrims into depositing money into a Canara Bank account. Sharma registered the domain in May 2024, and the website was taken down following the Crime Branch's intervention. Police seized electronic devices, SIM cards, and identification documents from the accused. A case has been registered under sections 419/420 of the Indian Penal Code.
