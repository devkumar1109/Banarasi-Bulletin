Rare Vine Snake Found in Dudhwa Reserve

2025-05-15T11:49:00+00:00

A brown morph of the vine snake, Ahaetulla longirostris, was discovered in Dudhwa Tiger Reserve, marking the second live sighting in Uttar Pradesh. The snake was found during routine tiger tracking patrols in Kishanpur Wildlife Sanctuary and was confirmed to be healthy before being released. This rare discovery highlights Dudhwa's rich biodiversity and its potential as a research hub for herpetological studies. Officials plan to strengthen biodiversity monitoring to uncover more ecological treasures and improve conservation efforts in the region.
