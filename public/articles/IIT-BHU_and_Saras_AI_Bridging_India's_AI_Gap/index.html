IIT-BHU and Saras AI: Bridging India's AI Gap

2025-04-30T19:34:00+00:00

IIT-BHU and the US-based Saras AI Institute, the world's first AI-exclusive online degree-granting institute, signed a MoU to advance AI education in India. This collaboration aims to bridge the industry-education gap in AI and empower the next generation of Indian innovators. The partnership will deliver a curriculum focused on applied learning, with Saras offering its foundational 'Powers of AI' course as an open elective for IIT-BHU students across all disciplines. They will also co-develop 'AI Evaluator', an AI-powered assessment tool for real-time student progress tracking and simplified evaluations. The MoU enables flexible learning pathways, including a Minor in AI, Associate Degree opportunities, and industry-recognized AI certifications through Saras AI Institute. Joint research, guest lectures, hackathons, workshops, and event representation are also included.
