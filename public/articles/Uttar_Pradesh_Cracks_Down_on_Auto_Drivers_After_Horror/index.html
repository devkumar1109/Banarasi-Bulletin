Uttar Pradesh Cracks Down on Auto Drivers After Horror

2025-03-24T09:53:00+00:00

Following a rape and murder in Lucknow, the Uttar Pradesh government has mandated strict background checks for all auto-rickshaw and e-rickshaw drivers. This directive aims to ensure only drivers with clean records can operate, while those with criminal histories will face immediate police action. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath expressed displeasure over police lapses, stating the incident could have been prevented with increased vigilance and monitoring of drivers, as previously implemented.
