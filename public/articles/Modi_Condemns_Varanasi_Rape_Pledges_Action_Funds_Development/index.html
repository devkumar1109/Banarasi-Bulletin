Modi Condemns Varanasi Rape, Pledges Action, Funds Development

2025-04-11T07:18:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Varanasi and was briefed on the alleged gang-rape of a 19-year-old woman in his constituency. Officials reported the victim was allegedly assaulted by 23 individuals over six days in various hotels after being sedated. Modi instructed officials to take the strictest action against the perpetrators and implement measures to prevent future incidents. Six suspects have been apprehended. During his visit, Modi inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for 44 development projects worth ₹3,880 crore, focusing on rural development.
