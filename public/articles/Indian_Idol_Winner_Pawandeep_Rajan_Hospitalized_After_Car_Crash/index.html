Indian Idol Winner Pawandeep Rajan Hospitalized After Car Crash

2025-05-06T07:45:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan was hospitalized after a car accident in Uttar Pradesh. He sustained multiple injuries including fractures to both legs, a head injury, and a right arm injury. Pawandeep is now conscious and stable but will undergo several surgeries. Two others in the car, his friend Ajay Mehra and driver Rahul Singh, are also receiving medical attention. The accident occurred at 2:30 am when the car collided with a parked truck. Initial investigations suggest the driver fell asleep at the wheel. Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami expressed his well wishes for Pawandeep's recovery.
