Holi Bloodbath: Girlfriend, Lover Kill Man in Varanasi

2025-03-20T06:08:00+00:00

A man named Diljeet was murdered in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, on Holi night (March 14). The victim was lured to a location by his girlfriend, who then shot him in the chest with her new lover's help. The murder was pre-planned, and the couple fled the scene on a TVS Splendor motorcycle. Using technical evidence and informants, the police tracked down the accused, who were arrested along with another woman involved in the crime. The weapon used in the murder is yet to be recovered.
