Metropolis Expands in Agra with Scientific Pathology Acquisition

2025-03-03T09:55:00+00:00

Metropolis Healthcare Limited acquired Scientific Pathology, an Agra-based diagnostic chain, for INR 55-83 crore. The deal involves Metropolis Clinical Pathology taking over Scientific Pathology's labs and collection centers in Agra and surrounding areas. The acquisition aims to expand Metropolis' presence in Uttar Pradesh's growing diagnostic market, estimated at ₹2,000 crore. Metropolis sees Agra's healthcare hubs and Scientific Pathology's 40-year legacy and B2C focus as key assets. The collaboration will enhance Metropolis' capabilities, expand access through a franchise model, and introduce its specialized tests to the region.
