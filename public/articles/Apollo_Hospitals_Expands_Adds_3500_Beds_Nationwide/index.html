Apollo Hospitals Expands, Adds 3,500 Beds Nationwide

2025-04-09T11:30:00+00:00

Apollo Hospitals is investing ₹6,000 crore over five years to add 3,500 beds across India. The first phase, costing ₹2,880 crore and finishing by FY26, will add 1,737 beds in Pune, Kolkata, Hyderabad, and Gurgaon. The second phase, investing ₹3,220 crore, will add 1,775 beds over 3-4 years in Chennai, Varanasi, Mumbai, and Lucknow. This expansion focuses on specialties like oncology, cardiology, neurosciences, and gastroenterology to address the rising incidence of non-communicable diseases.
