Nepal Royals Rally Sparks Diplomatic Row

2025-03-12T07:41:00+00:00

A row has erupted in Nepal after a rally for former King Gyanendra Shah displayed posters of Indian Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. Thousands of Shah's supporters gathered in Kathmandu, calling for the reinstatement of the monarchy, which was abolished in 2008. Shah's return comes amidst growing dissatisfaction with Nepal's current political system, with many blaming it for instability and corruption. The presence of Adityanath's photo has angered many in Nepal, as it is seen as interference in their internal affairs.
