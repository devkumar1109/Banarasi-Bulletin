Ganga Expressway: Tech, Speed, and Safety Take Flight

2025-05-04T06:43:00+00:00

The Ganga Expressway, connecting Meerut and Prayagraj, is nearing completion and is expected to be inaugurated in November 2025. The expressway, initially estimated at Rs 36,230 crore, will significantly reduce travel time between the two cities to six hours. The Indian Air Force recently conducted successful night combat drills with Rafale, Sukhoi, and Mirage jets on the expressway's airstrip in Shahjahanpur. The Uttar Pradesh government is utilizing Swiss AI and sensor technology from ETH Zurich and RTDT Labs to monitor the expressway's quality in real time, ensuring safety and minimizing the risk of accidents. This technology detects and fixes road defects during construction, improving driver comfort and reducing future repair costs.
