Uttar Pradesh IPS Officer Shiradkar Promoted to DG

2025-06-11T20:02:00+00:00

SB Shiradkar, a 1993-batch IPS officer, has been promoted to the rank of Director General (DG) in the Uttar Pradesh Police. Shiradkar currently serves as the Additional Director General (ADG) of the Lucknow Zone. He previously held important positions including Police Commissioner of Lucknow and ADG Intelligence. Shiradkar met with Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath after his promotion, in what was described as a courtesy call.
