Vadodara Crash: Law Student's Claim of Pothole Sparks Inquiry

2025-03-15T07:53:00+00:00

A road accident in Vadodara, Gujarat, claimed the life of a 34-year-old woman and injured several others. Rakshit Chaurasia, a law student, allegedly drove his car into multiple vehicles, including two active vehicles and an electric vehicle. Chaurasia, who is in police custody, claims he was not drunk and was driving at 50 km/ph. He attributes the accident to a pothole causing his car to touch another vehicle, triggering the airbag and hampering his vision. The police have not yet released the alcohol test results. Multiple police teams are investigating the incident.
