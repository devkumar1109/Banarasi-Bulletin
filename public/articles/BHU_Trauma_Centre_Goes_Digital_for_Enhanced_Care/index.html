BHU Trauma Centre Goes Digital for Enhanced Care

2025-05-01T06:06:00+00:00

The Trauma Centre of BHU's IMS launched digital initiatives to enhance patient-centric trauma care. The center implemented a Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS), replacing film-based diagnostics with advanced digital imaging. Other initiatives include mobile PACS integration, an app-based grievance redressal system ('e-X-Ray'), empanelment and integration with the National Digital Health Infrastructure, a dedicated Trauma Centre website, and an e-payment gateway. The launch event was attended by dignitaries including Mayor Ashok Tiwari, BHU ex-officiating VC Professor VK Shukla, and BHU VC Professor Sanjay Kumar.
