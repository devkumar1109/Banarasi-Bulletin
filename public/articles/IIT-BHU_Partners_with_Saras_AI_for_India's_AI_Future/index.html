IIT-BHU Partners with Saras AI for India's AI Future

2025-04-30T19:34:00+00:00

IIT-BHU has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the US-based Saras AI Institute to advance AI education in India. The collaboration aims to bridge the industry-education gap in AI and empower the next generation of innovators. Saras AI Institute will offer its foundational "Powers of AI" course as an open elective for all IIT-BHU undergraduate students. IIT-BHU and Saras will co-develop an AI-powered assessment tool called "AI Evaluator" to track student progress and simplify evaluations. The MoU also enables flexible learning pathways, including a Minor in AI, Associate Degree opportunities, and industry-recognized AI certifications. Joint research, guest lectures, hackathons, and workshops are also part of the agreement.
