Varanasi Teen Held Captive, Gang-Raped for Seven Days

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly kidnapped and gang-raped by 23 men over seven days, between March 29 and April 4. The victim, a Class 12 student, went missing after a friend took her to a hookah bar. She returned home on April 4 and reported the incident to the police. The accused include former classmates and individuals the victim had interacted with on Instagram. Police have filed an FIR against all 23, six are in custody, and 11 remain unidentified. The incident began at a hookah bar, where the girl was allegedly drugged before being taken to different hotels and raped.
