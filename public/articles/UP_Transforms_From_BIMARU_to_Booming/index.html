UP Transforms: From BIMARU to Booming.

2025-03-26T00:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak highlighted the state's significant development in the last eight years under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. He emphasized improvements in infrastructure, including roads, electricity, water, education, and healthcare, contrasting the state's past "BIMARU" label with its current progress. Pathak announced Lucknow will host the largest Ambedkar Memorial. BJP MLA Rajeshwar Singh praised the government's achievements, citing job creation for over 7.5 lakh youths, a jump in the "Ease of Doing Business" ranking, and substantial investment proposals. Singh also mentioned a crackdown on crime, with 222 criminals killed in encounters, thousands injured, and over 20,000 arrested.
