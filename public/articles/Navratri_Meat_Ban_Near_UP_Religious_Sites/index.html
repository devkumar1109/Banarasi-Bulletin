Navratri: Meat Ban Near UP Religious Sites

2025-03-30T06:44:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Government has implemented a ban on the sale of meat within a 500-meter radius of religious places during the Navratri festival. This decision was announced in a statement published by The Hindu on March 30, 2025.
