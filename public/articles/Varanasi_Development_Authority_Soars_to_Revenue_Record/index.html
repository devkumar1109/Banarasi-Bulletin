Varanasi Development Authority Soars to Revenue Record

2025-04-06T19:26:00+00:00

The Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) achieved record revenue collection of Rs 37,106.23 lakh in the 2024-25 financial year, surpassing the target of Rs 27,537.59 lakh by 134.75%. This represents a 34.64% increase compared to the previous year. VDA also achieved nearly 100% expenditure on construction works, spending Rs 6,434.48 lakh out of the allocated Rs 6,516.34 lakh. The authority streamlined the map approval process through various initiatives, including forming a map facility cell, providing updated standards, recognizing high-performing engineers, and addressing technical difficulties through workshops. These efforts resulted in a significant increase in map approvals, with the VDA approving maps for 14 big hotel projects, 19 group housing projects, and three layout proposals.
