Varanasi Jewelers Shot, Robbed in Broad Daylight

2025-03-09T19:32:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a jeweller, Siyaram (53), and his son Vikas (24), were shot and robbed of cash and jewellery by three unidentified men on a motorcycle. The incident occurred at their shop in the Aharak area around 3:30 pm on Sunday. Police arrived at the scene, found CCTV footage of the robbers, and rushed the injured to a private hospital, where they are in stable condition. A massive checking drive has been launched across the district.
