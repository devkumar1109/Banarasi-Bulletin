Uttar Pradesh: Woman Dies By Suicide, Note Cites Illness

2025-06-04T16:44:00+00:00

A 28-year-old woman, Shalvi Gupta, died by suicide in Hamirpur district, Uttar Pradesh, India. She set herself on fire on her home's rooftop. Police suspect depression as the cause, citing a suicide note where she attributed her decision to her prolonged illness. Authorities are investigating the incident and the body has been sent for post-mortem examination. This tragic event highlights the urgent need for mental health awareness and support.
