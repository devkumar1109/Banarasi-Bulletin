Hate Speech Costs MLA Two Years

2025-06-01T19:36:00+00:00

Abbas Ansari, SBSP MLA from Mau Sadar, was sentenced to two years imprisonment for a hate speech incident in 2022. The court stated there is no place for hate speech in public service, especially when it aims to influence elections or threaten officials. The judgment highlighted that such speech harms both the nation and society, and that the accused's history of criminal activity and his family's political background were considered during sentencing. Ansari's speech, delivered during his election campaign, threatened state government officials with retribution after the elections, potentially disrupting communal harmony.
