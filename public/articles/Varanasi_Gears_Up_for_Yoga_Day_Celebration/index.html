Varanasi Gears Up for Yoga Day Celebration

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Preparations are underway in Varanasi for International Yoga Day on June 21. The main event will be held at Kashi Vishwanath Dham. A Yoga Week, starting June 15, will feature sessions at various locations including ghats, village panchayats, urban areas, schools, and health centers. District officials are ensuring all necessary preparations are in place for the Yoga Week opening at Namo Ghat on June 15. Yoga organizations are encouraged to conduct daily sessions at their designated locations. Youth welfare officers are collaborating with Yuvak Mangal Dal members to train them in yoga protocols for village-level sessions.
