Adityanath in Delhi: Farmers Front and Center

2025-06-10T08:17:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath met Union Home Minister Amit Shah in New Delhi. Adityanath also addressed the Kisan Samman Samaroh in Auraiya, highlighting the government's support for farmers since 2014. He mentioned initiatives like the Soil Health Card Scheme, PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, and Prime Minister's Agricultural Insurance Scheme. The government waived loans for 86 lakh farmers in 2017. PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, benefiting 12 crore farmers nationwide, is the world's largest direct benefit transfer scheme, with over Rs 3.25 lakh crore transferred.
