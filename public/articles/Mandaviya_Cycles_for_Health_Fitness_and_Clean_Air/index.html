Mandaviya Cycles for Health, Fitness, and Clean Air

2025-03-23T16:17:00+00:00

Union Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya led over 500 riders in Lucknow during the 'Fit India Sundays on Cycle' initiative. He emphasized that cycling not only improves health but also builds character and contributes to a healthier environment. The 3-km ride was joined by officials from the Sports Authority of India, MyBharat initiative, and the Physical Education Foundation of India. Over 100 athletes from the local National Centre of Excellence participated, performing Zumba routines before the cycling drive. Mandaviya encouraged citizens to incorporate cycling into their daily routines to combat obesity and reduce air pollution.
