Krishna Takes Helm as UP's New DGP

2025-05-31T18:33:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991-batch IPS officer, has assumed charge as the Director General of Police (DGP) of Uttar Pradesh. He succeeds Prashant Kumar who retired on Saturday. Krishna has a distinguished career, serving in various prominent roles including SSP Lucknow, DIG Lucknow Range, and ADG Agra Zone. Notably, he also served as IG (Operations) of the Border Security Force (BSF). Krishna's appointment was considered a strong contender due to his successful leadership in conducting the UP Police Constable recruitment exam, restoring public trust after a previous paper leak controversy.
