Shami Receives Death Threat, Demands Ransom

2025-05-06T12:42:00+00:00

Mohammed Shami, Indian cricketer, received a death threat via email on May 4th. The threat, demanding ₹1 crore ransom, originated from an email address belonging to Rajput Sindhar. Shami's brother filed an FIR with the Cyber Crime Police Station in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh, under relevant IPC and IT Act sections. This incident follows a similar death threat against India head coach Gautam Gambhir. The Delhi Police is investigating Gambhir's case and confirmed he is under protection. Shami is currently playing in the IPL 2025 for Sunrisers Hyderabad.
