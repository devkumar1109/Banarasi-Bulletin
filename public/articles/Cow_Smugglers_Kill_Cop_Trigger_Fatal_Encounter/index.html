Cow Smugglers Kill Cop, Trigger Fatal Encounter.

2025-05-18T08:59:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, three cow smugglers fatally struck a police head constable with their pickup van during an attempt to escape. An ensuing encounter resulted in the death of one smuggler and the apprehension of two others. The incident took place while the smugglers and their associates were trying to flee the scene.
