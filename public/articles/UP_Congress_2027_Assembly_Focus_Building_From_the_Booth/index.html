UP Congress: 2027 Assembly Focus, Building From the Booth

2025-04-23T19:10:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Congress has resolved to focus on the 2027 state assembly elections, aiming to prepare ground in all 403 constituencies. They are calling 2025 "the year of organisational rebuilding," with a focus on strengthening district and city units. The party plans to create and train cadre down to the booth level, with deadlines set for forming district, block-level, and booth executives. They condemned the recent terrorist attack in Pahalgam and observed a two-minute silence for the victims. UP Congress in-charge Avinash Pande criticized the BJP for alleged personal vendetta, citing the Enforcement Directorate's action in the National Herald case as an example of misuse of state machinery. He urged party workers to organize a Gandhian response.
