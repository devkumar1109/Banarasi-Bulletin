Faith and Order: India's Religious Observances

2025-03-28T10:16:00+00:00

In Varanasi, all fish and meat shops within the municipal corporation limits will be closed during Navratri (March 30 to April 6) to respect the religious sentiments of devotees. Shopkeepers violating the order will face strict action. Meanwhile, Meerut police have warned against unauthorized roadside prayers ahead of Eid-ul-Fitr and the last Friday prayers of Ramzan. Violators may face legal action, including passport cancellation and driving license revocation. Police urge people to pray at local mosques or designated Eidgahs. The Meerut administration is working with religious leaders and stakeholders to ensure peaceful celebrations.
