Gorakhpur Transformed: Yogi Adityanath's Development Drive

2025-04-05T15:21:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the new Tal Ring Road in Gorakhpur, highlighting the city's transformation from a crime-ridden area to a development hub. The road, situated along Ramgarh Tal, aims to alleviate traffic congestion. Adityanath pledged to eliminate poverty in Uttar Pradesh within three years and discussed the recent Waqf Amendment Bill aimed at preventing illegal land occupation. He inaugurated and laid the foundation for 629 development projects worth Rs 654 crore, including the Rohin Barrage, which will benefit farmers. Under Adityanath's leadership, Uttar Pradesh has become India's second-largest economy, driven by infrastructure development, irrigation projects, educational reforms, and safety measures. The state has shifted from "one district, one mafia" to "one district, one medical college".
