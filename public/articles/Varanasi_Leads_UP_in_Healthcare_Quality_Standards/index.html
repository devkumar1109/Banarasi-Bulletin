Varanasi Leads UP in Healthcare Quality Standards

2025-03-20T18:11:00+00:00

Varanasi topped Uttar Pradesh in NQAS (National Quality Assurance Standards) certification for medical facilities. Four urban primary health centres (Madanpura, Pandeypur, Town Hall, Durgakund) and four Ayushman Arogya Mandirs (Barai, Singhpur, Ajgara Pratham, Lalpur) received certification. This brings the total number of certified centres in Varanasi to 29. The certification recognizes excellence in areas like pregnancy care, child health, family planning, and disease management. The process involves assessment based on seven quality standards.
