Varanasi Leads UP in Healthcare Quality Standards

2025-03-20T18:11:00+00:00

Varanasi topped Uttar Pradesh in NQAS (National Quality Assurance Standards) certification. The Government of India certified four urban primary health centers (Madanpura, Pandeypur, Town Hall, and Durgakund) and four Ayushman Arogya Mandirs (Barai, Singhpur, Ajgara Pratham, and Lalpur). Varanasi secured first position in UP with a total of 29 centers receiving NQAS certification. The certification is awarded for seven standards, including care in pregnancy and childbirth, neonatal and infant health services, and management of communicable diseases.
