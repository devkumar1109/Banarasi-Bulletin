Hyderabad: Job Fraud, Child Trafficking, Tunnel Tragedy

2025-02-26T02:34:00+00:00

The Hyderabad Cyber Crime Police arrested a man from Uttar Pradesh for job fraud. The police are investigating the case further. In a separate incident, Rachakonda police busted an interstate child trafficking racket, arresting 11 people and rescuing four infants. Justice Kunuru Lakshman of the Telangana High Court heard a criminal case filed against KTR over remarks he made about the Chief Minister. BRS Working President KT Rama Rao demanded a judicial investigation into the SLBC tunnel collapse. Rescue efforts are ongoing at the Srisailam Tunnel collapse site, but no survivors have been found.
