Sharma's Stellar Varanasi Tenure: New Role as CM Secretary

2025-04-22T18:59:00+00:00

Kaushal Raj Sharma, the outgoing divisional commissioner of Varanasi, has been appointed secretary to the chief minister. During his five and a half years in Varanasi, Sharma oversaw the completion of 320 projects worth Rs 27,250 crore and progress on 101 projects worth Rs 15,340 crore. He received the Best Civil Servant Award from the Prime Minister and other accolades. Sharma played a key role in maintaining law and order, managing the Covid pandemic, and providing hospitality to foreign diplomats. He also oversaw elections at various levels and ensured smooth execution of court-ordered actions at the Gyanvapi complex. His tenure saw numerous high-level visits, including those of the Prime Minister, President, Vice President, and Governor.
