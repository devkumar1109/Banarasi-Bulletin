Uttar Pradesh Prepares for Safe, Clean Maha Shivratri

2025-02-25T15:44:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed officials to ensure comprehensive security and cleanliness measures at temples across the state for Maha Shivratri. Adityanath emphasized the need for high cleanliness standards and devotee safety during a meeting at the Gorakhnath Temple. Instructions were given to manage traffic effectively, deploy adequate police personnel, and prioritize women's security. Regulation of temporary festival shops was also included in the directives.
