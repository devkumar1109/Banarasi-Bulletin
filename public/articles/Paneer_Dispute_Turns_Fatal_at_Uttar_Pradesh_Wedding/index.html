Paneer Dispute Turns Fatal at Uttar Pradesh Wedding

2025-04-29T05:21:00+00:00

A wedding in Hamidpur, Uttar Pradesh, turned violent when a local youth drove a minibus into the mandap, injuring five people after an argument over food. The dispute began when the youth demanded more paneer and was struck on the head with a ladle by the bride's father. The groom's father and the bride's uncle are among the critically injured and are receiving treatment at a trauma center in Varanasi. The accused fled the scene and is currently absconding. Police have launched a search operation to apprehend him. The wedding ceremony was briefly halted but resumed the following day with police intervention.
