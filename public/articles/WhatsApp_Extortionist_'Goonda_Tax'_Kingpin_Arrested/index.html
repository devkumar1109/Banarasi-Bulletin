"WhatsApp Extortionist 'Goonda Tax' Kingpin Arrested"

2025-03-09T13:02:00+00:00

Brijesh Chauhan, a notorious criminal who extorted 'goonda tax' from Maharashtra-based traders through WhatsApp calls and threats, has been arrested by the UP STF from Ballia. Maharashtra Police sought the UP STF's help to apprehend Chauhan, who had multiple cases against him in Maharashtra and was evading arrest. Chauhan used to threaten businessmen with a pistol and demand money. A case was registered against him in Panvel City Police station for extortion. The UP STF, in a joint operation with Panvel City Police, arrested Chauhan near Kunwar Singh Tiraha Rampur in Ballia.
