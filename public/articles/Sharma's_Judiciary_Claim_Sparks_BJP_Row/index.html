Sharma's Judiciary Claim Sparks BJP Row

2025-04-20T12:12:00+00:00

BJP leader Dinesh Sharma sparked controversy with remarks on the judiciary, claiming the President is supreme and no one can challenge her. BJP president J P Nadda distanced the party from Sharma's statements, similar to his distancing from remarks made by BJP MP Nishikant Dubey. Sharma, a former UP Deputy CM and Lucknow Mayor, has deep RSS roots and is known as an approachable leader. He began his political career with the RSS student wing and rose through the ranks of the BJP. Sharma's political achievements include serving as Lucknow Mayor for two terms and holding positions like BJP national vice-president and Gujarat in-charge.
