Mentally Ill Woman Found Dead, Foul Play Suspected

2025-04-13T10:23:00+00:00

The body of 50-year-old Rachna, a mentally unfit woman from Dayanatpur village in Hapur district, was found in a dry canal in Hathras on Sunday. A 'dupatta' was tied around her neck, leading police to suspect foul play. Rachna, who suffered from mental illness, routinely left home every morning and returned in the evening. She went missing on Saturday and her body was discovered the following day. Police have initiated an investigation with the help of a forensics team. The body has been sent for post-mortem to determine the cause of death. Rachna is survived by her husband, a farmer, and two adult sons.
