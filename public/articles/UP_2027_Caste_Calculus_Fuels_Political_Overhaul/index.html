UP 2027: Caste Calculus Fuels Political Overhaul

2025-03-26T08:57:00+00:00

Political parties in Uttar Pradesh are preparing for the 2027 Assembly elections with organizational overhauls. The BJP, under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, has appointed 70 new district presidents, aiming for a balanced representation of castes like Brahmins, Thakurs, OBCs, and SCs. The Congress and Samajwadi Party are also restructuring their organizations to strengthen their electoral prospects. Caste alliances and grassroots mobilization are expected to play crucial roles in the upcoming election battle.
