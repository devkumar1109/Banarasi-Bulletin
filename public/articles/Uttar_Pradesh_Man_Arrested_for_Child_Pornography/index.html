Uttar Pradesh Man Arrested for Child Pornography

2025-05-17T16:05:00+00:00

A man in Uttar Pradesh has been booked for uploading child pornography online. The National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) portal, under the Home Ministry, received a complaint regarding the videos and directed the police to register a complaint. Circle Officer (CO) Abhishek Singh confirmed the development on Saturday.
