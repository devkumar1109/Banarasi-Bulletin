Honeymoon Murder: Lover Used, Mastermind Escapes

2025-06-11T23:08:00+00:00

Investigators now suspect Sonam Raghuwanshi is the mastermind behind the honeymoon murder in Meghalaya. They believe she manipulated Raj Kushwaha, her alleged lover, with promises of love, using him to commit the crime. Sonam allegedly planned to elope with someone else and Raj unknowingly facilitated her escape. She had a pre-arranged hideout in Indore, where she stayed while police searched for her. When her plan unraveled, she fled to Uttar Pradesh, where she was finally traced. Sonam's whereabouts and the address of her Indore hideout remain unknown, but Meghalaya police plan to bring her back to Indore for evidence verification.
