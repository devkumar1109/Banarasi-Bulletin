Varanasi Reacts: Unity, Mourning, and Demands for Action

2025-04-23T17:40:00+00:00

Following the Pahalgam terror attack, Varanasi witnessed protests and prayers. Kashi Vishwanath Dham hosted an Ati Rudra recital, while the BJP postponed its Waqf Amendment awareness drive. Muslim institutions like Anjuman Intejamia Masajid and the Muslim Mahila Foundation condemned the attack, expressing sympathy for the victims. A joint protest march by Vishal Bharat Sansthan and Muslim Women's Foundation culminated in a symbolic cremation of terrorists. They demanded action against Pakistan and called for religious leaders to issue a Fatwa against terrorists. The BJP Kashi region president and spokesman announced the postponement of workshops in mourning for the victims.
