Houthi Hypersonics Target Truman, Raise Alarm

2025-03-19T07:00:00+00:00

The USS Harry Truman has been targeted by Houthi forces four times in three days, raising concerns about maritime security and escalating tensions in the region. Hypersonic missiles were used in these attacks, as well as in a separate strike on an Israeli airbase, demonstrating the increasing sophistication of weaponry involved in the conflict. These incidents highlight the volatile situation and the need for global attention to prevent further escalation. The use of hypersonic missiles adds a new dimension to the conflict, emphasizing the complex geopolitical landscape.
