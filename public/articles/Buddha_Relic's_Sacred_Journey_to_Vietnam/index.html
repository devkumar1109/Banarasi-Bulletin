Buddha Relic's Sacred Journey to Vietnam

2025-05-01T09:21:00+00:00

Monks, bhikkhunis, diplomats, and the Sangha offered prayers to the Holy Buddha Relic in New Delhi before its journey to Vietnam. The relic, transported by an Indian Air Force aircraft, will be in Vietnam from May 2 to 21, visiting four cities including Ho Chi Minh City, where the United Nations Day of Vesak (UNDV) event will take place. Vietnam's Ambassador to India and Sri Lanka's Acting High Commissioner also participated in the prayers. The Indian delegation, led by Minister Kiren Rijiju, will enshrine the relic at significant sites like Thanh Tam Monastery, Ba Den Mountain, Quan Su Monastery, and Tam Chuc Monastery. This cultural exchange is facilitated by the Ministry of Culture.
