Meerut Holi Prayer Row: Case Filed Against Open Namaz

2025-03-15T08:12:00+00:00

Police in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh have filed a case regarding open namaz (Muslim prayers) performed at a private university during Holi celebrations. The incident reportedly took place despite a ban on open namaz in public places.
