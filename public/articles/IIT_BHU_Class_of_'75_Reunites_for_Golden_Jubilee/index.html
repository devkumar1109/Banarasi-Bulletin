IIT BHU Class of '75 Reunites for Golden Jubilee

2025-03-17T13:28:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) Varanasi celebrated the Golden Jubilee Reunion of its Class of 1975. Around 180 people, including 100 alumni from around the world, attended the event at the Annie Besant Lecture Theatre. The alumni reminisced about their time at the institute and their career paths. They expressed gratitude to IIT (BHU) for their education and pledged support for future students through a legacy program. Director Prof. Amit Patra and Dean Prof. Hiralal Pramanik emphasized the importance of alumni engagement. The event was coordinated by K Chandrashekhar, and a two-minute silence was observed for deceased alumni.
