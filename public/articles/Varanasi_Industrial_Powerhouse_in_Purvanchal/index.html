Varanasi: Industrial Powerhouse in Purvanchal

2025-05-26T17:07:00+00:00

Under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's leadership, Varanasi has emerged as a key industrial hub in Uttar Pradesh's Purvanchal region. Improved law and order, robust infrastructure, and initiatives like the Global Investors Summit have attracted businesses to the city. Currently, 48 industrial projects worth Rs 1,180.95 crore are operational in Varanasi, generating 3,472 jobs. An additional 48 projects worth Rs 5,702.18 crore are expected to launch soon, creating 12,110 jobs. Notable companies operating in Varanasi include Saaswad Heat Transfer and Engineering Private Limited, Hilton Hotels Group's Double Tree franchise, Amazon's contracted warehouse, and Banaras Beads, which is collaborating with Tata's Indian Hotels Company Limited to build a Ginger Budget Hotel.
