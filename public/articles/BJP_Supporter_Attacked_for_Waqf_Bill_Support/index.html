BJP Supporter Attacked for Waqf Bill Support

2025-04-04T07:38:00+00:00

An elderly Muslim man, Zahid Saifi, a BJP supporter in Uttar Pradesh's Sambhal district, was attacked outside a mosque for supporting the Waqf Amendment Bill. The attack occurred after evening prayers when Saifi defended the bill amidst criticism from others. He was allegedly beaten with sticks and a sharp weapon, injuring his ear, by three men identified as Rizwan, Naushad, and Shoaib. Saifi filed a police complaint and received medical treatment. His brother-in-law, Ashfaq Saifi, former Uttar Pradesh Minority Commission chairman, revealed that the family has received threats after the attack. The Waqf (Amendment) Bill was passed in both houses of Parliament and awaits the President's approval.
