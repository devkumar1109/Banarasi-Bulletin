JEE Main 2025: Shift 2 Easier, Math Hurdles in Shift 1

2025-04-03T04:05:00+00:00

The JEE Main 2025 Session 2 exam commenced on April 2nd. Day 2, April 3rd, saw two shifts with students reporting that math was tougher in shift 1, while both math and physics were considered easier in shift 2. Chemistry was deemed moderately challenging in both shifts. The JEE Main 2025 exam is conducted in two shifts, from 9 am to 12 pm and 3 pm to 6 pm. Admission to NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs is granted through JoSAA counselling based on JEE Main scores. The top 2.5 lakh rank holders are eligible to take the JEE Advanced exam.
