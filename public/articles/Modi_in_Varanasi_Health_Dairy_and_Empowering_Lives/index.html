Modi in Varanasi: Health, Dairy, and Empowering Lives

2025-04-11T09:08:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Varanasi and praised the impact of the Ayushman Bharat scheme, particularly on the elderly. He highlighted the Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana, which provides free treatment to citizens over 70, regardless of income. Varanasi has issued nearly 50,000 cards under this scheme. Modi also lauded the success of Banas Dairy in empowering women and transforming livelihoods in the region. He attributed India's status as the world's largest milk producer to the efforts of farmers and government initiatives in the dairy sector. These initiatives include linking livestock owners with Kisan Credit Cards, increasing loan limits, providing subsidies, and reviving cooperative societies.
