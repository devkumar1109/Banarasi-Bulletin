Jail Harassment: Daughter Seeks Euthanasia for Mother

2025-04-06T09:16:00+00:00

A woman in Varanasi has written to President Droupadi Murmu requesting permission for euthanasia. She claims her mother, a former deputy jailor, has been subjected to mental and physical harassment by the Varanasi District Jail Superintendent, Umesh Kumar Singh, for filing a complaint. The daughter alleges Singh used caste-based slurs and threatened her mother's career and family. She further states that despite several complaints, Singh remains in his position and has even been allegedly involved in exploiting female inmates. The daughter expresses fear for her mother's safety and her family's well-being, claiming that Singh will destroy them.
