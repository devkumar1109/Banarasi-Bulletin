Wrestler-COP: Chaudhary Grapples With Crime

2025-03-21T06:24:00+00:00

Sambhal CO Anuj Chaudhary is a decorated wrestler-turned-police officer. Born in Bahedi village, Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, he won two silver medals at the 2002 and 2010 National Games and two bronze medals at the Asian Championships. He received the Laxman Award in 2001 and the Arjuna Award in 2005, which helped him get recruited through the sports quota in the UP government. Chaudhary became a Deputy SP (DSP) in the 2012 batch. Known for his bold statements, he criticized misinformation during the Sambhal violence. He is recognized for his towering physique, commitment to fitness, and strong social media following.
