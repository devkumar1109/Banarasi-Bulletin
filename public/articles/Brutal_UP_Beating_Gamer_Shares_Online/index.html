Brutal UP Beating, Gamer Shares Online

2025-03-18T16:08:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Auraiya, a man was brutally beaten with belts and pipes by four men. The assault was recorded in four videos and posted as Instagram stories by one of the perpetrators, an account named "bad_jatin_8152" or "Gamer". The videos, which have since been removed, show the victim pleading for mercy while being whipped and bloodied. Police are investigating the incident, aiming to determine the cause and apprehend the accused. No complaint has been filed yet.
