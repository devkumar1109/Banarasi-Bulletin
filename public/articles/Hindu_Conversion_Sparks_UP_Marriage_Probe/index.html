Hindu Conversion Sparks U.P. Marriage Probe

2025-04-10T02:04:00+00:00

A 30-year-old mother of three, previously known as Shabnam, converted to Hinduism and married a 12th-grade student in Amroha district, Uttar Pradesh. She goes by Shivani now and has been married twice before, her previous marriages ending in divorce. She first married a man in Meerut, then Taufiq, who became disabled after an accident. Shivani recently divorced Taufiq and initiated a relationship with the 18-year-old student. Police are reviewing the circumstances of the marriage, as Uttar Pradesh has an anti-conversion law. The student's father supports the union.
