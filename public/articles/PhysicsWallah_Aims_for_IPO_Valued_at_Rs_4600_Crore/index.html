PhysicsWallah Aims for IPO, Valued at Rs 4,600 Crore

2025-03-19T05:04:00+00:00

PhysicsWallah (PW), an edtech unicorn founded by Alakh Pandey, is preparing for an Initial Public Offering (IPO) to raise Rs 4,600 crore. The company, known for its YouTube channel with 4.6 crore subscribers and over 55 lakh paid students, secretly filed documents with SEBI. Pandey, who started teaching in Class 8 to support his family, built PhysicsWallah after struggling financially and even seeing his parents sell their home. The company operates offline centers in 105 cities and employs over 14,000 people.
