Vedic Scholar Shastri Receives Padma Shri

2025-04-29T19:22:00+00:00

Varanasi-based Vedic scholar Dravid Ganeshwar Shastri was awarded the Padma Shri by President Droupadi Murmu. Shastri, known for determining auspicious timings for significant events like the Ram Lalla consecration in Ayodhya and Kashi Vishwanath Temple Corridor in Varanasi, is a self-taught scholar who received his education from a traditional Gurukul. He possesses extensive knowledge of various Vedic texts, scriptures, and fields like astrology, jurisprudence, and Ayurveda. Shastri has dedicated his life to promoting Vedic knowledge and practices, serving as an examiner for the Shri Girvanvagvardhini Sabha, and providing evidence for the preservation of the Ram Setu. He has received several accolades, including 'Jyotish-Vidya-Ratnam' from Shri Kanchi Kamakoti Peetham and 'Manikya-Gauravam' from Shri Manikya Prabhu Sansthanam.
