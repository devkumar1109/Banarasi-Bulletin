UP Lawlessness: Akhilesh Blasts BJP

2025-04-13T20:01:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party president Akhilesh Yadav criticized the BJP government in Uttar Pradesh, accusing it of failing to maintain law and order. He claimed that criminals are operating freely with the government's tacit approval, leading to an increase in crime like murder, loot, and rape. Yadav cited the alleged attack on Samajwadi Party functionary Harish Mishra in Varanasi as an example of the deteriorating law and order situation. He alleged that the police are under pressure from those in power and are unable to protect the public. Yadav asserted that the BJP's "zero-tolerance policy" on crime is ineffective and that Samajwadi Party workers are prepared to confront these challenges.
