Modi in Varanasi: Healthcare, Women, and Dairy Triumphs

2025-04-11T09:08:00+00:00

During his Varanasi visit, PM Modi praised the Ayushman Bharat scheme, particularly its impact on elderly healthcare. He emphasized the Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana, which provides free treatment to citizens aged 70 and above, regardless of income. Varanasi has distributed the highest number of these cards, with nearly 50,000 issued. Modi also lauded Banas Dairy for empowering women and transforming livelihoods in Kashi. He highlighted its role in making many women financially independent. He recognized India's position as the world's largest milk producer, attributing it to government initiatives like linking livestock owners with Kisan Credit Cards and increasing loan limits.
