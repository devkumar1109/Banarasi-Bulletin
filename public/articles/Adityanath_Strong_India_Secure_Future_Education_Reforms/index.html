Adityanath: Strong India, Secure Future, Education Reforms

2025-05-09T08:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath hailed Operation Sindoor, describing it as a demonstration of a strong and confident India. He emphasized that India won't tolerate threats to its security and will respond decisively. During a ceremony in Lucknow, Adityanath distributed appointment letters to 494 assistant teachers and 49 lecturers, highlighting the transparent recruitment process. He urged educators to adapt to changing times and use innovative teaching methods to engage students. He also laid the foundation stone for mini stadiums in 23 government inter colleges and recognized schools for setting up Atal Tinkering Labs and ICT labs. Adityanath praised the reforms in secondary education since 2017, citing the end of widespread cheating and the success of initiatives like Operation Kayakalp and Project Alankar.
