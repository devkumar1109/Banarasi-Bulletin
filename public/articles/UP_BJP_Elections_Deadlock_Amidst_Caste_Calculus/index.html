UP BJP Elections: Deadlock Amidst Caste Calculus

2025-03-14T04:54:00+00:00

The BJP's organisational elections in Uttar Pradesh are in a deadlock. District president elections, a prerequisite for the state presidential election, are yet to be completed in the state. The party faces challenges with numerous contenders and is carefully considering caste equations for representation. While Bihar has re-elected its state president, the process in UP, Madhya Pradesh, and West Bengal remains incomplete. The BJP aimed to finish its National President election by January 21st, requiring 50% of state organisational elections to be concluded, including key states like UP.
