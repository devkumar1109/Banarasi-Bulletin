Ganga Expressway: Adityanath Drives Progress

2025-04-27T15:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the under-construction Ganga Expressway and directed officials to complete it on time without compromising quality. The 594-km expressway, connecting Meerut to Prayagraj, is expected to boost economic activities. Adityanath visited construction sites in Hardoi, Shahjahanpur, and Hapur, traveling five kilometers on the expressway to assess road quality. He was briefed about the use of advanced German technology and praised the implementation of innovative methods. The expressway is divided into four groups, with the Adani Group constructing three and IRB Infrastructure Developers constructing one under the PPP model. An airshow is scheduled for May 2 and 3 at a 3.5 km-long airstrip being constructed on the expressway.
