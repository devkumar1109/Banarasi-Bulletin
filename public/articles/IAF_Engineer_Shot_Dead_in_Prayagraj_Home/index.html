IAF Engineer Shot Dead in Prayagraj Home

2025-03-29T12:14:00+00:00

Satya Narayan Mishra, a 51-year-old Chief Work Engineer of the Indian Air Force (IAF), was shot dead at his residence in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred at his residence in the Engineer Colony inside the Air Force Station in Bamrauli area. An unidentified youth entered Mishra's residence and shot him through the window. Mishra's wife and son discovered him injured and rushed him to the hospital, but he was pronounced dead. Police, including SOG, forensic, and surveillance teams, are investigating the incident. They are examining CCTV footage from inside and outside the Air Force premises and reviewing security arrangements. The motive behind the murder remains unknown.
