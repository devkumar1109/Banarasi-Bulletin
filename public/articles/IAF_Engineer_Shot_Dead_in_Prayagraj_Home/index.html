IAF Engineer Shot Dead in Prayagraj Home

2025-03-29T06:52:00+00:00

An Indian Air Force civil engineer, SN Mishra (51), was shot dead at his residence in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. An unknown assailant fired at him through his window while he was sleeping. Mishra sustained a chest injury and was transported to the Army hospital, where he succumbed to his wounds. Police are investigating the incident and will register a case after receiving a complaint from Mishra's family. Mishra is survived by his wife, son, and daughter.
