Goat Dispute Turns Deadly in Ballia

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

In Ballia district, a 30-year-old man, Awadhesh Kumar, was beaten to death by two juvenile boys from his neighboring family. The incident stemmed from a dispute over Kumar's goat eating food grains near the neighbor's doorstep. Kumar was attacked with sticks in a field after an altercation. He was taken to the district hospital but was declared dead. Police recovered the sticks used in the attack and apprehended the accused near a railway crossing. The juveniles have been produced before the juvenile justice board.
