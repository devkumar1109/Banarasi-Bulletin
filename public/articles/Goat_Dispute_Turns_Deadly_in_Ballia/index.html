Goat Dispute Turns Deadly in Ballia

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

In Ballia district, a 30-year-old man named Awadhesh Kumar was beaten to death by two adolescent boys from his neighboring family. The dispute arose when Kumar's goat entered the neighbor's house and started eating grains. An argument ensued, escalating to a physical attack in a field with sticks. Kumar was left unconscious and died at the district hospital. Police have recovered the sticks used in the attack and apprehended the juvenile suspects, who were presented before the juvenile justice board.
