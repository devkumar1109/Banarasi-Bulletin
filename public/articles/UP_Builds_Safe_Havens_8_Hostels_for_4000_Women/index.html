UP Builds Safe Havens: 8 Hostels for 4,000 Women.

2025-04-16T19:07:00+00:00

Eight women hostels are being constructed in Uttar Pradesh under the SASCI scheme. Each hostel will accommodate up to 500 women and will be located in Lucknow, Gautam Budh Nagar, and Ghaziabad. The Centre allocated Rs 381.56 crore for the project, with the first instalment of Rs 251.8296 crore already released. The state finance department transferred the funds to the women welfare department. A construction agency has been assigned to the project, and work is expected to begin soon. These hostels aim to provide safe, accessible, and affordable accommodation for working women in the state.
