Uttar Pradesh: Aiming for Sports Goods Glory

2025-04-08T17:10:00+00:00

The FICCI Sports Committee - Uttar Pradesh Chapter held its debut event, a Round Table Discussion on the Sports Goods Manufacturing Industry of Uttar Pradesh, at IMT Ghaziabad on April 8, 2025. The event brought together over 20 sports manufacturers, policymakers, athletes, and academics to discuss challenges and opportunities for the industry. Kanishka Pandey, Chair of the UP Chapter, emphasized the need for comprehensive policy frameworks, research-backed advocacy, and global competitiveness. Notable participants included Padma Shri Ashok Dhyanchand, Rachna Govil, former Executive Director of the Sports Authority of India, and representatives from brands like SG and BDM. The Round Table Discussion aimed to position Uttar Pradesh as a global hub for sports goods manufacturing.
