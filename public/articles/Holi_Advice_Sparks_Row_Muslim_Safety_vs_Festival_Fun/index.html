Holi Advice Sparks Row: Muslim Safety vs. Festival Fun

2025-03-06T15:54:00+00:00

A police official in Uttar Pradesh's Sambhal sparked controversy by advising Muslims to stay indoors on Holi if they feel uncomfortable with colors. Circle Officer Anuj Chaudhary equated the significance of Holi for Hindus to Eid for Muslims, stating that both communities should respect each other. His remarks, made at a peace committee meeting, drew criticism from the Samajwadi Party and Congress, who accused him of acting as a BJP agent. Chaudhary urged Muslims to either stay home or be understanding of Holi traditions, highlighting that Holi is celebrated once a year while Friday prayers occur 52 times annually. The meeting aimed to ensure communal harmony during the festival, which coincides with Friday prayers during Ramzan.
