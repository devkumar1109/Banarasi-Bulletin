Sarees Salute "Operation Sindoor"

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers created special sarees inspired by the Indian Air Force (IAF) to honor 'Operation Sindoor,' a recent military operation. The sarees feature imagery of weapons like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale, showcasing the strength and capability of the Indian armed forces. This initiative highlights the weavers' dedication to honoring India's military and their creativity in blending tradition with contemporary themes.
