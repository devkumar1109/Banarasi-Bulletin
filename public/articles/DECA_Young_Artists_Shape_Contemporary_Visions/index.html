DECA: Young Artists Shape Contemporary Visions

2025-04-15T18:43:00+00:00

To mark World Art Day, 10 young artists from Banaras Hindu University's Faculty of Visual Arts are showcasing their work in an exhibition titled "DECA." The three-day exhibition, beginning April 16th at the Ahivasi Art Gallery, explores contemporary themes like human relationships, identity, and socio-cultural changes. Each artist brings a unique perspective shaped by their experiences, reflecting both individual artistry and the strength of collaboration. Curated by Suresh Chandra Jangid, the exhibition highlights influences from traditional Indian art, abstract symbolism, and environmental consciousness.
