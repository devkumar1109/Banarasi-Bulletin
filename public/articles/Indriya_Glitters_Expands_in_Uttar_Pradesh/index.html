Indriya Glitters, Expands in Uttar Pradesh

2025-04-06T13:03:00+00:00

Aditya Birla Group's jewellery brand, Indriya, opened its first store in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, as part of its expansion strategy in the state. The brand plans to reach 21 stores across 10 Indian cities, including Kanpur and Noida. CEO Sandeep Kohli cited Uttar Pradesh's large population and cultural affinity for jewellery as key factors for the expansion. Indriya currently operates in 10 cities, including Delhi, Mumbai, and Hyderabad, and aims to capitalize on the predicted growth of the Indian jewellery market to USD 80-100 billion.
