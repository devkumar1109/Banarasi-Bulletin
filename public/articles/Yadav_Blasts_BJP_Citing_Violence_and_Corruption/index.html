Yadav Blasts BJP, Citing Violence and Corruption

2025-04-15T18:46:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party president Akhilesh Yadav advised party workers to avoid controversial statements, citing recent events like the Karni Sena's display in Agra. He criticized the BJP, drawing parallels to Hitler's regime and calling Karni Sena their "troopers." Akhilesh also addressed the Yogi Adityanath government's claims of zero tolerance against crime, highlighting the deaths of a Dalit youth in police custody and a Brahmin in Basti, alleging the government prioritizes protecting the accused over victims. He criticized the BJP's handling of investments and development, accusing them of prioritizing commissions over incentives and highlighting alleged corruption within the government.
