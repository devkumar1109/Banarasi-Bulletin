IIT Dreams: JEE Advanced 2025 Results Near

2025-05-20T06:03:00+00:00

The JEE Advanced 2025 exam, conducted by IIT Kanpur on May 18th, saw lakhs of aspirants vying for seats at prestigious IITs. Results, including All India Rank (AIR), will be released on June 2nd. Students with ranks between 10,000 and 30,000 can secure admission to various IITs and courses. IIT BHU offers programs like Bachelor of Architecture, Ceramic Engineering (Dual Degree), and Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology. IIT Dhanbad provides options in Applied Geology, Environmental Engineering, and Mining Machinery Engineering. IIT Bhilai offers Materials Science and Metallurgical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Mechatronics Engineering. IIT Bhubaneswar offers programs in Civil Engineering, Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering with M.Tech. specializations. Students meeting the cutoff must register for JoSAA counselling, where seat allocation is based on choices and JEE Advanced AIR.
