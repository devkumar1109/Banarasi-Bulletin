Kumbh Mela Stampede: Blame Game Amidst Tragedy

2025-02-25T14:25:00+00:00

A stampede at the Maha Kumbh Mela resulted in 30 deaths. Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath defended the state's arrangements, accusing the previous SP government of neglect while stating that everyone who sought something at the gathering found it. He used controversial language, saying "vultures got only dead bodies" and "pigs got dirt." Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav criticized Adityanath's words, calling them inappropriate and accusing the government of exploiting the event for political gain. Yadav urged people to respond with sympathy rather than anger to Adityanath's remarks.
