Rampur Shocker: Man Arrested for Gruesome Assault

2025-04-17T07:51:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man was arrested in an encounter by Uttar Pradesh Police for the alleged sexual assault of an 11-year-old deaf-mute Dalit girl in Rampur district. The victim suffered multiple injuries, including burn marks on her private parts allegedly caused by cigarette butts. Police said she was attacked after leaving her home with her sister to collect cow dung. The accused was identified through CCTV footage and fired at police during his arrest, resulting in a leg injury. He is currently hospitalized under police custody. The victim was first treated at a primary health center and later transferred to Meerut district hospital.
