Engineer Found Dead in Lucknow Dam, Congress Blames Lawlessness

2025-03-28T06:02:00+00:00

Vivek Kumar Soni, a Public Works Department (PWD) assistant engineer in Lucknow, was found dead in Indira Dam on Thursday. Soni had been missing for two days. His wife filed a missing person's report on Tuesday after he left home and did not return. Police found Soni's motorcycle near the Indira Canal on Wednesday and recovered his body after a search aided by the SDRF. The body has been sent for post-mortem. The Uttar Pradesh Congress criticized the state's law and order situation, stating that Soni's death highlights a lack of safety for citizens.
