Uttarakhand Chopper Crash Claims Seven Lives

2025-05-08T16:02:00+00:00

A private helicopter crashed near Gangnani in Uttarkashi district, Uttarakhand, killing six tourists and the pilot. The helicopter, carrying seven people, departed from Dehradun for the Gangotri shrine but crashed while en route. The deceased have been identified as Vijayalakshmi Reddy, Robin Singh, Radha Agarwal, Ruchi Agarwal, Kala Chandrakant Soni, and Vedanti. Maktoor Bhaskar, the sole survivor, sustained serious injuries and was airlifted to AIIMS Rishikesh. The helicopter fell into a deep gorge, prompting a rescue operation by the SDRF and police. Chief Minister Pushkar Singh Dhami ordered an investigation into the incident. The crash occurred amidst a weather warning for Uttarakhand, with light to moderate rain, lightning, and strong winds forecast for the region.
