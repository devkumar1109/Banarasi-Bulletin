Girl, Books, and Bulldozers: UP Demolition Sparks Outrage

2025-04-02T12:50:00+00:00

A viral video from Uttar Pradesh shows eight-year-old Ananya Yadav running with her schoolbag as bulldozers demolished shanties in Ambedkar Nagar. Ananya's family had lived on the land for decades, but the administration, despite an ongoing legal battle, proceeded with the demolition. The video captures Ananya's desperate attempt to save her books as a nearby shed catches fire. The incident sparked a political firestorm, with Ananya's image becoming a symbol of resilience against bureaucratic injustice. The Supreme Court has reportedly taken notice of the incident.
