UP Tightens Grip: Adityanath on "Urban Naxals" and Security

2025-05-12T23:45:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath directed officials to maintain heightened security and take strict action against "urban naxals" following Operation Sindoor. He emphasized a crackdown on cow smuggling and mining in Varanasi zone, ordering the auction of seized vehicles from smugglers. Adityanath praised Prime Minister Modi's address, calling Operation Sindoor a pledge to protect women's dignity. He stated India will no longer remain silent against terrorism, vowing to retaliate on its own terms. During his Varanasi visit, Adityanath ordered increased CCTV installation and verification of suspicious individuals. He also instructed officials to complete ongoing development projects within the timeframe while ensuring quality standards.
