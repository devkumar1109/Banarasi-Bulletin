Varanasi Rape Allegation: Twisted Tales and Counterclaims

2025-04-17T11:08:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi alleged she was raped by 23 men between March 29 and April 4. 14 individuals have been arrested in connection with the case. The accused families counterclaim that the victim extorted money from them and presented videos as proof. They shared videos allegedly showing the woman in "questionable postures" at ghats and public locations between March 29 and April 4. The families also allege the victim did not initially report the alleged rapes to the police. A 6-member SIT led by a DCP rank officer is investigating the case.
