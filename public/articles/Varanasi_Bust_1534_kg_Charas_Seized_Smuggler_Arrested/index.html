Varanasi Bust: 1.534 kg Charas Seized, Smuggler Arrested

2025-03-12T19:02:00+00:00

In Varanasi, 1.534 kg of charas worth Rs 7.5 lakh was seized. Alok Raj Chaubey, a 20-year-old from Buxar, Bihar, was arrested for smuggling the narcotics from Nepal. Chaubey admitted to selling the charas to local peddlers and profiting from the trade for an extended period.
