Ganga Ghats Now Fee-Based for Events

2025-03-25T18:28:00+00:00

The Varanasi Municipal Corporation will now charge organizers ₹880 per square meter for events held on the Ganga ghats. Previously, permission was granted for free. Organizers must apply for permission through the Smart Kashi app at least 15 days in advance, providing photographs of the event location and detailed event information. Applications will be reviewed by a zonal officer. This new system will be implemented in a few days.
