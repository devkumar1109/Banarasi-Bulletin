BJP Presidential Race: Holi to Bengaluru, South in Focus

2025-03-07T11:00:00+00:00

The BJP is set to elect a new national president after Holi and before the RSS's key meeting in Bengaluru. The party needs to complete organizational elections in 50% of the states before proceeding with the national president election. So far, elections have been held in 13 out of the required 18 states. Key states like Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and West Bengal are facing political challenges that have slowed down the election process. Speculation is rife about potential candidates, particularly from southern India, but the party's focus is currently on completing the organizational elections in the remaining states.
