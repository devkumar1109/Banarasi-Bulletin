Harmony over Holi: UP Minister Calls for Unity

2025-03-08T12:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister Gulab Devi called for unity and respect among communities amid controversy over a circle officer's remarks comparing Holi and Jumma namaz. Devi defended the officer's right to individual opinion while emphasizing peaceful celebrations for all. She highlighted national leaders promoting a harmonious environment, suggesting they uphold Ram Rajya. The circle officer aimed to ensure communal harmony by stressing the unique nature of each festival. Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav criticized the divisiveness of such statements. Authorities are preparing for peaceful celebrations.
