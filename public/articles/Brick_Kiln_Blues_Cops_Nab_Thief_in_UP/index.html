Brick Kiln Blues: Cops Nab Thief in UP

2025-02-27T07:58:00+00:00

In August 2023, a Dombivli resident reported a house break-in where gold and silver jewelry worth Rs 21.26 lakh was stolen. Police identified Rajesh Rajbhar, a suspected history-sheeter, as the prime accused. Rajbhar was traced to a village in Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh, where he was hiding. Three policemen from Dombivli traveled to UP and posed as brick kiln workers to gather information about Rajbhar's whereabouts. Over 10 days, they interacted with locals and eventually apprehended him. Rajbhar is accused in over 20 theft cases in the Mumbai suburbs and had previously been arrested and granted bail.
