Midwest Ski Gems: Minnesota Makes the Cut

2025-02-28T15:53:00+00:00

Travel + Leisure's 2024 list of the top Midwest ski resorts includes two Minnesota locations: Giants Ridge Recreation Area in Biwabik and Afton Alps in Hastings. The list also features resorts from Wisconsin, Michigan, Illinois, Indiana, Ohio, and Iowa. Other notable Minnesota ski resorts not on the list include Lutsen and Spirit Mountain. Minnesota has nineteen ski areas that are part of the Minnesota Ski Areas Association.
