Bank Manager Foils Supreme Court Scam on Retired Professor

2025-05-22T19:15:00+00:00

A retired BHU professor, Dr. PR Gupta, was saved from falling victim to a cyber fraud by a vigilant bank manager in Varanasi. Fraudsters impersonated Supreme Court officials, pressuring Dr. Gupta to transfer money to settle a fabricated bank fraud case. The bank manager, noticing Dr. Gupta's distress, contacted the police. Inspector Lanka Shivakant Mishra and the cybercrime cell intervened, preventing the transfer and educating Dr. Gupta about the scam. Dr. Gupta later released a video expressing his gratitude to the police and bank officials for their timely assistance.
