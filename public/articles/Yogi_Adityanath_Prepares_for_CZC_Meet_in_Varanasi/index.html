Yogi Adityanath Prepares for CZC Meet in Varanasi

2025-06-17T14:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is in Varanasi ahead of the Central Zonal Council (CZC) meet on June 24th. The meet will be chaired by Union Home Minister Amit Shah and attended by Chief Ministers of Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Uttarakhand and Uttar Pradesh: Mohan Yadav, Vishnu dev Sai, Pushkar Singh Dhami, and Adityanath respectively. The meeting will be held at Varanasi and is being prepared for by senior officials. Jal Shakti Minister Swatantra Dev Singh, Labour and Employment Minister Anil Rajbhar, and Ministers of State Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra 'Dayalu' accompanied Adityanath during his visit and inspection of the venue.
