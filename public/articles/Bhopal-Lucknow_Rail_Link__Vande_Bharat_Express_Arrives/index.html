Bhopal-Lucknow Rail Link: Vande Bharat Express Arrives

2025-04-16T15:41:00+00:00

The Indian Railways is expected to launch a new Vande Bharat Express train connecting Bhopal (Madhya Pradesh) and Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh). This high-speed train will reduce travel time from 10-12 hours to 6-8 hours, covering a distance of 720 kilometers. The train is likely to have eight chair car coaches with 564 seats, including executive class seating. Ticket fares are expected to be significantly higher than regular trains. The exact route map, stoppage details, and launch date are yet to be announced by the Indian Railways. If launched, this will be Bhopal's second Vande Bharat Express, with the other connecting to Mumbai and Patna.
