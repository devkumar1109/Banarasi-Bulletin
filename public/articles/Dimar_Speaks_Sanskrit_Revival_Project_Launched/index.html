Dimar Speaks Sanskrit: Revival Project Launched

2025-03-26T04:47:00+00:00

Uttarakhand's Dimar village, a historic Sanskrit learning center, is launching a project to make Sanskrit a spoken language. The State Sanskrit Academy will deploy trainers in April to encourage locals to use Sanskrit in daily conversations. Dimar, designated as an 'Aadarsh Sanskrit Village', already has a Sanskrit school established in 1910. This initiative is part of a larger project to promote spoken Sanskrit in one village from each of Uttarakhand's 13 districts. The academy will provide learning materials and collaborate with scholars to ensure success. Residents of Dimar welcome the project, believing it will revive their linguistic heritage and encourage everyday interactions in Sanskrit.
