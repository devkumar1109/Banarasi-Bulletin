UP 2027: Caste Calculus Shakes Political Landscape

2025-03-26T08:57:00+00:00

The 2027 Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections are approaching, and political parties are making strategic moves to secure victory. The BJP, currently in power, is restructuring its organization by appointing 70 new district presidents, with a focus on caste representation to ensure a balanced mix of Brahmins, Thakurs, OBCs, and SCs. The Congress and Samajwadi Party (SP) are also undergoing overhauls to strengthen their electoral prospects and challenge the BJP's dominance. These parties are prioritizing new leadership, caste alliances, and grassroots mobilization to appeal to a wider voter base.
