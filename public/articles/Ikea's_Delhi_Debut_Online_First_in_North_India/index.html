Ikea's Delhi Debut: Online First in North India

2025-03-03T03:02:00+00:00

Ikea launched its online services in Delhi-NCR and nine satellite cities on March 1st, marking its long-awaited entry into North India. The retailer is prioritizing e-commerce over physical stores in India, a departure from its usual strategy. This allows Ikea to test market demand and consumer preferences before establishing physical stores. The move is driven by India's large and digitally active consumer base. Ikea's online launch was preceded by a social media campaign that engaged consumers and built anticipation. The campaign included Metro wraps resembling Ikea furniture boxes and billboards featuring consumer comments.
