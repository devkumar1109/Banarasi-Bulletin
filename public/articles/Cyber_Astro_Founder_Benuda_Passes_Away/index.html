Cyber Astro Founder Benuda Passes Away

2025-05-20T05:38:00+00:00

Satrajit Majumdar, known as Benuda, founder of Cyber Astro (a subsidiary of CyberMedia), passed away recently. Dr JN Pandey, a former colleague, recalls meeting Benuda in 1997 at BHU where he was seeking an astrologer to join his venture. Impressed by Benuda's vision, Pandey left BHU to join Cyber Astro in Delhi. There, Benuda introduced Pandey to Pradeep Gupta, Chairman of CyberMedia Group. The trio collaborated on Cyber Astro's future direction. Pandey describes Benuda as a mentor and guiding force throughout his career.
