BJP Leader Poisoned to Death in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-11T03:51:00+00:00

Gulfaam Singh Yadav, a 60-year-old BJP leader and former candidate against Mulayam Singh Yadav in the 2004 Gunnaur Assembly bypoll, was poisoned to death in Sambhal, Uttar Pradesh. Two unidentified men on a motorcycle approached Yadav at his residence, injected him with a poisonous substance, and fled. Yadav was taken to a hospital but died en route to Aligarh. Circle Officer Deepak Tiwari confirmed the incident and stated that a police team has been deployed to investigate. No formal complaint has been filed by Yadav's family yet.
