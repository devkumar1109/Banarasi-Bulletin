Preity Zinta: Family, Faith, and IPL Dreams

2025-04-29T09:31:00+00:00

Preity Zinta revealed she discussed children with Virat Kohli after a recent RCB vs. PBKS IPL match. She mentioned showing each other pictures and talking about their children. Zinta also addressed speculation about her joining the BJP, stating visiting temples and the Maha Kumbh doesn't equate to a political entry. She emphasized her pride in her Indian heritage and explained that she and her husband are raising their children as Hindus. Zinta expressed frustration with the politicization of her religious choices.
