Varanasi's Urban Dream Takes Shape

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi will see its first urban township project, spanning 150 acres, near the newly constructed international cricket stadium at Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) approved the project at its recent meeting. The stadium, with a 30,000 seating capacity, is expected to be completed by the end of 2025 and host an international cricket match in 2026. The VDA also inaugurated a new record room with a capacity for 80,000 files, expanding its storage capacity to 255,266 files. A citizen app and complaint management system portal were launched, allowing citizens to register complaints easily. The board meeting also approved the construction of a main access road between Transport Nagar Scheme and NH-2, and awarded RITES Ltd the contract for DPR, design, and construction supervision of VDA projects. Amendments to the Building Construction and Development Bylaws-2008 were also approved.
