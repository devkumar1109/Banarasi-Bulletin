Reporting on Caste: Uncovering India's Hidden Hierarchy

2025-05-27T09:46:00+00:00

This guide from the Global Investigative Journalism Network (GIJN) focuses on understanding and reporting on caste issues in India and beyond. The caste system, a hierarchical social structure dating back over 3,000 years, still significantly impacts individuals' lives in India, Bangladesh, and Pakistan. Understanding caste is crucial for understanding socioeconomic dynamics and addressing systemic inequalities in these countries. The guide highlights that while the caste system is most visible among Hindus, it exists across all religions in India. It also notes that castes are not static and have evolved over time. Journalists are advised that understanding the caste system can provide valuable insight into individuals' social backgrounds, politics, economic standing, and viewpoints. The guide offers practical advice on effectively reporting caste-related stories.
