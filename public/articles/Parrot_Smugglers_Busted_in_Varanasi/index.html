Parrot Smugglers Busted in Varanasi

2025-06-01T19:39:00+00:00

Forest officials in Varanasi arrested three smugglers and seized a bus containing 28 live parrots being illegally transported from Jharkhand. The smugglers, identified as bus driver Dhananjay Singh, conductor Abhay Singh alias Kanhaiya, and Md Arif, were apprehended in the Andharapul area. The parrots are protected under the Wildlife Protection Act of 1972, and legal proceedings are underway against the accused under various sections of the Act. Forest Conservator Ravi Kumar Singh urged citizens to report any instances of illegal wildlife trade to the designated phone numbers.
