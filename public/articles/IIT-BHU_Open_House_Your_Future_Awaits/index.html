IIT-BHU Open House: Your Future Awaits

2025-06-06T15:13:00+00:00

IIT-BHU will host an online open house session for JEE (Advanced) 2025 qualifiers on June 11 from 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM. The session, chaired by IIT-BHU director Prof Amit Patra, will feature key academic and administrative leaders. Prospective students can interact with the leadership team, gain insights into the admission process, hostel facilities, campus security, and other aspects of life at IIT-BHU. Registration is mandatory and closes on June 10 at 6 PM. Registered candidates will receive access to the online session via Google Meet on June 11.
