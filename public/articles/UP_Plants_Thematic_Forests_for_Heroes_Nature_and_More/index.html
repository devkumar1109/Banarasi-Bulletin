UP Plants Thematic Forests for Heroes, Nature, and More

2025-05-21T12:14:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is launching thematic forests across the state. 'Shaurya Van' will honor martyrs, 'Atal Van' will commemorate Atal Bihari Vajpayee, and 'Ekta Van' will mark Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel's 150th birth anniversary. 'Eklavya Van' will honor Birsa Munda, 'Oxy Van' will combat pollution, 'Gopal Van' will support cow shelters, and 'Triveni Van' will feature sacred trees near the Ganga and Yamuna rivers. The initiative also includes 'Ek Ped Maa Ke Naam 2.0' to encourage tree plantation in honor of mothers.
