Drunken Ride Home on a CBI Bike

2025-03-06T02:29:00+00:00

A tipsy man in Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Nagar, Uttar Pradesh, mistakenly rode home on a CBI officer's motorbike after a night of drinking. The officer, investigating a railway exam paper leak, realized his bike was missing and CCTV footage showed the man taking it. The man returned the bike the next day with an apology, and no charges were pressed. The CBI officer was more focused on his team's success in arresting 26 suspects and seizing Rs 1.2 crore in the paper leak case.
