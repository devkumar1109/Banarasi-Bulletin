Uttar Pradesh: Safe Haven for Elderly Transgender Citizens

2025-04-30T13:51:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has extended old-age home facilities to transgender individuals aged 60 and above. This initiative aims to provide shelter and ensure a dignified and secure life for these elderly members of the transgender community. The government stated its commitment to caring for the well-being of all its citizens, including the transgender population.
