UP Assembly Concludes Session, Adjourned Indefinitely

2025-03-05T13:21:00+00:00

The first legislative session of 2025 in Uttar Pradesh concluded on Wednesday, adjourned indefinitely after 72 hours and 56 minutes. The Assembly conducted ten sub-sessions with 69 hours and 20 minutes dedicated to legislative activities and 3 hours and 36 minutes of adjournments. Assembly Speaker Satish Mahana thanked party leaders, while Parliamentary Affairs Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna urged members to fulfill promises made during the session. Leader of Opposition Mata Prasad Pandey praised the role of technology in improving legislative processes.
