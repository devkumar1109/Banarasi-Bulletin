Varanasi Airport Halted by Bomb Threat, Passenger Arrested

2025-04-27T10:28:00+00:00

A bomb threat prompted a three-hour delay at Varanasi Airport on Sunday. The threat was made on a flight scheduled to depart for Bangalore. Authorities conducted a thorough investigation, searching the airport runway and the aircraft. A Canadian-origin passenger was arrested in connection with the threat, having allegedly claimed there was a bomb in their bag. Security was heightened at the airport following the recent Pahalgam terror attack. After the all-clear was given, the flight departed for Bangalore. Police are investigating the motive behind the bomb threat.
