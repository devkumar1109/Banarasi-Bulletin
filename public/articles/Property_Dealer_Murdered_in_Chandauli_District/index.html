Property Dealer Murdered in Chandauli District

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29), from Ghazipur district was murdered in Chandauli district on Thursday night. Pawan, who was constructing a house, was attacked by unidentified assailants while transporting water from his in-laws' residence to the construction site. He sustained severe injuries and was declared dead at the hospital. Chandauli police have initiated an investigation into the case.
