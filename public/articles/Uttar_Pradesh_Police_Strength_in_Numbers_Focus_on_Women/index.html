Uttar Pradesh Police: Strength in Numbers, Focus on Women

2025-06-14T19:49:00+00:00

Since 2017, the Uttar Pradesh Police has recruited over 2.18 lakh personnel, significantly increasing its strength to nearly four lakh, including around 36,000 women personnel. The recent recruitment of 60,244 constables highlights the government's commitment to modernizing the force. Major recruitment drives occurred in 2020 with 49,568 personnel joining, including 5,966 women, and in 2024 with 8,362 sub-inspectors and equivalent ranks, including 1,618 women sub-inspectors. UP Police also made history with the largest-ever sports quota recruitment in 2024. A new recruitment drive is planned to fill 28,138 vacancies, including 4,543 sub-inspector and 22,053 constable positions, further bolstering field-level policing in both urban and rural areas.
