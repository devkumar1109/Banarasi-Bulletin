Varanasi Division Green: 1.64 Crore Saplings to be Planted

2025-05-14T23:44:00+00:00

A mega plantation drive will commence in July in the Varanasi division, aiming to plant 1.64 crore saplings across Varanasi, Ghazipur, Chandauli, and Jaunpur districts. The forest department has allocated 280,000 saplings for Varanasi, 1,050,000 for Ghazipur, 1,455,400 for Jaunpur, and 3,640,600 for Chandauli. Other departments, including rural development, Panchayati Raj, and agriculture, have also been assigned targets for planting a total of 10,397,520 saplings. Teak, rosewood, mango, mahua, neem, and drumstick saplings will be distributed to farmers and producer groups. Urban development, housing development, and other departments will plant shade and ornamental trees in urban areas, parks, and vacant spaces. The horticulture department will focus on planting Langra mango saplings.
