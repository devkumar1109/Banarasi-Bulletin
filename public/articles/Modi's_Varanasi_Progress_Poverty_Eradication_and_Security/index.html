Modi's Varanasi: Progress, Poverty Eradication, and Security

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

In his 11 years as MP for Varanasi, Prime Minister Narendra Modi transformed service into commitment, good governance into culture, and security into a top priority, according to UP Deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya. He claims 27 crore people rose above the poverty line, receiving basic amenities like food, housing, and healthcare. Maurya criticized opposition leaders Rahul Gandhi and Akhilesh Yadav, accusing them of anti-national tendencies. He predicts the Samajwadi Party's demise due to public rejection of Akhilesh Yadav's politics. Maurya highlighted India's progress in various sectors, including security, economy, technology, infrastructure, and space science. He noted India's self-reliance in defense with indigenous weapons like S-400, BrahMos, and Tejas, and strengthened border security through operations like Sindhu.
