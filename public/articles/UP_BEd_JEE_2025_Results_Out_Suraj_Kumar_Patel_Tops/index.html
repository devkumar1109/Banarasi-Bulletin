UP BEd JEE 2025 Results Out, Suraj Kumar Patel Tops

2025-06-18T02:32:00+00:00

The UP BEd JEE Result 2025 has been declared by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi on June 18, 2025. Candidates can check their results on the official website, bujhansi.ac.in. A total of 3,44,546 candidates registered for the exam, held on June 1st with an 89% turnout. The exam was conducted in 751 centers across 69 districts of Uttar Pradesh. The UP BEd JEE Result 2025 was announced by UP’s Higher Education Minister Yogendra Upadhyay and Minister of State for Higher Education Rajni Tiwari. The exam authority used 12,000 CCTV cameras and 3,600 biometric devices to ensure transparency and prevent malpractice. Suraj Kumar Patel topped the exam with a score of 362.662. The list of top 10 rank holders and their scores is available on the official website.
