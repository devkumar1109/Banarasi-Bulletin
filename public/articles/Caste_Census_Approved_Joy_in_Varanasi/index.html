Caste Census Approved: Joy in Varanasi

2025-05-01T07:02:00+00:00

The Union Cabinet has approved a caste-based census in India. Residents of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh celebrated the decision, hailing it as a "historic decision" and expressing joy with traditional festivities like playing drums and applying colored powder. The decision comes after a long-standing demand for a caste census, which citizens hope will lead to more inclusive policymaking. Union Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw announced the inclusion of caste-based data in the upcoming national census, stating that it will be conducted transparently. The national census, originally scheduled for 2020, was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
