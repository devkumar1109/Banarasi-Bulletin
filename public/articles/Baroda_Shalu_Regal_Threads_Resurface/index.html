Baroda Shalu: Regal Threads Resurface

2025-03-05T07:52:00+00:00

Maharani Radhika Raje Gaekwad of Baroda recently showcased a rare 1964 Baroda Shalu saree, originally worn by Rajmata Shubhangini Raje for her wedding. This exquisite textile, woven in Benaras using fine cotton, tissue, and real gold threads, was commissioned exclusively for the royal family. Once highly sought after, the Baroda Shalu saree disappeared by the mid-1970s. In 2019, a revival project was initiated by Radhika Raje and Asif Shaikh of the CDS Art Foundation to preserve this regal textile.
