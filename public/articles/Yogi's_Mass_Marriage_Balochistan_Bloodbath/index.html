Yogi's Mass Marriage, Balochistan Bloodbath

2025-05-27T14:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath attended a mass wedding ceremony in Gorakhpur, distributing gifts to 1200 couples participating in the CM Mass Marriage Scheme. The scheme, launched in 2017, provides financial and material support for marriages, aiming to eliminate dowry and child marriage while promoting equality across caste, region, and religion. The financial assistance has been increased from Rs 20,000 to Rs 1 lakh per couple. Meanwhile, fresh allegations of extrajudicial killings have surfaced in Pakistan's Balochistan province. Three missing persons were reportedly killed in a staged encounter by the Counter Terrorism Department in Barkhan district, and a youth was found dead in Awaran under suspicious circumstances after being summoned by security forces.
