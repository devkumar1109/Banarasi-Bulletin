Holi Row Turns Fatal: Gurugram Man Strangles Wife

2025-03-22T17:31:00+00:00

In Gurugram, a man was arrested for strangling his wife, Renu, during an argument about returning home for Holi. He used a scarf to kill her, then locked her body in their rented room in Bhangrola village and fled. Police discovered the body on March 7th and registered a case at the Kherki Daula police station. The accused was apprehended from Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, by a Manesar crime unit team.
