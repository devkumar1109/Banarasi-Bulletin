Bali-Delhi Flight Diverted to Varanasi

2025-06-18T18:26:00+00:00

An Air India flight (AI 2146) traveling from Bali to Delhi was diverted to Varanasi due to bad weather on Tuesday. The Airbus A321, carrying 187 passengers, landed in Varanasi at 4:20 pm after receiving clearance from ATC. The passengers remained on board for over two hours before the flight departed for Delhi at 7 pm when weather conditions improved. The flight originated from Denpasar International Airport in Bali and was scheduled to land at Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi. Flight tracking website FlightRadar24 confirmed the diversion.
