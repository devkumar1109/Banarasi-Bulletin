Temple Priest's Home Burgled: 6 Arrested

2025-05-21T18:09:00+00:00

Six people were arrested for the burglary at Sankat Mochan temple priest's residence in Varanasi. The trio injured in an encounter with police during the raid were identified as Vicky Tiwari, Jitendra Singh alias Golu Patel, and Rakesh Dubey. The other arrested accused are Dilip alias Bansi Chaubey, Atul Shukla, and Shani. Police recovered stolen jewelry worth over Rs 1 crore and Rs 3 lakh cash. CCTV footage showed three masked men committing the theft while three others assisted them. All the accused had previously worked at the priest's home.
