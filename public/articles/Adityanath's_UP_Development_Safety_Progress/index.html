Adityanath's UP: Development, Safety, Progress.

2025-03-24T08:13:00+00:00

Yogi Adityanath's eight-year tenure as Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister has been marked by significant development across various sectors. Key achievements include: * **Rural Development:** Over 2.62 crore toilets constructed, free ration distribution to 14.7 crore people during the pandemic, 1.86 crore Ujjwala LPG connections, and 56 lakh houses built for the underprivileged. * **Law and Order:** Establishment of police commissionerates in seven districts and improved police infrastructure. * **Infrastructure:** Major infrastructure projects undertaken, including expressways and highways. * **Healthcare:** Focus on improving healthcare facilities and services. The BJP's continued success in UP elections reinforces the government's popular support.
