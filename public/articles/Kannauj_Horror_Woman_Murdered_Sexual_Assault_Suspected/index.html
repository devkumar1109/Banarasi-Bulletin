Kannauj Horror: Woman Murdered, Sexual Assault Suspected

2025-05-04T14:30:00+00:00

A 40-year-old woman was murdered in Kannauj, Uttar Pradesh. Her body was found in a farm with facial and other injuries, suggesting physical assault. Bloodstains and broken bangles indicate violence. While the cause of death requires a post-mortem, police suspect she may have been sexually assaulted before being killed. Police are investigating the motive, considering family disputes, property issues, or robbery as possibilities. The woman's saree is missing, but her blouse and petticoat were found, suggesting an attempt to disrobe her. This incident follows a recent honor killing in the same area where a teenage girl was murdered by her father and disposed of.
