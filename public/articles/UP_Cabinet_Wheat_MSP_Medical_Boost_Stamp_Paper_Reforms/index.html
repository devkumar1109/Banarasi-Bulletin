U.P. Cabinet: Wheat MSP, Medical Boost, Stamp Paper Reforms

2025-03-10T10:53:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Cabinet approved 19 proposals, including wheat procurement at the MSP of Rs 2,425 per quintal from March 17 to June 15. 6,500 procurement centers will be set up across the state. The Cabinet approved the establishment of new medical and nursing colleges. Land will be transferred from the Ballia district jail and the Government Agricultural School in Bulandshahr for these colleges. The government will withdraw physical stamp papers valued between Rs 10,000 and Rs 25,000 to prevent misuse. Additionally, the Cabinet approved revised administrative and financial sanctions for a 300-bed gynaecology block and a 100-bed paediatric block at the Uttar Pradesh Institute of Medical Sciences in Saifai. Land will be repurposed for industrial and defense projects.
