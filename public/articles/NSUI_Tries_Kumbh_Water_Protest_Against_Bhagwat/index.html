NSUI Tries Kumbh Water Protest Against Bhagwat

2025-04-04T17:00:00+00:00

NSUI activists attempted to block RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat's convoy in Varanasi on Thursday during his four-day visit. The activists, led by Vikas Singh, wanted to offer Bhagwat the sacred water from the Maha Kumbh as a protest. They accused Bhagwat of engaging in "politics of Hindutva" but not practicing it, pointing out his absence from the Maha Kumbh religious ritual. Police intervened, detaining several NSUI members after an altercation.
