Navratri Meat Ban: UP Enforces Restrictions Near Shrines

2025-03-29T16:21:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has banned the sale of meat and eggs within 500 meters of religious places during Navratri to maintain sanctity. Illegal slaughterhouses have also been ordered shut. The ban, citing 2014 and 2017 orders, will be strictly enforced on April 6th, Ram Navami, with a complete ban on animal slaughter and meat sales. District-level committees, comprising police, health, and food safety officials, will monitor the situation. Violators will face action under the UP Municipal Corporation Act and the Food Safety Act.
