10 Underrated Bollywood Gems You Must See

2025-05-25T07:10:00+00:00

The article lists 10 underrated Bollywood films worth watching. "Chhorii" (2021), starring Nushrratt Bharuccha, is a horror film with a social message about gender violence, available on Amazon Prime Video. "Ludo" (2020) by Anurag Basu, features Abhishek Bachchan, Rajkummar Rao, and Sanya Malhotra in a dark comedy crime film with interwoven stories. "Udaan" (2010) is a coming-of-age drama about a teenager expelled from boarding school and returning to his oppressive father's home. Other films mentioned are "Tamasha," "Vicky Donor," "Masaan," "Raman Raghav 2.0," "Andhadhun," "Tumbbad," and "NH10."
