Varanasi: Soulful City of Festivals and Peace

2025-03-15T22:27:00+00:00

Varanasi is a popular destination for spiritual seekers and festival lovers. The city offers a range of activities, including exploring street food, learning about local legends, and finding solitude at soulful spots like Rewa Ghat and Manikarnika Ghat. Varanasi is also a great destination for backpackers. The best time to visit is during the festival season. For those seeking inner peace, there are numerous retreats and meditation centers in the city.
