Dhami Empowers Uttarakhand Farmers Digitally

2025-05-17T15:34:00+00:00

Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami launched the 'e-Rupi' system, a digital payment initiative for farmers, enabling direct subsidy payments via e-vouchers for purchasing essential supplies. The system will facilitate transparent, fast, and middleman-free transactions, with farmers receiving subsidy amounts through e-vouchers accessible via SMS or QR code. Alongside e-Rupi, four agricultural policies were introduced: the Kiwi Policy, Dragon Fruit Policy, Apple Post-Harvest Harvesting Scheme, and Millet Mission. These policies aim to boost Uttarakhand's agricultural sector and promote agricultural diversity, ultimately increasing farmers' income and addressing migration concerns.
