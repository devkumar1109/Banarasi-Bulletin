Excise Officer Fraud: Eight Woman Victims Exposed

2025-03-23T19:11:00+00:00

Rajan Singh, a man from Sonbhadra, has been accused of posing as an excise officer to defraud and marry multiple women, primarily schoolteachers. He allegedly duped Kiran, a teacher from Sant Kabir Nagar, of Rs 42 lakh after promising to buy land and build a house in Lucknow for them. He also allegedly took Rs 10 lakh from another teacher, Sarita of Ambedkarnagar, whom he married in 2014. Police believe Rajan has married eight to nine women in total. After Kiran filed a police report, Sarita also came forward with her own allegations. Rajan was apprehended by the Sonbhadra police and is currently under interrogation.
