UP Shines: Solar Powers Water Mission

2025-04-21T13:55:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh received the Prime Minister's Award for successfully integrating solar power into its Jal Jeevan Mission projects. This initiative powered 33,157 out of 41,539 water projects, generating 900 MW of daily electricity. Solar implementation reduced water supply costs by over 50% and significantly decreased carbon emissions. The project, led by PM Modi and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, sets a benchmark for sustainable development in water management.
