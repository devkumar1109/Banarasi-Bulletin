Modi's UP Push: Infrastructure, Power, and Development

2025-05-30T12:53:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated several infrastructure and power projects in Uttar Pradesh. This includes the Kanpur Metro's new section connecting Chunniganj to Kanpur Central, widening of G.T. Road, and extensions to the Panki and Ghatampur Thermal Power Plants. He also inaugurated 132 kV substations in Greater Noida and rail overbridges in Kanpur to support power project logistics. Additional developments include a tertiary treatment plant in Kanpur and the laying of foundation stones for a 220 kV substation in YEIDA and road projects under the defence corridor. PM Modi also distributed benefits to beneficiaries of various government schemes.
