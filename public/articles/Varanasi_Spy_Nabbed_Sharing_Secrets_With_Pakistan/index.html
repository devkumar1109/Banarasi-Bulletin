Varanasi Spy Nabbed: Sharing Secrets With Pakistan

2025-05-22T13:02:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (UP ATS) arrested Tufail from Varanasi for allegedly spying for Pakistan. Tufail shared sensitive information, including photos of strategic locations like Rajghat, Gyanvapi, and the Red Fort, with Pakistani nationals. He was also active in WhatsApp groups sharing videos of extremist Maulana Saad Rizvi and spreading provocative messages about Babri Masjid and Sharia law. Tufail is believed to have been in contact with nearly 600 Pakistanis, including a woman whose husband serves in the Pakistani Army. The UP ATS is investigating the full extent of his activities and network.
