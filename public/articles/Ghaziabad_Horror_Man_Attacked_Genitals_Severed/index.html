Ghaziabad Horror: Man Attacked, Genitals Severed

2025-03-03T11:27:00+00:00

In Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, intruders attacked a man, severing his genitals and fleeing with the body parts. The victim's son alleges that a transgender person named Paro orchestrated the crime with accomplices. The attack occurred on the night of February 28th, when the intruders overpowered the victim, administered an unknown substance, and severed his genitals before fleeing. The victim is hospitalized in Meerut, and his son has filed a police complaint accusing Paro and her accomplices of the attack.
