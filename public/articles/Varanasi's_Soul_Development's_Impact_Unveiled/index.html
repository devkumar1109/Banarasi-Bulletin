Varanasi's Soul: Development's Impact Unveiled

2025-03-11T12:07:00+00:00

Abhishek Pandey, a Varanasi-born artist now based in Pune, explores the impact of development on his hometown through his first solo exhibition, "Tandra." Pandey's work reflects on the rapid urbanization of Varanasi, exemplified by the widening of narrow lanes and the modernization of sacred ghats. He contrasts the traditional, spiritual essence of Varanasi, known for its cremation rituals and the concept of "moksha" (liberation), with the intrusive influence of technology. The exhibition, curated by Sheena Maria Piedade, is divided into three sections representing the past, present, and future, prompting viewers to contemplate the hidden costs of development.
