Tragedy Strikes 'Kalash Yatra' Ritual, Two Drowned

2025-05-05T15:31:00+00:00

A 21-year-old man, Ajit Pandey, drowned during a 'kalash yatra' religious ritual in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred at Chanuki Ghat while bathing in the river with his 18-year-old brother, Shantanu. Villagers rescued both brothers but Shantanu was declared dead at a nearby health center. Ajit is currently out of danger. An investigation is underway and the body has been sent for post-mortem. The 'kalash yatra' was part of a temple consecration ceremony.
