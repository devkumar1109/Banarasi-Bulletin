UP Police Podcast Exposes ₹100-Crore Insurance Fraud

2025-06-01T23:57:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police launched a new crime-focused podcast series, "Beyond the Badge," to enhance transparency and public engagement. The podcast, initiated by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, features documentary-style narratives of real criminal cases investigated by UP Police. The first episode delves into a ₹100-crore insurance fraud case in Sambhal district, highlighting the role of organized crime syndicates exploiting government schemes. Superintendent of Police Anukriti Sharma, who led the investigation, explains how the syndicates used forged documents, manipulated Aadhaar data, and bribed officials to claim insurance payouts from orchestrated deaths or faked ones. The podcast showcases how UP Police used data analytics, technical surveillance, and collaboration with institutions to track the scam and make key arrests. SP Sharma urges citizens to be vigilant about safeguarding their personal information.
