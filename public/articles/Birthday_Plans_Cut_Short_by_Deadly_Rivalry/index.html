Birthday Plans Cut Short by Deadly Rivalry

2025-03-24T10:55:00+00:00

A 20-year-old man, Manish Prajapati, was shot dead in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, on Sunday night due to an old rivalry. The incident occurred at Prajapati's home while he was preparing for his birthday celebrations. A group of men arrived on motorcycles, argued with him, and shot him before fleeing. The victim was rushed to the hospital but was declared dead on arrival. Police have registered a case against the named accused based on a complaint filed by Prajapati's family and are actively searching for the suspects. The murder stemmed from a previous clash between Prajapati's family and the accused two months ago, which resulted in an attempted murder case against Prajapati.
