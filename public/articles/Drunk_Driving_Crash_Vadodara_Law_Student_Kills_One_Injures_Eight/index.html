Drunk Driving Crash: Vadodara Law Student Kills One, Injures Eight

2025-03-14T06:09:00+00:00

Rakshit Chaurasia, a 23-year-old law student, allegedly driving under the influence, hit three vehicles in Vadodara, killing Hemali Patel and injuring eight others. Chaurasia was driving his friend Meet Chauhan's car. Locals thrashed Chaurasia after the incident, captured on CCTV. Police arrested Chaurasia and are investigating if alcohol was a factor. Hemali Patel's husband, Purav Patel, is critically injured. Chaurasia faces charges including culpable homicide, while Chauhan's blood samples were taken for alcohol tests.
