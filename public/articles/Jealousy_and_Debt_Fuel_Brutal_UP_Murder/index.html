Jealousy and Debt Fuel Brutal UP Murder

2025-04-27T08:39:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Bhuvar Niranjan Pur, a man named Nohar Chaudhary, along with his first wife Vidyavati and daughter Lakshmi, murdered his second wife, Sunita. Sunita was strangled with a dupatta and beaten with a stick. Police stated that the motive was Sunita's frequent financial demands from Nohar due to her illness, leading to frustration and the plot to kill her. The trio was arrested on Saturday.
