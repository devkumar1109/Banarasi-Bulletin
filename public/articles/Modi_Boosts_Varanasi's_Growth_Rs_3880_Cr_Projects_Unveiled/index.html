Modi Boosts Varanasi's Growth: Rs 3,880 Cr Projects Unveiled

2025-04-11T07:51:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for development projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. He emphasized the significant development witnessed in Banaras over the past decade, highlighting its modernization while preserving its heritage. Modi stated that Kashi is now a center of Purvanchal's economic map and is driving the region's development. He mentioned the inauguration of projects focusing on infrastructure, clean water access, education, healthcare, and sports facilities. Modi expressed his commitment to improving amenities for all regions, families, and youth, positioning these initiatives as milestones in transforming Purvanchal.
