Lucknow's Kukrail Safari: Adventure Unleashed

2025-03-22T09:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will launch the 'Kukrail Night Safari and Adventure Park' in Lucknow next month. The project, costing over Rs 1,500 crore, will be built in two phases. The first phase, worth Rs 631 crore, will begin in April and take 24 months to complete. The park will span 855.07 acres and feature 38 animal enclosures housing species like lions, tigers, and leopards. Amenities in the first phase include an art gallery, 7D theatre, and a tram service.
