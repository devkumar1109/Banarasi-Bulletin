College Proctor Accused of Sex Abuse, Hunt Underway

2025-03-18T04:31:00+00:00

The Chief Proctor of PC Bagla Degree College in Hathras, Rajneesh, has been booked for allegedly sexually exploiting female students. He allegedly assaulted the students under the guise of helping them pass exams, secure jobs, and make other promises. A case has been registered under multiple sections of the Indian Penal Code (IPC), and three teams have been formed to arrest him. Relevant evidence is being collected. Aparna Yadav, Vice President of the Uttar Pradesh State Women's Commission, condemned the incident and emphasized its seriousness. She stated that the Chief Minister has instructed officials to ensure justice for the victims. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed law and order and district development during a meeting in Varanasi on March 12.
