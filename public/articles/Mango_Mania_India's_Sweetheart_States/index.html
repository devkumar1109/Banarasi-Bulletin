Mango Mania: India's Sweetheart States

2025-04-11T10:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is India's largest mango producer, contributing over 4.5 million metric tons annually, or 25-26% of the national total. The state's Gangetic plains and historical cultivation practices have led to renowned varieties like Dasheri, Langra, Chausa, and Safeda. Andhra Pradesh is the second largest producer at 1.2 million metric tons, known for Banganapalli and Neelam mangoes. Bihar follows with 800,000 metric tons, primarily cultivating the Deshi variety. Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu round out the top six producers, contributing significant amounts to India's mango production.
