Varanasi Runs, Swims for Clean Ganga

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with events focused on clean Ganga and water conservation. A marathon, "Run for Clean Ganga", was organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, with participants running 6 km from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat. Abhishek Yadav won the men's category, and Rebi Pal won the women's category. The forest department, alongside NGO Dolphin Friends, inaugurated a "Dolphin Safari" at Dhakwa Ghat, aiming to educate the public about Gangetic Dolphin conservation. Forest conservator Ravi Kumar Singh emphasized increased efforts to protect the dolphins. Officials reiterated the importance of clean water and highlighted ongoing efforts to purify the Ganga.
