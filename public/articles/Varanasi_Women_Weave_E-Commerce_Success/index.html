Varanasi Women Weave E-Commerce Success

2025-04-27T19:10:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will provide free e-commerce training to 150 women entrepreneurs associated with Varanasi's One District One Product (ODOP) initiative. The two-day training program will be conducted by a private company, Yuki, focusing on online trading, branding, digital marketing, and accessing national and international markets. The initiative aims to empower women artisans who produce Banarasi silk, pink enamelling, lacquerware, and wooden toys, products recognized for their geographical indication (GI) status. Varanasi's ODOP products generate an annual turnover of Rs 1,500 crore, with around 25% of the 100,000 artisans being women.
