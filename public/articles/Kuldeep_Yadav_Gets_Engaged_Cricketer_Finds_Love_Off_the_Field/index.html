Kuldeep Yadav Gets Engaged: Cricketer Finds Love Off the Field

2025-06-04T17:09:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Kuldeep Yadav got engaged to his childhood friend Vanshika in a private ceremony in Lucknow. The engagement was attended by close friends and family, including several cricketers from Uttar Pradesh. Vanshika is from Kanpur and her father works for LIC. Kuldeep recently concluded the IPL 2025 season with Delhi Capitals, taking 15 wickets in 14 matches. He has been a key player for India in limited-overs cricket since his debut in 2017, with over 180 wickets in ODIs. He is known for his "chinaman" delivery and mastery of flight and variations. Kuldeep is looking to make a comeback in Test cricket.
