DC Douses LSG Fire, Restrict Total to 159

2025-04-22T16:06:00+00:00

In an IPL 2025 match at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium, Delhi Capitals (DC) restricted Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) to 159/6. LSG started strongly with openers Aiden Markram and Mitchell Marsh adding 51 runs in the first six overs. Markram top-scored with a 33-ball 52, but DC bowlers made a strong comeback in the middle overs to limit LSG's total.
