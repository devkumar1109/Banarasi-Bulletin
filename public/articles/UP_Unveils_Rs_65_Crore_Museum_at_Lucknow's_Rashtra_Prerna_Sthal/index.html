UP Unveils Rs 65 Crore Museum at Lucknow's Rashtra Prerna Sthal

2025-03-29T15:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will build a museum at Lucknow's Rashtra Prerna Sthal with a budget exceeding Rs 65 crore. Construction is set to begin soon by the Lucknow Development Authority. The project also includes a cafeteria in the Old Kothi at the State Museum and library expansion. The Uttar Pradesh Project Corporation Limited will execute the museum project, with a combined budget of Rs 4.87 crore for the cafeteria and library expansion.
