Honeymoon Turns Deadly: Wife Suspected in Meghalaya Murder

2025-06-09T02:42:00+00:00

Raja Raghuvanshi, a tourist from Indore, was allegedly murdered during his honeymoon in Meghalaya. His wife, Sonam, is suspected to have orchestrated the killing by hiring assailants. Sonam surrendered to Uttar Pradesh police in Ghazipur, while three suspects were arrested in raids in Uttar Pradesh and Indore. The arrested assailants confessed to being hired by Sonam. Meghalaya Chief Minister Conrad K Sangma commended the police for making a breakthrough in the case within a week.
