Madrassa Literacy Gap: No English Names

2025-04-28T07:40:00+00:00

A surprise inspection of a madrassa in Uttar Pradesh found that none of the Class 10 students could write their names in English. This revelation highlights a potential literacy gap in the education system. The incident has sparked concerns about the quality of education being provided at the madrassa and calls for improvement in English language instruction.
