Baba Yaga Downed: Drones Redefine Warfare

2025-05-17T04:00:00+00:00

Following failed peace talks in Istanbul, Russia's Defence Ministry released footage of its forces intercepting Ukraine's "Baba Yaga" drone bombers. These drones, originally designed for agriculture, have been repurposed for military operations by Ukraine, often utilizing Starlink technology for long-range strikes. Russia counters these attacks with kamikaze drones and sniper teams, highlighting the increasing use of advanced technology in warfare. The "Baba Yaga" drones, large rotorcraft, demonstrate how readily available technology can be adapted for military purposes. The ongoing conflict underscores the evolution of warfare, with both sides employing innovative tactics and drones to gain an advantage.
