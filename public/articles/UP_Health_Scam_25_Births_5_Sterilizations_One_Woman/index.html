UP Health Scam: 25 Births, 5 Sterilizations, One Woman

2025-04-12T03:12:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a health department scam was uncovered where a 35-year-old woman's identity was used to fraudulently claim 25 childbirths and five sterilizations over a 30-month period. The scheme was exposed during an audit at a community health center (CHC) in Fatehabad. The fraudulent claims were made to obtain government benefits.
