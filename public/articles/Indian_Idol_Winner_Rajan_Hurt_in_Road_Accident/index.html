Indian Idol Winner Rajan Hurt in Road Accident

2025-05-05T12:16:00+00:00

Pawandeep Rajan, winner of Indian Idol Season 12, was in a serious car accident on May 5, 2025, at 3:40 am on National Highway 9 in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh. His car collided with a stationary truck. Rajan suffered multiple limb fractures and is currently stable and conscious at Fortis Hospital in Noida. He will undergo several surgeries. The accident occurred while Rajan was traveling to Delhi from Champawat in Uttarakhand for a program. Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami expressed his well wishes for Rajan's recovery.
