Triple Talaq Fuels Suicide, Officer Suspended

2025-05-01T07:25:00+00:00

A 24-year-old woman, Saniya, in Uttar Pradesh's Gorakhpur died by suicide after her husband, Salauddin, pronounced triple talaq over the phone. Saniya had been facing continuous dowry harassment from her in-laws, a complaint filed by her mother with the local police was ignored. A sub-inspector, Jay Prakash Singh, has been suspended for inaction. Saniya's mother, Asiya, lodged a complaint stating that despite dowry being given, Saniya faced persistent harassment. Saniya had returned to her maternal home on April 26 after Salauddin abandoned her. She took the extreme step following a phone call from Salauddin on Monday night where he allegedly verbally abused her and pronounced triple talaq.
