Time Loop Romance: "Bhool Chuk Maaf" Hits Theaters

2025-05-15T12:26:00+00:00

"Bhool Chuk Maaf", a romantic comedy starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi, is set to release in theaters on May 23, 2025. The film, directed by Karan Sharma and produced by Maddock Films, revolves around a man trapped in a time loop during his wedding preparations in Varanasi. The release date was delayed due to legal complications stemming from a planned direct-to-OTT release, which led to a lawsuit from PVR INOX. The Bombay High Court intervened, ordering an 8-week theatrical exclusivity period. The film features a supporting cast including Raghubir Yadav, Sanjay Mishra, Zakir Hussain, and Seema Pahwa. The music, composed by Tanishk Bagchi, includes original songs and re-enacted hits. "Bhool Chuk Maaf" promises a unique blend of romance, humor, and science fiction.
