Maha Kumbh 2025: Divine Dip Draws Record Crowds

2025-02-26T14:32:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh 2025 concluded today, February 26th, with a record 1.01 crore devotees taking a holy dip at the Triveni Sangam on Mahashivratri. The 45-day event saw over 63 crore devotees visit, exceeding initial estimates of 40 crore. Six special bathing dates were observed, including three 'Amrit Snan' (divine baths). Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath celebrated the success of the event, expressing his wishes for blessings from Lord Shiva and the Ganges River. Union Home Minister Amit Shah also attended the Maha Shivratri celebrations in Coimbatore. KDM, a mobile accessories brand, installed 90 mobile charging stations, powering over 16 lakh phones during the Kumbh Mela.
