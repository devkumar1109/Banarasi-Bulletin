Delhi Hotelier Murder: Suspects on the Run

2025-04-03T03:06:00+00:00

A Delhi hotelier, Sagar, was kidnapped and brutally murdered. His body was found in Uttar Pradesh's Shamli district with multiple gunshot wounds and a bludgeoned head. Sagar went missing on March 27 after leaving his hotel in Tilak Nagar, Delhi. His family reported him missing and suspected certain individuals. Police formed teams to track the suspects through technical and manual surveillance. Despite extensive efforts, the prime suspects remain at large, prompting authorities to issue non-bailable warrants. The murder case was initially registered as unidentified at Kandla police station in Shamli on March 27, but Sagar was confirmed as the deceased on April 2.
