Love, Music, and Mayhem: New Releases This Week

2025-05-12T04:32:00+00:00

This week, from May 12 to 18, several new movies and web series are releasing on OTT platforms. On Netflix, "Bhool Chuk Maaf," a romantic comedy starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi, will premiere on May 16. The film, set in Varanasi, follows a hopeless romantic named Ranjan who tries to win back his ex-girlfriend. JioHotstar will release "Hai Junoon," a musical drama about a disciplined musical legend who mentors a group of college students, also on May 16.
