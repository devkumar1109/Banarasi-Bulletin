Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Accused in 7-Day Rape Ordeal

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi reported being abducted and gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned home on April 4, disclosing the ordeal to her family. Police filed an FIR based on her mother's complaint, charging the accused under sections related to gang rape, outraging modesty, administering poison, wrongful confinement, and criminal intimidation. Six suspects have been arrested, and authorities are pursuing the remaining individuals. The woman alleged multiple instances of rape at various locations, including cafes, hotels, and a warehouse.
