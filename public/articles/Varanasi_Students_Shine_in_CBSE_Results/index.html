Varanasi Students Shine in CBSE Results

2025-05-13T19:08:00+00:00

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) results are out, with students from various schools in Varanasi achieving exceptional results. Sunbeam Academy students excelled, with Atharv Lakhotia topping Class XII Arts with 97.6%. In Class X, Shubhangi Pandey secured 97.2% marks. DALIMSS Sunbeam group of schools also saw impressive performances, with Yashaswi Rai achieving 99% in Arts stream. Aryan International School’s Suryansh Gupta topped Class XII Arts with 99.4%, while Central Girls Hindu School and Central Hindu Boys School also reported high pass percentages and excellent individual performances. Students from Padav branch of Jaipuria Schools achieved 100% pass percentage in the district.
