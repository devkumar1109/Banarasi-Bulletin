Dowry Deaths Shock UP, Two Women Perish

2025-04-09T15:22:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Pilibhit district, 26-year-old Sonkali was burnt alive by her husband Naresh Kumar and his parents for failing to meet their dowry demands. Sonkali's father-in-law and mother-in-law, Janki and Bhagwan Devi, also subjected her to physical and mental abuse. Sonkali died on April 6th after narrating the events to her brother. Police have registered a case of dowry death. In a separate incident in Budaun, 21-year-old Tarawati, married eight months ago, allegedly died by consuming poison at her in-laws' home. Her father, Omkar, alleges that his daughter was killed by her in-laws due to dowry demands, claiming he had previously filed a complaint.
