Varanasi Battles Bugs: Health Campaign Launched

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, chikungunya, and diarrhea. The campaign aims to prevent waterborne and communicable diseases through anti-larvae spraying and fogging. Health officials identified 68 dengue hotspots in urban areas and 56 in rural areas. In 2025, only 22 dengue cases, 2 malaria cases, and 18 chikungunya cases were reported compared to significantly higher numbers in 2023 and 2024. SVM Hospital Bhelupur introduced new services, including an ultrasound machine, digital X-ray, dental department, eye examination, outpatient, and inpatient services, including mental health OPD.
