IIIT-UP: Tech Hub with Affiliated Colleges

2025-06-06T09:51:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Uttar Pradesh (IIIT-UP), is a Deemed university located in Devghat, Jhalwa, Allahabad. It offers B.Tech. programs in Computer Science and Information Technology. The content lists affiliated colleges under IIIT-UP but does not provide their names. IIIT-UP is affiliated with Ennexa Technologies Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Kottayam, Kerala. Ennexa offers customer support from Monday to Friday, 10 AM to 5 PM, at 1800 425 0053.
