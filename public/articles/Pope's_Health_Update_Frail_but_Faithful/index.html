Pope's Health Update: Frail but Faithful

2025-03-17T14:00:00+00:00

Pope Francis, 88, was hospitalized over a month ago for double pneumonia. The Vatican released the first photo of the Pope since his hospitalization, showing him frail in a wheelchair. Despite his health challenges, Pope Francis remains committed to his duties. There is no announced date for his discharge, raising concerns about his ability to continue leading the Church and prompting speculation about potential changes in Vatican leadership.
