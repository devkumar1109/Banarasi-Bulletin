UP Unveils Digital Audio Tours for Tourist Destinations

2025-05-31T11:02:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is launching a digital tourism initiative featuring QR code-based audio tours at 100 key tourist destinations. The initiative aims to enhance tourist experience and cultural understanding by offering 5-7 minute audio narratives about each location. These narratives will be available in 10 regional languages and 5 international languages, recorded by professional voiceover artists with background music and ambient sounds. The project will cover iconic sites like the Taj Mahal, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and Shri Ram Janmabhoomi, with weather-resistant QR codes installed at each location. The audio tours will be accessible through the UP Tourism website and mobile apps, with offline options available. Content will be reviewed by historians, archaeologists, and cultural experts to ensure accuracy and relevance.
