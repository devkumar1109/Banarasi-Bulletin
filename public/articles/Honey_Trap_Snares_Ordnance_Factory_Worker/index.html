Honey Trap Snares Ordnance Factory Worker

2025-03-14T11:35:00+00:00

An employee of an ordnance factory in Firozabad, Uttar Pradesh, named Ravindra Kumar, has been arrested by the Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) for sharing classified defense information with Pakistan's intelligence agency, ISI. Kumar was caught after falling victim to a honey trap orchestrated by an ISI operative identified as 'Neha'. He allegedly shared sensitive documents, including daily production reports, stock details, and requisitions, with the handler. The ATS received intelligence about Kumar's activities and conducted a detailed interrogation, which revealed his involvement in leaking classified information. ADG of UP ATS, Nilabja Choudhary, urged sensitive institutions to strengthen security protocols and conduct thorough checks on employees to prevent future breaches.
