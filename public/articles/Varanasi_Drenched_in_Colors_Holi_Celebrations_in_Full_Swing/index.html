Varanasi Drenched in Colors: Holi Celebrations in Full Swing

2025-03-15T20:05:00+00:00

Holi celebrations in Varanasi began with Holika bonfire on Thursday night and continued through Friday and Saturday. Naga Sadhus and saints, who had been in Prayagraj for the Kumbh Mela, played Holi in Varanasi before departing. At the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, a special Kachnar flower gulal, sent by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, was offered to Lord Vishwanath Mahadev. This tradition was started in 2024. Celebrations in the city involved families playing with colors in their neighborhoods and groups of youths gathering at ghats along the Ganga. As the day progressed, crowds thinned due to the hot weather, but areas leading to prominent ghats remained busy.
