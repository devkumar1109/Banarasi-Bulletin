Pathak Attacks Yadav's "DNA", Sparks Political Fire

2025-05-20T07:51:00+00:00

Brajesh Pathak, a leader in the Bharatiya Janata Party, has criticized Akhilesh Yadav, leader of the Samajwadi Party, renewing an attack on Yadav and his party's ideology. Pathak went so far as to say he would be willing to have a thesis written about the "DNA" of the Samajwadi Party, implying he believes its core values are questionable. This statement highlights ongoing political tension between the BJP and the Samajwadi Party in Uttar Pradesh.
