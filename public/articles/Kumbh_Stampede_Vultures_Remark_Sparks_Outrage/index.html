Kumbh Stampede: "Vultures" Remark Sparks Outrage

2025-02-25T14:25:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath faced criticism for his "vultures got dead bodies, pigs got dirt" remark regarding the Maha Kumbh stampede, which resulted in 30 deaths. Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav condemned Adityanath's language, urging sympathy over anger and accusing the government of exploiting the event for political gain. Adityanath defended the state's arrangements, claiming everyone found what they sought at the gathering. He also criticized the previous Samajwadi Party government for neglecting Kumbh preparations. The stampede prompted Yadav to question the government's handling of the event and highlight the lack of support for families searching for their loved ones.
