Kashyap's "Nishaanchi": Democracy Under Siege

2025-06-16T14:26:00+00:00

Anurag Kashyap's political thriller "Nishaanchi" (meaning "The Marker") is set to release on August 23, 2025. The film follows Iqbal Khan, a former political strategist entangled in a plot to undermine Indian democracy. He exposes the corruption, leading to a dangerous pursuit by hired killers and betrayals. Shot in locations like Lucknow, Varanasi, Bhopal, and Delhi, "Nishaanchi" features a cast including Vijay Varma, Radhika Apte, Manav Kaul, Sobhita Dhulipala, and a cameo by Pankaj Tripathi. Kashyap's signature style—tense dialogues, extended takes, and morally ambiguous characters—promises a gripping and shocking cinematic experience.
