Truck crushes youths returning from wedding

2025-05-05T06:53:00+00:00

Three youths were killed in Mahoba, Uttar Pradesh, when an overloaded truck crushed them near Chhani Mod on the Kanpur-Sagar National Highway. The victims, Pramod Kushwaha (25), Bharat (17), and Jeetu (15), were returning home after a wedding ceremony. The truck driver fled the scene, but police recovered the vehicle and are attempting to apprehend him. This tragic incident follows two other fatal accidents in Mahoba in recent months: two youths were killed by an unidentified vehicle in March, and four devotees died in a car-truck collision in February.
