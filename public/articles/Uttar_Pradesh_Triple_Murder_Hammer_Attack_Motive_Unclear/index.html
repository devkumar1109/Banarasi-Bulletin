Uttar Pradesh Triple Murder: Hammer Attack, Motive Unclear

2025-05-26T11:26:00+00:00

In Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, a man named Lalji (62), his sons Guddu Kumar (32) and Yadveer (28), were murdered on Sunday night. Unidentified assailants attacked them with hammers at their shop near Nevada bypass, killing all three. The assailants also tampered with the CCTV camera, taking the DVR. Four mobile phones were recovered from the scene. Police suspect an old enmity may be the motive behind the killings and have formed eight teams to apprehend the culprits. Forensic teams are collecting evidence, and police are reviewing nearby CCTV footage.
