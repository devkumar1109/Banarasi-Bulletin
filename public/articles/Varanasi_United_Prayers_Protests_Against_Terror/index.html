Varanasi United: Prayers, Protests Against Terror

2025-04-23T17:40:00+00:00

Following the terror attack in Pahalgam, Jammu & Kashmir, Varanasi witnessed widespread protests and prayers. The Kashi Vishwanath Temple Trust held an 'Ati Rudra Path' seeking Lord Shiva's intervention against terrorism. Muslim institutions, including Anjuman Intejamia Masajid and Muslim Women's Foundation, condemned the attack and expressed condolences to the victims' families. A protest march culminated in a symbolic cremation of terrorists, demanding action against Pakistan and a Fatwa against terrorist elements. The BJP postponed its Waqf Reform Awareness Campaign workshops in mourning for the deceased tourists, while other groups organized marches and burned effigies of terrorism.
