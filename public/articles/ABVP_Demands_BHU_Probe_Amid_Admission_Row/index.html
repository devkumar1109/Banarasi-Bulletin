ABVP Demands BHU Probe Amid Admission Row

2025-04-20T15:05:00+00:00

The ABVP, the student wing of the RSS, has demanded that the Banaras Hindu University (BHU) conduct an impartial investigation into irregularities in the Hindi department's admission process for researchers. They allege that certain political groups and "anti-social elements" are attempting to influence the inquiry, which is reviewing objections by two candidates regarding the validity of EWS certificates and transparency in the admission process. ABVP also accuses these groups of spreading misinformation against Bhaskaraditya Tripathi, the ABVP BHU Unit Secretary. They urge the university administration to take action against those found guilty and curb disruptive activities to maintain a safe academic environment.
