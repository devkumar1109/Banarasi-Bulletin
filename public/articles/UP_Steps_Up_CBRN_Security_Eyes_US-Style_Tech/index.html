UP Steps Up CBRN Security, Eyes US-Style Tech

2025-06-08T10:19:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is strengthening security measures against CBRN threats by collaborating with DRDO and ECIL. The state plans a multi-layered safety mechanism, incorporating foreign technology-based systems and advanced robotic surveillance devices inspired by U.S. security protocols. This initiative aims to secure key locations like the state assembly and airports, marking a significant advancement in India's approach to internal security. Consultation with Uttar Pradesh Police, paramilitary forces, and disaster management bodies is underway.
