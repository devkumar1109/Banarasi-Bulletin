IIT-BHU Expands Seats in High-Demand Departments

2025-06-08T18:46:00+00:00

IIT-BHU Varanasi has increased seats in nine popular departments for the 2025-26 academic year. A total of 111 seats were reallocated from four less popular departments (ceramic, metallurgical, mining, and pharmaceutical engineering) to high-demand departments like computer science, electronics, and mathematics. The total number of seats offered for first-year students remains at 1,589. This change aims to better meet student demand and industry needs. Mining engineering saw the largest reduction in seats, losing 41. Departments like chemical, civil, and electrical engineering saw increases in seats.
