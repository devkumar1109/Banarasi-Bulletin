Modi in Varanasi: Justice for Rape Victim, Development Push

2025-04-11T07:31:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Varanasi and was briefed on the alleged gang-rape of a 19-year-old woman. Officials reported that 23 individuals allegedly assaulted the victim over six days, sedating her and moving her between hotels. Six suspects have been arrested. Modi instructed officials to take the strictest action against the perpetrators and implement measures to prevent future incidents. During his visit, Modi inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for 44 development projects worth ₹3,880 crore, focusing on rural development, drinking water schemes, anganwadi centers, libraries, and infrastructure.
