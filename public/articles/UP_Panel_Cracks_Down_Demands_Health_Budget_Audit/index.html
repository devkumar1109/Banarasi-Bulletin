UP Panel Cracks Down, Demands Health Budget Audit

2025-04-24T18:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly's Estimates Committee has ordered a crackdown on unregistered buses and called for a budget audit of the health department. The committee, led by MLA Amit Agarwal, reviewed budget allocations and scheme implementation across various departments. The transport department was instructed to target buses with expired registrations and expedite the development of a structured city transport system. The power department was tasked with identifying and recovering payments from large defaulters. An inquiry into the health department's budget allocation and expenditure revealed discrepancies, prompting a 15-day deadline for a report on budget utilization.
