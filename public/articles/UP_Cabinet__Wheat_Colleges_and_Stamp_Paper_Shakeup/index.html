UP Cabinet: Wheat, Colleges, and Stamp Paper Shakeup

2025-03-10T10:53:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Cabinet approved 19 proposals, including wheat procurement at the MSP of Rs 2,425 per quintal from March 17 to June 15. 6,500 procurement centers will be set up across the state. The Cabinet approved the establishment of a new medical college in Ballia, using 14.05 acres of land from the district jail. A nursing college will be built in Bulandshahr using land from the Government Agricultural School. High-value stamp papers valued between Rs 10,000 and Rs 25,000 will be withdrawn to prevent misuse. The Cabinet also approved revised administrative and financial sanctions for a 300-bed gynaecology block, including a 100-bed paediatric block, at the Uttar Pradesh Institute of Medical Sciences in Saifai.
