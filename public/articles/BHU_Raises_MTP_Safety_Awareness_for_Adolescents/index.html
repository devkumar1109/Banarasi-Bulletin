BHU Raises MTP Safety Awareness for Adolescents

2025-04-24T07:46:00+00:00

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) hosted a health awareness session focusing on the safe use of Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP) kits and adolescent well-being. Prof. Mamta from the Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology emphasized the dangers of MTP kit misuse and stressed the importance of medical guidance. The session covered proper usage, side effects, and safety precautions of MTP kits. It also addressed menstrual hygiene and the significance of HPV vaccination for cervical cancer prevention. The event was organized by the Sathiya Kendra at BHU's Institute of Medical Sciences in collaboration with Mamta Health Institute and the National Health Mission, Uttar Pradesh.
