Kashyap in Hot Water: Brahmin Community Outrage

2025-04-22T06:57:00+00:00

A case has been registered against filmmaker Anurag Kashyap in Varanasi for allegedly making a derogatory remark against the Brahmin community on his social media platforms. The FIR was filed by Varanasi resident Govind Chaturvedi, who claimed Kashyap's statement hurt the sentiments of the Brahmin community and insulted Sanatan Dharma. Kashyap has been booked under Section 299 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita for outraging religious feelings and Section 67 of the Information Technology Act for publishing obscene material.
