Krishna's 10-Point Plan: Safer UP, Tech-Driven Policing

2025-06-02T10:54:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's new DGP, Rajeev Krishna, has announced a 10-point policing roadmap. The plan prioritizes a zero-tolerance approach to crime, especially organized crime and crimes against women. Krishna emphasizes leveraging technology, including AI, for smarter policing and cybercrime prevention. He aims to improve citizen services, enhance police welfare, and promote professionalism through specialized training. Public grievance redressal will be a key focus, with a commitment to responding to citizen concerns promptly and empathetically. Maintaining law and order will remain a top priority, with strict action against those who disrupt peace.
