Uttar Pradesh Teen Bags JEE Main 2025 Top Spot

2025-03-06T04:50:00+00:00

Chirag Singh, a 17-year-old from Uttar Pradesh, topped the JEE Main 2025 January session with a 100 NTA percentile. He shifted to Lucknow in Class 9 for better education and dedicated himself to coaching for JEE Main in Class 11. Chirag aims to pursue Computer Science Engineering from IIT Bombay, followed by IIT Delhi and IIT Kanpur. Despite his outstanding score, he will also appear for the April session to potentially improve his marks. Over 12.58 lakh candidates took the JEE Main exam, with 14 scoring a perfect 100 NTA percentile.
