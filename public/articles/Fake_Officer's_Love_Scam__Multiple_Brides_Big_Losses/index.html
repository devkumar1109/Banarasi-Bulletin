"Fake Officer's Love Scam: Multiple Brides, Big Losses"

2025-03-23T19:11:00+00:00

Rajan Singh, a man from Sonbhadra, has been arrested for posing as an excise officer and marrying multiple women. He is accused of defrauding at least eight to nine women, mostly schoolteachers, by gaining their trust and blackmailing them emotionally. One victim, Kiran, alleged that he married her in 2019 and duped her of Rs 42 lakh after convincing her to take a loan for a house. Another victim, Sarita, claimed he married her in 2014, took Rs 10 lakh from her, and prevented her from visiting his home. Police are investigating how many other women Rajan may have married and defrauded. He is currently in custody and facing charges of fraud and cheating.
