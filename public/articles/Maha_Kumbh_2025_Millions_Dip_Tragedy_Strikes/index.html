Maha Kumbh 2025: Millions Dip, Tragedy Strikes

2025-02-26T21:56:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj concluded on February 26th with over 66 crore attendees taking a dip in the Sangam. The event, which began on January 13th, saw record-breaking footfall. Viral moments included 'Monalisa,' a 16-year-old garland seller from Madhya Pradesh who gained internet fame for her striking appearance. Tragically, stampedes at the mela site and New Delhi Railway Station (NDLS) resulted in the deaths of over 45 people, highlighting the challenges of managing such a large gathering.
