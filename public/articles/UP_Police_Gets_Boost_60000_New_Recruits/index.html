UP Police Gets Boost: 60,000 New Recruits

2025-06-18T12:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath held a 'Janta Darshan' at Gorakhnath temple, addressing public grievances. The UP government has recruited 2.16 lakh police personnel in the last eight years, aiming to modernize the force. Union Home Minister Amit Shah distributed appointment letters to 60,244 new constables recruited through the UP Police Constable Recruitment Exam 2024, held across 67 districts to fill over 60,000 vacancies. Shah praised the UP Police for its significant role in maintaining law and order and acknowledged the role of CM Adityanath in its transformation.
