Yogi Slams SP, Boasts UP's Welfare Achievements

2025-02-25T07:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath criticized the Samajwadi Party (SP) during a budget session, stating their government focused on naming schemes after themselves while his government prioritizes people's welfare. He highlighted achievements in the health sector, including medical colleges in every district, appointed medical staff, organized health fairs, established blood banks, and expanded SGPGI, which now functions as a 1200-bed hospital. CM Yogi lauded the success of the Mahakumbh in Prayagraj, calling it a 'Panchteerth' that brought together five pilgrimage sites. He emphasized the large number of devotees who attended, despite criticism from opposition parties.
