Drunk Man Rides CBI Inspector's Bike, Returns with Apology

2025-03-06T02:29:00+00:00

A tipsy man in Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Nagar, Uttar Pradesh, mistakenly rode home on a CBI inspector's motorbike after a night of drinking. Realizing his error, he returned the bike to a nearby police outpost with an apology the next morning. The CBI officer, who was investigating a railway exam paper leak, chose not to press charges. The officer's team had a bigger success, arresting 26 suspects and seizing Rs 1.2 crore in cash related to the paper leak case.
