Maurya Asks Banerjee to Watch "Chhaava" on Aurangzeb

2025-03-21T09:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya urged West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee to watch the Hindi film 'Chhaava' to understand the historical figure Aurangzeb, whom Maurya considers cruel. Maurya, who attended a screening of 'Chhaava' with colleagues, highlighted the film's importance in understanding historical narratives. He criticized Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav for not addressing a statement made by a party MP regarding a historical figure known for forced religious conversions. 'Chhaava,' a biographical account of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, directed by Laxman Utekar, starring Vicky Kaushal, has completed five successful weeks at the box office.
