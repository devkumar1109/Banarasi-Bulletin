Dubai Job Scam: Hyderabad Arrests Call Center Fraudster

2025-02-25T10:15:00+00:00

Hyderabad cybercrime officials arrested Dharmender Kumar alias Rohan (30) from Uttar Pradesh for job fraud. He operated a call center in New Delhi, cheating job seekers with promises of jobs in Dubai. Kumar was involved in five cases across India, including one in Hyderabad where he defrauded a 49-year-old man of Rs 15.8 lakh. He used fake job postings and impersonated reputable companies to lure victims. Kumar and his accomplices used professional language, conducted fake interviews, and created fake employee profiles to build trust. They ultimately tricked victims into providing personal and financial information.
