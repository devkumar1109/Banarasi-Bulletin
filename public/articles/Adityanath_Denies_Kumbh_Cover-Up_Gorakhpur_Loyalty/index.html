Adityanath Denies Kumbh Cover-Up, Gorakhpur Loyalty

2025-03-09T08:13:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath refuted claims that his government concealed deaths during the Maha Kumbh stampede on January 29, where 30 pilgrims lost their lives. Adityanath also stated his political future lies with Gorakhpur, rejecting any notion of being a successor to any political position, and emphasizing his preference to return there over moving to Delhi.
