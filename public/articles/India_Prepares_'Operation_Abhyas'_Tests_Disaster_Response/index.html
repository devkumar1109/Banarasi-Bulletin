India Prepares: 'Operation Abhyas' Tests Disaster Response

2025-05-07T13:46:00+00:00

Nationwide mock drills, codenamed 'Operation Abhyas,' are being conducted across various locations in India. These drills involve activating sirens, practicing evacuations, and rehearsing disaster response procedures. The drills aim to enhance preparedness and coordination in handling emergencies.
