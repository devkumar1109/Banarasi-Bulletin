India Triumphs, Tragedy Strikes Young Fan

2025-03-15T06:37:00+00:00

The Indian men's cricket team won their third ICC Champions Trophy title, defeating New Zealand in a historic victory. Tragically, during the match, 14-year-old Priyanshi from Uttar Pradesh died of a heart attack while watching with her family. Speculation arose linking her death to Virat Kohli's early dismissal, but her father, Ajay Pandey, refuted these claims, stating she collapsed before the Indian batting lineup began. Pandey stated he was away at the market when the incident occurred and rushed her to the hospital, but she was pronounced dead upon arrival. The family emphasized the incident was a coincidence and urged people to avoid unfounded rumors.
