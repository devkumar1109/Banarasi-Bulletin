Varanasi Rape: Teen Drugged, Held Hostage, Repeatedly Assaulted

2025-04-07T13:40:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by seven men over a week (March 29 to April 4). The victim, a 12th-grade student, was lured to a hookah bar by a friend, drugged, and taken to various hotels where she was held captive and repeatedly raped. Six men have been arrested, while the search continues for the remaining suspects. The victim's statement revealed that some of the perpetrators were her social media friends and former classmates. An FIR has been registered under sections including gang rape, wrongful confinement, criminal intimidation, and administering harmful substances.
