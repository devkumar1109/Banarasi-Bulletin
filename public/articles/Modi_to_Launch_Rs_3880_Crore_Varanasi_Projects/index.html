Modi to Launch Rs 3,880 Crore Varanasi Projects

2025-04-09T18:33:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, on Friday, April 11, 2025, to launch projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore. Key projects include road infrastructure development, such as a bridge between Varanasi Ring Road and Sarnath, flyovers, and a highway underpass. Modi will also inaugurate and lay the foundation stone for various transmission substations and upgrades to the city's electricity distribution system. He will inaugurate a transit hostel and barracks for security personnel and lay the foundation stone for new administrative buildings at police stations. Additionally, Modi will inaugurate educational institutions, rural libraries, anganwadi centers, and sports infrastructure projects.
