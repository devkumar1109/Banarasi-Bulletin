BLW Employee Throws Gold in Taiwan

2025-06-09T19:09:00+00:00

Indian athlete Rohit Yadav, an office superintendent at Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), won the gold medal in the men's javelin throw at the Taiwan Athletics Open 2025. Yadav achieved a throw of 74.42 meters, securing victory in the event held on June 7-8 in Taipei. This win highlights the growing prowess of Indian athletes on the global stage and showcases BLW's support for sporting talent.
