Varanasi: Purvanchal's Rising Industrial Powerhouse

2025-05-26T17:07:00+00:00

Varanasi is emerging as a major industrial hub in Purvanchal, Uttar Pradesh, under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's leadership. Improved law and order and infrastructure, along with initiatives like the Global Investors Summit, are driving this growth. Already operational in Varanasi are 48 industrial projects worth Rs 1,180.95 crore, generating 3,472 jobs. These include companies like Saaswad Heat Transfer and Engineering Private Limited, Hilton Hotels Group's Double Tree franchise, and Amazon's contracted warehouse with Madhavan LLP. Additionally, Banaras Beads is collaborating with Tata's Indian Hotels Company Limited to build the Ginger Budget Hotel. Another 48 projects worth Rs 5,702.18 crore are expected to launch soon, creating 12,110 jobs.
