Gorakhpur Gets Global Cricket Stadium

2025-05-20T09:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will have its fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur, built on a 50-acre plot at Tal Nadaur. Estimated to cost ₹236.40 crore, the stadium will accommodate 30,000 spectators and is expected to be completed within 18 months. The project, initiated by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, will feature a 45-acre main complex with entry gates, security checks, stands, and pavilions. The stadium will have parking for 1,500 vehicles, two stands with 14,490 seats each, a North Pavilion with VIP and media areas, and seven main cricket pitches along with four practice pitches. The stadium is designed to meet global standards and will also serve as a multi-purpose venue for large-scale events.
