UP Gears Up: Handball Cluster Kicks Off

2025-04-08T00:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-2025 in Lucknow. The tournament features 75 teams and 1,341 players, representing various state police departments and central forces. Adityanath highlighted the state's investment in sports infrastructure, including 84 stadiums, 67 multipurpose halls, and specialized facilities like wrestling halls and shooting ranges. Over 65,000 individuals in village-level Mangal Dal groups have received sports kits to promote grassroots development. Uttar Pradesh has recruited over 500 athletes into the police force and houses 740 players in 44 sports hostels across 18 districts. Three sports colleges in Gorakhpur, Lucknow, and Etawah offer world-class infrastructure and trained instructors.
