Varanasi Shiv Baraat: Tradition vs. Kumbh Crowd

2025-02-26T18:41:00+00:00

The traditional Shiv Baraat procession in Varanasi, originally scheduled for Thursday due to heavy crowds from the Maha Kumbh, was moved back to Mahashivratri evening following intervention from Minister Ravindra Jaiswal. The procession, featuring a tableau showcasing the Maha Kumbh and a "Masane ki Holi" performance, began at various city locations and culminated at Chittaranjan Park. The decision to postpone the event sparked debate with opposition parties criticizing the break from tradition.
