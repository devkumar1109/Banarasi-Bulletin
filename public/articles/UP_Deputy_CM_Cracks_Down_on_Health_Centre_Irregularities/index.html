UP Deputy CM Cracks Down on Health Centre Irregularities

2025-04-17T18:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak conducted a surprise inspection at the Harnahee Community Health Centre, criticizing health officials for operational irregularities. Pathak demanded full-time doctor availability at the CHC and mandated periodic community meetings to improve public trust in healthcare services. He warned against private practice by government physicians and plans to conduct more surprise inspections. Pathak emphasized the need to improve public healthcare and make government hospitals better than private facilities.
