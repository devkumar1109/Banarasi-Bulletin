UP Mandates Birth Reporting in Hospitals

2025-06-13T05:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has made childbirth reporting mandatory for private hospitals. This policy aims to improve maternal and child health outcomes by tracking births and identifying potential issues.
