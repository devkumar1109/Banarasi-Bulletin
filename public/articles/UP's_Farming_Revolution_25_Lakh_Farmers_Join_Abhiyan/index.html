UP's Farming Revolution: 2.5 Lakh Farmers Join Abhiyan

2025-05-31T18:46:00+00:00

The Vikasit Krishi Sankalp Abhiyan 2025, launched by Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, has seen the participation of nearly 2.5 lakh farmers in its first few days. The scheme, aimed at boosting agricultural productivity and farmer income, targets 50 lakh farmers in UP and 1.5 crore nationally. Farmer-scientist dialogues are being organized across 75 districts, with 135 locations per district. Union and state ministers participated in events across various villages, discussing new agricultural technologies and government schemes with farmers. Topics covered include horticulture, plant protection, soil testing, balanced fertilizer use, animal husbandry, organic farming, and marketing.
