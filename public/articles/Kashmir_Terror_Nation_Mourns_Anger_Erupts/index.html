Kashmir Terror: Nation Mourns, Anger Erupts

2025-04-23T12:02:00+00:00

A terrorist attack in Pahalgam, Jammu and Kashmir, killed at least 26 people, mostly tourists. The attack sparked widespread condemnation in Uttar Pradesh, with political parties and social organizations expressing grief and outrage. Samajwadi Party president Akhilesh Yadav criticized the BJP for politicizing the tragedy and accused them of security lapses. Protests and condolences were held in cities like Lucknow and Varanasi. Religious and community leaders called for swift action against the perpetrators.
