Varanasi Jewelers Shot, Robbers on the Run

2025-03-09T19:32:00+00:00

A jeweller, Siyaram (53), and his son Vikas (24) were shot and robbed in Varanasi, India. The incident occurred on Sunday afternoon when three unidentified men on a motorcycle entered their shop and fired at them before fleeing with cash and jewellery. Both victims were rushed to a private hospital and are reported to be out of danger. Police found CCTV footage of the robbers and have launched a massive checking drive in the district.
