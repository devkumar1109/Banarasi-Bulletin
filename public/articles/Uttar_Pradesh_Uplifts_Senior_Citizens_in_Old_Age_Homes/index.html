Uttar Pradesh Uplifts Senior Citizens in Old Age Homes

2025-04-04T01:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is improving healthcare facilities for senior citizens in old age homes. Under the Ayushman Bharat scheme, elderly residents will receive enhanced healthcare. Additionally, a program will utilize the professional expertise of seniors, offering financial incentives to promote self-reliance. Each old age home accommodates up to 150 residents and operates under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. The government also provides old-age pensions, religious pilgrimage trips, and a face-recognition attendance system for transparency. To address grievances, 216 reconciliation officers have been appointed at the tehsil level, and District Magistrate-led committees oversee old age home management.
