BMI Checks, Sports Boost Health for Students' Future

2025-03-11T19:42:00+00:00

Governor Anandiben Patel urged schools to incorporate Body Measurement Index (BMI) assessments to monitor children's health. Addressing a sports competition, she emphasized the importance of daily exercise for digestion and overall well-being, citing India's cricket victory as an example. Patel stressed the need for girls' participation in sports to promote physical fitness and encourage proper nutrition for future generations. She instructed officials to ensure regular medical check-ups for girls in primary education. Composite School, Pindra won the district-level PM Shri Annual Sports and Cultural Competition.
