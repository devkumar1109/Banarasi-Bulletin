Yogi Adityanath: "Terrorism to Swallow Pakistan"

2025-05-14T06:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath asserted that "one day terrorism will swallow Pakistan" following the successful execution of Operation Sindoor. He lauded Operation Sindoor as a response to Pakistan's transgressions, stating that those who threaten India's security will face dire consequences. Adityanath expressed gratitude to Prime Minister Modi for visiting the Adampur Air Base and bolstering the morale of the armed forces. CM Yogi also confirmed India's use of BrahMos missiles during the operation.
