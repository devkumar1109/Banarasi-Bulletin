Elderly Man Arrested for Child Rape in UP

2025-04-30T09:33:00+00:00

A 67-year-old man, Ramji Rawat, has been arrested in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh for the rape of a five-year-old girl. The incident occurred on Monday evening as the girl was walking to a religious site. Rawat allegedly assaulted her, and the girl reported the incident to her grandmother. The girl's father subsequently filed a police complaint. Police have registered a case under the relevant sections of the POCSO Act and sent Rawat to jail. The victim has been sent to the district hospital for a medical examination.
