Varanasi Shuts Meat Shops, Enforces Navratri Tradition

2025-03-29T02:04:00+00:00

For the first time, Varanasi will close all meat, fish, and poultry shops during Chaitra Navratri, starting Sunday. Mayor Ashok Kumar Tiwari emphasized strict compliance, despite Eid coinciding. He stated that the nine-day closure is a tradition and respects the sanctity of Navratri for Hindus. The executive committee also banned cigarette sales near religious sites and schools, while approving a licensing fee for cigarette vendors. Previously, the VMC had closed 26 non-vegetarian food shops near Kashi Vishwanath temple.
