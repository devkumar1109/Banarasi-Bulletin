Drunk Driver Kills Woman, Injures Daughter in Holi Rampage

2025-03-14T18:26:00+00:00

In Vadodara, Gujarat, a drunk driver, 20-year-old law student Rakshit Chaurasia, killed Hemani Patel while she was buying Holi colours with her daughter. The accident occurred around 12:30 am near Muktanand Crossroads. Chaurasia, who is from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was heard shouting "another round" after the crash. He has been arrested, while his friend and co-passenger, Mit Chauhan, is being investigated. Three others, including Patel's daughter, were injured and hospitalized. Chaurasia's car belonged to Chauhan.
