Uttar Pradesh Extends Food Business Registration Deadline

2025-05-07T13:11:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has extended its food business registration campaign until May 31. Initiated by the Food Safety and Drug Administration (FSDA) in April, the drive aims to ensure compliance with the Food Safety and Standards Act. All food businesses, from street vendors to restaurants, must register and obtain licenses by the deadline. Non-compliance will result in strict action, including closure of businesses. District-level officers are holding camps to facilitate registration and licensing. The campaign is aimed at protecting public health by regulating food operations and ensuring safety standards are met.
