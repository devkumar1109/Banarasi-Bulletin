BHU Students Rise: Deforestation Protests Echo Nationally

2025-04-04T10:23:00+00:00

Students at Banaras Hindu University protested against deforestation by the Telangana government near Hyderabad University. They condemned the destruction of 400 acres of forest land, claiming it harms wildlife and environmental protection. The protesters criticized both the Telangana and BJP governments for prioritizing corporate interests over environmental concerns and democratic values. They cited instances of deforestation in Chhattisgarh and Ladakh, alleging that the BJP government sold forests to Adani and corporations, displacing residents and harming the environment. The students expressed concern over the Telangana government's alleged police repression of protesting students and Chief Minister Revanth Reddy's anti-protester statements. They believe the alliance between politics and profit-driven corporations ultimately harms the common people.
