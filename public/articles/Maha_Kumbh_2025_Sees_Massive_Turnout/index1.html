"Divine Dipping Point: Maha Kumbh 2025 Breaks Records, Boosts Economy, and Inspires Philanthropy"

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

Here is a 150-word summary of the article: The Maha Kumbh 2025 has seen a massive turnout of over 62 crore devotees taking a holy dip at Triveni Sangam in Prayagraj in just 42 days. The event has had a significant impact on Kashi and Ayodhya, with over 2 crore devotees visiting the Kashi Vishwanath Temple and 69 lakh people booking homestays in Ayodhya. The Khel Maha Kumbh, a sports event, saw participation from over 800 young athletes and renowned sports personalities. The Maha Kumbh has set a new benchmark in waste management, with a "zero discharge" initiative ensuring no polluted water enters the river. The event is expected to boost Uttar Pradesh's GDP by Rs 3.25 to Rs 3.5 lakh crore. Over 5,670 people have pledged to donate their eyes, and more than one lakh people have been examined for their eyes. Several celebrities and leaders, including Prime Minister Narendra Modi, have visited the Maha Kumbh and praised the arrangements.
