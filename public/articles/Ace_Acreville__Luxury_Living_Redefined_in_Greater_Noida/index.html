Ace Acreville: Luxury Living Redefined in Greater Noida

2025-04-02T09:13:00+00:00

Ace Group launched its new residential township, Ace Acreville, in Greater Noida. The 100-acre township is located along the Yamuna Expressway and features world-class amenities and a focus on sustainable living. The launch event, held at the India Expo Mart, featured comedian Rohit Thakur and marked a significant milestone in the region's real estate landscape. Ace Acreville aims to redefine luxury living by offering a modern urban haven for its residents.
