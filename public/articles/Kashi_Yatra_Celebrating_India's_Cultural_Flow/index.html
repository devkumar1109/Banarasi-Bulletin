Kashi Yatra: Celebrating India's Cultural Flow

2025-03-07T15:02:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) hosted Kashi Yatra, a prestigious cultural festival, from March 7 to 9. This year's theme, 'Cultural Flow: The Cultural Heritage', celebrated India's rich cultural legacy. The festival was inaugurated by IIT (BHU) director Prof Amit Patra, who emphasized its importance in promoting cultural harmony and artistic expression. Renowned Bharatanatyam dancer Geeta Chandran opened the festival with a captivating performance, followed by a classical music evening by Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt. Kashi Yatra 2025 features various events like music, dance, literature, theatre, and folk art competitions and workshops.
