Modi Unveils Rs 3,880 Crore Development Bonanza for Varanasi

2025-04-09T18:33:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Varanasi on Friday and inaugurate development projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore. This includes road projects like a bridge between Varanasi Ring Road and Sarnath, flyovers, and a highway underpass tunnel, totaling over Rs 980 crore. He will also inaugurate transmission substations and lay the foundation stone for new ones, totaling over Rs 1,820 crore. Other projects include a transit hostel for police, new administrative buildings, a polytechnic college, a government college, rural libraries, anganwadi centers, and school renovations. Modi will also lay the foundation stone for a synthetic hockey turf and a mini-stadium, promoting sports infrastructure.
