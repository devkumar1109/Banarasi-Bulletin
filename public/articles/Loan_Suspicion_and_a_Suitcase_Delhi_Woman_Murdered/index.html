Loan, Suspicion, and a Suitcase: Delhi Woman Murdered

2025-06-06T12:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh police solved the murder of Neelesh from Delhi, whose body was found in a suitcase. Her partner, Satendra Yadav, confessed to strangling her due to financial disputes stemming from a ₹5.5 lakh loan she gave him, and suspicions of infidelity. Evidence collected includes CCTV footage and the vehicle used in the crime. Yadav disposed of the body near a canal road to avoid toll taxes. Authorities are preparing to file a charge sheet against him.
