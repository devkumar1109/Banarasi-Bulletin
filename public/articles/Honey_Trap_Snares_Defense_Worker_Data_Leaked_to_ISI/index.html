Honey Trap Snares Defense Worker, Data Leaked to ISI

2025-03-14T11:35:00+00:00

An employee of an ordnance factory in Firozabad, Uttar Pradesh, Ravindra Kumar, was arrested by the Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) for leaking confidential defense information to Pakistan's ISI. Kumar was lured into sharing sensitive documents, including daily production reports and stock details, through a honey trap orchestrated by a Pakistani operative named 'Neha'. The ATS, acting on intelligence, interrogated Kumar and confirmed his involvement in leaking classified information. The arrest highlights the threat posed by ISI operatives using honey traps to extract sensitive data. ADG of UP ATS, Nilabja Choudhary, urged sensitive institutions to strengthen security protocols and conduct thorough background checks on employees to prevent future breaches.
