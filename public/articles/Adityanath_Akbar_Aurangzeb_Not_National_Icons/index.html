Adityanath: Akbar, Aurangzeb Not National Icons

2025-03-08T15:54:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that Mughal emperors Akbar and Aurangzeb are not national icons. He characterized their mindset towards Hindus as the same and accused them of conspiring against India's traditions. Adityanath unveiled a statue of Maharana Pratap, praising his dedication to self-respect, faith, and the nation, calling him a "true national hero." He highlighted Maharana Pratap's resistance to Akbar's rule and his reclaiming of Mewar's lost territories. Adityanath also mentioned Veer Shivaji and Guru Gobind Singh as examples of India's true heroes.
