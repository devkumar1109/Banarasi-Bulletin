Azmi Defends Aurangzeb, Sparks Outrage

2025-03-03T16:47:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party leader Abu Azmi sparked controversy by defending Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb, stating he was "not a cruel ruler." Azmi cited Aurangzeb's construction of temples and alleged protection of a Hindu girl from a priest as evidence. He argued that power struggles during Aurangzeb's reign were political, not religious, and that his army included Hindus. Azmi criticized the "distorted" portrayal of Aurangzeb in modern films and denied he was a cruel ruler. Maharashtra Deputy CM Eknath Shinde condemned Azmi's remarks, demanding an apology and calling for treason charges.
