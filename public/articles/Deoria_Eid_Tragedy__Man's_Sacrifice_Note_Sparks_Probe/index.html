Deoria Eid Tragedy: Man's Sacrifice, Note Sparks Probe

2025-06-07T18:47:00+00:00

On Eid-ul-Azha in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, 60-year-old Ish Mohammed Ansari allegedly committed suicide by slitting his throat. He was found in a hut near his home. Local reports suggest he left a note indicating self-sacrifice for spiritual reasons, but police haven't confirmed this. Ansari was taken to the district medical college and later transferred to Gorakhpur Medical College, where he died from his injuries. Police are investigating the incident, considering all possibilities.
