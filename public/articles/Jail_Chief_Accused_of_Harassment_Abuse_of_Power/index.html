Jail Chief Accused of Harassment, Abuse of Power

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending an inquiry into a harassment complaint filed by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of corruption, harassment, and misconduct, claiming he pressured her for inappropriate activities and threatened her job when she resisted. She released a video and complaint letter, leading to her transfer to Naini central jail in Prayagraj. Kanojia's daughter filed a police complaint alleging Singh's mental and physical harassment, including obscene gestures and demands for sexual exploitation in exchange for benefits. A departmental probe is underway against both Singh and Kanojia.
