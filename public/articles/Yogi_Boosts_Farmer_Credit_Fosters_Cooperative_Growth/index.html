Yogi Boosts Farmer Credit, Fosters Cooperative Growth

2025-03-21T18:51:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath increased the credit limit for rural cooperative societies from Rs 10 lakh to Rs 15 lakh to support farmers. This announcement was made at the 61st annual meeting of UP Cooperative Banks. Adityanath highlighted the importance of cooperative banks for farmers, young entrepreneurs, and MSMEs. He encouraged banks to assist young entrepreneurs linked to the Mukhyamantri Yuva Udyami Vikas Abhiyan. Cooperative banking will be introduced in Balrampur district. The credit-deposit ratio of Uttar Pradesh Cooperative Banks has improved from 44% in 2017 to 61%.
