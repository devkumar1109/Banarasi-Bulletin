Mentally Ill Woman Found Dead in Canal

2025-04-13T10:23:00+00:00

The body of 50-year-old Rachna, a mentally unfit woman from Hapur district, was found in a dry canal in Hathras on April 13th. A 'dupatta' was found tied around her neck, leading police to suspect foul play. Rachna's family reported she had been mentally ill and left home every morning, returning in the evening, but didn't return on Saturday. Her husband is a farmer and she is survived by two adult sons. The body has been sent for post-mortem to determine the cause of death, and further action will be taken based on the report.
