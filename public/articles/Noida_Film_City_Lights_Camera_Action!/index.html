Noida Film City: Lights, Camera, Action!

2025-05-24T21:38:00+00:00

Construction of Noida Film City, Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's ambitious project, is expected to begin by the end of May or June. The project, located in YEIDA, aims to establish Uttar Pradesh as a major film production and entertainment hub. Bayview Projects LLP, owned by filmmaker Boney Kapoor, has been selected as the developer and has completed most preparatory work. The first phase will include 13-14 film studios and a film institute spread over 3 lakh sq meters. The entire project, covering 1,000 acres, is estimated to cost Rs 1,510 crore and will be completed in three phases over eight years.
