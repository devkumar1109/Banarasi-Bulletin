Lawrence Bishnoi Gang Shooter Eliminated in Encounter

2025-05-30T05:33:00+00:00

Naveen Kumar, a sharpshooter affiliated with the Lawrence Bishnoi gang, was killed in an encounter with a joint team of UP STF and Delhi Police Special Cell in Hapur district, Uttar Pradesh. Naveen, a resident of Loni in Ghaziabad, was wanted under MCOCA for over 20 serious criminal cases including murder and robbery. He was apprehended during a joint operation based on intelligence that he was heading to Hapur to commit a crime. During the operation, Naveen opened fire on the police team, leading to a retaliatory exchange which resulted in his death. His elimination is considered a significant setback to the Lawrence Bishnoi gang's activities in the region.
