Lucknow's New Museum: Arts, History and Growth

2025-03-29T15:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is constructing a new museum at Lucknow's Rashtra Prerna Sthal, aligning with Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's vision. The project, with a budget exceeding Rs 65 crore, is overseen by the Lucknow Development Authority and Uttar Pradesh Project Corporation Limited. An initial installment of Rs 22.55 crore has been disbursed. The museum will feature art installations and administrative checks for curation. Additionally, the project includes a cafeteria expansion at the Old Kothi in the State Museum and library expansions. The combined budget for these additions is Rs 4.87 crore.
