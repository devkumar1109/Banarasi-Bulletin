Jail Chief Accused, Deputy Seeks Justice

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending an inquiry into a harassment complaint filed against him by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment and harassing her for not fulfilling his demands. She claimed she and her family were living in fear. A video and complaint letter were released by Kanojia, prompting the DG (prisons) to order the inquiry. Kanojia's daughter also filed a police complaint against Singh, alleging mental and physical harassment, and inappropriate demands. The DIG (prisons) stated that investigations are ongoing, and action will be taken based on the findings.
