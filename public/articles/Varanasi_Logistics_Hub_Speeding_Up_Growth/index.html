Varanasi Logistics Hub: Speeding Up Growth

2025-05-05T19:00:00+00:00

Varanasi Division Commissioner S Rajalingam has directed officials to expedite the multi-modal terminal and freight village projects. The multi-modal terminal at Ramnagar will have a sewage treatment plant and multimedia modal jetty. A 100-acre Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP) is being developed on the Varanasi-Chandauli border. The MMLP will be connected to NH-7, NH-2, a 5.1 km railway line, and Ralhupur Multi-Modal Terminal, facilitating cargo transportation across India and through National Waterway-1 to Haldia. The projects are expected to generate investment and employment opportunities.
