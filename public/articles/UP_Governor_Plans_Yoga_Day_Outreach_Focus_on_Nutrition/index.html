U.P. Governor Plans Yoga Day Outreach, Focus on Nutrition

2025-05-07T15:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel chaired a virtual meeting to plan International Yoga Day celebrations on June 21st. She emphasized expanding yoga outreach and urged institutions to start celebrations on June 1st. Patel highlighted yoga's cultural significance and proposed initiatives involving local communities and schools. Universities were directed to conduct yoga sessions in coordination with Anganwadi centers and schools, implementing the Ayush Ministry's 10-point agenda. The Governor requested health reports on the benefits of these programs and advocated for BMI checks of female students to address malnutrition. She also suggested year-round nutrition plans and yoga sessions for pregnant women to reduce childbirth complications.
