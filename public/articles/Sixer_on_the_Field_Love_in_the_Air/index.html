Sixer on the Field, Love in the Air

2025-06-09T04:27:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Rinku Singh and Samajwadi Party MP Priya Saroj got engaged in Lucknow on June 8th. The intimate ceremony was followed by a social media announcement from the couple. Priya wore a light pink and white lehenga, while Rinku opted for a cream achkan with silver embroidery. The couple's wedding is planned for November 2025 in Varanasi. Priya is the daughter of three-time MP and current Samajwadi Party MLA, Toofani Saroj.
