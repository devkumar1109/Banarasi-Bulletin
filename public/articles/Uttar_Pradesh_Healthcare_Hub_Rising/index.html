Uttar Pradesh: Healthcare Hub Rising

2025-03-18T06:09:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has transformed into a healthcare hub with 80 operational medical colleges (44 government and 36 private) and a growing network of hospitals. The state has seen a significant increase in medical seats, with 5,250 in government medical colleges and 6,550 in private undergraduate courses. Additionally, 350 new seats were added in three new medical colleges established under the Public Private Partnership (PPP) model. Post-2017, Uttar Pradesh witnessed a surge in super-specialty hospitals, research institutes, and advancements in AYUSH medical education. The government has established an Ayurvedic College in Ayodhya and a government homeopathic medical college in Varanasi, further boosting the AYUSH sector. With 2,110 Ayurvedic, 254 Unani, and 1,585 Homeopathic hospitals, Uttar Pradesh continues to strengthen its healthcare infrastructure.
