Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train: Two Hours to a New Era

2025-03-24T13:29:00+00:00

The Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train is a proposed 760 km high-speed rail corridor connecting Varanasi and Howrah at speeds up to 350 km/h, significantly reducing travel time to just over two hours. The route will pass through key cities like Buxar, Arrah, Patna, Gaya, Dhanbad, Asansol, Durgapur, and Burdwan, with approximately 260 km of elevated tracks within Bihar. The project is currently in the Detailed Project Report (DPR) stage, with surveys underway to finalize the route and assess land requirements. Land acquisition has begun in Bihar, and officials await railway approval for construction to start.
