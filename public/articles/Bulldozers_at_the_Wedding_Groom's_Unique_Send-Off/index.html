Bulldozers at the Wedding: Groom's Unique Send-Off

2025-02-24T10:21:00+00:00

In Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, a groom named Rahul Yadav opted for a unique send-off for his bride, Karishma. Instead of traditional luxury cars, the wedding procession included nearly a dozen bulldozers, creating a surprising and memorable spectacle. The unexpected appearance of the heavy machinery initially startled onlookers, given the recent association of bulldozers with the UP government's crackdown on rioters.
