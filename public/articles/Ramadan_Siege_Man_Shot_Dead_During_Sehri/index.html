Ramadan Siege: Man Shot Dead During Sehri

2025-03-15T03:55:00+00:00

A man named Haris was shot dead in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh while waiting to have sehri, the pre-dawn meal during Ramadan. The incident occurred around 3:15 am on Friday (March 14) when four assailants on motorcycles opened fire, striking Haris with seven bullets. CCTV footage shows one attacker shooting Haris multiple times, followed by another firing three more shots. Haris was rushed to the hospital but succumbed to his injuries. Police suspect the killing was due to an enmity but are investigating other possibilities. A case has been registered, and the probe is ongoing.
