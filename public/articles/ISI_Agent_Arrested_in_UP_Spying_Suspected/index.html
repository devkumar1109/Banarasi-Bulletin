ISI Agent Arrested in UP, Spying Suspected

2025-05-18T22:14:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested Shahzad, a suspected ISI agent, in Moradabad on May 18th. Shahzad is accused of spying for Pakistan's intelligence agency, sharing sensitive information, and aiding ISI operations in India. He allegedly worked under the guise of a cross-border smuggler, establishing contact with ISI handlers during his trafficking activities. Shahzad is accused of providing sensitive information to the ISI and facilitating the movement of individuals from Uttar Pradesh into Pakistan to work for the agency. He also procured Indian SIM cards for ISI operatives in Pakistan. The investigation is ongoing, and Shahzad is being legally processed.
