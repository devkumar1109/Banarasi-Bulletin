Gorakhpur Gears Up for Cricket Spectacle

2025-05-20T09:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will soon have its fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur. The ₹236.40 crore project, announced by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, will be built on a 50-acre plot in Tal Nadaur and is expected to accommodate 30,000 spectators. The stadium, designed by the planning department, will have a 45-acre main complex with entry gates, security checks, stands, and pavilions. Construction is set to begin soon and is estimated to take 18 months. The stadium will feature seven main pitches and four practice pitches, along with parking for 1,500 vehicles. The North Pavilion will include VIP seats and media facilities. Besides international cricket matches, the multi-purpose venue will host large-scale events.
