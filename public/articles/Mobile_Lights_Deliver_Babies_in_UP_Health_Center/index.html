Mobile Lights Deliver Babies in UP Health Center

2025-05-30T09:36:00+00:00

Four women delivered babies at a government health centre in Uttar Pradesh with the aid of mobile phone lights. This occurred due to the absence of electricity at the facility. The incident highlights the lack of basic infrastructure in some government health centres, especially in rural areas.
