Caste Census Approved: India Celebrates Landmark Decision

2025-05-01T07:02:00+00:00

The Union Cabinet approved a caste-based census, a "historic decision" celebrated by Varanasi residents with drums and colored powder. Residents hailed Prime Minister Narendra Modi's decision, calling it a landmark step towards inclusive policymaking. Union Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw announced the inclusion of caste-based data in the upcoming national census, stating it would be conducted transparently. The national census, originally scheduled for April 2020 but delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic, will now include caste data.
