Varanasi: From Heritage to Industrial Hub

2025-05-26T17:07:00+00:00

Under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, Varanasi is emerging as a major industrial hub in Uttar Pradesh's Purvanchal region. Improved infrastructure and a business-friendly environment, fostered by initiatives like the Global Investors Summit, are driving this growth. Currently, 48 industrial projects worth Rs 1,180.95 crore are operational in Varanasi, generating 3,472 jobs. These include companies like Saaswad Heat Transfer and Engineering Private Limited, Hilton Hotels Group's franchise Double Tree, and Amazon's contracted warehouse with Madhavan LLP. Additionally, 48 more projects worth Rs 5,702.18 crore are in the pipeline, projected to create 12,110 jobs. Notable among these is Banaras Beads' collaboration with Tata's Indian Hotels Company Limited to build the Ginger Budget Hotel.
