Uttar Pradesh: Worksheets for Inclusive Learning

2025-06-12T05:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has launched "Sugamya Worksheets" in collaboration with UNICEF to support nearly 3 lakh children with special needs. The worksheets, covering Hindi and Mathematics for classes 1 to 8, are designed to enhance foundational learning and integrate these children into mainstream education. Developed with simple language, vibrant visuals, and engaging formats, the worksheets are being distributed in phases to ensure individual student progress. Teachers will guide students through the worksheets using various activities, and special educators will provide support and monitor progress. The state government views this initiative as a significant step towards inclusive education.
