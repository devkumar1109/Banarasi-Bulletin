UP Budget 2025-26: Rs 8.08 Lakh Crore Passed

2025-03-04T14:35:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Assembly has passed the state's budget of over Rs 8 lakh crore for the financial year 2025-26. Finance Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna presented the budget of Rs 8.08 lakh crore on February 20th. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath supported the budget and urged the Assembly to pass it. The budget was approved by a majority vote after a thorough discussion.
