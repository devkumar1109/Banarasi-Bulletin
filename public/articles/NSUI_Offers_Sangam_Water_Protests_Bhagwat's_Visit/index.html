NSUI Offers Sangam Water, Protests Bhagwat's Visit

2025-04-04T17:00:00+00:00

NSUI activists protested against RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat's visit to Varanasi on Thursday, attempting to block his convoy. They aimed to offer him Sangam water from the Maha Kumbh, symbolizing their belief that Hindutva should be practiced, not just politically espoused. The activists, led by Vikas Singh, shouted slogans and confronted Bhagwat's convoy in the Sigra area. This resulted in an altercation with police, leading to the detention of several NSUI members.
