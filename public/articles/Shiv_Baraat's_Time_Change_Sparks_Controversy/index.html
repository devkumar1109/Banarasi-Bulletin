Shiv Baraat's Time Change Sparks Controversy

2025-02-25T10:18:00+00:00

The annual 'Shiv Baraat' procession in Varanasi, traditionally held on Maha Shivratri, has been rescheduled to the day after due to expected crowd influx from the Kumbh Mela. This is the first change in the 42-year-old tradition. The decision has sparked political controversy, with Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai criticizing the BJP government for allegedly interfering with the city's cultural practices. Rai has called upon Prime Minister Modi, who is the MP for Varanasi, to ensure the procession proceeds on its traditional schedule.
