Sustainable Style Wins: Anjana Bohra Founder Awarded

2025-03-01T10:21:00+00:00

Ankita Bohra, founder of sustainable fashion brand Anjana Bohra, is a recipient of the Womenpreneur Awards 2025. Launched in 2021, Anjana Bohra employs 50 people and provides livelihoods for over 500 artisans across India, specializing in modern designs using traditional textiles like Bandhani and Banarasi. The brand's "Best out of Waste" initiative transforms leftover fabrics into accessories, minimizing waste. Anjana Bohra's commitment to sustainability and inclusivity has driven 50% year-on-year revenue growth and a 15% profit after tax. Ankita collaborates with underrepresented artisan communities, preserving ancient techniques and empowering families. The brand embraces inclusivity with diverse models and custom fits, catering to all body types. Anjana Bohra has garnered a loyal following of over 150,000 on Instagram and 20,000 on Facebook.
