India Sets Deadline for Pakistani Nationals' Exit

2025-04-26T19:42:00+00:00

The Indian Home Ministry (MHA) has set a deadline for police departments to ensure the exit of Pakistani nationals from India. The deadline is today, April 26, 2025. This directive comes as part of ongoing efforts to regulate the presence of foreign nationals in the country.
