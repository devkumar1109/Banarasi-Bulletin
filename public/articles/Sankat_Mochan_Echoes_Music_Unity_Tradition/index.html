Sankat Mochan Echoes: Music, Unity, Tradition

2025-04-17T08:05:00+00:00

The 102-year-old Sankat Mochan music festival commenced in Varanasi with a flute recital by Padma Vibhushan awardee Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia. The six-day festival, concluding on April 21st, is held at the Sankat Mochan Hanuman temple and features 11 Padma awardees and 16 emerging artistes, including Muslim performers, upholding its tradition of inclusivity. This year's event marks the 102nd edition and continues the festival's legacy of spiritual depth and artistic excellence, attracting audiences who have been attending for decades.
