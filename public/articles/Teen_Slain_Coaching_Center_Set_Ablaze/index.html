Teen Slain, Coaching Center Set Ablaze

2025-03-05T11:40:00+00:00

Two individuals have been arrested and jailed for the murder of 17-year-old Divyanshu Kumar, a Class XII student, in West Champaran district. Divyanshu was attacked and stabbed to death outside a coaching center on Tuesday evening after a clash between current and former students. His brother witnessed the attack and rushed him to the hospital, but he was pronounced dead. Assailants also set fire to the coaching center, but the flames were extinguished by fire tenders. Police are investigating the motive behind the clash and are reviewing CCTV footage to identify additional suspects.
