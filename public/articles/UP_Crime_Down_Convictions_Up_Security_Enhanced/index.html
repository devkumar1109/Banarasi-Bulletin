UP: Crime Down, Convictions Up, Security Enhanced

2025-02-24T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state's law and order has become a model for the country, citing a decline in crime rates according to NCRB data. He highlights a significant rise in convictions for crimes against women, with 51 death sentences and 6,287 life sentences handed down in Operation Conviction from July 2023 to December 2024. Adityanath also points to the arrest of 130 terrorists and 171 Rohingyas by the strengthened Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) since 2017. The government has implemented police commissionerate systems in major cities and established new ATS field units. Additionally, three women PAC battalions are being formed, and over 1.56 lakh personnel have been recruited into various police positions.
