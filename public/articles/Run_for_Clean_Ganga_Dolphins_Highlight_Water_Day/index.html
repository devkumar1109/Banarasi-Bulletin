Run for Clean Ganga, Dolphins Highlight Water Day

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, 'Run for Clean Ganga', and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized jointly by the Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw enthusiastic participation from residents and foreigners. Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal emerged as the winners in the men's and women's categories, respectively. The forest department, in collaboration with NGO Dolphin Friends, celebrated 'World Water Day' and 'Earth Hour' at Dhakwa Ghat with a dolphin safari launched to educate the public about dolphin conservation. The events aimed to raise awareness about the importance of freshwater, sustainable management of water sources, and the need to protect the Gangetic Dolphin.
