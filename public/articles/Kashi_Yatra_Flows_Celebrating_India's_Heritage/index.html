Kashi Yatra Flows: Celebrating India's Heritage

2025-03-07T15:02:00+00:00

Kashi Yatra, IIT (BHU)'s annual cultural festival, commenced on March 7th with the theme "Cultural Flow: The Cultural Heritage". The three-day festival, running until March 9th, aims to showcase India's rich cultural legacy. Renowned Bharatanatyam dancer Geeta Chandran inaugurated the event with a captivating performance, followed by Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt's mesmerizing classical music recital. Kashi Yatra features a diverse range of programs, competitions, and workshops encompassing music, dance, literature, theatre, and folk art. IIT (BHU) director Prof Amit Patra emphasized the festival's importance in promoting cultural harmony and artistic expression, connecting the younger generation to their roots.
