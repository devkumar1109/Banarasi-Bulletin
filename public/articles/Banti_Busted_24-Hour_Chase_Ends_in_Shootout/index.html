Banti Busted: 24-Hour Chase Ends in Shootout

2025-04-04T17:02:00+00:00

Wanted criminal Rajesh Singh alias Banti, who shot at a man named Nishant in Varanasi on Wednesday night, was arrested within 24 hours. Police recovered an illegal pistol, live and used cartridges from Banti, who sustained a bullet injury during the encounter. Banti was wanted in 33 cases across various districts. He admitted to shooting Nishant following a dispute over overtaking. Nishant works at an e-marketing company warehouse and was returning home on his motorcycle when attacked by four car-borne assailants.
