Airbag, Pothole, and Panic: Vadodara Crash Kills One

2025-03-15T07:14:00+00:00

A 20-year-old man, Rakshit Chaurasiya, crashed his vehicle into five people in Vadodara, India, killing one woman and injuring eight others. Chaurasiya, a law student, claims the accident occurred when his view was obstructed by the emergency airbag after hitting a pothole. He denies being drunk. Police suspect Chaurasia was driving under the influence and are investigating the incident. The deceased woman has been identified as Hemali Patel. Chaurasiya, who sustained facial injuries, maintains that he was driving at 50 kilometers per hour and only saw a scooter and another vehicle ahead.
