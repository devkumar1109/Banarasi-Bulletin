Uttar Pradesh Storm: 15 Dead, Widespread Damage

2025-05-22T15:09:00+00:00

A severe storm swept through Uttar Pradesh, claiming 15 lives and causing widespread damage. The storm triggered incidents like tree falls, collapsed walls, and power outages. Meerut was particularly hit, with three deaths, including a doctor, due to rain-related incidents. Saharanpur district saw two deaths from lightning strikes. A fire engulfed an oil factory in Budaun, fueled by strong winds and the presence of chemicals. A powerful explosion occurred at the factory, but no casualties were reported. In Bijnor, a constable died after his motorcycle hit a fallen tree. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath ordered immediate relief and compensation measures for the affected families.
