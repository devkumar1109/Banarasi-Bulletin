Perfect Score: Uttar Pradesh Teen Tops JEE Main 2025

2025-03-07T02:48:00+00:00

Chirag Singh, a 17-year-old from Uttar Pradesh, topped the JEE Main 2025 January session with a 100 percentile score. He aims to pursue Computer Science Engineering at IIT Bombay, followed by IIT Delhi and Kanpur. Despite achieving a perfect score, Chirag will also take the April session to further improve his marks. He balanced his studies with coaching at Allen Online and dedicated time to revision and assignments. Chirag credits his success to his passion for Physics, Maths, and Chemistry, and his family's support. Over 12.58 lakh candidates appeared for JEE Main 2025, with 14 students, including one female, achieving a 100 NTA score.
