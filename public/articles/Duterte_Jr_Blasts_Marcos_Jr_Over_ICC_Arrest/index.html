Duterte Jr. Blasts Marcos Jr. Over ICC Arrest

2025-03-16T13:30:00+00:00

Davao City Mayor Sebastian Duterte publicly criticized President Ferdinand Marcos Jr. during the Araw ng Dabaw celebration. He expressed anger over the International Criminal Court (ICC)'s arrest of his father, former President Rodrigo Duterte, accusing Marcos Jr. of betrayal. Duterte recalled allowing Marcos Sr.'s burial at the Libingan ng mga Bayani in 2016, emphasizing the perceived lack of loyalty from Marcos Jr. The incident has intensified the tension between the two political families following the ICC arrest, which has drawn attention from both local and international observers.
