Bollywood Stars Grace Maha Kumbh Mela 2025

2025-02-25T18:47:00+00:00

Singer Udit Narayan attended the Maha Kumbh Mela 2025 with his wife Deepa, expressing gratitude to the Indian and Uttar Pradesh governments for organizing the event. Several Bollywood celebrities, including Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif, Vicky Kaushal, Raveena Tandon, and Anupam Kher, also participated in the Mela, drawn by its spiritual significance. The Kumbh Mela, held every 12 years, is a major spiritual gathering that attracts millions. Udit Narayan, who began his career in the 1980s, has sung numerous songs in 36 languages and won numerous awards.
