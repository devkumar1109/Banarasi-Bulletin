Instagram Confession: Brothers Kill for Past Revenge

2025-05-21T05:16:00+00:00

In Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, 55-year-old Ravindra Singh was shot dead by two brothers, Vikki and Nikhil Kumar. The brothers confessed to the murder on Instagram, claiming revenge for Singh's elopement with their mother 20 years ago. Police have registered a murder case under BNS section 103 and are searching for the absconding brothers. They are considering invoking the Gangster Act. Locals say Singh eloped with the brothers' mother, who later returned, but the sons harbored a grudge against him.
