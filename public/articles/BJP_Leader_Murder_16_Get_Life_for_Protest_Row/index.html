BJP Leader Murder: 16 Get Life for Protest Row

2025-06-13T15:34:00+00:00

A fast track court in Varanasi sentenced 16 men to life imprisonment for the murder of local BJP leader Pashupati Nath Singh. The incident occurred in October 2022 after Singh protested against open drinking near his house. An altercation escalated, resulting in Singh and his son Rajan being attacked with sticks and iron rods. Pashupati Singh died from his injuries while Rajan sustained severe injuries. Police initially booked 18 individuals, with 16 arrested. The trial for the remaining two continues. Rajan Singh pursued justice for his father.
