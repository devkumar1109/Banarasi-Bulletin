Padma Awards: Honoring Excellence Across Fields

2025-04-28T17:36:00+00:00

President Droupadi Murmu conferred Padma Awards to 71 individuals, including four Padma Vibhushan, 10 Padma Bhushan, and 57 Padma Shri awards. The recipients represent diverse fields such as art, trade, industry, sports, literature, and spirituality. Notable awardees include violinist L. Subramaniam, filmmaker Shekhar Kapur, actor Nandamuri Balakrishna, and cricketer Ravichandran Ashwin. The late Osamu Suzuki, former CEO of Suzuki Motor Corporation, received the Padma Vibhushan posthumously. Vedic scholar Ganeshwar Shastri Dravid was honored for his contributions to determining auspicious timings for religious ceremonies. American author Stephen Knapp received the Padma Shri for his work on Vedic culture and spirituality.
