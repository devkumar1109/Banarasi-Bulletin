Kashi Yatra: IIT BHU Celebrates India's Cultural Tapestry

2025-03-07T15:02:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) in Varanasi inaugurated Kashi Yatra, a prestigious cultural festival, on March 7th. The three-day event, themed "Cultural Flow: The Cultural Heritage", celebrates India's rich artistic legacy. The festival features performances, competitions, and workshops in music, dance, literature, theatre, and folk art, bringing together artists and enthusiasts from across the country. The inauguration included a Bharatanatyam performance by Geeta Chandran and a classical music performance by Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt. IIT (BHU) director, Prof Amit Patra, emphasized the festival's importance in promoting cultural harmony and connecting younger generations to their roots.
