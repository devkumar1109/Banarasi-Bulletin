Selfie Point Sparks Patriotism, Controversy

2025-06-06T02:49:00+00:00

The Moradabad Municipal Corporation created a selfie point themed on Operation Sindoor, aimed at promoting patriotism among youth. The initiative celebrates India's response to a recent terror attack in Kashmir's Pahalgam. The spot, featuring figures like Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, sparked controversy after three youths alleged mistreatment by security personnel. Operation Sindoor was launched against threats from Pakistan and Pakistan-occupied Kashmir, an event that led some families to name their newborns "Sindoor."
