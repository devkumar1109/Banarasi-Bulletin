Heat-Proof Crops: Minister Tackles Farmer Challenges

2025-06-11T23:06:00+00:00

Union minister Ramnath Thakur urged farmers to adopt heat-tolerant crop varieties during a dialogue program organized by the Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR). He emphasized the importance of research benefiting farmers and stressed the need to disseminate new technologies and motivate their adoption. Thakur also inspected a demonstration of the direct seeded rice method implemented by IRRI, a sustainable technique that saves water and increases production. He honored five farmers and provided 10 with Digital Soil Health Cards. During the program, farmers discussed challenges like nilgai damage, water scarcity, pest management, market access, and seed availability. Thakur directed immediate solutions to these issues.
