Varanasi Preps for Global Yoga Day Celebration

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for International Yoga Day on June 21st. A series of events, Yoga Week, will begin on June 15th, culminating in the main event at Kashi Vishwanath Dham on June 21st. Yoga sessions will be held at various ghats, village panchayats, urban areas, schools, and health centers. The district magistrate has instructed officials to ensure all preparations are complete for Yoga Week's opening at Namo Ghat. Yoga organizations are encouraged to conduct daily sessions, and the youth welfare officer will work with Yuvak Mangal Dal members to train them in yoga protocols for village sessions.
