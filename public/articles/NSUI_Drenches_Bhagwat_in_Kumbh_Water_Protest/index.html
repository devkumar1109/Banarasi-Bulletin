NSUI Drenches Bhagwat in Kumbh Water Protest

2025-04-04T09:43:00+00:00

NSUI activists protested against RSS Chief Mohan Bhagwat during his four-day visit to Varanasi. They attempted to offer him Maha Kumbh water, criticizing his absence from the religious ritual and questioning his commitment to Sanatan tradition. The activists, led by Vikash Singh, alleged that Bhagwat only engages in the politics of Hindutva but avoids practicing it. The police detained several NSUI activists during the protest.
