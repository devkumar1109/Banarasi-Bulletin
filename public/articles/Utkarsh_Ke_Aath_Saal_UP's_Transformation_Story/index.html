Utkarsh Ke Aath Saal: UP's Transformation Story

2025-03-27T19:00:00+00:00

The three-day event 'Utkarsh Ke Aath Saal' concluded in Gorakhpur, marking eight years of the Uttar Pradesh government's policies. Fisheries minister Sanjay Nishad highlighted the state's transformation under CM Yogi Adityanath, citing improved road and rail connectivity, employment generation, and welfare schemes. He emphasized UP's progress from a crime-ridden state to a thriving economy, now India's second-largest. Nishad credited PM Modi's vision and CM Yogi's innovations for successful pandemic management and the 'One District, One Product' initiative. He also noted the empowerment of the fishing community through welfare schemes and healthcare benefits.
