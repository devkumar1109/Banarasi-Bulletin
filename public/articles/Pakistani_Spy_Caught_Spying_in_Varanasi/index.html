Pakistani Spy Caught Spying in Varanasi

2025-05-22T18:17:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested Pakistani spy Tufail in Varanasi for allegedly sending sensitive intelligence information to handlers in Pakistan. The ATS had been tracking Tufail's movements and gathered intelligence on his espionage activities. The investigation revealed Tufail's involvement in gathering and transmitting sensitive information to Pakistan. The nature of the information and the methods used by Tufail are currently under investigation. Authorities are probing whether Tufail acted alone or as part of a larger network. The arrest raised concerns about the security of sensitive information in the state.
