Gorakhpur Gears Up for International Cricket Glory

2025-05-19T11:35:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is constructing its fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's dream project, the stadium will be built on a 50-acre site at Tal Nadaur. The Rs 236.40 crore project is expected to be completed in 18 months. The stadium will have seven main pitches and four practice pitches with a seating capacity of 30,000 spectators. It will be designed to meet International Cricket Council (ICC) standards and host international cricket matches and other large-scale events. The stadium will be accessible via a connecting road to NH-24.
