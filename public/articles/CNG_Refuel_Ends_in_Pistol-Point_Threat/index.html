CNG Refuel Ends in Pistol-Point Threat

2025-06-16T10:08:00+00:00

In Hardoi, Uttar Pradesh, a girl identified as Surush Khan alias Ariba Khan threatened a petrol pump worker with a revolver. The incident occurred when the worker asked Khan to step out of her car during CNG refueling. Khan became enraged and pointed the gun at the worker's chest, threatening to shoot him. The worker, Rajnish Kumar, tried to de-escalate the situation but Khan and her family allegedly assaulted him. The entire incident was captured on CCTV. Police arrived promptly, taking Khan, her family, and the weapon into custody. A case has been registered under multiple sections of the IPC, and the CCTV footage will be crucial to the investigation.
