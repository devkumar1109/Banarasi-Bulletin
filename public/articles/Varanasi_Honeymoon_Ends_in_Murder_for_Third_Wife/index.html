Varanasi: Honeymoon Ends in Murder for Third Wife

2025-05-17T10:04:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, Raju Pal, killed his third wife Aarti Pal (26), just a week after their marriage. Aarti, from Jaunpur district, was Raju's third wife after two previous marriages ended in separation. Clashes were reported between the couple immediately after their wedding on May 9th. Police have apprehended Raju for the murder.
