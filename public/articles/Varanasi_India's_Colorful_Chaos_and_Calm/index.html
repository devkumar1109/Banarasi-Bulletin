Varanasi: India's Colorful Chaos and Calm

2025-03-17T15:00:00+00:00

Varanasi, India's oldest city, offers a vibrant cultural and spiritual experience. Tourists from Germany and England express their love for the city's unique atmosphere, finding it both loud and beautiful. Despite the city's bustling nature, visitors appreciate the calmness and colorfulness. Many compare Varanasi to Thailand but find it more hectic, with Holi festivities adding to the energy. Visitors have also traveled through Rajasthan, visiting cities like Jaisalmer, Jaipur, and Agra, highlighting the diverse experiences India offers. Although they expected all of India to be like Varanasi, they found it busier elsewhere. Overall, they appreciate the hospitality and vibrant culture of India.
