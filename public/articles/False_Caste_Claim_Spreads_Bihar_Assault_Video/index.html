False Caste Claim Spreads Bihar Assault Video

2025-04-15T14:47:00+00:00

A video circulating on social media showing a man and woman being harassed and assaulted by several men is falsely claimed to depict a Dalit couple attacked for passing through an 'upper caste road' in Uttar Pradesh. The incident actually occurred in the Saran District of Bihar in 2021. Indian news sources Hindustan and Navbharat Times reported on the incident, stating that it took place on September 27, 2021, in the Chanwar area of Lakshmanchak. The Saran District Administration confirmed the incident and shared details of the offenders, location, and time. Four of the six accused were arrested.
