Hospital Heroes: Disaster Training Concludes at IMS-BHU

2025-03-08T18:54:00+00:00

A six-day disaster management training program concluded at IMS-BHU in Varanasi. The program, organized in collaboration with the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, focused on public health emergencies for hospital administrators. 37 participants from Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Haryana, and Daman and Diu learned about disaster preparedness, hospital safety, patient triage, evacuation procedures, and fire control. The training aimed to equip participants with the knowledge and skills to effectively manage public health emergencies in hospitals.
