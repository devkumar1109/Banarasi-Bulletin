Pan Masala Spitting Sparks UP Assembly Debate

2025-03-04T07:11:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh assembly speaker Satish Mahana revealed that some members have been spitting pan masala inside the House. He saw the incident on video but chose not to name the MLA publicly. Mahana cleaned the affected area himself and urged legislators to discourage such behavior, stressing the importance of maintaining decorum and cleanliness. He called for the responsible MLA to confess. This incident has sparked discussions about discipline within the Assembly. Meanwhile, Uttar Pradesh finance minister Suresh Kumar Khanna presented the state budget for 2025-26, which is 9.8% higher than the previous year's budget. Chief minister Yogi Adityanath highlighted the budget's focus on infrastructure development.
