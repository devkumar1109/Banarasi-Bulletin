Newlywed Beaten to Death, Husband Arrested

2025-05-18T07:10:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Amauli village, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested for murdering his 26-year-old wife, Aarti Pal. This occurred just one week after their wedding. Aarti was Raju Pal's third wife. Clashes reportedly began between the couple immediately after their marriage on May 9th. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death. Neighbours alerted the police who found Aarti injured and transported her to Narpatpur Community Health Centre, where she was declared dead.
