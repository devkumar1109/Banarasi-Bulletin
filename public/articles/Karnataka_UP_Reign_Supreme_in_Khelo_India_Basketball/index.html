Karnataka, UP Reign Supreme in Khelo India Basketball

2025-05-15T16:10:00+00:00

In the Khelo India Youth Games 2025 Bihar, Karnataka girls and Uttar Pradesh boys clinched gold in basketball. Karnataka girls dominated Haryana with a total team effort, led by Nidhi Umesh's shooting and Nidhi Srinivas' playmaking. Uttar Pradesh boys edged past Rajasthan in a closely contested match, thanks to crucial scoring by Sanjay Mahakumbh and Anuj Divakar. Rajasthan, despite a strong performance, settled for silver, marking their second consecutive loss in the finals. Tamil Nadu secured bronze in the boys' category, defeating Delhi.
