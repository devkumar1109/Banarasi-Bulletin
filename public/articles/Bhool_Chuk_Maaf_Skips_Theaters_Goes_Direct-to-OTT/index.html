"Bhool Chuk Maaf" Skips Theaters, Goes Direct-to-OTT

2025-05-08T08:00:00+00:00

Due to heightened security concerns following tensions between India and Pakistan, the film "Bhool Chuk Maaf" starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi will now have a direct-to-OTT release on Amazon Prime Video on May 16th. The film was originally scheduled for a theatrical release on May 9th, but the makers, Maddock Films, postponed it in a show of patriotic support. The film, directed by Karan Sharma, is a family entertainer set in Varanasi, revolving around a man trapped in a time loop on his wedding day. "Bhool Chuk Maaf" also features Seema Pahwa, Sanjay Mishra, and Raghubir Yadav.
