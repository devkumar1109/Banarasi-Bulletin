St. Stephen's Grad Curates Global Future at WEF

2025-04-08T01:30:00+00:00

Sashwat Pandey, a 21-year-old graduate of St Stephen's College, Varanasi, has been selected as one of the youngest curators in the World Economic Forum's Global Shapers Community. He will attend the Global Shapers Annual Summit in Geneva from July 9 to 11. Pandey, a previous Asia Chair of the International Association of Political Science Students, was also involved with India's G20 youth engagement group, Y20 India, and represented India at UNESCO's Summit on the Future of Youth and Entrepreneurship Education. In his new role, Pandey aims to empower Kashi's youth by focusing on education, governance, and youth empowerment.
