Varanasi: Breaking HIV Stigma, Building Safety

2025-03-03T18:14:00+00:00

An advocacy workshop on HIV prevention and public awareness was held in Varanasi under the National AIDS Control Program. The workshop, organized by the Comprehensive Security Center (CSC), focused on dispelling misconceptions about HIV and providing comprehensive security for at-risk individuals. The health department highlighted its efforts in providing better treatment and counseling to HIV-infected individuals. Discussions focused on AIDS prevention programs, including awareness campaigns and testing initiatives. The CSC emphasized the confidentiality of patient data and encouraged individuals seeking advice or testing to contact government centers without hesitation.
