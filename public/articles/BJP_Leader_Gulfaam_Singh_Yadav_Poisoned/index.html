BJP Leader Gulfaam Singh Yadav Poisoned

2025-03-11T03:51:00+00:00

Gulfaam Singh Yadav, a senior BJP leader in Uttar Pradesh, was poisoned to death on Monday in Sambhal district. Yadav, 60, was sitting at his residence when two unidentified men on a motorcycle injected him with a poisonous substance before fleeing. He was rushed to a hospital but died en route to Aligarh for further treatment. Yadav had contested against Mulayam Singh Yadav in the 2004 bypoll from Gunnaur Assembly constituency and held several key positions within the BJP. The police have deployed a team to investigate the case.
