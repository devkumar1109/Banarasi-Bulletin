Truck Scam: Man Arrested for Rs 4 Crore Fraud

2025-05-24T18:43:00+00:00

Abhishek Yadav, a 28-year-old man from Madhya Pradesh, was arrested in Varanasi for selling 20 trucks worth Rs 4 crore to scrap dealers using fake registration documents. Yadav would rent trucks from owners, promising higher-than-standard rental rates, and then sell them to scrap dealers named Shafiq alias Chakki in Nagpur for Rs 5 lakhs each. The police investigation began after receiving complaints about missing trucks and suspicious booking agents. Yadav contacted truck owners through Facebook brokers, bribing them to gain the owners' trust. Yadav sold trucks belonging to owners from Jhansi, Kanpur, Prayagraj, Sonbhadra, and Bhognipur, Kanpur Dehat district.
