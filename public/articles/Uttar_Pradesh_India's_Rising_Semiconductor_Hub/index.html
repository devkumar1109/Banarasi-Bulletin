Uttar Pradesh: India's Rising Semiconductor Hub

2025-05-23T13:01:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is rapidly becoming a key player in India's semiconductor mission with investments exceeding ₹30,000 crore. Two major projects, a ₹3,706 crore joint venture between HCL and Foxconn near Jewar airport to produce display driver chips, and a ₹27,000 crore Tarc Semiconductors plant in Noida for IC and microchip production, are driving this growth. Launched just 17 months ago, the state's Semiconductor Policy offers attractive incentives like capital subsidies, land discounts, and stamp duty exemptions, attracting global investors. Uttar Pradesh already contributes significantly to India's mobile phone and component production, further solidifying its position in the semiconductor landscape.
