Hijab Ripped, Couple Brutally Attacked in Muzaffarnagar

2025-04-14T14:49:00+00:00

In Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, a 20-year-old Muslim woman and her Hindu male companion were attacked by a group of men. The woman was stripped of her hijab and both she and the man were physically assaulted. The incident occurred while they were returning from collecting a loan EMI. A video of the attack went viral, prompting police to register an FIR under IPC sections related to assault, rioting, and outraging modesty. Six individuals have been arrested, and police are identifying more suspects from the video.
