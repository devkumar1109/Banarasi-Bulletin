Lucknow Hospital Fire: 200 Evacuated, Probe Launched

2025-04-15T22:38:00+00:00

A fire broke out on the second floor of Lucknow's Lok Bandhu Raj Narayan Combined Hospital on Monday night, prompting the evacuation of 200 patients. While no injuries were reported, the incident caused panic. The Uttar Pradesh government has formed a five-member committee to investigate the cause of the fire and potential negligence. The committee, headed by the Director General of Medical and Health Services, has 15 days to submit its report. The hospital refuted claims of patient deaths due to the fire, stating that a patient who was referred from a private hospital passed away before the incident. The second floor sustained damage, but doctors continued to treat around 1,400 patients on the ground floor.
