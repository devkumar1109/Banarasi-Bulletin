Faith, Festivities, and Flourishing Business

2025-03-30T14:27:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh witnessed a surge in devotees at major temples in Sambhal, Varanasi, and Ayodhya for Navratri celebrations. Markets were also bustling with activity ahead of Eid. Comprehensive security measures, including police deployments and drones, were implemented in response to recent communal tensions to ensure peace during the festivities. Despite security concerns, business flourished, particularly in the clothing and footwear sectors. The All India Industry and Trade Board reported impressive sales, anticipating record temple visitations during the festive season.
