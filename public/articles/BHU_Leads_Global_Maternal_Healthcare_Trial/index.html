BHU Leads Global Maternal Healthcare Trial

2025-04-12T18:46:00+00:00

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) launched the REACH Trial, a multi-country clinical study aiming to improve maternal healthcare. The trial, conducted in collaboration with Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College (JNMC), will take place across 27 tertiary-level hospitals in seven countries: Argentina, Kenya, India, Nigeria, Uganda, South Africa, and the United Kingdom. The trial involves 6,200 women and will last 54 months, including a 12-month preparation phase, a 30-month enrolment phase, and a 12-month final analysis, publication, and dissemination phase. BHU's Institute of Medical Sciences, along with PGIMER Chandigarh, MGIMS Wardha, SCB Medical College Cuttack, and Ballari Medical College, are participating in the trial in India.
