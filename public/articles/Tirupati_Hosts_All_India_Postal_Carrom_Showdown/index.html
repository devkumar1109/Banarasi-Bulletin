Tirupati Hosts All India Postal Carrom Showdown

2025-03-04T09:03:00+00:00

The 27th All India Postal Carrom Tournament began in Tirupati on March 4th, 2025. Thirteen Postal Circle teams from across India, representing both men's and women's categories, are participating. The tournament is being held at the Multi Sports Indoor Stadium, Tirupati. The event was inaugurated by Sri Venkateswara University Vice-Chancellor Prof Ch Appa Rao. The teams participating include Andhra Pradesh, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal, and Delhi. The tournament is expected to run for five days.
