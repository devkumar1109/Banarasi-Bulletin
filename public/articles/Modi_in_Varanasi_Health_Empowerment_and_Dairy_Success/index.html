Modi in Varanasi: Health, Empowerment, and Dairy Success

2025-04-11T09:08:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Varanasi and emphasized the transformative impact of the Ayushman Bharat scheme, particularly for the elderly. He highlighted the Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana, which provides free treatment to citizens over 70, regardless of income. Varanasi has distributed the highest number of these cards, with nearly 50,000 issued. Modi also lauded Banas Dairy's success in empowering women and transforming livelihoods. He credited the dairy for lifting many women in Purvanchal from poverty to financial independence. Modi acknowledged India's position as the world's largest milk producer and detailed government initiatives to support the dairy sector, including livestock vaccinations and the Rashtriya Gokul Mission.
