Varanasi Transformed: 458 Projects Worth Rs 40,536 Crore

2025-03-27T19:00:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Minister of state for stamp and court registration fees Ravindra Jaiswal highlighted extensive development work undertaken by both the central and state governments. Over the past eight years, 458 projects worth Rs 40,536 crore were completed across various sectors like roads, bridges, drinking water, sewage, education, health, energy, tourism, and urban development. Notably, 102 projects related to roads and bridges costing Rs 22,408 crore were completed, with additional projects worth Rs 4,955 crore currently underway. The Smart City project saw 34 projects worth Rs 877.65 crore completed, including the Kashi Integrated Command Control Centre and the Varanasi Sports Complex. 60 sewerage and drinking water projects worth over Rs 1,000 crore were completed, with additional projects worth Rs 2,100 crore in progress. Additionally, 43 health-related projects worth Rs 1,870.36 crore were completed, including the Cancer Institute and a super specialty hospital at BHU.
