Love Scam, Gold Theft, and a Deadly Accusation

2025-03-04T04:50:00+00:00

A 40-year-old man from Uttar Pradesh, Abhishek Singh, died in a Mangaluru lodge. Before his death, Singh recorded a video accusing CISF Assistant Commandant Monica Sihag of deception. He alleged that Sihag hid her marital status, emotionally and physically exploited him, stole ₹8 lakh worth of gold, and had multiple affairs. Mangaluru police have launched an investigation, examining digital records, call logs, and financial transactions to verify Singh's claims. Sihag's alleged involvement in fraud and manipulation has raised concerns. The CISF has not yet released an official statement. Singh's death has sparked discussions about emotional exploitation, financial fraud in relationships, and mental health.
