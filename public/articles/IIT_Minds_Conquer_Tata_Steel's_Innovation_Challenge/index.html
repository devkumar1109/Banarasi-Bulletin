IIT Minds Conquer Tata Steel's Innovation Challenge

2025-03-10T13:03:00+00:00

The 10th edition of Tata Steel's 'Mind Over Matter' Innovation Challenge concluded with Namita Dubey from IIT (ISM) Dhanbad winning the title. Ravu Ravi Teja from IIT (BHU) Varanasi secured 1st runner-up, and Kurapati Harshavardhan from IIT (BHU) Varanasi took 2nd runner-up. The competition saw a record 660+ registrations from 37 institutes. Winners received cash prizes, certificates, and Pre-Placement Offers for Management Trainee positions in Tata Steel's R&D team. Finalists also received Pre-Placement Interviews. Winning teams will mentor under Tata Steel's R&D leadership to develop prototypes of their ideas.
