Varanasi Man Attacks Mumbai Teen For Ignoring Him

2025-05-27T06:04:00+00:00

A 19-year-old man from Varanasi was arrested for attacking a 17-year-old girl in Kandivali, Mumbai. The accused, Rahul Singh, allegedly attacked the girl with a brick for ignoring his calls and advances. The incident occurred on May 24 when the victim was walking with a friend. Singh had traveled from Varanasi specifically to Mumbai because the victim had stopped answering his calls. The victim sustained serious injuries and was hospitalized. Singh has been booked under the BNS and POCSO Act.
