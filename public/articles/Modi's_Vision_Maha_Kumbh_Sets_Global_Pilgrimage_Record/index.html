Modi's Vision: Maha Kumbh Sets Global Pilgrimage Record

2025-02-27T15:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath credited Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the successful organization of Maha Kumbh 2025, calling it a global event. Over 66 crore pilgrims visited Prayagraj during the 45-day event, setting new records in religious tourism. Adityanath highlighted Modi's vision of integrating faith and economy, emphasizing the potential of spiritual tourism in Uttar Pradesh. He stated that 64 crore pilgrims and tourists visited various religious sites across the state in 2024 alone. The Maha Kumbh led to the development of five spiritual tourism corridors connecting major religious sites in Uttar Pradesh.
