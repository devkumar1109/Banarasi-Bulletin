Newlywed Beaten To Death By Husband In India

2025-05-17T10:26:00+00:00

A 26-year-old woman, Aarti Pal, was beaten to death by her husband, Raju Pal, in Varanasi, India, just a week after their wedding. Raju, who has a history of failed marriages, assaulted Aarti during a domestic dispute. He informed her family about the assault, leading to the police being alerted. Aarti was pronounced dead at Narpatpur Community Health Centre. Raju has been arrested and is currently under police interrogation. This tragic incident highlights the issue of domestic violence, particularly in the context of recent marriages.
