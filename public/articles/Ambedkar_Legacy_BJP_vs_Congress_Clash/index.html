Ambedkar Legacy: BJP vs. Congress Clash

2025-04-15T16:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized the Congress for disrespecting Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's legacy. He accused the Congress of defeating Ambedkar in elections, opposing his inclusion in the first cabinet, and altering the Constitution's preamble during the Emergency. Adityanath also claimed the Congress denied Ambedkar a memorial in Delhi and withheld the Bharat Ratna until Atal Bihari Vajpayee's government. In contrast, he praised the BJP for honoring Ambedkar through memorials and reservation policies. He urged the public to remember Ambedkar's message of education, organization, and agitation, emphasizing his struggle against caste discrimination.
