Ray's Cinematic Legacy: 6 Must-Watch Films

2025-05-02T15:15:00+00:00

Satyajit Ray, a renowned Bengali filmmaker, is celebrated on his 104th birth anniversary. Six must-watch Ray films are available for streaming: * **Pather Panchali (Prime Video):** Ray's debut film introduces Apu, a young boy facing poverty. * **Aparajito (Prime Video):** The second part of the Apu Trilogy, focusing on Apu's adolescence. * **Sonar Kella (Prime Video):** A family-friendly detective adventure featuring Feluda in Rajasthan. * **Jalsaghar (Prime Video):** Examines a declining zamindar's obsession with his past glory through music and dance. * **Mahanagar (Zee5):** A romantic drama starring Uttam Kumar and Suchitra Sen, exploring family values and modernity. * **Agantuk (Prime Video):** A thought-provoking film about family, tradition, and modernity.
