Golden Treats: World's Most Expensive Mangoes

2025-05-02T10:20:00+00:00

This article lists the top 10 most expensive mango varieties in the world. The Miyazaki mango from Japan, known as the "Egg of the Sun," is the most expensive, costing ₹25,000 - ₹30,000 per kg. Other expensive varieties include the Noorjahan from India (₹500 - ₹1,000 per piece), the Alphonso (Hapus) also from India (₹400 - ₹800), and the Kohitur mango, a royal mango from West Bengal, India (₹600 - ₹1,200). The article provides descriptions and price ranges for each variety, highlighting their unique characteristics and rarity.
