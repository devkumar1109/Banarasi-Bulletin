Kumbh's Blessing: Holy Water Flows to 75 UP Districts.

2025-02-27T17:50:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is distributing Sangam water from the Maha Kumbh Mela to residents across 75 districts. This initiative follows Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's promise to provide the holy water to those unable to attend the event. Fire tenders returning from the Kumbh will carry the sacred water to their respective districts. The distribution is set to start on Friday, allowing residents to conduct rituals at home. Over 66 crore devotees attended the Maha Kumbh Mela, held every 12 years, at the Triveni Sangam in Prayagraj.
