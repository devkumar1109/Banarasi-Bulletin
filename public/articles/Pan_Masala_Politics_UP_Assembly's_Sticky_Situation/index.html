Pan Masala Politics: UP Assembly's Sticky Situation

2025-03-04T07:11:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Assembly Speaker Satish Mahana revealed that some MLAs have been spitting pan masala inside the House. He personally cleaned the affected area after viewing video footage of the incident and urged the responsible MLA to confess. Mahana stressed the importance of maintaining decorum and cleanliness within the Assembly, emphasizing that such acts tarnish the House's dignity. Meanwhile, Uttar Pradesh Finance Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna presented the state budget for 2025-26, totaling over 8,08,736 crore Rs, a 9.8% increase from the previous year. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath highlighted the budget's focus on infrastructure development.
