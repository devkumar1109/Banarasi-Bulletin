Amethi Tragedy: Two Deaths in Separate Incidents

2025-05-15T11:45:00+00:00

Two separate incidents in Amethi, Uttar Pradesh, resulted in two deaths. An unidentified man was found dead on railway tracks near Tikaria crossing, likely hit by a train. Police are working to identify him. In a separate incident, 70-year-old Om Prakash Agrahari died after a speeding car hit his e-rickshaw near Kanakpur village. Agrahari was taken to Musafirkhana CHC where he was pronounced dead. The car driver fled the scene, while the vehicle has been seized.
