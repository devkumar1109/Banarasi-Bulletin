Varanasi-Kolkata Expressway: 6 Hours to a New Dawn

2025-04-05T08:18:00+00:00

The Varanasi窶適olkata expressway, part of the Bharatmala Project, is under construction and aims to reduce travel time from 12-14 hours to 6 hours. The 610-690 km expressway will span Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, and West Bengal, with the first 27 km phase from Varanasi to Chainpur already seeing land acquisition completed. The expressway is expected to boost tourism, agriculture, and industry, creating jobs and providing direct connectivity. It will feature a speed limit of 120 km/h. The project, costing 35,000 crore rupees, is being built in 13 phases and will cover 323 km in West Bengal, 244 km in Bihar, 196 km in Jharkhand, and 22 km in Uttar Pradesh.
