Varanasi Waves with Tiranga Pride for Operation Sindoor

2025-05-24T18:47:00+00:00

On Saturday, Varanasi witnessed a grand Tiranga Yatra celebrating Operation Sindoor, executed successfully by Indian Armed Forces. The event, initiated by BJP, aimed to foster patriotism and solidarity for the armed forces. Thousands participated, including students, NCC, NSS, civil defense personnel, ex-servicemen, and officials. Deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya and BJP state president Bhupendra Chaudhary led the yatra, which commenced at Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapeeth's sports ground and culminated at Sampurnanand Sanskrit University.
