Adityanath Slams Opposition Silence on Murshidabad Violence

2025-04-17T18:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized the opposition for their silence on the violence that erupted in Murshidabad, West Bengal. He questioned why the opposition parties have not condemned the incident and demanded action against those responsible. Adityanath's statement highlights the political tensions surrounding the violence in Murshidabad and the differing stances of the ruling BJP and opposition parties.
