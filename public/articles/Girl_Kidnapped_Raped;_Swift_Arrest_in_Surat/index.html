Girl Kidnapped, Raped; Swift Arrest in Surat

2025-03-15T23:56:00+00:00

A six-year-old girl was kidnapped and raped in Surat's Katargam area. The incident occurred while the victim and her mother were sleeping on a handcart. The accused, identified as 25-year-old Ajay Verma, took the girl to Katargam sports complex where the assault took place. Surat Crime Branch arrested Verma within five hours of the crime, utilizing CCTV footage and human intelligence. Police acted swiftly, registering an offence under relevant sections of the BNS Act for kidnapping and rape. The victim underwent surgery and is currently in the ICU.
