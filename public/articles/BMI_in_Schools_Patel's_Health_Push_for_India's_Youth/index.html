BMI in Schools: Patel's Health Push for India's Youth

2025-03-11T19:42:00+00:00

Governor Anandiben Patel urged the inclusion of Body Mass Index (BMI) in schools to assess children's health. She emphasized the importance of regular exercise, citing India's victory in the ICC Champion Trophy as an example, and suggested incorporating playtime into school schedules. Patel stressed the need for girls to engage in sports for physical fitness and to benefit future generations. She instructed officials to promote medical check-ups for girls in primary education. The Governor awarded winners at the district-level PM Shri Annual Sports and Cultural Competition, with Composite School, Pindra securing the first position.
