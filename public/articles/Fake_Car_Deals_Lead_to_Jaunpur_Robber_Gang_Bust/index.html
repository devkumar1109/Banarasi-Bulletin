Fake Car Deals Lead to Jaunpur Robber Gang Bust

2025-03-21T17:43:00+00:00

A robbers' gang has been busted in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, with the arrest of seven members. Led by Jitendra Prasad Yadav, the gang lured victims with offers of cheap second-hand cars. They would then hold the buyers hostage, assault them, and steal their money. One victim, Himendra Nishad, managed to escape and report the crime to the police. Authorities recovered Rs 11,000 in cash, three steel chains, ropes, a country-made revolver, and live cartridges.
