Varanasi Gets Urban Township, Records Room Boost

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi will get its first urban township, spanning 150 acres, near the international cricket stadium at Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority approved the project at its recent meeting. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation for the cricket stadium in September 2023. The stadium, costing Rs 451 crore, will have a capacity of 30,000 spectators and is expected to be completed by the end of 2025. The VDA also inaugurated a new record room, expanding its storage capacity to 2,55,266 files. A citizen app and complaint management system portal were also launched, allowing for easier complaint registration. approvals were granted for a new access road between Transport Nagar Scheme and NH-2.
