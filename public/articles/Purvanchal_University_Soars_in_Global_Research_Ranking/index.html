Purvanchal University Soars in Global Research Ranking

2025-03-17T18:47:00+00:00

Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University ranked second among Uttar Pradesh state universities and 125th in India in the Nature Index Ranking 2025. The university scored 2 with a share of 0.74, reflecting its research excellence. This achievement is attributed to its publications in high-impact research journals. The Nature Index measures research quality and impact, evaluating institutions based on research paper count and contribution ratio. Purvanchal University's Vice-Chancellor, Prof. Vandana Singh, emphasizes the university's focus on quality research, particularly in science, technology, social sciences, and innovation. The university received an A+ ranking from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council and a Rs 7.13 crore grant under the DST-PURSE scheme.
