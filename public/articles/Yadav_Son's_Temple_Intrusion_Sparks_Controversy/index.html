Yadav Son's Temple Intrusion Sparks Controversy

2025-06-15T03:20:00+00:00

Tej Pratap Yadav, son of RJD leader Lalu Prasad Yadav, sparked controversy after a video surfaced showing him inside the restricted 'Red Zone' of Varanasi's Kashi Vishwanath Temple. This high-security area, which includes the sanctum sanctorum, prohibits mobile phones and unauthorized access. The temple administration has launched an investigation, sharing information with the CRPF and local police to identify security lapses and take action against responsible personnel. The investigation will also verify the video's authenticity and determine if any rules were violated.
