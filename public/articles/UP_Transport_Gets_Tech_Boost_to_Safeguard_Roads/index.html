UP Transport Gets Tech Boost to Safeguard Roads

2025-03-09T10:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's transport department will undergo advanced technology training to reduce road accidents and improve governance. The Institute of Driving Training and Research in Rae Bareli will become a Centre of Excellence, providing specialized training sessions for officers at all levels. This initiative, led by the additional transport commissioner Fariduddin, aligns with Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's vision. The training will cover topics like data-driven policy-making, enforcement strategies, and e-governance, bringing the department in line with national and global best practices.
