Man Kills Third Wife a Week After Wedding

2025-05-18T02:46:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Uttar Pradesh's Varanasi district, Raju Pal, killed his third wife, Aarti Pal (26), just a week after their marriage on May 9. Police report that clashes began immediately after the wedding. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death at Narpatpur Community Health Centre. Raju has been arrested, and the body sent for post-mortem.
