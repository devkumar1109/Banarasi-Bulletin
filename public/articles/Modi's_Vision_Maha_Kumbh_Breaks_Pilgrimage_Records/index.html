Modi's Vision: Maha Kumbh Breaks Pilgrimage Records

2025-02-27T15:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath credited Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the successful organization of Maha Kumbh 2025, calling it a global event. Over 66 crore pilgrims visited Prayagraj for the event, setting records in religious tourism. Adityanath highlighted Modi's vision of integrating faith and economy, emphasizing the potential of spiritual tourism in Uttar Pradesh. He mentioned that 64 crore pilgrims visited religious sites in the state in 2024 alone. The Maha Kumbh led to the development of five spiritual tourism corridors connecting key religious sites across Uttar Pradesh, facilitating travel for millions of devotees.
