India Chips In: HCL-Foxconn JV to Boost Semiconductors

2025-05-15T13:55:00+00:00

The Indian cabinet has approved a semiconductor unit in Uttar Pradesh, a joint venture between HCL and Foxconn. This decision is a significant step towards boosting India's semiconductor manufacturing capabilities. The project is expected to attract substantial investments and create numerous job opportunities in the region.
