IIT Enrollments Surge: India's Tech Talent Boom

2025-03-12T09:04:00+00:00

The number of students enrolled in India's 23 IITs has doubled in the past decade, rising from 65,000 to 1.35 lakh. The government allocated over Rs 1,830 crore to five IITs established after 2014 (Hyderabad, Kanpur, BHU Varanasi, Tirupati, and Patna) to support their development and accommodate over 6,500 additional students. Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman announced in the Union Budget 2025 that IIT Patna will see expanded hostel and campus facilities, and 10,000 new seats will be added in medical colleges and hospitals nationwide next year, aiming for a total of 75,000 new seats over five years. A new AI center for education will also be established with a Rs 500 crore budget.
