Kashi Temple Welcomes Women with VIP Darshan

2025-03-08T05:56:00+00:00

On International Women's Day 2025, Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi introduced a special VIP darshan facility for women. The dedicated entrance through Gate Number 4 provided hassle-free entry and included health checkups. This initiative allowed women to experience a more convenient and respectful darshan experience on the occasion.
