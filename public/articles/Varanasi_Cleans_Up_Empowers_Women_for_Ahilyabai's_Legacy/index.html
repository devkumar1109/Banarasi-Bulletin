Varanasi Cleans Up, Empowers Women for Ahilyabai's Legacy

2025-05-30T02:53:00+00:00

The Varanasi Municipal Corporation (VMC) launched a two-day mega cleanliness drive and women's empowerment programme to mark Ahilyabai Holkar's 300th birth anniversary. The drive, held across the city's 21 sanitation sub-zones, included honor ceremonies for female sanitation workers, connecting them with government schemes, and distributing safety kits. Activities also encompassed special cleaning of ghats and drains, awareness campaigns, health check-up camps, seminars for public representatives, and school, college, and university-based awareness activities focusing on women's empowerment. Municipal Commissioner Akshat Verma participated in the drive, inspecting drain cleaning and distributing safety kits to sanitation workers.
