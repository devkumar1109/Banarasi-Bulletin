Akshay Kumar Praises Kumbh Mela Arrangements

2025-02-24T07:54:00+00:00

Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar took a holy dip at the Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj and praised Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath for the well-organized arrangements. Kumar, who also visited in 2019, noted significant improvements compared to his previous experience. He highlighted the presence of prominent figures like Ambanis and Adanis, as well as other celebrities, who have attended the event. The Maha Kumbh Mela, which began on January 13, will conclude on February 26 and has attracted numerous celebrities including Vicky Kaushal, Sonali Bendre, and Vijay Deverakonda.
