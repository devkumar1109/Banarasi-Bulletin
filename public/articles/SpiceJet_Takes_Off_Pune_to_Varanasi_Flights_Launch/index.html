SpiceJet Takes Off: Pune to Varanasi Flights Launch

2025-03-30T18:45:00+00:00

SpiceJet launched a new flight from Pune to Varanasi starting March 30. The flight will operate six days a week, except Saturdays, at 12.30am from Pune. Pune airport saw no other additions to its flight schedule, with 104 out of 109 available slots already taken by airlines. The airport recorded 204 flight movements on Sunday. Pune airport continues to offer domestic connections to 34 destinations and international flights to Singapore, Dubai, and Bangkok. The Indian aviation industry witnessed a 5.5% increase in weekly flights compared to last year, with Indian carriers operating 25,610 weekly departures between March 30 and October 25.
