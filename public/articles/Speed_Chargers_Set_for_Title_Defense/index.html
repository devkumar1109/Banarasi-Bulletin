Speed Chargers Set for Title Defense

2025-03-01T20:11:00+00:00

Speed Chargers defeated Prime Health City 4-1 in the ongoing cricket tournament, setting up a title defense against Legends in the final on Sunday. Rakesh Seth and Rajesh Narain led the charge for Speed Chargers, while Ramswaroop Tigerz will face Prime Health City for the third-place finish. The winner will receive Rs 5 lakh, and the runners-up will get Rs 3 lakh. Lucknow won their opening match in the Senior Open State-Level Men's Handball Championship, defeating Amethi 19-12, led by Shubham's seven goals. Mumtaz PG College secured a 50-run victory over DAV College in the inaugural match of the 52nd Sundari Devi Memorial Cricket Tournament.
