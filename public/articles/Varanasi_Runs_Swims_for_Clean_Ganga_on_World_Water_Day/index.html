Varanasi Runs, Swims for Clean Ganga on World Water Day

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, "Run for Clean Ganga," and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw over 6 km of running from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat, with Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal winning the men's and women's categories respectively. The forest department, alongside NGO Dolphin Friends, celebrated World Water Day and Earth Hour at Dhakwa Ghat. Ravi Kumar Singh, forest conservator of Varanasi Circle, inaugurated the "Dolphin Safari," highlighting efforts to protect the Gangetic Dolphin. The events emphasized the importance of water conservation and the need to protect the Ganga River.
