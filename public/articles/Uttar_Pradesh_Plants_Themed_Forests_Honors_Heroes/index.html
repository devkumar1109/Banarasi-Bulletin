Uttar Pradesh Plants Themed Forests, Honors Heroes

2025-05-21T12:14:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is launching a state-wide initiative to develop thematic forests. 'Shaurya Van' will honor martyrs and freedom fighters in each district. Other themed forests include 'Atal Van' for Atal Bihari Vajpayee, 'Ekta Van' for Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, 'Eklavya Van' for Birsa Munda, 'Oxy Van' for pollution control, 'Gopal Van' for cow shelters, and 'Triveni Van' near the Ganga and Yamuna rivers. The initiative also includes 'Ek Ped Maa Ke Naam 2.0,' encouraging tree planting in honor of mothers.
