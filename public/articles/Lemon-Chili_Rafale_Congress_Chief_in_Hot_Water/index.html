Lemon-Chili Rafale: Congress Chief in Hot Water

2025-05-08T17:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Congress President Ajay Rai faces legal trouble for displaying a toy Rafale jet adorned with lemons and chilies. The act, deemed insulting to India's defense forces, has sparked a political storm with the BJP accusing Rai of disrespect. A local Hindu group member filed a complaint, leading to an FIR against Rai under sections related to promoting enmity and harming national integration. Rai's actions received coverage in Pakistani media, potentially damaging India's defense reputation.
