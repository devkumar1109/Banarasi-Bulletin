"Rai Takes Flight With 'Rafale' Toy, Chides BJP"

2025-05-08T20:41:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Congress president, Rai, was booked for a symbolic display of a toy plane labeled 'Rafale' during a press conference in Varanasi. Rai questioned the BJP-led Centre's inaction regarding the use of Rafale jets following the April 22 Pahalgam terror attack. He hung 'nimbu-mirchi' (lemon and chillies) on the toy, criticizing the government.
