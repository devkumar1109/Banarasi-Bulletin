Shami's Family on MNREGA Amid Cricket League Debate

2025-03-26T08:40:00+00:00

According to a report, Mohammed Shami's sister and brother-in-law have been registered under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MNREGA) scheme in Uttar Pradesh. This comes amid ongoing discussions about the participation of Indian cricketers in overseas leagues. Former cricketer Karthik recently stated that Indian stars shouldn't be allowed in foreign leagues unless domestic cricket receives adequate support.
