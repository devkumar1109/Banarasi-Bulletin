Interstate Crime Ring Busted in Valsad Chase

2025-03-06T14:21:00+00:00

Three interstate criminals were arrested by the MBVV police after a hot chase in Valsad, Gujarat. The trio, Shamim Shahabuddin (31), Maqsood Amin (37), and Mohammed Arif Rashid (24), stole a ₹38 lakh truck from Kashimira, Maharashtra, on March 4th. The crime branch unit tracked them using CCTV footage and toll records, leading to the arrest in Valsad. The suspects are linked to previous offenses in Mathura (UP) and Alwar (Rajasthan), including dacoity and vehicle theft. The stolen truck has been recovered and the accused handed over to the Kashigaon police for further investigation.
