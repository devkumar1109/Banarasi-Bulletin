Vaish Strikes Gold in North Zone Bowling Championship

2025-06-12T20:28:00+00:00

Sumit Vaish from Uttar Pradesh won the 1st North Zone Tenpin Bowling Ranking Tournament, defeating Dhruv Sarda from Delhi by 49 pinfall. The tournament concluded in Lucknow on June 13, 2025, at Funtura Bowling Center. Vaish scored a total of 431, while Sarda scored 382. Sarda led in the first game, but Vaish dominated the second game, securing the victory. R Kannan, vice-president of the Tenpin Bowling Federation of India (TBFI), was the chief guest at the closing ceremony.
