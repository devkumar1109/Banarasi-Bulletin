Kathak Steps Empower Bihar Schoolgirls

2025-06-10T04:11:00+00:00

Pallavi Raj, a 25-year-old teacher in Uchkagaon village, Bihar, is empowering schoolgirls through Kathak. She teaches them in a school and a temple courtyard, transforming it into a space of expression and hope. Pallavi's passion for Kathak began in childhood and she trained for six years under Guru Vinod Kumar Sinha. Originally from Gopalganj, Bihar, she studied at Banaras Hindu University. Pallavi's initiative highlights the power of art to uplift and inspire, particularly in a region facing educational challenges.
