Cop Chase Ends in Criminal Arrest, Gun Recovered

2025-03-07T07:38:00+00:00

In Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh, police arrested Kamlesh Yadav, a criminal involved in 16 cases, after an encounter. Police recovered a firearm and a motorcycle from Yadav. During a checking drive, police noticed Yadav and another suspect on a motorcycle without helmets. When signaled to stop, they attempted to run over the police and fled. An exchange of fire ensued, injuring Yadav in the leg. The other suspect escaped. Yadav is currently hospitalized and further legal proceedings are underway.
