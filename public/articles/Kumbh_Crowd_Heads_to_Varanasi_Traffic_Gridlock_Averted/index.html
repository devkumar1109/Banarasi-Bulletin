Kumbh Crowd Heads to Varanasi, Traffic Gridlock Averted

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela concludes in Prayagraj on Maha Shivratri, Wednesday, leading to a surge in pilgrims expected in Varanasi. The Varanasi administration and police anticipate the largest-ever crowd in the district due to pilgrims traveling from the Maha Kumbh. To manage the influx, a comprehensive traffic management plan is being strictly implemented, including traffic diversions, barricades, and vehicle restrictions. Varanasi Police Commissioner Mohit Agarwal emphasized security arrangements to ensure smooth public movement and prevent traffic congestion. Designated holding areas at railway stations guide passengers to their trains, and waiting arrangements are provided for travelers to Prayagraj, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, and other destinations.
