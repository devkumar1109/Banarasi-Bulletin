Varanasi's Shiva-Inspired Cricket Temple Rises

2025-04-25T03:46:00+00:00

Varanasi is constructing a new 30,000-seater cricket stadium, the Varanasi International Cricket Stadium, costing Rs 452 crore and scheduled to be completed by April 2026. The Shiva-inspired stadium design includes crescent-shaped canopies, trident-shaped floodlights, and a media center in the shape of a pellet drum. The stadium will feature nine pitches (four red soil, three black soil, and two mixed wickets), dormitories for a proposed academy, solar panels, water recycling, and EV recharging facilities. Construction is being handled by L&T and the stadium could potentially host IPL matches as early as next season.
