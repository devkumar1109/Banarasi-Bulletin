UP Board 2025 Results: Girls Shine Again

2025-04-26T13:05:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) declared the UP Board 2025 results on April 26, 2025. Girls outperformed boys in both High School (Class 10) and Intermediate (Class 12) exams. The pass percentage for High School was 90.11%, with girls achieving 93.87% compared to boys' 86.66%. Intermediate results showed an overall pass rate of 81.15%, with girls securing 86.37% and boys 76.60%. Institutional candidates generally performed better than private candidates. Results are available on upmsp.edu.in, upresults.nic.in, and via SMS. Students can download provisional marksheets and collect original ones from their schools. Revaluation applications and compartment exams will be available in July 2025.
