Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand Triumph in Hockey Championship

2025-03-06T03:14:00+00:00

Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand won their Division A matches in the 15th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship. Madhya Pradesh secured a win thanks to Neelu Dadiya's penalty corner goal in the 53rd minute, giving them three crucial points. The location of the championship is Panchkula, Haryana.
