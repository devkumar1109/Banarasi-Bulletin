Varanasi's Tourism Gets a Two-Month Makeover

2025-05-30T21:44:00+00:00

The Indian Ministry of Tourism has given Varanasi officials two months to implement visible changes to enhance tourism. Secretary V Vidyavathi emphasized focusing on 16-20 key locations, including temples, ghats, and Sarnath. Key initiatives include: * Beautification and cleaning of ghats, improved infrastructure, and enhanced transportation. * Promoting spiritual tourism through temple development and local artisan crafts. * Training and improved facilities for guides and artists. * Organizing cultural events and festivals. * Promoting Varanasi online and involving locals in tourism businesses. The government aims to create a sustainable and long-term impact on tourism in Varanasi while preserving its cultural and historical significance.
