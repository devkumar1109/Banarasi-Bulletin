Moradabad Man Busted for ISI Espionage

2025-05-19T05:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Special Task Force (STF) arrested Shahzad from Moradabad for alleged espionage for Pakistan's ISI. Intelligence inputs revealed his involvement in cross-border smuggling and espionage activities threatening India's national security. Shahzad allegedly shared sensitive information about India's security infrastructure with ISI handlers and engaged in smuggling cosmetics, clothes, spices, and other goods. His activities, suspected to be ongoing for several years, included frequent visits to Pakistan. He has been booked under Indian espionage and national security laws.
