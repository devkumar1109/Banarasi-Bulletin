Paneer Fury: Bus Crashes Wedding Mandap

2025-04-29T05:21:00+00:00

A wedding in Uttar Pradesh turned violent when a local youth, angered over not being served more paneer, drove a minibus into the mandap. Five people were injured, three critically, including the groom's father and the bride's uncle. The incident occurred after the youth got into a dispute with the bride's father, who struck him with a ladle. The accused fled the scene and is currently absconding. Police have launched a search operation. The wedding was briefly halted but resumed the following day.
