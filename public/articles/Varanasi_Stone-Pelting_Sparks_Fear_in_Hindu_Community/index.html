Varanasi: Stone-Pelting Sparks Fear in Hindu Community

2025-03-06T18:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a video showing boys from a particular community throwing stones in the Kotwa area went viral, leading to a police investigation. Around 15 Hindu families in the area claim their houses were targeted with stones, instilling fear among them. They allege a targeted campaign to force them out, citing the recent killing of a Hindu man named Bhaiyalal Patel and the recovery of his partially burnt body. Police have arrested some individuals in connection with Patel's death, while others remain at large. The Hindu families have installed CCTV cameras in their homes after the incident, capturing the stone-pelting. They also allege the deliberate conversion of an area around a temple into a garbage dumping ground.
