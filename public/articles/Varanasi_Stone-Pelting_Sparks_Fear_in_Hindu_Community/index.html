Varanasi: Stone-Pelting Sparks Fear in Hindu Community

2025-03-06T18:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, after a video of boys from a particular community throwing stones in the Kotwa area went viral, police registered a case and started an investigation. Around 15 Hindu families in the area claim they are being targeted by stone-pelting, living in fear after the killing of Bhaiyalal Patel in February. The police arrested some suspects in Patel's case, but others are still at large. Hindu families installed CCTV cameras after the killing, and one captured the stone-pelting incident. They allege their community is being deliberately targeted and pressured to leave. A local youth claims that a temple area was converted into a garbage dump.
