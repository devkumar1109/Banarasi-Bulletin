Uttar Pradesh Seduces Tourists at PATA Travel Mart

2025-06-06T10:54:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will showcase its tourism potential at the PATA Travel Mart in Bangkok from August 26 to 28. The state aims to attract international tourists and investors by highlighting its cultural, spiritual, and historical richness. A 36-square-meter stall will feature iconic sites like Sarnath and Varanasi, with interactive displays like LED walls and Augmented Reality panels. Uttar Pradesh will also conduct a promotional campaign in Bangkok, including a roadshow for travel industry stakeholders and media. Tourism ads will run during peak hours and promotional content will be displayed at key locations.
