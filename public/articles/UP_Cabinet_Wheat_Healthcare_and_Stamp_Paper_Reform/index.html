UP Cabinet: Wheat, Healthcare, and Stamp Paper Reform

2025-03-10T10:53:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Cabinet approved 19 proposals, including wheat procurement at the MSP of Rs 2,425 per quintal from March 17 to June 15. 6,500 procurement centres will be operational. The Cabinet approved the establishment of new medical and nursing colleges. 14.05 acres of land from the Ballia district jail will be used for a new medical college, and 4,570 sq metres of land from the Government Agricultural School in Bulandshahr will be used for a nursing college. Additionally, revised administrative and financial sanctions were approved for a 300-bed gynaecology block at the Uttar Pradesh Institute of Medical Sciences in Saifai, Etawah. The state government will withdraw physical stamp papers valued between Rs 10,000 and Rs 25,000 to prevent misuse.
