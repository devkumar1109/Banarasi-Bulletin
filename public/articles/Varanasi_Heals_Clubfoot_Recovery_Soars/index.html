Varanasi Heals: Clubfoot Recovery Soars

2025-04-27T19:07:00+00:00

A health scheme in Varanasi, under the Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram and Anushka foundation, has helped 70 children with clubfoot recover since 2019. Out of 512 children treated, 64 have fully recovered. In April 2025, six children underwent surgery for clubfoot. The Ponseti method, involving casting and a minor outpatient procedure, is used to treat clubfoot. Free and proper treatment is ensured by notifying the RBSK team if a child is affected. Registration for treatment and monitoring can be done at sabalkashi.com. Clubfoot affects one in 800 children.
