Varanasi: Ex-Lover's Rage, Man Murdered

2025-03-15T20:05:00+00:00

In Varanasi, 24-year-old Sunil Kumar was shot dead outside his residence near DAV College on Friday night. Initial police investigation suggests Sunil was killed by the boyfriend of his ex-girlfriend during an argument. DCP Kashi Zone Gaurav Baswal confirmed that a case has been filed against unidentified killers and teams are working to apprehend the suspect. Sunil had been in a relationship with another girl but his family arranged his marriage with someone else. Following the murder, Sunil's family and neighbors blocked the college road in protest. Police officials assured them of swift action, leading to the protest being called off.
