Jagannath Temple Gets Drone Defense System

2025-05-23T07:51:00+00:00

The Odisha government is installing anti-drone technology at the Shree Jagannath Temple in Puri to enhance security. Discussions between the Law and Police Departments are in advanced stages, with a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) already drafted. The system will intercept and disable drones detected within a specified radius of the temple. The Jagannath Temple Administration is prepared to financially support the project. This move aims to prevent unauthorized aerial activity and safeguard the temple from potential threats, particularly in light of increasing drone misuse in sensitive areas across India.
