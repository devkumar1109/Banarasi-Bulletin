BJP Leader Killed in Deoria Road Accident

2025-05-22T13:35:00+00:00

BJP leader Purnamasi Chauhan died in a road accident in Uttar Pradesh's Deoria district. Chauhan and his relative, Kundan, were riding a motorcycle when it was hit by a speeding car. The car driver fled the scene. Both Chauhan and Kundan sustained severe injuries. Chauhan was declared dead upon arrival at the hospital, while Kundan was referred to a medical college in Gorakhpur. Chauhan was 65 years old and served in various political positions, including twice as mandal general secretary and once as mandal president of Desahi Deoria.
