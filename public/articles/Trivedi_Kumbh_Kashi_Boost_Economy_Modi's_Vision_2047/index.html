Trivedi: Kumbh, Kashi Boost Economy, Modi's Vision 2047

2025-02-24T19:47:00+00:00

BJP spokesperson and Rajya Sabha MP Sudhanshu Trivedi claimed that the Maha Kumbh Mela contributed around Rs 3 lakh crore to the Indian economy, representing 1% of the GDP. Trivedi praised the Union Budget 2025-26, stating it reflects Prime Minister Narendra Modi's vision for a developed India by 2047. He highlighted the success of schemes like Jan Dhan Yojana, Mudra Loan, and Startup India. Trivedi also emphasized the economic benefits of the Kashi Vishwanath Corridor in Varanasi, stating that tourist visits increased from 30.78 lakh to 11 crore after its completion.
