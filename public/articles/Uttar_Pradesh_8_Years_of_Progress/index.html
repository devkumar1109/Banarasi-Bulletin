Uttar Pradesh: 8 Years of Progress

2025-03-24T08:13:00+00:00

The Yogi Adityanath government in Uttar Pradesh has completed eight years in power, marking significant development across various sectors. Key achievements include: * **Rural Development:** Construction of over 2.62 crore toilets, free ration distribution to 14.7 crore people during the pandemic, provision of 1.86 crore Ujjwala LPG connections, and construction of over 56 lakh houses for the underprivileged. * **Law and Order:** Establishment of police commissionerates in seven districts and creation of quick response teams to enhance security. * **Infrastructure:** Development of expressways, airports, and other infrastructure projects to boost connectivity and economic growth. * **Healthcare:** Expansion of healthcare facilities and implementation of schemes to improve healthcare access for all. * **Women's Safety:** Initiatives to empower women and ensure their safety, including the implementation of the "Mission Shakti" program. The BJP's continued success in the state reflects the government's commitment to progress and development.
