Water Dispute Turns Deadly: Uncle Gets Life Sentence

2025-05-24T05:27:00+00:00

In Kasganj, Uttar Pradesh, 46-year-old Rambabu Singh was sentenced to life imprisonment for killing his 36-year-old nephew, Jaikishore Singh, in 2019. The murder stemmed from a dispute over irrigation rights. Rambabu shot Jaikishore with an illegal firearm during an argument with his brother, Chadrabhan Singh, over the use of a shared tubewell. Rambabu was arrested the day after the incident and a chargesheet was filed. The court convicted him and sentenced him to life imprisonment with a fine of Rs. 1,17,000.
