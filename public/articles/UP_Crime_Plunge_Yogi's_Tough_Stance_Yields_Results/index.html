UP Crime Plunge: Yogi's Tough Stance Yields Results

2025-03-30T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has witnessed an 85% reduction in serious crimes over the past eight years. Robbery cases have decreased by 84.41% and loot cases by 77.43% since 2016. The Yogi Adityanath government's zero-tolerance policy and enhanced surveillance measures, including CCTV cameras, are credited with this success. Large-scale operations targeting organized crime have resulted in the seizure of over Rs 142 billion in illegal assets.
