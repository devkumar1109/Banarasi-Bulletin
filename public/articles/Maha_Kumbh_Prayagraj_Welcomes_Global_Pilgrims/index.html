Maha Kumbh: Prayagraj Welcomes Global Pilgrims

2025-03-05T18:17:00+00:00

Prayagraj witnessed a record 55 lakh foreign tourists during the 45-day Maha Kumbh, surpassing previous records. Tourism in Uttar Pradesh saw a surge, with 65 crore tourists visiting in 2024, including 23 lakh foreigners. Prayagraj attracted 4.79 lakh foreign tourists in 2024, a significant increase from previous years. Diplomats and devotees from 116 countries, including Nepal, America, Britain, and Canada, participated in the religious event. The influx of tourists benefited local businesses, hotels, and the tourism sector, generating substantial revenue. The tent city, a major attraction, was fully booked, and the chopper service also proved immensely popular.
