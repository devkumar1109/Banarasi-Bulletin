UP Board 2025 Results: Girls Shine, Pass Rates High

2025-04-26T05:31:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has announced the UP Board 2025 results. The overall pass percentage for High School (Class 10) is 90.11%, with girls outperforming boys at 93.87% compared to 86.66%. For Intermediate (Class 12), the pass percentage is 81.15%, with girls achieving 86.37% and boys 76.60%. Institutional candidates generally performed better than private candidates. Results are available on upmsp.edu.in and upresults.nic.in. Students can also check via SMS or DigiLocker. Provisional marksheets are available online; original marksheets must be collected from schools. Revaluation applications and compartment exams will be available in July.
