Synchrotron Science: IIT-BHU Bridges India-Germany Research

2025-02-27T18:41:00+00:00

IIT-BHU hosted a Synchrotron X-ray User Outreach Workshop in collaboration with DST and JNCASR. The workshop aimed to introduce Indian researchers to PETRA III, a cutting-edge synchrotron facility in Germany. Over 700 researchers registered, with 100 carefully selected participants from 17 states, emphasizing inclusivity. Subhas Sarkar, former union education minister, highlighted the importance of collaborative research. Prof. Kanishka Biswas, Principal Coordinator at JNCASR, discussed the project's significance. Poonam Yadav, Scientist at DST's Nano and Advanced Materials Division (NAMD), emphasized DST's role in fostering collaboration between India and Germany for accessing PETRA III. Dr. Amit Patra, IIT-BHU Director, recognized the potential for groundbreaking discoveries through the workshop.
