Kumbh Kumbaya: UP's Spiritual Boom

2025-03-05T00:18:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj drew an estimated 65 crore visitors, boosting tourism in Ayodhya and Varanasi as well. Religious tourism presents a significant opportunity for economic development in eastern Uttar Pradesh. The state government should focus on developing underutilized Buddhist religious sites to further capitalize on this trend. Tourism's economic benefits extend beyond revenue and jobs, encompassing infrastructure investment, sustainable development, and cultural heritage preservation. India, with its rich religious history, has always been a tourist destination, and Uttar Pradesh has benefited from sites like the Taj Mahal. The growing Indian diaspora further amplifies the importance of religious tourism in the country.
