UP Aims for $1 Trillion with New GCC Policy

2025-05-07T06:18:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government has approved the Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy to attract global investment and establish the state as a "global service centre." The policy aims to help UP achieve its $1-trillion economy goal. The policy offers incentives to attract global companies in IT, banking, healthcare, engineering, and next-generation technologies, capitalizing on UP's large talent pool. Officials expect the policy to draw multinational companies looking for high-quality work at lower costs. The state already houses 1700 GCCs and anticipates rapid growth, with recent investments from companies like Microsoft and MAQ, setting up large development centers in Noida.
