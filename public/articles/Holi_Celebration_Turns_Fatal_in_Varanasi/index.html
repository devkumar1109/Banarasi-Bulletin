Holi Celebration Turns Fatal in Varanasi

2025-03-15T20:05:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man, Sunil Kumar, was shot dead outside his residence near DAV College in Varanasi on Friday night. Police suspect Sunil was killed by the boyfriend of his ex-girlfriend. The assailant fled the scene. Sunil was returning home after celebrating Holi with friends and relatives when the incident occurred. Family and neighbors blocked the college road in protest on Saturday. DCP Kashi Zone Gaurav Baswal confirmed that a case has been registered against unidentified killers and teams are working to apprehend the accused.
