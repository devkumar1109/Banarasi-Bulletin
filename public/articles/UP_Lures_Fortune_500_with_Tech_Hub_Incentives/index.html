UP Lures Fortune 500 with Tech Hub Incentives

2025-05-18T13:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched the Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy 2024 to attract Fortune 500 companies and boost foreign direct investment (FDI). The policy aims to transform Noida, Lucknow, Kanpur, and Varanasi into tech hubs, creating over 2 lakh jobs in the next five years. The policy offers incentives like land subsidies (30-50%), stamp duty exemption, capital subsidies (up to Rs 25 crore), SGST reimbursement, interest subsidy, and operational subsidies. Customized packages are available for Fortune 500 companies and those with FDI exceeding Rs 100 crore. The policy also supports innovation and startups with cost reimbursement, IPR subsidies, and grants for setting up centers of excellence.
