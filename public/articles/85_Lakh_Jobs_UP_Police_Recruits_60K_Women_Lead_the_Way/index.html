8.5 Lakh Jobs: UP Police Recruits 60K, Women Lead the Way

2025-06-14T10:19:00+00:00

Union Home Minister Amit Shah and CM Yogi Adityanath will distribute appointment letters to 60,244 newly recruited UP Police constables on June 15th at the Defence Expo Ground in Lucknow. This includes 12,048 women constables, marking a significant increase in female representation in the UP Police. The recruitment drive, which saw 1174 examination centres across 67 districts, signifies the significant employment opportunities created in UP over the past eight years, with 8.50 lakh government jobs provided. CM Yogi Adityanath also felicitated 166 meritorious students who topped their respective boards in the 2024-25 academic year.
