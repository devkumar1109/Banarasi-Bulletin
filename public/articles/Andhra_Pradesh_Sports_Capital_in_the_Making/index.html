Andhra Pradesh: Sports Capital in the Making

2025-06-12T17:14:00+00:00

The Andhra Pradesh government plans to establish an international sports university and a sports city spanning over 2,000 acres in Ibrahimpatnam, Vijayawada. This move aims to position Amaravati as the 'sports capital' of India. The state has proposed hosting the 2026 Khelo India Games in Vijayawada, expecting approval soon. The Indira Gandhi Municipal Stadium in Vijayawada is being upgraded to host international events at a cost of ₹70 crore. The state also plans to build stadia in every assembly constituency and develop sports infrastructure in 46,000 government schools.
