Nagpur Cafe Owner Murder: Five Arrested

2025-04-28T07:41:00+00:00

The Nagpur Crime Branch arrested five individuals in connection with the murder of Avinash Bhusari, owner of 'Sosha Café'. The accused, including Bunty alias Shailesh Vinod Hiranwar, Babu Hiranwar, Adarsh Walke, Deepu Meshram, and Shibu Yadav, were apprehended following a special operation. The murder was allegedly orchestrated by Bunty and Babu Hiranwar due to a dispute with the Shekhu Gang, targeting Bhusari because he was a relative of a member. The arrests followed a complex investigation as the accused constantly changed locations. Police tracked them using technical intelligence and apprehended them in Bhandara and Gondia. Commissioner of Police Ravindra Singhal led the operation, bringing relief to the city.
