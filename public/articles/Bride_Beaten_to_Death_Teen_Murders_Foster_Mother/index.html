Bride Beaten to Death, Teen Murders Foster Mother

2025-05-18T06:15:00+00:00

A 26-year-old woman named Aarti Pal was allegedly beaten to death by her 44-year-old husband Raju Pal in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, just a week after their marriage. The couple had tied the knot on May 9th, and tensions escalated into violence, leading to Aarti's death on the night of May 16th. Police have arrested Raju, and Aarti's body has been sent for post-mortem. Separately, a 13-year-old girl and two male friends have been arrested for allegedly murdering the woman who raised her since infancy. The 54-year-old victim, Rajalaxmi Kar, was reportedly smothered to death, and the trio allegedly plotted the murder to gain control of her property.
