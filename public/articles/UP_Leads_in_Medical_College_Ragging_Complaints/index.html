UP Leads in Medical College Ragging Complaints

2025-03-26T02:45:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh reported the highest number of ragging-related complaints in medical colleges in 2024, with 33 cases. Bihar followed with 17, Rajasthan with 15, and Madhya Pradesh with 12. The National Medical Commission (NMC) has implemented stringent measures against ragging, including regular monitoring, follow-up with medical institutions, and strict penalties for non-compliance. These measures include the Prevention and Prohibition of Ragging in Medical Colleges and Institutions Regulations, 2021, online undertakings from students and their guardians, CCTV cameras in vulnerable areas, and an NMC portal for lodging complaints.
