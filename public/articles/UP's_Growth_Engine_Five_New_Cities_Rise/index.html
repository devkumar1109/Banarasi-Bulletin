UP's Growth Engine: Five New Cities Rise

2025-04-06T16:15:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh plans to develop five new cities east of Noida, fueled by the upcoming Noida International Airport opening in 2025. These include New Noida, a 20,911-hectare industrial hub focusing on IT, electronics, and manufacturing, with a projected 1.2 million jobs by 2040. Heritage City (Raya Urban Centre) will leverage religious tourism and Braj culture, while New Agra will focus on tourism and hospitality, enhancing the Taj Mahal experience. Tappal-Bajna will transform into an industrial township, and IITGN will also be developed as an industrial center.
