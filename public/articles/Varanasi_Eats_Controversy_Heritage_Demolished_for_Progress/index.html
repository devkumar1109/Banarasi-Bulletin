Varanasi Eats Controversy: Heritage Demolished for Progress

2025-06-18T17:03:00+00:00

The demolition of two iconic Varanasi eateries, Pahalwan Ki Lassi and Chachi Ki Kachori, for a road-widening project has sparked controversy. Locals mourn the loss of culinary heritage and cultural landmarks, citing the shops' long history and unique charm. Opposition party Samajwadi Party criticized the demolition, stating it has "hurt the soul of Varanasi." BJP mayor Ashok Tiwari defended the action, claiming it improves public convenience and that proper compensation will be given to affected shopkeepers. He asserted the "taste of Kashi" remains unchanged despite the location change.
