Bihar to Zoom: Bullet Train on Track for August.

2025-03-09T06:02:00+00:00

Bihar is set to receive its first bullet train, part of the 800-kilometer Varanasi-Patna-Howrah High-Speed Rail Corridor. The 260-kilometer section within Bihar will run on an elevated track, passing through Patna, Buxar, Ara, Jehanabad, and Gaya. The National High-Speed Rail Corporation Limited (NHSRCL) is leading the project, with the Detailed Project Report expected by August. The bullet train will operate at a top speed of 350 km/h, drastically reducing travel time. The project is divided into two phases: the first connecting Varanasi to Howrah, and the second extending the route from Delhi to Varanasi, creating a high-speed network linking Delhi to Howrah. The train will feature modern amenities like automatic doors, CCTV cameras, and mobile charging points.
