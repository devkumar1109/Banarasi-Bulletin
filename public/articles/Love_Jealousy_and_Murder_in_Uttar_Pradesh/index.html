Love, Jealousy, and Murder in Uttar Pradesh

2025-04-01T06:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is experiencing a rise in murders linked to extramarital affairs. Several cases, including the Saurabh Rajput murder in Meerut, have come to light. A psychological expert attributes these incidents to growing impulsiveness and the prioritization of emotions over logic, warning that jealousy and possessiveness are driving people to violence. In Auraiya, Pragati Yadav allegedly conspired with her lover to murder her husband, Dilip, just two weeks after their "forced marriage." They hired a contract killer, who fatally shot Dilip. Police arrested Pragati, her lover, and the contract killer based on CCTV footage and recovered weapons and other evidence.
