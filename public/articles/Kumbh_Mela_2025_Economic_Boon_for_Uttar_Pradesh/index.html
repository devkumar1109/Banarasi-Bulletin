Kumbh Mela 2025: Economic Boon for Uttar Pradesh

2025-02-27T07:41:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela 2025 in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, concluded on Maha Shivratri, generating over Rs 3 lakh crore ($360 billion) for the state's economy. Over 66.21 crore devotees participated, exceeding the initial estimate of 40 crore and business projections of Rs 2 lakh crore. The event significantly boosted businesses in hospitality, transport, food, and consumer goods, benefiting nearby cities and towns. Uttar Pradesh's government invested over Rs 7,500 crore in infrastructure improvements, including flyovers, expanded roads, upgraded railway stations, and a modern airport terminal. An additional Rs 1,500 crore was allocated for Kumbh Mela arrangements.
