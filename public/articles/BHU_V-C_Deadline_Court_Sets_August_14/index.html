BHU V-C Deadline: Court Sets August 14

2025-03-14T00:02:00+00:00

The Allahabad High Court has set a final deadline for the appointment of a new Vice-Chancellor for Banaras Hindu University (BHU). The court has directed the search committee to finalize the appointment by August 14, 2023. This comes after ongoing legal challenges and delays in the appointment process. The court emphasized the urgency of filling the vacant position, stressing the need for administrative stability at the university.
