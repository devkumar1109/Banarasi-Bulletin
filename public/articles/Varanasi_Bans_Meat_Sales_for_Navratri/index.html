Varanasi Bans Meat Sales for Navratri

2025-03-29T02:04:00+00:00

For the first time, Varanasi will close meat, fish, and poultry shops during Chaitra Navratri, beginning Sunday. Mayor Ashok Kumar Tiwari instructed strict compliance with the closure, despite Eid coinciding with the festival. The decision was made after an executive member proposed closing meat shops during Navratri. The executive committee also banned cigarette sales near religious sites and schools and approved licensing for cigarette vendors. The mayor emphasized respecting the tradition of Navratri, a sacred period for Hindus, and urged Muslims to consider the sentiment.
