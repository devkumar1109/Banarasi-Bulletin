BharatBenz Opens Mega Lucknow Workshop

2025-05-20T05:44:00+00:00

BharatBenz has inaugurated Uttar Pradesh's largest day-and-night workshop in Lucknow. Spanning 60,000 sq. ft., the facility boasts 15 service bays and employs 85 trained professionals to service up to 7,500 vehicles annually. Strategically located on the Lucknow-Kanpur Highway, this workshop primarily supports BharatBenz fleets involved in local infrastructure projects and local bus operators. This expansion strengthens BharatBenz's service network in Uttar Pradesh, which now includes 15 centers. An additional 10 workshops are planned for the state to meet growing demand for commercial vehicle service.
