Rajkummar Rao's Maalik: Gangster Action Hits July 11

2025-04-25T09:22:00+00:00

Rajkummar Rao's gangster action thriller, Maalik, is scheduled to release on July 11, 2025. Originally slated for a June 20 release, the film's release has been pushed back by three weeks. Directed by Pulkit and produced by Kumar Taurani and Jay Shewakramani, Maalik stars Rao as a ruthless crime boss, alongside Prosenjit Chatterjee, Manushi Chhillar, Huma Qureshi, and Medha Shankr. Filming took place in Lucknow, Varanasi, Unnao, and Kanpur, with Rao reportedly performing intense action sequences. Besides Maalik, Rao is gearing up for the release of the romantic drama Bhool Chuk Maaf on May 9, 2025, and the ensemble thriller Toaster, slated to begin filming mid-2025.
