WFI Ban Lifted: Wrestling Resumes, Athletes Rejoice

2025-03-11T09:12:00+00:00

The Wrestling Federation of India (WFI) ban has been revoked by the Union Sports Ministry following the intervention of Prime Minister Modi, Home Minister Amit Shah, and Sports Minister Mandaviya. WFI president Sanjay Singh expressed gratitude for the decision, stating it will benefit the country. The suspension had prevented camps and events, hindering the development of wrestlers, particularly from disadvantaged backgrounds. Singh announced the launch of wrestling leagues by June-July and again by November-December, aiming to provide athletes with greater exposure and opportunities.
