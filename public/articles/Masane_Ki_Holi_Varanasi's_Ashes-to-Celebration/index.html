Masane Ki Holi: Varanasi's Ashes-to-Celebration

2025-03-11T06:12:00+00:00

Aghoris and Naga Sadhus celebrated Masane Ki Holi in Varanasi, marking the start of Holi festivities. The celebrations at Harishchandra Ghat involved smearing pyre ashes, symbolizing the cycle of life and death. Devotees engaged in rituals, offering prayers and lighting lamps to honor spirits. A procession featuring Baba Masan Nath culminated at the ghat. In Pandeypur, a Holika Dahan site was designed to resemble a cremation ground, highlighting the unique theme of "Masane Ki Holi." The event drew tourists and devotees, captivated by the spiritual and cultural spectacle.
