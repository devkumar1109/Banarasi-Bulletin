Wife, Lover Plot ₹2 Lakh Murder in Auraiya

2025-03-26T07:22:00+00:00

In Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh, a man named Dilip Yadav was murdered by contract killers hired by his wife, Pragati, and her lover, Anurag Yadav. Anurag allegedly planned the murder for ₹2 lakh. Dilip died after sustaining injuries on March 19th. Police identified one of the shooters, Ramji Nagar, through surveillance footage and arrested him along with his aide. Both Ramji's sister and Anurag's family have demanded justice for Dilip, stating that those responsible should be punished regardless of their familial ties. The police are continuing their investigation to apprehend other individuals involved in the crime.
