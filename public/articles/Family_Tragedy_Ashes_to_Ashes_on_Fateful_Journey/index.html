Family Tragedy: Ashes to Ashes on Fateful Journey

2025-04-19T13:30:00+00:00

A family of four was killed in a car crash in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh. The accident occurred when their car collided with a stationary truck. The family, headed to Prayagraj to immerse their deceased son's ashes, included an elderly couple, their son-in-law, and the driver. Three died at the scene, and another succumbed to injuries at the hospital. Two survivors, the son-in-law's wife and her son, are in critical condition. The deceased were residents of Jhansi district. The accident is believed to be linked to the family's recent loss, as their younger son had recently drowned.
