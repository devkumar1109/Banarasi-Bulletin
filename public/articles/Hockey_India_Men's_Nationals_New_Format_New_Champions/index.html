Hockey India Men's Nationals: New Format, New Champions

2025-04-03T18:11:00+00:00

The 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025 will be held from April 4 to 15, 2025, at Jhansi's Major Dhyan Chand Hockey Stadium. The tournament will adopt a new Division-based format with promotion and relegation, similar to the Senior Women National Championship. Thirty teams will be divided into three Divisions: Division A (top 12 teams), Division B, and Division C. Division A features defending champions Hockey Association of Odisha and runners-up Hockey Haryana. Teams compete for the championship title in Division A, promotion to Division A in Division B, and a spot in Division B in Division C.
