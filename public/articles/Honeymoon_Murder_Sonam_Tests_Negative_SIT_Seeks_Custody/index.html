Honeymoon Murder: Sonam Tests Negative, SIT Seeks Custody

2025-06-11T07:53:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi, accused in the murder of her husband Raja Raghuvanshi, has tested negative for pregnancy. She was arrested in Indore along with four others and brought to Shillong for investigation. A special investigation team (SIT) will seek police custody of all five accused and reconstruct the crime scene in Sohra, Meghalaya, where Raja's body was found. The SIT conducted searches in Indore and Ghazipur, securing six-day and three-day remand for the accused respectively. Raja and Sonam went missing during their honeymoon in Meghalaya on May 23, and his body was discovered in a gorge on June 2.
