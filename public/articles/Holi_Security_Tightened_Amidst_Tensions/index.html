Holi Security Tightened Amidst Tensions

2025-03-13T11:57:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is bolstering security for Holi celebrations on March 14th as it coincides with Friday namaz. This heightened security follows controversial remarks by some politicians and a police officer. The state police plan increased patrols, surveillance drones, and monitoring of social media for provocative content. Mosque administrations have adjusted prayer times and will cover mosques to prevent color staining. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has endorsed the timing changes, emphasizing mutual respect and mindful celebrations.
