St. Stephen's Grad Curates Global Change at 21

2025-04-08T01:30:00+00:00

Sashwat Pandey, a 21-year-old graduate from St. Stephen's College, Varanasi, has been named one of the youngest curators in the World Economic Forum's Global Shapers Community. He will attend the Global Shapers Annual Summit in Geneva. Sashwat previously served as the Asia Chair of the International Association of Political Science Students and was involved in India's G20 youth engagement group, Y20 India. He aims to empower Varanasi's youth by focusing on education, governance, and youth empowerment.
