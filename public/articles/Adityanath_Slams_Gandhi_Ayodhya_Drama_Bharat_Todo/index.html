Adityanath Slams Gandhi: "Ayodhya Drama", "Bharat Todo"

2025-03-26T05:51:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized Rahul Gandhi, calling him a "namuna" for allegedly keeping the Ayodhya controversy alive. Adityanath termed Gandhi's nationwide march a "Bharat Todo Abhiyan" and accused him of working against India's interests. He questioned the Congress party's governance over the past ten decades, stating that they did not prioritize making India a "Shresht Bharat." Adityanath contrasted this with the current government's achievements, highlighting initiatives like the Ram Mandir construction, abolition of triple talaq, and promotion of the Kumbh Mela, which he claimed have garnered support from the entire nation under PM Modi.
