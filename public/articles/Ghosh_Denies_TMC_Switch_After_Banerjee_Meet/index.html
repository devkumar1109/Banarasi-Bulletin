Ghosh Denies TMC Switch After Banerjee Meet

2025-05-01T06:05:00+00:00

BJP leader Dilip Ghosh denied speculation about joining the Trinamool Congress (TMC) after meeting West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee at the Digha Jagannath temple. The meeting, which took place on May 1, 2025, fueled rumors of a potential shift in political allegiance for Ghosh. However, he clarified that the meeting was purely social and did not indicate any change in his political stance.
