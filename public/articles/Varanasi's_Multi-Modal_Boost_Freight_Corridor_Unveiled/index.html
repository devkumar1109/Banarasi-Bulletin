Varanasi's Multi-Modal Boost: Freight Corridor Unveiled

2025-05-05T19:00:00+00:00

Varanasi's Division Commissioner S Rajalingam has ordered the swift completion of the multi-modal terminal and freight village projects. He inspected the multi-modal terminal site at Ramnagar, reviewing the proposed sewage treatment plant (STP) and various vessels. The freight village, spanning 100 acres on the Varanasi-Chandauli border, will be connected to NH-7, NH-2, a 5.1 km railway line, and the Ralhupur Multi-Modal Terminal. This will establish a freight corridor from northeast to south India, facilitating cargo transportation to Haldia via National Waterway-1. The project is expected to create significant investment and employment opportunities.
