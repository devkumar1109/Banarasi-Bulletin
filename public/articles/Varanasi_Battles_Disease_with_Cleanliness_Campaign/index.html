Varanasi Battles Disease with Cleanliness Campaign

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like waterborne, dengue, malaria, and chikungunya. The campaign, flagged off by UP ministers Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra, emphasizes public participation in preventing waterlogging and maintaining cleanliness. In 2025, only 22 dengue, 2 malaria, and 18 chikungunya cases were reported compared to significantly higher numbers in 2023 and 2024. SVM Bhelupur hospital upgraded its services with an ultrasound machine, digital X-ray, dental department, eye examination, and mental health OPD. The campaign focuses on 68 dengue hotspots in urban and 56 in rural areas. A Dastak campaign will run from April 10 to 31.
