Varanasi Battles Disease with Cleanliness Campaign

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, and chikungunya. UP ministers Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra flagged off the campaign, urging residents to prevent waterlogging and maintain cleanliness. MLA Saurabh Srivastava highlighted the success in reducing disease cases compared to previous years, attributing it to inter-departmental coordination. The district identified 68 dengue hotspots in urban areas and 56 in rural areas for targeted intervention. SVM Bhelupur Hospital introduced new facilities, including an ultrasound machine, digital X-ray, and outpatient services, to enhance medical care. The Dastak campaign, focusing on door-to-door health checks, will run from April 10 to 31.
