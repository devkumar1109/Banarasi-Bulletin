Faithful Journey Ends in Fatal Crash

2025-04-19T13:30:00+00:00

A family of four died and two others were injured after their car collided with a stationary truck in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh. The family was traveling to Prayagraj to immerse their deceased son's ashes in the Ganga River. The deceased were identified as Ram Kumar Bhargawa (71), his wife Kamlesh Bhargawa (67), their son-in-law Prag Chaubey (50), and driver Shubham Yadav (35). Survivors, Charu Bhargawa (36) and her son Kashvik (12), are in critical condition. The accident occurred on National Highway-2, near Sujanipur crossing in Khaga. The family's tragedy stems from the recent drowning death of their younger son, Aditya Bhargawa, during a visit to the Omkareshwar Temple.
