Mizoram Hockey Stars Shine, Set Olympic Sights

2025-04-23T20:06:00+00:00

Five under-16 girls from Mizoram were selected by the Sports Authority of India (SAI) to join National Centres of Excellence (NCOEs) for hockey. The players were chosen after the 2024-25 ASMITA league season and are considered potential contributors to the Indian women's national team for the 2026 and 2032 Olympics. This selection highlights the growing talent pool in Mizoram, a state traditionally known for football and boxing. SAI Shakti won the ASMITA sub-junior hockey league for the third consecutive time, defeating Madhya Pradesh Hockey Academy in the final.
