BHU Professor Wins Award for Green Hydrogen Innovation

2025-03-04T10:08:00+00:00

Prof. S. Srikrishna from Banaras Hindu University's Department of Biochemistry received the Visitor's Award for Innovation from President Droupadi Murmu. The award was presented at the Visitor's Conference 2024-25 held at Rashtrapati Bhavan on Monday. Prof. Srikrishna was honored for his pioneering work in developing indigenous innovation in quantum technology to support the National Green Hydrogen Mission and its environmental goals.
