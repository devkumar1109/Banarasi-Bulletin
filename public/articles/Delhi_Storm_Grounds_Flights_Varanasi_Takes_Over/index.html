Delhi Storm Grounds Flights, Varanasi Takes Over

2025-05-25T18:31:00+00:00

Due to a storm in Delhi, at least five flights, including one international, were diverted to Varanasi's Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport late Saturday. The diverted flights included four IndiGo flights destined for Delhi and one Air India flight. The storm caused heavy rain and thunderstorms in Delhi, temporarily halting air traffic at the Indira Gandhi International Airport and leading to the diversion of approximately 49 flights, including 17 international ones.
