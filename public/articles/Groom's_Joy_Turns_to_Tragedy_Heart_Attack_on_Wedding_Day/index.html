Groom's Joy Turns to Tragedy: Heart Attack on Wedding Day

2025-05-17T15:23:00+00:00

A 25-year-old groom, Pravin, died of a heart attack during his wedding in Jamkhandi, Karnataka. He collapsed shortly after tying the mangalsutra around the bride's neck. Witnesses say he complained of chest pain before collapsing. Paramedics rushed him to a private hospital, but he was pronounced dead. The incident highlights the concerning trend of increasing heart attacks in young adults. Earlier this year, a 23-year-old woman died of a heart attack during a wedding sangeet ceremony in Madhya Pradesh.
