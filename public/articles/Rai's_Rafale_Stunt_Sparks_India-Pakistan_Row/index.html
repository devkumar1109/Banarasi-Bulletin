Rai's Rafale Stunt Sparks India-Pakistan Row

2025-05-05T06:07:00+00:00

Congress leader Ajay Rai criticized the BJP government over the Pahalgam terror attack, using a toy Rafale plane with "nimbu mirchi" (lemon and chillies) attached to symbolize warding off evil. This remark drew attention from Pakistani media outlet ARY News. BJP retaliated, accusing Rai of demoralizing armed forces, making fun of Hindu culture, and showing the INDIA alliance's true face by supporting Pakistan. BJP spokesperson Sudhanshu Trivedi highlighted Rai's disrespect towards the Rafale jets and their connection to surgical strikes.
