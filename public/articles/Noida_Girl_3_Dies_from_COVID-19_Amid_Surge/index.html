Noida Girl, 3, Dies from COVID-19 Amid Surge

2025-06-05T14:31:00+00:00

A three-year-old girl from Noida died of COVID-19 at Delhi's Chacha Nehru Hospital on Thursday. She was admitted due to dehydration and also had pneumonia. This death comes amidst a rise in COVID-19 cases in Noida. 20 new cases were reported, bringing the total to 158. Two patients are hospitalized, while 7 have recovered. There are currently 151 active cases in the district. To combat the rise, the Noida health department is expanding COVID-19 testing to primary and community health centers. They urge citizens to wear masks, maintain social distancing, and seek testing if they experience symptoms such as cough, fever, cold, or difficulty breathing.
