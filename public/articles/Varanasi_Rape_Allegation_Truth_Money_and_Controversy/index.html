Varanasi Rape Allegation: Truth, Money, and Controversy

2025-04-19T11:07:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi alleged she was raped by 23 people between March 29 and April 4. 14 of the accused have been arrested. Families of some accused claim the woman demanded money to remove names from the complaint and questioned why she didn't seek help during the alleged assault. They also point to social media posts showing her seemingly unharmed. Police are investigating CCTV footage that shows her riding a bike during the alleged timeframe and inconsistencies in her account. A Special Investigation Team (SIT) has been formed to examine the allegations and counterclaims. The victim's mother wants to meet PM Modi and seek justice for her daughter.
