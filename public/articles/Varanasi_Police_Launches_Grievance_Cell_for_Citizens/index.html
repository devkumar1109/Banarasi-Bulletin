Varanasi Police Launches Grievance Cell for Citizens

2025-06-05T19:00:00+00:00

A Public Grievance Cell has been established at the Varanasi district police headquarters to address public complaints. Commissioner of Police Mohit Agrawal appointed Additional Commissioner of Police (crime) Rajesh Kumar Singh as the head of the cell, with two subordinates to assist him. The cell will operate daily from 10 am to 2 pm, focusing on addressing grievances from residents, particularly those from remote areas. The aim is to ensure that complaints are heard properly, feedback is provided, and effective action is taken.
