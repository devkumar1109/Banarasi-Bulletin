Varanasi to Unfold Yoga Week for International Day

2025-06-01T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi is preparing for International Yoga Day on June 21st. A series of events, Yoga Week, will commence on June 15th and culminate with the main event at Kashi Vishwanath Dham on June 21st. Yoga sessions will be held at various ghats, village panchayats, urban areas, schools, and health centers. The district magistrate reviewed preparations and instructed officials to ensure all necessary arrangements are in place for Yoga Week's grand opening at Namo Ghat. Yoga organisations are encouraged to conduct daily sessions.
