UP Upskills OBC Youth for AI, Drones, and Cyber

2025-04-24T13:30:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is modernizing its computer training scheme to include AI, drone technology, and cybersecurity courses. The initiative, launching in 2025-26, aims to improve employability for educated but unemployed OBC youth. The Backward Classes Welfare Department is preparing a proposal and budget allocation for the program. The revamped curriculum and trainer skills will be implemented through new training institutes. Physical verification of beneficiaries and a robust monitoring system will ensure transparency and authenticity. Minister Narendra Kashyap emphasized the government's commitment to inclusive development and industry-relevant training.
