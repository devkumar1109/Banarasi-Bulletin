Ram Temple Plot: ATS Raids, Teen Arrested

2025-03-04T19:39:00+00:00

The UP ATS conducted searches in Lucknow, Azamgarh, Mau, and Ballia following the arrest of 19-year-old Abdul Rehman in Faridabad for plotting an attack on the Ram temple in Ayodhya. Rehman was carrying two hand grenades when apprehended and was allegedly in contact with radical elements linked to international terror groups. The searches focused on tracing individuals potentially connected to the plot, including verifying information about their communications. Meanwhile, Rehman, a former e-rickshaw driver, was reportedly struggling with a congenital heart defect and his health deteriorated in recent months. His behavior changed, he became withdrawn, and increased travel to Faizabad, Delhi, and Visakhapatnam raised local suspicion.
