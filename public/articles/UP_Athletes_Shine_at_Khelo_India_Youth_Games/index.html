UP Athletes Shine at Khelo India Youth Games

2025-05-25T21:38:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi praised the performance of three Uttar Pradesh athletes at the Khelo India Youth Games-2025 in Bihar. Sheikh Zeeshan from Varanasi set a new record in triple jump (15.66 meters) and won a silver medal. Qadir Khan from Bareilly broke the national record twice in the 400-meter race, while Tushar Chaudhary from Bijnor won gold in weightlifting, lifting a total of 289 kg. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath also lauded the athletes, attributing their success to hard work, discipline, and determination. Modi mentioned these achievements in his Mann Ki Baat program, emphasizing the growing strength of the "Khelo India" vision.
