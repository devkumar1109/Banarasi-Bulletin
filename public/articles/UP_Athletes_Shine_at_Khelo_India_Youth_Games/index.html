UP Athletes Shine at Khelo India Youth Games

2025-05-25T21:38:00+00:00

Three athletes from Uttar Pradesh, Sheikh Zeeshan, Qadir Khan, and Tushar Chaudhary, made a significant impact at the recent Khelo India Youth Games-2025 held in Bihar. Zeeshan, a triple jumper from Varanasi, set a new record of 15.66 metres and won a silver medal. Qadir Khan from Bareilly broke the national record twice in the 400-meter race. Tushar Chaudhary, hailing from Bijnor, secured a gold medal in weightlifting by lifting a total of 289 kg. Prime Minister Narendra Modi praised their achievements in his Mann Ki Baat program, calling the games a "big hit" and highlighting the athletes' exceptional performance. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath also commended the athletes, attributing their success to their hard work, discipline, and determination.
