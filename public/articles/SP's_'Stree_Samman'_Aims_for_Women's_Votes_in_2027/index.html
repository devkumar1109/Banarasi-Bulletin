SP's 'Stree Samman' Aims for Women's Votes in 2027

2025-03-04T07:41:00+00:00

The Samajwadi Party (SP) is gearing up for the 2027 Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections and has announced the 'Stree Samman-Samriddhi Yojana' if voted to power. Party president Akhilesh Yadav emphasized the scheme's aim to empower women, a significant voting bloc in the state. A video featuring actress Ankita Lokhande highlights women's challenges and the SP's commitment to their welfare. The video tackles issues like empowerment, education, unemployment, and inflation. With 7.15 crore women voters in UP, parties are vying for their support, following the increased participation of women voters in recent elections.
