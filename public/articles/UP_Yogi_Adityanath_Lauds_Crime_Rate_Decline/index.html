UP: Yogi Adityanath Lauds Crime Rate Decline

2025-02-24T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state's law and order has become a national model, citing a significant decline in crime rates according to National Crime Records Bureau data. He highlights a decrease in cases of dacoity, loot, murder, rioting, kidnappings for ransom, dowry deaths, and rape since 2017. Adityanath points to "Operation Conviction," resulting in 51 death sentences and thousands of life and lengthy prison terms for criminals, particularly those involved in crimes against women. The government has also strengthened the Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) and implemented the police commissionerate system in major cities. Adityanath announced the establishment of new women PAC battalions and the recruitment of over 1.5 lakh police personnel since 2017.
