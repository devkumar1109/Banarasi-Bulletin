Anuj, Vineeta Shine at U20 Wrestling Nationals

2025-04-21T15:33:00+00:00

Anuj and Vineeta won gold in the men's freestyle 61kg and women's 62kg categories respectively at the U20 National Wrestling Championships. Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, and Delhi were the top performing states, with multiple medal wins. Anuj defeated Sumit (Haryana) in the 61kg final, while Vivek (Uttar Pradesh) won the 74kg title against Saurabh (Haryana). In Greco Roman wrestling, Services, Delhi, and Haryana secured wins in their respective weight classes.
