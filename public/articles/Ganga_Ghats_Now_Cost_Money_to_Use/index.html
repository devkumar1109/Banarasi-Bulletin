Ganga Ghats Now Cost Money to Use

2025-03-25T18:28:00+00:00

The Varanasi civic body will now charge organizers a fee for events held on the Ganga ghats. The fee is ₹880 per square meter and must be paid through the Smart Kashi app at least 15 days in advance. Organizers will need to submit applications on the app which include photographs of the chosen location and detailed event information. This new system will be implemented in a few days. Previously, organizers only needed permission from the civic body, with no fee charged.
