UP to Get International Film City, Boney Kapoor Leads the Way

2025-03-25T03:48:00+00:00

Filmmaker Boney Kapoor met Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath in Lucknow and announced the upcoming shilanyas (foundation stone laying) of the International Film City by Bayview Bhutani near Jewar International Airport. This state-of-the-art film city, located at Y.E.I.D.A, aims to become a major hub for filmmaking with its modern infrastructure and proximity to the airport. Under CM Adityanath's leadership, Uttar Pradesh has positioned itself as a film-friendly state with incentives and streamlined permissions for filmmakers. Kapoor, known for producing iconic films, is contributing to the industry's growth beyond filmmaking through this project.
