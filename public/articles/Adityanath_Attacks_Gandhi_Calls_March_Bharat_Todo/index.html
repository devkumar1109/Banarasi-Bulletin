Adityanath Attacks Gandhi, Calls March "Bharat Todo"

2025-03-26T08:13:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized Rahul Gandhi, calling him a "namuna" and accusing him of prolonging the Ayodhya controversy. Adityanath dubbed Gandhi's nationwide march a "Bharat Todo Abhiyan," alleging it serves India's interests. He questioned the Congress party's performance over the past ten decades, particularly their lack of progress on issues like national development. Adityanath contrasted this with the current government's achievements, citing the Ram Mandir construction, abolition of triple talaq, and promotion of the Kumbh Mela.
