Pawandeep Rajan Recovering After Serious Car Crash

2025-05-08T13:15:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan is out of the ICU after a serious car accident on May 5th in Uttar Pradesh. Rajan sustained multiple fractures and a head injury when his SUV collided with a truck. He underwent a 6-hour surgery to address some of the fractures and is currently in a private room under observation. He is expected to undergo further surgery in 3-4 days to address the remaining fractures and injuries.
