Congress Aide Nabbed in Varanasi Crime Spree

2025-06-06T08:39:00+00:00

Amit Pathak, personal assistant to Uttar Pradesh Congress Committee President Ajay Rai, and his accomplice were arrested in Varanasi for multiple robberies, thefts, and molestation cases. The arrests stem from FIR number 102/2025, charging the duo with extortion, burglary, robbery, insult, criminal intimidation, and molestation. Authorities are investigating the case thoroughly, with the arrests raising concerns about the involvement of political associates in criminal activities.
