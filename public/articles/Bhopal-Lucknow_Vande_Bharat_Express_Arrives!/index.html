Bhopal-Lucknow: Vande Bharat Express Arrives!

2025-04-16T07:46:00+00:00

The Bhopal-Lucknow Vande Bharat Express is set to launch in the coming months, significantly reducing travel time between the two cities to 7-8 hours. This semi-high-speed train will offer a comfortable and efficient journey with modern amenities and faster speeds compared to existing indirect train services, which take 10-12 hours. The train will have eight chair car coaches with 564 seats, including an executive class. Ticket prices are expected to be higher than regular trains, comparable to Rajdhani or Duronto Express. While the final route map, stoppages, and launch date are yet to be announced, the Bhopal-Lucknow Vande Bharat Express is expected to offer a premium travel experience with features like comfortable seating, air-conditioning, Wi-Fi, and onboard entertainment.
