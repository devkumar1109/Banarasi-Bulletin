Paneer Protest: Minibus Slams Wedding Mandap

2025-04-29T05:21:00+00:00

A wedding in Hamidpur, Uttar Pradesh, turned violent when a youth, upset over not receiving more paneer, drove a minibus into the wedding mandap. Five people were injured, including the groom's father and the bride's uncle, who are in critical condition. The dispute began when the bride's father struck the youth with a ladle after he demanded more paneer. The accused fled the scene and is currently absconding. Police have launched a search operation to apprehend him. The wedding ceremony was briefly halted but resumed the following day with the help of police and local residents.
