Putin's Limousine Explodes Near FSB HQ

2025-03-30T15:30:00+00:00

An explosion occurred near the Federal Security Service (FSB) headquarters in Moscow, involving a £275,000 Aurus Senat limousine linked to Vladimir Putin's fleet. The incident has sparked speculation about an assassination attempt or internal sabotage. Russian special services have heightened security measures. Authorities are investigating the cause of the explosion, with theories ranging from internal Kremlin conflict to involvement of Western intelligence or rogue groups. The incident has heightened global concern and attention, especially given warnings against any attacks on Poland or its allies, which would face strong retaliation.
