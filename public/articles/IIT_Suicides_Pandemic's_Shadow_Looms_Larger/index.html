IIT Suicides: Pandemic's Shadow Looms Larger

2025-03-29T07:38:00+00:00

Over the past five years, 20 students allegedly died by suicide across five top IITs: IIT-Madras, IIT-Delhi, IIT-Kanpur, IIT-Kharagpur, and IIT-Guwahati. IIT-Madras and IIT-Delhi had seven suicides each, IIT-Kharagpur four, and IIT-Guwahati two. Data shows a rise in suicides post-pandemic. IIT-Madras saw three suicides in 2023, while IIT-Delhi reported two each in 2023, 2024, and 2021. IIT-Kharagpur had one suicide each in 2020, 2022, 2023, and 2024. IIT-Guwahati reported suicides in 2022 and 2024. The majority of those who died by suicide were male.
