Ration Row: Politicians Clash Over KYC

2025-05-14T17:08:00+00:00

A dispute over KYC verification at a ration shop in Gorakhpur escalated into a clash between two local politicians, Vinod and Irshad Ahmad. The fight stemmed from a longstanding political rivalry. Vinod allegedly pushed Irshad, leading to a physical altercation. Vinod is currently hospitalized. CCTV footage of the incident is being examined by police. Despite the apparent nature of the altercation, some individuals are attempting to spread narratives with communal undertones, linking it to Pakistan and the Indian Army. The SHO of Tiwaripur police station stated that action will be taken based on factual verification.
