UP Cabinet: Agniveers Benefit, New Shops, Investment Boost

2025-06-03T22:15:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh cabinet, led by CM Yogi Adityanath, approved several key decisions. Ex-Agniveers will receive a 20% reservation in direct recruitment for state police, PAC, Reserve Cavalry, and fire services. They will also get a three-year age relaxation. To improve the public distribution system, the cabinet approved the construction of "Annapurna" buildings across the state. These model fair-price shops will be funded through various schemes, with 75-100 buildings per district planned annually. The cabinet also approved the Industrial Investment and Employment Promotion Policy, aiming to attract investments and generate employment.
