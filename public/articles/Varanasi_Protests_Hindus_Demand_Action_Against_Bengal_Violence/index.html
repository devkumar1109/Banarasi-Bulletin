Varanasi Protests: Hindus Demand Action Against Bengal Violence

2025-04-14T18:58:00+00:00

Social activists, including women from Vishal Bharat Sansthan (VBS), protested in Varanasi over violence in Murshidabad, West Bengal, sparked by protests against the Waqf Act. They demanded the arrest and dismissal of West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee, accusing her of supporting the violence and claiming Hindus are being targeted and forced to flee. Protesters allege that Bangladeshi Muslims, under the guise of opposing the Waqf Bill, attacked Hindu homes, committed atrocities against women, and harmed children. VBS President Rajeev Shriguruji stated that West Bengal has become unsafe for Hindus, and Hindus are being killed and raped.
