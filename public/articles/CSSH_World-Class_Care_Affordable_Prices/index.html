CSSH: World-Class Care, Affordable Prices.

2025-03-26T08:10:00+00:00

Chhatrapati Shivaji Subharti Hospital (CSSH) in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, is a tertiary care hospital dedicated to providing world-class healthcare at affordable prices. Unlike corporate hospitals focused on profits, CSSH prioritizes accessibility for middle- and lower-income families. The hospital boasts state-of-the-art technology and a research-driven approach, often implementing cutting-edge treatments even if they aren't immediately profitable. This ensures patients have access to the latest medical advancements. CSSH's infrastructure and equipment surpass many government medical colleges in India.
