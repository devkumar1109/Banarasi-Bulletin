Rajkummar Rao: Beyond the Underdog

2025-04-01T06:52:00+00:00

Rajkummar Rao is set to star in Vikramaditya Motwane's upcoming sports biopic. The film will be based on a real-life athlete and explore a unique protagonist unlike typical underdog sports stories. The project is expected to be a fast-paced two-month shoot following several months of intense preparation. Rao is currently promoting his comedy-romance "Bhool Chuk Maaf," co-starring Wamiqa Gabbi, which releases on May 9, 2025. He also has "Maalik" and Netflix's "Toaster" in his 2025 lineup.
