Caste Census Cheers: Varanasi Celebrates Historic Win

2025-05-02T10:53:00+00:00

The Union Cabinet approved a caste-based census, prompting celebrations in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Residents celebrated with drums and colored powder (gulal), hailing it as a "historic decision". Chandrakant Singh and Vinay Rai, locals, expressed their happiness, stating it was a landmark step and a fulfillment of a long-standing demand. Prime Minister Narendra Modi's decision to conduct the census was widely welcomed.
