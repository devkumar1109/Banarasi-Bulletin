Supreme Court: No Honor in Murder

2025-04-18T23:53:00+00:00

The Supreme Court has ruled that the death of a young woman in Uttar Pradesh was a clear case of murder, rejecting the police's attempt to classify it as an "honour killing." The court expressed anger at the state police's handling of the case, highlighting their failure to protect the victim. The victim's family had allegedly killed her for marrying a man outside her caste. The court's verdict suggests that it will not tolerate attempts to justify violence against women in the name of honour.
