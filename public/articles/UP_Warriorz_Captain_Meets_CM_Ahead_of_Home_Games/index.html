UP Warriorz Captain Meets CM Ahead of Home Games

2025-03-01T10:01:00+00:00

Ahead of the Women's Premier League (WPL) home leg in Lucknow, UP Warriorz captain Deepti Sharma and team owner Jinisha Sharma met Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. They invited him to attend their matches at Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium. Jinisha Sharma expressed gratitude for the CM's wishes, stating they inspire the team to perform well. Deepti Sharma also expressed honor in meeting the CM and appreciation for his support. UP Warriorz are preparing for their home stretch in Lucknow during Season 3 of the WPL.
