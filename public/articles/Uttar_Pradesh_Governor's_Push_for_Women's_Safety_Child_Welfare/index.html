Uttar Pradesh: Governor's Push for Women's Safety, Child Welfare

2025-03-17T15:38:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel called for a "zero tolerance" approach to crimes against women, urging law enforcement vigilance and village-level awareness campaigns against issues like child marriage and drug abuse. She praised Maharajganj district officials for adopting 40 villages to transform them into models through government schemes. Patel distributed welfare benefits, including preschool education kits, appointment letters for Anganwadi workers, and nutritional kits, emphasizing the critical role of Anganwadi centers in child development. She also lauded the recruitment of 210 new, highly educated Anganwadi workers. Patel stressed the need for effective implementation of welfare schemes, ensuring they reach all beneficiaries. She later visited Siddharthnagar, distributing Anganwadi resource kits and emphasizing the importance of connecting children with education for India's development.
