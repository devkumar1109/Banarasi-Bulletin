UP Ditches Paper, Embraces Smart Vehicle Cards

2025-03-11T17:15:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is replacing traditional vehicle registration booklets with chip-enabled smart cards. This initiative, part of the Digital UP Mission, aims to enhance security, streamline processes, and increase transparency. Smart cards will store vehicle and ownership data, reducing fraud and bureaucratic delays. The embedded microchip technology allows for instant verification of vehicle data by law enforcement and transport authorities. This modernization effort is expected to prevent fraud and duplication while offering greater convenience to vehicle owners.
