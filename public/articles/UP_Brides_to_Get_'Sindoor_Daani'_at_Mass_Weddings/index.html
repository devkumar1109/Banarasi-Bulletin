UP Brides to Get 'Sindoor Daani' at Mass Weddings

2025-05-28T13:47:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's Social Welfare Minister, Asim Arun, announced that brides participating in the state's mass wedding scheme (Mukhyamantri Samuhik Vivah Yojana) will receive a 'sindoor daani,' a traditional symbol of marriage. This initiative, alongside increased financial assistance raised to Rs 1 lakh from Rs 51,000, aims to support women's dignity and uphold tradition. The scheme, led by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, provides couples with financial aid and other gifts. Minister Arun praised Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Minister Adityanath's leadership, crediting their governance with India's economic growth.
