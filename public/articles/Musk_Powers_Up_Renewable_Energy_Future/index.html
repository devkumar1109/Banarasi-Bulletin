Musk Powers Up Renewable Energy Future

2025-05-05T06:44:00+00:00

Errol Musk has joined Servotech Renewable Power System's global advisory board. His role will focus on advancing Servotech's global growth, sustainability, and AI-driven innovation strategies. Musk will engage with key government and investor groups, guide media engagement, and contribute to Servotech's brand promotion. His initiatives include visiting Servotech's manufacturing facilities in India, participating in roundtable discussions, and attending quarterly Global Advisory Board meetings. Servotech aims to transform the renewable energy landscape by 2027 with Musk's strategic guidance.
