Sarnath's Light and Sound Show Gets 8-D Upgrade

2025-05-09T15:13:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is revamping the light and sound show at Sarnath, showcasing Lord Buddha's life. The upgraded show will use 8-D technology, 4K quality, and 3-D effects for an immersive experience. It will be extended from 25 to 45 minutes, covering all locations associated with Buddha's life. The show will feature a 7-channel audio system, an analogue digital surround system, and advanced laser-based technology. It will be available in Hindi and English initially, with plans to add Pali. The upgraded show is expected to launch in the next two months.
