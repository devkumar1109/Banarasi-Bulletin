Youth Power: Adityanath Pushes Innovation in Governance

2025-03-28T14:53:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath emphasized youth leadership at the Viksit Bharat Youth Parliament Festival, stressing collaboration between the legislature, executive, and judiciary for effective governance. He encouraged young participants to develop leadership skills and innovate for societal benefit, noting that the platform nurtures leadership qualities across various fields. Adityanath highlighted the transformative role of technology in governance, citing e-Vidhan for digitizing legislative processes and DBT systems for streamlining benefit distribution. He believes technology enhances governance and accessibility.
