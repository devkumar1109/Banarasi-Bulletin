Elderly Man's Tragic Eid Sacrifice Attempt

2025-06-08T05:46:00+00:00

In Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, an elderly man named Ish Mohammad allegedly slit his own throat as a sacrifice on Eid-ul-Azha. He was found in a hut near his residence with a knife beside him. A note addressed to his family stated that he was offering himself as a sacrifice to Allah and his messenger. The 60-year-old man was taken to the district medical college and then referred to Gorakhpur Medical College, where he succumbed to his injuries. Police are investigating the incident and treating it as a suicide.
