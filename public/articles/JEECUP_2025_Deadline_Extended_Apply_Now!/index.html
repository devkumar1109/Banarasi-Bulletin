JEECUP 2025: Deadline Extended, Apply Now!

2025-04-30T12:40:00+00:00

The deadline to register for JEECUP 2025 has been extended to May 10, 2025. The exam will be held from May 20 to 28, 2025. The application fee for general and OBC candidates is Rs 300, while SC and ST candidates need to pay Rs 200. The application process can be completed online at jeecup.admissions.nic.in. Candidates who have passed the UP-Board Class 10 or 12 exams in 2025 are eligible to apply. JEECUP offers admission to over 77 diploma courses in various polytechnic institutes across Uttar Pradesh.
