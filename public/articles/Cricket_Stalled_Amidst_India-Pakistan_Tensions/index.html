Cricket Stalled Amidst India-Pakistan Tensions

2025-05-12T12:57:00+00:00

Due to heightened tensions between India and Pakistan following the Pahalgam attack and subsequent 'Operation Sindoor', several sporting events have been disrupted. The Indian Premier League (IPL) has been suspended for one week, with the May 9th match between Punjab Kings and Delhi Capitals halted mid-game. The BCCI expressed support for the Indian Armed Forces and is considering resuming the tournament in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, or Chennai, with the ECB offering to host it in England. Pakistan's own T20 tournament, the Pakistan Super League (PSL), has been postponed after a blast near the Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, allegedly caused by an Indian drone.
