Ambedkar's Legacy Lives On in UP Hostels

2025-03-05T11:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced that all new hostels for Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe students will be named after Bhimrao Ambedkar. Adityanath criticized opposition parties for neglecting Ambedkar's legacy, highlighting the BJP government's initiatives, including the construction of the Ambedkar International Centre and the creation of the 'Panch Teerth'. The government plans to develop key historical sites in Uttar Pradesh and implement welfare schemes, digital libraries, and other projects to promote social justice and heritage. Adityanath emphasized the BJP's commitment to continuing Ambedkar's work under their governance.
