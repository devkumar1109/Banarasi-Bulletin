Ambedkar's Legacy Lives On in UP Hostels

2025-03-05T11:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced that all new hostels for Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe students will be named after Bhimrao Ambedkar. Adityanath criticized opposition parties for neglecting Ambedkar's legacy while highlighting BJP initiatives like the Ambedkar International Centre and the 'Panch Teerth'. He promised developments at key historical sites in Uttar Pradesh, including welfare schemes and digital libraries, to continue fostering social justice and regional heritage.
