SpiceJet Flies to Varanasi, Pune Airport Booms

2025-03-30T18:45:00+00:00

SpiceJet launched a new flight from Pune to Varanasi starting March 30th, operating six days a week. This is the only addition to Pune airport's summer schedule, with 104 out of 109 available slots already booked by airlines. The airport director confirmed continued domestic network strengthening with 34 destinations, and international connections to Singapore, Dubai, and Bangkok. Congestion remains a concern, particularly during arrivals, with criticism aimed at the cab pick-up system. Nationally, the summer schedule saw a 5.5% increase in weekly flights compared to last year.
