Modi Unveils Rs 3,880 Crore Varanasi Development Push

2025-04-11T07:51:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for development projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. He emphasized the significant progress Varanasi has made in the last decade, highlighting its modernization while preserving its heritage. Modi noted that Kashi has become a center for Purvanchal's economic growth, citing projects focusing on infrastructure, water supply, education, healthcare, and sports. He stressed the government's commitment to improving amenities for all regions, families, and youth, aiming to transform Purvanchal into a developed region. The Prime Minister expressed his deep connection to Kashi, acknowledging the love and support he receives from the people.
