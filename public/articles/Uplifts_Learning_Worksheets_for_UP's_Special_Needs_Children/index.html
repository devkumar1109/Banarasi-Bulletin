Uplifts Learning: Worksheets for UP's Special Needs Children

2025-06-12T05:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has launched 'Sugamya Worksheets' in partnership with UNICEF to benefit nearly 3 lakh children with special needs. These worksheets, covering Hindi and Mathematics for classes 1 to 8, use simple language and engaging formats to make learning accessible. A total of 1,900 worksheets were developed, distributed to all districts and are being delivered to schools. Distribution is phased, with students receiving one set at a time. Teachers are instructed to use various activities to engage students with the worksheets. Special educators will visit schools to ensure proper implementation and support students. This initiative aims to enhance foundational learning, build confidence, and integrate differently-abled children into mainstream education.
