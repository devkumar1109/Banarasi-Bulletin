Perfect Score: UP Teen Tops JEE Main 2025

2025-03-07T02:48:00+00:00

Chirag Singh, a 17-year-old from Uttar Pradesh, has topped the JEE Main 2025 January session with a perfect 100 percentile score. He aspires to study Computer Science Engineering at IIT Bombay, followed by IIT Delhi and IIT Kanpur. Singh, who shifted to Lucknow for better educational opportunities, managed his time between school, coaching at Allen Online, and family time. Despite his perfect score, he plans to take the JEE April session to further improve his marks. This year, over 12.58 lakh candidates appeared for JEE Main, with 14 students achieving a 100 NTA score. Five toppers are from Rajasthan, two each from Delhi and Uttar Pradesh, and one each from Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Telangana, and Maharashtra.
