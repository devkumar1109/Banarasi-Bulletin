Holi Security Tightened in UP Amidst Coinciding Festivals

2025-03-13T05:46:00+00:00

To ensure peaceful Holi celebrations, the Uttar Pradesh Police has intensified security measures. This includes increased patrolling, drone surveillance, and CCTV monitoring, especially in communally sensitive areas. Authorities are also monitoring social media for any inflammatory content or fake news, as Holi and 'jumma' namaz coincide this year. Additional police units have been deployed in sensitive locations, and barricades have been set up at strategic points to manage crowds.
