False Bomb Threat Causes Panic on IndiGo Flight

2025-04-27T19:10:00+00:00

A Canadian national, Yohanathan Nishanth (23), was arrested after claiming to have a bomb on board an IndiGo flight from Varanasi to Bengaluru on Saturday night. The incident caused panic and a five-hour security check by CISF and bomb disposal squad. Nishanth allegedly made the claim while the plane was taxiing to the runway, becoming agitated after being asked to return to his seat. He also shouted religious slogans. No explosives were found. Passengers spent the night at the airport and the flight departed for Bengaluru on Sunday morning. The Canadian high commission has been informed. Police suspect Nishanth was under the influence of alcohol.
