Gulabi Meenakari: Pink Hues Paint a Bright Future

2025-04-30T09:33:00+00:00

Varanasi's Gulabi Meenakari, a pink enameling craft, is experiencing a resurgence in both domestic and international markets. The craft, recognized as a GI product and an ODOP (One District One Product), generates over Rs. 12 crore in annual business, employing hundreds of people, especially women. Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath have supported the craft, helping revive it from declining demand and counterfeit products. The government has provided training, loans, and grants while also creating market opportunities through marketing efforts. Demand for Gulabi Meenakari products, including women's jewelry and corporate gifts, is increasing from both domestic and international markets.
