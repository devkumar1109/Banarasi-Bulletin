Gulabi Meenakari: Pink Hues Paint a Bright Future

2025-05-02T14:43:00+00:00

Gulabi Meenakari, Varanasi's GI-tagged pink enameling craft, is thriving. After receiving GI status in 2015 and ODOP recognition in 2018, the craft has seen a resurgence. The annual business is estimated at Rs 12-15 crore, employing 250-300 people, with 30% being women. Efforts by the central and state governments, including training, loans, grants, and marketing initiatives, have revitalized the craft and attracted young artisans. Orders are pouring in from both domestic and international markets, particularly for women's jewellery, wedding gifts, and corporate gifting. Prime Minister Modi and Chief Minister Adityanath have promoted Gulabi Meenakari on the global stage, contributing to its success.
