Wife, Son-in-Law Plot Husband's Murder for Rs. 50,000

2025-03-29T19:22:00+00:00

In Sonbhadra, Uttar Pradesh, police arrested Sunita Devi for plotting her husband Ram Naresh Yadav's murder. She conspired with her son-in-law Alok Yadav, who hired Sandeep Kumar (15), Ankit Kumar, and Mayank Kol to carry out the killing. The murder weapon, a rod, as well as the deceased's phone and the motorcycle used in the crime were recovered. Sunita paid the killers Rs. 50,000 via Alok Yadav. The motive was Ram Naresh's alleged affair with another woman and his harassment of Sunita and their children. Police announced a Rs. 25,000 reward for the team.
