Varanasi Gangrape: 2 More Arrested, PM Orders Strict Action

2025-04-16T06:42:00+00:00

Two more arrests have been made in the Varanasi gangrape case, bringing the total number of arrests to 15. Prime Minister Narendra Modi intervened, directing officials to take strict action against the accused. The arrests were made based on CCTV analysis, facial mapping, and local information. Raj Khan and Mohammad Shahbaz were apprehended during a late-night raid. They have admitted to their involvement in the crime. Police are searching for several other suspects who have gone underground. The 19-year-old victim was abducted on March 29 and gang-raped for seven days before being abandoned in a semi-conscious state. She is currently battling Hepatitis-B.
