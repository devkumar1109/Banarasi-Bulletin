Doctor's Cold Cure: Cigarette Shock in UP Clinic

2025-04-16T13:15:00+00:00

A doctor in Uttar Pradesh, Suresh Chandra, is under investigation for allegedly making a child smoke a cigarette to treat a cold. The incident occurred at the primary health center in Kuthaund, Jalaun district. The authorities have initiated a probe into the matter.
