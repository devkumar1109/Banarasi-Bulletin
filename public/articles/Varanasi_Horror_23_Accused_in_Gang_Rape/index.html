Varanasi Horror: 23 Accused in Gang Rape

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men, including former classmates and Instagram contacts, over a week. The victim went missing after visiting a hookah bar with a friend on March 29th and returned home on April 4th, revealing the ordeal to her family. Police have filed an FIR against all 23 accused; six have been apprehended, while 11 remain unidentified. The girl alleges she was drugged with a spiked cold drink at the hookah bar and taken to different hotels where she was repeatedly assaulted. The investigation is ongoing.
