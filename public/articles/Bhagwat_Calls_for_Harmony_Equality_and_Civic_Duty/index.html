Bhagwat Calls for Harmony, Equality, and Civic Duty

2025-04-05T19:08:00+00:00

RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat called for a society based on harmony and equality, emphasizing the sharing of temples, water, and resources without caste discrimination. Addressing students and academicians in Varanasi, Bhagwat highlighted the need for social construction and urged youths to connect with the RSS through service and civic duty. He stressed the importance of strengthening families and promoting self-reliance by using indigenous products. Bhagwat also urged people to fulfill their civic duties, such as wearing helmets, following traffic rules, and maintaining cleanliness. He asked academicians to focus on developing values among students, particularly in rural areas.
