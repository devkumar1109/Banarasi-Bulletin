Kumbh Mela Disrupts Varanasi's Shiv Baraat

2025-02-25T10:18:00+00:00

The annual 'Shiv Baraat' procession in Varanasi, typically held on Maha Shivratri, has been rescheduled to the following day due to expected crowd congestion from the Kumbh Mela. This marks the first time in 42 years that the tradition has been altered. The decision has sparked political controversy, with Uttar Pradesh Congress chief Ajay Rai criticizing the BJP government's interference in Varanasi's cultural practices and calling for Prime Minister Modi's intervention to reinstate the traditional schedule. Rai argues that changing the procession's timing disrespects sacred customs.
