Azad Ravan Eyes Panchayat Power, Challenges BJP

2025-06-14T19:37:00+00:00

Chandrashekhar Azad 'Ravan', the chief of Azad Samaj Party (ASP), held a public meeting in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, emphasizing that the upcoming Panchayat elections will showcase his party's growing influence. He stated that the ASP's fight is primarily against the BJP and that the party aims to be the strongest force after the elections, carrying the momentum into the 2027 state assembly elections. Azad rejected the possibility of an alliance with the Samajwadi Party and criticized the BSP for failing to represent the interests of marginalized communities. He also accused the Yogi Adityanath government of irregularities in job recruitment for backward classes.
