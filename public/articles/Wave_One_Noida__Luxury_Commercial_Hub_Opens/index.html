Wave One Noida: Luxury Commercial Hub Opens

2025-03-10T10:02:00+00:00

Wave One Noida, a 40-story commercial building, is now open to businesses, investors, and retailers. Located in the heart of Noida's business district, it offers world-class office spaces, premium retail outlets, a club area, fine dining, and entertainment. The building features a luxurious entrance lobby, dedicated parking, and over 31 high-speed lifts. Its design aims to redefine the commercial landscape and provide an elite corporate environment. A religious ceremony, Path Shri Sukhmani Sahib, Kirtan and Ardaas, was held on March 4th, 2024, to mark the opening.
