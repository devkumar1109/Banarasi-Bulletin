Politics Meets Cricket: Saroj and Singh Tie the Knot

2025-06-09T04:59:00+00:00

Priya Saroj, the youngest MP from Uttar Pradesh, and Rinku Singh, a celebrated cricketer, are set to marry on November 18, 2025. Saroj, a Supreme Court lawyer, entered politics unexpectedly and is quickly making her mark. Singh rose from humble beginnings as a gas cylinder delivery boy to IPL stardom, known for his incredible match-winning performance. Their connection began through a mutual friend's father, a cricketer, and blossomed over a year of courtship. The engagement ceremony in Lucknow, attended by prominent figures from both politics and cricket, symbolized their unique cross-sphere union. Despite their public profiles, they maintained a private relationship until their ring exchange.
