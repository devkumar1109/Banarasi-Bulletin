Preity Zinta's Spiritual Journey to Maha Kumbh

2025-03-05T10:37:00+00:00

Actress Preity Zinta recently took a religious trip to Varanasi with her mother, concluding their Maha Kumbh pilgrimage in Prayagraj. Due to heavy crowds, they had to navigate the city using various modes of transportation, including cars, auto rickshaws, and cycle rickshaws. Preity observed the crowds to be decent and respectful, highlighting the positive energy surrounding the religious pilgrimage. She expressed that her mother's happiness throughout the journey was the greatest reward, emphasizing the power of faith and shared experiences.
