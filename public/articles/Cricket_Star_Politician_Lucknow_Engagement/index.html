Cricket Star, Politician: Lucknow Engagement

2025-06-09T09:10:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Rinku Singh and Samajwadi Party MP Priya Saroj got engaged in Lucknow on Sunday. The engagement ceremony was attended by prominent SP leaders, including Akhilesh Yadav and Dimple Yadav. Rinku Singh plays for Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) and Priya Saroj won the Machhlishahr seat in the 2024 general elections. The couple will marry on November 18, 2025, in Varanasi.
