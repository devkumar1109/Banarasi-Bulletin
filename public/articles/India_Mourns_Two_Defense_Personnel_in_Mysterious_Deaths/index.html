India Mourns Two Defense Personnel in Mysterious Deaths

2025-03-29T06:04:00+00:00

A 51-year-old Indian Air Force civil engineer, SN Mishra, was shot dead in his official residence in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. An unidentified assailant fired through his window while he was asleep, resulting in a fatal chest wound. In a separate incident, Lieutenant Colonel Diwarkar Singh, 49, was found dead in the parking area of a housing complex in Ranchi. Preliminary investigations suggest he may have fallen from a building, but the cause of death is pending post-mortem results. His family alleges he was under work-related stress. An internal investigation is underway.
