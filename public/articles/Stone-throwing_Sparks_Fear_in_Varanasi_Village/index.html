Stone-throwing Sparks Fear in Varanasi Village

2025-03-06T18:30:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a video showing boys from a particular community throwing stones in the Kotwa area sparked fear among 15 Hindu families who claim their houses were targeted. Police, examining CCTV footage, are investigating if the boys were aiming at streetlights or other targets. Tensions rise as this incident follows the killing of a Hindu man, Bhaiyalal Patel, in February, with some suspects still at large. Hindu families in the Muslim-dominated village allege deliberate targeting and fear for their safety. They point to the installation of CCTV cameras after Patel's murder and the recent stone-pelting incident captured on one of these cameras.
