Uttar Pradesh's Tele-Manas: Mental Health for All

2025-04-07T14:28:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government's Tele-Manas program provides free and confidential mental health support to over 525 individuals daily. Operated through four centers across the state, the program offers 24/7 access to 64 trained counselors. Launched in October 2022, Tele-Manas has benefited over 3.45 lakh people through helpline numbers 14416 and 1800-891-4416 (toll-free). The program was recently recognized by the Central Government as one of the best in the country, ranking third among large states.
