Aligarh School: Student Forced to Defile Pakistani Flag

2025-04-30T17:26:00+00:00

A student at a school in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, was forced to urinate on the Pakistani flag. Police have registered a First Information Report (FIR) in connection with the incident. The incident took place on April 30, 2025, and details about the student's identity and the accused individuals are not yet available. This incident has sparked outrage and condemnation, highlighting concerns about intolerance and respect for national symbols.
