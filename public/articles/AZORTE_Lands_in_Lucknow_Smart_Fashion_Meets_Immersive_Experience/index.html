AZORTE Lands in Lucknow: Smart Fashion Meets Immersive Experience

2025-03-20T01:48:00+00:00

AZORTE, a fashion retailer known for its contemporary styles, technology integration, and immersive shopping experience, has opened its first store in Uttar Pradesh at Lulu Mall, Lucknow. The new store offers a curated selection of Western and Indian wear, footwear, accessories, home essentials, and beauty products. AZORTE incorporates tech-enabled innovations such as smart trial rooms, fashion discovery stations, endless aisles, and self-checkout kiosks. Located at Amar Shaheed Path, Golf City, Lulu Mall, which spans 2.2 million sq. ft., is now home to AZORTE, strengthening its reputation as a premier shopping destination.
