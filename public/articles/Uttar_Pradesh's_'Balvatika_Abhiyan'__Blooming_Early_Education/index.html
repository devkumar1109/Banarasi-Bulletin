Uttar Pradesh's 'Balvatika Abhiyan': Blooming Early Education

2025-04-15T13:50:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched the 'Balvatika Abhiyan,' a two-week campaign from April 16 to 29, focused on transforming early childhood education. The initiative aims to enhance pre-primary education by converting Anganwadi centers into Balvatikas, providing structured learning opportunities for children aged 3-6. The campaign features community-driven activities, including parades, village assemblies, interactive sessions, and creative contests to promote early education and encourage community and parent participation. The ultimate goal is to ensure every child aged 3-6 is enrolled in Balvatika classes, preparing them for formal schooling and strengthening their educational foundation.
