Run for Clean Ganga: Varanasi Celebrates Water Day

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, 'Run for Clean Ganga,' and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw 6km run from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat, with Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal winning the men's and women's categories respectively. Ayush Minister Dayashankar Mishra Dayalu flagged off the event, stressing the importance of public awareness in cleaning the Ganga. The forest department celebrated World Water Day and Earth Hour at Dhakwa Ghat, launching a 'Dolphin Safari' to educate the public on dolphin conservation. Forest Conservator Ravi Kumar Singh emphasized efforts to protect the Gangetic Dolphin in the region.
