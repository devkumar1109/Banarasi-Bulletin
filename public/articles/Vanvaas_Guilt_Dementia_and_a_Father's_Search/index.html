"Vanvaas": Guilt, Dementia, and a Father's Search

2025-03-18T15:47:00+00:00

Utkarsh Sharma, starring in the film "Vanvaas," discusses the theme of "kid guilt" – the conflict between career ambitions and family. He emphasizes how work pressures can lead to neglecting loved ones, leaving children feeling estranged from their parents. "Vanvaas" tells the story of a retired father with dementia who is abandoned by his sons on his birthday. He embarks on a journey to find his family, encountering a thief along the way. The film explores themes of abandonment, dementia, and strained family relationships, highlighting the importance of cherishing loved ones.
