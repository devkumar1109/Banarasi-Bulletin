UP: Growth Engine Fueled by Modi, Reforms

2025-04-14T10:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state has become a growth engine for India, contributing to national development and realizing the vision of a developed India. He attributes this growth to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's leadership and investor-friendly policies. Uttar Pradesh has achieved a more than doubling of its GSDP and per capita income, becoming the second-largest state economy in the country. Adityanath contrasts this progress with the previous government, alleging they destroyed the state's economy and left it unable to pay employee salaries on time. He credits his government's efforts in stopping fund leakages, utilizing technology, and introducing reforms for the state's financial stability.
