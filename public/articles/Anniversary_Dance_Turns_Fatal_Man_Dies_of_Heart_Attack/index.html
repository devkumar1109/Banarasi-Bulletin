Anniversary Dance Turns Fatal: Man Dies of Heart Attack

2025-04-04T05:45:00+00:00

A 50-year-old shoe trader, Wasim Sarwat, from Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, died of a heart attack while dancing with his wife at their 25th wedding anniversary celebration on April 2nd. The incident occurred at a private hall and was captured on video, which has since gone viral. Sarwat was rushed to the hospital but doctors pronounced him dead. A senior cardiologist stated that such cases often stem from undiagnosed heart conditions, leading to sudden cardiac arrest. Sarwat is survived by his wife, Farah, a school teacher, and two sons.
