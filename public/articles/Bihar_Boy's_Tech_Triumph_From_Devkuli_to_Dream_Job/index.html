Bihar Boy's Tech Triumph: From Devkuli to Dream Job

2025-05-13T12:03:00+00:00

Aditya Vats, a man from Devkuli village, Buxar district, Bihar, has achieved success by studying hard and landing a job at a top tech company. Vats excelled academically, scoring 97.8% in his class 10 exams in 2019. He completed his schooling at DAV Patna and DAV Hazaribag. His story is an inspiration to those from underprivileged backgrounds, demonstrating that with determination and hard work, one can overcome limitations and achieve their dreams.
