Cop Caught at Wedding, Aunt's Past Claims Trap Him.

2025-05-08T18:17:00+00:00

A police constable in Chandauli district, Uttar Pradesh, was arrested at his wedding ceremony. He was accused by his paternal aunt of promising to marry her after years of physical relationship and fathering her 4-year-old son. The aunt had filed an FIR against him months ago. Instead of resolving the case, the constable went ahead with his marriage, leading to his arrest. The constable was arrested from Padumnathpur village and sent to jail.
