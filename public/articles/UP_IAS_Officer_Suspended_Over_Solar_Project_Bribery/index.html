UP IAS Officer Suspended Over Solar Project Bribery

2025-03-20T15:18:00+00:00

Abhishek Prakash, a 2006 batch IAS officer and IIT Roorkee alumnus, has been suspended by the Yogi Adityanath government in Uttar Pradesh. The suspension follows a complaint alleging Prakash demanded a 5% commission for approving a solar project. Vishwajeet Dutt, the complainant, claimed Prakash's office directed him to contact Nikant Jain, who demanded the commission and advance payment. CM Yogi has vowed to crack down on corruption, with this case adding to several ongoing investigations against IAS and PCS officers in the state. Seven suspended officers, including Ghanshyam Singh, were recently given new postings. Prakash, who hails from Bihar, has a distinguished career including serving as District Magistrate in several districts.
