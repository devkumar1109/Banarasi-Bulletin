UP Handball Dominates, Reaches National Final

2025-03-29T20:08:00+00:00

Hosts Uttar Pradesh defeated Maharashtra 13-7 to advance to the final of the 47th National Junior Girls Handball Championship. They will face Himachal Pradesh, who beat Rajasthan 20-16 in the other semi-final. Rajasthan and Maharashtra shared the bronze medal. Uttar Pradesh dominated the match, leading 6-5 at halftime and securing the victory with heightened intensity in the second half. Suman scored four goals for UP, while Ananya contributed three. Usha was named Player of the Match. The final will be held on Sunday and will be attended by Deputy CM Brijesh Pathak.
