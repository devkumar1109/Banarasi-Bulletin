Home Guard Dies by Suicide at Police Bungalow

2025-05-26T17:10:00+00:00

A 38-year-old home guard, America Patel, died by suicide on Sunday night at the commissioner of police's bungalow in Varanasi. Patel hanged himself in a corner of the CP's camp office premises. ACP Cantt Vidush Saxena confirmed Patel's suicide and stated that his body was sent for a post-mortem examination. Patel's wife informed officials that he had been battling depression, insomnia, and irritability for a long time and had expressed suicidal thoughts recently. DCP Varuna zone Pramod Kumar mentioned that Patel was experiencing personal health issues.
