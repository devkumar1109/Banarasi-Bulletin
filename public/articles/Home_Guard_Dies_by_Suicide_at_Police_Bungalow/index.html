Home Guard Dies by Suicide at Police Bungalow

2025-05-26T17:10:00+00:00

A 38-year-old home guard, America Patel, died by suicide at the commissioner of police's bungalow in Varanasi on Sunday night. Patel, who was deployed at the bungalow, hanged himself in a corner of the camp office premises. Officials believe Patel was suffering from depression, insomnia, and irritability, and had been seeking treatment for a long time. His wife informed officials that he had been stating his intention to end his life in recent days. ACP Cantt, Vidush Saxena, confirmed the incident and stated that Patel's body was sent for post-mortem. DCP Varuna zone, Pramod Kumar, said the home guard was troubled by personal health issues, and further investigation is underway.
