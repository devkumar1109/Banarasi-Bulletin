Indriya Shines in Lucknow, Eyes UP Expansion

2025-04-06T13:03:00+00:00

Aditya Birla Group's jewellery brand, Indriya, has opened its first store in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, marking its expansion into the state. CEO Sandeep Kohli highlighted Uttar Pradesh's large population and cultural affinity for jewellery as key factors. Indriya plans to open additional stores in major cities like Kanpur and Noida. With the recent openings in Lucknow and Vijayawada, Indriya now operates 21 stores across 10 Indian cities. Kohli expressed optimism about the Indian jewellery market's growth potential, despite volatile gold prices.
