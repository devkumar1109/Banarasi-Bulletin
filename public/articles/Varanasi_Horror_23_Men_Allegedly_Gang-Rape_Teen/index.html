Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Allegedly Gang-Rape Teen

2025-04-08T05:20:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men, including former classmates and Instagram contacts, over a period of seven days. The ordeal began on March 29 when she went missing after visiting a hookah bar with a friend. She returned home on April 4 and reported the incident to her family. Police have filed an FIR against all 23 accused. Six have been arrested, while 11 remain unidentified. The victim alleged that she was drugged with a spiked drink at the hookah bar and taken to various hotels where she was raped.
