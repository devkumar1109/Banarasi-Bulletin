Ritual Bath Turns Tragic: Brother Drowns

2025-05-05T15:31:00+00:00

A 21-year-old man, Ajit Pandey, drowned during a religious ritual, 'kalash yatra,' at Chanuki Ghat in Uttar Pradesh's Deoria district. He was bathing in the river with his younger brother, Shantanu, as part of a temple consecration ceremony. Both brothers struggled in deeper waters, and villagers rescued them. Shantanu was declared dead at a nearby health center, while Ajit is reported to be out of danger. Police are investigating the incident, and the body has been sent for post-mortem.
