Uttar Pradesh Farmers Master New Farming Techniques

2025-03-25T18:59:00+00:00

A two-day training program on direct seeded rice (DSR) and zero tillage wheat (ZTW) was held at the International Rice Research Institute-South Asia Regional Centre (ISARC) in Varanasi. 70 farmers from Eastern Uttar Pradesh participated. The program focused on sustainable rice-wheat cultivation techniques, including expert discussions, technology showcases, and field demonstrations. Experts highlighted the benefits of DSR and ZTW, such as reduced labour costs, water savings, and improved productivity. ISARC emphasized its commitment to supporting farmers with improved technologies and market linkages. Farmers received hands-on experience at ISARC's Mechanisation Hub, learning about seed drill calibration, equipment maintenance, and best agronomic practices.
