Sharma Scrutinizes Varanasi's Water, Waste Plans

2025-03-17T18:47:00+00:00

Urban Development Minister AK Sharma reviewed municipal corporation and water corporation projects in Varanasi. He emphasized quality drain cleaning and requested updates on closing drains flowing into the Ganga. The minister sought information on sewage treatment using Phytoremediation and Geotube methods. Sharma instructed zonal officers to conduct daily cleanliness inspections and coordinate waste removal. He also asked for information on drinking water supply for the upcoming summer. The minister directed the Jal Nigam to complete sewer line installation surveys for remaining wards and ensure timely implementation.
