Maharajganj Students Empowered by Digital Awareness

2025-04-29T07:12:00+00:00

A digital awareness campaign in Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh, significantly increased students' knowledge of digital skills and technology's role in their lives and careers. Organized by a youth group focused on community empowerment, the campaign featured sessions highlighting real-world applications of digital technology across industries, entrepreneurship, and employment. Students engaged in practical demonstrations and interactive discussions, exploring how technology can shape their futures. Guest speaker Prince Dwivedi further enhanced the event's value.
