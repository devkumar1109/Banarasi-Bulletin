Yogi Cracks Down on Tax Evasion, Seeks Bigger Share

2025-06-08T06:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath reviewed the State Tax Department in Lucknow, emphasizing transparency, technical efficiency, and strict enforcement in tax collection. He directed officials to take action against shell companies and fake registered firms, highlighting tax evasion as a national crime hindering development and public welfare. CM Yogi met with the 16th Finance Commission, seeking an increased share of central taxes for Uttar Pradesh, aiming for 50% instead of the current 41%. The state government presented its achievements over the past eight years to the commission, emphasizing successful progress.
