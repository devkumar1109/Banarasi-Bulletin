Sanskrit Speaks: UP's Missed Call to Revival

2025-05-29T12:35:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's initiative has trained over 53,000 people, half of them women, in spoken Sanskrit. Launched in 2018, the program utilizes the 'Missed Call Yojana' and innovative training methods to make Sanskrit accessible. Participants undergo a 20-day structured program, with additional sessions and internships for deeper understanding. The 'Grihe-Grihe Sanskritam' initiative aims to integrate Sanskrit into homes and communities, showing positive results according to Dr. Vachaspati Mishra.
