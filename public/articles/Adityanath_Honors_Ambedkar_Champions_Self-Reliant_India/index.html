Adityanath Honors Ambedkar, Champions Self-Reliant India

2025-04-14T16:13:00+00:00

On Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's 134th birth anniversary, Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath paid tribute to Ambedkar's contributions to social justice and the Indian Constitution. Adityanath emphasized the importance of education and fulfilling responsibilities for protecting rights, urging youth to build a self-reliant India inspired by Ambedkar's ideals. He highlighted Ambedkar's role in introducing equal voting rights in 1952 and lauded the transformation in Uttar Pradesh's investment climate. Adityanath praised national initiatives enhancing economic and social welfare and acknowledged the university's academic achievements.
