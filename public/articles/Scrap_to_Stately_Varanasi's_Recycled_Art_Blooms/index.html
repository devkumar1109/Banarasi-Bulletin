Scrap to Stately: Varanasi's Recycled Art Blooms

2025-05-08T18:20:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a beautification project is underway, installing 62 unique sculptures crafted from scrap materials at prominent locations. The initiative, a collaboration between the Varanasi Development Authority, Banaras locomotive works, and the Ministries of Railways and Culture, aims to promote environmental conservation and artistic creativity. The sculptures, already partially installed, embody themes like women's empowerment, yoga, sports, and cultural heritage, each reflecting the significance of its location. Additional beautification elements include landscaping, greenery, and night lighting. The project not only enhances the city's aesthetics but also highlights its social and cultural values while promoting sustainable development through material reuse.
