Greater Noida: India's Medical Device Hub Takes Shape

2025-06-18T01:32:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government launched a scheme to boost medical device manufacturing in Greater Noida. YEIDA is allotting 21 industrial plots in Sector-28 to medical device manufacturers. Applications are open until July 7th. The plots range from 1,000 to 2,100 square meters, with premiums starting at Rs. 77.30 lakh. The scheme prioritizes high-growth sectors like cancer care, radiology, and IVD. Greater Noida's Medical Device Park will be the largest in India. The location offers connectivity via the Yamuna Expressway and proximity to key infrastructure projects like the Noida International Airport and the Electronics Manufacturing Cluster.
