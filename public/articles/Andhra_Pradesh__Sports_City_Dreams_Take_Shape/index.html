Andhra Pradesh: Sports City Dreams Take Shape

2025-06-12T17:14:00+00:00

The Andhra Pradesh government plans to establish an international sports university and a sports city spanning over 2,000 acres in Vijayawada. Sports Authority of Andhra Pradesh (SAAP) chairman A Ravi Naidu announced the initiative, aiming to make Amaravati the "sports capital" of India. The state has proposed to host the 2026 Khelo India Games in Vijayawada. The Indira Gandhi Municipal Stadium in Vijayawada is undergoing an upgrade to host international events, with a cost of ₹70 crore. The government also plans to build stadia in every assembly constituency and develop sports infrastructure in 46,000 government schools.
