Cricket Star Rinku Singh to Wed Politician Priya Saroj

2025-06-01T11:12:00+00:00

Indian cricketer Rinku Singh will be engaged to Samajwadi Party MP Priya Saroj on June 8th in Lucknow. The wedding is scheduled for November 18th at Hotel Taj in Varanasi. Priya's father, Toofani Saroj, confirmed the engagement and said it will be a private affair with close family and friends. He shared that the families arranged the marriage after meeting in Aligarh. Rinku, 27, has played for India in both ODIs and T20Is and currently plays for the Kolkata Knight Riders in the IPL. Priya, 26, is a first-time MP from the Machhlishahr constituency in Jaunpur.
