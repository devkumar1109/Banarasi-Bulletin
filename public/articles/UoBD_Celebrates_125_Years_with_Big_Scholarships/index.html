UoBD Celebrates 125 Years with Big Scholarships

2025-05-07T07:16:00+00:00

The University of Birmingham Dubai (UoBD) is offering 125 scholarships covering up to 50% tuition for students entering in September 2025. The scholarships celebrate the university's 125th anniversary and are open to both undergraduate and postgraduate students in fields like engineering, health sciences, and business. Indian students are eligible for the 125 Scholarship and an additional scholarship of up to 40% if they don't qualify for the full 125%. Scholarships are awarded based on academic merit and are offered by invitation only.
