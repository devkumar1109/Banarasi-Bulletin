Sand Dispute Turns Deadly, Three Killed in Bihar Village

2025-05-26T01:30:00+00:00

Three people were killed and two seriously injured in a family dispute in Bihar's Ahiyapur village. The altercation, sparked by a disagreement over the unloading of sand and stone, escalated into a physical fight and gunfire. An alleged video shows both groups initially fighting with sticks before gunfire erupted. One relative, Om Prakash Singh, has been arrested, and another detained for questioning. Nineteen relatives and three associates have been named in the case. The local police chowkidar, Ritesh Pandey, has been suspended for failing to report the escalating dispute to the SHO, which police believe could have prevented the tragedy.
