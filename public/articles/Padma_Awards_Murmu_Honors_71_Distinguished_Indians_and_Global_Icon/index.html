Padma Awards: Murmu Honors 71 Distinguished Indians and Global Icon

2025-04-28T17:36:00+00:00

President Droupadi Murmu conferred Padma Awards to 71 distinguished individuals. The honors included four Padma Vibhushan, ten Padma Bhushan, and 57 Padma Shri awards. Padma Vibhushan recipients included violinist L. Subramaniam and the late Osamu Suzuki, former CEO of Suzuki Motor Corporation. Padma Bhushan awardees included filmmaker Shekhar Kapur, actor Nandamuri Balakrishna, actor S. Ajith Kumar, and the late singer Pankaj Udhas. Notable Padma Shri recipients included playback singer Jaspinder Narula, cricketer Ravichandran Ashwin, and Vedic scholar Ganeshwar Shastri Dravid. American author Stephen Knapp also received the Padma Shri.
