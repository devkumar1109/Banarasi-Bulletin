Wife, Lovers Kill Electrician, Dismember Body

2025-05-13T19:10:00+00:00

Devendra Ram, a 62-year-old retired electrician, was murdered by his wife Maya and her three lovers: Mithilesh Patel, Anil Yadav, and Satish Yadav. The crime took place in Ballia, India, on May 8. Human limbs were found in a field, leading to the arrest of Maya and Mithilesh. Devendra's torso was recovered from a well, and police are searching for his head, allegedly dumped in the Ghagra river. Anil and Satish were arrested after a police encounter. They confessed to the murder and the weapon used was recovered. Police investigations revealed that Devendra lived separately from his wife, with their children residing elsewhere.
