Meerut Transformed: Adityanath Hails BJP Development

2025-03-09T13:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath praised the BJP government's development initiatives in Meerut, emphasizing improved connectivity through the country's first regional rapid transit system and a newly inaugurated 12-lane expressway. The Ganga Expressway, connecting Meerut to Prayagraj with plans for extension into Uttarakhand, is nearing completion. Meerut will also be home to the state's first sports university, named after hockey legend Dhyan Chand, scheduled to open in 2025. Adityanath credited Prime Minister Narendra Modi's guidance for Meerut's transformation.
