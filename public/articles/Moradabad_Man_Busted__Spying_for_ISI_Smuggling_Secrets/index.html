Moradabad Man Busted: Spying for ISI, Smuggling Secrets

2025-05-19T05:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Special Task Force (STF) arrested Shahzad from Moradabad for allegedly spying for Pakistan’s ISI. Intelligence inputs revealed his involvement in cross-border smuggling and espionage activities. Shahzad is accused of sharing sensitive information related to India's security infrastructure with ISI handlers for several years. He allegedly smuggled goods across the India-Pakistan border while secretly transmitting critical intelligence to foreign agents. Shahzad has been booked under Indian espionage and national security laws. His frequent trips to Pakistan are under investigation.
