Blue Drum Gift at Wedding Sparks Online Outrage

2025-04-19T16:30:00+00:00

A wedding in Uttar Pradesh's Hamirpur went viral after the groom's friends gifted him a large blue drum. The incident sparked online criticism, with many deeming the gift insensitive, especially considering the association of "blue drum" with a tragic murder case in Meerut where a woman and her lover dismembered a husband and hid the body in a blue drum. Netizens condemned the act, calling it inappropriate and questioning the humor behind the gift on a joyous occasion.
