Kashmir Fury: Varanasi United Against Terror

2025-04-23T17:40:00+00:00

Following the Pahalgam terror attack, Varanasi witnessed widespread protests and prayer events. Kashi Vishwanath Dham hosted an Ati Rudra recital, while the BJP postponed a Waqf Amendment awareness drive. Muslim institutions like Anjuman Intejamia Masajid and the Muslim Women's Foundation condemned the attack, expressing condolences to the families of the victims. A protest march, jointly organized by Vishal Bharat Sansthan and the Muslim Women's Foundation, culminated in a symbolic cremation of terrorists. Participants called for action against Pakistan and demanded a Fatwa expelling terrorists from Islam. Various groups, including lawyers, social and voluntary organizations, also held marches and burned effigies of terrorists.
