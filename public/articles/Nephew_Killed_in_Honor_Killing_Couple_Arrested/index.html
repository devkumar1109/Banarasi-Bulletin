Nephew Killed in "Honor" Killing, Couple Arrested

2025-04-11T04:37:00+00:00

A man and wife in Bahraich, Uttar Pradesh, have been arrested for murdering their 22-year-old nephew, Bhanu Pratap. The victim's body was found in a sack near a school on March 29 with a rope around his neck. Police investigation, including CCTV analysis and mobile phone records examination, led to the arrest of Chetram Gautam and his wife, Sundari Devi. The couple confessed to killing Bhanu after finding him in a "compromising situation" with a girl from their family. They strangled him, placed him in a sack, and dumped the body. Bhanu's father had previously filed a complaint accusing Chetram and his family of the murder. The case has sparked outrage in the community, highlighting the dangers of familial violence and honor killings.
