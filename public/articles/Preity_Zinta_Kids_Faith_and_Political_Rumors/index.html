Preity Zinta: Kids, Faith, and Political Rumors

2025-04-29T09:31:00+00:00

Preity Zinta revealed she was discussing pictures of their children with Virat Kohli after a recent IPL match between RCB and PBKS. Zinta, who has been living in the US with her husband and twins, addressed speculation about her potential political entry, clarifying that visiting religious sites and being proud of her Indian heritage doesn't equate to joining any political party, particularly the BJP. She emphasized her decision to raise her children as Hindus and expressed frustration at the politicization of her personal choices.
