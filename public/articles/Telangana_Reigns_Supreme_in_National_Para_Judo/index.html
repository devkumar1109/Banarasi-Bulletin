Telangana Reigns Supreme in National Para Judo

2025-02-26T19:20:00+00:00

Telangana won the overall championship in the sub-junior category of the National Para Judo Championships held in Lucknow. They secured two gold and one bronze medal, while Uttar Pradesh came in second with one gold, three silver, and two bronze medals. Savita from Karnataka and Riku Gujjar from Madhya Pradesh were named the best judokas in the girls' and boys' categories respectively. Sports Hostel Lucknow qualified for the semi-finals of the 2nd Late Pandit Ram Avtar Mishra State Open Hockey Tournament after victories against Sports College Lucknow 'A' and Sports Hostel Jhansi. In the Lucknow Golf League, Legends defeated Speed Chargers 4-1 to advance to the final.
