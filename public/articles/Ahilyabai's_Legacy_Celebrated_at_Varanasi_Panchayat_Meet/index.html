Ahilyabai's Legacy Celebrated at Varanasi Panchayat Meet

2025-05-31T14:52:00+00:00

A Panchayat representative conference was held in Varanasi on May 30, 2025, to celebrate the 300th birth anniversary of Ahilyabai Holkar. UP Finance Minister Suresh Khanna highlighted Prime Minister Modi's initiatives like Veer Bal Day, Constitution Day, and Ahilyabai's birth anniversary celebrations. He also emphasized India's economic growth and women's empowerment under the current government. District panchayat president Poonam Maurya praised Ahilyabai for her contributions to women's upliftment, opposing Sati, supporting widow remarriage and property rights, and her dedication to temple and environmental conservation.
