BJP Reshuffles UP Leadership, Eyes Strength

2025-03-16T14:04:00+00:00

The BJP appointed 70 new district unit presidents in Uttar Pradesh, including five women and 26 returning leaders. The appointments aim to strengthen the party's presence across six regions in the state. Key appointments include Vijay Maurya as Lucknow district president and Anand Dwivedi for the city unit. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath and BJP state president Bhupendra Singh Chaudhary congratulated the new officials. An additional 28 district unit leaders are expected to be announced soon.
