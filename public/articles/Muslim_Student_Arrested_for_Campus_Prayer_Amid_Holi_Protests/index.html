Muslim Student Arrested for Campus Prayer Amid Holi Protests

2025-03-17T07:43:00+00:00

A Muslim student, Khalil Pradhan, was arrested in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh for offering prayers in an open area of the Intellectual Institute of Management and Technology University. The arrest followed protests by Hindu groups who objected to a video circulating online that purportedly showed students performing namaz on the university campus during Holi celebrations.
