Fake Diplomat Nabbed in Ghaziabad Sting

2025-03-14T06:04:00+00:00

A man impersonating the Oman High Commissioner was arrested in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. The individual used a fake identity and diplomatic passport to gain access to sensitive information and individuals. Authorities are investigating the extent of the impersonation and the man's motives.
