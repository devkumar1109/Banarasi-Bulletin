UP Fights Floods: Adityanath Leads with Drones and Dams

2025-02-28T17:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is leading efforts to address the state's persistent flooding. He has ordered a comprehensive review of river conditions, prioritizing dredging and embankment construction. Drone surveys will be used for precise data collection to inform planning. Adityanath praised the completion of 1,575 flood mitigation projects, emphasizing the importance of timely execution and budget adherence. These projects have safeguarded millions of people from flooding.
