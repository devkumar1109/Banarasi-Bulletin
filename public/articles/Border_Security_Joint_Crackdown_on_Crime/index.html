Border Security: Joint Crackdown on Crime

2025-04-03T18:59:00+00:00

Panchkula and Himachal Pradesh police will launch a joint operation to curb crime in border areas. They will share lists of proclaimed offenders and absconding criminals for joint apprehension. Properties of drug traffickers residing in Himachal but operating in Haryana will be demolished. Joint operations will target active drug peddlers and traffickers, and combing operations will monitor relocated groups from Parwanoo to prevent illegal activities. Raids will be conducted against mining mafia, and buses from Uttar Pradesh and Bihar will be monitored for smuggling. Joint action will also be taken against extortion gangs active in Baddi and Parwanoo.
