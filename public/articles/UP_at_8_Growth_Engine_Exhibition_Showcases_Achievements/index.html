UP at 8: Growth Engine Exhibition Showcases Achievements

2025-03-20T12:03:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will celebrate eight years of its 'Seva, Suraksha, Sushasan' policy with a three-day exhibition event from March 25 to 27. The event, themed ‘UP: Growth Engine of Bharat,’ will showcase achievements of the state and central governments under the BJP in the past eight years. Exhibits will highlight successes of welfare schemes, infrastructure projects, new industrial units, and flagship initiatives like expressways, hospitals, medical colleges, and universities. Chief Secretary Manoj Kumar Singh held a video conference with divisional commissioners and district magistrates to ensure smooth execution of the events.
