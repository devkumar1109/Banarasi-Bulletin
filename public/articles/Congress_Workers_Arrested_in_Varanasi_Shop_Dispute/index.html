Congress Workers Arrested in Varanasi Shop Dispute

2025-06-07T01:50:00+00:00

Congress workers were arrested in Varanasi on Thursday for allegedly harassing a woman and forcibly evicting her from her shop in the Chetganj area. The arrests followed a complaint by the woman. The Congress party claims the arrests are a frame-up. This incident has sparked a political row in Uttar Pradesh.
