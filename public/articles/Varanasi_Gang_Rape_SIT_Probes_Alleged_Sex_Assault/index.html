Varanasi Gang Rape: SIT Probes Alleged Sex Assault

2025-04-18T04:15:00+00:00

A 6-member SIT led by a Deputy Commissioner of Police will investigate the alleged gang rape of a 19-year-old woman in Varanasi. The victim accused 23 individuals, and 14 have been arrested. The alleged assaults took place over several days in March and April. The victim claims she was intoxicated and raped by multiple men. The victim's family alleges money was extorted from them and that the woman was seen in compromising positions in videos. Authorities are searching for the remaining suspects.
