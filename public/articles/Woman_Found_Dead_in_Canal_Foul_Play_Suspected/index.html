Woman Found Dead in Canal, Foul Play Suspected

2025-04-13T10:50:00+00:00

The body of 50-year-old Rachna, a mentally unfit woman from Hapur district, was found in a dry canal in Hathras on April 13th. Police suspect foul play as a dupatta was found tied around her neck. Rachna's family states she routinely left home in the mornings and returned in the evenings but did not return on Saturday, April 12th. The body was sent for post-mortem to determine the cause of death. Rachna's husband is a farmer and she is survived by two adult sons. Additional Superintendent of Police (ASP) Ashok Kumar Singh stated that an investigation is underway with forensic assistance.
