Celebrating Ahilyadevi: NCW Honors Legacy in Varanasi

2025-05-29T13:16:00+00:00

The National Commission for Women (NCW) will host a two-day program in Varanasi to celebrate the 300th birth anniversary of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar. The event will be held at RUDRAKSH – International Cooperation and Convention Centre on May 31st. NCW chairperson Vijaya Rahatkar will deliver the keynote address and an exhibition showcasing Ahilyadevi Holkar's life and legacy will be inaugurated. The celebrations begin on May 30th with a lecture by Rahatkar at Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith University, followed by a tribute at Shubham Lawn and Banquet. A painting competition and rangoli competition will also be held.
