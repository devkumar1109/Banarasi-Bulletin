UP Strengthens Police Force with Mass Recruitments

2025-06-18T12:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath held a 'Janta Darshan' at Gorakhnath temple in Gorakhpur on Wednesday, addressing public grievances. The UP government has recruited 2.16 lakh police personnel in the last eight years, aiming to transform the police force into a 'modern police'. Union Home Minister Amit Shah distributed appointment letters to 60,244 newly recruited constables in Lucknow on Sunday. Shah emphasized the significance of the UP Police and credited Yogi Adityanath for restoring its strength. The Uttar Pradesh Police Constable Recruitment Exam 2024, held across 67 districts, aimed to fill over 60,000 vacancies.
