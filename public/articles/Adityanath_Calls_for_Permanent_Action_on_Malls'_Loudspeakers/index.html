Adityanath Calls for "Permanent Action" on Malls' Loudspeakers

2025-03-12T22:52:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has called for "permanent action" against the use of loudspeakers at religious places. His statement comes amidst rising tensions over noise pollution from religious gatherings. Adityanath emphasized the need to respect the sentiments of all communities and ensure peace and harmony. While the specific actions he envisions remain unclear, his call highlights the government's commitment to addressing the issue of loudspeakers at religious sites.
