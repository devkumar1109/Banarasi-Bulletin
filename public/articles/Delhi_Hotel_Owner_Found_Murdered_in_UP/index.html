Delhi Hotel Owner Found Murdered in UP

2025-04-03T09:15:00+00:00

Sagar, a businessman from West Delhi, was found murdered in Shamli, Uttar Pradesh, after being reported missing. Sagar, who owned a leased hotel in Tilak Nagar, disappeared on March 27. Delhi Police registered an abduction case and formed investigation teams to track the suspects. On March 27, a murder case was registered at Shamli's Kandla police station, and Sagar's body was identified on April 2. Delhi Police are working with Uttar Pradesh authorities to locate the suspects, against whom non-bailable warrants have been issued.
