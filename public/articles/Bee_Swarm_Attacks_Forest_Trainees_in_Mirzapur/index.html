Bee Swarm Attacks Forest Trainees in Mirzapur

2025-05-28T19:13:00+00:00

A swarm of bees attacked a group of 54 forest officer trainees from Kanpur Nagar in Mirzapur district, injuring 16 officers. Four officers sustained critical injuries and were referred to the district hospital. The incident occurred near a nursery in Rajapura village while the trainees were visiting the Chandan Forest Rest House. Blankets and fire were used to protect the officers, and they were evacuated from the forest. Divisional forest officer Arvind Raj Mishra visited the site and confirmed that all officers are now out of danger.
