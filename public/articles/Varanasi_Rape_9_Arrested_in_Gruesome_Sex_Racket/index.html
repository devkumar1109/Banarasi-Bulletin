Varanasi Rape: 9 Arrested in Gruesome Sex Racket

2025-04-08T17:01:00+00:00

Nine people have been arrested in connection with the gang rape of a 19-year-old girl in Varanasi. The victim was allegedly assaulted by 23 men over a week. Among the arrested are businessmen, shop and hotel staff, and students. One suspect, who operates a hookah bar, is believed to be the leader of the sex racket. He was previously charged with running a sex racket in 2022. Police are continuing to search for the remaining accused, including three identified individuals and 11 unidentified ones. The investigation revealed that the victim was drugged and subjected to continuous physical abuse.
