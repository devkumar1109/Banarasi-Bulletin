Bengal's "Ram Rajya," Modi's Fund Gripe, Political Shifts

2025-04-06T02:50:00+00:00

Union Minister Sukanta Majumdar stated that the upcoming times will see the establishment of "Ram Rajya" in Bengal, citing the large Hindu turnout for Ram Navami processions. Prime Minister Narendra Modi, during his visit to Rameswaram, indirectly criticized the DMK government, saying that despite increased central funding for Tamil Nadu, some parties still complain about resources. Meanwhile, former Kerala minister M. A. Baby was elected as the new CPI(M) general secretary. In Karnataka, the BJP is launching a 16-day agitation against price rise and Muslim quota in government contracts, starting Monday. President Droupadi Murmu has approved the Waqf (Amendment) Bill, prompting reactions from various political parties.
