Cow Smuggler Shot, Arrested in Uttar Pradesh

2025-04-19T06:31:00+00:00

A cow smuggler named Imran was injured and arrested following an encounter with police in Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh. Imran opened fire on the police during the encounter, resulting in retaliatory fire that injured him. Circle Officer (Jansath) Yatendra Singh Nagar confirmed the incident. The encounter took place on April 19, 2025.
