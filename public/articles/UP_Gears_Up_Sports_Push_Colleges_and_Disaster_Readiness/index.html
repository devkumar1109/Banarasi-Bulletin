UP Gears Up: Sports Push, Colleges, and Disaster Readiness

2025-05-22T18:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has ordered the accelerated and high-quality completion of all sports infrastructure projects. He envisions establishing sports colleges in each state division, elevating them to Centres of Excellence affiliated with the Sports University. Adityanath emphasized the importance of frequent reviews for these projects and stressed disaster management training for Prantiya Rakshak Dal (PRD) personnel. He highlighted the state's growing sports infrastructure and its commitment to developing world-class sporting facilities.
