Varanasi Embraces Yoga Week Ahead of International Day

2025-06-16T00:06:00+00:00

International Yoga Week commenced in Varanasi on June 16, 2025, leading up to International Yoga Day on June 21. A grand inauguration took place at Namo Ghat, attended by dignitaries including MLA Neelkanth Tiwari and District Magistrate Satyendra Kumar. Simultaneous yoga sessions were held across the district in educational institutions, parks, ghats, jails, and village panchayats. Events included standard yoga protocols led by instructors Ashish Upadhyay, Abhay Swabhimani, and Raksha Kaur. The Government Ayurvedic College and Hospital also hosted a special yoga session with mass participation, featuring Surya Namaskar, Pranayama, and various asanas. The week-long celebrations will continue with daily events such as group yoga practices, seminars, and awareness rallies.
