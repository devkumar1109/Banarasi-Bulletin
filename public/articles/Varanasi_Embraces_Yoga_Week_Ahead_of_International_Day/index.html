Varanasi Embraces Yoga Week Ahead of International Day

2025-06-16T00:06:00+00:00

International Yoga Week commenced in Varanasi on June 16th, leading up to International Yoga Day on June 21st. The week-long celebration began with a mass yoga session at Namo Ghat, attended by dignitaries and led by experienced instructors. Simultaneous sessions were held at various locations across the district, including educational institutions, parks, ghats, jails, and panchayats. The Government Ayurvedic College and Hospital also celebrated with a special yoga session featuring students, teachers, doctors, and staff. Events will continue throughout the week, including group yoga practices, seminars, slogan competitions, awareness rallies, and street plays.
