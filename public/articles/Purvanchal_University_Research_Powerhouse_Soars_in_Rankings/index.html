Purvanchal University: Research Powerhouse Soars in Rankings

2025-03-17T18:47:00+00:00

Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University ranked second in Uttar Pradesh and 125th in India in the Nature Index Ranking 2025. The university achieved a score of 2 with a share of 0.74, reflecting its research output and academic excellence. Purvanchal University's success is attributed to publications in high-impact research journals, showcasing its scientific research capabilities. Vice-chancellor Prof Vandana Singh emphasized the university's focus on research quality, leading to significant contributions in various fields like science, technology, and medicine. The university also holds an A+ ranking from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council and received Rs 7.13 crore under the DST-PURSE scheme.
