AI-Powered Kumbh Mela: Millions Dive in Safety

2025-02-26T05:32:00+00:00

The Divine Maha Kumbh, the world's largest religious gathering, has seen nearly 64 crore devotees take a holy dip. To manage the massive crowds, advanced AI-driven systems are being used. 1700 cameras, including 500 AI-enabled ones, are monitoring the situation, estimating crowd density and tracking individuals. Data and alerts are sent to a cloud-based dashboard at Prayagraj’s integrated command centre, helping authorities maintain law and order. The Kumbh Mela is expected to end after the Amrit Snan on Mahashivratri.
