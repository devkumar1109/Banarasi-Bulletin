Varanasi Preps for Grand Akshaya Tritiya Mass Wedding

2025-04-25T17:49:00+00:00

Officials in Varanasi inspected the Sankuldhara Pond in Khojwa to prepare for a mass wedding event on Akshaya Tritiya (April 30). The event will include a procession with horse-drawn carriages, altars around the pond, a main stage, and arrangements for food and parking. Officials reviewed cleanliness, parking, road repairs, lighting, pond cleaning, and security. The event is expected to draw many families of brides and grooms.
