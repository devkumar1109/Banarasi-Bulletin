Ganga Conservation: Empowering Districts for Clean Water

2025-03-24T13:49:00+00:00

The State Clean Ganga Mission (SMCG-UP) held a three-day workshop in Lucknow to strengthen Ganga conservation efforts. The workshop focused on empowering District Project Officers (DPOs) with training on financial, technical, and administrative aspects of project implementation. The event emphasized the importance of District Ganga Committees in maintaining the river's cleanliness and continuous flow. DPOs received training on budget allocation, District Ganga Committee functions, the Ganga District Project Management System (GDPMS) Portal, and wastewater management. The workshop highlighted the need for public participation alongside government efforts in tackling pollution and conserving the Ganges, emphasizing its cultural and religious significance.
