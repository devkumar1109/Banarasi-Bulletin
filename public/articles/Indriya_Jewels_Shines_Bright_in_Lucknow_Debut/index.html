Indriya Jewels Shines Bright in Lucknow Debut

2025-04-06T13:03:00+00:00

Aditya Birla Group's jewellery brand, Indriya, has opened its first store in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, marking its expansion into the state. CEO Sandeep Kohli highlighted Uttar Pradesh's large population and cultural affinity for jewellery as key drivers for this move. Indriya plans to reach 21 stores across 10 Indian cities, including Kanpur and Noida in Uttar Pradesh. The brand recently opened stores in Lucknow and Vijayawada, joining existing locations in Delhi, Mumbai, and Hyderabad. Kohli expressed optimism about the Indian jewellery market's potential growth to USD 80-100 billion, despite volatile gold prices.
