Lover's Plot: Meerut Couple Murders Husband, Dismembers Body

2025-03-19T08:23:00+00:00

In Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, a woman named Muskan (27) and her lover, Sahil (25), allegedly murdered Muskan's husband, Saurabh Rajput (29), a merchant navy employee. The crime occurred on March 4th. After stabbing Saurabh to death, the couple dismembered his body, placed the remains in a cement-filled drum, and concealed it. Police arrested Muskan and Sahil based on suspicion and recovered the body after the duo confessed to the crime during interrogation. Muskan reportedly attempted to mislead her husband's family by sending messages from Saurabh's phone. Following the murder, she and Sahil went on a vacation to Shimla and Manali. A case has been registered against the accused, and the body has been sent for post-mortem.
