Blue Drum Gift Sparks Outrage at Indian Wedding

2025-04-19T16:30:00+00:00

In a bizarre incident, a groom in Hamirpur, Uttar Pradesh received a large blue drum as a wedding gift from a friend. The video of the gift went viral, sparking outrage and criticism online. Netizens condemned the gesture, deeming it insensitive, especially considering the infamous "blue drum" incident in Meerut where a woman and her lover murdered her husband and concealed his body in a drum. Many expressed their shock and questioned the appropriateness of using such a symbol on a joyous occasion like a wedding.
