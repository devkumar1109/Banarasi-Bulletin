Gorakhpur to Get Seven Divine Gateways

2025-05-28T19:13:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has approved a plan to construct seven grand gateways at Gorakhpur's borders. Proposed by Gorakhpur MP Ravi Kishan Shukla, the gateways will symbolize the divine heritage of the Nath Panth and showcase the city's cultural legacy. They will be built along key entry routes and feature depictions of Guru Gorakhnath and other Siddha Yogis, highlighting the city's religious and philosophical roots. The project, overseen by the Gorakhpur Development Authority, is expected to enhance the city's aesthetics and cultural pride.
