Tipsy Ride: Drunk Man Borrows CBI Officer's Bike

2025-03-06T02:29:00+00:00

A tipsy man in Uttar Pradesh mistakenly rode home on a CBI officer's motorbike after a night of drinking. The officer, who was in town investigating a railway exam paper leak, discovered his bike missing and CCTV footage revealed the man's mistake. The man returned the bike to the police the next morning with an apology. The CBI officer chose not to press charges, as his team had made significant progress in the paper leak case, arresting 26 suspects and seizing Rs 1.2 crore.
