Varanasi Gang Rape: 9 Arrested, 11 Still At Large

2025-04-09T04:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. The police arrested nine accused and sent them to judicial custody. The victim, who went missing on March 29th after visiting a ghat with a friend, was found unconscious on April 4th. She revealed that she was lured, intoxicated, and raped repeatedly. The police are searching for the remaining accused, eleven of whom are unidentified. The victim's father appealed to Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath for justice, requesting severe punishment for the perpetrators.
