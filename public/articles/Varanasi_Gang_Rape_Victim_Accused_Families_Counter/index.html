Varanasi Gang Rape: Victim Accused, Families Counter.

2025-04-17T11:08:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi alleges she was gangraped by 23 individuals between March 29 and April 4. She claims she was drugged and assaulted at multiple locations. 14 people have been arrested so far, with the remaining suspects still at large. The accused's families counterclaim that the victim extorted money from them and present videos as evidence. They allege she was seen in "questionable postures" at various locations. The families also question why she didn't report the alleged rapes to police earlier. A 6-member SIT led by a DCP rank officer has been formed to investigate the case.
