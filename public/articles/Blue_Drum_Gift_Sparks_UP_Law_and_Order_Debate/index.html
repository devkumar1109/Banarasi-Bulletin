Blue Drum Gift Sparks UP Law and Order Debate

2025-04-02T07:48:00+00:00

A Samajwadi Party spokesperson, Deepak Ranjan, gifted a blue drum to Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak, sparking a political debate tied to the Meerut murder case. The case involves Saurabh Rajput, a Merchant Navy officer, who was murdered by his wife, Muskan Rastogi, and her lover, Sahil Shukla. They dismembered Rajput's body and hid it in a blue drum filled with cement. The blue drum has become a symbol of the horrific crime, leading to a surge in online discussion and memes. Ranjan's gift is seen as a jab at the Uttar Pradesh government's handling of law and order. The incident has also impacted the sales of blue drums in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh.
