Muslim Women Celebrate Ram Navami in Varanasi

2025-04-06T19:26:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Muslim women celebrated Ram Navami by performing a grand aarti for Lord Ram. Led by the Muslim Mahila Foundation and Vishal Bharat Sansthan, the event saw women expressing joy over the passing of the Waqf Amendment Bill, viewing it as a blessing from Lord Ram. They believe the bill, alongside the Triple Talaq Bill, supports Muslim women and reinforces their shared cultural heritage. The women emphasized that Lord Ram is a symbol of Indian culture and their ancestor, highlighting their commitment to unity and communal harmony. They see the celebration as a message of peace and love, capable of silencing those spreading hatred.
