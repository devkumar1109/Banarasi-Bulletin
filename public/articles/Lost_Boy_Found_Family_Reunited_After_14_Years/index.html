Lost Boy Found, Family Reunited After 14 Years

2025-04-02T18:57:00+00:00

Neeraj Kannaujiya, who went missing from Mumbai at age 8, was reunited with his family in Varanasi after 14 years. He was found by railway police in Gudur, Andhra Pradesh, and taken in by the Swarna Bharat Trust. The Trust, led by Deepa Venkat, cared for Neeraj, providing him with education, skills training, and a loving home. He learned Telugu and excelled in his studies, eventually earning job opportunities. Deepa facilitated Neeraj's reunion with his family, who were overjoyed to see him after years of believing him lost.
