Uttar Pradesh: Fear and Fury Grip the State

2025-04-06T10:43:00+00:00

A 14-year-old girl was kidnapped and gang-raped in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh. An FIR has been registered against a youth and two unidentified persons. In Muzaffarnagar, a girl was abducted, gang-raped, and the accused recorded the act, threatening her with dire consequences if she disclosed it. Ahead of the Waqf Bill discussion in Lok Sabha, Delhi Police tightened security, and UP Police is also on high alert. UP DGP ordered tight security measures for Eid, emphasizing a well-structured zone and sector plan. Sambhal CO Anuj Chaudhary sparked controversy with his remark, "If you want to feed seviyan, you must eat gujiya," at a peace committee meeting. UP Police is on high alert for Holi and Friday prayers, conducting flag marches in sensitive areas.
