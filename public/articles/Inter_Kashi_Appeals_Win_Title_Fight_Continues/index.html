Inter Kashi Appeals Win: Title Fight Continues

2025-06-17T15:18:00+00:00

Inter Kashi won their first appeal at CAS against the AIFF regarding a three-point deduction, but haven't secured the I-League title. The AIFF had initially awarded the title to Churchill Brothers after overturning a decision that favored Inter Kashi in a match against Namdhari FC. CAS ruled in favor of Inter Kashi, directing the AIFF to pay them compensation and ordering Churchill Brothers and Namdhari FC to also contribute. Inter Kashi still has a second appeal pending at CAS, which could determine the final I-League champion.
