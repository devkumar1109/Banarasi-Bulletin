Varanasi Transformed: 458 Projects, Rs 40,536 Crores Invested

2025-03-27T19:00:00+00:00

Since 2017, the Central and Uttar Pradesh governments have jointly completed over 458 development projects in Varanasi worth Rs 40,536 crores. These projects encompass roads, bridges, drinking water, sewage, education, health, energy, tourism, urban development, and police welfare. Over Rs 22,000 crores were spent on road and bridge construction, including 102 completed projects. Railway, airport, and waterway projects worth over Rs 1,500 crores were also completed. The Smart City project saw 34 projects worth Rs 877.65 crores completed, including the Kashi Integrated Command Control Centre and the Varanasi Sports Complex. Over 60 sewerage and drinking water projects costing Rs 1,000 crores were completed. In the health sector, 43 projects worth Rs 1,870.36 crores were completed, including the Cancer Institute and Homi Bhabha Cancer Hospital.
