Love-Struck Killer Stops Wedding, Then Flees

2025-06-18T02:53:00+00:00

Mohd Saddam, 26, was arrested in Rampur, Uttar Pradesh for murdering Nihal, 25, a groom-to-be, the day before his wedding. Saddam was in love with the bride, Gulbasha, and killed Nihal to prevent the marriage. He lured Nihal out posing as the bride's cousin and strangled him with an accomplice, Mohd Farman. Saddam confessed to the crime, led police to Nihal's body and phone, and attempted to flee by firing on police, resulting in him being shot in the leg. An FIR has been registered against Saddam and Farman for murder and destruction of evidence.
