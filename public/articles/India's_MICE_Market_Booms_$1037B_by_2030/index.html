India's MICE Market Booms: $103.7B by 2030

2025-05-05T14:53:00+00:00

India's Meetings, Incentives, Conferences and Events (MICE) market is projected to reach $103.7 billion by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 13%. This growth is attributed to policy decisions, infrastructure modernization, and development of new MICE destinations like Varanasi and Kochi. India's G20 presidency has acted as a catalyst, highlighting the country's readiness to host international events. The meetings segment dominates the market with a 42.91% revenue share. The MICE sector benefits the Indian economy through business opportunities, knowledge sharing, training, and skill development. Both central and state governments are actively involved in promoting and facilitating MICE growth.
