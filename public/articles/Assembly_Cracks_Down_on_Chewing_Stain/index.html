Assembly Cracks Down on Chewing Stain

2025-03-10T07:27:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Assembly Speaker Satish Mahana banned paan masala and gutkha use in the Assembly after an MLA left a stain on the main hall floor. Violators, including MLAs and staff, will be fined Rs 1,000, with a second offense resulting in public shaming. CCTV cameras will monitor for violations, with the Speaker considering additional cameras to cover blind spots. Removed for security reasons, fixed dustbins may be reinstated to encourage cleanliness. Assembly staff expressed frustration over the difficulty of removing paan masala stains.
