BJP Leader Dies in Deoria Road Accident

2025-05-22T13:35:00+00:00

BJP leader Purnamasi Chauhan (65) died in a road accident in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh. Chauhan and his relative Kundan were on a motorcycle when they were hit by a speeding car. The driver fled the scene, leaving the car behind. Chauhan was declared dead at Maharshi Devraha Baba Medical College, while Kundan was referred to a medical college in Gorakhpur due to critical injuries. Chauhan held several political positions, including serving twice as the mandal general secretary and once as mandal president of Desahi Deoria. At the time of the accident, he was the sector in-charge of Desahi Mandal. Police are investigating the incident.
