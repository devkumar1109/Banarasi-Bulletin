IIT BHU Boosts Seats in High-Demand Programs

2025-06-08T08:20:00+00:00

IIT BHU has increased 111 seats in its most in-demand departments for students selected through JEE Advanced 2025. A total of 1,589 seats will be available for first-year students in 2025-26, the same as the previous year. The seat distribution has been revised, with 111 seats reduced from four departments (Ceramic Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering, Mining Engineering, and Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology) and reallocated to nine high-demand departments. The institute aims to align its academic programs with student aspirations and industry needs, providing more opportunities in preferred fields.
