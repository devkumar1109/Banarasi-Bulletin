Ram Temple Plot Foiled, Teen Arrested With Grenades

2025-03-04T19:39:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh ATS conducted searches in Lucknow, Azamgarh, Mau, and Ballia following the arrest of Abdul Rehman, a 19-year-old from Ayodhya, for allegedly plotting an attack on the Ram temple. Rehman was apprehended in Faridabad with two hand grenades. Investigations revealed his contact with radical elements linked to international terror outfits. The ATS visited homes of individuals with questionable credentials, verifying information regarding their communications, particularly emails. The agency clarified that these were routine exercises and not raids. Rehman, an e-rickshaw driver, was allegedly influenced by radical ideology and had become increasingly withdrawn in recent months. His frequent travels to various cities raised local suspicion.
