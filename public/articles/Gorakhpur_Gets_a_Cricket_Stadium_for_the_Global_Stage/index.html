Gorakhpur Gets a Cricket Stadium for the Global Stage

2025-05-19T13:19:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is constructing a fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur, scheduled to be completed in 18 months at a cost of Rs 236.40 crore. The stadium will have seven main pitches and four practice pitches, accommodating 30,000 spectators. Located on a 50-acre site at Tal Nadaur, the stadium will be developed using the Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) model to meet global standards set by organizations like the ICC. Accessibility will be improved with new roads connecting to the Gorakhpur-Varanasi Highway.
