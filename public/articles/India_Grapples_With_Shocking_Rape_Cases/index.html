India Grapples With Shocking Rape Cases

2025-03-17T01:17:00+00:00

In Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, a 14-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped at gunpoint and gang-raped. The incident occurred on March 14th, and an FIR has been registered against Suraj Soni and two unidentified individuals under sections of the Bhartiya Nyaya Sanhita and the POCSO Act. Separately, a British woman was allegedly raped and molested by two men at a Delhi hotel. Police have arrested two individuals, charging one with rape and the other with molestation. The accused befriended the woman on social media, and she had traveled from the UK to meet him.
