Time Loop Wedding: "Bhool Chuk Maaf" Sets New Release Date

2025-05-15T12:26:00+00:00

"Bhool Chuk Maaf", starring Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi, will release in theaters on May 23, 2025, after a series of release date changes and legal complications. The film, set in Varanasi, revolves around Ranjan, a man trapped in a time loop during his wedding preparations. Initially slated for a May 9 release, the film's producers announced a direct-to-OTT release, leading to a lawsuit by PVR INOX. The Bombay High Court ordered an 8-week theatrical exclusivity period, resulting in the film's theatrical release on May 23 and an OTT release on June 6. The film features a strong supporting cast including Raghubir Yadav, Sanjay Mishra, Zakir Hussain, and Seema Pahwa. The music is composed by Tanishk Bagchi with lyrics by Irshad Kamil, featuring a mix of original songs and re-enacted hits.
