Maha Kumbh Ends, Varanasi Prepares for Pilgrim Surge

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj concludes on Wednesday, Maha Shivratri, with a massive crowd expected for the final holy bath. Varanasi anticipates its largest-ever crowd on this occasion as pilgrims from Prayagraj will travel to the city to visit the Kashi Vishwanath temple. To manage the influx, Varanasi police have implemented a strict traffic management plan, including traffic diversions, barricades, and vehicle restrictions. Holding areas have been set up at railway stations to guide passengers to their trains and ensure smooth movement. The Varanasi Police Commissioner, Mohit Agarwal, emphasizes the security arrangements to prevent traffic congestion and ensure public safety.
