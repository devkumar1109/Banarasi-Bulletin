Fake Cop, Real Losses: Munfed Arrested in Varanasi

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

Munfed (26) from Mathura was arrested in Varanasi for duping government job seekers of lakhs of rupees. He posed as Deepak Kumar Ranawat, the public relations officer of the Varanasi police commissionerate, to lure victims. Munfed collected personal details of police officials from social media to create fake appointment letters. He confessed to defrauding multiple women, including Anita Yadav of Mainpuri, who lost Rs 16 lakh. Police recovered fake appointment letters, jewellery, a police sub-inspector's uniform, and Ranawat's nameplate from Munfed. Ranawat has been removed from his public relations post and attached to police lines.
