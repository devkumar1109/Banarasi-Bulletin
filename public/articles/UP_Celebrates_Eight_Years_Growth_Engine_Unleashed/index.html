UP Celebrates Eight Years: Growth Engine Unleashed

2025-03-24T20:51:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is celebrating its eight years in power with a three-day mega event starting Tuesday. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath will lead the celebrations from Gorakhpur, while Deputy CMs Brajesh Pathak and Keshav Prasad Maurya will be in Lucknow and Varanasi respectively. At least one minister, MLC, or board member will be present in each of the 75 districts. The theme of the event is 'UP: The growth engine of the government of India,' highlighting the state's achievements and linking them to the central government's works. The celebrations will include cultural programmes, exhibitions showcasing district achievements, and public addresses by government representatives. Special focus will be given to the CM Yuva Udyami Yojna, offering loans to youth for entrepreneurship.
