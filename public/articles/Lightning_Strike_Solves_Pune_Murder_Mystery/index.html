Lightning Strike Solves Pune Murder Mystery

2025-06-12T05:01:00+00:00

Pune Police solved a murder case using CCTV footage illuminated by lightning. The victim, a woman, was attacked with a sharp weapon on May 20, 2025, in Ambegaon Budruk. Police analyzed 320 CCTV cameras and 1,200 TVS rickshaw records, finally identifying the suspect, Shahrukh Shakeel Mansoor (27), from Uttar Pradesh. A key breakthrough came when CCTV footage, briefly illuminated by lightning, captured the victim in a black-hooded TVS rickshaw. This led police to Shahrukh's rickshaw, which was found at the crime scene. He was arrested after a 3,200 km investigation spanning from Pune to Uttar Pradesh.
