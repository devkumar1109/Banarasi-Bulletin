Lightning Strike Solves Pune Murder Mystery

2025-06-12T05:01:00+00:00

Pune Police solved a murder case using CCTV footage illuminated by lightning. The victim, an unidentified woman, was attacked on the night of May 20, 2025, on the service road between Shaheed Colonel Patil Petrol Pump and Mhasicha Gotha in Ambegaon Budruk. The footage showed the victim in a black-hooded TVS auto-rickshaw. Police analyzed records of 1,200 TVS rickshaws and identified the suspect's vehicle. Through further CCTV analysis and a 3,200 km journey to Uttar Pradesh, they arrested Shahrukh Shakeel Mansoor, a 27-year-old from Bijnor district.
