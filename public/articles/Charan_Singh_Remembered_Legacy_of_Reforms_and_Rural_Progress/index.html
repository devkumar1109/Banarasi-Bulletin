Charan Singh Remembered: Legacy of Reforms and Rural Progress

2025-05-29T12:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath and other leaders paid tribute to former Prime Minister Chaudhary Charan Singh on his death anniversary. CM Yogi lauded Charan Singh's contributions to land reforms, rural development, and transparency in governance in Uttar Pradesh. Vice-President Jagdeep Dhankhar also paid respects at Kisan Ghat in Delhi, remembering Charan Singh's lifelong dedication to farmers and urging the farming community to engage in agricultural trade. Charan Singh, a prominent figure in the Janata Party, was known for his land reforms in Uttar Pradesh and his prolific writings on social and economic issues.
