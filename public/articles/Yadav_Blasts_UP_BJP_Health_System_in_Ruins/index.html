Yadav Blasts UP BJP: Health System in Ruins

2025-05-13T13:51:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party (SP) President Akhilesh Yadav criticized the BJP government in Uttar Pradesh for ruining the state's health services. He accused the government of negligence in hospitals, lack of adequate medicines and treatment, and corruption in procurement. Yadav claimed that medical colleges lack essential facilities and that district hospitals are inadequate. He also pointed to incomplete medical college buildings and fire incidents in hospitals, highlighting a lack of safety standards. Yadav further alleged that the government has not provided sufficient budgets to medical colleges and that specialist doctors are in short supply.
