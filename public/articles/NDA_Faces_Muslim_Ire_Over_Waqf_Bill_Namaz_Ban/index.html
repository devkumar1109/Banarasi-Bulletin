NDA Faces Muslim Ire Over Waqf Bill, Namaz Ban

2025-04-01T15:50:00+00:00

The NDA is facing criticism from minority communities over two issues: the controversial Waqf (Amendment) Bill and a ban on namaz on roads and rooftops in Uttar Pradesh. The Muslim organisation Imarat-e-Shariah boycotted Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar's iftar, and the All India Muslim Personal Law Board called for a boycott of iftar parties in Andhra Pradesh hosted by Chief Minister N. Chandrababu Naidu. While some BJP-ruled states like Rajasthan allowed peaceful Eid-ul-Fitr prayers, Uttar Pradesh administrations banned Alvida namaz in several districts, sparking concern about restrictions on religious practices.
