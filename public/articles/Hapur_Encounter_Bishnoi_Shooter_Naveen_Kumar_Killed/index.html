Hapur Encounter: Bishnoi Shooter Naveen Kumar Killed

2025-05-29T02:42:00+00:00

A sharpshooter named Naveen Kumar, linked to the Lawrence Bishnoi gang, was killed in a police encounter in Hapur, Uttar Pradesh. The encounter occurred Wednesday night involving a joint team of the UP STF's Noida unit and Delhi Police's Special Cell. Kumar sustained fatal injuries during the exchange of fire and was pronounced dead at a local hospital. He was wanted in several serious cases, including murder, and was booked under the MCOCA. Police records reveal 20 criminal cases registered against Kumar in Delhi and Uttar Pradesh.
