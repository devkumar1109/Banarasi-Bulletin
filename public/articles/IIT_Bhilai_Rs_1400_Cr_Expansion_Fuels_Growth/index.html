IIT Bhilai: Rs 1,400 Cr Expansion Fuels Growth

2025-05-16T09:20:00+00:00

IIT Bhilai is undergoing a major expansion with a Rs 1,400 crore investment from the Central Government. This will increase the institute's student capacity from 1,250 to 2,750, graduating over 2,700 engineers annually. The expansion includes two new engineering departments, one science department, a large advanced laboratory, three hostels, a visitor hostel, staff residential quarters, and a sports complex. IIT Bhilai will also house Chhattisgarh's first dedicated research park, spanning 15,000 square meters. This initiative aims to boost technical education, regional growth, foster innovation, and support local startups.
