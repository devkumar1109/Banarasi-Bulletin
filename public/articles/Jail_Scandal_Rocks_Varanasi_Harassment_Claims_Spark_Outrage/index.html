Jail Scandal Rocks Varanasi: Harassment Claims Spark Outrage

2025-03-18T14:05:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is investigating harassment allegations against Varanasi Jail Superintendent Umesh Singh by a woman deputy jailer, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of inappropriate remarks and personal intrusions. She was subsequently transferred to a different district. The investigation, led by Amita Dubey, will include two women officers from Lucknow. UP Congress chief Ajay Rai criticized the government for transferring Kanojia instead of taking action against Singh.
