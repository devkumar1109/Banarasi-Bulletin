Maha Kumbh 2025: Spiritual Surge in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-06T10:24:00+00:00

Maha Kumbh 2025 significantly boosted spiritual tourism in Uttar Pradesh, attracting 65 crore devotees and tourists to religious sites like Ayodhya, Kashi, and Prayagraj. The event saw record attendance and cleanliness, with Prayagraj alone hosting 66.30 crore visitors in 45 days. To enhance connectivity, the government developed five spiritual corridors linking prominent religious sites across the state. These corridors connect Prayagraj to Mirzapur, Kashi, Ayodhya, Gorakhpur, Lucknow, Naimisharanya, Lalapur, Rajapur, Chitrakoot, Mathura-Vrindavan, and Bundelkhand Expressway via Agra. The UP government invested Rs 7,300 crore in infrastructure projects, including roads, flyovers, underpasses, and corridors. Facial recognition technology and 3,000 CCTV cameras were used to track visitors and ensure security. The event is estimated to have generated a growth of Rs 3.5 lakh crore for the state's economy.
