Jindal IVF Expands, Bringing Hope to Northern India

2025-03-27T11:30:00+00:00

Jindal IVF is expanding its reach in Himachal Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh by establishing partner centres in Shimla, Solan, and Saharanpur. This move aims to improve accessibility to fertility treatments, particularly IVF, in Northern India. Jindal IVF's flagship centre in Chandigarh remains the largest in the region, employing 140 professionals including doctors specializing in reproductive health. The institution emphasizes its commitment to providing world-class, compassionate, and cutting-edge ART services.
