Yadav Slams 90-Hour Workweek, Prioritizes Work-Life Balance

2025-03-03T16:04:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party (SP) chief Akhilesh Yadav criticized suggestions for a 90-hour workweek, arguing that quality of work is more important than quantity. He stated that economic growth benefits only a few, and a healthy work-life balance fosters creativity and productivity. Yadav also accused the current Uttar Pradesh government of rampant corruption, claiming it hinders economic progress. Amitabh Kant, India's G20 Sherpa and former Niti Aayog CEO, had previously advocated for longer work hours, citing the work ethics of Japan, South Korea, and China as examples for India's economic growth aspirations.
