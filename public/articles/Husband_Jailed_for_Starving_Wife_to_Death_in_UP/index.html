Husband Jailed for Starving Wife to Death in UP

2025-05-18T09:02:00+00:00

A court in Uttar Pradesh sentenced Manoj, a resident of Kareli village, to life imprisonment for starving his wife Mamta to death. Manoj locked Mamta in a room without food or water for a week following a quarrel. Additional District Government Advocate (ADGC) Sunil Pandey confirmed the sentencing.
