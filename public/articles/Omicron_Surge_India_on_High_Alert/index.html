Omicron Surge: India on High Alert

2025-05-29T06:44:00+00:00

India is experiencing a surge in COVID-19 cases, driven primarily by Omicron sub-lineages. Kerala leads with 519 active cases and three deaths, prompting mandatory testing and close monitoring by health authorities. Maharashtra has 210 active cases, including one death, while Delhi reports 104 active cases with no casualties. Karnataka has 47 active cases, and Gujarat has 83 active cases, with new Omicron sub-variants detected. Health officials in these states are urging caution, particularly in densely populated areas, and assuring the public that healthcare facilities are prepared to handle any potential increase in cases.
