MP vs Punjab: Hockey Championship Showdown

2025-04-13T14:40:00+00:00

Hockey Madhya Pradesh and Hockey Punjab will face off in the finals of the 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025. Hockey Madhya Pradesh defeated Manipur Hockey 5-3 in the semi-finals, with Yousuf Affan and Ali Ahmad scoring braces. Hockey Punjab edged out Uttar Pradesh Hockey 4-3, with Jasjit Singh Kular scoring two goals. The quarter-finals saw Hockey Madhya Pradesh defeat Hockey Maharashtra in a shootout, Manipur Hockey win against Hockey Unit of Tamil Nadu in a shootout, Hockey Punjab triumph over Hockey Haryana 3-2, and Uttar Pradesh Hockey beat Hockey Karnataka 3-1.
