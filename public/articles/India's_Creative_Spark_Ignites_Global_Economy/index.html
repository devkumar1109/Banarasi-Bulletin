India's Creative Spark Ignites Global Economy

2025-03-16T11:02:00+00:00

India's participation in the global creative economy is growing, contributing significantly to world GDP. Creative cities integrate cultural industries like music, art, and design into urban policies, promoting innovation, diversity, and sustainable development. Creative industries, encompassing heritage, arts, media, and functional creations, contribute over $2.25 trillion to the global economy and create nearly 30 million jobs. India's creative economy is valued at over $200 billion, generating millions of jobs across traditional arts, media, and digital platforms. It has a global influence through Bollywood, music, handicrafts, and IT-based creative industries. The UNESCO Creative Cities Network (UCCN), established in 2004, fosters cooperation among cities prioritizing creativity for sustainable development. Nearly 350 cities from 90 countries are part of the network.
