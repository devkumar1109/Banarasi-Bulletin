Holi Harmony: UP Celebrates Safely

2025-03-14T14:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh celebrated Holi peacefully under tight security. Authorities deployed drones and CCTV for surveillance to prevent unrest, particularly during Friday prayers. Lucknow hosted a Bollywood-themed celebration, while Shahjahanpur held a traditional procession with robust security. Mathura and Vrindavan observed religious gatherings with a focus on unity. Messages of communal harmony were emphasized at the Deva Sharif Dargah. Social media was also closely monitored for any potential issues.
