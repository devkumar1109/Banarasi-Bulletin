Congress Aide Arrested in Varanasi Crime Spree

2025-06-06T08:39:00+00:00

Amit Pathak, personal assistant to Uttar Pradesh Congress Committee President Ajay Rai, was arrested in Varanasi for multiple robberies, thefts, and molestation cases. The arrests were made by Varanasi Police who registered FIR number 102/2025 against Pathak and his accomplice. Charges include extortion, night burglary, robbery, insult, criminal intimidation, and molestation. This incident raises concerns about the involvement of political associates in criminal activities. Varanasi Police are investigating the matter thoroughly. The arrests highlight the commitment to enforcing the law, regardless of political connections.
