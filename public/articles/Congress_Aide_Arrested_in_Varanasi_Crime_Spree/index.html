Congress Aide Arrested in Varanasi Crime Spree

2025-06-06T08:39:00+00:00

Amit Pathak, personal assistant to Uttar Pradesh Congress Committee State President Ajay Rai, has been arrested in Varanasi for multiple robberies, thefts, molestation, and extortion. Police registered a case against Pathak and his accomplice under FIR number 102/2025, charging them with various offenses under the Indian Penal Code. The arrests have raised concerns about the involvement of political figures' associates in criminal activities. Varanasi police are investigating the case further. This incident highlights the commitment to enforcing the law equally on all individuals, regardless of their political affiliations.
