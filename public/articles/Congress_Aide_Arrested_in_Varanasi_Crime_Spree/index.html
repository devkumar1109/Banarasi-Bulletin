Congress Aide Arrested in Varanasi Crime Spree

2025-06-06T08:39:00+00:00

Amit Pathak, personal assistant to Uttar Pradesh Congress Committee President Ajay Rai, was arrested in Varanasi for multiple robberies, thefts, and molestation cases. Pathak was apprehended alongside his accomplice. A case has been registered against them under FIR number 102/2025, including charges of extortion, burglary, robbery, insult, criminal intimidation, and molestation. This arrest raises concerns about the involvement of political associates in criminal activities. The Varanasi police are investigating the matter thoroughly. Authorities have assured strict action against the accused, emphasizing zero tolerance towards crime.
