Varanasi Prepares for Scorching Heatwave

2025-04-25T17:49:00+00:00

In response to the predicted rise in temperatures, Varanasi officials have issued directives to strengthen heatwave response measures. The health department has been instructed to reserve beds for heatwave patients, ensure cool rooms, maintain ORS and fluid supplies, and stock essential medicines. Municipal authorities are tasked with providing clean drinking water, arranging water tankers, identifying shaded areas for water stalls, utilizing LED screens for safety messages, and implementing road water sprinkling. The animal husbandry department must ensure adequate water provisions for livestock, implement sprinkler systems, and educate villagers about animal safety. The fire department has been directed to maintain 24/7 sub-centres, conduct fire audits, and train village representatives in fire prevention.
