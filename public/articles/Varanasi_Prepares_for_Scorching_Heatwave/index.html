Varanasi Prepares for Scorching Heatwave

2025-04-25T17:49:00+00:00

In Varanasi, officials have issued directives to strengthen heatwave response measures due to the predicted rise in temperatures. The health department is tasked with reserving beds for heatwave patients, ensuring cool rooms, maintaining ORS and fluid supplies, and conducting awareness campaigns. Municipal authorities must provide clean drinking water, coordinate water tanker arrangements, identify shaded areas for water stalls, and utilize public address systems for safety messages. The animal husbandry department is responsible for livestock management awareness, ensuring adequate water provisions, implementing sprinkler systems, and educating villagers about animal safety. The fire department must maintain 24/7 operational sub-centres, conduct fire audits, and train village panchayat representatives in fire prevention.
