UP Gears Up: 8 Years of Progress on Display

2025-03-20T12:03:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will celebrate eight years of its 'Seva, Suraksha, and Sushasan' policy with a three-day exhibition event from March 25 to 27. The events, themed 'UP: Growth Engine of Bharat,' will showcase the achievements of the state and central governments, focusing on welfare schemes, infrastructure projects, new industrial units, and flagship initiatives like expressways, hospitals, medical colleges, and universities. The exhibition will primarily feature photographs highlighting these successes. Chief Secretary Manoj Kumar Singh held a video conference with officials to ensure smooth execution of the events.
