Honeymoon Murder: Pre-Planned, Gruesome Attack

2025-06-17T11:44:00+00:00

Meghalaya Police successfully reconstructed the crime scene in the murder of Raja Raghuvanshi, a newlywed on his honeymoon. The reconstruction, involving the accused wife Sonam Raghuvanshi and three others, revealed a pre-planned attack. Raja was hit with three blows, first by Vishal, then Anand, and finally Akash. Police confirmed Sonam's confession and her role in destroying Raja's phone. The accused discarded the body in a gorge near Sohra. One machete used in the murder is still missing and being recovered by the SDRF. The police arrested Sonam and four others: Akash Rajput, Vishal Singh Chauhan, Raj Singh Kushwaha, and Anand.
