Honeymoon Horror: Bride Murdered by Groom in India

2025-05-18T07:10:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, has been arrested for murdering his 26-year-old third wife, Aarti Pal, just one week after their wedding. The couple, who married on May 9th, reportedly started clashing immediately after the ceremony. On Thursday night, Raju Pal brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death. Neighbors alerted the police who took Aarti to the Narpatpur Community Health Centre, where she was pronounced dead. Police have arrested Raju and sent Aarti's body for post-mortem.
