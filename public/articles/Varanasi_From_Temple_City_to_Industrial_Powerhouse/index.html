Varanasi: From Temple City to Industrial Powerhouse

2025-05-26T17:07:00+00:00

Under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's leadership, Varanasi is transforming into a major industrial hub in Uttar Pradesh's Purvanchal region. Improvements in law and order, infrastructure, and initiatives like the Global Investors Summit have created a favorable business environment. In Varanasi, 48 industrial projects worth Rs 1,180.95 crore are already operational, generating 3,472 jobs. An additional 48 projects worth Rs 5,702.18 crore are set to launch soon, creating 12,110 jobs. Notable companies like Saaswad Heat Transfer and Engineering Private Limited, Hilton Hotels Group's Double Tree franchise, and Amazon's contracted warehouse are already operating in the city. Banaras Beads is partnering with Tata's Indian Hotels Company Limited to build the Ginger Budget Hotel.
