UP CM: COVID Threat Minimal, Stay Vigilant

2025-05-21T15:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath assures the public that COVID-19 currently poses no immediate threat despite rising global concerns over the JN.1 subvariant. He instructed health units to remain vigilant and prepared, ensuring resources like ICUs and oxygen plants are operational. Adityanath emphasized proactive measures against seasonal diseases like dengue and malaria, calling for collaboration between health departments and local administrations. This comes as Maharashtra reported two COVID-related deaths in Mumbai, highlighting the urgency of preparedness.
