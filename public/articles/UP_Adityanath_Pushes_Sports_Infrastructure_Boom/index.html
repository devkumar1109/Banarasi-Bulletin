UP: Adityanath Pushes Sports Infrastructure Boom

2025-05-22T18:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed officials to prioritize and ensure the timely completion of all sports infrastructure projects. He emphasized the development of sports colleges in every state division, aiming to establish them as Centres of Excellence affiliated with the Sports University. Adityanath stressed the importance of regular reviews for these projects and highlighted the need for disaster management training for Prantiya Rakshak Dal personnel. He envisions a comprehensive system to provide top-tier facilities for athletes and enhance sports development in the state.
