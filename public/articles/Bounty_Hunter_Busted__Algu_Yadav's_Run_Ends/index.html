Bounty Hunter Busted: Algu Yadav's Run Ends

2025-02-25T18:11:00+00:00

A criminal carrying a Rs 25,000 bounty was arrested in Sonbhadra district following an encounter with police. The suspect, identified as Vishal alias Algu Yadav, was involved in several loot cases. He sustained a bullet injury in the leg during the encounter and was admitted to the district hospital. Police recovered a country-made revolver and a motorcycle from him.
