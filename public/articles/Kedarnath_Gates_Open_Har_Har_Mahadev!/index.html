Kedarnath Gates Open: Har Har Mahadev!

2025-05-02T02:21:00+00:00

The portals of Kedarnath Dham, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, were opened for devotees on May 2, 2025, amid chants of "Har Har Mahadev." Uttarakhand Chief Minister Pushkar Singh Dhami was present at the ceremonial opening, which included a puja performed by Dhami and devotional tunes played by the Indian Army’s Garhwal Rifles band. Flower petals were showered on devotees from choppers. Earlier, the portals of Yamunotri and Gangotri temples were opened on Akshay Tritiya, with Gangotri's gates opening at 10:30 am and Yamunotri's at 11:55 am. Badrinath Dham will open on May 4 at 6 am. CM Dhami wished devotees a safe and fulfilling journey.
