Varanasi Development: CM Adityanath Addresses Ashram Overcrowding

2025-05-13T12:45:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed development projects in Varanasi, focusing on transportation, tourism, and construction efficiency. He met representatives from Apna Ghar Ashram, a shelter for the homeless, who requested additional space due to overcrowding. The ashram, originally designed for 400 residents, currently houses 600 individuals, forcing some to sleep outdoors. Adityanath assured support and suggested expanding the ashram's facilities to other districts in Uttar Pradesh.
