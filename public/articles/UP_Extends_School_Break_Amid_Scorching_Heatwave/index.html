UP Extends School Break Amid Scorching Heatwave

2025-06-14T17:14:00+00:00

Due to the severe heatwave in Uttar Pradesh, the Uttar Pradesh Basic Education Council extended school holidays for classes up to 8 until June 30th. Schools were originally scheduled to reopen on June 16th. Teachers and staff are still required to be present at schools. Academic activities will resume on July 1st. UP Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak urged citizens to take precautions during the heatwave, noting that hospitals are on high alert with beds reserved for heat-related emergencies. The heatwave has particularly affected South Uttar Pradesh, but gradual respite is expected from June 14th. Ayodhya District Magistrate Nikhil Tikaram Funde highlighted proactive steps taken to mitigate the heatwave's impact, including providing shade and water for residents and animals, and reserving special beds for heatwave patients in hospitals.
