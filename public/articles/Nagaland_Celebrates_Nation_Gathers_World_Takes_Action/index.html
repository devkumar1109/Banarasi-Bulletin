Nagaland Celebrates, Nation Gathers, World Takes Action

2025-06-14T20:53:00+00:00

In Nagaland, World Blood Donor Day and Domestic Workers’ Day were observed. Congress criticized BJP for abstaining from voting on a Gaza resolution and alleged BJP of running a "fake news factory". Meanwhile, ISRO successfully conducted a rocket launch test with a payload. Nationally, Mahesh Kumar topped the NEET-UG exam. In Manipur, over 300 rifles and war-like stores were recovered. In Assam, 38 people were arrested in Dhubri amid communal tensions. Internationally, Iran deemed upcoming nuclear talks with the US "meaningless". South Korea's President ordered measures to curb anti-Pyongyang leafleting. 170 nations pledged to expand marine protected areas and combat pollution.
