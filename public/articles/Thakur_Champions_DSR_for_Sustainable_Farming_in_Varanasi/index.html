Thakur Champions DSR for Sustainable Farming in Varanasi

2025-06-13T04:21:00+00:00

Union Minister Ramnath Thakur visited Varanasi to promote sustainable farming practices and DSR technology as part of the Viksit Krishi Sankalp Abhiyan. At Paniyara village, he witnessed a field demonstration of DSR, highlighting its benefits in water conservation, emission reduction, and improved efficiency. The International Rice Research Institute – South Asia Regional Centre (ISARC) showcased DSR technology and its ability to achieve uniform row and plant spacing for easier weed control. ISARC, in collaboration with the Uttar Pradesh government, is implementing 200 hectares of DSR cluster demonstrations across eight districts in the Varanasi and Gorakhpur divisions. Minister Thakur praised farmers for adopting DSR and acknowledged the role of science-backed innovations in climate-resilient farming.
