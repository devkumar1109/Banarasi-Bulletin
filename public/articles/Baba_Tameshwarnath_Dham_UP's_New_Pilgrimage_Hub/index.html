Baba Tameshwarnath Dham: UP's New Pilgrimage Hub

2025-05-26T13:29:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced the development of Baba Tameshwarnath Dham in Sant Kabir Nagar on the lines of Ayodhya and Kashi. The project, worth Rs 1,515 crore, includes a dedicated corridor for devotees and will focus on rejuvenating the site into a grand pilgrimage destination. Adityanath emphasized that the development will not displace residents, and a rehabilitation plan will be implemented to safeguard local traders and Gosains. He cited successful models like the Shri Ram Temple in Ayodhya and Kashi Vishwanath Dham in Varanasi, highlighting the potential for Baba Tameshwarnath Dham to accommodate a large number of devotees. The CM also inaugurated 528 development projects across various sectors in the district, emphasizing their contribution to enhancing the quality of life and providing a renewed identity to Sant Kabir Nagar.
