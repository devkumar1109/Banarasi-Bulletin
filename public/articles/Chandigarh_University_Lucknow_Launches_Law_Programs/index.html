Chandigarh University Lucknow Launches Law Programs

2025-05-26T08:47:00+00:00

The Bar Council of India has granted approval to Chandigarh University Lucknow to offer LL.B. and five-year integrated law programs starting in the 2025 session. The university will offer 120 seats for LL.B. and 240 seats for integrated programs. The School of Legal Studies will offer BA LL.B (Hons), BBA LL.B (Hons), and three-year LL.B programs, blending legal principles with technology and global perspectives. The curriculum covers areas like criminal, civil, taxation, corporate, international arbitration, cyber, IT, and e-commerce laws. Dr. Abhishek Kumar Singh, an experienced legal academic, has joined as the academic head of the School of Legal Studies. He emphasizes the evolving legal profession and the importance of a future-ready legal education.
