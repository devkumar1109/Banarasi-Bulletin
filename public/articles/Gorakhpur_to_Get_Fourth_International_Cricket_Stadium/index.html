Gorakhpur to Get Fourth International Cricket Stadium

2025-05-19T13:19:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is constructing its fourth international cricket stadium in Gorakhpur. Scheduled for completion in 18 months, the Rs 236.40 crore facility will have seven main pitches and four practice pitches, accommodating approximately 30,000 spectators. Located on a 50-acre site at Tal Nadaur, the stadium is being built using the Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) model to ensure global standards as per the ICC. The project includes accessibility improvements like new roads linking to the Gorakhpur-Varanasi Highway.
