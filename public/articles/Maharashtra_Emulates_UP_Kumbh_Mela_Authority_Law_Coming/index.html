Maharashtra Emulates UP: Kumbh Mela Authority Law Coming

2025-03-23T18:00:00+00:00

The Maharashtra government plans to establish a Kumbh Mela Authority Law similar to Uttar Pradesh's. This move aims to streamline the organization and management of the upcoming Nashik Kumbh Mela. The new law will likely grant the authority greater control over various aspects of the event, including infrastructure development, security, and sanitation.
