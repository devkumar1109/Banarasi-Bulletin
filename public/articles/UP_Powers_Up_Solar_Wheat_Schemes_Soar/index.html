UP Powers Up: Solar, Wheat Schemes Soar

2025-04-11T03:40:00+00:00

Union Minister Pralhad Joshi reviewed solar and wheat procurement schemes in Uttar Pradesh. The state reaffirmed its goal of achieving 22 GW solar energy capacity. Joshi praised Uttar Pradesh's progress in implementing PM-KUSUM and PM Surya Ghar schemes, highlighting their benefits for farmers. He visited a solar pump project under PM-KUSUM C-1, showcasing its successful implementation and electricity generation. Joshi also inspected a wheat procurement center, observing the efficient operations through the e-POP machine. Farmers reported improved weighing accuracy and faster payments through e-POP. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath expressed gratitude for the central government's support in implementing these schemes.
