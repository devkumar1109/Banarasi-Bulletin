Mau's HIV Crisis: Unprotected Sex Fuels Surge

2025-03-12T15:09:00+00:00

Mau district in Uttar Pradesh is experiencing a surge in HIV cases, with 2,394 confirmed cases reported, including 85 children. Officials cite unprotected sex as a key contributing factor. The breakdown of cases reveals 1,147 men, 1,151 women, and 85 children (83 boys and 2 girls) infected with HIV. Additionally, 11 transgender individuals have also tested positive. Despite awareness campaigns launched by the health department and district administration, the number of HIV cases continues to rise. HIV/AIDS remains a significant public health concern due to the lack of a definitive cure.
