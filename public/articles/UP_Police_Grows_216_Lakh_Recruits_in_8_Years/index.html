UP Police Grows: 2.16 Lakh Recruits in 8 Years

2025-06-18T12:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath held a 'Janta Darshan' program at Gorakhnath temple in Gorakhpur, addressing public grievances. The UP government has recruited 2.16 lakh police personnel in the last eight years, aiming to transform the UP Police into a 'modern police' force. Union Home Minister Amit Shah distributed appointment letters to 60,244 newly recruited civil police constables in Lucknow. The Uttar Pradesh Police Constable Recruitment Exam 2024, held across 67 districts, aimed to fill over 60,000 vacancies.
