Sankat Mochan: Night Four Brims with Musical Brilliance

2025-04-20T18:50:00+00:00

The fourth night of the six-day Sankat Mochan Music Festival in Varanasi featured a variety of classical music and dance performances. Kathak dancer B Anuradha Singh opened the evening, followed by a sitar and violin jugalbandi by Pandit Sahitya Kumar Nahar and Pandit Santosh Kumar Nahar. Ustad Wasifuddin Dagar performed a vocal recital, and Sneha Shankar, known from Indian Idol, sang bhajans, thumris, and film songs. Vocalists Prabhakar Kashyap and Diwakar Kashyap, and Kankana Banerjee also performed, showcasing various musical styles and ragas.
