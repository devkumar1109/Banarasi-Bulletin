Ganga Darpan: Where River Meets Education

2025-05-20T21:30:00+00:00

The Ganga Darpan Interpretation Centre in Varanasi, developed by the Wildlife Institute of India, is promoting biodiversity education and conservation. The centre features a 3D model of the Ganga River showcasing its diverse wildlife, including dolphins, crocodiles, and turtles. With over 62 species of birds on display, the centre has attracted over 20,000 visitors through workshops, educational sessions, and programs focused on river conservation and civic responsibility. The centre highlights the Ganga's historical and cultural significance, aiming to shift perceptions from a polluted river to one appreciated for its natural beauty.
