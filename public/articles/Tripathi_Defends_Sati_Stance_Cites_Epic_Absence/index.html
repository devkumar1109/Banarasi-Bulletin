Tripathi Defends Sati Stance, Cites Epic Absence

2025-03-06T10:29:00+00:00

Amish Tripathi, author, defended his stance on Sati, arguing that there are no instances of Sati depicted in Indian epics. He made this statement in response to criticism he received for his views on the practice. Tripathi believes his interpretation of historical and literary texts supports his position.
