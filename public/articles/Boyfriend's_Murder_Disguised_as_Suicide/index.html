Boyfriend's Murder Disguised as Suicide

2025-04-02T17:35:00+00:00

A 20-year-old woman, Aishwarya Marrivanivalasa, was murdered by her boyfriend P Rambabu (27) in Visakhapatnam. Rambabu strangled Aishwarya after she demanded marriage. They attempted to disguise the murder as suicide by hanging her body from a tree in Salur mandal, but police discovered strangulation marks and ruled it a murder. Rambabu has been arrested and the case will be transferred to Arilova police station in Vizag for further investigation. Two of Rambabu's friends, who helped dispose of the body, will also be arrested and charged with destroying evidence.
