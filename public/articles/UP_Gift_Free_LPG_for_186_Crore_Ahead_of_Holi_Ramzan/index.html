UP Gift: Free LPG for 1.86 Crore Ahead of Holi, Ramzan

2025-03-12T11:41:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced free LPG cylinder refills for 1.86 crore beneficiaries of the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana during Holi and Ramzan. The state government will allocate Rs 1,890 crore for this initiative. Adityanath highlighted the scheme's impact in providing clean cooking energy and alleviating challenges faced by families before its implementation. He also emphasized ongoing welfare programs for women, including financial aid and housing. He praised Uttar Pradesh's economic growth under Prime Minister Modi's leadership, making it the nation's second-largest economy.
