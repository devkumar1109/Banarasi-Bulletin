Varanasi Honors Ambedkar: Legacy Lives On

2025-04-14T18:49:00+00:00

Varanasi celebrated Ambedkar's birth anniversary with various events. A program at the Collectorate, chaired by District Magistrate S Rajalingam, highlighted Ambedkar's role in drafting the Indian Constitution. Minister Ravindra Jaiswal paid tribute at Ambedkar Park, emphasizing the importance of following his ideals. The Varanasi Development Authority also organized a tribute ceremony. A two-day national seminar at BHU concluded with a blood donation camp and a seminar on Ambedkar's contributions to justice, brotherhood, equality, economics, and women's empowerment.
