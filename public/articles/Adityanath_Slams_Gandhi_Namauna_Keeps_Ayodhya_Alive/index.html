Adityanath Slams Gandhi: "Namauna" Keeps Ayodhya Alive

2025-03-26T08:13:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized Rahul Gandhi, calling him "namuna" for allegedly keeping the Ayodhya controversy alive. Adityanath termed Gandhi's nationwide march a "Bharat Todo Abhiyan" and accused him of working against India's interests. He questioned the Congress party's work over the past ten decades, stating that they should explain what they did during that time. Adityanath highlighted initiatives like the Ram Mandir construction, triple talaq abolition, and Kumbh Mela promotion as examples of progress under Prime Minister Modi's leadership.
