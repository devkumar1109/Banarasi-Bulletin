Krishna Citadel, Banke Bihari Defy Closure Call

2025-03-14T00:32:00+00:00

The article reports on the Krishna Citadel and Banke Bihari Temple defying a boycott call. Despite pressure from Hindutva groups, the temple stands firm against demands for its closure. The temple authorities remain open to the public, offering prayers and rituals as usual. This defiance highlights the ongoing tensions between religious groups and underscores the importance of religious freedom. No further details are provided regarding the reasons behind the boycott call or the specific demands made by the Hindutva groups.
