Honeymoon Slay: Wife Arrested in Husband's Murder

2025-06-09T14:45:00+00:00

Sonam Raghuvanshi has been arrested for allegedly orchestrating her husband Raja Raghuvanshi's murder during their honeymoon in Meghalaya. The Meghalaya Police arrested three contract killers and tracked Sonam down after a 17-day manhunt across multiple states. Police believe Sonam, with the help of her lover and two associates, planned the murder in advance. Sonam made a phone call to her family, leading to her arrest at a roadside dhaba in Uttar Pradesh. Raja's family is demanding a CBI probe into the case.
