ED Seizes Assets in UP's NRHM Healthcare Scam

2025-03-11T08:57:00+00:00

The Enforcement Directorate (ED) has attached assets worth Rs. 89.84 lakh in connection with the NRHM scam in Uttar Pradesh. The scam involves the misuse of funds allocated for improving healthcare services in rural areas. The CBI alleges large-scale irregularities and embezzlement of crores of rupees by government officials, politicians, and contractors. The scam came to light after the murders of two Chief Medical Officers (CMOs) in 2010 and 2011. The deaths of the CMOs and the subsequent arrest and mysterious death of a Deputy CMO in a Lucknow jail sparked public outrage and investigations.
