Hoax Bomb Threat Grounds IndiGo Flight in Varanasi

2025-04-27T13:23:00+00:00

A Canadian national was detained at Varanasi airport after making a hoax bomb threat on an IndiGo flight bound for Bengaluru on Saturday night. The passenger, who has not been identified, claimed to be carrying a bomb, prompting the aircraft to be moved to an isolation bay for a thorough inspection. No explosive material was found. The IndiGo crew alerted the Air Traffic Control (ATC), and the flight was grounded following standard security protocols. After clearance from security agencies, the flight departed for Bengaluru on Sunday morning. Police are questioning the detained passenger, and a detailed investigation is underway.
