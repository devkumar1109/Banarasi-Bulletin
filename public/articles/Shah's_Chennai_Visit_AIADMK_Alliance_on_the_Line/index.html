Shah's Chennai Visit: AIADMK Alliance on the Line

2025-04-11T01:56:00+00:00

Union Home Minister Amit Shah's visit to Chennai on Friday is crucial as the BJP aims to revive its alliance with the AIADMK ahead of the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections. The AIADMK has reportedly placed conditions for the alliance, including choosing its own chief ministerial candidate and controlling alliance decisions. Shah's meeting with state BJP functionaries and RSS ideologue S Gurumurthy will address these issues. Meanwhile, developments in BJP ally PMK saw founder S Ramadoss replacing his son Anbumani Ramadoss as president. The BJP faces challenges in expanding its footprint in South India, with the exception of Karnataka and Telangana.
