IIT BHU Spinoff P3C Seeks Perovskite Solar Breakthrough

2025-03-14T07:16:00+00:00

Indian startup P3C Technology and Solutions, a spinoff of IIT-BHU Varanasi, is testing perovskite solar modules with 15% efficiency. The largest modules tested measure 30 cm2. P3C is conducting durability and performance tests at India's National Institute of Solar Energy (NISE) and plans to send samples to the U.S. Department of Energy's National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) for validation. The company aims to initially produce these modules at a pilot 60 MW manufacturing facility.
