UP's Green Expressway: Faster Trips, Greener Growth

2025-04-03T06:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is constructing a 380-km Ghaziabad-Kanpur Expressway, aiming to reduce travel time between the cities from eight hours to five and a half hours. The expressway, developed under the Green Highway Policy, will connect nine districts: Ghaziabad, Hapur, Bulandshahr, Aligarh, Kasganj, Farrukhabad, Kannauj, Unnao, and Kanpur. The project is expected to boost economic growth, improve connectivity, create jobs, and encourage tourism. The expressway will initially be a four-lane highway, expandable to six lanes in the future. Planned completion is 2026, with eco-friendly initiatives like tree plantations incorporated.
