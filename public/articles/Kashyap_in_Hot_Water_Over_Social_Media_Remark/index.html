Kashyap in Hot Water Over Social Media Remark

2025-04-22T06:57:00+00:00

Anurag Kashyap was accused of hurting the sentiments of the Brahmin community with a remark shared on his social media. A case was registered against him in Varanasi's Bhelupur police station under Section 299 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita and Section 67 of the IT Act. Complainant Govind Chaturvedi alleged that the statement was divisive and intended to insult Sanatan Dharma.
