IIT BHU '85 Reunion: Sparks of Innovation Ignite

2025-03-04T10:01:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) Varanasi recently hosted a 40th reunion for its Electrical Engineering batch of 1985. Organized by the Student Alumni Interaction Cell (SAIC), the event saw alumni, faculty, and current students engage in nostalgic activities and discussions. Highlights included a meeting with the IIT (BHU) Director Prof. Amit Patra and Dean Prof. Hiralal Pramanik, a session with current faculty, a tour of classrooms and labs, a student-alumni interaction, and a cycle ride to Visvesvaraya Hostel. The reunion fostered a sense of community and provided current students with valuable insights from accomplished alumni. Prof. Patra emphasized the success of alumni as a testament to the institute's strong foundation and the enduring bonds between the alumni and their alma mater.
