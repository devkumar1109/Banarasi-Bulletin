Shiva-Inspired Stadium Rises in Varanasi

2025-03-12T16:39:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected the construction progress of Varanasi's international cricket stadium, inspired by Lord Shiva. This will be the state's third such venue after Kanpur and Lucknow. The stadium's design incorporates elements inspired by Lord Shiva, including crescent-shaped roofs, trident-shaped floodlights, and ghat-inspired seating. The project received a significant financial investment with the Uttar Pradesh government allocating Rs 121 crore for land acquisition and the BCCI contributing Rs 330 crore for construction. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone in 2023. Upon completion, the stadium will accommodate over 30,000 spectators and is expected to host international and domestic matches.
