Yoga Guru Baba Sivanand Passes at 128

2025-05-04T06:59:00+00:00

Baba Sivanand, a Padma Shri awardee renowned for his contributions to yoga and spirituality, passed away at the age of 128 in Varanasi. He was admitted to BHU Hospital on April 30 and died on Saturday night. Prime Minister Narendra Modi expressed his condolences, calling Baba Sivanand's death an "irreparable loss" and highlighting his lifelong dedication to yoga and sadhana. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath also paid tributes, praising Baba Sivanand's unparalleled contribution to the field of yoga. Baba Sivanand's body is at his residence for public viewing, with last rites scheduled for later in the evening.
