Yoga Guru Baba Sivanand Passes at 128

2025-05-04T06:57:00+00:00

Padma Shri awardee Baba Sivanand, a renowned yoga and spirituality guru, passed away at 128 in Varanasi due to health complications. He was admitted to BHU Hospital on April 30 and breathed his last on Saturday night. His body is currently at his residence for public homage, with last rites scheduled later in the evening. Prime Minister Narendra Modi expressed profound grief, calling Baba Sivanand's death an "irreparable loss" and highlighting his lifelong dedication to yoga and spirituality. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath also paid tribute to the guru, praising his significant contributions to the field of yoga.
