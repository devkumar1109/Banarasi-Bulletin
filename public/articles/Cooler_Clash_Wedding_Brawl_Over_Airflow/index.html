Cooler Clash: Wedding Brawl Over Airflow

2025-06-02T07:25:00+00:00

A wedding in Sipri Bazar, Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, turned chaotic when guests from the groom's side blocked the airflow from a cooler. This led to a brawl between the bride and groom's families, with guests pushing, shoving, and throwing punches, chairs, and even kitchen utensils. The incident, caught on video, went viral on social media, prompting amusement and criticism.
