NSUI Douses Bhagwat With Kumbh Criticism

2025-04-04T09:43:00+00:00

NSUI activists protested against RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat during his visit to Varanasi. They attempted to offer him Maha Kumbh water, criticizing his absence from the religious ritual and questioning his commitment to Sanatan tradition. The activists alleged that Bhagwat engages in Hindutva politics but avoids practicing it. Police detained several NSUI members during the protest.
