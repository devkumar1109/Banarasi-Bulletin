"Bhool Chuk Maaf" Gets Two New Songs, May 9 Release

2025-04-06T04:29:00+00:00

The release of the Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi-starrer "Bhool Chuk Maaf" has been moved to May 9. The delay allows for the addition of two new songs to the romantic comedy. One song, a peppy promotional number composed by Tanishk Bagchi, was filmed at a studio in Mira Road. The second song, a romantic bachelorette party themed track, will be shot in Varanasi in mid-April. Both songs are choreographed by Vijay Ganguly and feature costumes by Sheetal Sharma. "Bhool Chuk Maaf" revolves around Rao's character, who gets stuck in a time loop a day before his wedding.
