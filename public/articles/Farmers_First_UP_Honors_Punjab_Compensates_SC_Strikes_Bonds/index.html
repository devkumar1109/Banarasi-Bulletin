Farmers First: UP Honors, Punjab Compensates, SC Strikes Bonds

2025-06-08T12:51:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath presided over the Kisan Samman Samaroh in Auraiya, emphasizing the state government's pro-farmer initiatives. He highlighted various programs aimed at supporting farmers, including financial assistance, infrastructure development, and market access. The event was attended by a large number of farmers who expressed their gratitude for the government's efforts. Punjab CM Bhagwant Mann has announced a compensation of Rs 1 crore and a government job for the sister of Shubhkaran Singh, a farmer who died during a protest. The Punjab government also plans to give Rs 1 crore to the family of Shubkaran Singh, a farmer who died during a protest. The Supreme Court has declared the electoral bonds scheme unconstitutional.
