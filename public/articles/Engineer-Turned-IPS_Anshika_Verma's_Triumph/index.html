Engineer-Turned-IPS: Anshika Verma's Triumph

2025-04-09T00:44:00+00:00

Anshika Verma, from Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, achieved her dream of becoming an IPS officer after pursuing both engineering and the UPSC exam. Born on September 13, 1998, she earned a B. Tech degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Galgotia College of Engineering and Technology. Despite her father's retirement from UPEL, Anshika persevered through the challenges of preparing for the UPSC exam, studying independently and practicing diligently. She didn't succeed on her first attempt but ultimately achieved her goal through unwavering dedication.
