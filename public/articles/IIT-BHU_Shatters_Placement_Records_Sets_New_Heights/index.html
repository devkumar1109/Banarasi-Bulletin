IIT-BHU Shatters Placement Records, Sets New Heights

2025-06-16T18:55:00+00:00

IIT-BHU achieved a record placement season in 2024-25 with over 1,373 job offers, including four international placements. Fifteen students secured annual packages exceeding Rs 1 crore, with the highest offer reaching Rs 2.20 crore. Over 300 companies participated in the placement drive, recruiting students for various sectors. IIT-BHU director Prof Amit Patra attributed the success to the institute's focus on an updated curriculum, interdisciplinary approach, strong alumni network, and practical training. The institute expressed gratitude to its recruiting partners and aims to deepen collaborations.
