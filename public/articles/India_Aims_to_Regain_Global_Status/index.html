"Reclaiming Global Tech Supremacy: India's Quest for Innovation and Tradition"

2025-02-23T11:12:00+00:00

External Affairs Minister S. Jaishankar stated that India aims to regain its global status as a technology source, with space and AI being key areas. He highlighted the New Education Policy as a step to prepare people for new world challenges. The space industry is driven by young people, with many universities having drone programs. Jaishankar also mentioned the Prime Minister's attendance at the AI Summit in Paris. He emphasized that Indian knowledge can make a difference, with tradition complementing technology, citing Yoga and traditional medicines as examples. Speaking at Kashi Tamil Sangamam 3.0, Jaishankar celebrated Saint Agastya's achievements, and at the Delhi University Literature Festival, he expressed concern over USAID funds influencing Indian elections, calling for transparency on those involved in such activities.
