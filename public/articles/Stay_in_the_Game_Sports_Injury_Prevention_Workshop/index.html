Stay in the Game: Sports Injury Prevention Workshop

2025-04-18T07:41:00+00:00

Banaras Hindu University's Sports Board will host a three-day workshop on sports injury prevention and rehabilitation from April 22-24. The workshop is open to athletes, coaches, and enthusiasts and aims to educate participants on managing and preventing sports injuries. Led by experienced trainers, the workshop will feature lectures, demonstrations, and hands-on sessions. Participants will learn about injury prevention, management, and rehabilitation techniques to maintain peak athletic performance. The workshop will be held from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM daily to accommodate students' academic schedules.
