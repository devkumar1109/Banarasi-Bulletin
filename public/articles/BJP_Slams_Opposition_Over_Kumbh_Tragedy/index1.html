"Politics Over Pilgrims: BJP Calls Out Opposition's 'Death Toll Drama' in Maha Kumbh"

2025-02-23T18:29:00+00:00

Here is a 150-word summary of the news article: BJP spokesman and Rajya Sabha member Sudhanshu Trivedi has accused the opposition of seeking political gain in the Maha Kumbh tragedy, questioning the death toll and using terms like "Mrityu Kumbh". Trivedi criticized Samajwadi Party supremo Akhilesh Yadav for doubting the death toll, saying it's an old habit of Yadav's to question government data. The BJP has praised the successful organization of Maha Kumbh in UP, saying it has boosted the economy of the region and played a major role in cultural awakening. Trivedi also took a jibe at the opposition over the issue of deportation of illegal Indian immigrants by the US, saying they are opposing India's efforts to tackle illegal immigration. Additionally, the BJP has highlighted the key features of the recently tabled Union budget, saying it serves the interests of all sections of society and will take the country forward in the direction of becoming a developed nation by 2047.
