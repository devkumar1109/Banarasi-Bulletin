Varanasi Rape: 14 Arrested, 9 Still Sought

2025-04-16T09:30:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman was allegedly drugged and raped by 23 individuals over a week in Varanasi. Police have arrested 14 suspects so far, with efforts continuing to apprehend the remaining 9. The case came to light on April 5th when the victim's parents filed a complaint. DCP Chandrakant Mina, who was leading the investigation, has been reassigned. During a visit to Varanasi on April 11th, Prime Minister Modi sought updates on the investigation from local officials. The victim was diagnosed with hepatitis B after a medical examination.
