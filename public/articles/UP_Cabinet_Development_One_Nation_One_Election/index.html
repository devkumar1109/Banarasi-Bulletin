UP Cabinet: Development, One Nation, One Election

2025-04-08T16:08:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh cabinet meeting discussed 14 key proposals, with Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak emphasizing swift implementation for the state's development. Minister Sanjay Nishad criticized previous governments for misusing public funds. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reiterated his support for 'One Nation, One Election,' arguing that frequent elections disrupt development, encourage corruption, and negatively impact the GDP. Adityanath stressed the importance of political stability for peace, security, and progress.
