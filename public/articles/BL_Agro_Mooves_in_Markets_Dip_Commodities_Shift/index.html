BL Agro Mooves in, Markets Dip, Commodities Shift

2025-03-11T04:50:00+00:00

BL Agro will invest ₹3,000 crore in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Rajasthan to develop cow breeding and dairy technology. The market saw a slight dip, with the SENSEX closing at 73,869.21, down 245.96 points, and the NIFTY at 22,406.55, down 53.75 points. Crude oil prices decreased by $9.00 to $5,769.00, while gold prices increased by ₹216.00 to ₹85,635.00. Silver prices also rose by ₹425.00 to ₹96,890.00.
