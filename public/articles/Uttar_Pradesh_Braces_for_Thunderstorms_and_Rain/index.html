Uttar Pradesh Braces for Thunderstorms and Rain

2025-05-26T07:36:00+00:00

The Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) predicts thunderstorms, rain, and strong winds (30-40 km/h) across Uttar Pradesh in the next 48-72 hours. Rainfall is expected in districts including Gorakhpur, Deoria, Kushinagar, and others. Lucknow may experience light rain and thunder, with a maximum temperature of 38°C. Western UP is also expected to see light rain over the next two days due to cyclonic circulation. 20 districts, including Agra, Aligarh, and Bareilly, recorded rainfall on Sunday.
