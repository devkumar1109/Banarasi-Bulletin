Wife, Lovers Implicated in Gruesome Murder

2025-05-14T10:59:00+00:00

Devendra Ram, a 62-year-old retired army electrician, was murdered and dismembered by his wife Maya and three of her lovers in Ballia. Human limbs were discovered in a field, leading to the arrest of Maya and one lover, Mithilesh Patel. Devendra's torso was found in a well, and police are searching for his head, allegedly dumped in the Ghagra river. Two more accused, Anil Yadav and Satish Yadav, were arrested after a police encounter. The weapon used in the murder was also recovered. Maya confessed to planning the murder with her lovers. Devendra lived separately from his wife, who resided with their children being away for studies and work.
