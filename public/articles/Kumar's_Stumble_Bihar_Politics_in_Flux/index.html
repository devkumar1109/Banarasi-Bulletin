Kumar's Stumble: Bihar Politics in Flux

2025-03-28T14:27:00+00:00

Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar, 74, has faced recent criticism for his public behavior, including seeming disorientation and erratic gestures during the national anthem. This has fueled speculation about his mental state and prompted opposition attacks, with Lalu Prasad's Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) calling for his resignation. The BJP, Kumar's coalition partner, has urged party unity despite the concerns, recognizing Kumar's continued influence over Bihar's electorate. The situation highlights the growing instability within Bihar's political landscape, with caste-based coalitions posing a challenge to Kumar's leadership.
