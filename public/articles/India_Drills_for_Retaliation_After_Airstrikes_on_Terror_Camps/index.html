India Drills for Retaliation After Airstrikes on Terror Camps

2025-05-07T15:58:00+00:00

Following Operation Sindoor, India conducted civil defense mock drills across various states. The drills included a blackout in Delhi, Punjab, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, and others, preparing citizens for potential Pakistani retaliation. Indian armed forces conducted precision airstrikes in Pakistan and PoK, targeting nine terror camps and hideouts associated with JeM and LeT. These strikes aimed to avenge the Pahalgam terror attack.
