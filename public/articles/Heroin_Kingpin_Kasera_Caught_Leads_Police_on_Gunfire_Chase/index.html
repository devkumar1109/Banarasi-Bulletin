Heroin Kingpin Kasera Caught, Leads Police on Gunfire Chase

2025-03-23T19:08:00+00:00

Three drug smugglers, including notorious heroin smuggler Nanhe Kasera, were arrested in Mirzapur on Saturday night. Police recovered heroin worth Rs 6.5 crore from Kasera. He led police to a location in Rajgarh, where he opened fire, injuring a police constable while attempting to escape. Police returned fire, injuring Kasera in the leg. Kasera was arrested and hospitalized. No drugs were found at the Rajgarh location, but country-made pistols were recovered.
