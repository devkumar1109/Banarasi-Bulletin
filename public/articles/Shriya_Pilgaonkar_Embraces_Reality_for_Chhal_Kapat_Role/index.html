Shriya Pilgaonkar Embraces Reality for "Chhal Kapat" Role

2025-06-09T14:01:00+00:00

Bollywood actress Shriya Pilgaonkar met with women police officers of Uttar Pradesh Police in Lucknow. She met with officials including ADG Padmaja Chauhan and learned about the functioning of the state's helpline 1090 for women. Pilgaonkar, who plays Inspector Devika in Zee5's "Chhal Kapat: The Deception," found the experience "eye-opening" and said it deepened her understanding of her character. Devika is a police officer who is a survivor of domestic violence.
