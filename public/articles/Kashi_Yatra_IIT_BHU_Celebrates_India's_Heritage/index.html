Kashi Yatra: IIT BHU Celebrates India's Heritage

2025-03-07T15:02:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) in Varanasi inaugurated "Kashi Yatra," an annual cultural festival celebrating India's heritage. The festival, running from March 7 to 9, features a variety of events including music, dance, literature, theatre, and folk art. This year's theme is "Cultural Flow: The Cultural Heritage." Renowned Bharatanatyam dancer Geeta Chandran opened the festival with a captivating performance, followed by Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt's classical music recital. The festival aims to connect younger generations with their roots and preserve India's diverse arts and traditions. IIT (BHU) director, Prof Amit Patra, emphasized Kashi Yatra's importance in promoting cultural harmony and artistic expression.
