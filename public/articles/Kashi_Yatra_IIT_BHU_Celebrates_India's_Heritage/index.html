Kashi Yatra: IIT BHU Celebrates India's Heritage

2025-03-07T15:02:00+00:00

IIT (BHU) in Varanasi inaugurated Kashi Yatra, a prestigious cultural festival celebrating India's rich heritage. The three-day event (March 7-9) focuses on the theme 'Cultural Flow: The Cultural Heritage', featuring performances, competitions, and workshops in various art forms. Renowned Bharatanatyam dancer Geeta Chandran and classical musician Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt performed at the opening ceremony. The festival aims to connect younger generations with their roots and preserve India's diverse arts and traditions. It is organized in collaboration with SPIC MACAY and showcases music, dance, literature, theatre, and folk art.
