Meerut Love Triangle Ends in Gruesome Murder

2025-03-19T07:36:00+00:00

In Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, a woman named Muskan allegedly killed her husband, Saurabh Rajput, with her lover, Sahil. The couple stabbed Saurabh to death on March 4th and dismembered his body. They then sealed the remains in a cement-filled drum. Police arrested Muskan and Sahil after recovering the body and conducting an investigation. The victim's family filed a complaint, and a case has been registered. Muskan attempted to mislead her husband's family by sending messages from Saurabh's phone after the crime. She and Sahil then went on vacation to Shimla and Manali.
