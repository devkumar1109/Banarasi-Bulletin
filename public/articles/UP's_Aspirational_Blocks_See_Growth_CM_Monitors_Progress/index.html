UP's Aspirational Blocks See Growth, CM Monitors Progress

2025-05-07T08:46:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has ordered senior officers to monitor progress in the state's 108 aspirational development blocks. These blocks, identified as lagging behind in development, are part of the nationwide Aspirational Blocks Programme. Progress on 24 indicators in these blocks exceeds the state average. Six Uttar Pradesh districts rank among the top 10 nationally in health, nutrition, and education. Aurai development block achieved the top overall delta ranking from March 2023 to March 2024. Eight Principal Secretary/Secretary level officers and 108 Special Secretary level officers will conduct three-day field inspections in the aspirational districts, reporting on observed changes and development progress. The CM emphasized the government's commitment to ensuring all districts and individuals benefit from development initiatives.
