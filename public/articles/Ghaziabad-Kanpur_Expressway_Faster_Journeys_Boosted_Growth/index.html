Ghaziabad-Kanpur Expressway: Faster Journeys, Boosted Growth

2025-04-03T06:23:00+00:00

A new 380-km Ghaziabad-Kanpur Expressway is being constructed in Uttar Pradesh, aiming to cut travel time between the cities from eight hours to 5 hours 30 minutes. Developed under the Green Highway Policy, the expressway will connect nine districts: Ghaziabad, Hapur, Bulandshahr, Aligarh, Kasganj, Farrukhabad, Kannauj, Unnao, and Kanpur. Expected to be completed by 2026, the project will initially be a four-lane highway, with the possibility of expansion to six lanes. The expressway is anticipated to boost economic growth, create jobs, encourage tourism, and improve connectivity across the state.
