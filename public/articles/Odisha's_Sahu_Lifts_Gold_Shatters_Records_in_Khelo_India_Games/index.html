Odisha's Sahu Lifts Gold, Shatters Records in Khelo India Games

2025-05-25T07:34:00+00:00

Odisha's Harshvardhan Sahu won gold and set two national youth records in weightlifting at the Khelo India Youth Games 2025. Prime Minister Narendra Modi acknowledged Sahu's achievement in his recent "Mann Ki Baat" radio address. Sahu's performance was highlighted alongside that of Asmita Dhone from Maharashtra and Tushar Chaudhary.
