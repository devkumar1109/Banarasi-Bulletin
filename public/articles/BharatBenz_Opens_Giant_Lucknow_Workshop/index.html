BharatBenz Opens Giant Lucknow Workshop

2025-05-20T05:44:00+00:00

BharatBenz has inaugurated its largest day-and-night workshop in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, spanning 60,000 sq. ft. The facility features 15 service bays and can handle 7,500 vehicles annually. A team of 85 trained professionals will provide round-the-clock maintenance and repair services, focusing on BharatBenz fleets involved in infrastructure projects and local bus operators. This expansion brings BharatBenz's service network in Uttar Pradesh to 15 centers, with plans for 10 more, demonstrating their commitment to meeting the growing demand for commercial vehicle service in the region.
