Trucks Turned Trash: Man's Facebook Scam Busted

2025-05-24T18:43:00+00:00

A 28-year-old man, Abhishek Yadav, was arrested in Varanasi for selling 20 trucks worth Rs 4 crore to scrap dealers using fake registration documents. Yadav, from Madhya Pradesh, would rent trucks from owners through brokers, then sell them for profit. He used Facebook to contact owners, offering higher-than-usual rental rates and bribing brokers to gain their trust. The trucks were sold to a scrap dealer named Shafiq alias Chakki in Nagpur for Rs 5 lakh each. The Sonbhadra police arrested Yadav after receiving multiple complaints about missing trucks and launching an investigation.
