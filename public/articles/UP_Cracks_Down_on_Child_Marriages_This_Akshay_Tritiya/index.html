U.P. Cracks Down on Child Marriages This Akshay Tritiya

2025-04-29T19:19:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is on high alert to prevent child marriages on Akshay Tritiya, a day often associated with such practices. Authorities are conducting extensive surveillance and awareness campaigns across districts. The Probation Department, along with police and social welfare officials, is actively monitoring potential child marriage incidents. The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006, sets the legal marriage age at 21 for boys and 18 for girls. Violation can lead to imprisonment and fines. The government is urging public cooperation and the reporting of any suspected child marriage cases through a dedicated helpline (1098).
