Tikait Heckled: Farmers Revolt in UP

2025-05-03T19:25:00+00:00

Hundreds of farmers in Uttar Pradesh protested against the heckling of farmer leader Rakesh Tikait. The protest erupted after Tikait was allegedly harassed by a group while addressing a gathering in Meerut. Police have lodged an FIR in connection with the incident and arrested one person. The farmers' protest highlights the ongoing tensions surrounding agricultural policies and farmer rights in India.
