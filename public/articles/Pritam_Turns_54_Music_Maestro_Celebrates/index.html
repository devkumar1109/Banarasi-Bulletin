Pritam Turns 54: Music Maestro Celebrates

2025-06-14T01:45:00+00:00

Musician Pritam Chakraborty is celebrating his 54th birthday. He recently composed music for the upcoming film "Metro In Dino," directed by Anurag Basu. Pritam charges Rs 5 crore per film and is considered one of India's highest-paid musicians. Some of his popular songs include "Tum Se Hi" from "Jab We Met," "Pehli Nazar Mein" from "Race," "Tum Hi Ho Bandhu" from "Cocktail," and "Itni Si Hasi" from "Barfi!". Pritam's career spans decades, with hits like "Dhoom," "Gangster," "Jannat," "Kismat Konnection," "Race," "Love Aaj Kal," and "Brahmastra" under his belt. "Metro In Dino" is the sequel to Anurag Basu's 2007 hit "Life in a… Metro" and will premiere on July 4.
