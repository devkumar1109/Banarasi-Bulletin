Bride's Mom, Groom Go Missing After Arranged Marriage

2025-04-29T17:28:00+00:00

In Gonda, Uttar Pradesh, a 25-year-old man, Sushil, eloped with his fiancée's mother, Rekha. The marriage between Sushil and Geeta had been arranged for four months, but the pair frequently conversed for hours, raising suspicions. Though the marriage was initially called off, Sushil and Rekha remained in contact, ultimately leading to their disappearance. Geeta and her family filed a missing person report at the Khodare police station, prompting an ongoing police search.
