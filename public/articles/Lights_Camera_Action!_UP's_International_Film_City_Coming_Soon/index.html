Lights, Camera, Action! UP's International Film City Coming Soon

2025-03-25T03:48:00+00:00

Filmmaker Boney Kapoor met Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath in Lucknow and announced the upcoming foundation stone laying ceremony for the International Film City by Bayview Bhutani near Jewar International Airport. The film city, located at Y.E.I.D.A, aims to become a major hub for filmmaking with state-of-the-art infrastructure and production facilities. Under CM Yogi Adityanath's leadership, Uttar Pradesh is actively promoting the film industry with incentives and streamlined permissions. This project signifies the government's commitment to boosting the entertainment sector in the state.
