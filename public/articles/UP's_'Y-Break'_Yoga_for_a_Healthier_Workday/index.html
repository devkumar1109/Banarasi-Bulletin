UP's 'Y-Break': Yoga for a Healthier Workday

2025-06-18T14:40:00+00:00

To promote wellness and yoga ahead of International Yoga Day (June 21), the Uttar Pradesh government is implementing the 'Y-Break' Yoga Protocol in all government offices. 'Y-Break' involves 5-10 minute yoga sessions tailored for office workers, focusing on gentle movements, deep breathing, and mindfulness to reduce fatigue and enhance focus. The Union government will utilize digital tools like the 'Namaste Yoga App' and 'Y-Break App' to promote yoga practices. Awareness campaigns and collaborations with NGOs and RWAs aim to integrate yoga into daily life for government employees and the wider community.
