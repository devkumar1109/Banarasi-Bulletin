Rao-Gabbi Romance: "Bhool Chuk Maaf" Hits Big Screen May 9

2025-03-27T07:01:00+00:00

Rajkummar Rao and Wamiqa Gabbi's film "Bhool Chuk Maaf," a romantic comedy set in Varanasi, is set to release theatrically on May 9. The film, originally scheduled for April 10, was pushed back to avoid clashing with another release. "Bhool Chuk Maaf" will stream on Amazon Prime Video after its theatrical run, likely 45 to 60 days later. Directed by Karan Sharma, the film also stars Sanjay Mishra and Seema Pahwa. The plot revolves around Ranjan (Rao), a romantic who lands a job to impress his love Titli (Gabbi), but a twist of fate disrupts their wedding plans, leading to a hilarious journey of love and second chances.
