Yogi's Tax Drive: Transparency, Efficiency, and Development

2025-06-07T22:43:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed the State Tax Department, emphasizing transparency, technical efficiency, and strict enforcement in tax collection. He directed officials to crack down on shell companies and fake registered firms, calling tax evasion a national crime hindering development. CM Yogi met with the 16th Finance Commission, requesting a 50% share in central taxes and at least 20% in central taxes for Uttar Pradesh. He highlighted the state's achievements over the past eight years during the meeting. CM Yogi also addressed a state-level workshop marking 11 years of the Modi government, emphasizing India's global standing and the need for planning programs to build on past successes.
