Wanted Gangster Fati Killed in Mathura Encounter

2025-03-09T07:37:00+00:00

A wanted criminal, Fati alias Assad, carrying a reward of Rs 1 lakh was killed in an encounter with police in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh. SSP Shailesh Pandey said police received intelligence that Fati, the leader of the Chhaimar gang, was hiding with three accomplices. Fati was wanted in over 3 dozen cases of loot, dacoity, and murder across multiple states. His accomplices escaped, and a combing operation is underway. This incident follows a recent operation where four members of the Tatlu gang were arrested after an encounter in Mathura.
