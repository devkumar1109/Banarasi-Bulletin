UPSIDA, IIT-BHU Join Forces for Bundelkhand Pharma Parks

2025-04-09T23:01:00+00:00

UPSIDA signed an MoU with IIT-BHU to develop Pharma Parks in Lalitpur district, Bundelkhand. The project spans 1,472 acres across five villages and will establish pharmaceutical industries. IIT-BHU's Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology (DOPET) will be a key knowledge and technology partner, providing research, skilled human resources, and innovative solutions to companies operating in the parks.
