Doctor Orders Child to Smoke for Cold, Faces Inquiry

2025-04-16T13:15:00+00:00

A doctor in Jalaun district, Uttar Pradesh, Suresh Chandra, is under investigation for allegedly making a child smoke a cigarette as a treatment for a cold. The incident took place at the primary health center in Kuthaund. This action has sparked outrage and prompted an inquiry into the doctor's conduct.
