Charas Bust: Smuggler Caught, Drugs Flow Halted

2025-03-12T19:02:00+00:00

On March 13, 2025, the anti-narcotics task force (ANTF) Ghazipur and Lanka police seized 1.534 kg of charas worth Rs 7.5 lakh from a Buxar-based smuggler, Alok Raj Chaubey. Chaubey admitted to bringing the narcotic from Nepal and selling it to local peddlers. The charas was recovered from Assi Nullah in Varanasi.
