Hindu Families Flee Azamgarh Amidst Religious Tensions

2025-06-16T04:25:00+00:00

Around 40 Hindu families in Azamgarh's Chhota Pura area are selling their homes due to alleged harassment and violence stemming from playing music during religious and wedding events. The families claim the district administration ignored their complaints after a clash between two communities on June 3rd. Police have assured increased security presence in the area following the families' planned migration.
