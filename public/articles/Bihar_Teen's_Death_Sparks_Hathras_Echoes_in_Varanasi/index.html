Bihar Teen's Death Sparks Hathras Echoes in Varanasi

2025-03-10T09:09:00+00:00

Opposition MPs are protesting the death of a 17-year-old girl from Bihar who was found dead in her Varanasi hostel room. The MPs allege rape and murder, drawing comparisons to the 2020 Hathras case. They demand justice and an inquiry, emphasizing the incident occurred in PM Modi's constituency. The girl, preparing for NEET exams, was found hanging on February 1st. Her family claims the police conducted a postmortem and performed last rites without their consent.
