Annu Kapoor's "Uttar Da Puttar": Faith vs. Hard Work

2025-05-14T15:36:00+00:00

Veteran actor Annu Kapoor started shooting for the Hindi film "Uttar Da Puttar" in Uttarakhand. Directed by Ravinder Siwach and produced by Sandip Kapur, the film explores the clash between faith in practices like Vastu and astrology and belief in hard work. The cast includes Rukhsar Rehman, Pawan Malhotra, Rajendra Sethi, Brijendra Kala, Sumit Gulati, Istayak Khan, Nitin Arora, Jeeveshu Ahluwalia, and Colonel Hunny Bakshi. Cinematography is by Udaysingh Mohite. The film will also be shot in Delhi and is expected to release later this year.
