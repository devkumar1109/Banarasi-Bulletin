Holi Paints Varanasi in Global Harmony

2025-03-14T06:24:00+00:00

Holi, the festival of colors, is being celebrated with enthusiasm across India, particularly in Varanasi, where it's become a significant cultural experience for foreign tourists. At Mint House in Varanasi, travelers from around the world join in the festivities, dancing and playing with colors. President Droupadi Murmu and Prime Minister Narendra Modi extended their warm wishes for Holi, emphasizing the festival's significance in promoting unity, love, and harmony. Defence Minister Rajnath Singh also conveyed his greetings, praying for the well-being of the people. Holi, which began on Thursday with Chhoti Holi, is celebrated with colors, music, and traditional festivities, symbolizing the triumph of good over evil.
