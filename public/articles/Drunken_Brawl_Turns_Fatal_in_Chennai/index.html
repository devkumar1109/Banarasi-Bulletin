Drunken Brawl Turns Fatal in Chennai

2025-03-22T18:23:00+00:00

A 19-year-old man, Jegan, has been arrested for the murder of 19-year-old college student Lokesh in Tiruvallur district, Chennai. Lokesh was visiting his grandmother when he was killed. Police investigation revealed that Jegan and Lokesh had a drunken brawl, leading to the murder. Lokesh's body was found two days ago in an empty plot, prompting villagers to alert the police.
