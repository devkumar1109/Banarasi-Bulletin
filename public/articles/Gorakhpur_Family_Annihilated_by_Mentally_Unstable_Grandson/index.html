Gorakhpur: Family Annihilated by Mentally Unstable Grandson

2025-02-28T14:56:00+00:00

In Gorakhpur, a 24-year-old man, Ram Dayal, suspected of mental instability, killed his grandfather Kuber Maurya, grandmother Draupadi, and grand-uncle Sadhu Maurya with a spade. The incident occurred after Ram Dayal attacked a buffalo, and his grandfather attempted to intervene. He chased the victims into fields and fatally struck them. Police recovered the spade and are investigating the motive. Ram Dayal's mother revealed he had been aggressive since a recent accident, brandishing sharp objects. The family has a history of mental health issues. Police confirmed a post-mortem examination and forensic analysis are underway.
