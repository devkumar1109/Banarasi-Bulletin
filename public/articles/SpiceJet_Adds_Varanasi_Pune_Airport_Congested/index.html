SpiceJet Adds Varanasi, Pune Airport Congested

2025-03-30T18:45:00+00:00

SpiceJet launched a Pune-Varanasi flight starting March 30, marking the addition of a new domestic destination to Pune airport. The flight operates six days a week, except Saturdays, at 12:30 am from Pune. Pune airport currently utilizes 104 out of 109 available slots, with no new international flights added in the summer schedule. The airport recorded 204 flight movements on Sunday, with a 5.5% increase in weekly flights nationwide compared to last year. Despite the increase, the airport continues to face congestion issues, particularly during arrivals.
