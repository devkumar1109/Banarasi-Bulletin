Mumbai Burglar's Jet-Setting Spree Foiled

2025-04-04T21:34:00+00:00

Rajesh Arvind Rajbhar, 32, from Uttar Pradesh, was arrested in Thane for multiple burglaries in Mumbai. Rajbhar flew into Mumbai, committed 10-12 burglaries over 15 days, and then flew back to Uttar Pradesh before police could apprehend him. He arrived in Mumbai on March 13, 2025, and targeted homes in Mulund, Bhandup, Nehru Nagar, and Ulwe. Police tracked him using CCTV footage and informant information. Stolen property, including 28 tolas of gold and 2 kg of silver, was recovered. Rajbhar's brother-in-law was previously arrested for burglaries in Solapur, suggesting a possible connection between the two.
