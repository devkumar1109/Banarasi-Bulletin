Ace Turtle Expands Wrangler Reach with Tech-Driven Stores

2025-05-14T05:52:00+00:00

Ace turtle, a technology-driven retail company, has launched two new Wrangler stores in Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. The stores, located in Phoenix United Mall, Bareilly, and New Market, Purnea, are equipped with omnichannel capabilities, serving both walk-in customers and online orders. This expansion marks Ace Turtle's commitment to bringing Wrangler to wider markets beyond major cities, leveraging technology for a seamless shopping experience. Ace Turtle holds the exclusive license for Wrangler, Lee, Toys“R”Us, Babies“R”Us, and Dockers in India and South Asia through a licensing deal with Kontoor Brands Inc., the parent company of Wrangler and Lee.
