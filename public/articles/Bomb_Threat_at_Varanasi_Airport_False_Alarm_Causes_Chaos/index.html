Bomb Threat at Varanasi Airport: False Alarm Causes Chaos

2025-04-27T19:10:00+00:00

A Canadian national, Yohanathan Nishanth, was arrested at Varanasi airport for falsely claiming to have a bomb on a Bengaluru-bound IndiGo flight. The incident caused panic and led to the flight being grounded, passengers evacuated, and a five-hour search by security personnel. No explosives were found. Nishanth, who appeared intoxicated, became agitated when asked to return to his seat and made alarming declarations about explosives and religious slogans. The Canada high commission was informed of the incident. After clearance from security agencies, the flight departed for Bengaluru on Sunday morning.
