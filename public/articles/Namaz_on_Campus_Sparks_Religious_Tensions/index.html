Namaz on Campus Sparks Religious Tensions

2025-03-17T07:43:00+00:00

A 22-year-old Muslim student was arrested in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, for offering namaz in an open area of the Intellectual Institute of Management and Technology University. The arrest followed protests by Hindu groups over a video circulating online that purportedly showed students performing namaz on campus during Holi celebrations. The incident highlights growing tensions surrounding religious expression in public spaces.
