Love Triangle Turns Deadly: Groom-to-Be Murdered

2025-06-18T02:53:00+00:00

In Rampur, Uttar Pradesh, Mohd Saddam, 26, was arrested for murdering Nihal, 25, a groom-to-be, a day before his wedding. Saddam confessed to killing Nihal to prevent his marriage to Gulbasha, whom Saddam was in love with. Saddam, posing as the bride's cousin, lured Nihal out and strangled him with an accomplice. CCTV footage led police to identify Saddam, who confessed and led them to Nihal's body and phone. During the arrest, Saddam snatched a police pistol and opened fire, resulting in him being shot in the leg. An FIR has been registered against Saddam and his accomplice under charges of murder and destruction of evidence.
