Uttar Pradesh Speaks Sanskrit: 53,000 Strong

2025-05-29T12:35:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has trained over 53,000 people, half of them women, in spoken Sanskrit under a government scheme initiated by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. The program, launched in 2018, aims to revive the ancient language through innovative methods like the 'Missed Call Yojana' and public engagement initiatives. Participants underwent a 20-day structured training program, with additional sessions and internships offered for deeper understanding. The 'Grihe-Grihe Sanskritam' initiative further promotes Sanskrit within homes and communities, demonstrating widespread interest in learning the language.
