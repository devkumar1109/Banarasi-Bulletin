Namaz Arrest Sparks Holi Row in Indian University

2025-03-17T07:37:00+00:00

A 22-year-old Muslim student, Khalil Pradhan, was arrested in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, for offering namaz in an open area of the Intellectual Institute of Management and Technology University. The arrest followed protests by Hindu groups over a video circulating online, purportedly showing students performing namaz on the university campus during Holi celebrations.
