Holi Advice Sparks Muslim Outrage

2025-03-06T15:54:00+00:00

A Uttar Pradesh police official, Anuj Chaudhary, sparked controversy by advising Muslims to stay indoors during Holi if they feel uncomfortable with colors. He drew a comparison between Holi for Hindus and Friday prayers for Muslims, stating Holi occurs once a year while Friday prayers happen 52 times. The Samajwadi Party and Congress criticized the official's remarks, accusing him of acting as a BJP agent.
