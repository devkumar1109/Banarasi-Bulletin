Modi's Development Dash: Sikkim to UP

2025-05-28T05:37:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will embark on a two-day visit to Sikkim, West Bengal, Bihar, and Uttar Pradesh on May 29th and 30th. In Sikkim, he will participate in the "Sikkim@50" celebrations and lay the foundation stone and inaugurate several development projects, including a new 500-bedded district hospital in Namchi. In West Bengal, PM Modi will lay the foundation stone for City Gas Distribution projects in Alipurduar and Cooch Behar districts. In Bihar, he will inaugurate Patna Airport's new terminal building and dedicate numerous development projects worth over Rs 48,520 crore in Karakat. Finally, in Uttar Pradesh, PM Modi will lay the foundation stone and inaugurate development projects worth around Rs 20,900 crore in Kanpur Nagar.
