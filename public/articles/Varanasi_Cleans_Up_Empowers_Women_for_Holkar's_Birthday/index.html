Varanasi Cleans Up, Empowers Women for Holkar's Birthday

2025-05-30T02:53:00+00:00

The Varanasi Municipal Corporation (VMC) launched a two-day mega cleanliness drive and women's empowerment program to commemorate Ahilyabai Holkar's 300th birth anniversary. The program, covering all 21 sanitation sub-zones, includes honoring female sanitation workers, connecting them with government schemes, and providing kits. The drive features special cleaning of ghats and drains, awareness campaigns, health check-up camps, and seminars on women's empowerment. Municipal Commissioner Akshat Verma inspected drain cleaning and participated in the cleanliness drive, distributing safety kits.
