Schoolgirl Loses Sight, Principal Accused

2025-02-27T11:04:00+00:00

A Class 3 student in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, named Himanshi, allegedly suffered vision loss in one eye after an incident at Bhogpur Mithoni Primary School. Her mother, Jyoti Kashyap, accuses the principal, Geeta Karal, of inflicting the injuries. Kashyap claims her daughter was beaten by the principal, but Karal denies this, stating Himanshi already had weak eyesight. She says the injury occurred when a classmate accidentally hit Himanshi's eye with her elbow. Kashyap's request for a medical certificate was denied, leading to an alleged threat towards the principal. Moradabad Basic Siksha Adhikari (BSA) Ajit Kumar has initiated an inquiry into the incident.
