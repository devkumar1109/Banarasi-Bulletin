Kashi Beckons: Tourism Booms in Varanasi

2025-05-05T04:45:00+00:00

Varanasi (Kashi) has seen a significant surge in tourism in the first quarter of 2025. Domestic tourist numbers jumped by 77.59% to 11.46 crore visitors, while foreign tourist arrivals increased by 34.21% to 1.5 lakh. This growth is attributed to the city's spiritual significance and improved infrastructure, including revamped ghats, better roads, sanitation, and modern amenities. The number of domestic tourists increased by nearly 8.89 crore compared to the same period last year.
