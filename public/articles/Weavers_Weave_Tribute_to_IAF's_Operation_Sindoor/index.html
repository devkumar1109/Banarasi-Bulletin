Weavers Weave Tribute to IAF's Operation Sindoor

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers have created special sarees inspired by the Indian Air Force (IAF) to honor Operation Sindoor. The sarees feature depictions of weapons such as BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale, symbolizing the strength and capabilities of the IAF. This unique initiative is a tribute to the bravery and sacrifices of the Indian armed forces.
