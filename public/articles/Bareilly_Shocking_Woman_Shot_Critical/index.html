Bareilly Shocking: Woman Shot, Critical

2025-03-30T06:26:00+00:00

A 42-year-old woman, Sonu, was shot and critically injured in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on Saturday night when Sonu went to a medical store. Four men in a black car took her to an isolated spot near Sita Rasoi and shot her before fleeing. Police are investigating whether she was kidnapped or went willingly with the men. Sonu sustained a gunshot wound to her chest and is hospitalized. Her family has not provided any information regarding the attack. Authorities are reviewing CCTV footage to identify the attackers. Police are exploring the motive behind the crime and working to apprehend the suspects.
