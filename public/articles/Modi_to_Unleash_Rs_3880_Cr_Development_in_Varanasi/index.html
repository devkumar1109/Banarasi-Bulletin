Modi to Unleash Rs 3,880 Cr Development in Varanasi

2025-04-09T18:33:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Varanasi on Friday and inaugurate development projects worth over Rs 3,880 crore. These include road projects costing over Rs 980 crore, such as a bridge between Varanasi Ring Road and Sarnath, flyovers, and a highway tunnel. He will also inaugurate three transmission substations worth over Rs 1,045 crore and lay the foundation stone for other infrastructure projects like a 220 KV substation, police facilities, and educational institutions. Modi will also initiate the construction of sports infrastructure, including a synthetic hockey turf and a mini-stadium.
