Modi to Boost Varanasi Infrastructure with Rs 3,884 Crore Projects

2025-04-10T13:05:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Varanasi on April 11th to launch infrastructure and development projects worth Rs 3,884 crore. Key projects include a Rs 652.64 crore six-lane tunnel under National Highway-31 for Babatpur Airport expansion, enabling 110 daily flights. Other initiatives include: - Rs 12 crore transport city in Mohansarai - Rs 6.15 crore mini stadium in Shivpur - Rs 345.12 crore for 130 rural drinking water schemes under the Jal Jeevan Mission - Over Rs 1,000 crore for a 400 kV power substation and transmission lines. The visit will also see the rejuvenation of 11 parks and installation of 35 new public artworks.
