Khichdi: India's Superfood Takes the World by Storm

2025-05-25T16:06:00+00:00

Khichdi, a traditional Indian dish, is gaining global popularity as a superfood. It is lauded for its nutritional value, being light on the stomach, packed with protein, and containing essential nutrients. Khichdi is celebrated for its versatility, being enjoyed by all ages and customizable with various ingredients. Chefs are incorporating khichdi into fine-dining menus, experimenting with new-age versions like Lassoni Khichdi and Truffle-Infused Moong Dal Khichdi, while respecting its humble origins. Food brands are capitalizing on the trend by offering ready-to-eat khichdi mixes. Khichdi holds cultural significance, being offered as prasadam in temples across India.
