UP Strengthens Police Force with Mass Recruitment

2025-06-18T12:12:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath held a 'Janta Darshan' at Gorakhnath temple in Gorakhpur, addressing public grievances. The Uttar Pradesh government has recruited 2.16 lakh police personnel in the last eight years, aiming to modernize the force. Union Home Minister Amit Shah distributed appointment letters to 60,244 new constables in Lucknow, highlighting the improved law and order situation under CM Adityanath. The Uttar Pradesh Police Constable Recruitment Exam 2024, held across 67 districts, aimed to fill over 60,000 vacancies.
