Uttar Pradesh Manufacturing: Rs 3 Lakh Crore Boom Ahead

2025-04-09T16:07:00+00:00

The manufacturing sector in Uttar Pradesh is projected to attract Rs 3 lakh crore in investment in 2025-26. This follows a strong performance in 2024-25 where the sector contributed Rs 2.81 lakh crore to the state's Gross State Value Addition (GSVA), achieving 77% of the annual target. The state government, under Invest UP 2.0, has created an investment-friendly environment, attracting Rs 50,000 crore in investments and achieving a 4% growth in the Index of Industrial Production (IIP). To further bolster the sector, the government is focusing on establishing new manufacturing units, attracting FDI, developing a land and raw material bank, and designating special investment regions.
