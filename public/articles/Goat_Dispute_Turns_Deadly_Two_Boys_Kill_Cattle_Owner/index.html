Goat Dispute Turns Deadly: Two Boys Kill Cattle Owner

2025-03-21T17:38:00+00:00

A 30-year-old cattle owner, Awadhesh Kumar, was beaten to death by two juvenile boys in Singhpur village, Ballia district, over a dispute involving his goat eating grains. Kumar's goat entered his neighbor's house and began consuming grains, leading to an altercation. Later that evening, the two boys attacked Kumar in a field with sticks, leaving him unconscious. Police arrived and transported Kumar to the district hospital, where he was pronounced dead. The juvenile suspects were apprehended while attempting to flee and face charges before the juvenile justice board.
