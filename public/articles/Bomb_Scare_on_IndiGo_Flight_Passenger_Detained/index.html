Bomb Scare on IndiGo Flight, Passenger Detained

2025-04-27T11:30:00+00:00

A Canadian passenger on an IndiGo flight from Varanasi to Bengaluru claimed to be carrying a bomb, triggering a security scare at Varanasi airport on Saturday night. The passenger was immediately detained by authorities. The aircraft was moved to an isolation bay and thoroughly inspected, but no explosive material was found. After clearance from security agencies, the flight departed for Bengaluru the following morning. A detailed investigation into the incident is ongoing.
