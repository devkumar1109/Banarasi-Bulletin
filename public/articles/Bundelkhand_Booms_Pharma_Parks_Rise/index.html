Bundelkhand Booms: Pharma Parks Rise

2025-04-09T23:01:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority (UPSIDA) signed a MoU with IIT-BHU to develop Pharma Parks in Lalitpur district, Bundelkhand. The project spans 1,472 acres across five villages and aims to establish pharmaceutical industries. IIT-BHU's Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology (DOPET) will be a key partner, providing research, skilled resources, and technological solutions to companies operating within the Pharma Parks.
