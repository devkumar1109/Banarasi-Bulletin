Ahilyabai's Legacy: Empowering Women, Building India

2025-05-31T14:52:00+00:00

A panchayat representative conference, organized by Varanasi district panchayat, celebrated the 300th birth anniversary of Ahilyabai Holkar at Rudraksha Connection Centre. UP Finance Minister Suresh Khanna highlighted Ahilyabai's virtues, bravery, and role in expanding the Malwa Empire. He also mentioned Prime Minister Modi's initiatives like Veerbal Day and 33% reservation for women in Parliament and Legislative Assembly, crediting them for India's economic growth and global standing. District panchayat president Poonam Maurya emphasized Ahilyabai's contributions to women's upliftment, including opposing Sati, supporting widow remarriage and property rights. She also highlighted Ahilyabai's commitment to temple construction, ghat development, and environmental conservation.
