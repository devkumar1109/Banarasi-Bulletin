DMRC Opens Tunneling Center for India's Innovation

2025-02-27T05:52:00+00:00

The Managing Director of Delhi Metro Rail Corporation Limited (DMRC) inaugurated the Centre of Excellence in Tunnelling and Tunnel Training Centre on February 25, 2025. The inauguration was attended by dignitaries from academic institutions like IIT Delhi and IIT BHU, and representatives from industries. This state-of-the-art facility, a joint initiative of DMRC and IIT BHU Varanasi, aims to advance tunnel design, construction, safety, and workforce training. The Centre features smart classrooms, an AR/VR lab, and a 25-meter true-scale tunnel model for practical training. A key highlight was the unveiling of a cutter head, signifying a step forward in training professionals in tunnel technology. The Centre aims to bridge the gap between research and real-world application, positioning India as a leader in tunnelling innovation.
