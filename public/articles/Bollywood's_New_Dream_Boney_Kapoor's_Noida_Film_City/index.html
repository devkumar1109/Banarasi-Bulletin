Bollywood's New Dream: Boney Kapoor's Noida Film City

2025-05-29T06:28:00+00:00

Veteran Bollywood producer Boney Kapoor is partnering with the Bhutani Group to construct a world-class Film City in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. Spanning 1,000 acres, the project will be developed in three phases over eight years, with an initial investment of ₹1,510 crore. The first phase (230 acres) will include 13-14 film studios, an underwater shooting facility, post-production centers, a cinema museum, and a film institute. The institute will offer professional courses in acting, editing, VFX, scriptwriting, and more across 3 lakh square feet. The ambitious project aims to generate over 5 lakh jobs and boost cultural tourism. While planning challenges and necessary approvals are underway, construction is set to begin before June 30, 2025.
