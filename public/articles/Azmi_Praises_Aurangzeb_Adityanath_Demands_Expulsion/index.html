Azmi Praises Aurangzeb, Adityanath Demands Expulsion

2025-03-05T15:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath demanded the expulsion of Samajwadi Party leader Abu Azmi from the party following his praise of Mughal emperor Aurangzeb. Azmi, who was suspended from the Maharashtra Assembly for the remainder of the session, stated he didn't consider Aurangzeb cruel and highlighted his construction of temples during his reign. Adityanath criticized Azmi's remarks and urged Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav to take action against him, suggesting Azmi should be brought to Uttar Pradesh for appropriate treatment.
