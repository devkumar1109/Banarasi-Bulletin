Varanasi Gets Clean Water, Clean City Push

2025-03-17T18:47:00+00:00

Urban Development Minister AK Sharma reviewed municipal corporation and water corporation projects in Varanasi. The meeting focused on drain cleaning, sewage treatment, and drinking water supply. The minister emphasized quality drain cleaning and progress on closing drains flowing into the Ganga. He instructed zonal officers to conduct daily cleanliness inspections and requested updates on Shujabad and Raman sewage plants. The minister also directed the installation of water ATMs in Varanasi and urged the Jal Nigam to expedite sewer line installation in 18 wards.
