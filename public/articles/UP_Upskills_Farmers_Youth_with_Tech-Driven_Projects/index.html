UP Upskills Farmers, Youth with Tech-Driven Projects

2025-05-09T23:06:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government and the World Bank launched two projects. 'UP AGREES', a $3.9 billion project, will provide technology-driven farming support to 28 districts in Purvanchal and Bundelkhand, benefiting nearly 10 lakh farmers, with 30% participation from women. The project includes training 500 farmers abroad and linking 10,000 women producer groups. 'AI Pragya' aims to train 10 lakh youth in AI and digital technologies, offering certifications in AI, machine learning, data analytics, and cybersecurity.
