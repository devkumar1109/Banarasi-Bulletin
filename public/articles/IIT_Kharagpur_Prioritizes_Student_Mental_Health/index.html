IIT Kharagpur Prioritizes Student Mental Health

2025-05-05T23:53:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur has instructed hostel wardens to interact more with students, emphasizing mental health support. This directive comes after a student died by suicide in the institute's hostel. The institute is implementing new measures to enhance mental well-being, including counseling services and peer support groups. IIT Kharagpur aims to create a more supportive and caring environment for its students.
