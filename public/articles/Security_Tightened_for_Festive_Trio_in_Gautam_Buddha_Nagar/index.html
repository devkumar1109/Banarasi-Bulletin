Security Tightened for Festive Trio in Gautam Buddha Nagar

2025-03-28T04:22:00+00:00

From March 28 to 31, Gautam Buddha Nagar police are enforcing Section 163 of the Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) to ensure security during Alvida Jumma, Cheti Chand, and Eid-ul-Fitr celebrations. Uttar Pradesh police have issued comprehensive directives for maintaining law and order across the state, emphasizing zone and sector schemes, strategic police placement, and preventing new customs. Officials are instructed to engage with peace committees and religious leaders, conduct flag marches, and inspect all venues. Increased foot patrolling, anti-sabotage inspections, and drone surveillance are also mandated.
