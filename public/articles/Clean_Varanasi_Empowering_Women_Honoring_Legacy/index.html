Clean Varanasi: Empowering Women, Honoring Legacy

2025-05-30T02:53:00+00:00

Varanasi's Municipal Corporation launched a two-day mega cleanliness drive and women's empowerment program on May 30th, 2025, to celebrate Ahilyabai Holkar's 300th birth anniversary. The initiative, covering all 21 sanitation sub-zones, includes honoring female sanitation workers, connecting them with government schemes, distributing kits, and special cleaning of ghats and drains. The drive also features awareness campaigns, health check-up camps, seminars, and activities focusing on women's empowerment in schools, colleges, and universities. On the first day, a major cleanliness drive covered areas like Baluabeer, Kotwali, and Tilbhandeshwar Park. Municipal Commissioner Akshat Verma participated, inspecting drain cleaning and distributing safety kits to sanitation workers.
