Lucknow Rape Sparks Driver Background Checks

2025-03-24T09:53:00+00:00

Following the rape and murder of a woman in Lucknow, the Uttar Pradesh government has mandated strict background checks for all auto-rickshaw and e-rickshaw drivers. This move aims to prevent future crimes against women and enhance public safety. Drivers with criminal histories will face immediate police action. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized the Lucknow Police Commissioner for lapses in vigilance, stating the incident could have been prevented with stricter monitoring of drivers as done previously.
