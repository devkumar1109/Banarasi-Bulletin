Uttar Pradesh: Revenge Kidnapping Sparks Manhunt

2025-05-04T18:55:00+00:00

In Ballia district, Uttar Pradesh, 55-year-old Ajay Tiwari was abducted from his residence by over a dozen individuals riding SUVs and motorcycles. The incident is suspected to be linked to an altercation on April 29th where Tiwari and his son confronted two youths who urinated outside their house. The youths were subsequently arrested, but it's believed the abductors may be associated with them. Police, including the crime branch, are actively involved in the rescue operation and apprehending the kidnappers. A massive search operation is underway across the district.
