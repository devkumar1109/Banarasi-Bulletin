Kumbh: God for Devotees, Controversy for Critics

2025-02-24T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized the Opposition for their criticism of the Maha Kumbh, stating that people found what they sought during the religious gathering. He said "vultures found corpses, pigs found filth, while devotees found God." Adityanath defended the event against allegations of caste-based discrimination, stating that anyone with good intentions was welcome. He also implied that previous governments lacked commitment to the Kumbh, highlighting the appointment of a non-Sanatani in a leadership role.
