India Takes Flight: Self-Reliant Fighter Jet Powerhouse

2025-05-15T18:00:00+00:00

India is expected to become self-reliant in fighter jet production within a few years, according to aviation scientist Kota Harinarayana. A robust manufacturing ecosystem and improved production technology have enabled this progress. India will soon meet the Indian Air Force's requirements for all fighter categories and may even become an exporter of defense products. The Indian Air Force plans to replace its aging MiG fleet, further bolstering the domestic aerospace industry. Harinarayana highlighted India's successful defense capabilities during Operation Sindoor, showcasing the strength of indigenous defense products. He also praised the successful test of Bhargavastra, a low-cost counter-drone system.
