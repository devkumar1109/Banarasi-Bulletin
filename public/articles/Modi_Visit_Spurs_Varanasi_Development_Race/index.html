Modi Visit Spurs Varanasi Development Race

2025-03-18T18:48:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi is expected to visit his Varanasi constituency in April or May, prompting a March 31st deadline for completing development projects. Divisional Commissioner Kaushal Raj Sharma ordered salary withholding for officials who missed a meeting reviewing project progress. Sharma emphasized timely project completion, citing Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's directives. Progress reports revealed 136 Jal Jeevan Mission schemes ready, 110 Anganwadi centers under construction, and 77 school buildings slated for renovation by the end of March. Road construction projects, barracks, a polytechnic, and four other roads are also nearing completion. The commissioner directed action against officials responsible for delays in the Smart Distribution Project, underground electrical infrastructure, and modernization projects.
