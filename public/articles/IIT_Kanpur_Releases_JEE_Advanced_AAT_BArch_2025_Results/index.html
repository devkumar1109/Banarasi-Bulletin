IIT Kanpur Releases JEE Advanced AAT BArch 2025 Results

2025-06-08T06:16:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur has released the JEE Advanced AAT BArch 2025 results. The exam was held on June 5th and results were declared on June 8th. Students who qualified for JEE Advanced 2025 and took the AAT can now apply for BArch programs at IIT BHU Varanasi, IIT Kharagpur, and IIT Roorkee. Results are available on jeeadv.ac.in using the admit card number and date of birth. Admission to BArch is based on JEE Advanced AAT scores and category-wise All India Rank. Candidates must also have at least 75% marks in class 12 (65% for SC, ST, and PwD categories).
