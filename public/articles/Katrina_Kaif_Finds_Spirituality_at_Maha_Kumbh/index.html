Katrina Kaif Finds Spirituality at Maha Kumbh

2025-02-24T15:27:00+00:00

Katrina Kaif visited the Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj with her mother-in-law, Veena Kaushal. She met with Swami Chidanand Saraswati and Sadhvi Bhagawati Saraswati at Parmarth Niketan Ashram. Swami Chidanand Saraswati is the head of Parmarth Niketan Ashram in Rishikesh, a prominent spiritual institution in India. He is known for his teachings on spirituality, yoga, meditation, and sustainable living. Kaif's visit to the Maha Kumbh, a significant religious gathering held every 12 years, blends spirituality with entertainment, inspiring youth to reconnect with their roots.
