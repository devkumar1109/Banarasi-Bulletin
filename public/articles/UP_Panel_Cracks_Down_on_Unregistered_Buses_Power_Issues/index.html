U.P. Panel Cracks Down on Unregistered Buses, Power Issues

2025-04-24T18:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly's Estimates Committee has directed district officials to intensify the crackdown on unregistered buses and install prepaid electricity meters in the Hindon floodplains. The committee also called for a health department budget audit due to an apparent mismatch between allocated funds and actual expenditure. The transport department was instructed to take action against buses with expired registrations and expedite the implementation of an organised city transport system. The power department was directed to identify large electricity defaulters and launch recovery drives. The stamp department was asked to collaborate with the district administration to secure land for a new office to improve registration services.
