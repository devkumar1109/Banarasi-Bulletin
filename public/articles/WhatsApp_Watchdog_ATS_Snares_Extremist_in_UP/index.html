WhatsApp Watchdog: ATS Snares Extremist in UP

2025-05-24T05:43:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested Mohammad Tufail from Varanasi for allegedly spreading extremist propaganda among Muslim youth. Tufail is suspected of having links to individuals in Pakistan and using WhatsApp to disseminate radical content, including videos of Maulana Saad Rizvi and inflammatory statements about the Babri Masjid demolition. The ATS is monitoring over 800 mobile numbers linked to Tufail's WhatsApp groups in eastern Uttar Pradesh. Tufail is accused of emotionally influencing young Muslims, promoting Sharia law as a governance foundation for both India and Pakistan, and urging the need for religious leaders to guide Muslim youth politically. He reportedly formed new WhatsApp groups after attending secret meetings and gatherings in Uttar Pradesh and Punjab.
