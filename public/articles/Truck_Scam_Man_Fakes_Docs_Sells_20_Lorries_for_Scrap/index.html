Truck Scam: Man Fakes Docs, Sells 20 Lorries for Scrap

2025-05-24T18:43:00+00:00

Abhishek Yadav, a 28-year-old man from Madhya Pradesh, was arrested in Varanasi for selling 20 trucks worth Rs 4 crore to scrap dealers using fake registration documents. Yadav would rent trucks from owners through brokers, offering higher-than-standard rates and bribing brokers to gain trust. After paying half the rental amount, he would sell the trucks to Shafiq alias Chakki, a scrap dealer in Nagpur, for Rs 5 lakh each. The police investigation began after receiving complaints about missing trucks and traced Yadav's activities through Facebook brokers in various districts.
