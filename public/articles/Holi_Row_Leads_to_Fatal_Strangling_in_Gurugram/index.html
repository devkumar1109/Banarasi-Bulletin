Holi Row Leads to Fatal Strangling in Gurugram

2025-03-23T05:00:00+00:00

In Gurugram, a man was arrested for strangling his wife, Renu, following an argument about returning home for Holi. He used a scarf to kill her, locked her body in their rented room in Bhangrola village, and fled. Police discovered Renu's body on March 7th. An FIR was filed at the Kherki Daula police station, and the accused was apprehended in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, on Friday.
