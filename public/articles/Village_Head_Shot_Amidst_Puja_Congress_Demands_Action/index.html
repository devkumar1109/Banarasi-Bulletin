Village Head Shot Amidst Puja, Congress Demands Action

2025-06-07T19:32:00+00:00

Ramesh Rai, a former village head in Mau, Uttar Pradesh, was shot in the head during a puja ceremony on June 7, 2025. The incident stemmed from an old enmity. Rai was referred to Varanasi for treatment. The Congress has demanded action from the authorities regarding the shooting.
