Congress Fights Back: National Herald Counteroffensive

2025-04-20T17:31:00+00:00

Congress leaders will hold press conferences in 57 cities from April 21 to 24 to counter the BJP's accusations in the National Herald case. They aim to expose what they call “BJP’s lies” and defend the Congress leadership against money laundering charges. Congress claims the charges are politically motivated and denies any wrongdoing. The party plans to highlight its stance on the Waqf (Amendment) Act and criticize the BJP's alleged targeting of the Supreme Court. Prominent leaders like Pawan Khera, P Chidambaram, Shashi Tharoor, Ashok Gehlot, and others will address reporters in various cities across India.
