Kumbh Mela's Boon: Eastern UP's Tourism Triumph

2025-03-05T00:18:00+00:00

The recent Maha Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj attracted nearly 65 crore pilgrims, boosting tourism in Ayodhya and Varanasi as well. This surge highlights the economic potential of religious tourism in eastern UP. The article argues that the UP government should focus on developing under-utilized Buddhist sites to further capitalize on this growth. Religious tourism not only generates revenue and jobs but also drives investment in infrastructure, promotes sustainable development, and preserves cultural heritage. It presents a significant opportunity for Uttar Pradesh, given its rich religious history and the growing interest in faith-based travel.
