YouTube Star's Lamborghini Crashes, Injures Two

2025-03-31T06:42:00+00:00

YouTuber Mridul Tiwari's Lamborghini Huracan, registered in Puducherry, hit two labourers in Noida's Sector 94 on Sunday. Deepak Kumar, a car dealer from Ajmer, was reportedly test driving the car when the accident occurred. Both labourers suffered leg fractures and are hospitalized. Kumar claimed a vehicle malfunction caused the accident and he was checking the car's condition for Tiwari, who plans to sell it. Mridul Tiwari, a 24-year-old Greater Noida resident with 18 million YouTube subscribers, is known for his comedy videos. The incident is under police investigation.
