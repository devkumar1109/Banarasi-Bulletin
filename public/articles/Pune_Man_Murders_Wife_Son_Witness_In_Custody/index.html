Pune Man Murders Wife, Son Witness In Custody

2025-05-06T05:54:00+00:00

A 28-year-old man from Pune's Dhayari area allegedly murdered his 26-year-old wife, Babita Nisar, by strangling her during a domestic dispute. Their eight-year-old son was present at the time. Rakesh Nisar attempted to dispose of the body by placing it in a gunny bag and carrying it on a bike. Police on night patrol spotted him around 1:30 am and apprehended him after a chase. Upon examining the bag, they discovered Babita's body. Rakesh has been arrested and charged under Section 103 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) at Nanded City Police Station.
