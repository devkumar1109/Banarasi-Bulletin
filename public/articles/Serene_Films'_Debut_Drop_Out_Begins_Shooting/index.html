Serene Films' Debut "Drop Out" Begins Shooting

2025-05-25T11:37:00+00:00

Serene Films, an Ahmedabad-based production house, began shooting its debut feature film "Drop Out" in Barabanki, Uttar Pradesh, with a traditional muhurat ceremony. The film was shot at the historic Government Inter College, Barabanki. "Drop Out," written and produced by Umashanker Yadav, who also stars in the lead role, is directed by Omkar Pethkar, who also wrote the dialogue and screenplay. The ceremony was attended by notable guests including poet and social worker Shri Pradeep Sarang, college officials, a fitness mentor, and the Director of Harakh Farmers’ Producer Company.
