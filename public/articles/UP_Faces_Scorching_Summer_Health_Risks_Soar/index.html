UP Faces Scorching Summer, Health Risks Soar

2025-04-06T06:14:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is predicted to experience a severe summer with prolonged heat waves from April to June, particularly impacting the Bundelkhand region. This intense heat poses significant risks to human and animal health. Authorities are taking steps to mitigate the effects, with district administrations implementing preventive measures and hospitals preparing for an increase in heat-related illnesses. Medical experts warn of substantial health risks due to the extreme temperatures.
