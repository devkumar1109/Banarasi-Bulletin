Mann Slams Bajwa's "Baseless" Bomb Claim

2025-04-14T15:57:00+00:00

Punjab Chief Minister Bhagwant Mann criticized Congress leader Partap Bajwa for claiming 50 bombs were smuggled into the state. Mann called the statement baseless, irrational, and aimed at terrorizing the people. He stated that neither state nor central agencies have any information to support Bajwa's claim. Mann accused Bajwa of misleading the public and urged the opposition to focus on issue-based politics instead. He attributed Bajwa's actions to envy towards his common background and commitment to public welfare. Mann emphasized the people's rejection of traditional parties and their preference for a new era of governance.
