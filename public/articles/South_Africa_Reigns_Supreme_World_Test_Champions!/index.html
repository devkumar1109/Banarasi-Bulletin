South Africa Reigns Supreme: World Test Champions!

2025-06-14T13:18:00+00:00

South Africa won the World Test Championship, defeating Australia by five wickets at Lord's. Kyle Verreynne's drive off Mitchell Starc sealed the victory, with South Africa chasing a target of 282. The win marks a historic moment for South African cricket, ending years of heartbreak and disappointment. Aiden Markram's 136 was instrumental in the chase, while Keshav Maharaj, Marco Jansen, and Kagiso Rabada celebrated a "stuff of dreams" victory. This triumph surpasses South Africa's previous achievements, including winning the Champions Trophy in 1998 and reaching the number one Test ranking twice.
