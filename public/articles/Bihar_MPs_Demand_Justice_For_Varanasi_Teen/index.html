Bihar MPs Demand Justice For Varanasi Teen

2025-03-10T08:07:00+00:00

Opposition MPs from Bihar protested outside Parliament demanding justice for a 17-year-old girl found dead in her hostel room in Varanasi. The incident, alleged to be a rape and murder, occurred in PM Modi's constituency and has drawn comparisons to the 2020 Hathras case. MPs from the RJD, CPI(ML)L, and Congress raised concerns about the police handling of the postmortem and last rites, demanding a thorough inquiry.
