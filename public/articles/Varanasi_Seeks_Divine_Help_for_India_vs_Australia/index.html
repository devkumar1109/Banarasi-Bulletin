Varanasi Seeks Divine Help for India vs. Australia

2025-03-04T05:44:00+00:00

Ahead of the ICC Champions Trophy 2025 semi-final match between India and Australia, fans in Varanasi performed a special puja at the Sarangnath Mahadev temple, praying for India's victory. Since their 2011 World Cup quarter-final win, India and Australia have faced each other four times in ICC ODI tournaments, with each team winning two matches. India's victories came in round-robin matches at the 2019 and 2023 World Cups, while Australia won the 2015 semi-final and the 2023 final. The two teams last met on November 19, 2023, and will clash again on March 4, 2025, in Dubai.
