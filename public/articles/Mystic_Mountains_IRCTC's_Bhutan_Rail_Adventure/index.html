Mystic Mountains: IRCTC's Bhutan Rail Adventure

2025-05-24T06:24:00+00:00

IRCTC is launching a 13-night, 14-day rail tour called 'Bharat-Bhutan Mystic Mountain' departing from Delhi on June 28, 2025. The tour accommodates 150 tourists and includes travel to India's Guwahati, Shillong, Cherrapunji and Bhutan's Thimphu, Punakha, and Paro. Tourists will experience spiritual and natural wonders, including the Kamakhya Temple, Umiam Lake, Seven Sisters Falls, Mawsmai Caves, Tashichho Dzong, Punakha Dzong, and Tiger's Nest Monastery. Activities include a sunset cruise on the Brahmaputra River, archery, a hot stone bath, and a cultural program. Prices range from ₹1,18,965 per person for Third AC to ₹1,58,850 for First AC (Coupe) and include train travel, 3-star hotel accommodation, and meals.
