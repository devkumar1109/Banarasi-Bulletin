UP Assembly Bans Pan Masala After Spitting Incident

2025-03-05T07:17:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Assembly has banned pan masala after members were caught spitting it inside the House. Speaker Satish Mahana expressed displeasure over the incident, stating he saw a video of a member spitting pan masala on the carpet. He acknowledged knowing the MLA's identity but refrained from naming them publicly. Mahana personally supervised the cleaning of the area and suggested taking money from the member to replace the carpet. He emphasized the Assembly's dignity and the responsibility of MLAs to uphold it, noting that 25 crore people in the state have faith in the institution.
