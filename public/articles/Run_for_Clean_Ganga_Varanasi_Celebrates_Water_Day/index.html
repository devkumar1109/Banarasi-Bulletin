Run for Clean Ganga: Varanasi Celebrates Water Day

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, "Run for Clean Ganga," and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw enthusiastic participation, with Abhishek Yadav and Rebi Pal winning the men's and women's categories respectively. The forest department celebrated World Water Day and Earth Hour at Dhakwa Ghat with Dolphin Friends, residents, and tourists. A dolphin safari was inaugurated to educate the public on dolphin conservation efforts. Ayush Minister Dayashankar Mishra Dayalu, CDO Himanshu Nagpal, and other dignitaries flagged off the marathon, emphasizing the importance of clean Ganga and public awareness.
