UP: Conclave Charts New Growth Avenues

2025-03-20T03:26:00+00:00

NDTV India will host 'Uttar Pradesh - Avsaron Ka Pradesh', a conclave in Lucknow on March 20, 2025. The event will feature policymakers like Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya, Ministers Dinesh Pratap Singh, Daya Shankar Singh and Nitin Agarwal, and Advisor Awanish Kumar Awasthi. Industry leaders, including CEO Mayank Agrawal of Gallantt Group and Professor Ratuldev Ghosh Choudhury from the Institute of Business Management, will also participate. Discussions will center on Uttar Pradesh's role in India's economic growth, focusing on sectors like culture, tourism, sports, and the state's business landscape. The conclave aims to create a roadmap for Uttar Pradesh's future and reflect on the recent Mahakumbh.
