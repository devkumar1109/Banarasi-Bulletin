UP Fights Malnutrition with New Nutrition Scheme

2025-05-03T18:36:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath launched the Mukhyamantri Suposhan Yojana to combat malnutrition. The scheme will provide nutritious morning meals to children aged 3-6 years at Anganwadi centres and expand the Take Home Ration (THR) program to all 75 districts. Adityanath emphasized incorporating local products like amla, millets, and jaggery into THR recipes. He also stressed 100% transparency and quality control in THR production, packaging, and distribution. The government will monitor stunting, underweight, and wasting among children to evaluate the scheme's effectiveness. Close coordination between local administration, health, and child development departments is crucial for successful implementation.
