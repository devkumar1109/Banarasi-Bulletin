Congress Chief's "Rafale" Toy Plane Sparks Row

2025-05-08T20:41:00+00:00

UP Congress president has been booked for a symbolic display of a toy plane model labelled "Rafale" at his Varanasi home. The display was made in response to the April 22 Pahalgam terror attack, where Rai questioned the BJP-led Centre's inaction in using the Rafale jets. He further hung "nimbu-mirchi" (lemon and chillies) on the toy plane, a common Hindu practice to ward off evil.
