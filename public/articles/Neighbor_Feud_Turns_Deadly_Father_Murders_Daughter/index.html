Neighbor Feud Turns Deadly, Father Murders Daughter

2025-03-06T18:25:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man named Mohit strangled his five-year-old daughter to death. The motive was the girl's continued visits to her neighbor's house despite Mohit's prior conflict with the neighbor. He took his daughter to a mustard field and killed her, then filed a missing person report to mislead the investigation. The child's body parts were found scattered in the field. During interrogation, Mohit confessed to the crime. Police have initiated legal proceedings against him.
