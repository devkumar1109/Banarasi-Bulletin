Love Triangle Turns Deadly in Lucknow

2025-06-16T20:26:00+00:00

A car driver, Sanjay, was murdered in Lucknow on Sunday night over an alleged love affair. Two men attacked Sanjay with a sharp weapon at his home, fatally injuring him. Sanjay was living with his childhood lover, Meera, who was also married. Police have detained four suspects, including Meera's husband, Sunil. Meera alleges that Sunil was behind the murder, driven by anger over her relationship with Sanjay. Sanjay was married for 12 years and had been estranged from his wife. Police are investigating the incident.
