Love Triangle Turns Deadly in Lucknow

2025-06-16T20:26:00+00:00

A car driver in Lucknow was murdered Sunday night over an alleged love affair. Sanjay, who was estranged from his wife and living with his lover Meera, was attacked with a banke by two men at his home. The attackers, who included Meera's husband Sunil, repeatedly struck Sanjay, despite pleas for mercy from his mother. Sanjay died after trying to escape through a nearby canal. Meera claims Sunil orchestrated the murder due to anger over her relationship with Sanjay. Police have detained four suspects, including Sunil, and are investigating the incident.
