Women Lead Charge to Clean Ganga

2025-03-01T08:45:00+00:00

A community workshop in Varanasi focused on raising awareness about Ganga water pollution and water purity. Organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation, Mothers for Mother, and OzGreen, the event brought together dozens of women to discuss solutions for preserving water resources. Environmentalist Sue Lennox presented water testing results from six locations in Kashi, highlighting the need for collective action to address Ganga pollution. Participants emphasized the crucial role women play in ensuring the cleanliness of the Ganga and protecting water sources for future generations. Prof. Vishwambharnath Mishra, President of Sankat Mochan Foundation, stressed the importance of women's active participation in freeing the Ganga from pollution.
