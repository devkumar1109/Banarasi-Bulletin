Varanasi Man Arrested for Spying for Pakistan

2025-05-22T13:02:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (UP ATS) arrested a Varanasi resident, Tufail, for allegedly spying for Pakistan. Tufail shared sensitive information, including photos of strategic locations like Rajghat, Namo Ghat, Gyanvapi, and the Red Fort, with Pakistani nationals. He was also part of WhatsApp groups where he shared videos of extremist Maulana Saad Rizvi and promoted provocative messages about revenge for Babri Masjid and Sharia law. Tufail was in contact with nearly 600 Pakistanis, including a woman named Nafisa whose husband is in the Pakistani Army. The UP ATS is investigating the full extent of Tufail's activities and network.
