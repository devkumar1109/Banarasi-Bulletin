Varanasi Man Arrested for Spying for Pakistan

2025-05-24T12:40:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorist Squad (UP ATS) arrested Mohammad Tufail in Varanasi for allegedly spying for Pakistan. Tufail joined WhatsApp groups linked to Pakistan and was instructed to contact Nafisa from Faisalabad, falling into a honey trap. He shared sensitive photos and information about locations with Nafisa and sent her gifts via Nepal and Punjab. The ATS is investigating Tufail's travel history, the nature of the sensitive material shared, and his involvement in religious gatherings. Neighbors reported large gatherings associated with the Jamaat regularly visiting Tufail's residence. Tufail created around 20 WhatsApp groups to spread provocative content and influence Muslim youths in cities like Varanasi, Mau, Azamgarh, and Lucknow. Over 500 mobile numbers linked to Tufail's WhatsApp groups are under surveillance.
