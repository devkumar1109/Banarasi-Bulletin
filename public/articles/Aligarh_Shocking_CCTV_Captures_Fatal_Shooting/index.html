Aligarh Shocking: CCTV Captures Fatal Shooting

2025-03-15T07:38:00+00:00

A 25-year-old man, Haris, was shot dead in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, on Friday morning. The incident, captured on CCTV, occurred around 3:17 am as Haris stood outside his house. Four assailants on two motorcycles opened fire on him, striking him multiple times. Haris attempted to shield himself and run but was shot down. The attackers fled the scene after firing approximately half a dozen bullets. Bystanders attempted to chase the assailants, and Haris was taken to a hospital where he succumbed to his injuries. Police are investigating the incident and attempting to identify the assailants using CCTV footage.
