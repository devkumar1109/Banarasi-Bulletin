Varanasi: 19-Year-Old Woman Gang-Raped by 23 Men

2025-04-09T04:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. The victim was found unconscious on April 4th and hospitalised. Nine accused were arrested and sent to judicial custody. The victim's father stated that she had gone to visit a friend on March 29th and came into contact with the accused. She was reportedly intoxicated and raped multiple times. The police are still searching for the remaining accused, 14 of whom are unidentified. The case has sparked outrage, with the victim's father appealing to Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath for justice.
