Man Arrested With Illegal Pistol in Nalasopara

2025-05-31T14:35:00+00:00

On May 30, 2025, Crime Branch Unit-2 of Vasai arrested a 23-year-old man, Shubham Kumar Rajnath Tiwari, in Nalasopara (East) for possessing an illegal country-made pistol and live cartridge. Acting on a tip-off, police apprehended Tiwari near Maa Vindhyavasini Mandir open ground. A search revealed the firearm and ammunition, valued at ₹45,400. A case was registered against Tiwari at Pelhar Police Station under IPC Section 3 and 25(1B)(a) of the Arms Act and Section 37(1)/135 of the Maharashtra Police Act, 1951. Authorities believe Tiwari intended to use the weapon to intimidate people in the area.
