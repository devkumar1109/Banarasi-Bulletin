Varanasi's Data-Driven Plan to Tame Crowds

2025-03-20T18:09:00+00:00

To tackle increasing crowds in Varanasi, the Varanasi Municipal Corporation presented a data-driven plan developed with Toyota Mobility Foundation, WRI India, and Urban Lab. The plan, based on three-year crowd survey data, highlights congestion hotspots like the stretch from Church to Dashashwamedh Ghat and Godowlia to Maidagin. It suggests using crowd mapping, creating a holding area in Beniabagh, and pre-assessing crowd growth. The "Sustainable Cities Challenge" workshop, involving global experts, aims to identify data-driven solutions for crowd management and pedestrian experience enhancement in Varanasi. The project emphasizes community insights to create efficient, inclusive, and sustainable solutions for Varanasi's mobility challenges.
