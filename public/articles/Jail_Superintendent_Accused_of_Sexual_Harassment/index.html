Jail Superintendent Accused of Sexual Harassment

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending inquiry into a harassment complaint lodged by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment and harassing her for not complying with his demands. She alleged Singh pressured her for sexual favors in exchange for benefits and threatened her job. Singh's actions led to Kanojia's transfer to Naini central jail in Prayagraj. Kanojia's daughter filed a police complaint against Singh, detailing similar allegations of harassment and pressure for inappropriate activities. The DIG (prisons) confirmed both Singh and Kanojia are under investigation, and future actions will be based on the findings.
