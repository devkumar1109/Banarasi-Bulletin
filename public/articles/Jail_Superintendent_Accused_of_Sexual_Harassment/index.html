Jail Superintendent Accused of Sexual Harassment

2025-03-19T16:24:00+00:00

Varanasi district jail superintendent Umesh Singh has been attached to Sonbhadra jail pending inquiry into a harassment complaint filed by his deputy jail superintendent, Meena Kanojia. Kanojia accused Singh of creating a corrupt environment and harassing her for not complying with his demands, alleging he pressured her for sexual favors in exchange for benefits. She also claimed Singh made obscene gestures and threatened her job. Singh has been replaced by Saurabh Srivastava, superintendent of Sonbhadra jail. Kanojia's daughter filed a separate complaint against Singh, detailing similar allegations of harassment and pressure. DIG (prisons) Rajesh Kumar Srivastava stated that investigations are underway against both Singh and Kanojia and further action will be taken based on the findings.
