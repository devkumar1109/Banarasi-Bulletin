IIVR Empowers UP Farmers with "Viksit Krishi Sankalp"

2025-05-30T21:36:00+00:00

The Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR) conducted a farmer awareness program, "Viksit Krishi Sankalp Abhiyan 2025," reaching 2,063 farmers across six districts in Uttar Pradesh. Over 50 scientists engaged in direct discussions with farmers, addressing challenges like nilgai menace, irregular rainfall, and market prices. Scientists provided information on natural farming, integrated pest management, and government schemes. The program covered Varanasi, Chandauli, Mirzapur, Sonbhadra, Bhadohi, and Kushinagar districts, with the highest participation in Kushinagar. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath lauded the campaign, calling it a foundation for "Viksit Bharat Abhiyan" and emphasizing the importance of scientists engaging with farmers at the grassroots level.
