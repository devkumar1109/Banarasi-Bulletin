Student Loses Eye Sight, School Clash Erupts

2025-02-27T11:04:00+00:00

A Class 3 student in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, named Himanshi, allegedly suffered vision loss in one eye after an incident at Bhogpur Mithoni Primary School. Her mother, Jyoti Kashyap, claims the principal, Geeta Karal, beat Himanshi, leading to the injury. However, the principal denies the allegations, stating that a classmate accidentally hit Himanshi in the eye with her elbow. Kashyap requested a medical certificate from the principal, which was denied. The situation escalated when Kashyap allegedly threatened the principal. The District Magistrate has initiated an inquiry into the incident.
