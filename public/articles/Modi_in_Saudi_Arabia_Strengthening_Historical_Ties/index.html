Modi in Saudi Arabia: Strengthening Historical Ties

2025-04-22T08:04:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi is on a two-day visit to Saudi Arabia at the invitation of Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman. Congress highlighted the historical ties between India and Saudi Arabia, recalling King Saud Bin Abdulaziz Al Saud's visit to India in 1955 and Jawaharlal Nehru's reciprocal trip in 1956. Modi emphasized the strategic depth and momentum gained in recent India-Saudi Arabia relations, encompassing defense, trade, investment, energy, and people-to-people ties.
