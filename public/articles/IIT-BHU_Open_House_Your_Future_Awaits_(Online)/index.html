IIT-BHU Open House: Your Future Awaits (Online)

2025-06-06T15:13:00+00:00

IIT-BHU will host an online open house session for JEE Advanced 2025 qualifiers on June 11 from 3:30 pm to 6:30 pm. The session will be chaired by IIT-BHU Director Prof Amit Patra and include key academic and administrative leaders. Prospective students can interact with the leadership team, learn about the admission process, hostel facilities, campus security, and other aspects of student life. Registration is mandatory and closes on June 10 at 6 pm. Only registered candidates will receive access to the online session via Google Meet.
