UP Manufacturing: 3 Lakh Crore Investment Boom by 2025

2025-04-09T16:07:00+00:00

The manufacturing sector in Uttar Pradesh is projected to attract an investment of Rs 3 lakh crore in the 2025-26 financial year. In 2024-25, the sector contributed Rs 2.81 lakh crore to the state's Gross State Value Addition (GSVA), achieving about 77% of the annual target. The state attracted Rs 50,000 crore in investments in the manufacturing sector during 2024-25 and recorded a 4% growth in the Index of Industrial Production (IIP). Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is focusing on developing the manufacturing sector under Invest UP 2.0 to boost the state's GSDP and create new employment opportunities. Efforts are underway to establish new manufacturing units, attract foreign direct investment (FDI), and develop land and raw material banks. Special investment regions are being designated in Aligarh, Unnao, Prayagraj, and Jhunsi.
