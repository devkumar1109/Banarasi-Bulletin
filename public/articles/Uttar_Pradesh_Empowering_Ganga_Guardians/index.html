Uttar Pradesh: Empowering Ganga Guardians

2025-03-24T13:49:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh State Clean Ganga Mission held a three-day workshop in Lucknow to improve Ganga conservation efforts. The workshop focused on empowering District Project Officers (DPOs) with training on financial, technical, and administrative aspects of project implementation. The event highlighted the importance of District Ganga Committees in maintaining the river's cleanliness and flow, stressing the need for public participation and concrete action plans. DPOs received training on budget allocation, District Ganga Committee functions, the Ganga District Project Management System (GDPMS) Portal, and wastewater management.
