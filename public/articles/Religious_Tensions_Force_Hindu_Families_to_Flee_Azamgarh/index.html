Religious Tensions Force Hindu Families to Flee Azamgarh

2025-06-16T04:25:00+00:00

In Azamgarh's Chhota Pura, around 40 Hindu families are selling their homes due to alleged harassment and violence stemming from playing music during religious events. The families claim the district administration failed to address their concerns after a June 3 clash between two communities. Police have assured increased security presence in the area following reports of the planned migration. Azamgarh ASP Madhuvan Kumar Singh confirmed they have contacted the families and promised adequate police deployment to ensure their safety.
