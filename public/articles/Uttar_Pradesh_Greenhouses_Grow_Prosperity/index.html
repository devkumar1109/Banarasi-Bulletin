Uttar Pradesh: Greenhouses Grow Prosperity

2025-06-11T14:58:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is promoting agricultural innovation by encouraging farmers to adopt greenhouse and polyhouse technology in 44 districts. These structures allow for year-round cultivation of vegetables and grains, protecting crops from extreme weather. The government is subsidizing construction costs, offering up to 50% grants, and providing technical training and expert guidance. Construction is underway or complete in 24 districts, with locations like Lucknow, Meerut, and Agra already having operational facilities. This initiative aims to boost farm income and ensure year-round availability of fresh produce.
