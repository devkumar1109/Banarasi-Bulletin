Varanasi Celebrates Women's Day: Equality, Empowerment Shine

2025-03-08T20:02:00+00:00

Various institutions in Varanasi celebrated International Women's Day with programs focusing on equality and empowerment. Mahila Mahavidyalaya, BHU, hosted a workshop and panel discussion on empowering women and girls. BHU also organized sports events and an oratory competition. DAV PG College held a seminar on gender equality, while Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW) hosted a ceremony with cultural performances highlighting women's achievements. Vishal Bharat Sansthan's Women's Council discussed the role of women in culture and tradition and addressed the undervaluation of domestic work.
