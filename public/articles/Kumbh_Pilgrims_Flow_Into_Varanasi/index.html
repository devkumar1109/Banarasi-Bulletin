Kumbh Pilgrims Flow Into Varanasi

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela concludes in Prayagraj on Maha Shivratri, Wednesday, prompting Varanasi to prepare for a surge in pilgrims. Officials anticipate heavy crowds as pilgrims from Prayagraj, where a massive final holy bath is expected, will travel to Varanasi to visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple. The Varanasi administration and police have implemented comprehensive crowd and traffic management plans, including restrictions on vehicle entry and traffic diversions. Holding areas have been designated at railway stations to manage passenger flow. Security arrangements are in place to ensure smooth public movement and prevent congestion.
