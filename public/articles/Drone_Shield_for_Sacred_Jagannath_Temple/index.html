Drone Shield for Sacred Jagannath Temple

2025-05-23T07:51:00+00:00

The Odisha government is installing anti-drone technology at the Shree Jagannath Temple in Puri to enhance security. Discussions between the Law Department and the State Police Department are in advanced stages, with a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) already drafted. The system will intercept and disable any unauthorized drones within a specified radius of the temple. The Jagannath Temple Administration is prepared to financially support the project. The move aims to prevent aerial threats and safeguard the temple's sanctity.
