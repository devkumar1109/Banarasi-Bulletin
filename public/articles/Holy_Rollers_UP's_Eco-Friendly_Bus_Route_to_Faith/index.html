Holy Rollers: UP's Eco-Friendly Bus Route to Faith

2025-04-22T17:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will launch an electric AC bus service connecting major religious cities like Kashi, Prayagraj, Ayodhya, and Sonbhadra. Five charging stations, four in Varanasi and one in Sonbhadra, will be operational within a month. Each bus can travel up to 150 kilometers on a single charge, taking 30 minutes to fully recharge. Equipped with amenities like CCTV cameras, GPS systems, and panic buttons, the air-conditioned buses will seat 28 passengers and offer eco-friendly travel for devotees while reducing pollution.
