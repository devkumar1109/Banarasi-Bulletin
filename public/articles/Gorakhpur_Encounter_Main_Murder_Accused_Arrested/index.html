Gorakhpur Encounter: Main Murder Accused Arrested

2025-06-10T18:01:00+00:00

Jugal Kishore, the prime accused in the murder of history-sheeter Dinesh Nishad, was arrested following a police encounter in Gorakhpur. Nishad was shot dead on June 7 by his cousins allegedly over a land dispute. Acting on a tip-off, police apprehended Jugal, who opened fire and was injured in the leg. A pistol and two empty cartridges were recovered from the scene. Jugal was taken to a hospital for treatment. Police said 11 people, including eight named accused, were involved in the murder.
