Gorakhpur Stadium: Yogi's Dream Takes Shape

2025-05-19T14:03:00+00:00

Construction of Gorakhpur International Cricket Stadium, a dream project of Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, has begun. The stadium, located on a 50-acre site at Tal Nadaur, Gorakhpur, is expected to be completed within 18 months with an investment of Rs 236.40 crore. The main stadium complex will cover 45 acres and have a seating capacity of approximately 30,000 spectators. It will feature seven main pitches and four practice pitches, meeting global standards including those of the ICC. The stadium, situated 23.6 km from the airport, will be connected to the Gorakhpur-Varanasi Highway (NH-24). Parking facilities will accommodate up to 1,500 vehicles and the east and west stands will each have a seating capacity of 14,490.
