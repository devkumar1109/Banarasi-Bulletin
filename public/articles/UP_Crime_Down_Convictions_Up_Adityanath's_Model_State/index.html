UP Crime Down, Convictions Up: Adityanath's "Model" State

2025-02-24T13:37:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state's law and order situation is a "model" for the country, citing a decline in crime rates according to NCRB data. He highlights a significant increase in convictions for crimes against women, with 51 death sentences and 6,287 life sentences handed down under the "Operation Conviction" initiative between July 2023 and December 2024. Adityanath credits the strengthened Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) with arresting 130 terrorists and 171 Rohingyas since 2017. He also points to police reforms, including the implementation of the police commissionerate system in major cities and the establishment of three new women PAC battalions.
