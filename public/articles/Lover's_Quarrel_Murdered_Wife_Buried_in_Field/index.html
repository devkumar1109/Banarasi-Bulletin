Lover's Quarrel: Murdered Wife Buried in Field

2025-04-12T06:43:00+00:00

A man in Uttar Pradesh murdered his wife and buried her body in a field. The arrest was made following a tip-off from the man's brother. Investigations revealed the man was having an affair with a differently-abled woman, a relationship his wife strongly opposed. A heated argument between the couple on the night of the incident led to the fatal assault.
