Uttar Pradesh: Unveiling India's Soul in Bangkok

2025-06-06T10:54:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government will showcase its tourism potential at the PATA Travel Mart in Bangkok from August 26 to 28. The state will highlight its cultural, spiritual, and historical assets through a 36-square-meter stall featuring iconic sites like Sarnath and Varanasi. Digital displays, including LED walls and Augmented Reality panels, will offer visitors an immersive experience. Uttar Pradesh will also conduct a promotional campaign in Bangkok, including a roadshow targeting over 100 travel industry and media stakeholders. Tourism ads will air during peak hours, and promotional content will be displayed in key locations.
