JEE Main 2025 Session 2 Results Out: Top IITs Revealed

2025-04-19T07:54:00+00:00

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has declared the JEE Main 2025 Session 2 results. Candidates can access their scorecards, final answer key and results at jeemain.nta.nic.in using their application number and date of birth. The April session of JEE Main 2025 was conducted from April 2 to 9 for various papers, including BE/BTech, BArch, and BPlanning. JEE Advanced 2025 will see a return to the pre-pandemic format, with the optional question structure in Section B being eliminated. Each subject will have five numerical questions in Section B, totaling 25. Based on NIRF 2024 rankings, the top 10 IITs are: IIT Madras, IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, IIT BHU, and IIT Mandi.
