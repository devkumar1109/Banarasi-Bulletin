Kannauj Woman Murdered, Robbery Suspected

2025-05-04T14:30:00+00:00

A 40-year-old woman was murdered in Kannauj, Uttar Pradesh, and her body was found in a farm. The body showed injuries suggesting physical assault. Bloodstains and broken bangles indicate violence. A post-mortem will confirm if she was sexually assaulted. Police are investigating the motive, considering family disputes or property issues. Her saree is missing, and villagers claim her earrings were pulled. Robbery may be a motive, but police are yet to confirm. This incident follows another case where a father killed his daughter in Asare village and tried to implicate her lover.
