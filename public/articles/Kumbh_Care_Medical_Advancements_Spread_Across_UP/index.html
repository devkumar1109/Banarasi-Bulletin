Kumbh Care: Medical Advancements Spread Across UP

2025-03-05T09:07:00+00:00

World-class medical equipment from the Maha Kumbh Mela Central Hospital in Prayagraj will be distributed to districts across Uttar Pradesh to enhance patient care. The equipment includes advanced machines, beds, and a 10-bed AI-enabled ICU. The distribution is pending approval from the Prayagraj divisional Commissioner and will be based on requests from district Chief Medical Officers. The Central Hospital, which served over 6 lakh patients during the Maha Kumbh, also deployed a special hi-tech mic that translated 22 regional and 19 international languages into Hindi or English. This technology will also be made available in other hospitals throughout Uttar Pradesh.
