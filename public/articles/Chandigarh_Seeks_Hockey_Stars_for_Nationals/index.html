Chandigarh Seeks Hockey Stars for Nationals

2025-03-16T21:45:00+00:00

Hockey Chandigarh will hold trials for its men's senior team on March 17th at 2 pm at the Sector 42 Sports Complex in Chandigarh. The trials aim to select players for the 15th Hockey India Senior National Championship, scheduled for April 4th to 15th in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. Only players registered with the local association are eligible to participate.
