UP Delivers Clean Water, Booms Jobs and Villages

2025-04-09T19:21:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has achieved 100% clean tap water access in 24,576 villages, benefitting nearly 4.86 crore villagers. This was accomplished through 79,44,896 new water connections. Mirzapur district leads with 1,769 fully covered villages, followed by Gorakhpur (1,372), Kushinagar (693), Hardoi (651), and Prayagraj (639). The initiative not only provides clean drinking water but also creates jobs. Each village employs five women facilitating connections and 13 individuals as plumbers, fitters, and electricians, generating livelihood opportunities for 18 people per village.
