India's TB Hunt Uncovers 3 Lakh New Cases

2025-03-25T12:20:00+00:00

The Union Minister of State for Health, Dr. Bharati Pawar, announced that 3.01 lakh new tuberculosis (TB) cases have been identified under a 100-day control campaign. This initiative aims to expedite the detection and treatment of TB cases across India.
