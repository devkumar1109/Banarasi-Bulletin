Temple Priest's Home Burglarized, Six Arrested

2025-05-21T18:09:00+00:00

Six people were arrested for the burglary of Sankat Mochan temple priest Prof Vishwambhar Nath Mishra's residence in Varanasi. The thieves stole jewelry worth over Rs 1 crore and Rs 3 lakh cash. Three of the six accused were injured in an exchange of fire with police during a raid in Domri area. One accused managed to escape. All the arrested accused had previously worked at the priest's home. Police recovered evidence and identified the injured as Vicky Tiwari, Jitendra Singh alias Golu Patel, and Rakesh Dubey, all from Bihar. The other arrested accused are Dilip alias Bansi Chaubey, Atul Shukla, and Shani.
