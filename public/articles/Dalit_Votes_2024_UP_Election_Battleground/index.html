Dalit Votes: 2024 UP Election Battleground

2025-04-20T16:39:00+00:00

The race for Dalit outreach is intensifying in Uttar Pradesh ahead of the 2024 Lok Sabha elections. Political parties are vying for the support of the Dalit community by highlighting their welfare schemes and making promises. The BJP, currently in power, is emphasizing its work on social justice and economic development for Dalits. Opposition parties, including the Samajwadi Party and the Bahujan Samaj Party, are focusing on addressing the historical injustices faced by Dalits and promising greater empowerment. The outcome of Dalit voter support could significantly influence the election results.
