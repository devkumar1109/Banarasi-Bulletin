Uttar Pradesh: Governor Pushes for Transgender Empowerment

2025-06-16T14:59:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel has called for improved implementation of welfare schemes for the transgender community. During a review meeting, she stressed economic empowerment, financial inclusion, and enhanced outreach to improve their living standards. Patel urged the establishment of 100 health centers to address health issues like HIV among transgender individuals. She also emphasized the need for more employment opportunities for this marginalized group. The Governor aims to integrate transgender individuals into mainstream society by focusing on their social and economic well-being.
