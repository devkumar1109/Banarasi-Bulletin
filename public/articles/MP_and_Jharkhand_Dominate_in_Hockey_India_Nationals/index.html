MP and Jharkhand Dominate in Hockey India Nationals

2025-03-06T03:14:00+00:00

Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand secured victories in Division A matches of the 15th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship in Panchkula. Madhya Pradesh defeated Uttar Pradesh 1-0 in a close encounter. Neelu Dadiya scored the winning goal for Madhya Pradesh in the 53rd minute, converting a penalty corner. Jharkhand triumphed over Punjab 4-1.
