Yogi Flags Off Lucknow's Rs 7,000 Crore Anant Nagar

2025-04-04T11:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the 800-acre Anant Nagar housing scheme in Lucknow, a project worth nearly Rs 7,000 crore. Located near the international airport, expressway, and Lucknow Ring Road, Anant Nagar will feature high-rise buildings, affordable housing plots, and spaces for educational institutions. The scheme aims to accommodate 1.5 lakh people, including 10,000 flats and seven plotted sectors. 5,000 housing units will be reserved for economically weaker sections (EWS) and lower-income groups (LIG), with 3,000 units under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY). Anant Nagar will also include a 130-acre park and an EduTech City, providing residents access to education and healthcare facilities.
