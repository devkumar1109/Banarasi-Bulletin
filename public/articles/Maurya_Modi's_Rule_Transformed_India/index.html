Maurya: Modi's Rule Transformed India

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

UP Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya hailed Prime Minister Modi's 11-year rule, claiming the government transformed service into commitment, good governance into culture, and prioritized security. He stated that 27 crore people rose above the poverty line and received basic amenities. Maurya criticized Congress and Samajwadi Party leaders, alleging they harbor anti-national sentiments. He accused Rahul Gandhi of speaking Pakistan's language and viewing Indian politics from a Chinese perspective. Maurya highlighted India's advancements in security, economy, technology, infrastructure, and space science under Modi's leadership. He mentioned India's self-reliance in defense, strengthened by indigenous weapons, and successful anti-terrorism operations.
