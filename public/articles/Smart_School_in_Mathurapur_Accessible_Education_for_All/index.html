Smart School in Mathurapur: Accessible Education for All

2025-03-18T07:17:00+00:00

A new, high-tech government school has been built in Mathurapur, Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, with an investment of Rs 1.30 crore. The school, part of the 'Operation Kayakalp' scheme, will be inaugurated on March 19 by State Minister for Basic Education Sandeep Singh. The school features smart classrooms with digital boards, well-equipped laboratories, and resources for inclusive education. It aims to enroll over 150 students in the next academic session. Designed as a 'Divyang-friendly' establishment, the school has ramps, railings, and assistive devices to cater to students with special needs. Specially trained educators will provide tailored support. The school prioritizes hygiene with RO-purified drinking water and separate restrooms for boys and girls. Minister Singh emphasizes the school's alignment with Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's vision of 'Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas,' promoting equitable educational opportunities.
