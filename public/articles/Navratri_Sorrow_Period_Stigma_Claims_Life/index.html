Navratri Sorrow: Period Stigma Claims Life.

2025-04-05T06:51:00+00:00

A 36-year-old woman in Uttar Pradesh, Priyansha Soni, died by suicide after beginning her menstrual cycle on the first day of the Navratri festival. Priyansha had been eagerly anticipating the nine-day festival and had prepared for the religious rituals. Due to her period, she felt unable to participate, leading to distress as menstruation is often considered impure in many traditions. Her husband, Mukesh Soni, tried to comfort her and offered to perform the rituals in her place, but she remained deeply upset. After breaking down in tears, she consumed poison and died.
