Yadav: BJP's Caste Bias in Ansari Disqualification

2025-06-06T11:50:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav accuses the Uttar Pradesh government of caste bias following the disqualification of SBSP MLA Abbas Ansari from the state assembly. Ansari was convicted in a hate speech case and sentenced to two years imprisonment, leading to his disqualification under the Representation of the People Act. Yadav alleges the BJP is selectively targeting Ansari due to his caste and past remarks by Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak. Yadav questions why government officials are not similarly held accountable for their own provocative statements.
