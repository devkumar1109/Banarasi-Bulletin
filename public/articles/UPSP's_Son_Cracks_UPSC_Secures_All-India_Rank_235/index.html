UPSP's Son Cracks UPSC, Secures All-India Rank 235

2025-04-25T09:27:00+00:00

Soham Lal, son of Uttar Pradesh Superintendent of Police Shailendra Lal and grandson of former Inspector General Chhote Lal Gyani, secured an All-India Rank of 235 in the UPSC CSE 2024. Hailing from Orai, Jalaun district, Soham is a graduate in Economics from Hindu College, Delhi University. This is his fourth attempt at the exam. Soham's sister, Shreya Utkarsh, is an engineer working in Germany.
