FIR at Your Doorstep: UP Police's New Initiative

2025-04-20T18:13:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police have launched a new initiative in Ghaziabad to deliver FIR copies directly to complainants' homes. This move aims to reduce the burden on victims, particularly women and senior citizens, who often face difficulties visiting police stations. The initiative was piloted over the weekend, with a resident named Chetna Mittal receiving her FIR copy at her doorstep after filing a complaint for stolen jewelry. Commissioner of Police J. Ravindra Gaur instructed the implementation of this citizen-friendly step to ensure victims feel supported and heard throughout the process.
