Gorakhpur Gets Ready for Global Cricket

2025-05-19T14:03:00+00:00

Construction of the Gorakhpur International Cricket Stadium has officially begun in Uttar Pradesh. The stadium, a dream project of Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, is expected to be completed within 18 months at a cost of Rs 236.40 crore. The 50-acre project will feature a 45-acre main stadium complex with seven main pitches and four practice pitches, accommodating 30,000 spectators. Located 23.6 km from the airport and near major transportation hubs, the stadium will be accessible via a connecting road to NH-24. Designed to meet global standards, including those of the ICC, the stadium will host international cricket matches and other large-scale events.
