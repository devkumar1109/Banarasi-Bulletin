UP Seeks to Scale Direct-Seeded Rice Technology

2025-05-27T13:17:00+00:00

On May 27, 2025, Uttar Pradesh Principal Secretary Anurag Yadav and a World Bank delegation visited the International Rice Research Institute – South Asia Regional Centre (ISARC) to discuss scaling Direct-Seeded Rice (DSR) technology in the state. Discussions focused on scalable strategies, convergence opportunities, and capacity-building to mainstream DSR across diverse agro-ecological zones. Key areas included enhancing seed systems, farmer training, policy alignment, and strengthening public-private partnerships. The visit follows a field trip to Paniyara village in Varanasi, and builds on the ongoing partnership between the Uttar Pradesh government, the World Bank, and IRRI to promote sustainable rice production.
