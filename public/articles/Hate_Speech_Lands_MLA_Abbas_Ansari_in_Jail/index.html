Hate Speech Lands MLA Abbas Ansari in Jail

2025-06-01T19:36:00+00:00

SBSP MLA Abbas Ansari was sentenced to two years imprisonment for making hate speech during the 2022 Assembly election campaign in Mau Sadar. The Chief Judicial Magistrate (MP-MLA) court stated that hate speech in public service, especially during elections, is unacceptable and can influence voters and officials. The court emphasized the seriousness of Abbas's threat to hold government officials accountable after the election, which could have disrupted communal harmony. Considering his criminal history and the severity of the offense, the court deemed a stricter punishment appropriate.
