RCB Celebrations Turn Tragic: Stampede Kills 11.

2025-06-05T05:19:00+00:00

A stampede during RCB's IPL victory celebrations in Bengaluru resulted in 11 deaths and 33 injuries. The incident occurred near the Chinnaswamy Cricket Stadium, where over 3 lakh people gathered despite the stadium's 35,000 capacity. Karnataka BJP President B Y Vijayendra criticized Deputy Chief Minister D K Shivakumar for claiming the government did not anticipate the crowd, accusing the government of prioritizing publicity over public safety. Vijayendra suggested the government learn crowd management from Uttar Pradesh, citing the successful management of the Kumbh Mela.
