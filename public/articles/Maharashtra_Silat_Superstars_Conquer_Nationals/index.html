Maharashtra Silat Superstars Conquer Nationals

2025-05-22T13:39:00+00:00

Maharashtra won the Overall Champion Trophy at the 13th Senior National Pencak Silat Championship held in Lucknow from May 9 to 12. The championship saw over 800 athletes from 28 states, 8 union territories, and the country's defense forces. Maharashtra topped the medal tally with 6 gold, 4 silver, and 4 bronze medals. Jammu & Kashmir secured the first runner-up position with 3 gold, 6 silver, and 10 bronze medals, while Uttar Pradesh came in second with 3 gold, 3 silver, and 6 bronze medals. The prize distribution ceremony was attended by dignitaries including Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brijesh Pathak and National President of the Indian Pencak Silat Federation Kishore Prakash Yewale. Top performers from the championship will represent their states in the upcoming Khelo India Beach Games.
