Four Friends Die in Gorakhpur-Varanasi Highway Crash

2025-05-31T11:05:00+00:00

Four friends from Sidhuapar village, Sunil Kumar, Pradumna Kumar, Arvind Kumar, and Rahul Kumar, died in a road accident on the Gorakhpur-Varanasi highway. The accident occurred around 9:30 am on Saturday when the friends were returning home on a single Apache bike and were hit by a speeding Grand Vitara car coming from Gorakhpur. The impact of the collision was severe, shattering both the bike and the car. Sunil, Pradumna, and Arvind died on the spot, while Rahul was critically injured and referred to the district hospital. Arvind had only recently returned from Bangkok a week prior. The accident has sparked concerns about road safety, particularly regarding speeding vehicles.
