Yogi Preps Varanasi for Modi's April Visit

2025-04-03T19:03:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reviewed preparations for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's visit to Varanasi in the second week of April. Yogi inspected the public meeting venue and directed officials to ensure security, crowd control, and adequate facilities like parking, toilets, and drinking water. He stressed the speedy revamp of cremation ghats, including Manikarnika and Harischandra ghats, before the monsoon. He also directed quality checks of the newly constructed Namo Ghat after a portion of its floor caved in. Yogi emphasized beautification of the Varuna Riverfront, expansion of the National Forensic University, and road widening in Dalmandi. He called for cleanliness campaigns, tap water access in all villages, and a ban on single-use plastics.
