Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Allegedly Gang-Rape Woman Seven Days

2025-04-07T10:10:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly gang-raped by over 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned on April 4. The survivor reported being taken to hotels and hookah bars where she was assaulted. Police have arrested six accused and registered a case under the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita (BNS) against all 23. The girl's family alleges she was drugged, confined, and repeatedly raped by different groups of men in various locations. She managed to escape on several occasions but was repeatedly abducted and assaulted. An investigation is ongoing to apprehend the remaining accused.
