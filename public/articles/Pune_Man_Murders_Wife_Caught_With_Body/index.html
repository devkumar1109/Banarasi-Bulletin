Pune Man Murders Wife, Caught With Body

2025-05-06T05:54:00+00:00

A 28-year-old man from Uttar Pradesh, residing in Pune's Dhayari area, murdered his 26-year-old wife, Babita, by strangling her. The incident occurred following a domestic dispute in the presence of their eight-year-old son. He attempted to dispose of the body by placing it in a gunny bag and transporting it on a bike. Police apprehended him at 1.30 am while on night patrol, discovering the body upon examining the bag. A case under Section 103 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) has been registered against the man at Nanded City Police Station, and further investigation is ongoing.
