False Bomb Threat Grounds Flight, Passengers Delayed

2025-04-27T19:10:00+00:00

A Canadian national, Yohanathan Nishanth, was arrested at Varanasi airport after claiming to have a bomb on a Bengaluru-bound IndiGo flight. The flight was grounded, passengers were evacuated, and a thorough search was conducted. No explosives were found. Nishanth, who appeared intoxicated, made the claim while the flight was moving from the apron to the runway after shifting seats and becoming agitated when instructed to return. The incident caused a five-hour delay, with passengers spending the night at the terminal. The flight eventually departed for Bengaluru the following morning after receiving clearance from security agencies.
