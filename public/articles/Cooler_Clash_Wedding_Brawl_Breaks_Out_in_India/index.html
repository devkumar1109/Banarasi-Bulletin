Cooler Clash: Wedding Brawl Breaks Out in India

2025-06-02T07:25:00+00:00

A wedding in Uttar Pradesh, India, erupted into chaos when guests began fighting over a cooler. The altercation started after guests from the groom's side blocked the airflow, leading to a heated argument with the bride's family. The brawl involved punches, kicks, and chairs being hurled at each other. A video of the incident went viral on social media, drawing amusement and criticism from internet users. The wedding took place in Sipri Bazar, Jhansi, on May 28th.
