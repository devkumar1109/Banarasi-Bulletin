Heat Scorches North, Rain Brings Relief South

2025-04-07T05:09:00+00:00

A heatwave is affecting Delhi-NCR, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Gujarat, and Rajasthan. However, light to moderate rain is forecast in Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, and the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, offering a respite from the heat. Northeast India is also expected to receive light to moderate showers, and heavy rainfall is predicted in parts of Assam, Meghalaya, and Bihar on April 8. Recommended destinations to escape the heat include: Karnataka (Hampi, Mysore Palace, Coorg, Bengaluru), Kerala (Munnar, Alleppey, Kovalam Beach, Periyar National Park), Tamil Nadu (Mahabalipuram, Meenakshi Temple, Ooty, Chennai, Kanyakumari), and the Andaman and Nicobar Islands (Radhanagar Beach, Port Blair’s Cellular Jail).
