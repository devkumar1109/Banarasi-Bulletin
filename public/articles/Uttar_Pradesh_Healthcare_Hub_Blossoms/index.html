Uttar Pradesh: Healthcare Hub Blossoms

2025-03-18T06:09:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has transformed into a healthcare hub with 80 operational medical colleges (44 government, 36 private) and a growing network of hospitals. The state has seen significant improvements in medical infrastructure, including an increase in medical seats (5,250 in government colleges, 6,550 in private undergraduate courses), new super-specialty hospitals, and research institutes. The government is promoting AYUSH medicine through initiatives like establishing an Ayurvedic College in Ayodhya and a Homeopathic Medical College in Varanasi. Uttar Pradesh now has 2,110 Ayurvedic, 254 Unani, and 1,585 Homeopathic hospitals.
