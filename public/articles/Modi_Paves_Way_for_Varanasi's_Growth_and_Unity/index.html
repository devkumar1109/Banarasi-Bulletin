Modi Paves Way for Varanasi's Growth and Unity

2025-04-11T16:11:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for 44 projects worth Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, his parliamentary constituency. He emphasized his government's commitment to inclusive development, contrasting it with the opposition's alleged focus on family-centric policies. Modi highlighted Varanasi's transformation over the past decade, citing advancements in healthcare and its emergence as a regional health hub. He mentioned the establishment of major hospitals from Delhi and Mumbai, improving medical access and patient dignity. Modi praised Varanasi's role in driving Purvanchal's development and its embodiment of India's cultural diversity. He also underscored initiatives like Kashi Tamil Sangamam, fostering national unity.
