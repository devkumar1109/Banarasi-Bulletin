Varanasi on High Alert: Leopard Lures Fear

2025-05-27T20:59:00+00:00

A leopard attack in Varanasi last week left three people injured and prompted a massive search operation. The leopard was last seen hiding in bushes on Friday but evaded capture despite attempts by forest officials to trap it. Authorities suspect the leopard entered Varanasi from Chandauli or the Kaimur Wildlife Sanctuary. The injured individuals are hospitalized in stable condition. Joint forest teams from Varanasi and Chandauli are conducting searches and advising villagers to remain vigilant. 12 villages along the Ganga river, with a combined population of 18,000, are on high alert, and villagers are taking precautions like night vigils and setting firecrackers to deter the animal.
