BJP Leader Killed in UP Road Accident

2025-05-22T13:35:00+00:00

BJP leader Purnamasi Chauhan, 65, died in a road accident in Uttar Pradesh's Deoria district on Thursday. Chauhan and his relative Kundan were on a motorcycle when a speeding car struck them near Shahpur Godam. The car driver fled the scene. Chauhan held several political positions, including twice serving as mandal general secretary and once as mandal president of Desahi Deoria. At the time of the accident, he was sector in-charge of Desahi Mandal. Kundan sustained critical injuries and was referred to a medical college in Gorakhpur.
