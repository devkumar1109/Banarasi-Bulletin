Varanasi's Crowded Streets: Data-Driven Solutions

2025-03-20T18:09:00+00:00

The Varanasi Municipal Corporation presented a data-driven plan to manage the city's increasing crowds. Developed by Toyota Mobility Foundation, WRI India, and Urban Lab, the plan analyzed crowd density over the past three years, identifying areas like Godowlia to Maidagin and the Assi area as most congested. The plan suggests creating a holding area in Beniabagh and using crowd mapping to test data. It aims to enhance mobility planning, improve accessibility, and transform pedestrian experiences. The 'Sustainable Cities Challenge' workshop, involving 10 global experts, focuses on data-driven solutions for managing crowd movement and pedestrian experiences in Varanasi.
