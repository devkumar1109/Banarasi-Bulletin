UP Police Welcomes 60,000 New Recruits, Women Lead the Way

2025-06-14T05:14:00+00:00

Union Home Minister Amit Shah and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath will distribute appointment letters to 60,244 candidates recruited as UP Police constables on June 15th. This includes 12,048 women, marking a significant increase in female representation within the force. This recruitment drive is part of a larger effort to provide government jobs in Uttar Pradesh, with 8.50 lakh individuals securing positions in the past eight years. The UP Police conducted one of the largest and most transparent recruitment drives in the country, involving 1,174 examination centers across 67 districts.
