Vance-Zelensky Clash: White House Tensions Flare.

2025-03-16T17:29:00+00:00

US Vice President JD Vance denied allegations that his argument with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky at the White House was staged. Vance stated he intervened only after Zelensky lost his temper, leading to Zelensky being asked to leave. Vance accused Zelensky of gambling with the lives of millions and being disrespectful to the US. Zelensky argued that Ukraine has been standing strong since the war's start and has been alone but safe. The heated exchange has sparked discussions about the Biden administration's changing stance on Ukraine and raised questions about US-Ukraine relations and future support. Analysts are speculating if this incident signals a shift in US policy or support for Ukraine.
