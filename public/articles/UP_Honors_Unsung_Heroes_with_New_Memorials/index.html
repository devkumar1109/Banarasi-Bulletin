U.P. Honors Unsung Heroes with New Memorials

2025-06-10T14:02:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced plans to build a memorial for Dalit icon Maharaja Bijli Pasi in Lucknow. This move recognizes historical figures often overlooked. Adityanath also inaugurated Maharaja Suheldev's statue and highlighted the BJP government's commitment to honoring unsung heroes. Other tributes include medical colleges and battalions named after Rani Lakshmibai and Avantibai Lodhi. A cultural center commemorating B R Ambedkar is also underway to provide educational opportunities to SC/ST students.
