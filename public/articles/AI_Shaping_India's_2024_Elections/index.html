AI: Shaping India's 2024 Elections

2025-04-14T03:35:00+00:00

AI played a significant role in the 2024 Lok Sabha elections. Political parties, including the BJP, utilized AI for language translation, as demonstrated by Prime Minister Modi's use of 'Bhashini' during a speech. AI-powered tools were also employed to create deepfakes, generate robocalls (over 50 million in the two months leading up to the elections), and spread misinformation. These technologies were used to target opponents, enhance politicians' images, and influence public perception.
