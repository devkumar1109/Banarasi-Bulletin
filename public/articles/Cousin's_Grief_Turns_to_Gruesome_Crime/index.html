Cousin's Grief Turns to Gruesome Crime

2025-05-23T02:06:00+00:00

A 13-year-old girl in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly raped by her 19-year-old cousin. The accused, who recently visited the girl's family following his wife's death, asked the girl to help with cooking and stayed overnight at her residence. He allegedly entered the girl's room while the family slept and raped her. When she tried to scream, he threatened her and beat her. The girl later complained of stomach pain and a doctor's examination revealed sexual assault. The accused has been booked for rape under relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and police are pursuing his arrest.
