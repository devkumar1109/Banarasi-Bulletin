Cousin's Grief Turns to Gruesome Crime

2025-05-23T02:06:00+00:00

A 13-year-old girl in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, was allegedly raped by her 19-year-old cousin. The accused, who recently visited the girl's family following his wife's death, asked the girl to help with cooking. He stayed overnight at her residence and allegedly raped her while she slept. The girl was threatened into silence and later complained of stomach ache, leading to a doctor's visit where the assault was discovered. The accused was booked for rape and under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. Police are pursuing his arrest and further investigation is ongoing.
