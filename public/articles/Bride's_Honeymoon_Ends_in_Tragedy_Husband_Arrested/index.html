Bride's Honeymoon Ends in Tragedy, Husband Arrested

2025-05-18T07:10:00+00:00

A 44-year-old man in Uttar Pradesh's Varanasi, Raju Pal, murdered his third wife, Aarti Pal (26), just one week after their wedding. The couple had been experiencing clashes since the marriage. On Thursday night, Raju brutally assaulted Aarti, leading to her death at Narpatpur Community Health Centre. Raju has been arrested, and Aarti's body has been sent for post-mortem.
