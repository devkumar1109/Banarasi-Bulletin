Blackmail Leads to Tragedy: Boys, 12, Kill 11-Year-Old

2025-05-31T10:26:00+00:00

Two 12-year-old boys in Uttar Pradesh have been detained for the murder of an 11-year-old boy. Police revealed that the motive behind the killing was blackmail. The 11-year-old had threatened to expose one of the suspects' misconduct with a girl to his family. During questioning, both suspects confessed to the crime.
