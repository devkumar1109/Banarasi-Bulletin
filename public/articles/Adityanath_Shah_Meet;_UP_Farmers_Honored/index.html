Adityanath, Shah Meet; UP Farmers Honored

2025-06-10T06:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath met Union Home Minister Amit Shah in New Delhi on Monday. Adityanath also addressed the Kisan Samman Samaroh in Auraiya, highlighting government initiatives to support farmers since 2014. These include the Soil Health Card Scheme, PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, and a loan waiver for 86 lakh farmers in 2017. Adityanath emphasized the importance of farmers in the government's political agenda under Prime Minister Modi. PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, which benefits 12 crore farmers nationwide, has become the world's largest direct benefit transfer scheme, transferring over Rs 3.25 lakh crore to farmers' bank accounts.
