UP Budget Exceeds Rs 8 Lakh Crore, Passes with Majority

2025-03-04T14:35:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Assembly has approved a budget exceeding Rs 8 lakh crore for the 2025-26 financial year. The budget, presented by Finance Minister Suresh Kumar Khanna on February 20th, was passed with a clear majority after a discussion and vote. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath supported the budget and urged the House to approve it. The Assembly Speaker, Satish Mahana, formally announced the budget's passage.
