Uttar Pradesh Greening for a Sustainable Future

2025-04-22T13:55:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is implementing various initiatives to promote environmental sustainability. These include using the Miyawaki method for rapid afforestation, aiming to absorb significant amounts of carbon dioxide. To combat plastic pollution, the state plans to construct roads using single-use plastic, repurposing 2,000 tonnes of waste. The government is also encouraging solar energy adoption through the 'PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana,' which has already provided over 10 lakh solar-powered homes. An arms licensing scheme promotes tree planting, and educational initiatives on environmental awareness are being conducted among school children.
