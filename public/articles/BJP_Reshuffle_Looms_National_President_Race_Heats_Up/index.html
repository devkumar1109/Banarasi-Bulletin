BJP Reshuffle Looms: National President Race Heats Up

2025-04-16T18:36:00+00:00

The BJP will begin its national president election process after April 20. Key state president announcements are expected soon for Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, and West Bengal. A recent meeting led by PM Modi focused on organizational reshuffling and the national president election. JP Nadda's term as national president, extended until June 2024, will end soon. The BJP has already appointed state presidents in several states, but major states like Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka, and West Bengal are yet to have new leaders.
