India Scorches: 20+ Cities Hit 40°C

2025-04-21T15:18:00+00:00

Over 20 Indian cities experienced temperatures exceeding 40 degrees Celsius on Monday, marking the hottest day of the season in many areas. Prayagraj was the hottest, recording 44.3 degrees Celsius, followed by Kanpur at 44 degrees Celsius. Other cities surpassing 40 degrees included Varanasi, Jhansi, Hamirpur, Churk, Sultanpur, Agra, Banda, and Lucknow. No respite from the heat is expected in the coming days, with the weather department predicting high temperatures and a heat wave warning for southern regions.
