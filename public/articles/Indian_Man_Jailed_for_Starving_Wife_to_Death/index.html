Indian Man Jailed for Starving Wife to Death

2025-05-18T09:02:00+00:00

A man from Kareli village in Uttar Pradesh, India, has been sentenced to life imprisonment for starving his wife to death. Manoj locked his wife, Mamta, in a room without food or water for a week following an argument. The Additional District Government Advocate (ADGC) Sunil Pandey confirmed the sentencing.
