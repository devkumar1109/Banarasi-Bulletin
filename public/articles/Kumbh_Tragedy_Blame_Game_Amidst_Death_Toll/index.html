Kumbh Tragedy: Blame Game Amidst Death Toll

2025-02-28T15:36:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh Mela in Prayagraj, which ended on February 26th, saw three stampede incidents resulting in deaths. Mamata Banerjee criticized the BJP government's arrangements, calling it a "Mrityu Kumbh". Prime Minister Modi apologized for inadequate arrangements while accusing "leaders who mock and ridicule religion". Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath also used harsh language against critics, calling them "vultures". The BJP has successfully used the Kumbh as a propaganda tool, elevating its significance beyond a religious event. Other political parties are struggling to counter this strategy.
