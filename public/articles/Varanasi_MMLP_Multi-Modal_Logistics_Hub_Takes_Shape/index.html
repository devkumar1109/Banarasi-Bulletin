Varanasi MMLP: Multi-Modal Logistics Hub Takes Shape

2025-03-12T12:29:00+00:00

The National Highways Logistics Management Limited (NHLML) and Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) signed a Memorandum of Understanding to build a state-of-the-art Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP) in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Spanning over 150 acres, the MMLP will be connected to National Highway 7, the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor, and National Waterway-1. It is also 30 km from Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport. The project, witnessed by Union Ministers Nitin Gadkari and Sarbananda Sonowal, aims to boost India's logistics sector, enhance trade efficiency, and drive economic growth by creating investment and job opportunities.
