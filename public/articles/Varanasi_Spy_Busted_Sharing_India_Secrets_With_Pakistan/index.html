Varanasi Spy Busted: Sharing India Secrets With Pakistan

2025-05-22T13:02:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Anti-Terrorism Squad (UP ATS) arrested Tufail from Varanasi for allegedly spying for Pakistan. Tufail shared sensitive information and photos of strategic locations like Rajghat, Namo Ghat, and the Red Fort with Pakistani nationals. He was in contact with approximately 600 Pakistanis, including Nafisa, whose husband is in the Pakistani Army. During the investigation, Tufail was found sharing videos of Tehreek-e-Labbaik leader Maulana Saad Rizvi and spreading provocative messages about Babri Masjid and Sharia law. He is believed to have circulated WhatsApp group links connecting locals with Pakistani networks.
