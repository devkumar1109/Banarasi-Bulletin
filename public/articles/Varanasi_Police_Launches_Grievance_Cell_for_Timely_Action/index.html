Varanasi Police Launches Grievance Cell for Timely Action

2025-06-05T19:00:00+00:00

A Public Grievance Cell was established at the Varanasi district police headquarters on June 6, 2025. Commissioner of Police Mohit Agrawal announced the initiative to address complaints from residents, particularly those from remote areas. Additional Commissioner of Police (crime) Rajesh Kumar Singh heads the cell, assisted by two subordinates. The cell operates daily from 10am to 2pm, providing a dedicated platform for public grievances. The aim is to ensure complaints are heard effectively and addressed promptly.
