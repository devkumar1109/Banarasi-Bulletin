Missing Teen Found, Fugitive Accomplice Arrested

2025-03-10T10:35:00+00:00

A 17-year-old girl who went missing from Samaypur Badli on February 27 was rescued from Budaun district, Uttar Pradesh. She had eloped with a man wanted for multiple crimes, including rape of a minor. Authorities used social media interactions and call detail records to locate the girl and the accused. The accused had previously been charged in a crime at the Samaypur Badli police station and is also linked to two other cases. Both the girl and the suspect are in police custody.
