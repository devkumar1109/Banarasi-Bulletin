Foxconn Eyes Uttar Pradesh for New Electronics Plant

2025-04-15T10:51:00+00:00

Foxconn, Apple's top global vendor, is in talks with the Uttar Pradesh government to establish an electronics manufacturing unit in the state. Invest UP proposed the investment opportunity during land allocation for the HCL-Foxconn chip joint venture, Vama Sundari, in YEIDA. Discussions center around a 300-acre plot in YEIDA. The venture will initially invest Rs 3,706 crore in an outsourced semiconductor assembly and test facility, creating 4,000 jobs. While the type of products Foxconn will manufacture is yet to be determined, the Taiwanese company has not commented on the development.
