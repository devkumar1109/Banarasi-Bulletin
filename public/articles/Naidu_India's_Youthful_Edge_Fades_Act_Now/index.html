Naidu: India's Youthful Edge Fades, Act Now

2025-03-06T13:51:00+00:00

Chandrababu Naidu, Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, highlighted the importance of population management in India. He stated that India's population advantage, with 65% of its population under 35, will last only until 2047. Naidu pointed to declining populations in Japan and Europe, emphasizing the need for planning beyond the current demographic advantage. He acknowledged that South India's early family planning initiatives led to a falling population and called for corrective measures to stabilize it. Naidu also shared a policy change in Andhra Pradesh, stating that individuals with fewer than two children are now ineligible to contest local body polls.
