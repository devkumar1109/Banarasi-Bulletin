Grief Over Son's Death Leads to Mother's Suicide

2025-03-21T17:41:00+00:00

A 56-year-old woman named Samlawati Devi committed suicide by jumping in front of a train in Varanasi, India. The tragedy occurred after her 27-year-old son, Vinay Kumar Singh, died in a road accident the previous night. Vinay was riding his motorcycle when he was involved in an accident near Paramapur village. He was taken to BHU's Trauma Centre but was pronounced dead. Samlawati Devi's husband had passed away six months earlier, leaving her to grieve the loss of both her son and her husband.
