HTAIn Workshop: Modern and Traditional Healthcare at BHU

2025-03-24T10:13:00+00:00

A two-day workshop on "Systematic Review, Cost-Effectiveness, and Meta-Analysis" was held at Banaras Hindu University, organized by Health Technology Assessment India (HTAIn). The workshop aimed to evaluate health technologies for effectiveness and cost-efficiency, emphasizing the integration of modern and traditional medicine for affordable, high-quality healthcare. Uttar Pradesh AYUSH Minister Daya Shankar Mishra Dayalu highlighted the government's commitment to providing affordable healthcare through programs like Ayushman Bharat. HTAIn research findings showed robotic surgery for prostate cancer is superior to conventional surgery despite higher costs, and HPV screening is more effective than current methods for cervical cancer screening.
