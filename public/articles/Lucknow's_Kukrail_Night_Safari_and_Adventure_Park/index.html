Lucknow's Kukrail: Night Safari and Adventure Park

2025-03-22T09:32:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh will launch the 'Kukrail Night Safari and Adventure Park' project in Lucknow next month. The project, spanning 855.07 acres of the Kukrail forest, is estimated to cost over Rs 1,500 crore and will be developed in two phases. The first phase, costing Rs 631 crore, will include 38 animal enclosures housing lions, tigers, and leopards, along with an art gallery, 7D theatre, and tram service. Construction of the first phase is expected to be completed in 24 months.
