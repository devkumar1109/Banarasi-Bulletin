Varanasi Officer Tewari Becomes PM's Private Secretary

2025-04-01T14:56:00+00:00

Nidhi Tewari, a 2014-batch IFS officer from Varanasi, has been appointed as Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Private Secretary. The appointment, effective immediately, was approved by the Appointments Committee of the Cabinet. Tewari previously served as Deputy Secretary in the PMO, managing foreign and security affairs under National Security Advisor Ajit Doval. Tewari's career highlights include achieving an impressive All India Rank of 96 in the UPSC Civil Services Examination in 2013 and working in the Ministry of External Affairs' Disarmament and International Security Affairs Division. Her appointment marks a generational shift in leadership within India's highest administrative offices.
