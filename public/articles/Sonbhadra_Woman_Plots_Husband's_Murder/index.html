Sonbhadra Woman Plots Husband's Murder

2025-03-29T19:22:00+00:00

Sunita Devi, a resident of Sonbhadra district, was arrested for plotting her husband Ram Naresh Yadav's murder. Police also arrested her son-in-law Alok Yadav and five others involved in the killing: Sandeep Kumar, Ankit Kumar, Mayank Kol, and two others. Sunita paid Rs. 50,000 to the murderers through Alok Yadav. The murder weapon, a rod, the deceased's mobile phone, and the motorcycle used in the crime were recovered. Sunita confessed to the crime, stating that she was frustrated by her husband's relationship with another woman and his harassment towards her and their children.
