Jaiswal Leads Goa Charge, Leaves Mumbai

2025-04-02T12:14:00+00:00

Yashasvi Jaiswal, India's Test opener, will captain Goa in the 2025-26 domestic season after leaving Mumbai. Jaiswal, who hails from Uttar Pradesh, had a quiet Ranji Trophy campaign for Mumbai in 2024-25 and withdrew from a semifinal match due to an ankle injury. He follows a trend of Mumbai cricketers moving to Goa, including Arjun Tendulkar, Siddhesh Lad, and Eknath Kerkar. Currently, Jaiswal is struggling for form in IPL 2025 for Rajasthan Royals, scoring only 34 runs in three matches.
