Varanasi United: Prayers, Protests, Condemnation

2025-04-23T17:40:00+00:00

Following the Pahalgam terror attack, Varanasi witnessed widespread protests and prayers. The Kashi Vishwanath Temple Trust organized an 'Ati Rudra Path', seeking Lord Shiva's blessings to counter terrorism. Muslim institutions like Anjuman Intejamia Masajid condemned the attack, expressing sympathy for the victims. A joint protest march by Vishal Bharat Sansthan and Muslim Women's Foundation culminated in a symbolic cremation of terrorists, demanding action against Pakistan. The BJP postponed its Waqf Reform Awareness Campaign workshops in mourning for the victims. Marches and burning of effigies of terrorism were also seen in various parts of the city.
