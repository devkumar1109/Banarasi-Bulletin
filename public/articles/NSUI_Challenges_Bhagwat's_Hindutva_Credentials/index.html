NSUI Challenges Bhagwat's Hindutva Credentials

2025-04-04T09:43:00+00:00

NSUI activists protested against RSS Chief Mohan Bhagwat during his visit to Varanasi, demanding he practices what he preaches about Hindutva. They attempted to offer him Maha Kumbh water, highlighting his absence from the religious ritual and questioning his commitment to Sanatan tradition. The activists alleged that Bhagwat engages in Hindutva politics but avoids living its tenets. Police detained several NSUI activists during the protest.
