UP's Film City Dreams Take Shape

2025-05-24T13:15:00+00:00

Construction of an international film city in Uttar Pradesh's YEIDA region will begin next month. The project, spearheaded by filmmaker Boney Kapoor's company Bayview Projects LLP, aims to establish UP as a major film production hub. Preparatory work, including land leveling and infrastructure development, is nearing completion. CM Yogi Adityanath will lay the foundation stone. The first phase will encompass 230 acres and feature 13-14 modern film studios and a film institute. The entire project, spanning 1,000 acres, will be executed in three phases over eight years at an estimated cost of Rs 1,510 crore.
