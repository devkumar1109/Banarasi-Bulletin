India's Wheelchair Stars Gear Up for Paralympic Dreams

2025-04-18T07:00:00+00:00

Twenty-one Indian women wheelchair basketball players are undergoing a 14-day intensive training camp at the Andhra Pradesh MedTech Zone (AMTZ). The camp, organized by the Wheelchair Basketball Federation of India (WBFI), aims to prepare the team for the upcoming Asia-Oceania Zone Games and the Asian Para Games, both serving as qualifying events for the 2028 Paralympic Games. AMTZ provides a fully accessible facility with residential facilities and round-the-clock support, enabling the athletes to train effectively. Coach Captain Louis George Meprath highlights the camp's significance in fostering team chemistry and building skills necessary for international competition.
