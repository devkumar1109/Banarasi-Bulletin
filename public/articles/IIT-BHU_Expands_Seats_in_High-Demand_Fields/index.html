IIT-BHU Expands Seats in High-Demand Fields

2025-06-08T18:46:00+00:00

IIT-BHU Varanasi increased seats in popular departments for JEE qualified students in the 2025-26 academic year. A total of 111 seats were reallocated from four departments with lower demand (ceramic, metallurgical, mining, and pharmaceutical engineering) to nine high-demand departments like computer science and engineering, electronics engineering, and mathematics and computing. Despite the reallocation, the total number of seats remains at 1,589, the same as last year. This move aims to cater to student aspirations and industry needs.
