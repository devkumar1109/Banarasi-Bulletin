BJP's UP Factions Stalemate District Appointments

2025-03-19T04:51:00+00:00

Factional feuds within the BJP are hindering the appointment of presidents for 28 districts in Uttar Pradesh. This internal conflict is causing delays and uncertainty in the party's organizational structure at the district level. The situation highlights the challenges the BJP faces in managing internal divisions and ensuring smooth functioning of its units.
