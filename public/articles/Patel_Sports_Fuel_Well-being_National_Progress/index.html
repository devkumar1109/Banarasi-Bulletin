Patel: Sports Fuel Well-being, National Progress

2025-05-02T16:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel emphasized the importance of sports for physical and mental well-being. Speaking at the 22nd All India Inter-College Sports Festival, she highlighted the benefits of sports, stating they teach discipline and foster national progress. Patel praised the participation of women in university sports, noting their increasing representation and medal wins. She urged universities to organize sports competitions for students up to the intermediate level, believing it would strengthen the nation.
