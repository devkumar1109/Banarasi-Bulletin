India's Mock Drills: A Show of Force After Sindoor Strikes

2025-05-07T15:58:00+00:00

India conducted civil defense mock drills across several states following Operation Sindoor, a retaliatory airstrike targeting Pakistani terror camps. The drills, which included a blackout, took place in Delhi, Punjab, Gujarat, UP, Jammu and Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, and others. Airstrikes destroyed nine terror hideouts in locations including Bahawalpur, Muridke, Tehra Kalan, Sialkot, Barnala, Kotli, and Muzaffarabad. Targeted sites included headquarters of JeM, LeT, and Hizbul Mujahideen. The operation was a response to the Pahalgam terror attack.
