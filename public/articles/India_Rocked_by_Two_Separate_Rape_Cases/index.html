India Rocked by Two Separate Rape Cases

2025-03-17T01:17:00+00:00

In Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, a 14-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped at gunpoint and gang-raped by three youths, including Suraj Soni. An FIR has been registered against Soni and two unidentified individuals under the Bhartiya Nyaya Sanhita and the POCSO Act. Separately, in Delhi, a British woman was allegedly raped and molested by two men at a hotel in Mahipalpur. One accused was arrested for rape and his accomplice for molestation. The woman had traveled to Delhi to meet a man she befriended on social media.
