Varanasi Hospital Gets Boost: SNCU, CCU, and FRU Upgrades

2025-04-17T18:12:00+00:00

Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Hospital in Varanasi will soon receive a sick newborn care unit (SNCU) and a critical care unit. This decision was made following a request from Chief Medical Superintendent Dr. Brijesh Kumar and approved by Dr. Pinky Jowel, MD NHM/secretary of Medical Health and Family Welfare, Uttar Pradesh. Dr. Jowel also announced the conversion of Urban CHC Sarnath and Shivpur into first referral units (FRUs) to expand medical services. During her inspection, she emphasized quality service provision and adherence to ENQUAS standards at various medical centers. She also transferred medical equipment to the hospital through CSR funds and stressed the importance of Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission integration, regular medicine supplies, and birth-death registration.
