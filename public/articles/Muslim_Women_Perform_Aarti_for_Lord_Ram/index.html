Muslim Women Perform Aarti for Lord Ram

2025-04-06T19:26:00+00:00

On Ram Navami, Muslim women in Varanasi performed aarti for Lord Ram, organized by the Muslim Mahila Foundation and Vishal Bharat Sansthan. They expressed happiness over the passing of the Waqf Amendment Bill in Parliament, believing it was passed with Lord Ram's grace. Muslim women asserted that the bill, along with the Triple Talaq Bill, supports Muslim women. They condemned attempts to divide society by inciting hatred between Hindus and Muslims, emphasizing that statements from religious and political figures risk leading the country towards division. The aarti was seen as a message of love, unity, and communal harmony.
