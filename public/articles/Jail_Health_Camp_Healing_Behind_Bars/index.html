Jail Health Camp: Healing Behind Bars

2025-02-23T18:32:00+00:00

The Indian Red Cross Society, Gorakhpur branch, organized a health camp at Gorakhpur district jail. 120 inmates underwent skin disease screenings and received medicine, while 110 received general medicine. Gynecologist Dr Pooja Shukla provided services for female inmates. 133 inmates received eye exams from doctors from Raji Hospital, with 30 receiving glasses. Jail superintendent J.K. Pandey expressed support for future health initiatives.
