Bee Swarm Attacks Forest Trainees, 16 Injured

2025-05-28T19:13:00+00:00

A swarm of bees attacked a group of 54 forest officers from Kanpur Nagar who were trainees visiting Chandan Forest Rest House in Mirzapur district. 16 officers were injured, with four in critical condition. The incident occurred near a nursery in Rajapura village. The injured officers were taken to Madihan Community Health Centre, and the four critically injured were transferred to the district hospital. Divisional forest officer Arvind Raj Mishra visited the site and confirmed that all officers are now out of danger.
