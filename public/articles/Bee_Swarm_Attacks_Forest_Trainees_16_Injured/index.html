Bee Swarm Attacks Forest Trainees, 16 Injured

2025-05-28T19:13:00+00:00

A swarm of bees attacked a group of 54 forest officers, trainees from Kanpur Nagar, in Mirzapur district, injuring 16. Four officers sustained critical injuries and were transferred to the district hospital. The incident occurred near a nursery in Rajapura village while the trainees were visiting Chandan Forest Rest House. Forest officers used blankets and fire to protect themselves and evacuate the trainees. Divisional forest officer Arvind Raj Mishra confirmed all officers are now out of danger.
