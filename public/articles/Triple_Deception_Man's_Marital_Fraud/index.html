Triple Deception: Man's Marital Fraud

2025-03-22T15:51:00+00:00

Rajan, a man from Uttar Pradesh, allegedly tricked three women into marriage by falsely claiming to be an excise department employee and a widower. He married Kiran in 2022 after convincing her to take a Rs 42 lakh loan for a house. Kiran filed a complaint after discovering Rajan's deception. Police investigations revealed Rajan had also married Sarita in 2014 and deceived Neelima Singh into marriage. Sarita had filed an FIR against him in 2016.
