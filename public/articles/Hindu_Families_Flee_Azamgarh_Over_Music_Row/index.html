Hindu Families Flee Azamgarh Over Music Row

2025-06-16T04:25:00+00:00

Around 40 Hindu families in Azamgarh's Muslim-dominated Chhota Pura are selling their homes due to alleged harassment and violence stemming from playing music during religious and wedding events. The families claim the district administration didn't address their complaints after a June 3 clash between communities. They feel forced to relocate due to ongoing threats and abuse. Azamgarh ASP Madhuvan Kumar Singh assured the families of increased police presence to guarantee their safety.
