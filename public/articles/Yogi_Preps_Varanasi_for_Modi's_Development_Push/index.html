Yogi Preps Varanasi for Modi's Development Push

2025-04-03T19:10:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Varanasi to oversee preparations for Prime Minister Narendra Modi's upcoming visit. Adityanath emphasized the importance of ensuring all projects scheduled for inauguration and foundation-laying were ready for the PM's arrival. The visit highlights Modi's focus on development projects in Varanasi, a significant constituency for him.
