Chowk Station Reimagined: Heritage Meets Modernity

2025-05-02T14:40:00+00:00

Varanasi's 125-year-old Chowk Police Station has been renovated and inaugurated as a heritage building. The Commissioner of Police, Mohit Agarwal, emphasized the importance of security and smooth traffic flow for the lakhs of devotees visiting Kashi Vishwanath Dham. The renovated station features modern facilities like an attractive entrance gate, a visitor room, and a park for tourists. Architect Priya Singh and others involved in the project received certificates of appreciation. The CP plans to renovate other police stations like Dashashwamedh and Kotwali following this model.
