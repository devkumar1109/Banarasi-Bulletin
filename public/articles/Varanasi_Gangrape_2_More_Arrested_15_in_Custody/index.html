Varanasi Gangrape: 2 More Arrested, 15 in Custody

2025-04-16T06:42:00+00:00

Two more arrests have been made in the Varanasi gangrape case, bringing the total to 15. The 19-year-old victim was abducted on March 29 and subjected to repeated gang rape for seven days. Prime Minister Narendra Modi intervened in the case, directing officials to take strict action against the accused. The arrests were made based on CCTV footage, facial recognition, and informant networks. The arrested suspects, Raj Khan and Mohammad Shahbaz, have reportedly confessed to their involvement. Police are searching for several other suspects who are believed to be in hiding. The survivor, who is battling Hepatitis-B, is receiving medical attention.
