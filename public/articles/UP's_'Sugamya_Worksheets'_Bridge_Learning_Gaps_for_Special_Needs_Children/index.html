UP's 'Sugamya Worksheets' Bridge Learning Gaps for Special Needs Children

2025-06-12T05:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched 'Sugamya Worksheets' in partnership with UNICEF to benefit nearly 3 lakh children with special needs. The worksheets, covering Hindi and Mathematics for classes 1 to 8, are designed using simple language and engaging formats to enhance foundational learning and integrate differently-abled children into mainstream education. A total of 1,900 worksheets were developed, with 1,300 for classes 1 to 5 and 600 for classes 6 to 8. Distribution is being done in phases, one set at a time, with teachers instructed to engage students through individual, pair, and group activities. Special educators will visit schools to ensure availability and provide support.
