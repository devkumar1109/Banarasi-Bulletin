Bayer and IRRI: Rice Research Drives Future Growth

2025-06-04T18:28:00+00:00

A four-member delegation from Bayer Crop Science visited the International Rice Research Institute - South Asia Regional Centre (ISARC) in Varanasi. The visit focused on reviewing collaborative efforts in rice research and development, particularly in areas like direct-seeded rice (DSR), hybrid rice, and the deployment of IRRI's DSR-fit rice varieties. The discussions aimed to advance these initiatives.
