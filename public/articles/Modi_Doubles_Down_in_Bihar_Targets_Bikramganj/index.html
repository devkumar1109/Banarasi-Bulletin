Modi Doubles Down in Bihar, Targets Bikramganj

2025-05-16T18:51:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Bihar on May 30th, his second visit in 20 days. He will inaugurate projects including the new Patna Airport terminal, Bihta Airport foundation, Patna-Sasaram four-lane road, and NTPC Nabinagar power plant capacity augmentation. Modi will also lay the foundation stone for the Varanasi-Ranchi expressway. The visit is significant as Modi will address a rally in Bikramganj, a region where the NDA faced losses in previous elections. Deputy CM Samrat Choudhary expects a large turnout and hopes the rally will boost NDA's position in the area.
