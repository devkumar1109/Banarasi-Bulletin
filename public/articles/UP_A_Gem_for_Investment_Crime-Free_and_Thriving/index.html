UP: A Gem for Investment, Crime-Free and Thriving

2025-05-08T16:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that the state provides a favorable environment for investment and business, citing its improved law and order and world-class infrastructure. He highlighted UP's economic progress over the past eight years, emphasizing its transformation from a state plagued by crime and unrest to one that is now crime-free and mafia-free. Adityanath urged the India Bullion and Jewellers Association (IBJA) to establish a dedicated Gems and Jewellery Park in UP, recognizing the sector's significant contribution to India's economy and job creation. He assured full government support to stakeholders in the gems and jewellery industry.
