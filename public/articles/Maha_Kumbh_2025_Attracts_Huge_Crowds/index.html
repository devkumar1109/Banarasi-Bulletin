"Sacred Tides: Maha Kumbh 2025 Draws Record 62 Crore Devotees to Prayagraj"

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj has attracted 62 crore devotees, impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. Over 2 crore devotees visited Kashi Vishwanath Temple and 69 lakh booked homestays in Ayodhya. Prime Minister Narendra Modi took a holy dip at Sangam. The event featured the Khel Maha Kumbh sports event, 'zero discharge' initiative, and cultural activities. A wastewater management system and eco-friendly solutions maintained hygiene. Four world record attempts were made, including a simultaneous cleaning campaign. The Netra Kumbh pledge saw 5,670 people pledge to donate their eyes. Prayagraj Airport handled unprecedented traffic, with flight frequencies quadrupling. Many leaders and celebrities visited, including Pawan Kalyan and Sunil Gavaskar. The average daily devotees bathing at Sangam is over 1.19 crore, with 50 lakh from Nepal.
