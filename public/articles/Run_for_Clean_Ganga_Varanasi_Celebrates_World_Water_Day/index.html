Run for Clean Ganga: Varanasi Celebrates World Water Day

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon, 'Run for Clean Ganga', and a dolphin safari. The marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw residents and foreigners running 6 km from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat. Abhishek Yadav won the men's category, while Rebi Pal won the women's category. A dolphin safari was inaugurated at Dhakwa Ghat, highlighting efforts to protect the Gangetic Dolphin. The events aimed to raise public awareness about the importance of freshwater conservation and sustainable management.
