Bounty Hunter Captured After Fiery Bhadohi Encounter

2025-02-28T06:51:00+00:00

Sachin Maurya, a wanted criminal with a 25,000 rupee bounty, was arrested in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh, following an encounter with Gyanpur police and district SWAT. Maurya was wanted for robbery and had previous cases against him in Bhadohi and Prayagraj. During the arrest, police recovered an illegal firearm, ammunition, and a motorcycle used in a February 21st necklace robbery near Marchwar village. Maurya sustained a gunshot wound to the leg during the encounter and is currently receiving treatment. Police are continuing their efforts to apprehend his accomplice who escaped.
