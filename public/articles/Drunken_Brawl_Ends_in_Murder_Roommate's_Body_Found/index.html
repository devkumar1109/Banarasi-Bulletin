Drunken Brawl Ends in Murder, Roommate's Body Found

2025-04-30T14:02:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man, Lavkush Raja Paswan, has been arrested from Unnao, Uttar Pradesh, for the murder of his roommate, Subhash Trilokbrij Vishwakarma, in Khalapur. The incident occurred on April 22 after a drunken brawl. Paswan allegedly killed Vishwakarma and attempted to conceal the crime by dumping the body near a railway bridge and covering it with stones. Railway staff discovered the body on April 26. Paswan was identified through CCTV footage and arrested in his village. He confessed to the crime during interrogation. The Raigad Local Crime Branch is investigating the case.
