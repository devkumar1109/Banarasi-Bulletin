UP Goes Digital: Smart Cards Revamp Vehicle Registration

2025-03-11T17:15:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is replacing traditional vehicle registration booklets with chip-enabled smart cards as part of its Digital UP Mission. This initiative aims to improve security, streamline processes, and enhance transparency. The smart cards store vehicle and ownership data, reducing fraud and bureaucratic delays. Embedded with microchip technology, the cards enable instant verification of vehicle data by authorities, modernizing the transport sector.
