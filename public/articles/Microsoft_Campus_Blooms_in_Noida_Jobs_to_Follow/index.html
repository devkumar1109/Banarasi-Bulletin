Microsoft Campus Blooms in Noida, Jobs to Follow

2025-03-08T07:29:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath laid the foundation stone for Microsoft's new campus in Noida's Sector 145 on March 8, 2025. The campus is expected to create numerous job opportunities for the youth. CM Adityanath hailed Microsoft's decision to establish their R&D center in Noida, stating it signifies Uttar Pradesh's emergence as a major investment destination. He also inaugurated two underpasses on the Noida Expressway to alleviate traffic congestion.
