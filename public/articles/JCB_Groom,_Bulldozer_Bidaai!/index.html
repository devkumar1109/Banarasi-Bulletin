JCB Groom, Bulldozer Bidaai!

2025-02-24T10:21:00+00:00

A groom in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, made a unique entrance at his wedding by arriving on a JCB. For his bride's "bidaai" (farewell), a procession of nearly a dozen bulldozers joined the event, surprising onlookers. This unconventional choice initially drew attention due to the recent association of bulldozers with the Uttar Pradesh government's actions against rioters. However, the groom's family clarified that the bulldozers were meant to symbolize a powerful and memorable send-off for the bride.
