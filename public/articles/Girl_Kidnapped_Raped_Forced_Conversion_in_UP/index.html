Girl Kidnapped, Raped, Forced Conversion in UP

2025-06-10T16:10:00+00:00

A girl was allegedly kidnapped, raped, and forced to convert to another religion in Uttar Pradesh. Four people, Meraj, Seraj, Meruddin alias Merajuddin Ansari, and Haseena Khatoon, have been arrested. The victim's mother filed a complaint accusing the men of kidnapping. Police registered a case under sections of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act. During the investigation, police discovered the girl was forced to convert and raped by Meraj, aided by Haseena Khatoon. Additional charges under BNS section 87 (kidnapping to compel for marriage) and the Uttar Pradesh Prohibition of Unlawful Conversion of Religion Act 2021 have been added.
