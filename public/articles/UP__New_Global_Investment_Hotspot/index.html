UP: New Global Investment Hotspot

2025-03-21T20:20:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Secretary Manoj Kumar Singh declared the state a global investment destination, attracting companies seeking scale and talent. He highlighted business-friendly policies, a skilled workforce, and robust infrastructure as key factors. Deloitte, a multinational firm, inaugurated its office in Lucknow, citing UP's growth as a global testing hub and booming electronics manufacturing center. Deloitte CEO Romal Shetty emphasized UP's potential, noting the state's need for 26-27% year-over-year GSDP growth to achieve a trillion-dollar economy by 2030.
