Panchayat Season 4: Back to the Village

2025-04-05T08:09:00+00:00

"Panchayat" Season 4 will premiere on Prime Video on July 2nd. The popular web series, completed its first three seasons and returns for a fourth after five years. The show features Jitendra Kumar, Neena Gupta, Raghubir Yadav, Chandan Roy, Sanvikaa, Faisal Malik, Durgesh Kumar, Sunita Rajwar, and Pankaj Jha. Created by Deepak Kumar Mishra & Chandan Kumar, and directed by Deepak Kumar Mishra and Akshat Vijaywargiya, "Panchayat" follows Abhishek Tripathi, an engineering graduate who becomes a secretary for a Gram Panchayat in Uttar Pradesh.
