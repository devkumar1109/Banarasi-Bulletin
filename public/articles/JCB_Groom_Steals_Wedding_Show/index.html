JCB Groom Steals Wedding Show

2025-02-24T10:21:00+00:00

In a unique twist on traditional wedding processions, a groom in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, arrived on a JCB bulldozer for his wedding send-off. The bride, Karishma, was escorted by nearly a dozen bulldozers, creating a memorable and unconventional spectacle. This unusual choice has sparked curiosity and attention online, highlighting the growing trend of families seeking creative ways to make their weddings stand out. The groom's arrival on the JCB contrasted with the typical luxury cars often seen at weddings.
