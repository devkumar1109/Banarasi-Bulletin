2025-03-22T15:51:00+00:00

Rajan, a man from Uttar Pradesh, is accused of conning three women into marriage. He falsely claimed to be an excise department employee and a widower. He married Kiran in 2022 and persuaded her to take a Rs 42 lakh loan for a house. Kiran discovered his deception and filed a police complaint. Investigation revealed Rajan had also married Sarita in 2014 and deceived Neelima Singh into marriage. Sarita had filed an FIR against him in 2016 after realizing his lies.
