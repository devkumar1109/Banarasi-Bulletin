Varanasi: Chaos, Culture, and Calm

2025-03-17T15:00:00+00:00

Varanasi, India's oldest city, offers a vibrant cultural and spiritual experience. Tourists from Germany and England, visiting during Holi, described it as loud yet beautiful and unlike anything they've seen before. They found the narrow lanes bustling with activity, comparing Varanasi's busy streets to Leicester Square at Christmas. Many visitors, who also traveled to Rajasthan and Agra, expressed a desire to return to India, drawn to its hospitality and vibrant culture. One visitor noted the people in Varanasi live in the moment, finding a sense of calmness amidst the chaos. Although some expected all of India to be like Varanasi, they found it busier than other places.
