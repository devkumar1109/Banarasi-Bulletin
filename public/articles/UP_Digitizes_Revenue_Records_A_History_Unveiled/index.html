UP Digitizes Revenue Records: A History Unveiled

2025-05-18T13:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is accelerating the digitization of old revenue records under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. The Stamp and Registration Department has nearly completed digitizing records from 2002 to 2017 and is now focusing on records from 1990 to 2001. The next phase will involve digitizing records predating 1990. This initiative aims to enhance access to information, streamline administrative procedures, and ensure the long-term preservation of records. Digitized records will be stored in a central repository, freeing up office space and reducing the burden on sub-registrar offices.
