Ayodhya Fizz: Coca-Cola Boosts Production, Jobs

2025-03-23T19:02:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated a ₹350 crore expansion of SLMG Beverages' plant in Ayodhya. This investment will double the plant's production capacity to 3,724 bottles per minute, creating 900 new jobs and bringing the total workforce to 2,500. The expansion, undertaken by Amrit Bottlers Private Limited, a subsidiary of SLMG Beverages, aims to meet the growing demand for Coca-Cola beverages in the region. SLMG Beverages plans to invest up to $1 billion in Uttar Pradesh and Bihar by 2030. The facility utilizes solar and wind energy, emphasizing sustainability. This project positions Ayodhya as a key industrial hub in Uttar Pradesh.
