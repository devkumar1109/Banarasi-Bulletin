Monalisa's Music Video: From Mela to Love Song

2025-06-12T07:15:00+00:00

Monalisa and singer Utkarsh Singh's music video teaser was released on June 12, 2025. The video showcases Utkarsh falling in love with Monalisa after seeing her picture. Monalisa is also set to debut in a film directed by Sanoj Mishra, who announced the project after his release from jail. Monalisa gained fame after going viral at the Mahakumbh Mela in February 2025 for selling Rudraksha malas. Her new album with Utkarsh Singh is scheduled for release on June 13, 2025.
