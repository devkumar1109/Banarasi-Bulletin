Kumbh Mela Boom: Spiritual Tourism Takes Off

2025-05-26T05:26:00+00:00

Online travel platforms MakeMyTrip and Ixigo experienced a surge in bookings during the Maha Kumbh, highlighting the significant potential of spiritual tourism. The event attracted a massive pilgrimage, leading to increased demand for travel and accommodation services. Both companies are now planning to invest heavily in this growing sector, capitalizing on the popularity of religious travel.
