Lost Boy Found: 14 Years, 2 States, One Reunion

2025-04-03T07:41:00+00:00

Neeraj Kannaujiya, who went missing from Mumbai at age 8, was reunited with his family in Varanasi after 14 years. He was found wandering in Gudur, Andhra Pradesh, and taken in by the Swarna Bharat Trust. The Trust cared for Neeraj, providing him with education, including learning Telugu, and training in various skills like plumbing and driving. Deepa Venkat, managing trustee of the Trust, played a key role in his upbringing and facilitating his reunion with his family.
