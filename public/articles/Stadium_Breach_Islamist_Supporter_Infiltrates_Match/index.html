Stadium Breach: Islamist Supporter Infiltrates Match

2025-02-26T04:39:00+00:00

During the Champions Trophy in Pakistan, a security breach occurred at Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium. An individual, allegedly a supporter of an Islamist political party, entered the field with a picture of Saad Rizvi and embraced New Zealand's Rachin Ravindra during a match against Bangladesh. Pakistan's Cricket Board is collaborating with security agencies to investigate the incident and has banned the intruder from all stadiums.
