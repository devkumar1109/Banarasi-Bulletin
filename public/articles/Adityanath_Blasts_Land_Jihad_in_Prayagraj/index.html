Adityanath Blasts "Land Jihad" in Prayagraj

2025-03-12T12:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath accused past governments of allowing "land jihad," resulting in the encroachment of ancient sites in Prayagraj, including Akshay Vat, Mata Saraswati Koop, Patalpuri, and Shringverpur. He stated that these "land mafias" controlled the holy city, impacting its heritage and significance. Adityanath highlighted that the holy Akshay Vat within Allahabad Fort was locked during colonial rule, preventing devotees from worshipping for centuries. He made these remarks at the 'Manthan: Kumbh and Beyond' Vichar Sangam event organized by the RSS-affiliated weekly Organiser.
