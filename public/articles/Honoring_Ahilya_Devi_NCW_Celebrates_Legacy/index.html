Honoring Ahilya Devi: NCW Celebrates Legacy

2025-05-29T18:45:00+00:00

The National Commission for Women (NCW) will host a two-day program in Varanasi to celebrate the 300th birth anniversary of Punyashlok Ahilya Devi Holkar, a revered Holkar dynasty ruler known for her leadership, social welfare, and women's empowerment. The program, held at RUDRAKSH – International Cooperation and Convention Centre, will feature a keynote address by NCW chairperson Vijaya Rahatkar, a special address by Dr. Kumar Vishwas, and an exhibition on Ahilya Devi Holkar's life and legacy. Events will also include a lecture at Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith University, a tribute at Shubham Lawn and Banquet, a painting competition, a rangoli competition, and a cultural performance.
