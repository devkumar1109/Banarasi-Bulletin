UP's New Corp to Safeguard 11 Lakh Outsourced Workers

2025-04-25T21:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is pushing for the establishment of the Uttar Pradesh Outsource Service Corporation (UPCOS) to improve the working conditions and security of over 11 lakh outsourced employees across 93 state administration departments. The corporation aims to address issues like salary deductions, delayed payments, lack of EPF/ESI benefits, and transparent recruitment. Key directives from the CM include: no employee removal without departmental recommendation, direct bank transfers of full remuneration by the 5th of every month, and timely EPF and ESI contributions. Agencies violating these regulations face strict action, including blacklisting, debarment, financial penalties, and legal proceedings.
