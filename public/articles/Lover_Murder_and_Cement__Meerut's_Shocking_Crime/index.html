Lover, Murder, and Cement: Meerut's Shocking Crime

2025-03-19T07:36:00+00:00

In Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, a woman named Muskan and her lover, Sahil, allegedly murdered Muskan's husband, Saurabh Rajput. Saurabh, a merchant navy employee, went missing on March 4th. The couple confessed to stabbing Saurabh to death, dismembering his body, and sealing it in a cement-filled drum. Police recovered the body and arrested both suspects. Muskan attempted to mislead Saurabh's family by sending messages from his phone. After the crime, she and Sahil went on a vacation to Shimla and Manali.
