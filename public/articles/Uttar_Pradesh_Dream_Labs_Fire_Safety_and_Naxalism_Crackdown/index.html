Uttar Pradesh: Dream Labs, Fire Safety, and Naxalism Crackdown

2025-06-13T22:02:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government plans to establish "Dream Labs" in secondary schools. These labs aim to bridge the gap between education and industry, fostering a collaborative environment for students. The government is also taking steps to enhance fire safety by recruiting over 2 lakh fire safety officers and personnel for private institutions. Additionally, Home Minister Amit Shah has pledged to eradicate Naxalism by March 2026.
