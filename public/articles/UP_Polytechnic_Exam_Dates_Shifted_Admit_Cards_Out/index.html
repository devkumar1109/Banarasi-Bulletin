UP Polytechnic Exam: Dates Shifted, Admit Cards Out

2025-05-28T05:23:00+00:00

The Joint Entrance Examination Council, Uttar Pradesh (JEECUP) has revised the UP Polytechnic Entrance Exam 2025 schedule. The exam will now be conducted from June 5 to June 13, 2025, instead of May 20 to May 28. Admit cards were released on May 27, 2025. The answer key will be published on June 13, 2025, followed by an objection submission window from June 13 to June 15, 2025. Results are expected to be announced on June 21, 2025. Candidates can download their admit cards from the official JEECUP website: https://jeecup.admissions.nic.in. The exam will be computer-based, consisting of objective-type multiple-choice questions. The exam duration is 150 minutes with a marking scheme of +4 for each correct answer.
