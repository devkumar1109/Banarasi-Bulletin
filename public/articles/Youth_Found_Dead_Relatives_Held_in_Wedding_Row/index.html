Youth Found Dead, Relatives Held in Wedding Row

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth from Barki village, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village on Saturday. Bind's father, Dinanath, alleged that his son was murdered by two relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar, who had invited him to a wedding ceremony. Dinanath stated that the accused called him late Friday night claiming Hemant attacked them and fled, after which he couldn't locate his son. Police have lodged a named FIR against the accused and are investigating the case. The body has been sent for autopsy and further action will be taken based on the investigation and autopsy report.
