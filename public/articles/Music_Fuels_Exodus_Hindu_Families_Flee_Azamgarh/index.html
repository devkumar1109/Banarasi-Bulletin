Music Fuels Exodus: Hindu Families Flee Azamgarh

2025-06-16T04:25:00+00:00

Forty Hindu families in Azamgarh's Chhota Pura are selling their homes due to alleged harassment and violence stemming from playing music during religious and wedding events. The issue escalated after a June 3 clash between communities, which residents claim the district administration failed to address. Families allege threats and abuse, forcing them to consider relocating. Azamgarh ASP Madhuvan Kumar Singh assured increased police presence and safety for the families.
