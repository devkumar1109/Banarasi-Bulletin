SP Bans Paan Masala, Gutkha in UP Assembly

2025-03-10T07:27:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Assembly Speaker Satish Mahana has banned paan masala and gutkha use in the Assembly following a stain left by an MLA. Violators, including MLAs and staff, face a Rs 1,000 fine with a second offence resulting in public shaming. The security control room will monitor for violations, and extra CCTV cameras are being considered to cover blind spots. Fixed dustbins, previously removed for security reasons, may be reinstated to encourage cleanliness. Assembly staff, who struggle to remove paan masala stains, welcome the ban.
