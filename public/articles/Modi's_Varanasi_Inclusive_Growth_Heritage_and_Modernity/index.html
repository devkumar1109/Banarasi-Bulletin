Modi's Varanasi: Inclusive Growth, Heritage, and Modernity

2025-04-11T16:11:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for 44 projects worth Rs 3,880 crore in Varanasi, his parliamentary constituency. He emphasized his government's commitment to "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas" (inclusive development), contrasting it with the Opposition's focus on "parivar ka saath, parivar ka vikas" (family-based development). Modi highlighted Varanasi's transformation over the past decade, becoming a hub of modernity, heritage, and economic growth. He cited the city's emergence as a healthcare center for Purvanchal, with major hospitals from Delhi and Mumbai becoming accessible. He praised initiatives like the Kashi Tamil Sangamam, which foster national unity, and acknowledged Uttar Pradesh's progress as a leading state in GI tagging.
