UP CM: COVID Threat Low, Vigilance High

2025-05-21T15:48:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has assured the public that COVID-19 does not currently pose an imminent threat despite rising global concerns about the JN.1 subvariant. While the Centre hasn't issued new guidelines, Adityanath has instructed health units to remain vigilant and prepared, ensuring operational ICUs and oxygen plants. He emphasized proactive measures against seasonal diseases like dengue and malaria, calling for collaboration between the Health department, Medical Education department, and local administrations. Two COVID-related deaths in Maharashtra, among patients with comorbidities, highlight the need for preparedness.
