Varanasi Police Launches Public Grievance Cell

2025-06-05T19:00:00+00:00

The Varanasi district police headquarters has established a Public Grievance Cell to facilitate complainants and ensure effective action on their grievances. The cell, headed by Additional Commissioner of Police (crime) Rajesh Kumar Singh, will function daily from 10am to 2pm. Commissioner of Police Mohit Agrawal made this announcement, emphasizing the need to address the concerns of complainants, especially those from remote areas. The cell aims to ensure that complaints are heard properly and appropriate action is taken. Agrawal also plans to be available at the police office to hear both public and police personnel concerns.
