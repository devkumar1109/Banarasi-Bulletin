Gorakhpur Link Expressway: Eastern UP Gets a Boost

2025-06-15T10:20:00+00:00

The Gorakhpur Link Expressway will be inaugurated on June 17th by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. Spanning 91.35 kilometers and costing Rs 7,283.28 crore, the expressway connects Gorakhpur to the Purvanchal Expressway in Azamgarh, significantly improving connectivity in eastern Uttar Pradesh. It is expected to reduce travel time between Gorakhpur and Lucknow to 3.5 hours. The expressway will benefit four districts: Gorakhpur, Sant Kabir Nagar, Ambedkar Nagar, and Azamgarh, by easing transportation and boosting local economies. An industrial corridor is planned along the expressway, attracting investments in various sectors. The project is designed to be sustainable, reducing fuel consumption and emissions.
