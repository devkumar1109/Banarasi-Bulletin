Ballia Suicide: Love, Calls, and YouTube Lead to Tru...

2025-03-28T10:05:00+00:00

A 20-year-old woman was found hanging from a tree in Ballia district on March 23. Initially suspected to be a murder, the case was solved by Ballia police after a thorough investigation. The post-mortem revealed no external injuries, and the woman's phone records indicated frequent calls to her fiancé and a local man in the Indian Army. She had also watched YouTube videos related to suicide before her death. Based on this evidence, the police concluded the case as suicide. No suspects are currently involved.
