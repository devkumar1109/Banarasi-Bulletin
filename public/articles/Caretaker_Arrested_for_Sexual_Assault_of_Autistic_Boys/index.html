Caretaker Arrested for Sexual Assault of Autistic Boys

2025-06-02T08:04:00+00:00

A 29-year-old caretaker at a special school in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, has been arrested for sexually assaulting two autistic boys, aged 9 and 13. The incident came to light when the boys' mother visited them and they revealed their ordeal. The caretaker, identified as Monu Pal, allegedly beat the children, burned them with cigarettes, and raped them. A case has been registered against Pal under sections of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. The police have sent the children for medical examination and informed the Child Welfare Committee. CCTV footage and other evidence from the school have been seized.
