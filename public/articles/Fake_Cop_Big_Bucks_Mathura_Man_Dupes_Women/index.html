Fake Cop, Big Bucks: Mathura Man Dupes Women

2025-06-11T19:04:00+00:00

Munfed (26) from Mathura was arrested in Varanasi for duping multiple women of lakhs by posing as Deepak Kumar Ranawat, a public relations officer of the police commissionerate. He used fake appointment letters and a police sub-inspector's uniform to lure victims seeking government jobs. Munfed confessed to defrauding Anita Yadav of Mainpuri (Rs 16 lakh), Poonam Arela of Agra, Kavita Jaiswal of Palwal, and several others. He obtained personal details of police officials from social media to impersonate them. Anita Yadav filed a complaint after paying Rs 16 lakh for her son's alleged job, which never materialized. Deepak Kumar Ranawat has been removed from his post and attached to police lines.
