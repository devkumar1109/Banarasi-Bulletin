Varanasi Horror: Seven Days of Terror, 23 Accused

2025-04-09T04:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over a period of seven days. The police found the victim unconscious on April 4th and have arrested nine accused, who were sent to judicial custody. The victim's father stated she was lured and taken away, intoxicated, and repeatedly raped. According to the police, the involvement of so many men suggests a planned attempt. Eleven of the accused remain unidentified. The Uttar Pradesh Police are continuing their investigation and search for the remaining suspects.
