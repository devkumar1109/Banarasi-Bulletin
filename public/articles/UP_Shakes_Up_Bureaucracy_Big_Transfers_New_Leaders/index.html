UP Shakes Up Bureaucracy: Big Transfers, New Leaders

2025-04-22T14:30:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government, led by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, conducted a major bureaucratic reshuffle, transferring 33 IAS officers and 3 senior IPS officers. Key changes include Vishal Singh appointed as the new Director of Information. Eleven district magistrates were transferred, including S Rajalingam, the incumbent DM of Varanasi, who was promoted to Varanasi Divisional Commissioner, and Satyendra Kumar, appointed as the new DM of Varanasi. Other notable appointments include Prerna Sharma, Hapur's DM, as Director of SUDA, and Amit Gupta, Principal Secretary (Stamps and Registration), as Principal Secretary (Transport) and Chairman of the UP Transport Corporation.
