Drunk Driving Crash: Vadodara Road Rage

2025-03-15T15:09:00+00:00

A car crash in Vadodara, Gujarat, has resulted in the death of a woman and injuries to four others. Rakshit Chaurasia, a 23-year-old law student, allegedly driving under the influence of alcohol, crashed his car into multiple vehicles. Chaurasia claims the car skidded due to potholes, denies being drunk, and expresses regret for the incident. Eyewitness footage shows Chaurasia appearing disoriented and shouting "Another round?" CCTV footage captures the car colliding with two scooters, dragging the riders. Janhvi Kapoor condemned the incident on social media, calling it "appalling and enraging".
