Bhojpuri Star's Viral Hotel Row

2025-05-05T07:01:00+00:00

Bhojpuri actress Anjana Singh publicly berated and dragged a crew member on the road in Basti, Uttar Pradesh over a hotel booking issue. The incident occurred after Anjana arrived at Hotel Maharaj late at night to find no reservation in her name. Video footage shows her angrily confronting the production team, accusing them of unprofessionalism. Anjana claimed she had instructed the team to book a new hotel, but wasn't informed about it. She continued shooting despite the issue, only to discover the lack of booking when she arrived at the hotel with her staff.
