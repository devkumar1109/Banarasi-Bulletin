Cattle Smugglers Foiled in Fiery Ghazipur Chase

2025-05-07T19:07:00+00:00

Two cattle smugglers, Sonu Yadav and Sabhajeet Yadav alias Shalu, were arrested following an encounter with police in Ghazipur district, Uttar Pradesh. The encounter occurred when police signaled a pickup van carrying the smugglers to stop, but the driver attempted to run over the officers and flee. During the ensuing chase, the van crashed and the smugglers opened fire at the police, who retaliated in self-defense, injuring both smugglers. Police recovered two country-made pistols, live cartridges, 11 cattle, and the pickup van from the suspects. Sonu Yadav has eight previous criminal cases against him, while Sabhajeet Yadav has two.
