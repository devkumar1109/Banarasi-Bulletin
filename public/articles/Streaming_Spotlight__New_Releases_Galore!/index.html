Streaming Spotlight: New Releases Galore!

2025-05-05T07:00:00+00:00

This week sees a diverse range of new releases on various OTT platforms. Netflix offers "Britain and The Blitz" documentary, the second season of survival competition "The Devil's Plan", and sports docuseries "Full Speed Season 2". Action thriller "Last Bullet" and romantic series "Forever" also premiere on Netflix. "Nonnas", starring Vince Vaughn and Susan Sarandon, is a heartwarming comedy about a man opening an Italian restaurant run by grandmothers. Netflix also brings the modern-day Indian royal rom-com "The Royals", starring Ishaan Khatter and Bhumi Pednekar. Amazon Prime Video presents the family series "Gram Chikitsalay", a story about a city doctor in a remote village. JioHotstar features the second season of "Poker Face" and "Conan O'Brien Must Go", with Bardem and Waititi joining Conan. ZEE5 releases the Bengali vigilante action thriller "Bohurupi".
