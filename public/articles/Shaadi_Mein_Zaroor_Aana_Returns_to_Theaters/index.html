"Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana" Returns to Theaters

2025-03-03T09:31:00+00:00

Rajkummar Rao and Kriti Kharbanda's 2017 film "Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana" is set to re-release in theaters on March 7th. The announcement was made by Kriti Kharbanda on social media, who expressed excitement for fans to experience the film again on the big screen. "Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana" is a love story about Satyendra and Aarti who navigate the complexities of arranged marriage, personal aspirations, and societal expectations. Directed by Ratnaa Sinha and produced by Vinod Bachchan, the film explores themes of love, betrayal, and personal growth. This re-release follows the trend of successful theatrical re-runs of other Bollywood films like "Sanam Teri Kasam" and "Bareilly Ki Barfi".
