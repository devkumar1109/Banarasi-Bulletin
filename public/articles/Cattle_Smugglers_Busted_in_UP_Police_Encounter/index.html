Cattle Smugglers Busted in UP Police Encounter

2025-05-07T19:07:00+00:00

Two cattle smugglers, Sonu Yadav and Sabhajeet Yadav alias Shalu, were arrested following an encounter with police in Ghazipur district, Uttar Pradesh. The encounter took place when the police tried to stop a pickup van carrying 11 cattle. The smugglers opened fire at the police, who retaliated in self-defense, injuring both suspects. Police recovered two country-made pistols, live cartridges, the pickup van, and the 11 cattle. Sonu Yadav has eight criminal cases pending against him, while Sabhajeet Yadav has two.
