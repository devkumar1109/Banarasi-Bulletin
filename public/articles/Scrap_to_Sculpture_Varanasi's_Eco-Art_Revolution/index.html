Scrap to Sculpture: Varanasi's Eco-Art Revolution

2025-05-08T18:20:00+00:00

Varanasi is undergoing a beautification project involving 62 unique sculptures crafted from scrap materials. The project, a collaboration between the Varanasi development authority, Banaras locomotive works, and the ministries of railways and culture, aims to promote environmental conservation and artistic creativity. 35 sculptures are already installed at various locations throughout the city, with another 27 set to be placed soon. The sculptures, representing themes like women's empowerment, yoga, sports, and cultural heritage, are strategically placed to reflect the unique character of each location. Landscaping, greenery, and night lighting are also being incorporated to enhance the overall aesthetic appeal. The initiative aims not only to beautify Varanasi but also to highlight its social and cultural values while promoting sustainable development through the reuse of materials.
