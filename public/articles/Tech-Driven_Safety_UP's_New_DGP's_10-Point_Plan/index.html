Tech-Driven Safety: UP's New DGP's 10-Point Plan

2025-06-02T10:54:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's new DGP, Rajeev Krishna, has announced a 10-point policing roadmap focused on technology, zero tolerance for crime, and improved public service. Krishna pledged to leverage AI for smarter policing and cybercrime prevention, while also prioritizing women's safety and public grievance redressal. He aims to enhance police professionalism through specialized training and improve citizen services. The DGP emphasized a strict approach against all crime, particularly organized crime, and promised a safe environment for citizens. Women's safety will be a core focus, with initiatives aimed at prevention and empowerment.
