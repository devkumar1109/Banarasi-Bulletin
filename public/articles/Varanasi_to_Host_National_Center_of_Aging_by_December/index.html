Varanasi to Host National Center of Aging by December

2025-03-03T18:18:00+00:00

The Regional Geriatric Centre in Varanasi is being upgraded to a 200-bed National Centre of Aging (NCA) by December. Approved by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare in February 2024, the project was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The NCA will feature specialized departments, diagnostic facilities, physiotherapy services, a research lab, and intensive care. It aims to increase postgraduate MD geriatric seats to 15 per year, enhancing training and research in geriatric medicine. The NCA will provide tertiary care, serve as a training hub, and collaborate with primary and secondary care services to ensure comprehensive elder care.
