Varanasi to Host National Center of Aging by December.

2025-03-03T18:18:00+00:00

The Regional Geriatric Centre in Varanasi is being upgraded to a 200-bed National Centre of Aging (NCA) by December. Approved by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare in February 2024, the project is being constructed by HLL Infra Tech services ltd. The NCA, spanning 2,045 square meters, will offer specialized geriatric care, including OPDs, diagnostic facilities, physiotherapy, and research labs. It aims to increase postgraduate MD geriatric seats to 15 per year, bolstering geriatric care expertise. The NCA will serve as a hub for training and capacity building in geriatric medicine, offering short-term courses for healthcare professionals. It will also collaborate with primary and secondary care services to ensure comprehensive care for elderly patients.
