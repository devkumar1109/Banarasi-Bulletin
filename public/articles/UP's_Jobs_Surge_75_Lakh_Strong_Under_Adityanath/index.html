UP's Jobs Surge: 7.5 Lakh Strong Under Adityanath

2025-03-22T10:39:00+00:00

Over 7.5 lakh government jobs have been created in Uttar Pradesh in the last eight years under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's leadership. Approximately 95,000 candidates were selected through the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) and the Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC). UPPSC selected 48,593 candidates, with a peak of 13,893 selections in 2019-20, while UPSSSC selected 46,032 candidates, with a high of 11,800 selections in 2022-23. The government launched the e-Adhiachan portal to ensure transparency and efficiency in the recruitment process.
