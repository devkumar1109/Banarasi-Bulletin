Shriya Pilgaonkar: Learning for "Chhal Kapat" Role

2025-06-09T14:01:00+00:00

Bollywood actress Shriya Pilgaonkar met with women police officers of Uttar Pradesh, including ADG Padmaja Chauhan. She was briefed about the workings of the state-of-the-art 1090 helpline dedicated to women's safety. This meeting provided Shriya with valuable insight for her role as Inspector Devika in the Zee5 series "Chhal Kapat: The Deception," where she portrays a police officer who is also a survivor of domestic violence. The series revolves around a murder mystery during a wedding celebration, with Inspector Devika tasked with unraveling the truth while confronting her own past trauma.
