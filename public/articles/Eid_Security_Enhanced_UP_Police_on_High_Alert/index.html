Eid Security Enhanced: UP Police on High Alert

2025-03-28T04:22:00+00:00

In anticipation of Eid-ul-Fitr, Alvida Jumma, and Cheti Chand, Gautam Buddha Nagar police have enforced Section 163 of the Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) from March 28 to March 31. This measure aims to bolster security and maintain order during the festivities. Uttar Pradesh police have issued comprehensive security directives, emphasizing police presence in sensitive areas, strategic deployment of forces, and preventive measures against potential troublemakers. DGP Prashant Kumar has instructed officials to engage with religious leaders, conduct flag marches, and maintain close monitoring of social media platforms. Restrictions on loudspeakers during prayers in Sambhal have also been implemented.
