Taj Mahal Gets Drone-Proof Shield

2025-05-25T15:16:00+00:00

The Taj Mahal will soon be protected by an anti-drone system to counter potential aerial threats. The decision follows heightened tensions after India's cross-border strikes on Pakistan in response to the Pahalgam massacre. The system, with a 7-8 kilometer range, will jam drone signals within a 200-meter radius of the main dome, rendering them inoperable. A dedicated police team is being trained to operate the equipment and respond to intercepted drones. Installation is expected to be completed soon.
