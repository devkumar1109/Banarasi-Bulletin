Krishna Takes Helm: Fifth Acting UP DGP in Two Years

2025-06-01T01:30:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991-batch IPS officer, is the new acting DGP of Uttar Pradesh, succeeding Prashant Kumar. This marks the fifth "acting" appointment since 2022 following Mukul Goel's removal as permanent DGP for "neglect of duty". Krishna is known for his integrity and innovation, having served as the founding chief of UP's ATS and implementing tech-driven policing initiatives like Operation Pehchaan. He has held key positions including SSP in Agra, Lucknow, and Bareilly, and is credited with decisive action against kidnapping gangs. Krishna comes from a family with a strong public service background. His wife is an IRS officer and his brother-in-law is an MLA.
