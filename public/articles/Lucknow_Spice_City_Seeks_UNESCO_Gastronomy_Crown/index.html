Lucknow: Spice City Seeks UNESCO Gastronomy Crown

2025-06-08T05:59:00+00:00

Lucknow, India, is vying for the UNESCO Creative City of Gastronomy title. The city is renowned for its rich culinary heritage, particularly its Mughlai cuisine and traditional sweets. Lucknow's application highlights its cultural significance, historical legacy, and commitment to preserving and promoting its culinary traditions.
