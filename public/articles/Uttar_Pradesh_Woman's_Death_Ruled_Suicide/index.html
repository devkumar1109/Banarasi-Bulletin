Uttar Pradesh Woman's Death Ruled Suicide

2025-03-28T10:05:00+00:00

A 20-year-old woman in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, was found hanging from a tree on March 23. Initial suspicion pointed towards murder due to her hands being tied. However, the post-mortem report revealed the cause of death as hanging with no external injuries. Investigation revealed the woman had watched YouTube videos related to suicide before her death. Her phone contained numerous calls to her fiance and a local army man, suggesting a possible suicide. Based on evidence, police have ruled out any involvement of suspects and concluded the case as suicide.
