Varanasi Battles Disease with Dastak Campaign

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1 to 31 to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, chikungunya, and diarrhea. The campaign was flagged off by UP ministers Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra 'Dayalu'. MLA Saurabh Srivastava highlighted the success in reducing cases of these diseases compared to previous years. According to the Chief Medical Officer, 68 dengue hotspots were identified in urban areas and 56 in rural areas. The Dastak campaign, focused on door-to-door health checkups, will run from April 10 to 31.
