Teja Students Shine in FIT India Quiz

2025-03-13T13:56:00+00:00

Teja Vidyalaya students Palla Hruthik and Kondru Naveen Kumar, both Class 10 students, secured the fourth position in the FIT India Quiz competition. The online competition, hosted by the Sports Authority of India and Union Ministry of Sports, saw 32 teams from across India compete. The duo won a cash prize of Rs.5 lakh. Sun Beam School, Varanasi won first place and Rs.25 lakh, Assisi Vidyaniketan, Ernakulam came second with Rs.15 lakh, and DAV School Gurugram, Haryana won third place and Rs.10 lakh.
