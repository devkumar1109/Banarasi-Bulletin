Varanasi Runs and Sails for Clean Ganga

2025-03-22T19:23:00+00:00

World Water Day was celebrated in Varanasi with a marathon and dolphin safari. The "Run for Clean Ganga" marathon, organized by Sankat Mochan Foundation and Mothers for Mother, saw over 6 km of running from Bhainsasur Ghat to Tulsi Ghat. Abhishek Yadav won the men's category, and Rebi Pal won the women's category. The event highlighted the importance of the Ganga's purification and featured senior citizen participation. The forest department, in collaboration with NGO Dolphin Friends, celebrated World Water Day and Earth Hour at Dhakwa Ghat with a "Dolphin Safari" launch. The safari aims to educate the public about Gangetic dolphin conservation efforts.
