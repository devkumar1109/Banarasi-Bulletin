Dharma Divide: BJP vs. SP-Congress on Unity

2025-06-11T12:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Minister Mayankeshwar Sharan Singh accused the Samajwadi Party (SP) and Congress of dividing 'Sanatan Dharma and Hindutva,' while promoting casteism and social disintegration. Singh praised Prime Minister Modi's leadership and India's progress, highlighting advances in defense and economic self-reliance. SP and Congress leaders refuted Singh's allegations, claiming the BJP is the real force behind social division and inter-group hatred. They criticized BJP's political strategy as divisive and polarizing, emphasizing their commitment to inclusive politics.
