UP School Summer Camp: Learning, Growing, Shining

2025-05-21T15:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Director General of School Education, Kanchan Verma, inaugurated Summer Camp 2025 for government school students on May 21st. The camp, held across the state, emphasizes holistic development through activities like yoga, health talks, storytelling, music sharing, digital literacy, traditional arts and crafts, environmental awareness, sports, and leadership building. The camp will conclude on June 10th with a closing ceremony and awards for outstanding students. The camp aims to enhance students' mental, emotional, and physical development through a balanced program of activities.
