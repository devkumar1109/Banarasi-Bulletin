BJP Leader Shoots Family in Suspected Honor Killing

2025-03-22T12:11:00+00:00

BJP leader Yogesh Rohilla shot his wife and three children in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh. His son and 11-year-old daughter died, while his wife and another child are in critical condition. Rohilla suspected his wife of infidelity, which allegedly motivated the act. He was apprehended at the scene with the weapon used in the crime. Police officials, including Saharanpur SSP Rohit Sajwan, rushed to the spot and launched an investigation. The incident occurred in Sangatheda village, causing shock and panic among residents.
