Yadav Son Sparks Security Row at Kashi Vishwanath Temple

2025-06-15T03:20:00+00:00

Tej Pratap Yadav, son of RJD leader Lalu Prasad Yadav, sparked controversy after a video surfaced showing him inside the 'Red Zone' of Varanasi's Kashi Vishwanath Temple. The 'Red Zone' is a high-security area within the temple complex where mobile phones and unauthorized access are strictly prohibited. The temple administration has ordered an investigation by the CRPF and local police. The video, posted by Yadav himself, shows him inside the restricted zone. He captioned it with a prayer to Lord Vishwanath and Mother Ganga. The temple CEO, Vishwa Bhushan Mishra, stated that action will be taken against any personnel found to be responsible for the security breach.
