Varanasi Honors Ambedkar's Legacy

2025-04-14T18:49:00+00:00

Varanasi observed Ambedkar's birth anniversary with various events. A program at the Collectorate, chaired by DM S Rajalingam, highlighted Ambedkar's role in drafting the Indian Constitution. Minister Ravindra Jaiswal paid tribute at Ambedkar Park, where participants resolved to follow his ideals. Varanasi Development Authority officials also paid homage. A two-day national seminar and competitive programs at BHU concluded with a blood donation camp and a seminar on Ambedkar's contributions to justice, brotherhood, equality, economics, and women's empowerment.
