Roorkee Restaurant Attack: Lover Sets Girlfriend Ablaze

2025-04-24T03:19:00+00:00

In Roorkee, Uttarakhand, a 28-year-old man named Prince Kumar attacked his 27-year-old girlfriend at a restaurant. He stabbed her, poured petrol on her, and set her ablaze. Following the attack, Kumar attempted suicide by slitting his throat. Both are in critical condition and have been transferred to a Dehradun medical facility. The incident stemmed from Kumar's family disapproving of their relationship, leading the woman's family to arrange her marriage elsewhere. Police are currently investigating the case.
