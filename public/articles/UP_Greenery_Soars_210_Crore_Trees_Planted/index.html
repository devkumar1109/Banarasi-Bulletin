UP Greenery Soars: 210 Crore Trees Planted

2025-03-13T09:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated that 210 crore trees have been planted across the state in the last eight years, resulting in a significant increase in forest cover. The government is collaborating with external institutions to monitor the survival rate of these plantations, reporting 70-75% survival for government-planted trees and 65-70% for privately and voluntarily planted trees. Adityanath emphasized the importance of these efforts in combating carbon emissions amidst rapid urbanization and industrial growth. He expressed concern about rising pollution in the Delhi-NCR region, highlighting the impact of burning wood and coal on air quality and public health, urging a switch to cleaner fuels.
