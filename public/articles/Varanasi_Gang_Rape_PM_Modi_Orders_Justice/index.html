Varanasi Gang Rape: PM Modi Orders Justice

2025-04-12T00:48:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi was allegedly gang-raped by 23 men over six days between March 29 and April 4. The perpetrators drugged and moved her between hotels and a hookah bar in the city. The victim's mother stated that her daughter is not well after the trauma and that she wants to meet Prime Minister Narendra Modi to demand justice. PM Modi, during a visit to Varanasi, received a briefing about the case and ordered the strictest action against those involved. 12 arrests have been made so far in connection with the case.
