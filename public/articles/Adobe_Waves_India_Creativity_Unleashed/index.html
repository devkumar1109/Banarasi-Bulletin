Adobe Waves India: Creativity Unleashed

2025-05-01T17:59:00+00:00

At the WAVES Summit, Adobe announced initiatives to bolster India's creative economy. They plan to open a new office in Noida by early 2026, their third in Uttar Pradesh. Adobe Express, their content creation app, has tripled monthly active users in India and will soon offer localized templates and lower pricing plans. A partnership with TCS will launch the Creative Experience Studio (ACES), using AI to deliver personalized content experiences. Adobe will also provide free Creative Cloud access, curriculum development, and faculty training to the Indian Institute of Creative Technology (IICT). They are partnering with Accenture to upskill students from design institutes and establish creativity clubs in schools. Finally, Adobe will offer discounted Creative Cloud subscriptions to sellers on the WAVES Bazaar marketplace and support top talent from the Creatosphere challenge.
