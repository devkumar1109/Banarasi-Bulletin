UP Cop's Son Cracks UPSC at Rank 235

2025-04-25T09:27:00+00:00

Soham Lal, son of Uttar Pradesh Police Superintendent of Police Shailendra Lal, has secured an All-India Rank of 235 in the UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) 2024. A native of Orai, Jalaun district, Soham is a graduate of Hindu College, Delhi University, where he studied Economics. This is his fourth attempt at the CSE. Soham attributes his success to his family's unwavering support and guidance.
