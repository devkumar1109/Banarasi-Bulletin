Hockey Frenzy: Day One Dominated

2025-04-05T01:48:00+00:00

Six teams secured victories on the first day of the 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025 in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. Hockey Rajasthan defeated Tripura Hockey 9-1, with Vishal Kumar scoring four goals. Hockey Arunachal won 5-0 against Hockey Jammu & Kashmir, while Chhattisgarh Hockey triumphed 10-0 over Hockey Gujarat. Hockey Himachal beat Hockey Association of Bihar 6-2, and Hockey Chandigarh secured a 13-1 victory against Hockey Andhra Pradesh. Goans Hockey also won their match.
