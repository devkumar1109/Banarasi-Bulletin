Boyfriend Murders Girlfriend, Stages Suicide

2025-04-02T17:35:00+00:00

A 20-year-old woman, Aishwarya Marrivanivalasa, was murdered by her boyfriend P Rambabu (27) in Vizag city. Rambabu strangled Aishwarya after a heated argument when she asked him to marry her. He took her body to a remote location in Manyam district and attempted to stage it as suicide by hanging. Police found strangulation marks and determined it was a murder. Rambabu has been arrested, and the case will be transferred to Arilova police station in Vizag for further investigation. Two of Rambabu's friends, who helped him dispose of the body, will also be arrested and charged with destroying evidence.
