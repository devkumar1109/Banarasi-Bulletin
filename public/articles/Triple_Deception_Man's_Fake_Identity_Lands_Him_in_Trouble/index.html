Triple Deception: Man's Fake Identity Lands Him in Trouble

2025-03-22T15:51:00+00:00

Rajan, a man in Uttar Pradesh, allegedly tricked three women into marriage by falsely claiming to be a widower and an excise department employee. He married Kiran in 2022, persuading her to take a Rs 42 lakh loan for a house. Kiran discovered Rajan's deception and filed a police complaint. Investigations revealed Rajan had previously married Sarita in 2014 and had also deceived a third woman, Neelima Singh. Sarita had filed an FIR against him in 2016 after discovering his lies. Police are investigating if Rajan has defrauded other women.
