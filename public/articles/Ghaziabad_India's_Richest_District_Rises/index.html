Ghaziabad: India's Richest District Rises

2025-06-10T06:27:00+00:00

Ghaziabad district in Uttar Pradesh is now India's richest district with only 7 percent of its population living below the poverty line. This information comes from a 2021 Niti Aayog report. While Ghaziabad had a poverty rate of 17 percent in 2016, the Niti Aayog reevaluated poverty levels based on health, education, and standard of living indicators. These indicators included nutrition, maternal and child health, birth and death rates, school attendance, and property ownership. The report states this comprehensive framework was used to determine the current poverty rate of 7 percent.
