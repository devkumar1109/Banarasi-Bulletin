Son Kills Father Over Land Dispute in Jaunpur

2025-04-21T18:25:00+00:00

Akhilesh, 35, was arrested for murdering his father, Prabhakar Singh, 65, in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh. Akhilesh confessed to hitting his father with a stick during a dispute over money from the sale of family land. The incident occurred on Friday in Deeh Ashrafabad. Akhilesh tried to mislead the police by changing clothes and spending time at a roadside dhaba before returning home. He later informed the police about the murder. Evidence, including a video of the argument and threats from Akhilesh's phone, led to his arrest.
