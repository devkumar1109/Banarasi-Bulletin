China-Linked Cyber Crime Ring Busted

2025-02-28T10:54:00+00:00

The UP STF uncovered an international cybercrime syndicate linked to Chinese nationals. Two key operatives were arrested on February 25, 2025. The syndicate used "digital arrests" to coerce victims into transferring funds, deceptive gaming and trading platforms to exploit victims, and mule accounts for money laundering. They employed Chinese messaging apps for illegal banking transactions and API tools to remotely link bank accounts. The syndicate laundered money through USDT cryptocurrency wallets, potentially funding activities aligned with China's geopolitical ambitions. The STF seized debit/credit cards, Aadhaar and PAN cards, passports, mobile phones, and cash.
