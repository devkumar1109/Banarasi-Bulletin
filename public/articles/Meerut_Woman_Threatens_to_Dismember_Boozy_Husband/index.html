Meerut Woman Threatens to Dismember Boozy Husband

2025-03-27T16:09:00+00:00

In Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, a woman threatened to dismember her alcoholic husband and seal his body parts in a drum if he didn't change his behavior. The husband, Bablu Kumar, reportedly has a troubled relationship with his wife due to his alcohol addiction. The couple, married for five years, has two children. This incident follows the recent "Muskan-Sahil" case in which a woman and her lover murdered her husband and disposed of his body parts in a drum. The recent case brings to light the dangers of domestic violence and substance abuse.
