UP DGME Sets Online Counseling Guidelines for PCPNDT Act

2025-03-23T04:01:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Directorate General of Medical Education (DGME) has released guidelines for the online counseling process for the Pre-Conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques (PCPNDT) Act, 2024. The guidelines are intended for healthcare professionals and provide details on the registration and login process for the online counseling platform. The DGME is responsible for implementing the PCPNDT Act, which aims to prevent sex-selective abortions and ensure the safe and ethical use of prenatal diagnostic techniques.
