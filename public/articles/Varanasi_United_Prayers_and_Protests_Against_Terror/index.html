Varanasi United: Prayers and Protests Against Terror

2025-04-23T17:40:00+00:00

Protests and prayers erupted across Varanasi following the Pahalgam terror attack. Kashi Vishwanath Dham hosted an Ati Rudra recital, while the BJP postponed a Waqf Amendment awareness drive. Muslim institutions, including Anjuman Intejamia Masajid and the Muslim Mahila Foundation, condemned the attack. A joint protest march by the Vishal Bharat Sansthan and Muslim Women's Foundation concluded with a symbolic cremation of terrorists. Chanting slogans against Pakistan, they demanded action against terrorism and urged religious leaders to issue a Fatwa against terrorists. The BJP Kashi region president and spokesman announced the postponement of scheduled workshops in mourning for the victims. Marches and burning of effigies of terrorists were also observed by various groups across Varanasi.
