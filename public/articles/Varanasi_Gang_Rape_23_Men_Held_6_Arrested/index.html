Varanasi Gang Rape: 23 Men Held, 6 Arrested

2025-04-07T10:10:00+00:00

A 19-year-old girl in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh was allegedly gang-raped by over 23 men over seven days (March 29 to April 4). The survivor was taken to various hotels and hookah bars where the accused committed the crime. Six accused have been arrested so far, and a case has been registered under the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita (BNS) against all 23 accused. The survivor returned home on April 4 and initially did not disclose the rape, but her family filed a police report on April 6 after she narrated the ordeal. Police are investigating and trying to apprehend the remaining accused.
