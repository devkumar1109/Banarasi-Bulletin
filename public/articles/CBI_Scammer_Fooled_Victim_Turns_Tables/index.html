CBI Scammer Fooled, Victim Turns Tables

2025-03-18T00:49:00+00:00

A man from Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, tricked an online scammer who tried to extort money from him. The scammer impersonated a CBI official, threatening Bhupendra Singh with a fabricated case involving obscene videos. Singh, realizing the scam, devised a plan and pretended to need Rs 3,000 to recover a pawned gold chain. The scammer, falling for the ruse, sent Rs 10,000 instead of the demanded Rs 16,000. The incident has gone viral on social media, with users praising Singh's cleverness and highlighting the increasing prevalence of digital arrest scams.
