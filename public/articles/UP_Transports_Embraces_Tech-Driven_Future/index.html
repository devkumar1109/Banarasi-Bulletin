U.P. Transports Embraces Tech-Driven Future

2025-03-09T11:04:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is training transport department officials in advanced technology. The Institute of Driving Training and Research (IDTR) in Rae Bareli is being developed as a Centre of Excellence for these programs. A dedicated training cell, headed by the additional transport commissioner, oversees the initiatives. The training will cover latest technology, data-driven policy-making, enforcement strategies, and e-governance. Refresher courses for existing staff and training for new officers will be conducted regularly. The curriculum will be tailored to Uttar Pradesh's needs, national trends, and global best practices.
