Yadav Slams BJP: Hitler Parallels, Corruption Claims

2025-04-15T18:46:00+00:00

Samajwadi Party president Akhilesh Yadav criticized the BJP, comparing their events to Hitler's and calling Karni Sena their "troopers." He accused the BJP of spending lavishly to defame the SP and Congress while endangering the Constitution. Akhilesh also targeted the Yogi Adityanath government's zero-tolerance policy against crime, citing the deaths of a Dalit youth in Azamgarh police custody and a Brahmin in Basti. He alleged the government prioritizes protecting the accused over helping victims. Additionally, Akhilesh criticized the BJP's handling of investments and development, mocking their land bank initiative. He accused them of prioritizing commissions over incentives and highlighted corruption within the government.
