Varanasi Fights Back: Disease Control Campaign Kicks Off

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1st to 31st to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, and chikungunya. The campaign includes anti-larvae spraying, fogging, and public awareness initiatives. The district has seen a significant decrease in cases of these diseases compared to previous years, with only 22 dengue cases, 2 malaria cases, and 18 chikungunya cases reported in 2025 so far. 68 dengue hotspots have been identified in urban areas and 56 in rural areas. The Dastak campaign, focusing on door-to-door health checks, will run from April 10th to 31st.
