Hockey India National Championship: Up for Grabs

2025-04-03T18:11:00+00:00

The 15th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2025 will be held from April 4 to April 15 at the Major Dhyan Chand Hockey Stadium in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh. The tournament will feature a new Division-based format with promotion and relegation, similar to the Senior Women National Championship. The 30 participating teams are divided into three divisions: Division A, Division B, and Division C. Division A comprises the top 12 teams, including defending champions Hockey Association of Odisha and runners-up Hockey Haryana. Teams in Division B will compete for promotion to Division A, while Division C teams will fight for a spot in Division B.
