Property Dealer Brutally Murdered in Chandauli

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29) from Ghazipur, was murdered in Chandauli district on Thursday night. Pawan was attacked by unidentified assailants while transporting water to his construction site near Naikot village. He was brutally beaten with bricks and rods, and later died at the hospital. Senior police officials have arrived at the scene and multiple teams are investigating the case.
