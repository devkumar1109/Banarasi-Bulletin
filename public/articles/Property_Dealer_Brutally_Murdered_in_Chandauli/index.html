Property Dealer Brutally Murdered in Chandauli

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29), was murdered in Chandauli district, Uttar Pradesh, on Thursday night. Yadav, a resident of Ghazipur district, was attacked by unknown assailants while transporting water from Janso Ki Madai to his under-construction house in Naikot village. The assailants used bricks and rods to attack him, and he succumbed to his injuries at the hospital. Senior police officials have reached the spot and multiple teams are investigating the case.
