Foxconn Eyes Uttar Pradesh for New Electronics Hub

2025-04-14T10:38:00+00:00

Foxconn, the major iPhone producer, is in talks with the Uttar Pradesh government to establish an electronics manufacturing unit in the state. Discussions were initiated by Invest UP during the land allocation for Foxconn's joint venture with HCL, Vama Sundari, which focuses on chip manufacturing. While the specific products Foxconn will manufacture in Uttar Pradesh are yet to be determined, the state government has offered 300 acres of land under YEIDA. The development comes amidst a surge in India's mobile phone exports, with iPhones accounting for Rs 1.5 lakh crore of the smartphone export value in FY 2024-25.
