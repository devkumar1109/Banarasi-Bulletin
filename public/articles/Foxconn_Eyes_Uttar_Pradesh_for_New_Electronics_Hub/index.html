Foxconn Eyes Uttar Pradesh for New Electronics Hub

2025-04-14T10:38:00+00:00

Foxconn is in talks with the Uttar Pradesh government to establish an electronics manufacturing unit in the state. Discussions were initiated by Invest UP during the land allocation for the HCL-Foxconn chip joint venture, Vama Sundari. The state government proposed 300 acres of land under YEIDA for the potential Foxconn unit. While details on the type of products to be manufactured are still under discussion, Foxconn's investment could further boost Uttar Pradesh's growing electronics manufacturing sector. The state already houses production units for leading mobile phone brands like Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Dixon, and Lava, contributing to over 80% of India's smartphone market share.
