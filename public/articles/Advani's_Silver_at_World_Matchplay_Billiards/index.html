Advani's Silver at World Matchplay Billiards

2025-04-12T16:36:00+00:00

Pankaj Advani won a silver medal at the WBL World Matchplay Billiards Championship in New Delhi. He lost to David Causier in a close final, 8-7. Advani initially led 2-0 but Causier rallied to win the decider.
