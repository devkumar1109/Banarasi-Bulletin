Adityanath Boosts BHU Health Infrastructure

2025-05-12T17:45:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inspected health infrastructure projects at Banaras Hindu University (BHU). He reviewed the construction of the National Centre for Ageing at IMS-BHU, a Rs 147.39 crore project, and the new Critical Care Centre at the Trauma Centre, costing Rs 119.47 crore. The National Centre for Ageing, the third in India after AIIMS Delhi and Madras Medical College, will provide comprehensive geriatric care with 200 beds. The Critical Care Centre will have a 150-bed unit, three modular operation theatres, intensive care units, and modern amenities. A separate 40-bed burn ward is also being established at the Trauma Centre.
