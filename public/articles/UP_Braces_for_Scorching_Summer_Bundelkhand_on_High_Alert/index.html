UP Braces for Scorching Summer, Bundelkhand on High Alert

2025-04-06T06:14:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is predicted to experience a severe summer with prolonged heat waves from April to June, particularly impacting the Bundelkhand region. The intense heat poses significant risks to human and animal health. The state government is taking preventive measures, instructing district administrations to implement safety protocols and hospitals to prepare for an increase in heat-related illnesses. Meteorological officials warn of severe consequences for Bundelkhand, urging residents to take precautions.
