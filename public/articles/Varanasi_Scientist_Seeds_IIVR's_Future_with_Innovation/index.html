Varanasi Scientist Seeds IIVR's Future with Innovation

2025-04-07T18:47:00+00:00

Rajesh Kumar, a vegetable scientist from Varanasi, has been appointed the new director of the ICAR-Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR). Kumar has a distinguished career, having developed 11 high-yielding vegetable varieties and hybrids, and published numerous research papers. He has also received several prestigious fellowships and awards for his contributions to the field. As director, Kumar plans to focus on developing climate-resilient and disease-resistant vegetable varieties using modern technologies like genome editing and CRISPR-Cas9.
