School Manager Shoots Student Over Dispute

2025-04-23T04:38:00+00:00

Hemant Singh, a Class 12 student in Varanasi, was shot dead by Ravi Singh, the manager of Gyandeep Public School, following a dispute with a teacher. Ravi Singh has been arrested, and the weapon used in the crime has been recovered. CCTV footage shows Ravi Singh taking Hemant inside a room at his residence where the shooting occurred. Hemant was rushed to a private hospital and then to BHU Trauma Centre but was declared dead. The DCP stated that Ravi Singh called Hemant to discuss the dispute with the teacher, leading to the fatal shooting. Forensic experts collected evidence from the scene, including the CCTV footage. Angry lawyers staged a protest outside the school before calling it off after officials assured them of the swift arrest of the accused.
