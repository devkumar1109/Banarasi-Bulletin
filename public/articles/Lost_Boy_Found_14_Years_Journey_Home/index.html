Lost Boy Found: 14 Years, Journey Home

2025-04-03T07:41:00+00:00

Neeraj Kannaujiya, who went missing from Mumbai at age 8, was reunited with his family in Varanasi after 14 years. He was found wandering in Gudur, Andhra Pradesh, by railway police and placed in the care of Swarna Bharat Trust. The Trust, specifically Deepa Venkat, the managing trustee, provided Neeraj with education, accommodation, and vocational training in plumbing, electrical work, driving, and vehicle repair. Neeraj, now a young man, was offered a job in Chennai by the Trust but chose to return to his family in Varanasi.
