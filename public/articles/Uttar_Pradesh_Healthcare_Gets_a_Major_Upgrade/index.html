Uttar Pradesh: Healthcare Gets a Major Upgrade

2025-03-17T14:38:00+00:00

In the past eight years, Uttar Pradesh has significantly improved its healthcare system. The state government has established 80 medical colleges, including 44 government and 36 private institutions, increasing MBBS and PG seats. The government has also focused on expanding AYUSH education, with 2,110 Ayurvedic, 254 Unani, and 1,585 Homeopathic hospitals currently operational. To further enhance medical research and innovation, IIT-Kanpur is establishing a School of Medical Research and Technology and a 500-bed super-speciality hospital. These initiatives aim to provide world-class medical education and healthcare to the people of Uttar Pradesh.
