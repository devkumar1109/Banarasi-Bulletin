Indian Idol Winner Critically Injured in Road Accident

2025-05-08T13:15:00+00:00

Indian Idol 12 winner Pawandeep Rajan was critically injured in a road accident in Uttar Pradesh on May 5. He suffered multiple major fractures and small injuries. Rajan underwent a 6-hour surgery to address some of his fractures and is currently in the Medical ICU. He is expected to undergo further surgery in 3-4 days. The accident occurred while Rajan was traveling to Delhi for an event in Ahmedabad.
