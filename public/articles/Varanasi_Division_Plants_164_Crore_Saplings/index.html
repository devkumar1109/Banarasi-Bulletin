Varanasi Division Plants 1.64 Crore Saplings

2025-05-14T23:44:00+00:00

A mega plantation drive will take place in July across the Varanasi division, aiming to plant 1.64 crore saplings. The division comprises Varanasi, Ghazipur, Chandauli, and Jaunpur districts. Varanasi district has a target of 280,000 saplings, Ghazipur 1,050,000, Jaunpur 1,455,400, and Chandauli 3,640,600. Various departments, including rural development, Panchayati Raj, agriculture, and horticulture, have been assigned targets to plant a variety of trees, including shade, fruit, teak, rosewood, mango, and mahua. Urban development, housing, public works, and defense departments will plant trees in urban areas, parks, and vacant spaces. The forest department will supply saplings to these departments free of cost.
