Hindi Row: Adityanath vs. Stalin on Language Policy

2025-03-27T08:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath criticized DMK leader MK Stalin for opposing the three-language policy, accusing him of creating divisions based on region and language. Adityanath stated that learning Hindi is important and questioned DMK's stance against it. In response, Stalin called Adityanath's remarks "political black comedy" and asserted that Tamil Nadu does not oppose any language but stands against its imposition and chauvinism. Stalin claimed the BJP is rattled by DMK's opposition to the three-language policy and delimitation.
