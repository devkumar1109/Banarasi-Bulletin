UP Exports: Aiming for Trillion-Dollar Growth

2025-04-18T16:10:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is finalizing a new export policy (2025-30) aimed at tripling exports to ₹5.10 lakh crore by 2030, up from ₹1.70 lakh crore in 2023-24. This move is crucial for the state's goal of becoming a trillion-dollar economy by 2029. The policy will offer several incentives to exporters, including increased financial assistance (₹25 lakh annually per unit) and capital subsidies up to ₹10 crore for export infrastructure development. A dedicated export promotion fund will support "Brand UP" at global events and provide up to ₹5 lakh to exporters for ECGC premium payments. The policy also focuses on promoting 12 champion service sectors identified by the Union government.
