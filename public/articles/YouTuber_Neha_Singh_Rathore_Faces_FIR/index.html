YouTuber Neha Singh Rathore Faces FIR

2025-05-21T09:54:00+00:00

A FIR has been registered against YouTuber Neha Singh Rathore in Varanasi for allegedly making derogatory remarks against Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The complaint was filed by the President of Shri Hanuman Sena, who accused Rathore of calling Modi a "coward" and "General Dyer." The case was registered under Section 197 (1)(a) and (d), and Section 353(2) of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS). This follows several other complaints filed against Rathore in Varanasi and a previous case registered in Lucknow in April for sedition related to comments about the Pahalgam terror attack.
