UP Taxman Gets Tech-Savvy Boost for Growth

2025-05-02T16:06:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has directed the State Tax Department to enhance transparency and employ advanced technology in tax administration. The department collected Rs 1,14,637.54 crore in 2024-25 and aims to achieve Rs 1.75 lakh crore in 2025-26. While April's GST and VAT collection of Rs 9,986.15 crore was deemed satisfactory, the CM highlighted the need for improvement in certain zones and sectors. He emphasized the importance of addressing field-level challenges and leveraging IT tools, AI, and data analytics for efficient tax collection and system transparency. Adityanath stressed that effective revenue management is crucial for Uttar Pradesh's development and urged the department to prioritize stakeholder engagement and continuous improvement.
