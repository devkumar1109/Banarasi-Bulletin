ATM Gang Bust: UP Criminals Caught in Chennai Heist

2025-05-26T09:00:00+00:00

Chennai police have apprehended a gang from Uttar Pradesh involved in ATM tampering and cash theft. The gang used sophisticated techniques to bypass security measures and withdraw money from ATMs. The police are investigating the extent of the gang's operations and the amount of money stolen. This bust highlights the increasing sophistication of ATM fraud and the need for enhanced security measures.
