Delhi Drenched: Heatwave Breaks, Rain Arrives

2025-06-14T07:19:00+00:00

Delhi's temperature reached 42°C last week, but relief is expected with rain and thunderstorms predicted by the IMD. Today's maximum temperature is expected to be around 39-41°C, but the rain and thunderstorms will likely lower the mercury. The orange heat wave alert for Delhi has been lifted. A western disturbance is expected to impact northwest India, bringing light rain and thunderstorms to Delhi. Uttarakhand will likely experience heavy rainfall until June 19, while Uttar Pradesh will see heavy rainfall between June 17 and 19. Rajasthan might experience dust storms and thundersqualls. The 24-hour average AQI in Delhi was 187, with several locations recording 'moderate' AQI.
