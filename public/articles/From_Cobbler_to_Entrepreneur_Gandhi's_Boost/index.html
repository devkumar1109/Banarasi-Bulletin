From Cobbler to Entrepreneur: Gandhi's Boost

2025-03-10T02:08:00+00:00

Ramchet, a cobbler from Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh, is launching his brand 'Ramchet Mochi' with support from Rahul Gandhi. Gandhi gifted Ramchet a machine for his work and arranged a meeting with designer Sudheer Rajbhar. Impressed by Rajbhar's successful leather business, Ramchet aims to scale his own venture. Since meeting Gandhi, Ramchet's business has grown significantly. He now employs two to three artisans and earns thousands of rupees each month, compared to his previous earnings of Rs 100-150.
