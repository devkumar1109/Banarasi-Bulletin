Training Plane Crashes in Aligarh, Pilot Safe

2025-05-04T14:45:00+00:00

A training plane crashed at Aligarh's Dhanipur Airport on Sunday. The plane, registered to Pioneer Aviation, hit a wall during landing. The pilot was the only occupant and reported no major injuries. The aircraft sustained damage to its front, one wing, and wheel. Authorities are currently investigating the cause of the crash, which is believed to be due to a technical snag.
