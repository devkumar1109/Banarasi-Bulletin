Ghazipur Double Murder: Two Shot Dead in Broad Daylight

2025-03-21T13:13:00+00:00

Two men, Aman Chauhan (20) and Anurag Singh (27), were shot dead in broad daylight in Uchauri Bhairospur village, Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh. Police received information about the bodies this afternoon and have launched an investigation. The victims were residents of Sidhauna village. Superintendent of Police Iraj Raja confirmed the incident.
