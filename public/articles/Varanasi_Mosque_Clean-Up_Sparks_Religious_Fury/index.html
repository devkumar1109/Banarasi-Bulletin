Varanasi Mosque Clean-Up Sparks Religious Fury

2025-04-08T10:30:00+00:00

A controversy erupted in Varanasi after BJP MLA Neelkanth Tiwari and supporters cleaned the Alamgir mosque without permission. Muslim religious bodies condemned the act, viewing it as an insult and a violation of Waqf property. They argue it's a repercussion of the Waqf Bill and claim the MLA entered with slippers on. The mosque caretaker stated the MLA and his supporters cleaned the compound with a street-sweeping broom. Tiwari justified the action as part of a cleanliness drive, claiming he and his supporters removed their slippers. The incident has sparked outrage and concerns about the potential impact of the Waqf Bill.
