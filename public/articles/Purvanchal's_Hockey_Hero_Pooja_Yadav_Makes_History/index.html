Purvanchal's Hockey Hero: Pooja Yadav Makes History

2025-04-18T08:10:00+00:00

Pooja Yadav, a 21-year-old from Varanasi, has made history as the first woman from the Purvanchal region to join the Indian women's hockey team. She earned her place on the 26-member squad for the upcoming Australia tour (April 26 - May 4) after impressing at the 2025 Senior Women’s National Championship. Yadav, who has been training at the national camp in SAI, Bengaluru since late March, hopes her journey will inspire other girls from Purvanchal.
