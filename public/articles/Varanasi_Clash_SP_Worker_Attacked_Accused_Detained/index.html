Varanasi Clash: SP Worker Attacked, Accused Detained

2025-04-13T14:31:00+00:00

A Samajwadi Party worker, Harish Mishra, was allegedly attacked with a knife by two individuals outside his house in Varanasi. Locals intervened, saving Mishra and apprehending the attackers. The accused, Avinash Mishra and another, claimed they were not affiliated with Karni Sena and were only seeking an explanation for Mishra's alleged derogatory statement about Maa Karni. Police have detained three individuals and registered a case based on complaints from both parties. While SP chief Akhilesh Yadav condemned the attack, police clarified that no confirmed link to Karni Sena has been established.
