UP Government Pledges Swift Action, Adityanath Champions 'One Nation, One Election'

2025-04-08T16:08:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak announced the state government's commitment to swiftly implementing 14 key proposals discussed in a recent Cabinet meeting. Minister Sanjay Nishad criticized previous administrations for misusing public funds. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath advocated for 'One Nation, One Election,' stating that frequent elections cause instability, hinder development, encourage corruption, and negatively impact GDP and growth. Adityanath emphasized the importance of political stability for peace, security, and development.
