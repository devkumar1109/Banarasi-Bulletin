NTPC Green Energy Soars with Solar Win and Sustainable Growth

2025-06-05T02:30:00+00:00

NTPC Green Energy secured a 1,000 MW solar project in UPPCL’s solar PV auction at a tariff of Rs 2.56 per kWh. This win comes after the company announced a strategic partnership with Honeywell Automation India to explore sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) production using captured CO₂ and green hydrogen from NTPC power plants. NTPC Green Energy's Q4 results showed a threefold jump in net profit to Rs 233 crore and a 22.5% rise in revenue to Rs 622.3 crore. The company aims for 40 GW of renewable capacity by FY 2032 and 14 GW of pumped hydro capacity within the next 6-9 years. NTPC Green Energy's stock has shown consistent growth driven by its renewable energy initiatives and strong performance.
