UP Summer Camp: Holistic Learning for All

2025-05-21T15:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Director General of School Education, Kanchan Verma, inaugurated the Summer Camp 2025 for government school students on May 21st. The camp, held across the state, focuses on holistic development through activities like yoga, storytelling, music sharing, digital literacy, traditional arts and crafts, environmental awareness, sports, and leadership building. Students engaged in activities like "Self-Introduction," "I am a Star," and "I am a Writer Too" on the first day. The camp will conclude on June 10th with a closing ceremony and awards for outstanding performers.
