Varanasi Gang Rape: SIT Probes, Arrests Made

2025-04-18T04:15:00+00:00

A six-member Special Investigation Team (SIT) led by a Deputy Commissioner of Police has been formed to probe the alleged gang rape of a 19-year-old woman in Varanasi. The victim accused 23 individuals, and 14 suspects have been arrested. The incident occurred between March 29th and April 4th, with the victim allegedly raped by multiple individuals on separate occasions. The victim's family claims she was intoxicated and taken away by the accused, and they allege money was extorted from them. Authorities are searching for the remaining suspects.
