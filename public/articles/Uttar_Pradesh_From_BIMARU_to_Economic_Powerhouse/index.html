Uttar Pradesh: From BIMARU to Economic Powerhouse

2025-03-24T07:40:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath claims the state has transformed from a lagging region to India's economic powerhouse in the past eight years. He attributes this success to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's policies. Before 2017, Uttar Pradesh was categorized as a "BIMARU" state, but now leads in multiple sectors. Adityanath highlights the agricultural sector's turnaround, with growth increasing from 5% in 2016-17 to over 13.5% currently. Food grain production has also surged from 557 lakh metric tonnes in 2016-17 to over 668 lakh metric tonnes last year, a nearly 20% increase. He credits this to the government's decision to waive farmers' loans worth Rs 36,000 crore.
