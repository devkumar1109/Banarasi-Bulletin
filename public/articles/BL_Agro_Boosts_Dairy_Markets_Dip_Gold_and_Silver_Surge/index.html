BL Agro Boosts Dairy, Markets Dip, Gold and Silver Surge

2025-03-11T04:50:00+00:00

BL Agro will invest ₹3,000 crore in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Rajasthan to develop cow breeding and dairy technology. The Indian stock market saw a slight dip with the Sensex closing at 73,869.21, down 245.96 points, and the Nifty at 22,406.55, down 53.75 points. Crude oil prices decreased by $9.00, reaching $5,769.00. Gold prices rose by ₹216.00, settling at ₹85,635.00, while silver prices increased by ₹425.00, closing at ₹96,890.00.
