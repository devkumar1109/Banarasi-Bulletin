Uttar Pradesh: Two Gruesome Crimes Shock State

2025-03-05T02:02:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Auriya district, a six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her 18-year-old relative. The incident occurred in a farmland where the accused was working. The victim, a Class-1 student, is in stable condition after being referred to the district hospital. Police have formed three teams to arrest the accused. In a separate incident in Ghaziabad, four men allegedly barged into a 42-year-old man's house, mutilated his private parts while he slept, and rendered him unconscious with an intoxicant. The victim, Sanjay Yadav, is receiving treatment in Meerut. His son, Prince Yadav, has filed a police complaint.
