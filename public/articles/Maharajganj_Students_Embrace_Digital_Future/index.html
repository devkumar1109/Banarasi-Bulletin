Maharajganj Students Embrace Digital Future

2025-04-29T07:12:00+00:00

A digital awareness campaign in Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh, significantly impacted students. Organized by a youth-driven group, the campaign emphasized the importance of digital skills and technology's role in everyday life and career development. Students actively participated in sessions showcasing practical applications of digital technology, exploring its influence on industries, entrepreneurship, and employment. Prince Dwivedi, a special guest, further enriched the event.
