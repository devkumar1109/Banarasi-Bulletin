Holi Harmony: UP Celebrates Peacefully Under Watchful Eye

2025-03-14T14:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh celebrated Holi peacefully under tight security. Authorities deployed drones and CCTV for surveillance and maintained a strong presence to prevent unrest. Celebrations were diverse, including a Bollywood-themed event in Lucknow and traditional processions in Shahjahanpur. Mathura and Vrindavan saw religious fervor, with events emphasizing unity. Despite coinciding with Friday prayers, interfaith harmony prevailed, exemplified by messages of unity at the Deva Sharif Dargah. Social media was closely monitored to ensure peaceful celebrations.
