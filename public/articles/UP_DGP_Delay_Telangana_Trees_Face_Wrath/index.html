UP DGP Delay, Telangana Trees Face Wrath

2025-05-21T16:31:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is delaying the appointment of a new DGP, with the acting DGP Prashant Kumar's term ending on May 31. The appointment process is shrouded in speculation, with potential candidates including Tilottama Verma, who would be India's first woman DGP of a state, and Daljit Singh Chaudhary, Alok Sharma, and Rajiv Krishna. Meanwhile, the Supreme Court has reprimanded the Telangana government for felling trees in the Kancha Gachibowli forest during a long weekend. The court has ordered the restoration of the forest within two months, threatening contempt charges against the state chief secretary if the order isn't followed.
