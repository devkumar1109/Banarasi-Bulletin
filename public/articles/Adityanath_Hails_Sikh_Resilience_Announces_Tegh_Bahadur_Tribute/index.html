Adityanath Hails Sikh Resilience, Announces Tegh Bahadur Tribute

2025-04-13T13:20:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath praised Guru Gobind Singh for resisting Mughal ruler Aurangzeb's cruelty and highlighted the importance of the Khalsa Panth. He commended the Sikh community's bravery and dedication to national values, citing Guru Gobind Singh's teachings as an inspiration. Adityanath expressed concern over religious conversions and Sikh youth migration, emphasizing the need to strengthen regions like Terai developed by Sikhs. He announced plans for a grand celebration in Uttar Pradesh marking Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji's 350th birth anniversary.
