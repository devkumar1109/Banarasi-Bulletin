BJP's UP Feud Delays District Appointments

2025-03-19T04:51:00+00:00

Factional feuds within the BJP are hindering the appointment of district presidents in Uttar Pradesh. The party is facing challenges in filling 28 district president positions due to internal conflicts and power struggles. This delay in appointments could impact the BJP's organizational strength and preparedness for upcoming elections.
