Wheat Feud, Fatal Strangling: UP Shocked

2025-04-06T16:04:00+00:00

In Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, two nephews, Ravindra and Jaipal, allegedly killed their 65-year-old uncle, Prahlad, over a dispute regarding the division of harvested wheat. The argument escalated, leading to the nephews attacking Prahlad with sticks, resulting in his death. A case has been registered, and the accused are on the run. Separately, in Bijnor, a woman, Shivani, was arrested for strangling her husband, Deepak, a railway employee. Initially, she claimed he died of a heart attack, but the post-mortem revealed the truth.
