Ambedkar Scheme: UP Aims to Eradicate Poverty

2025-04-14T16:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath announced a new state-level anti-poverty scheme that will be named after Dr. B.R. Ambedkar. The scheme aims to uplift economically disadvantaged communities and provide them with opportunities for progress. Details regarding the scheme's specific features and implementation plan were not disclosed in the provided text.
