Maha Kumbh 2025: Faith, Sports, and Sanitation Triumph

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

Maha Kumbh 2025 in Prayagraj attracted 62 crore devotees, significantly impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi saw over 2 crore devotees since the event began, while 69 lakh have booked homestays in Ayodhya through the Divya Ayodhya app. The Khel Maha Kumbh, featuring 800 athletes and sports personalities, aimed to promote India's indigenous sports. A "zero discharge" initiative effectively managed wastewater from over 1.5 lakh toilets daily, setting a global sanitation benchmark for large-scale events. Prime Minister Narendra Modi participated in the event, taking a holy dip and performing puja.
