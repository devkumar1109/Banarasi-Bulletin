IIT (BHU) Electrical Engineers Reunite After 40 Years

2025-03-04T10:01:00+00:00

IIT (BHU), Varanasi, celebrated the 40th reunion of its Electrical Engineering batch of 1985. The event, organized by the Student Alumni Interaction Cell (SAIC), brought together alumni, faculty, and current students. Highlights included a meeting with the IIT (BHU) Director, Prof. Amit Patra, and Dean (Resource and Alumni), Prof. Hiralal Pramanik, a nostalgic cycle ride to Visvesvaraya Hostel, and a traditional lunch at Rajputana Hostel. Alumni shared insights with current students and reminisced about their time at the institute. Prof. Patra praised the alumni's success and their contributions to various fields. The reunion fostered nostalgia, camaraderie, and a sense of community among the IIT (BHU) family.
