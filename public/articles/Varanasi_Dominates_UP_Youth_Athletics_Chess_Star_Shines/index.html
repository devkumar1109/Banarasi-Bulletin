Varanasi Dominates UP Youth Athletics, Chess Star Shines

2025-03-02T19:44:00+00:00

Varanasi won the UP State Youth Athletics Championship 2025 with three gold, four silver, and four bronze medals. Saharanpur and Sonbhadra shared the runner-up position. Lucknow's Ashita won gold in the girls' long jump, and Komal Yadav secured gold in the girls' 400m event. Rashi Singh from UPAA won two gold medals. Varun Rajput of Etawah topped the boys' heptathlon, while Priya Mishra of Sitapur secured first place in the girls' division. Medhansh Saxena won the 43rd Shivani Cup Special Open Chess Tournament, remaining unbeaten with the highest score of six points. The Inheritor won the Army Cdr's Cup in the Thoroughbred category at the Lucknow Race Course. Lucknow, Mau, Gorakhpur, and Amethi Hostel advanced to the semifinals of the State-Level Open Senior Men's Handball Tournament.
