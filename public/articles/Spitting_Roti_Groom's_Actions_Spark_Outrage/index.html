Spitting Roti: Groom's Actions Spark Outrage

2025-02-24T18:33:00+00:00

A viral video from a wedding in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, shows a man spitting on roti while making it. The incident occurred at Prem Mandap on February 21 and sparked outrage on social media. Local police have detained the individual involved after confirming the video's authenticity. Superintendent of Police (city) Ayush Vikram Singh stated that further action will be taken. The video has prompted widespread condemnation and discussion online.
