Temple Priest's Home Looted, Six Arrested

2025-05-21T18:09:00+00:00

Six individuals were arrested in connection with the burglary of Sankat Mochan temple priest Prof Vishwambhar Nath Mishra's residence in Varanasi. The thieves stole jewelry worth over Rs 1 crore and Rs 3 lakh in cash. Three of the accused were injured in an exchange of fire with police during a raid in Domri area. The injured were identified as Vicky Tiwari, Jitendra Singh alias Golu Patel, and Rakesh Dubey, all residents of Kaimur Bhabua (Bihar). Three others, Dilip alias Bansi Chaubey, Atul Shukla, and Shani, were also arrested. One suspect managed to escape. All the accused had previously worked at the priest's residence.
