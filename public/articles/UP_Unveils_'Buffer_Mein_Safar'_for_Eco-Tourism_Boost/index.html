UP Unveils 'Buffer Mein Safar' for Eco-Tourism Boost

2025-05-24T12:40:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched the ‘Buffer Mein Safar Yojana’ to promote eco-tourism and boost local employment. The initiative will develop new safari routes within the buffer zones of tiger reserves like Dudhwa, Pilibhit, and Uttar Kheri, focusing on Bhira and Mohammadi. Local communities will be involved in guiding and running small restaurants, strengthening their economic participation. The project aims to reduce human-wildlife conflict and offer tourists thrilling jungle adventures. New tourist circuits are being developed in areas like Bhira, Mohammadi Range, and South Kheri, including Semrai Lake, a significant bird migration site. Safari operations have also begun in the Kartaniya Ghat buffer zone near the Nepal border.
