John Abraham: Water's Global Success, India's Missed Opportunity

2025-03-13T13:12:00+00:00

John Abraham's 2005 film *Water*, directed by Deepa Mehta, received global recognition, including an Oscar nomination for Best Foreign Language Film. Despite critical acclaim and praise from Hollywood legends like Steven Spielberg and Charlize Theron, the film faced challenges and a delayed release in India, limiting its visibility within the country. Abraham, who played a man from Varanasi in the film, shared his experience in a recent interview, highlighting the contrast between the film's international reception and its limited recognition in India.
