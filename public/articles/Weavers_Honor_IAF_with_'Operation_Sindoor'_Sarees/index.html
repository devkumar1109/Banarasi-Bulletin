Weavers Honor IAF with 'Operation Sindoor' Sarees

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers created special sarees inspired by the Indian Air Force (IAF) to commemorate 'Operation Sindoor'. The sarees feature designs incorporating weaponry like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale, honoring the Indian Army's efforts. The sarees are a tribute to the IAF's role in the operation.
