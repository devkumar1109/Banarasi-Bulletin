Wife, Lover Plot ₹2 lakh Murder in Auraiya

2025-03-26T07:22:00+00:00

In Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh, a man named Dilip Yadav was murdered by contract killers hired by his wife, Pragati, and her lover, Anurag Yadav. The police identified the killers, Ramji Nagar and his aide, who were arrested along with Anurag. Anurag had a four-year relationship with Pragati and planned the murder for ₹2 lakh. Pragati's brother and the brother of the arrested Ramji Nagar expressed support for the victim and demanded justice, emphasizing punishment for those involved regardless of family ties. The police are continuing their investigation to apprehend other individuals involved in the crime.
