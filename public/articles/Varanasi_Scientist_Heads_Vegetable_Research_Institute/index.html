Varanasi Scientist Heads Vegetable Research Institute

2025-04-07T18:47:00+00:00

Rajesh Kumar, a vegetable scientist from Varanasi, has been appointed as the director of ICAR-Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR), Varanasi. Kumar, born in 1974, holds a PhD from G B Pant University of Agriculture and Technology and has contributed significantly to vegetable research. He has developed 11 high-yielding vegetable varieties and hybrids, including tomato, chilli, and pea varieties. Kumar has received numerous awards and fellowships for his work, including recognition from the National Academy of Agricultural Sciences and the Indian Society of Vegetable Science. As director, he plans to focus on developing climate-resilient and disease-resistant vegetable varieties using modern technologies like genome editing.
