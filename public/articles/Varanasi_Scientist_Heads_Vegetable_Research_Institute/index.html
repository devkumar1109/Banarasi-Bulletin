Varanasi Scientist Heads Vegetable Research Institute

2025-04-07T18:47:00+00:00

Rajesh Kumar, a vegetable scientist from Varanasi, has been appointed as the director of ICAR-Indian Institute of Vegetable Research (IIVR), Varanasi. Kumar, born in 1974, has a distinguished career in vegetable research, contributing to the development of 11 high-yielding vegetable varieties and hybrids. He has also authored numerous research papers and received several prestigious fellowships and awards for his work. As director, Kumar aims to focus on developing climate-resilient, disease-resistant, and high-quality vegetable varieties using modern technologies like genome editing and molecular markers.
