School Rape: Dalit Boys Arrested, Video Threat.

2025-05-21T15:15:00+00:00

Five boys aged 12 to 15, from the Dalit community, have been arrested for allegedly raping a 12-year-old girl inside a school in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on May 8, when the boys lured the girl to the school building, drugged her, and committed the crime. They filmed the assault and threatened to post the video online if she spoke out. A neighbor saw the video and alerted the girl's mother, who filed a police complaint on May 20. The boys have been taken into custody and will be brought before the Juvenile Justice Board.
