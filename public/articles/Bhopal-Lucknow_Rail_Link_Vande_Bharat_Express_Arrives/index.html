Bhopal-Lucknow Rail Link: Vande Bharat Express Arrives

2025-04-16T15:41:00+00:00

The Indian Railways is expected to launch a new Vande Bharat Express train connecting Bhopal and Lucknow, reducing travel time to 6-8 hours. This will be the third Vande Bharat Express train for Bhopal, with services also connecting to Mumbai and Patna. Currently, Bhopal has around 15 indirect trains to Lucknow, taking 10-12 hours and becoming overcrowded during festivals. The new train will offer a faster, more comfortable, and convenient option with fewer stops, high speeds, and premium facilities. It will have eight chair car coaches accommodating 564 passengers, including executive class seating. Ticket fares are expected to be significantly higher than regular trains, but the final route map, stoppage details, and launch date are yet to be announced.
