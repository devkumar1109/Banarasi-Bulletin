India's Motorsports Needs: PPP Power For Growth

2025-06-15T19:00:00+00:00

Motorsport in India requires strong public-private partnerships (PPPs) for sustainable growth, according to industry executives. The Uttar Pradesh government's deal to host MotoGP is seen as a positive step, with potential to boost tourism, employment, and infrastructure. Past challenges with Formula One (F1) in India, including regulatory and taxation issues, highlight the need for supportive government policies. JK Tyre & Industries, a key player in Indian motorsport, invests 15% of its annual marketing budget in the sport and has spent approximately ₹200 crore developing motorsport infrastructure and talent. Vicky Chandhok, former president of FMSCI, attributes F1's exit from India to a lack of government support and red tape.
