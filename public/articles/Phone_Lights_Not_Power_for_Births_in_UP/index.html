Phone Lights, Not Power, for Births in UP

2025-05-30T09:36:00+00:00

In a stark illustration of healthcare challenges in Uttar Pradesh, four women gave birth with the assistance of mobile phone lights at a government health center. The incident highlights the lack of basic infrastructure and essential services in rural areas, where power outages are frequent. This underscores the urgent need for improved healthcare facilities and reliable electricity supply in remote regions. The incident has raised concerns about the quality of healthcare accessible to women in underserved areas.
