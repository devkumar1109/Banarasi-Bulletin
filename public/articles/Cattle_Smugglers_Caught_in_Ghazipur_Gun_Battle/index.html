Cattle Smugglers Caught in Ghazipur Gun Battle

2025-05-07T19:07:00+00:00

Two cattle smugglers, Sonu Yadav and Sabhajeet Yadav alias Shalu, were arrested in Ghazipur district, Uttar Pradesh, following an encounter with police. The incident occurred early Wednesday morning when police attempted to stop a pickup van carrying 11 cattle. The smugglers opened fire on the police, who retaliated in self-defense, injuring both men in the legs. Two accomplices escaped. Police recovered two country-made pistols, live cartridges, the van, and the cattle. Sonu Yadav has eight criminal cases pending against him, while Sabhajeet Yadav alias Shalu is booked in two cases.
