Adityanath Beefs Up UP Security With New Battalions

2025-04-20T00:56:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is strengthening the state's security infrastructure by constructing new campuses for the Uttar Pradesh Special Security Force (UPSSF). The 3rd Battalion will have residential and non-residential facilities built in Prayagraj, on 42.03 acres of land allocated in Arail area. A 4th Battalion will be established in Mathura. The construction and development of these campuses will cost a significant sum.
