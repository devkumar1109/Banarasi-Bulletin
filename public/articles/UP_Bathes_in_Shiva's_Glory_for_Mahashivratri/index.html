UP Bathes in Shiva's Glory for Mahashivratri

2025-02-26T14:58:00+00:00

Thousands of devotees celebrated Mahashivratri at Shiva temples across Uttar Pradesh, marking the union of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath performed rituals in Gorakhpur, including 'rudrabhishek' and 'havan'. A vibrant procession with 'naga sadhus' and 'akharas' proceeded to the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi. Major temples in Ayodhya and Prayagraj also saw a large turnout for the sacred 'jalabhishek'. The celebrations highlighted the deep-rooted faith and cultural unity associated with Mahashivratri.
