BJP Leader Poisoned, Dies in Uttar Pradesh

2025-03-11T05:09:00+00:00

Gulfam Singh Yadav, a BJP leader in Uttar Pradesh's Sambhal district, died after being injected with poison by three unidentified assailants at his farm in Daftara village. The assailants arrived on a motorcycle, administered the injection, and fled. Yadav was transported to Aligarh for treatment but died en route. Police are investigating the case, but no formal complaint has been filed by Yadav's family.
