Triple Murder: Two Arrested, Relative on the Run

2025-05-27T18:19:00+00:00

Two persons have been arrested in connection with the Jaunpur triple murder case, where a welding workshop owner and his two sons were found dead on Monday morning. The third accused, Arvindar Nagar alias Golu, a relative of the victims, is still at large. A cash reward of Rs 25,000 has been announced for his arrest. The murders are suspected to be the result of a nine-year-long family dispute. The Jaunpur SP has suspended three police personnel for laxity in handling the case and related criminal incidents.
