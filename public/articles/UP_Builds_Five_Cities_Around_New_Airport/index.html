UP Builds Five Cities Around New Airport

2025-04-06T10:46:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh plans five new cities east of Noida, driven by the upcoming Noida International Airport. The projects include New Noida, a 20,911-hectare industrial hub focusing on IT, electronics, and manufacturing, aiming to create 1.2 million jobs by 2040. Heritage City, alternatively called Raya Urban Centre, will leverage religious tourism and Braj culture. New Agra will develop a tourism and hospitality district. Tappal-Bajna will transform into an industrial township, and IITGN will focus on industrial development. This urban expansion spans 56,000 hectares and aims to stimulate economic growth in the state.
