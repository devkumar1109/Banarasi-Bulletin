Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand Score Wins in Hockey Nationals

2025-03-06T03:14:00+00:00

In the 15th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand secured wins in Division A matches. Madhya Pradesh defeated their opponents thanks to Neelu Dadiya's penalty corner conversion in the final quarter. The win earned them three crucial points. Jharkhand also triumphed in their Division A match. The championship is being held in Panchkula.
