Varanasi Horror: Rape, Hepatitis, and 23 Accused

2025-04-14T16:08:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman, allegedly gang-raped by 23 individuals over seven days in Varanasi, has tested positive for Hepatitis B. Doctors suspect the infection may have resulted from intoxicants injected by the accused during the crime. The victim also underwent an HIV test, with the first report being negative. She has been transferred to Sir Sundar Lal Hospital where her condition is improving. 13 out of 23 accused have been arrested so far. An FIR has been registered under various sections of the BNS against the accused, 12 of whom have been identified by the victim. Prime Minister Narendra Modi expressed deep concern over the incident and directed officials to take strict action against those involved.
