Varanasi Soars: Tourism Booms in Holy City

2025-05-05T04:45:00+00:00

Varanasi saw a significant surge in tourism in the first quarter of 2025. Domestic tourist arrivals jumped by 77.59% to 11.46 crore visitors, while foreign tourist arrivals increased by 34.21% to 1.5 lakh. This growth is attributed to Varanasi's spiritual appeal and improved infrastructure, including revamped ghats, better roads, sanitation, and modern amenities. The city welcomed 2.56 crore visitors in the first quarter of 2024, compared to 11.46 crore in the same period of 2025.
