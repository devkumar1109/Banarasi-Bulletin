From Milkman's Daughter to Hockey Heroine

2025-04-22T02:44:00+00:00

Pooja Yadav, 20, from Varanasi, has become the first woman from her city to play for the Indian hockey team. Her journey began when her father, a milkman, bought her first pair of playing shoes from a local shopkeeper on credit. Pooja played cricket with boys growing up, which instilled in her a belief in her abilities. Despite facing skepticism from some villagers, she persevered with the support of her family, especially her mother and sisters. Pooja secured a spot at the Lucknow sports hostel to access better training and resources.
