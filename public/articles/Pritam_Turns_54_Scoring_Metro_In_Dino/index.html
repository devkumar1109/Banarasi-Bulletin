Pritam Turns 54: Scoring "Metro In Dino"

2025-06-14T01:45:00+00:00

Musician Pritam Chakraborty is celebrating his 54th birthday. He is composing music for the upcoming film "Metro In Dino," a sequel to Anurag Basu's 2007 hit "Life in a… Metro." Pritam is reported to charge Rs 5 crore per film, making him one of the highest-paid musicians in India. His career spans decades, with hits including "Dhoom," "Gangster," "Jab We Met," "Race," "Love Aaj Kal," "Cocktail," and "Brahmastra." Some of his most popular songs include "Tum Se Hi," "Pehli Nazar Mein," "Tum Hi Ho Bandhu," and "Itni Si Hasi." "Metro In Dino" premieres on July 4 and stars Aditya Roy Kapur, Sara Ali Khan, Pankaj Tripathi, Neena Gupta, Konkona Sen Sharma, Ali Fazal, Fatima Sana Shaikh, Anupam Kher, Kay Kay Menon, and Saswata Chatterjee.
