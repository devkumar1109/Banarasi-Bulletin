Holi's Colors Paint India with Unity and Joy

2025-03-14T06:24:00+00:00

Holi, the festival of colors, is being celebrated with enthusiasm across India. In Varanasi, foreign tourists have been participating in Holi celebrations for the past two decades, experiencing the cultural event at Mint House. President Droupadi Murmu and Prime Minister Narendra Modi extended their Holi greetings, emphasizing the festival's significance in promoting unity and love. Defense Minister Rajnath Singh also wished for the public's well-being and health. Holi began on Thursday with Chhoti Holi, with people celebrating with colors, music, and traditional festivities. The festival symbolizes the triumph of good over evil.
