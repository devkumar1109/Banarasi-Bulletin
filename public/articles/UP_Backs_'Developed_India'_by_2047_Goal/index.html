UP Backs 'Developed India' by 2047 Goal

2025-05-24T16:18:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath pledged the state's support for 'Developed India by 2047' at the 10th NITI Aayog Governing Council meeting in New Delhi. The meeting, chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, focused on the national agenda 'Viksit Bharat@2047'. Adityanath emphasized Uttar Pradesh's crucial role in achieving this vision and stated the state's commitment to becoming a 'Developed State' by 2047 as an integral member of 'Team India'.
