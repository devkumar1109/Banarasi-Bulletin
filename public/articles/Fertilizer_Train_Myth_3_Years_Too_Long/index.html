Fertilizer Train Myth: 3 Years Too Long

2025-03-06T08:00:00+00:00

A viral claim stated that an Indian Railways goods train took over 3 years to reach its destination, traveling from Visakhapatnam to Basti in Uttar Pradesh. The train, carrying 1,316 bags of fertilizer, reportedly departed in 2014 and arrived in 2018, making headlines globally. However, the Press Information Bureau (PIB) debunked this claim, stating that no Indian Railways goods train has ever taken that long to reach its destination.
