UP Health Scam: One Woman, 25 Fake Births

2025-04-12T03:12:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a health department scam was uncovered where a 35-year-old woman's identity was used to falsely claim 25 childbirths and five sterilizations over a period of 30 months. The fraudulent scheme was exposed during an audit at a community health centre (CHC) in Fatehabad. This scam highlights a serious issue of misappropriation of government benefits intended for maternal and child healthcare.
