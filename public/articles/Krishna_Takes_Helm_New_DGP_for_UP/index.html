Krishna Takes Helm: New DGP for UP

2025-05-31T17:36:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991 batch IPS officer, has been appointed as the new Director General of Police (DGP) of Uttar Pradesh, replacing Prashant Kumar. Krishna brings over 30 years of experience to the role, having served in various key positions including ASP in Bareilly, Kanpur, and Aligarh, and SSP in cities like Etawah, Mathura, and Gautam Buddha Nagar. His appointment comes at a crucial time with many senior officers set to retire. Krishna's proximity to Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath is notable, as he was entrusted with police recruitment examinations in the state. His new role presents challenges such as maintaining law and order, tackling crime, and ensuring citizen safety.
