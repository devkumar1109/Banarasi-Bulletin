Uttar Pradesh: India's Sweet Success Story

2025-05-03T12:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is known as the "Sugar Bowl of India" due to its leading role in sugarcane cultivation and sugar production. The state contributes over 35% of India's total sugar output, fueled by fertile plains, abundant water, and a favorable climate. Sugarcane is crucial to Uttar Pradesh's economy, supporting over 4 million farming families and powering over 120 sugar mills. The crop's byproducts, like molasses and ethanol, also contribute to energy and alcohol production. The western UP region, with districts like Meerut, Muzaffarnagar, and Saharanpur, is particularly known for its sugarcane belt and dense sugar mill population.
