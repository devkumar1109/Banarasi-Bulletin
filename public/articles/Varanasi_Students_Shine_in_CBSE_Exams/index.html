Varanasi Students Shine in CBSE Exams

2025-05-13T19:08:00+00:00

CBSE Class 10 and 12 results were announced, with students from various schools in Varanasi achieving outstanding results. Sunbeam Academy had a 100% pass rate in Class 12, with Atharv Lakhotia topping the arts stream with 97.6%. DALIMSS Sunbeam group of schools also saw exceptional performance, with Yashaswi Rai scoring 99% in arts stream. Aryan International School's Suryansh Gupta achieved 99.4% in arts stream in Class 12, while Suraj Maharana secured 98.6% in Class 10. Central Girls Hindu School of BHU and Central Hindu Boys School also witnessed impressive results, with various students scoring above 95%. Jaipuria Schools, Padav branch, achieved a 100% pass rate in Class 12, with Pragya Rungta securing the top position in the district.
