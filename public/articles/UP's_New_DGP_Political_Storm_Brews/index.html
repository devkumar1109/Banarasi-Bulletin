UP's New DGP: Political Storm Brews

2025-06-01T05:26:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991-batch IPS officer, has been appointed as the new Director General of Police (DGP) of Uttar Pradesh, replacing Prashant Kumar who retired on Saturday. Samajwadi Party (SP) chief Akhilesh Yadav criticized the appointment, calling it "another acting DGP" and questioning the apparent discord between the central and state leadership. Yadav suggested the people of Uttar Pradesh and the state's law-and-order situation are suffering due to the conflict between Delhi and Lucknow. He questioned the ability of the new DGP to escape political influence and deliver impartial justice.
