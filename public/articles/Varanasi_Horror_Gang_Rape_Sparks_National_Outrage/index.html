Varanasi Horror: Gang Rape Sparks National Outrage

2025-04-16T04:52:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi was allegedly drugged and gang-raped by 23 men over six days in March-April 2025. 13 suspects have been arrested and a senior police officer removed due to allegations of negligence. Prime Minister Narendra Modi intervened, demanding the strictest action against the perpetrators and systemic reforms to prevent such crimes. The survivor's family and activists are demanding swift justice. The incident has sparked nationwide outrage and protests, highlighting concerns about police accountability and the need for stronger protections for women.
