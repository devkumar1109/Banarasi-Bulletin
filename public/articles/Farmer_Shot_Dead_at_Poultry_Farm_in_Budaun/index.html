Farmer Shot Dead at Poultry Farm in Budaun

2025-03-09T11:19:00+00:00

A 32-year-old farmer, Rampal, was shot dead by unidentified men while sleeping at a poultry farm in Budaun district, Uttar Pradesh. The incident occurred on Saturday night around 2:00 am when two men entered the farm and shot Rampal in the head before fleeing. Rampal's wife, Ashlekh, informed the police. A police team led by Circle Officer Sanjeev Kumar Singh reached the spot, and the body was sent for post-mortem. Police have launched an investigation and are recording statements from Rampal's wife and others.
