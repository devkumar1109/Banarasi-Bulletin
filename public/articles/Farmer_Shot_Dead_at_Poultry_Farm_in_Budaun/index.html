Farmer Shot Dead at Poultry Farm in Budaun

2025-03-09T11:19:00+00:00

A 32-year-old farmer named Rampal was shot dead by unidentified men in Budaun district, Uttar Pradesh on Saturday night. The incident occurred at a poultry farm where Rampal resided with his wife, Ashlekh. Two men entered the farm around 2:00 am and shot Rampal in the head before fleeing. Police have launched an investigation based on Ashlekh's complaint and are recording statements from witnesses. Circle Officer Sanjeev Kumar Singh assured that the case will be solved soon.
