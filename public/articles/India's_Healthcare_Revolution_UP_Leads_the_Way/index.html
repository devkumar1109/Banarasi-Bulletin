India's Healthcare Revolution: UP Leads the Way

2025-03-08T11:52:00+00:00

India has seen an unprecedented improvement in health facilities over the last decade, according to Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. He highlighted the increase in AIIMS from six to 22 in the last 10 years and the addition of 40 new medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh since 2017. The state now offers free dialysis, CT scan, and MRI facilities in every district and organizes weekly 'Mukhyamantri Arogya Mela' health awareness events. UP leads the country with 10 crore people enrolled in the Ayushman Bharat scheme, and additional health insurance is being extended to various state employees. Adityanath also sees potential for India to become a leader in health tourism.
