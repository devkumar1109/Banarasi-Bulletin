Bomb Threat on Bengaluru-Bound IndiGo Flight, Passenger Detained

2025-04-27T12:44:00+00:00

A Canadian national on a Bengaluru-bound IndiGo flight from Varanasi sparked a panic after claiming to have a bomb. The incident occurred on Saturday night, leading to the aircraft being moved to an isolation bay for inspection. No explosives were found. The passenger was detained and questioned by security officials. The flight, grounded after the threat, was cleared to depart for Bengaluru on Sunday morning following a thorough security check. Varanasi airport director Puneet Gupta stated that the IndiGo crew promptly informed the Air Traffic Control about the passenger's claim. A detailed investigation into the matter is underway.
