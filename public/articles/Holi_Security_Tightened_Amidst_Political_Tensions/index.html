Holi Security Tightened Amidst Political Tensions

2025-03-13T11:57:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is enhancing security for Holi celebrations, coinciding with Friday namaz on March 14th, due to recent controversial remarks from politicians. Increased patrols, surveillance drones, and peace committees will be deployed statewide to prevent unrest. Mosque administrations are adjusting prayer times and covering mosques to prevent color staining. Social media will be monitored for provocative content. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath supports the adjusted prayer times, emphasizing mutual respect during the festivities.
