Krishna Takes Reins: Acting DGP for UP

2025-06-01T01:30:00+00:00

Rajeev Krishna, a 1991-batch IPS officer, has been appointed as the acting DGP of Uttar Pradesh, replacing Prashant Kumar who retired on May 31. This marks the fifth consecutive "acting" appointment since 2022. Krishna is known for his integrity, innovative policing strategies, and family history of public service. He previously served as the director general of vigilance, chairman of the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board, and founding chief of the UP Anti-Terror Squad. Krishna implemented technology-driven initiatives like Operation Pehchaan and e-Malkhana to modernize the force. Mukul Goel, the previous permanent DGP, was removed in May 2022 for "neglect of duty".
