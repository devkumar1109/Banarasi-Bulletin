UP Digitizes Past Revenue Records

2025-05-18T13:58:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is accelerating the digitization of old revenue records under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. The Stamp and Registration Department has nearly completed digitizing deeds from 2002 to 2017 and is now focusing on records from 1990 to 2001. A tender process for digitizing deeds from 1990 to 2001 is underway. The third phase will involve digitizing records from before 1990. This initiative aims to improve access to information, save resources, and modernize administrative procedures. Digitization will make revenue documents easily accessible, freeing up office space and ensuring long-term record safety.
