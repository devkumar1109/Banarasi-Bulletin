India United Against Terror: Sindoor Diplomacy Succeeds

2025-06-11T08:14:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi met with over 50 opposition and NDA leaders who participated in Operation Sindoor, a diplomatic outreach against terrorism. The delegations, including Congress' Shashi Tharoor and Manish Tewari, visited 33 countries and the EU to present India's stance on terrorism and peace. PM Modi praised the bipartisan effort, calling it a unified representation of India's voice globally. This marks a rare moment of consensus on foreign policy, with the government and opposition collaborating to elevate India's global image and counter terrorism diplomatically.
