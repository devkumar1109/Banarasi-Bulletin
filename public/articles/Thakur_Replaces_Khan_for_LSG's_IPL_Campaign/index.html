Thakur Replaces Khan for LSG's IPL Campaign

2025-03-23T07:32:00+00:00

Shardul Thakur has joined Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) as a substitute for Mohsin Khan, who suffered an ACL tear in his right knee during the Vijay Hazare Trophy. Thakur was signed from the Registered Available Player Pool (RAPP) for his reserve price of INR 2 crore. Mohsin represented Uttar Pradesh in domestic cricket. Thakur, who went unsold during the IPL 2025 auction, has played for five franchises in the IPL and has 95 matches and 67 wickets to his name. LSG begins their campaign against Delhi Capitals on March 24th in Visakhapatnam.
