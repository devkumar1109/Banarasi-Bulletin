UP Board Exams 2025: Evaluation Begins, Cheating Crackdown

2025-03-16T07:02:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has begun the evaluation of High School and Intermediate answer sheets for the 2025 board exams. The evaluation process will take place at 261 centers across the state from March 19 to April 2. Strict measures are in place to ensure fairness, including CCTV surveillance, designated personnel at entrances, and training for examiners. Over 51 lakh candidates registered for the exams, with 48,76,219 appearing on the first day. UPMSP detected 9 cases of cheating and identified 14 impersonators during the exams. In 2024, over 29 lakh students took the Class 10 exam and 24 lakh took the Class 12 exam.
