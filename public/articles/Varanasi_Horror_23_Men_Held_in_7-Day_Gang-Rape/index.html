Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Held in 7-Day Gang-Rape.

2025-04-07T10:10:00+00:00

In Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, a 19-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by over 23 men over seven days. The girl disappeared on March 29 and returned on April 4. Her family filed a missing report and later a complaint alleging gang-rape. The survivor claims the accused took her to hotels, hookah bars, and a warehouse where she was drugged and raped repeatedly. Six accused have been arrested, and police are investigating to apprehend the remaining 17. The case has been registered under various sections of the Bharatiya Nyay Sanhita (BNS) including gang rape, outraging modesty, and criminal intimidation.
