U.P. Exports Aim for $61.5 Billion by 2030

2025-04-21T21:01:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is aiming to triple the state's exports to $61.5 billion (₹5 trillion) by 2030. This ambitious target, outlined in a new export policy for 2025-30, comes despite global trade uncertainties and tariffs. The state sees potential for growth in electronic and engineering goods, leveraging its existing industrial base. Key export destinations for Uttar Pradesh include the US, UAE, Vietnam, UK, Nepal, and Germany.
