Trailer Overturns, Woman Killed in UP Accident

2025-04-12T10:12:00+00:00

A woman, identified as 65-year-old Gayatri, was killed when a trailer overturned onto her SUV in Mau district, Uttar Pradesh on Friday evening. Her son, Prashant Pandey (36), sustained severe injuries. The accident occurred near Kachhikala village on a four-lane road, causing a significant traffic jam. The mother-son duo was returning to Varanasi from their ancestral village in Surajpur. Gayatri was declared dead on arrival at the district hospital, while Prashant was referred to a higher medical facility. Police worked to clear the overturned trailer and restore traffic flow.
