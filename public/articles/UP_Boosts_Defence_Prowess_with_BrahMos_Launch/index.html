UP Boosts Defence Prowess with BrahMos Launch

2025-05-12T07:00:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the BrahMos Aerospace Integration and Testing Facility and the Strategic Material Technology Complex in Lucknow virtually. The inauguration took place on National Technology Day, with Defence Minister Rajnath Singh also participating. Adityanath highlighted the state's progress in achieving self-reliance in the defence sector under Prime Minister Modi's "Make in India" initiative. He congratulated officials from DRDO and the people of Uttar Pradesh.
