Varanasi Horror: 19-Year-Old Gang-Raped for Seven Days

2025-04-07T08:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a 19-year-old woman reported being gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. The woman went missing on March 29th and returned home on April 4th, detailing the horrific ordeal to her family. Police have filed an FIR and arrested six individuals under charges including gang rape, outraging modesty, wrongful confinement, and criminal intimidation. The investigation is ongoing, with efforts focused on apprehending the remaining accused.
