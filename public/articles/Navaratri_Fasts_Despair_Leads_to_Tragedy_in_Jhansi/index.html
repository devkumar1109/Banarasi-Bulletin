Navaratri Fasts: Despair Leads to Tragedy in Jhansi

2025-04-03T05:58:00+00:00

In Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, 36-year-old Priyansha Soni died by suicide after consuming poison due to her inability to observe Navaratri fasts and prayers because of her menstrual cycle. Priyansha was deeply upset about missing the festivities despite preparations. Her husband, Mukesh Soni, attempted to console her, but she took the drastic step. Priyansha was hospitalized but discharged after her condition improved. However, she deteriorated again and died at the district hospital. Police have registered a case and initiated a detailed investigation.
