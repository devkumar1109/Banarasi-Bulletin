Friend's Enmity Turns Fatal in Munambam Murder

2025-04-06T17:08:00+00:00

Sminu Antony, a 44-year-old man from Munambam, was found murdered near his home with head and face injuries. Police arrested his long-time friend, Saneesh, 32, for the crime. Sminu's gold chain and mobile phone were missing. The investigation revealed that the duo had a falling out due to personal reasons, and Saneesh had been experiencing financial difficulties. Saneesh met Sminu on the day of the murder and attacked him during an argument. Police believe robbery was not the sole motive, and there was personal enmity involved. Sminu had blocked Saneesh's number, but the accused managed to contact him and the meeting ended in a fatal attack.
