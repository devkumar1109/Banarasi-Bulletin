Hijab, Boy Thrashed: Religious Fury in UP

2025-04-14T13:40:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a Muslim girl was stripped of her hijab and assaulted by a mob, along with a Hindu boy. The mob initially targeted the individuals based on suspicion but upon discovering the boy's religious identity, they proceeded to thrash him. The incident was recorded on video. This act of violence highlights religious intolerance and the potential for mob justice in the region.
