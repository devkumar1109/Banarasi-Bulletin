Lawyer-MP Priya Saroj to wed cricketer Rinku Singh

2025-06-07T12:18:00+00:00

Priya Saroj, a Supreme Court lawyer and Samajwadi Party MP representing Machhlishahr constituency, is set to engage to cricketer Rinku Singh on June 8th in Lucknow. Saroj, at 25, became India's youngest female Parliamentarian after winning the 2024 Lok Sabha elections. She holds a B.A. from Delhi University and an LLB from Amity University, and previously worked at the Supreme Court. Saroj's political background stems from her father, prominent SP leader Tufani Saroj. The couple met through a mutual connection, Saroj's friend's father, who is a cricketer and acquainted with Rinku.
