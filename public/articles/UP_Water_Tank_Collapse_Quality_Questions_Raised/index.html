UP Water Tank Collapse: Quality Questions Raised

2025-05-29T12:18:00+00:00

A newly constructed Jal Jeevan Mission water tank in Chunka village, Uttar Pradesh, collapsed, causing public outrage due to allegations of substandard construction. Despite no casualties, the incident, which involved a tank serving the village for two months and costing Rs 531.50 lakh, has sparked demands for accountability. Local BJP MLA Asha Maurya criticized the negligence, suggesting collusion between water corporation officials and contractors. This marks the second such incident in Sitapur within a year, raising concerns about construction quality and oversight within the Jal Jeevan Mission.
