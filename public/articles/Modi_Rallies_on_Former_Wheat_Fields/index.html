Modi Rallies on Former Wheat Fields

2025-04-03T19:09:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi's upcoming public meeting in Varanasi will be held on land previously used for wheat farming. Fifteen bighas of land in Mehadiganj were selected after consent from landowners. The district administration harvested the wheat crop using a combined harvester to ensure timely preparations for the event. The agricultural arrangements included clearing the fields, installing German hangers, barricading, constructing the stage, and building a helipad. The administration's intervention ensured the farmers received their yield and stubble, while also facilitating the smooth execution of the public meeting.
