India's Women's Justice: Conviction Rates Dip

2025-03-08T08:28:00+00:00

India's average conviction rate in cases of crimes against women in 2022 was 25.3%, a decline from previous years. Uttar Pradesh had the highest conviction rate at 70% for every 100 completed trials, followed by Bihar at 61%. Six states, including Odisha, West Bengal, and Gujarat, had conviction rates below 10%. Andhra Pradesh and Assam were the worst performers with conviction rates below 5%. Conviction rate refers to the percentage of cases where a guilty verdict is reached after a trial is completed.
