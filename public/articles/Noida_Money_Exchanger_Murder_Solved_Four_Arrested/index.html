Noida Money Exchanger Murder Solved: Four Arrested

2025-06-15T07:56:00+00:00

Noida Police have solved the murder of money exchanger Ompal Bhati, who was killed on June 11 in Sector 12. Four accused, Mukul Sharma, Akash Upadhyay, Ajay Sharma, and Aryan Yadav, have been arrested. Mukul and Akash lured Bhati to a rented flat, robbed him of a bag containing foreign currency, and shot him when he resisted. Ajay, Mukul's father, and Aryan helped the duo escape. Police recovered Rs 10.3 lakh in Indian and foreign currencies, two pistols, cartridges, a motorcycle, and two mobile phones.
