Man Caught With Pistol Near Temple in Nalasopara

2025-05-31T14:35:00+00:00

In Nalasopara (East), Maharashtra, the Crime Branch Unit-2 arrested a 23-year-old man, Shubham Kumar Rajnath Tiwari, for possessing an illegal country-made pistol and a live cartridge. Acting on a tip-off, police apprehended Tiwari near Maa Vindhyavasini Mandir open ground on May 30, 2025, at 8:43 pm. A search revealed the firearm and ammunition, valued at ₹45,400. Tiwari, originally from Uttar Pradesh, faces charges under IPC Section 3 and 25(1B)(a) of the Arms Act and Section 37(1)/135 of the Maharashtra Police Act, 1951. The arrest was led by Police Inspector Sameer Ahirrao and his team.
