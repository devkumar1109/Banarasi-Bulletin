UP CM Mourns Pope Francis, Hails Global Impact

2025-04-21T16:41:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath expressed condolences over the death of Pope Francis at age 88. Adityanath recognized Pope Francis as the supreme spiritual leader of the Catholic Christian community, praising his compassion, humility, and dedication to service. He highlighted Pope Francis's significant contributions to the global community and his legacy as the first Latin American pontiff. Adityanath offered prayers for Pope Francis's soul and wished strength for his followers during this time of grief.
