Sikkim, UP Begin U20 Football Championship with Wins

2025-05-11T16:32:00+00:00

Sikkim and Uttar Pradesh secured victories in their opening matches of the Swami Vivekananda U20 Men's National Football Championship Group C. Sikkim defeated Ladakh 3-1, with Deepjay Sharma and Choden Lepcha scoring in the second half after Jigmet Nurboo's own goal gave Sikkim an early lead. Uttar Pradesh edged past Punjab 1-0, with Pravesh Kumar's 69th-minute goal proving to be the difference. Manipur, the third team in the group, will play their first match on Tuesday. Only the group winner advances to the quarter-finals.
