Gangster Maurya Caught After Bhadohi Shootout

2025-02-28T06:51:00+00:00

Sachin Maurya, a member of an inter-district gang with a 25,000 rupee bounty, was arrested after an encounter with Gyanpur police and district SWAT in Bhadohi. Maurya was wanted for robbery and had previous cases in Bhadohi and Prayagraj. He was arrested for a February 21st robbery where he and an accomplice snatched a necklace from a woman. Police recovered an illegal firearm, ammunition, and the motorcycle used in the crime. Maurya was shot in the leg during the encounter and is in stable condition. Police are pursuing his accomplice.
