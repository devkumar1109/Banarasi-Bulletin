Infant Dies in MP Car Flyover Crash

2025-03-13T07:47:00+00:00

A three-month-old infant died in a car accident on NH-30 near Maihar, Madhya Pradesh. The car fell off a flyover while trying to avoid a tractor coming from the wrong direction. Nine other passengers, all residents of Uttar Pradesh, were injured. The infant, Rudra Rawat, succumbed to their injuries at the hospital. Three others are in critical condition. Police are investigating the accident and searching for the tractor driver.
