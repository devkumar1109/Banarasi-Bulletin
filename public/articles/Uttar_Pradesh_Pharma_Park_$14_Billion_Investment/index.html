Uttar Pradesh Pharma Park: $1.4 Billion Investment

2025-06-10T05:34:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government aims to attract Rs. 12,000 crore (US$ 1.40 billion) in private investment for a mega Bulk Drug Pharma Park in Lalitpur district. The park, spanning 1,472 acres, will receive Rs. 250 crore (US$ 29.18 million) in state investment for infrastructure development. This initiative aims to strengthen India's pharmaceutical sector and create 14,000 direct and indirect jobs. India's pharmaceutical market, currently valued at Rs. 5,56,790 crore (US$ 65 billion), is projected to reach Rs. 10,27,920 crore (US$ 120 billion) by 2030. Uttar Pradesh is expected to contribute approximately 17% to domestic pharmaceutical sales. UPSIDA recently collaborated with CII to organize a conference in Gujarat to attract investors to the pharma park.
