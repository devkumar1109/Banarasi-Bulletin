Brotherly Love: UP Couple Elopes, Marries

2025-06-06T17:34:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh's Sant Kabir Nagar district, Khushboo married her brother-in-law, Amit, after her husband, Kallu, left for Mumbai for work six months ago. Kallu had moved to Mumbai shortly after their wedding. Khushboo grew closer to her brother-in-law, Amit, during Kallu's absence. When the family discovered their relationship, they eloped. Family mediation efforts failed, and the Nagar Panchayat chairman facilitated their wedding at his office with the consent of family members and local leaders. Kallu chose not to attend.
