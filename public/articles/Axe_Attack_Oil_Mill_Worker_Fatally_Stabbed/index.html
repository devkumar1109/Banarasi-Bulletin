Axe Attack: Oil Mill Worker Fatally Stabbed

2025-06-12T17:18:00+00:00

Rajkumar Vishwakarma, a mentally disturbed man, fatally attacked Gulam Ali (32) at an oil mill in Bhadohi district. Vishwakarma struck Ali's neck with an axe before fleeing the scene. Ali was rushed to the hospital but succumbed to his injuries. The accused was arrested and the weapon used in the attack was recovered. Police deployed a heavy force in the village to maintain peace.
