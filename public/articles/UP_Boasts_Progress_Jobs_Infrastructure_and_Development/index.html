UP Boasts Progress: Jobs, Infrastructure, and Development

2025-03-26T00:42:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak highlighted significant improvements in the state during the last eight years of the Yogi Adityanath government. Pathak emphasized advancements in infrastructure, including roads, electricity, and water, and improvements in education and healthcare. He stated that Uttar Pradesh has gained momentum and is dedicated to serving the poor, backward, and underprivileged. BJP MLA Rajeshwar Singh praised the government's achievements, noting that over 7.5 lakh youths secured government jobs, the state moved from 14th to 2nd in the "Ease of Doing Business" ranking, and ₹15 lakh crore of investment proposals have been realized. Singh also mentioned a strong crackdown on crime, with 222 criminals killed in encounters and thousands arrested. The government also plans to build the largest Ambedkar Memorial in Lucknow.
