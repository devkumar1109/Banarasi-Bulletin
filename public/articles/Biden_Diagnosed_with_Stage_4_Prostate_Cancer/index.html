Biden Diagnosed with Stage 4 Prostate Cancer

2025-05-19T04:00:00+00:00

Former U.S. President Joe Biden, 82, has been diagnosed with stage 4 prostate cancer that has spread to his bones. Biden experienced urinary symptoms before the diagnosis. Doctors say the cancer is hormone-sensitive and manageable despite its severity. The Gleason score for Biden's cancer is 9 out of 10, indicating a high risk. Biden previously worked on the Cancer Moonshot initiative. His health has been a topic of discussion since his presidency, and he stepped away from the 2024 presidential race.
