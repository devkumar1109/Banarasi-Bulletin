Ashes of Holi: Varanasi's Unique Celebration

2025-03-10T08:22:00+00:00

Masan Holi, a unique celebration in Varanasi, will be observed on March 11, 2025, at Manikarnika Ghat and Harishchandra Ghat. Instead of colors, this Holi involves playing with ashes from pyres. The tradition is believed to bring happiness, prosperity, and blessings from Lord Shiva. Masan Holi signifies Varanasi's cultural identity and spiritual nature, symbolizing the transience of life and the cyclical nature of existence. It begins after the aarti of Mahashmashan Nath at Harishchandra Ghat, with saints and Shiva devotees playing Holi with the ashes after worshipping Lord Shiva.
