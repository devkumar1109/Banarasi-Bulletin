Kannappa Cast Meets Yogi Adityanath

2025-04-09T08:00:00+00:00

Prabhu Deva and Vishnu Manchu met Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath in Lucknow to promote their upcoming film "Kannappa". CM Adityanath signed the film's poster and wished the team success. The film, a mythological epic based on the story of Lord Shiva's devotee Kannappa, was originally slated for release on April 25 but has been postponed. Vishnu Manchu stars as Kannappa alongside Preity Mukhundhan. The film also features Mohanlal, Akshay Kumar, Prabhas, and Kajal Aggarwal. Prabhu Deva is reportedly choreographing a song for the movie.
