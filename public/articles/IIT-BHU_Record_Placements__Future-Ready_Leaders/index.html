IIT-BHU: Record Placements, Future-Ready Leaders

2025-06-16T18:55:00+00:00

IIT-BHU achieved a record placement season in 2024-25, with over 1,373 job offers, including four international placements. Fifteen students secured annual packages exceeding Rs 1 crore, with the highest offer reaching Rs 2.20 crore. Over 300 companies participated in the drive, recruiting for roles across various sectors. The institute attributes this success to its updated curriculum, interdisciplinary approach, strong alumni network, and practical training. IIT-BHU director Prof Amit Patra highlights their focus on preparing students to be "future-ready leaders."
