Pakistanis Deported After Pahalgam Terror Attack

2025-04-26T17:40:00+00:00

Following a terror attack in Pahalgam that killed 26 tourists, India has deported most Pakistani nationals on short-term visas. The Home Minister, Amit Shah, oversaw the process, which was largely completed in Uttar Pradesh by April 27. Authorities are monitoring Pakistani nationals on long-term visas, many of whom are seeking Indian citizenship. The attack prompted a swift diplomatic response from India, revoking visas and emphasizing its stance on terrorism.
