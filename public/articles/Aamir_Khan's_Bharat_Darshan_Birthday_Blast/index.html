Aamir Khan's "Bharat Darshan" Birthday Blast

2025-03-17T07:37:00+00:00

Aamir Khan celebrated his 60th birthday on March 14. Sixteen years ago, he went on a "Bharat Darshan" disguised as a tourist to promote his film 3 Idiots. He traveled across India, blending in with locals and even helping an elderly person find their childhood home in Varanasi. Aamir surprised reporters by disappearing during a press conference and leaving a recorded message challenging them to unmask him. He later shared details of his journey, including meeting a young fan in Gujarat and a guide in Mahabalipuram who didn't recognize him. Aamir also enjoyed a traditional Bengali vegetarian meal at Sourav Ganguly's home in Kolkata.
