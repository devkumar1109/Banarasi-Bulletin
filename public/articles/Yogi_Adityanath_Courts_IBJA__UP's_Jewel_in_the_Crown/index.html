Yogi Adityanath Courts IBJA: UP's Jewel in the Crown

2025-05-08T16:10:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath addressed the India Bullion and Jewellers Association (IBJA) conclave in Lucknow, emphasizing UP's improved law and order and business-friendly environment. He highlighted the state's progress in strengthening its economy over the past eight years, emphasizing its crime-free status and robust infrastructure. Adityanath called for a Gems and Jewellery Park in UP, urging IBJA to create a comprehensive model encompassing design, technology, and export. He stressed UP's demographic advantage with a population of 25 crore, serving a wider market of 30 crore people. The CM assured traders of government support through enhanced security and open dialogue, citing initiatives like the 'Safe City' project.
