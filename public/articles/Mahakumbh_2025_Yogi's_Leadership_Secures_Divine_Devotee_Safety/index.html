Mahakumbh 2025: Yogi's Leadership Secures Divine Devotee Safety

2025-02-28T12:08:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh 2025 in Prayagraj concluded successfully on Mahashivaratri. DIG Mahakumbh Mela Kshetra, Vaibhav Krishna, praised CM Yogi Adityanath's leadership and support for the police, drawing a parallel to Lord Ram's benevolence towards his troops in the Ramayana. Krishna highlighted CM Yogi's unflinching support in ensuring a smooth and secure event for over 66 crore devotees. CM Yogi commended the Uttar Pradesh Police for their commitment and cooperation in managing the complexities of the event, emphasizing the safe and secure participation of a massive crowd.
