Ballia Baby Born Under Open Sky, Probe Launched

2025-05-23T17:01:00+00:00

In Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, a woman gave birth outside a community health center under the open sky. The incident occurred when an ambulance driver allegedly dropped her off near the hospital gate instead of taking her to the labor room. A video of the event has gone viral, prompting a probe by health authorities. A three-member investigation team led by Additional Chief Medical Officer Dr Vijay Yadav has been formed. The team will submit a report, and action will be taken against those found responsible.
