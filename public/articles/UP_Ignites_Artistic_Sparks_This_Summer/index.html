U.P. Ignites Artistic Sparks This Summer

2025-04-28T19:07:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh State Lalit Kala Akademi will host summer art camps across all 75 districts from May 1 to June 30. The camps, divided into two age groups (10-17 and 18-25), will offer daily expert-led training in various art forms from 8 am to 10 am. Sessions will be held at the Akademi campus in Lucknow, Lalit Kala Institute in Agra, Allahabad University's fine arts department, and Gorakhpur. The workshop, aligned with CM Yogi Adityanath's vision, aims to foster creativity and inspire interest in visual arts. Participants will receive certificates upon completion.
