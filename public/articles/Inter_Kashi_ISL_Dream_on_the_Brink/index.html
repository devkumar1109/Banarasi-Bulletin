Inter Kashi: ISL Dream on the Brink

2025-04-14T05:14:00+00:00

Inter Kashi, a Varanasi-based football club, is on the brink of promotion to India's top football league, the Indian Super League (ISL). The club, founded in 2023, is awaiting a decision by the All India Football Federation (AIFF) regarding a disputed match. Despite being in their second year, Inter Kashi has made significant strides, signing English professional Peter Hartley and hiring renowned Spanish coach Antonio Habas, the most successful coach in ISL history. Club president Prithiijit Das believes Inter Kashi's presence in Uttar Pradesh, the country's most populous state, has the potential to shift the state's sporting culture, which currently favors cricket and hockey.
