Jealousy Leads to Shooting, Wife Critical

2025-04-23T13:12:00+00:00

In Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, Shakeel alias Nanhe, son of Kant Nagar Panchayat president Munara Begum, allegedly shot his wife Nigar alias Reena (28) on suspicion of infidelity. The incident occurred at their home in Kant town. Nigar is currently in critical condition at the medical college. Shakeel fled the scene but was later arrested. Superintendent of Police Rajesh Dwivedi visited the medical college to investigate the incident. Nigar reported that Shakeel suspected her of infidelity, leading him to shoot her.
