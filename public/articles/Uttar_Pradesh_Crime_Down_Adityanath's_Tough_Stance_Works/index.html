Uttar Pradesh: Crime Down, Adityanath's Tough Stance Works

2025-03-16T12:14:00+00:00

Under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, Uttar Pradesh has seen a significant reduction in crime. Since 2017, the state has implemented a zero-tolerance policy, resulting in 222 criminals killed in encounters and over 8,000 injured during operations. Illegal assets worth Rs 14,200 crore have been confiscated or demolished. Crimes against women and minors have also decreased, with harsher penalties for offenders. Dedicated task forces have reclaimed land from illegal occupation. These reforms have made Uttar Pradesh a national model for law and order.
