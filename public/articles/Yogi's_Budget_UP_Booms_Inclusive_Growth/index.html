Yogi's Budget: UP Booms, Inclusive Growth

2025-03-04T15:16:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath presented the 2025-26 budget, highlighting the state's fiscal health and growth. He cited a boatman family's earnings of Rs 30 crore during the Mahakumbh Mela, showcasing inclusive development. The budget focuses on capital expenditure and strong tax receipts achieved through efficient fiscal management without new taxes. CM Yogi praised the efforts of workers during Mahakumbh, including health, cleanliness, and security personnel. He credited Prime Minister Modi's leadership for transforming Uttar Pradesh's economy.
