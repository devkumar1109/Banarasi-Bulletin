India's Young Feet Ache: Plantar Fasciitis on the Rise

2025-02-26T06:30:00+00:00

Plantar fasciitis, a condition causing heel inflammation, is rising among India's young working population, particularly those aged 18-35. A 2023 study found 12% of urban young adults experience plantar fasciitis-like symptoms, often attributed to prolonged standing during commutes on crowded metros and buses. Dr. Satyendra Singh states that prolonged standing on hard surfaces can contribute to the condition, which is recognized as an overuse injury in young athletes. A 2024 Urban Health Research Foundation survey found 35% of urban Indian professionals aged 18-35 experience foot pain after long metro or bus rides, with 22% reporting pain consistent with plantar fasciitis.
