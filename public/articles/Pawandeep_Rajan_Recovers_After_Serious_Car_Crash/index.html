Pawandeep Rajan Recovers After Serious Car Crash

2025-05-06T06:20:00+00:00

Singer Pawandeep Rajan was involved in a serious car accident near Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, on May 5 while traveling to Delhi. He sustained multiple fractures and underwent a six-hour surgery. Rajan's team confirmed he is under observation in the Medical ICU and will require another surgery in a few days. The accident occurred around 3:40 a.m., and the singer was initially operated on at a nearby facility before being transferred to a hospital in Delhi NCR. His team expressed gratitude for the fan support and prayers, stating that Rajan is doing "absolutely fine now."
