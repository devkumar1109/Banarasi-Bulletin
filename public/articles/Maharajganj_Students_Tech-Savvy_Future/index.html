Maharajganj Students: Tech-Savvy Future

2025-04-29T07:12:00+00:00

A digital awareness campaign in Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh, significantly impacted students by emphasizing the importance of digital skills and technology's role in everyday life and career development. Organized by a youth-driven group focused on community empowerment through education, the campaign involved enthusiastic student participation. Sessions showcased practical applications of digital technology, highlighting its influence on industries, entrepreneurship, and employment. Interactive demonstrations and discussions encouraged students to explore how technology could shape their futures. Special guest Prince Dwivedi further enriched the event with his insights.
