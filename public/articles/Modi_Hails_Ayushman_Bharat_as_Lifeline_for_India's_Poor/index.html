Modi Hails Ayushman Bharat as Lifeline for India's Poor

2025-04-14T12:31:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Varanasi and lauded the Ayushman Bharat scheme as a lifeline for the poor. The program provides health insurance coverage to millions of underprivileged Indians. During his visit, PM Modi emphasized the scheme's role in improving healthcare access and reducing financial burdens on families.
