Lover's Plot: Murder, Dismemberment, and a Honeymoon.

2025-03-19T07:36:00+00:00

In Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, a woman named Muskan (27) and her lover, Sahil (25), allegedly murdered Muskan's husband, Saurabh Rajput (29), a merchant navy employee. The crime occurred on March 4th. According to police, the couple stabbed Saurabh to death, dismembered his body, and placed the remains in a cement-filled drum. They were arrested after confessing to the crime during interrogation. The body has been recovered and sent for post-mortem. Saurabh was reported missing on March 4th, prompting an investigation. Muskan attempted to mislead the victim's family by sending messages from Saurabh's phone. After the murder, she and Sahil reportedly went on vacation to Shimla and Manali.
