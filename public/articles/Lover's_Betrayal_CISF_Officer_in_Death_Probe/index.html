Lover's Betrayal: CISF Officer in Death Probe

2025-03-04T04:50:00+00:00

A 40-year-old man from Uttar Pradesh, Abhishek Singh, was found dead in a Mangaluru lodge. Before his death, Singh recorded a video accusing CISF Assistant Commandant Monica Sihag of deception, alleging she hid her marital status, emotionally and physically exploited him, stole ₹8 lakh worth of gold, and had multiple affairs. Mangaluru police have registered a case and are investigating Singh's claims, examining digital records, call logs, and financial transactions. The alleged involvement of a CISF officer in fraud and emotional manipulation has raised concerns. Authorities plan to summon individuals connected to the case. Singh's death has sparked discussions about emotional exploitation, financial fraud in relationships, and mental health.
