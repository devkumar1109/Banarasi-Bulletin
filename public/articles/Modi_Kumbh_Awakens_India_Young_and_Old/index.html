Modi: Kumbh Awakens India, Young and Old.

2025-02-27T05:56:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi reflected on the concluding Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj, emphasizing the significant participation of young people, challenging the perception of the event as solely for older generations. He highlighted the event as a "Maha Yagya of India’s unity" that awakened the nation's conscience. Modi praised the administration for successfully managing the event, noting the immense scale with over double the population of America attending. He acknowledged criticism but stated the Kumbh would be a case study for management, political, and spiritual experts, adding a new dimension to India's journey.
