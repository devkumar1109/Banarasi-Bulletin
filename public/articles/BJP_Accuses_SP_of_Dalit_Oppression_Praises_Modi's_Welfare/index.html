BJP Accuses SP of Dalit Oppression, Praises Modi's Welfare

2025-05-05T19:10:00+00:00

BJP state general secretary (organisation) Dharampal Singh accused the previous Samajwadi Party government under Akhilesh Yadav of oppressing Dalits and filing false cases against them. He claims that the SP prioritized Muslim appeasement and caste-based division, while the BJP has consistently worked to uplift SC/ST communities and ensure their representation in high positions. Singh criticized Congress for its alleged opposition to Dr. B.R. Ambedkar and its attempts to weaken constitutional rights, including reservations. He highlighted Prime Minister Narendra Modi's initiatives like Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and providing free ration during the Covid-19 pandemic as examples of the BJP's commitment to social welfare.
