Varanasi Fights Disease with New Campaign

2025-04-01T18:45:00+00:00

Varanasi launched a special campaign from April 1st to 31st to control communicable diseases like malaria, dengue, and chikungunya. The campaign, flagged off by UP ministers Ravindra Jaiswal and Dayashankar Mishra, includes anti-larvae spraying and fogging. District officials highlighted a significant decrease in cases compared to previous years, attributing it to inter-departmental coordination and public awareness. SVM Bhelupur hospital introduced new facilities like an ultrasound machine, digital X-ray, and mental health OPD to provide enhanced medical services. The campaign will also include the Dastak campaign from April 10th to 31st to further address disease control.
