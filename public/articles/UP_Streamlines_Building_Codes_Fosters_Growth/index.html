UP Streamlines Building Codes, Fosters Growth

2025-04-29T09:28:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh is set to implement new building construction and development bylaws called "Uttar Pradesh Building Construction and Development Byelaws 2025". Key changes include deregulation for smaller buildings, allowing construction without permission for residential plots up to 100 square meters and commercial buildings up to 30 square meters. The new bylaws also propose removing height restrictions for certain buildings and introducing an online approval system for building plans in sanctioned layout areas designed by licensed professionals. Furthermore, the bylaws aim to streamline the process for service professionals by allowing them to use up to 25% of the FAR for their offices without separate map approvals.
