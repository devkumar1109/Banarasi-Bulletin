Four Teens Killed in Uttar Pradesh Truck Overturn

2025-06-16T04:53:00+00:00

Four teenage boys aged 14 to 16 were killed in a road accident in Uttar Pradesh's Sitarpur district. The boys were on their way to a wedding when a truck carrying rice husk overturned, crushing them. The victims, Munna, Sufian, Altaf, and Alfaj, were residents of Bahraich district. Local residents rushed them to a community health centre, but they were pronounced dead on arrival. Police have launched an investigation into the accident.
