Yogi's Green Birthday: MP to Ram Darbar

2025-06-04T12:49:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath will celebrate his 53rd birthday on June 5, which also coincides with World Environment Day. Adityanath, known for his environmental interests, will mark the occasion with the Prana Pratishtha of Ram Darbar and other deities in Ayodhya's Ram temple. Born Ajay Singh Bisht on June 5, 1972, Adityanath has been a five-time Lok Sabha MP and became Uttar Pradesh's Chief Minister in 2017. He is a prominent figure in the Ram Mandir movement and took sanyaas in 1994 at the age of 22.
