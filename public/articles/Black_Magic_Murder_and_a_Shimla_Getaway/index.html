Black Magic, Murder, and a Shimla Getaway

2025-03-21T07:41:00+00:00

Sahil Shukla, accused of murdering ex-navy officer Saurabh Rajput, exhibited a deep obsession with black magic. His room was covered in satanic graffiti, tantric symbols, and cryptic phrases, along with beer cans and bidi stubs. Police believe Sahil's wife, Muskan Rastogi, and her lover, exploited his superstitions by convincing him that his deceased mother wanted Saurabh dead through fake Snapchat accounts. Saurabh was allegedly drugged, stabbed, and dismembered. His body was concealed in a cement-filled drum for two weeks. The duo then went on a 12-day vacation to Shimla. Police are investigating whether Sahil's occult beliefs played a role in the murder.
