UP Leads With Real-Time Vaccine Disease Watch

2025-05-01T18:34:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has become the first state to implement real-time digital surveillance of vaccine-preventable diseases (VPDs) using its Unified Disease Surveillance Portal (UDSP). This initiative, launched on May 1st, will monitor six VPDs: polio, measles, rubella, diphtheria, pertussis, and tetanus. It will facilitate real-time case reporting, accurate data collection, and quick detection of outbreaks, enabling swift implementation of effective strategies. The UDSP, developed by the UP government post-Covid, integrates disease surveillance data, providing accurate and timely information for better planning and monitoring of immunization programs. Citizens will also have online access to their lab reports. The state is conducting a school-based TD vaccination campaign, administering free vaccines to students in grades 5 and 10.
