Indian Relay Team Blazes to New National Record

2025-04-30T16:01:00+00:00

The Indian men's 4x100m relay team set a new national record of 38.69 seconds at the Indian Open Relay Competition in Chandigarh. This surpassed the previous record of 38.89 seconds set in 2010. The team consisted of Gurindervir Singh, Animesh Kujur, Manikanta Hoblidhar, and Amlan Borgohain. Tamil Nadu came second in the men's 4x100m relay with a time of 39.83 seconds, followed by Army in third with 41.40 seconds. The competition also saw several other notable results in various relay categories for men, women, and U-20 athletes.
