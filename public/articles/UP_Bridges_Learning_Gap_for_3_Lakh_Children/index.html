UP Bridges Learning Gap for 3 Lakh Children

2025-06-12T05:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh has launched 'Sugamya Worksheets' in collaboration with UNICEF to support nearly 3 lakh children with special needs. The worksheets, covering Hindi and Mathematics for classes 1 to 8, are designed to enhance foundational learning and integrate differently-abled children into mainstream education. Developed using simple language, vibrant visuals and engaging formats, the 1,900 worksheets are being distributed in phases to ensure proper assessment and learning. Special educators will visit schools to provide support and guidance, with district coordinators reviewing their monthly reports.
