AyodhYatra App: Digital Pilgrimage Unveiled

2025-03-06T06:45:00+00:00

The Ayodhya Development Authority (ADA) has launched the AyodhYatra app, a virtual pilgrimage experience available on Android and iOS. The app offers live darshans, personalized pujas, and 360-degree temple tours of key Ayodhya sites like the Ram Mandir, Kanak Bhawan, Hanuman Garhi, and Saryu Ghats. It also features live-streamed pujas from over 20 temples and incorporates metaverse technology for immersive experiences of Ayodhya's vibrant streets and festivals, including Deepotsav. Future plans include virtual tours of other major spiritual sites like Kashi Vishwanath, Krishna Janmabhoomi, and Mahakaleshwar, creating a digital pilgrimage network. AyodhYatra also provides personalized astrology services.
