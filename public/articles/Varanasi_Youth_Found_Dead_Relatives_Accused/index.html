Varanasi Youth Found Dead, Relatives Accused

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth from Barki village, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, alleged murder and filed a police complaint. Dinanath stated that Hemant was called to a wedding by his relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar, on Friday. Around 11 pm, the accused called him claiming Hemant attacked them and fled. The next morning, the body was discovered in the canal. Kapsethi police have lodged a named FIR against Pintu and Anil Kumar and are questioning them. The body has been sent for postmortem and further investigation is underway.
