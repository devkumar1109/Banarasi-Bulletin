Yadav Tosses Gold at Taiwan Athletics Open

2025-06-09T19:09:00+00:00

Indian athlete Rohit Yadav won gold in the men's javelin throw at the Taiwan Athletics Open 2025, achieving a throw of 74.42 meters. The event took place in Taipei, Taiwan, from June 7-8 and featured top athletes from across Asia. Yadav, an office superintendent at the Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), showcased the sporting excellence of Indian Railways and BLW on the international stage. BLW General Manager Naresh Pal Singh congratulated Yadav, highlighting his hard work and dedication as evidence that BLW nurtures sporting talent.
