Varanasi Protesters Rage Against Mamata, Demand Her Dismissal

2025-04-14T09:31:00+00:00

In Varanasi, social activists and women from the Vishal Bharat Institute protested against West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee, burning her effigy. They accused her government of supporting violence against Hindus in Murshidabad under the guise of opposing the Waqf Bill. Protesters alleged that Bangladeshi Muslims are attacking Hindu homes, committing atrocities against women and children, and forcing Hindus to flee. They demanded Banerjee's dismissal and arrest, claiming she is disregarding Baba Saheb Ambedkar's ideals while Hindus are facing persecution in her state. The protest also called for a boycott of such leaders.
