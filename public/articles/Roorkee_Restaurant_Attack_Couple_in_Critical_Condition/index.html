Roorkee Restaurant Attack: Couple in Critical Condition

2025-04-24T03:19:00+00:00

In Roorkee, Uttarakhand, a 28-year-old man named Prince Kumar attacked his 27-year-old girlfriend at a restaurant, stabbing her and setting her ablaze after pouring petrol on her. Kumar then attempted suicide by slitting his throat. Both individuals are in critical condition and have been transferred to a Dehradun medical facility. The incident stemmed from Kumar's family disapproving of their relationship, leading the woman's family to arrange a marriage for her elsewhere. Bystanders alerted the police, who are currently investigating the case.
