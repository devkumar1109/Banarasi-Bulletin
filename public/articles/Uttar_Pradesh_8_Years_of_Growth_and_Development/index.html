Uttar Pradesh: 8 Years of Growth and Development

2025-03-24T08:13:00+00:00

The Yogi Adityanath government in Uttar Pradesh has completed eight years in power, marking significant development across various sectors. Key achievements include: * **Rural development:** Over 2.62 crore toilets constructed, free ration distributed to 14.7 crore people during the pandemic, 1.86 crore Ujjwala LPG connections provided, and 56 lakh houses constructed for the underprivileged. * **Law and order:** Establishment of police commissionerates in seven districts, creation of a special task force, and a significant decline in crime rates. * **Infrastructure:** Extensive road and highway development projects, improved connectivity, and investment in public transport. * **Healthcare:** Expansion of medical facilities, increased access to healthcare services, and a focus on maternal and child health. The government's efforts have led to improved living standards, economic growth, and enhanced security for the people of Uttar Pradesh.
