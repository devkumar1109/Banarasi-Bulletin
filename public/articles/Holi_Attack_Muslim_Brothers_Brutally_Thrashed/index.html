Holi Attack: Muslim Brothers Brutally Thrashed

2025-03-12T13:49:00+00:00

Two Muslim brothers, Niyaz Ahmed and Khurshid Ahmed, were brutally assaulted in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh during a Holi donation drive. A group of about a dozen individuals attacked them with sharp weapons and sticks, leaving them with severe injuries. The attackers hurled religious slurs and forced the brothers to chant "Jai Shri Ram." Niyaz was attacked while returning from prayers and Khurshid intervened, also suffering injuries. A viral video shows Niyaz with a head injury and bloodstained shirt. He claims he knows the attackers but has no personal conflict. An FIR has been filed at Sarnath Police Station following public outrage on social media. Police are investigating the incident.
