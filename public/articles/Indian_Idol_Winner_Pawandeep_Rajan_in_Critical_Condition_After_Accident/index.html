Indian Idol Winner Pawandeep Rajan in Critical Condition After Accident

2025-05-06T06:20:00+00:00

Singer Pawandeep Rajan, winner of Indian Idol Season 12, was involved in a serious car accident on May 5 near Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. He suffered multiple fractures and underwent a six-hour surgery. Rajan's team stated that he was unconscious and in severe pain after the accident, requiring a second surgery in 3-4 days. He is currently in the Medical ICU under observation. His team expressed gratitude for the support and prayers from fans worldwide.
