Uttar Pradesh's $1 Trillion 'Sanatan' Tourism Push

2025-03-11T22:54:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's government plans to leverage religious tourism to boost its economy, aiming for a $1-trillion dollar target. Inspired by the success of the recent Maha Kumbh in Prayagraj, the government will develop religious destinations, corridors, and parikrama paths. The focus is on places like Mathura, Naimisharanya, and Chitrakoot, promoting them as part of a "Sanatan culture" package. A significant Rs 1,000 crore has been allocated in the current budget for infrastructure development and a "vedic science" centre. The government aims to improve visitor experience with amenities and camp facilities every 50 km on major highways.
