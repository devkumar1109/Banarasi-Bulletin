Varanasi: 7-Day Gang Rape, 6 Arrested

2025-04-07T08:45:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a 19-year-old woman alleged she was gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. She went missing from home on March 29th and returned on April 4th, reporting the incident to her family. Police have arrested six individuals based on her complaint, filed under various sections of the BNS Act. The woman claims she was held captive and assaulted repeatedly at different locations, including cafes, hotels, and a warehouse. She was allegedly drugged and raped on multiple occasions. Police are continuing their investigation and efforts to apprehend the remaining accused.
