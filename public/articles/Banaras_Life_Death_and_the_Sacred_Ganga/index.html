Banaras: Life, Death, and the Sacred Ganga

2025-03-22T21:17:00+00:00

An exhibition at DAG in Delhi showcases visual representations of Banaras from the colonial era to the present day. The exhibition highlights the city's vibrant essence and explores the intersection of life and death rituals along the sacred Ganga. Photography and videography restrictions at the cremation spaces in Banaras have recently been relaxed. The article mentions Manikarnika and Harishchandra ghats as significant cremation sites, known for offering a liminal space between life and enlightenment in Hindu tradition. The text describes the everyday life surrounding these ghats, where rituals of death coexist with everyday activities.
