Varanasi: Seven Days of Terror, Six Arrested

2025-04-07T07:22:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a 19-year-old woman reported being gang-raped by 23 men over seven days. She went missing on March 29 and returned home on April 4. Police have arrested six men: Raj Vishwakarma, Samir, Ayush, Sohel, Danish, and Anmol. The woman alleges she was drugged, confined, and repeatedly assaulted. The case involves multiple locations, including cafes, hotels, and a warehouse. Police are continuing their investigation to apprehend the remaining suspects.
