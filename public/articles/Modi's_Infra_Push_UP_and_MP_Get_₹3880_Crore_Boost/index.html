Modi's Infra Push: UP and MP Get ₹3,880 Crore Boost

2025-04-10T08:09:00+00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh on April 11th. In Varanasi, he will lay the foundation stone and inaugurate development projects worth over ₹3,880 crore, including road projects, a bridge, and flyovers. He will also inaugurate power transmission substations and associated transmission lines, and projects to improve police facilities and expand education infrastructure. Modi will then travel to Madhya Pradesh, performing darshan and pooja at Guru Ji Maharaj Temple in Isagarh and addressing a public program at Anandpur Dham.
