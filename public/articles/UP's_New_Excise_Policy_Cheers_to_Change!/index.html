UP's New Excise Policy: Cheers to Change!

2025-04-02T10:04:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh's new excise policy for 2025-26 is effective. Key changes include replacing separate liquor and beer shops with composite shops, allowing country liquor shops to sell beer, and introducing low-alcohol bars in major cities. Bars and premium retail outlets can now operate in IT and ITeS establishments exceeding 20,000 square meters. Liquor tasting is permitted in wineries, breweries, and distilleries, and retail shops can be opened within these facilities. A 90 ml category has been added to the regular IMFL section. For the first time in seven years, all liquor shop licenses, including cannabis shops, composite shops, and model shops, will be awarded through an e-lottery system.
