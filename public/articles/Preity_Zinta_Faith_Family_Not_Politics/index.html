Preity Zinta: Faith, Family, Not Politics

2025-04-30T10:56:00+00:00

Actress Preity Zinta addressed rumors of joining the BJP after visiting religious sites in India. She clarified that her visits to the Maha Kumbh Mela and Kashi Vishwanath temple do not signify a political move. Zinta explained that living abroad has deepened her love for India and she wants to instill that pride in her children, who are half-Indian. She emphasized that being proud of one's identity and visiting religious sites should not be misconstrued as political affiliation.
