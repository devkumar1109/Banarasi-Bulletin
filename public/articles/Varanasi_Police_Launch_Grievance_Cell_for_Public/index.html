Varanasi Police Launch Grievance Cell for Public

2025-06-05T19:00:00+00:00

A Public Grievance Cell has been established at the Varanasi district police headquarters to address public complaints. Commissioner of Police Mohit Agrawal announced the cell, appointing Additional Commissioner of Police (crime) Rajesh Kumar Singh as its head. Two subordinates will assist Singh. The cell will operate daily from 10am to 2pm, primarily benefiting complainants from remote areas. The cell aims to ensure proper hearing of complaints and direct concerned station in-charges for feedback, preventing disappointment and facilitating effective action. Agrawal intends to be available at the office to address public and police personnel concerns.
