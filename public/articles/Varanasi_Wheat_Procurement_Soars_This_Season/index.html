Varanasi Wheat Procurement Soars This Season.

2025-04-21T18:19:00+00:00

Wheat procurement in Varanasi division has increased by 3,746.88 metric tons this year compared to 2024, reaching 9,711.59 metric tons so far. The government has implemented measures to benefit farmers, including direct purchase from their homes, waiving verification for up to 100 quintals of wheat, and providing a support price of Rs 2,425 per quintal (Rs 150 higher than last year). Mobile vans are visiting farms to purchase crops, and payments are credited to farmers' Aadhaar-linked accounts within 48 hours. The government aims to procure 127,500 metric tons of wheat in the division.
