Kohli Fan's Death: Stadium Stampede Tragedy

2025-06-07T05:14:00+00:00

A 14-year-old girl, Divyanshi, died in a stampede at the M Chinnaswamy Stadium during a Royal Challengers Bengaluru victory celebration on Wednesday. Her mother, Ashwini, surprised her with a trip to the event despite warnings about the large crowd. Divyanshi, a devoted Virat Kohli fan, was separated from her mother and aunt during the stampede. Ashwini regrets ignoring her father's advice and blames herself for her daughter's death. Divyanshi was among 11 people who died in the tragic incident.
