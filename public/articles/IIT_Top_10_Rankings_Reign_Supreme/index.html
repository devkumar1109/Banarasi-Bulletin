IIT Top 10: Rankings Reign Supreme

2025-04-25T09:27:00+00:00

According to the 2024 NIRF rankings, the top 10 IITs in India are: IIT Madras, IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, IIT Varanasi, and IIT Dhanbad. These institutes are ranked based on five parameters: Research and Professional Practice, Teaching and Learning Resources, Graduation Outcome, Perception, Outreach and Inclusivity. IITs offer various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including Bachelor of Technology (BTech), Bachelor of Science (BSc), Bachelor of Architecture, Dual Degree programs (B.S/M.S, B.Tech/M.Tech), Integrated programs (B.S/M.S, M.Tech), and Dual Degree programs combining BTech/MBA or B.S/MBA. Admission to these programs is through the JEE (Advanced) exam.
