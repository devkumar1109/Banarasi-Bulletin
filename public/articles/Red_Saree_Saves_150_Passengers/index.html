Red Saree Saves 150 Passengers

2025-04-02T01:12:00+00:00

A 70-year-old woman named Omvati Devi from Uttar Pradesh saved 150 train passengers by waving her red saree to warn the driver of a broken railway track. Three years ago, Omvati noticed the damaged track and, remembering that red signals danger, used her saree to alert the approaching train. The driver, seeing the bright red fabric, stopped the train in time, preventing a potential disaster.
