Fugitive Banti Arrested After Road Rage Shooting

2025-04-04T17:02:00+00:00

Rajesh Singh alias Banti, a wanted criminal with 33 cases against him, was arrested within 24 hours of shooting at a man named Nishant. The incident occurred near Lamahi village in Varanasi after a dispute over overtaking. Police recovered an illegal pistol, live and used cartridges from Banti's possession. He sustained a bullet injury during a cross-firing with police during an operation called 'Chakravyuh'. Banti confessed to the shooting. Nishant, who works at an e-marketing company, was returning home on his motorcycle when attacked by four car-borne assailants.
