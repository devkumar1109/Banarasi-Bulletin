UPSC 2024: Legacy Continues, Soham Lal Achieves Rank 235

2025-04-25T09:32:00+00:00

Soham Lal, son of Uttar Pradesh SP Shailendra Lal and grandson of former IG Chhote Lal Gyani, secured an all-India rank of 235 in the UPSC CSE 2024. Hailing from Jaurakheda village in Jalaun district, Soham completed his schooling at DPS Noida and earned a BA in Economics from Delhi University's Hindu College. This marks his fourth attempt at the exam. His sister, Shreya Utkarsh, is an engineer working in Germany.
