Varanasi Battles High Blood Pressure With Awareness Campaign

2025-05-17T23:31:00+00:00

To mark World Hypertension Day, Varanasi's health department launched a month-long campaign focused on public awareness and screening. The campaign emphasizes the importance of regular blood pressure checks, especially for individuals over 30. Activities include walkathons, rallies, quizzes, and screening camps at various locations. Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, can lead to serious health problems like heart attacks, strokes, and kidney disease. Preventive measures include maintaining a balanced diet, exercising regularly, avoiding smoking and alcohol, and managing stress. People with high blood pressure should seek medical treatment and monitor their blood pressure regularly.
