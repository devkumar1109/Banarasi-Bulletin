Navy Officer Murder: Wife, Partner Confess, Family Seeks Justice

2025-03-23T03:20:00+00:00

Saurabh Rajput, an ex-merchant navy officer, was allegedly murdered by his wife Muskan and her partner Sahil on March 4. Rajput's body was dismembered and hidden in a drum. Muskan confessed to the crime after her father urged her to tell the truth. Rajput's mother has appealed to PM Modi and UP CM Yogi Adityanath for a thorough investigation and the death penalty for the culprits. She also expressed concern for her granddaughter's safety and wants custody of her. Muskan's parents have also condemned her actions and called for capital punishment.
