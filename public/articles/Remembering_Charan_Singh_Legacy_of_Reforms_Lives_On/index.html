Remembering Charan Singh: Legacy of Reforms Lives On

2025-05-29T12:30:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath and other leaders paid tribute to former Prime Minister Chaudhary Charan Singh on his death anniversary. CM Yogi highlighted Singh's contributions to land reform, rural development, and transparency in governance in Uttar Pradesh. Vice President Jagdeep Dhankhar also paid respects, remembering Singh's lifelong dedication to farmers and urging them to engage in agricultural trade. Singh, a key figure in the Janata Party, was renowned for his land reforms in Uttar Pradesh and his prolific writings on societal and economic reforms.
