Aishwarya Rai Embraces Indian Heritage at Cannes 2025

2025-05-24T06:15:00+00:00

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan celebrated Indian heritage at Cannes 2025, wearing a custom black gown by Gaurav Gupta with a Banarasi cape embroidered with a Bhagavad Gita shloka. The shloka, "Karmanye Vadhikaraste Ma Phaleshu Kadachana," emphasizes the importance of action without attachment to results. Rai was accompanied by her daughter, Aaradhya, and her look was completed with bold red lips, glowing skin, and wavy hair. As a global brand ambassador for L'Oréal, Rai represented India on the world stage, showcasing her country's beauty and culture.
