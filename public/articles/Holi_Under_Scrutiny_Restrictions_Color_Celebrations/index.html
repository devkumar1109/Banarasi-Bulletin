Holi Under Scrutiny: Restrictions Color Celebrations

2025-03-13T17:46:00+00:00

Holi celebrations across India are facing tight regulations due to political sensitivities and local concerns. In Uttar Pradesh, Section 163 restricts large gatherings in Lucknow until May 12, citing ongoing examinations. West Bengal's government faced controversy for advising against Holi in Shantiniketan to protect the environment, but has not imposed a ban. Telangana implemented stringent guidelines, including banning alcohol sales, restricting vehicle movement in groups, and prohibiting applying colors to unwilling individuals. Bihar has banned DJs during Holi celebrations.
