Varanasi Logistics Hub: Boosting Freight and Employment

2025-05-05T19:00:00+00:00

Varanasi Division Commissioner S Rajalingam has directed officials to expedite the multi-modal terminal and freight village projects. The multi-modal terminal at Ramnagar is under development, with a sewage treatment plant (STP) being planned in collaboration with various departments. The 100-acre freight village, developed by NHLML on the Varanasi-Chandauli border, will be a state-of-the-art Multi-Modal Logistics Park (MMLP). It will be connected to NH-7, NH-2, a 5.1 km railway line, and the Ralhupur Multi-Modal Terminal, facilitating cargo transportation from northeast to south India via National Waterway-1. The project is expected to create investment and employment opportunities.
