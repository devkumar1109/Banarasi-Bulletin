UP Jobs Boom: 7.5 Lakh Positions Filled in Eight Years

2025-03-22T10:39:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government claims to have created over 7.5 lakh government jobs in the past eight years under Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's leadership. Approximately 95,000 candidates were selected through the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) and the Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) during this period. The Personnel Department played a key role in streamlining recruitment processes, including the launch of the e-Adhiachan portal to ensure transparency and efficiency. CM Adityanath distributed appointment letters to newly selected candidates through 35 programs between November 2020 and November 2024.
