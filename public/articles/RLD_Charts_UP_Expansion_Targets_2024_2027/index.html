RLD Charts UP Expansion, Targets 2024, 2027

2025-05-26T20:37:00+00:00

The Rashtriya Lok Dal (RLD) will hold its state executive meeting in Mathura on Tuesday. Key agenda items include expanding the party's grassroots presence across Uttar Pradesh, particularly targeting Muslims and strengthening its base in eastern UP. RLD chief Jayant Chaudhary will address the meeting, focusing on a robust membership drive, engaging with youth, farmers, and marginalized communities. Discussions will also cover strategies for the 2024 panchayat and 2027 state elections. RLD plans to appoint dedicated functionaries to oversee activities in Purvanchal districts, setting up block and village-level committees.
