GIAN Upskills India with Omics Data Analysis Course

2025-06-10T19:20:00+00:00

A week-long Global Initiative of Academic Networks (GIAN) course on "Models and Algorithms For Omics Data Analysis" was held at BHU from June 2 to 6. The course, organized by the School of Biotechnology, aimed to enhance India's scientific and technological capabilities. Prof Rajeev Kumar Azad from the University of North Texas, USA, delivered expert insights during the program. The GIAN initiative, under the Ministry of Education, seeks to improve academic resources and accelerate quality improvement in the country.
