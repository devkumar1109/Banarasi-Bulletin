UP Unveils Jungle Safari Train with Panoramic Views

2025-05-17T13:26:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh launched India's first Vistadome jungle safari train, linking Katarniaghat Wildlife Sanctuary to Dudhwa Tiger Reserve. The train features Vistadome coaches with large glass windows and transparent roofs, offering panoramic views of the lush forest landscapes. The 107-kilometer journey lasts approximately 4 hours and 25 minutes, running on weekends with a planned expansion to daily service. The train departs from Bichia station at 11:45 AM and arrives in Mailani station at 4:10 PM, stopping at nine stations along the route. Tickets cost Rs 275 per person. The initiative aims to boost eco-tourism and attract more visitors to the region.
