Varanasi Braces for Record Kumbh Mela Pilgrim Surge

2025-02-26T00:20:00+00:00

The Maha Kumbh Mela concludes on Wednesday in Prayagraj, leading to an anticipated surge of pilgrims in Varanasi. The Varanasi administration and police are prepared for the influx, anticipating it to be the largest crowd in the district's history. A comprehensive traffic management plan is in place, including barricades, diversions, and vehicle restrictions to curb congestion. Designated holding areas at railway stations will guide passengers to their trains. The Maha Kumbh site is located 130 km from Varanasi, and a large number of pilgrims are expected to travel to Varanasi after taking a final holy bath in Prayagraj.
