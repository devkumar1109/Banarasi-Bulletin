UP Police Recruitment: Caste Claims Debunked

2025-03-16T11:20:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment & Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has denied allegations of caste-based discrimination in the recent police recruitment drive. The Board clarified that candidates are selected based on verified caste certificates, not surnames. This statement comes after rumors spread on social media claiming caste-based selection. The UPPRPB emphasized that the document verification process involves scrutiny by Sub-Divisional Magistrate and Deputy Superintendent of Police level officers who verify original caste certificates. The recruitment drive, which filled 60,244 constable vacancies, saw the written exam conducted between August 23 and August 31, with final results published on March 13.
