UP Police Recruitment: Caste Claims Debunked

2025-03-16T11:20:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment & Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has denied allegations of caste-based discrimination in its recent police constable recruitment drive. The UPPRPB clarified that candidates are selected based on verified caste certificates, not surnames. This statement comes after rumors spread on social media alleging caste-based bias. The recruitment drive filled 60,244 constable vacancies, with the final results released on March 13th. The UPPRPB emphasized that the Document Verification (DV) Board, consisting of Sub-Divisional Magistrate and Deputy Superintendent of Police level officers, meticulously verifies original caste certificates before declaring candidates successful.
