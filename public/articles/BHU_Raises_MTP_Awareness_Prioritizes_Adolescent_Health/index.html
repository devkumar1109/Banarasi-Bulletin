BHU Raises MTP Awareness, Prioritizes Adolescent Health

2025-04-24T07:46:00+00:00

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) hosted a health awareness session focusing on the safe use of MTP kits and adolescent wellbeing. Prof. Mamta from the Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology emphasized the dangers of MTP kit misuse and the importance of medical guidance. The session covered correct usage, side effects, safety precautions, menstrual hygiene, and the significance of HPV vaccination for cervical cancer prevention. The event was organized in collaboration with Mamta Health Institute for Mother and Child, New Delhi, and the National Health Mission, Uttar Pradesh.
