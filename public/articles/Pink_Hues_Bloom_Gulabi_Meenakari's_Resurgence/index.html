Pink Hues Bloom: Gulabi Meenakari's Resurgence

2025-05-02T14:43:00+00:00

Gulabi Meenakari, the pink enameling craft of Varanasi, is experiencing a revival thanks to government support and promotion. Designated a Geographical Indication (GI) product in 2015 and an One District One Product (ODOP) in 2018, the craft has seen its annual business grow to Rs 12-15 crore, employing 250-300 people, 30% of whom are women. Government initiatives, including training, loans, grants, and marketing efforts, have led to a resurgence in demand, both domestically and internationally. Orders for Gulabi Meenakari products, including necklaces, earrings, rings, and anklets, are increasingly coming in for weddings, return gifts, and corporate gifting. Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath have actively promoted Gulabi Meenakari, showcasing it to foreign guests and contributing to its global recognition.
