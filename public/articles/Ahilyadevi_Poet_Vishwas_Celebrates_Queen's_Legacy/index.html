Ahilyadevi: Poet Vishwas Celebrates Queen's Legacy

2025-05-31T17:52:00+00:00

Poet Dr. Kumar Vishwas praised Ahilyadevi Holkar's contributions to governance and India's pilgrimage sites at a National Commission for Women event celebrating her 300th birth anniversary. Vishwas highlighted women's proven capabilities in leadership, citing Ahilyadevi as a shining example. He emphasized the ancient Indian tradition of women's leadership, referencing Vedic history. NCW Chairperson Vijaya Rahatkar described Ahilyadevi as a role model for women, praising her courage, humility, dedication, and political acumen. The event also featured an exhibition and rangoli art celebrating Ahilyadevi's life.
