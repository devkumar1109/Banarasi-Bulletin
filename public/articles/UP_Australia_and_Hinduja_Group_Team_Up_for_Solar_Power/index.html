UP, Australia, and Hinduja Group Team Up for Solar Power

2025-04-22T14:24:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Smart Energy Council of Australia and the Hinduja Group to promote renewable energy development in the state. The MoU focuses on solar skill development, technology transfer, building robust solar and green hydrogen value chains, and fostering bilateral investment and student exchange. The partnership aims to help Uttar Pradesh achieve its target of 22,000 MW solar capacity and contribute to India's goal of 500 GW renewable energy capacity by 2030. The agreement is effective from 2025 to 2027.
