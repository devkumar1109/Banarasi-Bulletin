Uttar Pradesh Honors Ambedkar's Legacy

2025-04-12T15:13:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh government is celebrating Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's 134th birth anniversary with statewide programs. Events include cultural performances, educational programs, a "Bhim Padyatra" march, and a grand event at the Ambedkar Mahasabha campus. A special exhibition will highlight significant moments from Dr. Ambedkar's life and his contributions to justice, equality, and social reform. These initiatives aim to familiarize youth with Dr. Ambedkar's life and principles and engage the public through various programs.
