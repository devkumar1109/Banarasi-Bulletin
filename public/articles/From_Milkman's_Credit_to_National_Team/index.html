From Milkman's Credit to National Team

2025-04-22T02:44:00+00:00

Pooja Yadav, a 20-year-old from Varanasi, has become the first woman from Benaras to play for the Indian women's hockey team. Her journey began when her father, a milkman, bought her first pair of hockey shoes on credit from a local shopkeeper. Pooja's early training involved playing cricket with boys, fostering her belief in her abilities. Despite facing skepticism from neighbors, her family, particularly her mother and sisters, encouraged her to pursue her dream. Pooja secured a spot at the Lucknow sports hostel to alleviate the financial burden on her family.
