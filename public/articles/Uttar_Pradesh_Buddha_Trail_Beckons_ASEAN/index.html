Uttar Pradesh: Buddha Trail Beckons ASEAN

2025-06-09T03:23:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh organized a six-day 'Bodhi Yatra' to showcase its Buddhist heritage, attracting 50 delegates from five ASEAN countries (Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam). The initiative, led by CM Yogi Adityanath and the Uttar Pradesh Tourism Department, aimed to boost tourism and global recognition of the state's Buddhist sites. Key destinations included Shravasti, Kapilvastu, Kushinagar, and Sarnath. Tourism and Culture Minister Jaiveer Singh emphasized the state's commitment to enhancing its global presence. Efforts are underway to improve infrastructure and visitor facilities while preserving the cultural significance of these sacred sites.
