Mumbai Man Jumps to Gold at World Masters Games

2025-06-14T23:03:00+00:00

Fareez Vasania, a 61-year-old former advertising professional from Mumbai, won three gold medals in high jump, long jump, and triple jump at the World Masters Games (WMG) in Taiwan. Vasania, who trained at the Dadar Parsi Colony Gymkhana, competed against former Olympians at the event, which attracted over 25,000 athletes from over 100 countries. He previously won a silver medal at the WMG in Sydney in 2009. Vasania's athletic background includes playing professional football for Air India and his current passion for billiards. He credits his success to his lifelong dedication to sports, influenced by his mother, a female motorcyclist, and his father, a boxer.
