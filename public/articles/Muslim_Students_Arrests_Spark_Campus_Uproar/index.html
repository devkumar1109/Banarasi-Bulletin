Muslim Students Arrests Spark Campus Uproar

2025-03-19T14:36:00+00:00

Six Muslim students were arrested for protesting the arrest of Khalid Pradhan, a fellow student detained for offering namaz on campus at IIMT University in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. Around 400 students participated in a peaceful silent demonstration demanding Pradhan's release. Police intervened, using lathis to disperse the crowd. The protesting students expressed frustration over the disparity in treatment, noting that Hindu pujas are also celebrated on campus. Authorities labelled the protest illegal.
