India Deports Pakistanis After Pahalgam Attack

2025-04-26T17:40:00+00:00

Following a terror attack in Pahalgam that killed 26 tourists, India has ordered the deportation of Pakistani nationals on short-term visas. Most visas were revoked by April 27th, with the Home Minister, Amit Shah, ensuring implementation across states. In Uttar Pradesh, the process is nearly complete, with one Pakistani national scheduled to depart by April 30th. Authorities are monitoring those on long-term visas, many of whom are seeking Indian citizenship.
