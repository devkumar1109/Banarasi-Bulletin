Varanasi Weavers Weave Tribute to Army's Might

2025-05-23T08:37:00+00:00

Varanasi weavers created special sarees inspired by 'Operation Sindoor' to honor the Indian Army. The sarees feature designs of weapons like BrahMos, S-400, and Rafale. This initiative aims to recognize the bravery and sacrifices of the armed forces.
