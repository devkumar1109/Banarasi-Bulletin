Deras: Beyond the Headlines, Seeking Solace

2025-05-04T16:36:00+00:00

Santosh K Singh's new book, "The Deras: Culture, Diversity and Politics," explores the complex world of Punjab's Deras, socio-religious institutions often misunderstood. Singh's research, sparked by observing laborers wearing Dera symbols, reveals the Deras' rich history and their role in Punjab's social and political life. While some Deras are controversial, Singh argues that they shouldn't define the entire spectrum. Many Deras, particularly those with large Dalit followings, offer solace and a sense of community, especially for Dalits seeking equality in a caste-ridden society. Deras, stemming from the South Asian tradition of philosophical dissent, provide a space for spiritual seekers outside established religions. Singh emphasizes the importance of understanding the diverse experiences within the Dera community beyond sensationalized narratives.
