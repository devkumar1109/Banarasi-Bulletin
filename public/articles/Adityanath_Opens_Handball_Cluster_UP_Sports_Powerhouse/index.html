Adityanath Opens Handball Cluster, UP Sports Powerhouse

2025-04-08T00:31:00+00:00

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated the All India Police Handball Cluster 2024-2025 in Lucknow. The event involves 75 teams, 1,341 players (including 336 women) from across India. Adityanath highlighted the state's investment in sports infrastructure, including 84 stadiums, 67 multipurpose halls, and specialized facilities. He mentioned over 65,000 men and women receiving sports kits through the Mangal Dal initiative. The UP Police has participated in over 500 international competitions, and medal-winning players are recruited into the force. 740 players are accommodated in 44 sports hostels across 18 districts, and three sports colleges offer world-class infrastructure and training.
