Ghazipur Dealer Murdered in Chandauli Attack

2025-03-07T14:59:00+00:00

A property dealer, Pawan Yadav (29), from Ghazipur district was murdered in Chandauli district on Thursday night. He was attacked by unknown assailants while transporting water from Janso Ki Madai to his under-construction house in Naikot village. The assailants used bricks and rods to assault Yadav, who succumbed to his injuries at the hospital. Police have launched an investigation into the case.
