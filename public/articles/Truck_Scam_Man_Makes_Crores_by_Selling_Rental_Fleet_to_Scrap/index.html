Truck Scam: Man Makes Crores by Selling Rental Fleet to Scrap

2025-05-24T18:43:00+00:00

Abhishek Yadav, a 28-year-old man from Madhya Pradesh, was arrested in Varanasi for selling 20 trucks worth Rs 4 crore to scrap dealers using fake registration documents. Yadav would rent trucks from owners through brokers, promising higher-than-standard rental rates, and then sell them to scrap dealers for a profit. The investigation began after multiple complaints about missing trucks and suspicious booking agents. Police discovered Yadav was contacting truck owners through Facebook brokers in various districts, bribing them to gain the owners' trust. Yadav sold the trucks to a scrap dealer in Nagpur for Rs 5 lakh each.
