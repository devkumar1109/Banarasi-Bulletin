"Politics and Pitch: BJP Slams Opposition as India Scores Big on Field and Economy"

2025-02-23T18:29:00+00:00

BJP spokesman Sudhanshu Trivedi accused the opposition of seeking political gain in the Maha Kumbh tragedy, targeting Samajwadi Party's Akhilesh Yadav for questioning the death toll. Trivedi praised the successful organization of Maha Kumbh in UP, citing its economic and cultural benefits. He also criticized the opposition for opposing India's efforts to tackle illegal immigration. Trivedi highlighted the Union budget's inclusive features, aiming to make India a developed nation by 2047. Additionally, India won against Pakistan in the Champions Trophy 2025, with Virat Kohli scoring an unbeaten ton, amidst geopolitical tensions and other global news, including Elon Musk facing backlash and Taylor Swift's Super Bowl halftime show condition.
