Varanasi Horror: 23 Men Accused in Gruesome Gang Rape

2025-04-16T10:22:00+00:00

A 19-year-old woman in Varanasi was allegedly drugged and gang-raped by 23 men over six days from March 29 to April 4, 2025. 13 suspects have been arrested and a high-level investigation is underway. Prime Minister Narendra Modi, during a visit to Varanasi, ordered the strictest action against the perpetrators and called for systemic reforms to prevent such crimes. The Uttar Pradesh police have registered an FIR against all 23 men and removed a senior officer from the case amid allegations of negligence. The survivor’s family and activists are demanding swift justice and stronger protections for women. Women's rights organizations are protesting, calling for comprehensive reforms in police training, victim support, and accountability mechanisms.
