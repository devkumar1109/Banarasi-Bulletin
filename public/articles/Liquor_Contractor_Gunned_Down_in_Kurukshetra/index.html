Liquor Contractor Gunned Down in Kurukshetra

2025-06-14T06:01:00+00:00

A liquor contractor named Shantanu was shot dead on Friday evening near Meena Market on the national highway in Shahabad, Kurukshetra district, Haryana. Two assailants on a sports bike fired seven bullets at Shantanu after he briefly stopped his car. Eyewitnesses reported the attackers firing eight rounds before fleeing. They abandoned their bike, stole a motorcycle at gunpoint, and escaped towards Ambala on the wrong side of the highway. Shantanu was pronounced dead on arrival at Adesh Hospital. Police have registered a case and intensified security measures, including blockades on major routes and city borders, as they search for the perpetrators.
