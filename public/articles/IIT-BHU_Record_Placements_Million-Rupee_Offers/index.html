IIT-BHU: Record Placements, Million-Rupee Offers

2025-06-16T18:55:00+00:00

IIT-BHU achieved record placements in 2024-25, with over 1,373 job offers, including four international placements. 15 students secured annual packages exceeding Rs 1 crore, with the highest offer reaching Rs 2.20 crore. Over 300 companies recruited students for roles in software development, analytics, finance, consulting, and core engineering. IIT-BHU Director Prof Amit Patra attributed the success to the institute's updated curriculum, interdisciplinary approach, and strong alumni network.
