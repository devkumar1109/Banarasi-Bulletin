Rampur Rapist Caught After Bloody Encounter

2025-04-17T07:51:00+00:00

A 24-year-old man was arrested in an encounter with Uttar Pradesh Police for allegedly sexually assaulting an 11-year-old deaf-mute Dalit girl in Rampur district. The victim suffered multiple injuries and burn marks on her body, allegedly caused by cigarette butts. She was found unconscious and naked by villagers. Police identified the accused through CCTV footage and hospital records. During the arrest attempt, the accused fired at the police, leading to an exchange of fire where he sustained a bullet injury to his leg. The girl was first taken to a primary health centre and then transferred to Meerut district hospital.
