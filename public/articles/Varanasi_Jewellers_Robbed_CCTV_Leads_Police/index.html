Varanasi Jewellers Robbed, CCTV Leads Police

2025-03-09T19:32:00+00:00

In Varanasi, a jeweller named Siyaram (53) and his son Vikas (24) were shot at and robbed of cash and jewellery by three unidentified men on a motorcycle. The incident occurred around 3:30 pm on Sunday at their shop in the Aharak area. Police reached the scene and rushed the injured father and son to a private hospital, where they are reported to be in stable condition. CCTV footage captured clear images of the robbers, and police have launched a massive search operation across the district.
