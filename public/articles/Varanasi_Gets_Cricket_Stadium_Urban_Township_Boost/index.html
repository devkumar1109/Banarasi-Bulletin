Varanasi Gets Cricket Stadium, Urban Township Boost

2025-06-10T23:02:00+00:00

Varanasi's first urban township will be built on 150 acres near the new international cricket stadium in Ganjari. The Varanasi Development Authority approved the project at its recent meeting. The stadium, with a seating capacity of 30,000, is being constructed at a cost of Rs 451 crore and is expected to open in 2026. The new township project was the only major one discussed at the meeting. The meeting also saw the inauguration of a new record room with a storage capacity of 80,000 files. A citizen app and complaint management system portal were also launched. Other approvals included a new access road between Transport Nagar Scheme and NH-2, and amendments to building bylaws.
