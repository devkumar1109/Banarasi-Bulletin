Youth Found Dead, Relatives Accused of Murder

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, alleged murder and filed a complaint against two relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar. According to Dinanath, Hemant was called to a wedding ceremony by the accused on Friday night. They later informed Dinanath that Hemant attacked them and fled. Hemant's body was discovered in the canal the next morning. Police have sent the body for postmortem, questioned the accused, and are investigating the case further.
