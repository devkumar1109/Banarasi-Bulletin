Youth Found Dead, Relatives Accused of Murder

2025-04-26T19:12:00+00:00

The body of Hemant Kumar Bind, a youth from Barki village, was found in a canal near Anarkali School in Dilawalpur village, Varanasi. His father, Dinanath, has alleged murder and filed a police complaint. Dinanath stated that Hemant was called to a wedding by relatives, Pintu and Anil Kumar, on Friday evening. Around 11 pm, the accused informed him that Hemant had attacked them and fled. The police have lodged a named FIR against Pintu and Anil Kumar and are questioning them. The body has been sent for postmortem and further investigation is underway.
