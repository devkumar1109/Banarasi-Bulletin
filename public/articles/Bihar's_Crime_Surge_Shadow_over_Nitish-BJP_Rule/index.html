Bihar's Crime Surge: Shadow over Nitish-BJP Rule

2025-04-03T15:27:00+00:00

Crime against women and children has surged in Bihar under the Nitish Kumar-BJP government, according to Congress spokesperson Ajoy Kumar. He cited NCRB data showing a 323% increase in crime cases between 2005 and 2022, with Bihar ranking second in murders nationally. Congress leaders also alleged increased crime against Dalits and Scheduled Castes in BJP-ruled states. They vowed to expose the Nitish-BJP government's record on law and order.
