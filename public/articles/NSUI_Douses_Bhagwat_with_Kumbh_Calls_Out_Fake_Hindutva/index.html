NSUI Douses Bhagwat with Kumbh, Calls Out "Fake Hindutva"

2025-04-04T09:43:00+00:00

NSUI activists protested against RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat during his four-day visit to Varanasi. They attempted to offer him Maha Kumbh water, criticizing his absence from the religious ritual and questioning his commitment to Sanatan tradition. The activists alleged that Bhagwat only practices Hindutva in speeches and propaganda, not in reality. Police detained several NSUI members during the protest.
