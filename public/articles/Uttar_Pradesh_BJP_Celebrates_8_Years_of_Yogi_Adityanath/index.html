Uttar Pradesh BJP Celebrates 8 Years of Yogi Adityanath

2025-03-23T16:28:00+00:00

The Uttar Pradesh BJP will celebrate eight years of Yogi Adityanath's government from March 24 to April 14 with district-level programs. The party will highlight the government's achievements in areas like village development, farmer welfare, women's empowerment, youth initiatives, and crime control. Beneficiary fairs, development seminars, and village council meetings will be held to showcase the BJP government's work. Cleanliness programs will be organized on April 14, Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar's birthday, to emphasize the government's commitment to social justice and harmony.
