Eid Tragedy: Man's Suicide Sparks Probe

2025-06-07T18:47:00+00:00

A 60-year-old man, Ish Mohammed Ansari, died by suicide on Eid-ul-Azha in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh. He reportedly slit his throat inside a hut near his home. Local residents claimed he left a note suggesting self-sacrifice for spiritual reasons, but police haven't confirmed its existence. Ansari's family took him to the district medical college, where he was later transferred to Gorakhpur Medical College, but he died from his injuries. Police are investigating the incident, considering all possibilities, while preliminary findings suggest self-infliction.
