Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train: Speedy Rail Link to Bihar

2025-03-24T13:29:00+00:00

The Varanasi-Howrah Bullet Train is a proposed 760 km high-speed rail corridor connecting Varanasi and Howrah, with speeds up to 350 km/h. The route passes through key cities like Buxar, Arrah, Patna, Gaya, Dhanbad, Asansol, Durgapur, and Burdwan, including approximately 260 km of elevated tracks within Bihar. The project aims to significantly reduce travel time between Varanasi and Howrah to just over two hours. Currently in the Detailed Project Report (DPR) stage, surveys are underway to finalize the route and assess land requirements. Land acquisition has begun in Bihar, awaiting railway approval for construction.
