Yeddyurappa Calls Siddaramaiah "Pakistan Agent"

2025-04-27T14:09:00+00:00

Karnataka BJP leader, B.S. Yeddyurappa, accused former Chief Minister Siddaramaiah of acting as a "Pakistan agent" following his comments on the Pahalgam terror attack. Yeddyurappa alleged that Siddaramaiah's statement downplaying the attack was insensitive and aimed at garnering sympathy for terrorists. This accusation comes amidst escalating political tensions in Karnataka.
