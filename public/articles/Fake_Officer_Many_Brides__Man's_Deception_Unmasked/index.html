"Fake Officer, Many Brides: Man's Deception Unmasked"

2025-03-23T19:11:00+00:00

Rajan Singh, from Sonbhadra, has been accused of posing as an excise officer to deceive and marry multiple women. Police are investigating how many women fell victim to his scheme. Eight to nine women, mostly schoolteachers, are believed to have been married by Rajan. One victim, Kiran, alleges that Rajan took Rs 42 lakh from her after marrying her in 2019. Another woman, Sarita, claims Rajan married her in 2014, took Rs 10 lakh, and prevented her from visiting his home. Rajan was apprehended and is currently being interrogated. Police are working to determine the full extent of his deception.
