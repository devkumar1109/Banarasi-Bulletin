Lightning Strike Leads Cops to Murderer

2025-06-12T05:01:00+00:00

Pune Police solved a murder case using CCTV footage captured during a lightning strike. The victim, a woman, was attacked on the night of May 20, 2025, and died on the spot. Police analyzed 320 CCTV cameras, 1,200 TVS rickshaws, and 150 records, leading to the identification of the accused, Shahrukh Shakeel Mansoor (27), from Uttar Pradesh. The investigation spanned over 3,200 kilometers. CCTV footage, briefly illuminated by lightning, showed the victim in a black-hooded TVS rickshaw. Shahrukh's rickshaw was identified through penalty records, ultimately leading to his arrest.
