Triple Deception: Man Marries Again,

2025-03-22T15:51:00+00:00

In Uttar Pradesh, a man identified as Rajan has been accused of tricking three women into marriage. He falsely claimed to be a widower and an excise department employee. Rajan married Kiran in 2022, convincing her to take a Rs 42 lakh loan for a house. He also previously married Sarita in 2014, with whom he had a child, and Neelima Singh. Kiran filed a complaint after discovering Rajan's lies about his employment. Further investigation revealed Rajan's other marriages and the deception involved.
