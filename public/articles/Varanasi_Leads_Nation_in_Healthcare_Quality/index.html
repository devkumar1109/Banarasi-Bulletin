Varanasi Leads Nation in Healthcare Quality

2025-06-12T23:29:00+00:00

Varanasi district has achieved a national first by having 104 medical units certified with the National Quality Assurance Standards (NQAS). This includes Shri Shiv Prasad Gupta Divisional Hospital, Community Health Center Cholapur, 10 urban and rural primary health centers, and 92 Ayushman Arogya Temples (health sub-centers). The certification reflects the district's commitment to quality healthcare and access to better services. Ayushman Arogya Temples scored well on seven standards set by the Indian government, covering areas like pregnancy care, child health, family planning, and management of communicable and non-communicable diseases. All remaining Ayushman Arogya temples in rural areas are currently enrolled for NQAS. Urban and rural community and primary health centers are also undergoing the assessment process.
