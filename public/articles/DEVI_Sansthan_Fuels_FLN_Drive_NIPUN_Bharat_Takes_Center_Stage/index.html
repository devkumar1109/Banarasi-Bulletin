DEVI Sansthan Fuels FLN Drive: NIPUN Bharat Takes Center Stage

2025-05-09T04:32:00+00:00

DEVI Sansthan's Leadership Circle 3, held in Lucknow, brought together over 600 educators and policymakers to discuss Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) and the NIPUN Bharat Mission. The event featured Chief Advisor to the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh, Awanish Kumar Awasthi, as the Chief Guest. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between DEVI Sansthan and the district administration of Asifabad, Telangana, to pilot the ALfA program. The initiative saw participation from 10 states and union territories, with a focus on empowering schools for NIPUN assessments. DEVI Sansthan highlighted UP's progress in NIPUN implementation, with 57,000 schools achieving NIPUN readiness out of 80,000 applications.
