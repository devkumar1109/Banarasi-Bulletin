Adani Power Wins 1,500 MW UP Thermal Contract

2025-05-06T12:42:00+00:00

Gautam Adani's Adani Power has secured a contract to supply 1,500 megawatts of power from a thermal power plant in Uttar Pradesh. The contract, awarded through a competitive bidding process, will see Adani Power supply power at a per unit cost of Rs 5.383. Uttar Pradesh's energy demand is rising due to economic growth, with peak demand exceeding 30,000 MW last year. This contract is significant for Adani Power, one of India's largest private thermal power producers, and adds to the Adani Group's expanding portfolio.
