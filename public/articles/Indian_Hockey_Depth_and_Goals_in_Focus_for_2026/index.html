Indian Hockey: Depth and Goals in Focus for 2026

2025-04-19T14:51:00+00:00

India's men's hockey head coach Craig Fulton expressed satisfaction with the depth of talent showcased by the top four teams at the recent Senior Men’s National Championships. Punjab, led by Hardik Singh, won the title, defeating Madhya Pradesh. Fulton highlighted the strong goalkeeping and the emergence of new players. He emphasized the need to build depth for the upcoming 18 months, crucial for the 2026 World Cup cycle. Fulton also identified areas for improvement, particularly in scoring field goals and tactical adaptability. He emphasized the importance of athleticism, game awareness, and quick tactical adjustments. Hockey India has invited former Australian striker Michael McCann to work on field goal conversions, a focus area for Fulton.
