Groom Arrives on JCB, Exits on Bulldozer Procession

2025-02-24T10:21:00+00:00

A wedding in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, saw a groom arriving on a JCB and leaving in a procession of bulldozers. The groom, Rahul Yadav, was sent off in this unique manner on February 23, 2025, by his bride's family. The sight of bulldozers, which have recently been used by the Uttar Pradesh government to demolish properties of rioters, surprised onlookers.
