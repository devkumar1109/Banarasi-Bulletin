Mahakumbh 2025: Faith, Sports, and Sanitation Success

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh 2025 in Prayagraj drew 62 crore devotees, significantly impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. Kashi Vishwanath Temple saw over 2 crore visitors since the event's start, while 69 lakh booked homestays in Ayodhya. The event included the Khel Maha Kumbh, promoting indigenous sports and aiming to include them in the 2036 Olympics. A notable achievement was the "zero discharge" initiative, managing wastewater from 1.5 lakh toilets daily without polluting the river, setting a global sanitation benchmark.
