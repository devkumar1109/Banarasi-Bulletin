Mahakumbh 2025: Faith, Tourism, and Green Success.

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh 2025 in Prayagraj attracted 62 crore devotees, significantly boosting religious tourism in Kashi and Ayodhya. Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi witnessed over 2 crore devotees since the Kumbh began, while Ayodhya saw 69 lakh homestay bookings via the Divya Ayodhya app. The event featured the "Khel Maha Kumbh," a sports event promoting indigenous Indian sports and aiming to include them in the 2036 Olympics. Mahakumbh 2025 implemented a successful "zero discharge" initiative, managing wastewater from over 1.5 lakh toilets daily without polluting the river, setting a global sanitation benchmark.
