U.P. Rajkiya Nigam: Officers Accused of Rs 130 Crore Embezzlement

2025-02-24T00:07:00+00:00

Five former officers of the Uttar Pradesh Rajkiya Nirman Nigam have been booked for embezzling Rs 130 crore. The case was registered based on a complaint filed by Sunil Kumar Malik, Additional Project Manager of the Dehradun unit of the organization.
