Bulldozer Baraat: Groom Arrives in Style

2025-02-24T10:21:00+00:00

In a unique wedding send-off in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, the groom, Rahul Yadav, arrived on a JCB and was escorted by nearly a dozen bulldozers. This unconventional baraat took place on February 23, 2025, as the bride, Karishma, departed for her new home. The unusual procession surprised onlookers, who were accustomed to grooms arriving in luxury cars. The use of bulldozers, recently linked to the Uttar Pradesh government, added a distinct and memorable touch to the celebratory event.
