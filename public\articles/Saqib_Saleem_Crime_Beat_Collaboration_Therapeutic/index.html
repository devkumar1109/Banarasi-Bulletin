Saqib Saleem: "Crime Beat" Collaboration Therapeutic

2025-02-23T19:00:00+00:00

Saqib Saleem describes working with filmmaker Sudhir Mishra on the ZEE5 series "Crime Beat" as "therapeutic." Saleem praises Mishra's youthful energy and empowering approach to directing. He believes the collaboration has made him a better actor. In "Crime Beat," Saleem portrays Abhishek Sinha, an ambitious young reporter from Varanasi. Despite a 14-year career, Saleem remains driven to showcase his versatility. Beyond acting, Saleem co-founded Elemen 3 Entertainment with Huma Qureshi and Mudassar Aziz. Their neo-noir crime comedy "Baby Do Die Do" starring Qureshi, Sikandar Kher, and Chunky Panday is in production and expected to release this year.
