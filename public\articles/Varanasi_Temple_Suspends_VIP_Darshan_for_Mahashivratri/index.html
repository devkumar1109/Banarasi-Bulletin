Varanasi Temple Suspends VIP Darshan for Mahashivratri

2025-02-23T15:26:00+00:00

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi has suspended VIP darshan for three days (February 25-27) due to the anticipated massive crowd of devotees on Mahashivratri. All protocol darshans are banned, and even VIP pass holders will have to follow the regular darshan procedure. Over one crore devotees have already visited the temple from February 1-17. The temple trust is appealing for cooperation in managing the crowd and ensuring the safety of devotees. Authorities have closed schools up to Class 8 in Varanasi due to overcrowding caused by the influx of pilgrims attending the Mahakumbh in Prayagraj. Heavy congestion is reported at transport hubs and key areas.
