Health Care Behind Bars: Red Cross Aids Gorakhpur Jail Inmates

2025-02-23T18:32:00+00:00

The Indian Red Cross Society, Gorakhpur branch, organized a health camp at Gorakhpur district jail on Sunday. 120 inmates underwent skin disease screenings and 110 received medicines. Gynecologist Dr. Pooja Shukla conducted health check-ups and provided treatments for female inmates. Raji Hospital doctors examined 133 inmates' eyesight and distributed glasses to 30. SP DK Pandey praised the initiative and pledged continued support for such programs.
