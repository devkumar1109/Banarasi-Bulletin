BJP Blasts Opposition Over Maha Kumbh Tragedy

2025-02-23T18:29:00+00:00

BJP spokesperson Sudhanshu Trivedi accused the opposition of seeking political gain from the Maha Kumbh tragedy. He criticized Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav for questioning the death toll and using terms like "Mrityu Kumbh." Trivedi also highlighted the success of the Maha Kumbh in boosting UP's economy and cultural awakening. He praised PM Modi's policies for changing the tourism landscape in India. Trivedi further criticized the opposition for opposing India's efforts to tackle illegal immigration, contrasting their stance with the BJP's strong position on the issue.
