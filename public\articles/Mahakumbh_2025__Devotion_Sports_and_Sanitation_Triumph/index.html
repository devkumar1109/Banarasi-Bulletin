Mahakumbh 2025: Devotion, Sports, and Sanitation Triumph

2025-02-24T03:43:00+00:00

The Mahakumbh 2025 in Prayagraj attracted 62 crore devotees, significantly impacting Kashi and Ayodhya. The event featured a sports event, Khel Maha Kumbh, promoting indigenous sports and aiming for inclusion in the 2036 Olympics. A unique "zero discharge" initiative successfully managed wastewater from 1.5 lakh toilets daily, setting a global sanitation benchmark. Prime Minister Narendra Modi participated in the event, taking a holy dip and performing puja. The Mahakumbh also saw a surge in footfall at the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi and a rise in homestay bookings in Ayodhya.
