India's Nuclear-Powered AI Ambitions

2025-02-24T12:18:00+00:00

India is strategically investing in nuclear energy to fuel its AI growth. The nation aims to develop a complete AI ecosystem, from semiconductor fabrication and data centers to foundational models, powered by reliable and cost-effective nuclear energy. India plans to invest Rs 20,000 crore in research and development of Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) to increase its nuclear capacity to 100 GW by 2047. This investment aligns with India's ambition to build AI models relevant to its context and culture, while also positioning itself as a major player in the global semiconductor industry. Nuclear energy is seen as crucial for powering the vast computational demands of AI infrastructure, ensuring India's competitiveness in the global AI race.
