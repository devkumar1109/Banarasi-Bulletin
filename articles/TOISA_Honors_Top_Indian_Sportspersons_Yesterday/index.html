"Stars of Sports Shine: TOISA Honors India's Finest in Lucknow"

2025-02-23T19:59:00+00:00

The Times of India Sports Awards (TOISA) honored top sportspersons in Lucknow. Uttar Pradesh was awarded the Best State for Sports Promotion, received by sports minister Girish Chandra Yadav. Special Recognition was given to athletes from UP, including Vijay Sharma, Shivpal Singh, and Shaili Singh. Young athletes Gukesh Dommaraju and Manu Bhaker were awarded by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. Other awardees included Suhas LY, Sumit Antil, Manish Narwal, Avani Lekhara, and Kapil Parmar. Abhinav Bindra received the Mentor of the Year award, while the Federation of the Year award went to the Paralympics Committee of India and Hockey India. Kapil Dev received the Lifetime Achievement Award, praising the country's sports development and thanking TOISA for recognizing players' hard work.
