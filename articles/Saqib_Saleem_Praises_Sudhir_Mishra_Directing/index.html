"Saleem Shines: Actor Hails Sudhir Mishra's 'Empowering' Direction in 'Crime Beat'"

2025-02-23T19:00:00+00:00

Saqib Saleem calls working with Sudhir Mishra 'therapeutic' as Mishra 'empowers' actors. Saleem plays Abhishek Sinha, a young reporter in 'Crime Beat', mentored by Amir Akhtar, played by Danish Hussain. Saleem remains hungry for versatile roles. He co-founded Elemen 3 Entertainment with Huma Qureshi and Mudassar Aziz, producing 'Baby Do Die Do' starring Huma Qureshi, Sikandar Kher, and Chunky Panday. 'Crime Beat' reviews praise Saleem as a show-stealer, while Sudhir Mishra champions women filmmakers and criticizes 'jingoistic' cinema.
