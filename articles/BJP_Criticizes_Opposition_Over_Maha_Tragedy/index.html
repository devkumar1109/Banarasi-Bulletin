"Politics Over Pilgrimage: BJP Slams Opposition Amid Maha Kumbh Tragedy"

2025-02-23T18:29:00+00:00

BJP spokesman Sudhanshu Trivedi stated that the opposition is seeking political gain in the Maha Kumbh tragedy. He criticized Samajwadi Party's Akhilesh Yadav for questioning the death toll and noted Yadav's past comments on Covid-19 vaccines and the Ram Lalla consecration. Trivedi praised the successful organization of Maha Kumbh, saying it boosted the economy and cultural awakening. He also highlighted the Union budget's benefits for all sections of society. Additionally, 12 Indians deported by the US to Panama have returned to India, amidst the government's efforts to tackle illegal immigration, which the opposition has been opposing, particularly the introduction of CAA and NRC.
