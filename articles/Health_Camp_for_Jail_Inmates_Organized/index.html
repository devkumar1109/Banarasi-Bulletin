"Jail Inmates Receive Medical Aid: Red Cross Society Organizes Health Camp Behind Bars"

2025-02-23T18:32:00+00:00

The Indian Red Cross Society, Gorakhpur branch, organized a health camp at the district jail on Sunday. 120 inmates underwent skin disease screening by Dr Ratnakar Shukla, and 110 received medicines. Gynecologist Dr Pooja Shukla conducted health check-ups for female inmates and provided necessary treatments. Additionally, doctors from Raji Hospital examined the eyesight of 133 inmates and distributed glasses to 30. SP DK Pandey praised the initiative and assured continued support for such programs, highlighting the importance of providing healthcare to inmates. The health camp aimed to address the medical needs of the inmates, and the organizers ensured that necessary treatments and medicines were provided.
